[Riverside]

Kasumi: Hey, Arisa, do you think those dark clouds are rain clouds?
Kasumi: It feels kinda humid too, like it could start raining any moment?
Arisa: Oh yeah, I think the forecast said rain.
Arisa: Rain on a day without P.E. is such a waste. Just makes walking annoying...
Arisa: Hey, Kasumi, did you even bring an umbrella?
Kasumi: Fufufu, glad you asked... Ta-da!
Kasumi: Look, look! I just bought this new folding umbrella!
Kasumi: See? It’s got stars on it and it’s super cute!
Arisa: Yeah, the color’s cute too.
Kasumi: Right? The size is perfect too—I fell in love at first sight~!
Arisa: Hey, don’t open it before it even rains.
Kasumi: But I wanna use it already!
Kasumi: Come on, if you had a new umbrella, you’d wanna use it right away too, wouldn’t you?
Arisa: Hmm~... Maybe not as much as you, but I’d be curious to try it out.
Kasumi: See! I want it to rain so badly, I’m thinking of making a teru teru bozu doll when we get to school!
Arisa: Ah, the one you hang upside down to make it rain.
Kasumi: Wanna make one with me?
Arisa: Nope. Seriously... getting that excited over a new umbrella is kind of a talent.
Kasumi: Ehehe, I know, right♪
Arisa: That wasn’t a compliment.
Kasumi: Oh, we’ve got practice after school today. We’re going home together, right? If it rains, want to share my umbrella?
Arisa: No thanks, I brought my own. And there’s no way we both fit under your tiny umbrella.
Kasumi: That’s not true~! If we just squeeze in like this...
Arisa: Whoa—hey, Kasumi!
Kasumi: See? You fit!
Arisa: Yeah, but half of your body’s sticking out...
Arisa: And we look ridiculous doing this when it’s not even raining! People are staring!
Kasumi: Aww~... fine. I’ll keep it folded till after school~.
Arisa: You should’ve done that in the first place—wait, crap!
Kasumi: What’s wrong?
Arisa: The time! We’re gonna be late!
Kasumi: What!? You’re right! Hurry, Arisa!
Arisa: Whoa—hey! Don’t pull my hand like that!~~!
(White cut)
(Background change)


<Rain or Shine Pt. 2>

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Tae Hanazono, Rimi Ushigome, Saya Yamabuki, Arisa Ichigaya)

[Recess]

[Hanasakigawa Girls' Academy - Class 3A]

Saya: Brought some bread for dessert, but it might be a little soggy from the rain... What do you think?
Kasumi: Mmm~! Totally fine! It's fluffy and sweet and super tasty!
Rimi: Yamabuki Bakery’s new anpan is amazing~. I could eat a dozen of these.
Saya: Really? I’m so glad to hear that~. I’ll tell my dad.
Arisa: Hey, don’t dig into dessert before you finish your lunch!
Rimi: Hehe, I couldn’t help myself...
Kasumi: You can’t expect me to resist when you say it’s a Yamabuki Bakery special~!
Tae: Munch munch... The choco cornet is delicious too.
Arisa: You too, huh!? And that’s not even a new item, O-Tae!
Tae: Wanna try one, Arisa? New anpan or choco cornet—take your pick.
Arisa: Then I’ll have the anpan. Honestly, I’ve been eyeing it since—wait, no!
Saya: Haha, we’ve got plenty, so don’t hold back.
Saya: Still, the rain’s really pouring. Think it’ll stop by the end of the day?
Kasumi: I hope it doesn’t!
Rimi: Huh? Why not?
Arisa: She got a new umbrella and can’t wait to use it.
Kasumi: Exactly! Wanna see it, everyone?
Arisa: Hey, Kasumi. Don’t open that in the classroom.
Kasumi: I know, I know~ Ta-da! Star-patterned folding umbrella!
Rimi: Wow, it’s so cute~!
Saya: With a design like that, I totally get why you’re excited to use it.
Tae: New umbrellas are the best. The sound changes.
Kasumi: The sound?
Tae: The sound of raindrops hitting it. Each umbrella sounds different.
Arisa: Ohh~...
Saya: I’ve never really thought about it, but you’re right.
Rimi: Even between plastic and fabric umbrellas, they sound totally different.
Kasumi: The sound of raindrops...
Rimi: Kasumi-chan?
Kasumi: That’s such a cool idea! I’m going outside now to listen to the rain!
Arisa: Hold on! We’ve got a class change next, no time for that!
Kasumi: Oh yeah!? Then after school—I will go listen for sure!
Saya: Haha. Hope it’s still raining for you, Kasumi.
