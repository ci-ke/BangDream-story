1999_Chisato Shirasagi_R5 Take It Easy Sometimes

Gacha phrase: Time with Kanon means everything to me.

<Two-Person Picnic Pt. 1>

(Character: Kanon Matsubara, Aya Maruyama, Hina Hikawa, Chisato Shirasagi, Maya Yamato, Eve Wakamiya)

[Chisato And Kanon's Apartment]

Chisato: Script, check. Materials, check... Everything's accounted for.
TV Newscaster: “The Kanto region will have clear skies all day today, without a single cloud in sight.”
Chisato: Good. But the temperature... It still looks a bit chilly. Since it’ll be an outdoor shoot for a long time, I should dress warmly just in case.
TV Newscaster: “Spring flowers are in full bloom all across the country. If you’re going out, be sure to...”
Kanon: Chisato-chan, good morning...
Chisato: Good morning, Kanon.
Chisato: I made breakfast, so be sure to eat. Sorry it’s nothing fancy.
Kanon: Wow, French toast! I love your French toast, Chisato-chan.
Chisato: Fufu, I’m glad. I’ll be home late again today, so make sure to lock up properly.
Kanon: Okay, got it. I’ll bring in the laundry too.
Kanon: You’ve been really busy with work lately. Should I take over making breakfast for a while?
Chisato: It’s fine. I can’t put all the burden on you.
Chisato: If anything, I feel bad for leaving so much of the housework to you.
Kanon: No, it’s totally fine! We help each other when things get busy.
Kanon: Besides, I want you to be able to relax at home, even just a little.
Chisato: Thank you, Kanon. I think work will settle down soon.
Kanon: I see. I hope you get some time to unwind.
Kanon: ...Lately, we haven’t really had time to talk. I’d love to just sit down and chat with you again sometime.
Chisato: Kanon...
Kanon: Ah, sorry! I know you’re busy, and I shouldn’t be saying things like that... Oh, but look! Check that out, Chisato-chan.
TV Newscaster: “This flower field is one of the largest in Kanto, featuring poppies, baby blue eyes, and many other flowers. It’s the perfect place for a picnic.”
Kanon: It’s so beautiful. The weather is great, too. I bet having a picnic there would feel amazing.
Chisato: A picnic...
Chisato: That’s it! Kanon, let’s go on a picnic next Sunday.
Kanon: Huh? But don’t you have work that day too, Chisato-chan?
Chisato: I’ll be done in the morning, so I should be home before noon.
Kanon: I see... But if we go right after you finish work, won’t you be too exhausted?
Chisato: I’ll be fine. Besides, I’d love to spend a relaxing day off with you too.
Kanon: ...Alright. If you’re sure, let’s go out on Sunday.
Chisato: Yes, and I promise I won’t overdo it.
Kanon: Oh! Then I’ll make a bento! Is there anything you’d like me to put in it?
Chisato: Are you sure?
Kanon: Of course! I can make it while you’re at work, so just tell me what you’d like.
Chisato: Then... Could you make asparagus wrapped in bacon?
Kanon: Asparagus and bacon? Got it! I’ll make a fantastic bento, so look forward to it.
Chisato: Fufu, I will. Alright, I should get going now.
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[One Week Later]

[Filming Studio]

Film Staff: And that concludes today’s shoot! Thank you, everyone!
Pastel＊Palettes: Great work, everyone!!
Chisato: (The time... It’s still before 10:00. Good, I’ll definitely make it in time for the picnic with Kanon.)
Aya: Chisato-chan, great job! I’m so glad we wrapped up early! Today’s the big picnic day, right?
Chisato: Oh... Was it that obvious?
Aya: Fufu, you’ve been checking the clock and smiling all morning.
Maya: Chisato-san, you’ve been looking forward to this so much. You even worked extra hard to adjust your schedule for today.
Eve: The weather is supposed to stay sunny all day, making it the perfect picnic day!
Hina: A picnic sounds nice~ I wanna go with my sis too~
Hina: You’re going to a big park full of flowers, right? If you see anything interesting, send us some pictures!
Chisato: Of course. Well then, I should get going...
Staff: Oh, good! Chisato-san, you’re still here!
Staff: I’m sorry for the sudden request, but do you have any time in your schedule today?
Chisato: ...Right now?
Staff: Yes. A publishing company nearby just requested an interview... Since they’ve always been good to us, it’s hard to turn them down... If you’re available, we’d really appreciate it.
Hina: Oh no~ Right at this timing?
Eve: Um, excuse me, but would it be possible to reschedule for another day?
Maya: Chisato-san has plans this afternoon. If possible, we’d like to do it some other time...
Chisato: Is this a typical interview at the main office? How long will it take?
Staff: Yes, it’s at the main office, and it shouldn’t take too long.
Chisato: Understood. I’ll head over now.
Aya: Chisato-chan... Are you sure?
Chisato: Thank you all for being considerate. But I am a professional.
Chisato: I should properly fulfill the work I’m given.
Staff: Thank you so much! I’ll go ahead and bring the car around right away!
Chisato: (It’s okay. There’s still time.)
Chisato: (I’ll get my work done properly... and still make it in time for the picnic with Kanon.)
(Black cut)

<Two-Person Picnic  Pt. 2>

(Character: Kanon Matsubara, Chisato Shirasagi)

[Chisato And Kanon's Apartment]

Kanon: It's already 14:00... She said she'd hurry back, but...
Kanon: Maybe something happened... I should send another message—
Chisato: Kanon! I'm so sorry for being late.
Kanon: Chisato-chan...!
Chisato: I’m really sorry. The interview took longer than I expected...
Kanon: No, it’s totally fine. You worked hard.
Kanon: It looks like you rushed back here... Do you want to take a break?
Chisato: No, I’m fine. I’m used to this level of busyness. Alright, let’s head out.
Kanon: Okay... Got it. Then, let’s go. I put a lot of effort into making the bento, so I hope you—
Kanon: Huh? Chisato-chan, could you turn around for a second?
Chisato: Turn around? ...Like this?
Kanon: Ah, I knew it. Your ribbon came undone. Let me fix it for you.
Chisato: ...Oh, I didn’t even notice. I must not have tied it properly in my rush to change.
Kanon: There, all done.
Chisato: Thank you, Kanon. Now, let’s finally head—oh?
Kanon: Is something wrong?
Chisato: ...It looks like I left my cardigan in the agency’s car.
Chisato: Sigh... What am I even doing? Just wait here for a moment. I’ll go grab another jacket from my room.
Kanon: ...Chisato-chan, are you sure you don’t want to take a break? You’re looking a little pale.
Chisato: But if we don’t leave soon, the sun will start setting...
Kanon: If that happens, we can just have the picnic another day.
Kanon: Sorry... You went out of your way to adjust your schedule for today, and now I’m saying this.
Kanon: But if you push yourself too hard and end up feeling worse...
Kanon: So, let’s just relax at home together today.
Chisato: Kanon...
Chisato: ...You’re right. I was getting too caught up in rushing.
Chisato: Would it be okay if we postponed the picnic?
Kanon: Of course. Besides, our plan was to take it easy together in the first place.
Chisato: ...Thank you. I’ve ended up making you worry instead.
Kanon: Not at all. Oh! Since we’re home, why don’t we eat the bento here?
Kanon: I’m really proud of how it turned out. Here you go!
Chisato: Wow... This is amazing, Kanon. Asparagus bacon rolls, sandwiches, even carrot râpée... The colors are beautiful, and it looks delicious.
Kanon: Fufu, that’s not all. Here, your favorite tea. I just poured it into the thermos, so it’s still warm.
Chisato: Thank you, Kanon.
Chisato: Mmm... It smells wonderful. It’s so relaxing.
Kanon: Yeah. I love this tea too.
Chisato: Fufu... Sitting here like this, it almost feels like we’re having a picnic at home.
Kanon: You’re right! I think this is my first time having a home picnic. Maybe we should spread out a picnic sheet too!
Chisato: Fufu, let’s do that after we finish our tea.
Kanon: Yeah, let’s! Oh, by the way, I saw a segment about an aquarium on the news earlier. There was this huge tank filled with jellyfish—
(Black cut)
Chisato: Zzz... Zzz...
Kanon: (Chisato-chan... I knew she was exhausted...)
Kanon: (Not just today, but this whole week, she’s been working so hard...)
Kanon: Chisato-chan. Great job today.
Chisato: Mmm...
Chisato: Mmm... Kanon...? When did I...?
Kanon: Good morning, Chisato-chan.
Chisato: I ended up dozing off on you, didn’t I? Was I too heavy?
Kanon: Nope. You looked so peaceful. I kind of wanted to keep watching you sleep.
Chisato: Geez, Kanon... Don’t tease me.
Kanon: Fufufu.
Chisato: But I guess... being next to you made me feel safe enough to relax.
Kanon: I’m glad you got to unwind a little.
Chisato: Spending time like this with you is really important to me. I’m so glad I made time for this.
Chisato: Kanon, thank you for everything.
Kanon: ...No, I should be the one thanking you, Chisato-chan.
Kanon: Hey, why don’t we cook dinner together tonight?
Chisato: That sounds wonderful. What should we make?
(Black cut)
(Background change)
