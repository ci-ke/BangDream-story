Marina: Hmm, that’s definitely a growing-pains kind of dilemma.

Chisato: And at the same time, it made me realize—I want us to always be idols who can bring people together like that, no matter how big our events get.
Marina: Yeah yeah, that’s a great mindset to have. If you keep thinking like that, Pastel＊Palettes will be just fine.
Chisato: Thank you. I hope we can continue to count on your support.
