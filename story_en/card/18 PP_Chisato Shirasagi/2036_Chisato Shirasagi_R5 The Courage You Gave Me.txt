<A Story Played on Strings>

(Character: Tsugumi Hazawa, Chisato Shirasagi, Tsukushi Futaba)

[Hazawa Coffee]

Tsukushi & Tsugumi: Welcome~!

Chisato: Thank you. Then I’ll take a seat by the window.
(Black cut)
Tsukushi: Have you decided on your order?
Chisato: I’ll have some tea... and the chiffon cake, please.
Tsukushi: Got it! I’ll bring it right out, so please wait just a moment.
Tsugumi: Great job at TIS, Chisato-san. I watched the whole thing online.
Chisato: Really? That makes me very happy. How was our performance?
Tsugumi: It was absolutely amazing...! I even got a little teary...
Tsugumi: It felt like Pastel＊Palettes and the fans were lifting up the whole venue together... It made me feel so warm inside.
Chisato: Yeah... Even while performing, I could really feel the fans’ support.
Tsukushi: Thank you for waiting. Here’s your tea and chiffon cake.
Chisato: Thanks, Tsukushi-chan.
Tsukushi: You were talking about TIS, right? I watched it online too, and I was really moved.
Tsukushi: I thought your performance in the finals was absolutely wonderful, too...
Chisato: That part was disappointing... but strangely, I don’t feel any regrets.
Tsugumi: Really?
Chisato: Yes. I think, both for Pastel＊Palettes and for myself, we gave it everything we had, and our goals became much clearer.
Chisato: I truly believe Pastel＊Palettes gained so much by competing in TIS.
Tsukushi: That’s amazing... If it were me, I think I’d be so down I wouldn’t recover for a while...
Tsukushi: You really are so cool, Chisato-senpai.
Chisato: That’s not true. If I’d been alone, I’d definitely have been discouraged... and full of regret.
Chisato: The only reason I can keep facing forward right now is because of the others—because my bandmates are with me.
Chisato: ...Though, maybe that was a bit too much.
Tsugumi: Fufu, Eve-chan said something really similar when she came in to work the other day. Right, Tsukushi-chan?
Tsukushi: Yes. She said something like, “We made it to the finals thanks to our members and all the fans...”
Chisato: So Eve-chan said something like that too...
Chisato: ......
Tsugumi: Chisato-san? Is something wrong?
Chisato: No... I just suddenly thought...
Chisato: The old me might have looked at not winning TIS and felt like I missed my chance.
Chisato: Of course, results matter in this world... but I’ve come to believe they’re not everything.
Chisato: The story we’ve built together our fans as Pastel＊Palettes is just as important... It’s something truly irreplaceable.
Tsugumi: Chisato-san...
Chisato: These fingers of mine are probably something irreplaceable too.
Tsukushi: Your fingers...?
Chisato: Yes. With these fingers, I’ve played the bass more times than I can count.
Chisato: At first, I was even thrown off by how stiff the strings were... but now I can play so many songs.
Chisato: Looking at them now fills me with pride.
Tsugumi: ......
Chisato: Tsugumi-chan. If you stare at my fingers that hard, it’s honestly a little embarrassing...
Tsugumi: Ah! I’m sorry, I didn’t mean to...
Tsugumi: I’ve always thought your fingers were kind of cool, Chisato-san, but now that I’ve heard your story, I think they’re even cooler.
Chisato: Oh my...
Tsugumi: Chisato-san’s fingers are the result of all the practice and effort you’ve put in up until now.
Tsugumi: Your fingers are the hands of the coolest bassist ever.
Chisato: The fingers of a bassist...
Chisato: Thank you... That really means a lot to me.
Tsukushi: Chisato-senpai, could you tell us more about TIS? You always looked like you were having fun on stage, but weren’t you nervous?
Chisato: I can’t go into too much behind-the-scenes detail... but sure, within reason, I can share a little.
Chisato: Now then, where should I start... During the first round of TIS, Aya-chan―
