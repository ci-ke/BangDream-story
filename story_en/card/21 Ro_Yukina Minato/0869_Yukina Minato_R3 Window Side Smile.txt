<Yukina's Search for a Hobby>

(Character: Yukina Minato, Lisa Imai)

[After School]

[Residential Area]

Lisa: I heard the news, Yukina~. You helped Saya look for a new hobby the other day, didn't you?
Yukina: If it can be considered that, seeing as I barely contributed anything.
Lisa: You say that, but even after that first day, you came back and went bouldering with them, right?
Lisa: Not only that. There was hula hooping and building radios? Sounds like you had a lot going on.
Yukina: After such an invitation, I simply did not wish to leave my duty half-finished. Finding a hobby within a single day did not seem reasonable.
Lisa: Looking for a new hobby in and of itself sounds kind of tough~.
Lisa: So, did you end up finding anything?
Yukina: She ultimately felt most comfortable with photography.
Lisa: Photography? Wow, was that someone else's idea?
Yukina: No. It was something Yamabuki-san discovered on her own.
Yukina: Aoba-san had initially brought along a camera to keep a record of events until a suitable hobby was found. However, Yamabuki-san began focusing most of her efforts on taking photos...
Yukina: The most appropriate outlet for her was not the act of taking photos... but, instead, sharing and discussing those memories with her friends.
Lisa: Hahaha, I see!
Lisa: I'd say that's a mission complete for Saya. How about you, Yukina?
Yukina: Me? Hm... As I mentioned before, I am unsure of how useful I was to Yamabuki-san, honestly speaking...
Lisa: No, no! That's not what I meant! I want to know if you also had a good time.
Lisa: Moca sure made it sound like you did.
Yukina: Yes. It was... refreshing to take the initiative and experiment with new things.
Lisa: I see~. So it was a good experience for you too, then.
Lisa: But hey. Doesn't that mean you also found a hobby for yourself?
Yukina: ...? No, I don't believe so.
Lisa: Huh? For real? But you had fun, right?
Yukina: I do not disagree with that assessment, as the time I spent was most rewarding.
Yukina: But to consider that as a hobby would be somewhat misleading.
Yukina: The one and only subject I wish to pursue is music.
Lisa: Well, I suppose that's true~. But hobbies are different, you know?
Lisa: A hobby is what you do when you need to take a breather or just want to have a little bit of fun.
Yukina: Yes, I agree.
Yukina: Yet that simply proves my point. Music is the most important matter in my life.
Lisa: Um... what do you mean?
Yukina: "Strengthening my body through hula hooping or bouldering would help me endure grueling moments while on stage. Our performances may improve should I take them up in earnest."
Yukina: "The refinement acquired through ballroom dancing may help Roselia's songs reach a new level of depth."
Yukina: ... Even when constructing my radio, I thought of how it allows for the transmission of sound to faraway places.
Yukina: In the end, no matter the situation, all of my thoughts lead back to music.
Lisa: Ah~... Okay, I get you now. You're as serious as they come...
Lisa: Anyway, I'm actually kind of relieved. Because that suits you, you know?
Yukina: I am simply unable to think of anything else.
Lisa: Yeah, I know♪ I'm trying to say that's pretty cool!
Yukina: Everything is thanks to what I experienced the other day.
Yukina: Music is born from emotion. And emotion is developed from experience...
Yukina: Which is why I intend to actively seek out other experiences from now on.
Lisa: Nice! Oh, hey! Speaking of experience... Look at what I found today.
Lisa: Ta-da~! A flyer for a cat cafe~! It just opened up near the station. Want to go check it out?
Yukina: Cat... cafe...?
Lisa: Uh-huh! Cute, aren't they? I thought you might like this, so I grabbed one from the station this morning to show you♪
Lisa: So~... how about it? Yukina?
Yukina: I will check my schedule. Time is limited, so we need to act quickly.
Lisa: Hahaha, okay~! I'll ask Ako and the others if they're free too!


<Putting My All into Music>

(Character: Yukina Minato)

[CiRCLE - Lobby]

Yukina: Hello, Marina-san, {{userName}}-san. I wish to schedule my next reservation.
Marina: Good afternoon, Yukina-chan. And you only just finished rehearsing too. So much passion~.
Yukina: Really? I see it as nothing special.
Marina: Oh, speaking of passion, I heard the news from Moca-chan. You helped Saya-chan find a new hobby, right?
Marina: It sounded like you had a lot of fun trying out new things!
Marina: And from what I gathered, there was a lot to choose from. Juggling, dancing, not to mention bouldering... Is that true?
Yukina: ... Yes. It was a selection of very interesting activities.
Yukina: Of course, that was not the reason why I participated.
Yukina: When Aoba-san initially brought up the matter, she failed to mention a few key details... I was quite surprised to learn we would be searching for hobbies.
Yukina: And yet, that was precisely how I was able to expose myself to many activities I would normally never consider.
Yukina: Of all the trials we undertook, I was most attracted to the final one, bouldering.
Marina: Bouldering involves climbing, right? On those special walls...
Yukina: Correct. It seems simple enough when watching from afar, but actually attempting it was no easy feat.
Yukina: Complete concentration using every nerve in the body, supporting one's self through muscle power alone... It definitely helps strengthen both of these areas.
Yukina: I am considering inviting Roselia to a bouldering session to help improve our future performances.
Marina: Wait, Yukina-chan... Were you thinking of the band that entire time?
Yukina: Yes, of course.
Marina: Hahaha. That does sound like what you would do.
Yukina: Lisa said the exact same thing. I feel it is only natural, personally...
Yukina: The sense of fulfillment that others receive through their hobbies is, in my case, undoubtedly redirected towards music.
Yukina: What I want to do, and what must be done... To me, they are one and the same.
Marina: I see~. And that's why everything in your mind is tied to music.
Marina: But hey, I think that's amazing. That means you can enjoy anything if it somehow leads back to music! A combination of leisure and practicality, I suppose?
Yukina: Yes. No matter the hobby, I can find enjoyment through a connection with music.
Yukina: I believe your assessment is absolutely true.
Marina: Gaining experience outside of music for the sake of music... Everyone has their own ways of having a good time.
Marina: Hearing everyone go on about new hobbies has me wanting to start one for myself.
Yukina: Fufu, I will spare no effort in helping you.
Marina: Really?! Alright, I'll be sure to get your opinion when the time comes.
Yukina: Contact me anytime. And now... about that reservation...
Marina: Ah, I totally forgot! Sorry, I'll handle that right away!
