<The Splendid Session>

(Character: Kaoru Seta, Maya Yamato, Yukina Minato)

[Shopping Mall - Food Court]

Kaoru: Thanks for coming with me, Maya. I'm really happy I got this theater magazine.
Maya: No problem at all! I'm glad I could help. Are you free after this? How about lunch together?
Kaoru: Sounds great. I'd love to... Oh? Isn't that...
Maya: Minato-san! Hello. Are you here for lunch?
Yukina: Yes, something like that.
Kaoru: We were just about to eat too. Would you like to join us?
Maya: That table over there just opened up, a four-seater. Let's sit together!
Yukina: Then I’ll join you.
Maya: Let's order what we like and regroup here.
Yukina: Got it.
(Black cut)
Maya: Ah, Kaoru-san, over here!
Kaoru: Fufu... everything on the menu looked so good, I had a hard time choosing. What did you get, Maya?
Maya: I went with the colorful veggie set. You got pasta, huh?
Kaoru: This fleeting tomato cream and those lovely shrimp completely captured my heart... Has Yukina returned yet?
Yukina: Sorry to keep you waiting.
Maya: No worries, we just got back too... Oh, ramen, Yukina-san?
Yukina: Yes.
Maya: That's a bit unexpected. I never had that image of you.
Kaoru: I see… So that’s just the mood you were in. Watching you slurp noodles must be a beautifully fleeting sight.
Yukina: Is that so? Well then, let’s eat before it gets cold.
Maya: Alright then, let’s eat.
Yukina & Kaoru: Let’s eat.
Kaoru: ...Th-this is...!
Kaoru: The refreshing tang and deep creamy richness... to think a tomato could become so gentle... I can almost see the craftsman's tireless effort. How fleeting...
Maya: Ahaha... Kaoru-san, you haven’t changed.
Yukina: ...
Maya: Ah, she meant to say, the pasta tastes good...
Yukina: No, I understood her. You can feel the creator’s passion in the flavor, like music resonating from the dish.
Maya: Music...?
Kaoru: What a beautiful expression, Yukina. So, what kind of music does that ramen play?
Yukina: Let’s see...
Yukina: The soup is light, like a friendly melody.
Maya: Melody...
Yukina: The noodles, firm with bite, feel like lyrics full of emotion.
Maya: Lyrics... huh...
Yukina: The menma and chashu support everything like the rhythm section.
Yukina: All the parts come together in perfect harmony, stealing the audience’s heart... I'm amazed to find such a wonderful session in a food court.
Maya & Kaoru: .........!
Kaoru: Ahh... Ahh...! Yukina… I'm at a loss for words, moved by how fleetingly you described ramen as a session.
Yukina: Is that so? You always say things like that too.
Maya: No, Kaoru-san’s style is different... But still! Ramen as a session—what does that even mean!?
Yukina: Recently, I witnessed an amazing session between noodles and soup. A session between Udagawa-san and Sato-san.
Yukina: That inspired me. I began to wonder if other ramen could hold the same kind of potential.
Kaoru: Ah, Hagumi mentioned something like that. She said she helped out at Galaxy Ramen with Tomoe-chan and Masuki-chan.
Maya: I see...
Maya: Honestly, I never thought Yukina-san would talk so passionately about something other than music.
Kaoru: It means Yukina finds fleeting beauty in more than just music.
Kaoru: Yukina... would you mind letting me experience that session as well?
Yukina: Of course. Let’s experience this ramen session together.


<The Essence of R>

Yukina: Ramen is the same. Noodles, soup, toppings…
Yukina: Only when they come together in perfect harmony on the bowl’s stage can you call it the ultimate session.
Yukina: Since you’ve tasted it too, I’m sure you understand what I mean.
