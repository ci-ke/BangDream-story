Hagumi: At Yuka-senpai’s wedding, when she used Nanamin’s book to share her love story— It made me think... ‘I wanna try making one too!’
Hagumi: But... I had no idea where to start by myself. So I’m so glad you offered to help me!

Nanami: Oh, that’s such a lovely idea~ I think it’ll turn out amazing.
Hagumi: Ehehe, thanks! And, you know, I’d love to make it a story that even kids who don’t know Hello, Happy World! can enjoy reading and smile!
Nanami: So... does that mean you’re planning to show it to someone specifically?
Hagumi: Yep! At an upcoming kindergarten concert, I’ll be doing a storybook reading! And I want to use this book for it!
Nanami: A kindergarten storytime, huh... Aww— Just imagining it already warms my heart~
Nanami: Then... we should probably make the book pretty big, right?
Hagumi: Good point! If it’s not big enough— the kids in the back won’t be able to see it!
Nanami: The quality of this book could really affect how exciting the live performance is... So, this is a pretty big responsibility...
Nanami: "But— Isn’t this the perfect time for some effort and grit?
Hagumi: Yeah! Thank you, Nanamin-sensei!
Nanami: Okay—so first, let's decide on the structure of the book...
Hagumi: Alright! I’m ready, Nanamin-sensei!
Nanami: Here you go, Hagumi-senpai.
Hagumi: So, here’s what I was thinking! I brought some drawings with me— Take a look!
Nanami: Wow—great job, Hagumi-senpai! Let me take a look... What do we have here...
Nanami: This is... Everyone from Hello, Happy World playing at the beach. You all look like you’re having a blast~ Where was this taken?
Hagumi: That’s Happy-Happy Island!
Nanami: ‘Happy-Happy Island’...?
Hagumi: It’s awesome! We swam, played on the beach, and even tried long-distance swimming! We had so much fun!
Nanami: That sounds like such a wonderful trip... Very youthful— Do you all go to Happy-Happy Island a lot?
Hagumi: Yeah! We go there whenever Kokoron says, ‘Let’s all go together!’ It’s always a blast!
Nanami: I see... That sounds so fun. Maybe I should invite Morfonica's members to visit a tropical island someday, too~
Hagumi: Oh, yes! That would be so cool! I bet everyone in Morfonica would love it!
Nanami: You think so? Alright— I’ll definitely invite them!
Nanami: Can I see the other drawings too?
Hagumi: Of course! Take a look!
Nanami: This one... Is everyone digging in the dirt for stones?
Hagumi: Yep! Not just stones, we were digging for fossils! We found some before!
Nanami: That's amazing... Fossil excavation... I want to try that with everyone too!
Hagumi: Yeah! If you discover any new species of dinosaurs, please let me know!
Nanami: Of course~
Hagumi: Oh, Nanamin! Look at this one, too! This was from when we did a live show at Smile Amusement Park!
Hagumi: And at the end, we all joined the parade together!
Nanami: A parade... That sounds so fun~ I wonder if I asked one of my classmates who works at an amusement park— Could we do something like that someday too...?
Nanami: All these memories seem so precious... I feel like I’ve learned a lot about how to spend time with friends.
Nanami: And you know what, once this pop-up book is finished— I just know everyone who reads it will smile.
Hagumi: You really think so...? Ahh—I hope so! Ehehe... Just thinking about it is already making me so excited!
Nanami: Hehe— Then let’s get started, shall we?
Hagumi: Yes— Please guide me, Nananmin-sensei!
