<Tsugu's Chocolate>

(Character: Moca Aoba, Nanami Hiromachi)

[CiRCLE Café]

Moca: Nom, nom... Mmm~, an afternoon curry bun. A true delicacy indeed~.
Nanami: Moca-senpai, sorry for keeping you waiting~.
Moca: Oh~, how's it going, Nanami-chin~?
Nanami: Ah, that paper bag... You went to Saya-senpai's bakery, didn't you?
Moca: Yup~. I loaded up on some freshly-baked buns~.
Nanami: I don't blame you at all. They do make very nice bread. Ah, I almost forgot. Here are the mangas you lent me. Thank you very much.
Moca: Whoa~, you read them all already? That was quick. There were ten plus volumes~.
Nanami: Well~, they were just so good that I ended up flying through them.
Moca: It is quite the interesting manga series, isn't it~? So, what part gave you the most feels~?
Nanami: Hmm, a lot of parts did, but I'd have to go with the end of volume seven~.
Nanami: I had a feeling the hat the main character always wore was fishy, but I never expected removing it would teleport everyone and Earth itself to another dimension.
Moca: Yeah, that was pretty unpredictable, all right~.
Nanami: Suddenly going from a relaxing slice-of-life story to a battle adventure was another thing I didn't see coming at all~.
Moca: That part also really blew me away the first time I read the manga~.
Moca: And the part where the demon lord, dragon, and the hat join forces to fight against the god was pretty exciting, wasn't it~?
Nanami: Yes, it really was~. Friendship, hard work, mystery, action. The manga has it all~. It's incredible.
Moca: The wait to find out what happens next is going to be torture, huh~? Nom, nom... Ah, want some buns~?
Nanami: Really? Thank you~, I'll have some then...
Nanami: Interesting...
Moca: Hm~? Is something wrong~?
Nanami: You usually get about the same amount of stuffed bread and sweet bread, but it looks like you only got stuffed ones today.
Moca: Oh~, an excellent observation. Your eyes are as keen as ever~.
Moca: You see~, I'm going to Tsugu's later, and she's going to be making me some chocolatey goodness, so I'm keeping my stomach free for the sugar payload~.
Nanami: I see. Ah, is this chocolate supposed to be for Valentine's Day?
Moca: Yup~. Tsugu puts a lot of work into her creations for us every year, so I always look forward to them~.
Moca: The chocolate gateau she made one time was so good my cheeks fell off. They practically went to another dimension~.
Moca: The journey I had to embark on to retrieve them was full of trials and tribulations~.
Nanami: That says a lot about the chocolate things she makes~.
Moca: And this year, she's mind-blowingly Tsugurific, so I have a feeling that whatever it is will be even yummier~.
Nanami: Wow, she's Tsugurific? As in the same Tsugurific you used last time?
Moca: That's the one~. Such Tsugurificness warrants equally high expectations, and it is why I am staying away from sweet breads today~.
Nanami: I see~! You must be really looking forward to it then.
Moca: I truly have been blessed with a wonderful childhood friend~. There are no bounds to my gratitude~.
Moca: If everything turns out the way I'm expecting, I might even have to bust open a can of Mocatastic when I get her a return gift~.
Nanami: Getting chocolates for friends for each other... I like it. It just screams stuff you do as a student.
Moca: So, what chocolate goodies are you getting for your band buddies~?
Nanami: I still haven't decided... It's not exactly something I've done a whole lot before, so I'm having major trouble.
Nanami: I do know I want to give them something that'll make them happy.
Moca: I see, I see. So I take it you wish to send their cheeks to another dimension~?
Nanami: Ahaha, it would be nice if that happened. Ah, do you think you could give me some advice about what to get by any chance?
Nanami: I have managed to narrow things down at least. Um, they're saved on my phone... This one, this one, and this one too...
Moca: Fu fu fu~, I shall bestow my sage advice on you, for I am an expert when it comes to the giving and taking of chocolates for friends~.
Nanami: Hooray~. Thank you so much.

<Like Air>

(Character: Moca Aoba)

[CiRCLE Café]

Marina: Phew~. How about we take a quick break now that we're done hauling in all the equipment?
Marina: Hm? That girl over there... Hey~, Moca-chan~.
Moca: Ah, Marina-san, {{userName}}-san. Hi~.
Marina: Hi. Wait... Were you in the middle of reading something? If so, we're sorry for interrupting.
Moca: No, no, no apology necessary~. I was just killing some time.
Marina: That's a relief. What's that you're reading, by the way? A novel?
Moca: Yup~. Tsugu recommended it to me before, but I figured I should give it another read~.
Marina: I see. So, what's the story about?
Moca: It's about these two high school girls who meet and then part. It's a warm and fuzzy story about their daily lives~.
Marina: Huh, I take it reading it makes you feel good inside?
Moca: Something like that, but the story goes into a lot of detail when it comes to how the characters are feeling, so it's actually a pretty solid read.
Moca: The ending, in particular, hits you hard with the feels~.
Marina: Interesting~. What exactly happens?
Moca: The main character and her friend say goodbye to each other, but it's not melodramatic at all. In fact, it's very cut and dry~.
Marina: I see. But it's still a goodbye, right? So I guess it must be pretty sad, no?
Moca: Hmm~, actually, it's surprisingly not sad~.
Moca: The story makes you feel like their friendship will probably continue even though they're apart, so there's that assurance, you know?
Moca: Also, they don't put it into words, but you can tell how much they trust each other.
Marina: Okay. So it's a story about their friendship?
Moca: Yup~, and how they're sort of like air.
Marina: Like air?
Moca: Mm-hmm. You don't usually thank air, but you absolutely need it, right~?
Moca: That's the sort of relationship they have. They're both just kind of there together, but also precious to one another. Their relationship does give me the feels~.
Marina: Fufu, it sounds like a really lovely story. If it's that good, I'm kind of curious myself.
Moca: I would definitely give it my seal of approval~.
Moca: Ah, I just remembered I was planning on going to Tsugu's. There are a lot of new special Valentine's Day items on the menu, and I intend to conquer them all~.
Marina: All right, good luck. See you!
Moca: Yup~. See ya~.
