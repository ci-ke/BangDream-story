Himari: Of course! It's spring, after all!
Moca: You're like that because it's spring~?
Himari: Yep! Doesn't this season put you in a kind of gung-ho mood?
Moca: Hmm~. Does it~?
Tsugumi: I know how Himari-chan feels. Our surroundings have changed after going up a grade, so it gives you a bracing feeling.
Himari: Yeah! That's my Tsugu. You totally get it~!
Moca: For me, spring actually puts me in a relaxed mood~.
Moca: Taking a nap in the warm spring sun feels so good, and it's just the best~. The saying goes, "In spring, one sleeps like a baby that doesn't yawn," right~?
Ran: You mean, "In spring, one sleeps a sleep that knows no dawn." ... Moca, you said it wrong on purpose, didn't you?
Moca: Yeah, that's the one~. I figured you'd know it, Ran~.
Himari: Y-you've got a point. My bed might be the greatest place to be for this time of the year...
Himari: It's not hot or cold, so it might be the best season for lazing about...
Moca: I know, right~? Spring is all about taking it easy~.
Moca: You can gaze at sakura as you eat tasty food~.
Moca: Like mugwort buns and green pea paste buns~. Hii-chan, what do you wanna eat~?
Himari: Huh~? What do I want to eat in spring~?
Himari: Let me think~. Maybe sakura mochi... Ah, strawberry daifuku are good, too!
Moca: Mhmm. You said it~.
Tomoe: Haha. She got completely swept up by Moca's pace.
Ran: Sounds like you two care more about food than flowers.
Moca: Now, now. Don't be so rude~. I would choose both the flowers and the food~.
Moca: It's spring, so it would be a waste to only have one of them~.
Moca: Isn't that right~, Hii-chan? You think so, too, right~?
Himari: Hmm~... Yeah. It's just like you said, Moca! You're totally right. It's spring... so we need both flowers and food...
Himari: ... Hey, I know! Why don't we have a sakura party?
Tsugumi: Huh? A sakura party?
Himari: Yeah! We can look at the sakura as we eat some good food!
Himari: We're practicing at Moca's house on Saturday, so we can have it on Sunday!
Himari: In the morning, we'll practice at CiRCLE! So we'll be set to have a sakura party after that!
Himari: Let's buy lots of tasty buns!
Tsugumi: Ah, there's a new kind coming out this weekend. I heard it's a spring-only sakura Danish.
Moca: Ohh~. I can't pass that up~.
Tomoe: ... Oh, the sakura are expected to be in full bloom right on Saturday. Talk about a nicely timed invitation.
Ran: So we're practicing, seeing sakura, and eating buns... It's like we're getting the works of our favorite activities.
Himari: And what's wrong with that? It's spring, after all. Let's do everything that we want to do!
Moca: ...
Moca: It's just like I thought. Having either just flowers or food would be boring~.
Moca: I'll have pretty flowers~. And I'll have tasty buns~...
Moca: And~...
Moca: (I'll have everyone with me.)
