<Comic Days Part 1>

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa)

[Recess]

[Haneoka Girls' Academy - Class 2A]

Moca: Lunch break at last~. Yawn~...
Ran: Dang... That was a monster yawn. You were asleep during class, weren't you?
Moca: Of course not~. I was only nodding off~.
Ran: So you were half asleep then.
Himari: I can't believe you didn't wake up. Do you have any idea how worried I was the whole time~?
Tomoe: Haha, you didn't budge no matter how much Himari poked you.
Tsugumi: Were you up late last night, Moca-chan?
Moca: Ohhh~. A most excellent deduction, my dear Tsugumi-kun. It is as you deduced. The new volume of a manga I've been waiting for finally got released after two years~.
Tomoe: Ah! Is it that detective manga?
Moca: Bingo. Correct, Tomo-chin~.
Ran: Huh, so you stayed up to read it, I guess.
Moca: Tsk, tsk, tsk. You underestimate me and the depth of my love far too much, my dear Ran-kun.
Moca: To ensure that I'd be at my best when I read it, I re-read the entire series again from volume one last night~.
Tomoe: Seriously? Pretty sure that's a lotta volumes. There's up to twenty or something, right?
Moca: Nope, forty-six.
Ran, Tsugumi, Himari & Tomoe: Forty-six?!
Moca: And thanks to all that re-reading, I couldn't be in a better state in terms of manga power.
Tsugumi: I-I see. I can tell you're really looking forward to it.
Moca: That I am~. And I intend to read it during lunch~.
Ran: Alright, alright, I get it. Let's head to the roof then.
Himari: Ah, hold on, will you? I need to go get my fluffy blanket~! The roof makes my legs freeze.
Tsugumi: I think I'll do the same. It's really cold today.
Ran: That reminds me. The weather forecast mentioned there'll be snow in the afternoon.
Himari: Seriously?! No wonder it's felt colder than usual~!
Moca: Oh no, the new buns I got from Yamabuki Bakery will become frozen~.
Tsugumi: Then how about we eat in the cafeteria? It's been a while since we last did.
Himari: I like that! I've heard their selection's improved a lot since the survey.
Tomoe: They're supposed to have more kinds of ramen too! Been thinking of checking it out once.
Ran: Nice. Let's hit the cafeteria then.
Moca, Himari, Tomoe & Tsugumi: Agreed~!
Tomoe: I gotta swing by the dance club real quick, so you guys go ahead without me.
Tsugumi: Sorry, I need to take care of some student council work... I'll try to wrap it up as soon as I can and meet you there.
Himari: Okay~! We'll grab some seats for you two♪
Tomoe: Thanks! I'll try to be there as soon as possible!
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Moca: Hm-hm-hmm~♪ To think that I'd get to enjoy the latest volume and some new buns at the same time~. Lunch couldn't get any better~.
Himari: Fufu, you look so happy. Is that manga that good?
Moca: Did you really just ask me that~? I lent you and Hii-chan the manga when we were in middle school~. Don't tell me you don't remember~.
Ran: Uh, you did?
Himari: Ahaha... I don't really remember either...
Moca: I can't believe you'd forget something that hits you so hard with the feels~. The main character's a detective who runs a detective agency with five friends.
Moca: And the team spirit is the source of the feels~. There's this one amazing scene where all five of them, who usually don't get along, come together when the main character's in trouble~.
Ran: I don't remember any of that.
Moca: Really~? How can you not~, Ran~?
Ran: If the manga's that good, lemme take a look. Seeing the artwork might help jog my memory.
Himari: Yeah! Show us, show us~.
Moca: Sure~. Hold on~... Huh~? Weird~.
Ran: ...? What is it?
Moca: The manga... It's not here.
Himari & Ran: What?!
Ran: Are you sure you didn't just leave it at home?
Moca: No way~. I left home with it. I'm sure I put it in my bag too~... Where could it be~?
Himari: Wh-when something like this happens, you should try retracing your steps. It worked for Tomoe!
Moca: Hmm~... I left home this morning with the manga~...
Himari & Ran: Alright.
Moca: I bought the new Yamabuki Bakery buns~... Said hello to the convenience store manager who was out in front cleaning~... Pet a black kitty who was by some bushes~...
Ran: Make detours much?
Moca: Then I met you two at the front gate, and we went to class together~...
Himari: When's the last time you remember having the manga?
Moca: Hmm~... That'd be when I left the house~...
Ran: ... Then maybe you dropped it somewhere.
Moca: W-woe is me~... My elegant afternoon has been ruined~...
