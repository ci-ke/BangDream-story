<Our Time Together Continues On>

(Character: Moca Aoba, Lisa Imai)

[Downtown]

Lisa: Moca~! Sorry to make you wait! I lost track of time thinking about what days to put down for my shift.
Moca: Ah, you're finally here~. Ugh~, I've been waiting forever and ever~.
Lisa: Ahaha. I'm really sorry. Hey, it'll be you and me tomorrow, too, right?
Moca: That's right~! We'll be together again tomorrow~.
Moca: But you seem busy lately. You gonna be okay working two days in a row~?
Lisa: I'll be fine~! I am busy these days... but it's only because there are a lot of things I need-... no, want to do...
Moca: No kidding~. You don't have to overdo it by trying to work on top of agency stuff~.
Lisa: I've got that under control. I'm not pushing myself too hard.
Moca: Really~? I feel like you're not taking any days to rest. You're super busy, right~?
Lisa: Hmm~. That's not totally true. I've been communicating a lot with my agency, and it's been helping me manage my schedule pretty well.
Lisa: But thanks for worrying about me, Moca.
Moca: I wasn't worried~, but, well, it sounds like you've got everything figured out~.
Moca: I didn't think we'd be working together again this soon though~.
Lisa: Huh? Really? Why's that?
Moca: Remember a few days ago when we walked home together after work~?
Moca: You already seemed super busy, so I figured you'd be working even less at the convenience store~.
Lisa: Yeah, the way things were then, I couldn't put in as many days for my shift~.
Moca: You weren't working as much, so we didn't have as many days together as we used to...
Moca: I thought you'd probably work fewer and fewer days until you just quit altogether~.
Moca: That's why I was so surprised to see that we were working together again so soon~.
Lisa: Fufu. So that's what's been going on in that head of yours. I'm sorry if I made you feel like I was leaving you.
Moca: It's not like I was sad or anything~. I was just thinking things wouldn't be as much fun if you weren't around~.
Moca: But knowing you, I bet you put in days to work so we could hang out, didn't you~?
Lisa: Wow~, busted. You know~, I wanted to see you so bad that I put in a shift that let us work together~♪
Moca: I knew it~. Still, I'm ready to walk home with you whenever I get the chance~. Even if I have to wait for a really, really long time~.
Moca: Just don't overdo it for my sake, okay~?
Lisa: ... Fufu. You always seem like your head's in the clouds, but when it comes to this kinda stuff, you're as sharp as a nail~.
Lisa: The time we spend at the convenience store and walking home together is super important to me.
Lisa: Being on stage isn't the only thing that matters. All the moments in my life like this, all these experiences... They mean the world to me.
Moca: Oh, boy~, you've got me blushing~. You like hanging with lil ol' me that much~?
Moca: Well~, I really like hanging with you, too~. Thanks for making time for me~.
Lisa: Really~? You mean that~?
Moca: Sure do~. I've never lied to you, right~?
Lisa: Fufu. I wonder about that~. But I know you're not just saying all this to be nice.
Moca: Not for a second~. But since you and I are best buds, I'll let you in on a little secret.
Lisa: What? Tell me~! Wait, this will probably be about bread, won't it?!
Moca: Huh? How'd you know~? Are you psychic~?
Lisa: Let's just say I know you~♪
Moca: So you really do love me~.
Lisa: Of course, I do, silly♪ Now what's this secret all about?
Moca: If we go to Yamabuki Bakery right now, we can get buns right out of the oven~.
Moca: Fufufu. I've never spoken a word of this to anyone. This is top secret information~. But you're special, so I'll tell you~.
Lisa: Fufu. For you, that really is top secret info. If we go now, can we really eat freshly baked bread?
Moca: That's right~. Come with me right now, and you'll see~. Then you'll know I'm telling the truth~.
Lisa: You're just saying that so you can go eat some fresh bread, aren't you?
Moca: Ah, you saw right through me~. This girl's starving after a long day of hard work~.
Lisa: There it is! Oh, well~. Guess I'll join you, then♪
Moca: Yay~! Hmm~, I wonder what kind of buns I should get...
Lisa: Me, too. Hmm~... Maybe I'll get something sweet.
Moca: Ah, how about we split whatever we buy~? That way we can enjoy twice as much bready-goodness~.
Lisa: Ahaha. Nice idea! Let's share what we get☆
