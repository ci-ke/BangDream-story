<To You On That Day Pt. 1>

(Character: Mashiro Kurata, Toko Kirigaya, Nanami Hiromachi, Tsukushi Futaba, Rui Yashio)

[At the Hiromachi Family Atelier]

Nanami: Toko-chan said she'd be a little late~
Tsukushi: Again? She said she had no plans for today.
Nanami: Well, well, let's just relax and have some tea. There are cookies too~
Mashiro: Wow! These cookies look delicious... I'll have one!
Rui: Thanks. I'll have one too.
Nanami: Go ahead, go ahead~
Toko: Sorry, everyone! I'm late!
Mashiro: Oh, Toko-chan.
Rui: Kirigaya-san, you're 15 minutes late.
Toko: My grandmother said I couldn't go out until I finished cleaning my room...
Rui: That’s not a valid excuse for being late.
Toko: I already said I’m sorry!
Tsukushi: Now, now, calm down, you two...
Nanami: Huh? Toko-chan, what’s that you’re holding...?
Toko: Oh, right! I found this while cleaning my room!
Nanami: 'Band Members Wanted'...?
Mashiro: This... is the poster I made back in my first year... for 'Band Members Wanted,' isn’t it?
Tsukushi: "Wow, it really is! Oh, so nostalgic! This is the one Mashiro-chan put up in Tsukinomori, right?"
Toko: Still, I can’t believe Shiro made a poster like this herself—so impressive!
Mashiro: Ah, well... back then, after watching a live concert with Toko-chan and Tsukushi-chan at CiRCLE... I guess I got swept up in the excitement and just made it on impulse...
Rui: So that means the live performance really moved you.
Toko: "Yeah, yeah, that live show had Afterglow, Hello, Happy World!, Pastel*Palettes, and even Roselia and Poppin'Party!"
Nanami: Wow, that’s an amazing lineup!
Tsukushi: At that time, Toko-chan got so excited she kept pushing closer to the front.
Toko: Fusuke, you were super hyped up too, weren’t you!
Mashiro: That day, when I saw Poppin'Party!'s stage for the first time... it was like the sound was glowing... I can still remember it clearly.
Nanami: It felt like the sound was glowing... Haha, that’s such a Shiro-chan way of expressing things. I can almost imagine it.
Mashiro: "After meeting Poppin'Party!, I wanted to start a band too, so I made this poster, and Morfonica came to life."
Mashiro: Ah, just thinking about it makes me really want to perform live again...
Toko: Totally! It’s about time we did another live, right!? Everyone else feels the same, don’t you?
Tsukushi: Yeah, I think so. It’d be nice if there was a good opportunity for a live show...
Nanami: I’ll look it up real quick~
Nanami: Oh, look, everyone. In a week, it seems like we can perform at CiRCLE. What do you think?
Toko: Sounds good! Let’s go with that!
Rui: Wait a second. If it’s in a week, it might be a bit tight in terms of preparation, don’t you think?
Toko: We’ll manage somehow! Besides, Shiro was the one who suggested it in the first place!
Mashiro: ...I-I’m sorry... But, if no one minds, I’d really like to do a live show...
Tsukushi: Mashiro-chan... Uh, Rui-san. Just this once, wouldn’t it be fine? I’ll work hard too...
Rui: Haah. Fine. Just this time.
Mashiro: Ah, thank you, everyone...!
Nanami: Alright, it’s decided then~. I’ll handle the application, so leave it to me~.
Rui: If it’s in a week, we need to decide on the setlist. Let’s finalize it before practice.
Mashiro: I... want to sing 'Daylight.'
Mashiro: It was popular in the last survey... and it’s our starting song, after all.
Tsukushi: Then, after 'Daylight,' how about 'Bloom Bloom'?
Nanami: Sounds good, sounds good! We should include some energetic songs too, so let’s add 'Yorube no Sunny, Sunny'!
Toko: Alright, I’d like to do 'One step at a time'!
Nanami: Rui, which song do you want to do?
Rui: "Let’s see... considering the balance of the setlist, I think 'fly with the night' would be a good choice."
Mashiro: "Oh, and... at the end, I’d like to sing 'Serenade of gratitude sound' too..."
Nanami: Oh, that’s great!
Tsukushi: It looks like it’s going to be a really fun setlist.
Mashiro: Yeah... Haha, I can’t wait to perform live...
(Black cut)
(Background change)
