<Sakura Illusion>

(Character: Ako Udagawa, Rinko Shirokane)

[Forest Park]

Ako: Wow...! They're in full bloom~!
Rinko: How beautiful...!
Ako: Isn't the view amazing, Rin-rin? There are sakura blossoms everywhere~!
Ako: Oh, it's a lot like that magic spell in NFO! Remember? Umm...
Rinko: "Twilight Illusion"...?
Ako: Yeah, that's it! Good job, Rin-rin!
Rinko: When we use that spell, the visual effects spread out all over the screen... like the never-ending sight of sakura in front of us...
Rinko: The sky and the ground... Everything is beautifully dyed with the color of spring...
Rinko: I never thought I'd see such beautiful scenery... Thank you for inviting me here...
Rinko: If it weren't for you... I wouldn't have known about this place...
Ako: Sis told me about it~. She knew a place that wasn't crowded, so we can see the sakura in peace!
Ako: I wanted to come with you, so I'm glad you're here!
Rinko: We still have some time before practice... so... let's enjoy the view...
Ako: Yeah! It really is beautiful~. Are you feeling what I'm feeling when I see all these sakura?
Rinko: What do you mean...?
Ako: It feels like something special is gonna happen!
Ako: Look, under that tree. There are so many sakura blossoms falling! We can't see what's on the other side!
Ako: If we take a step there, who knows what kind of strange world we might jump into...!
Rinko: There is certainly... a flurry of petals... under that big tree...
Ako: If we stand inside it, we could probably gain special powers~.
Ako: I, the great demon princess Ako, shall awaken by gaining the special powers hidden within the sakura blossoms!
Ako: ... Just kidding! I like to imagine stuff like this when it's sakura season~.
Rinko: I think I understand how you feel...
Rinko: When I gaze at the sakura... I can hear the wind blowing and the flowers rustling... It's as if... time is standing still for a moment...
Rinko: I read in a book once... that in the past... they were thought to have special powers...
Rinko: Just like us... those before us... also felt something special while looking at sakura...
Ako: That's so cool~. You know so much, Rin-rin!
Rinko: Speaking of which... the period when they start to bloom... is said to be the best part of spri-
Rinko & Ako: Ahh...!
Rinko: Th-the wind was so strong...
Ako: Yeah, it took me by sur-
Ako: Whoa...! Rin-rin, look! The petals are falling like rain!
Rinko: Mm-hmm... It's like... we've been... sucked into an entirely different world...
Rinko & Ako: ...
Ako: Oh! Rin-rin, look!
Ako: Just as I thought~! If I move around super fast like this, the petals start swirling in the air~!
Ako: Does it look like I'm controlling them, Rin-rin?
Rinko: It does... It looks like... magic... You're so cool, Ako-chan...
Ako: Ehehe, really~?  Behold my magic! Haha, just kidding~.
Ako: C'mon, Rin-rin, try it with me! It's so much fun!
Rinko: Whoa... A-Ako-chan...?!
Ako: I call upon the energy of light hiding within the sakura blossoms... to heed the great wind's commands... and awaken from your eternal slumber! Show yourself!
Ako: Akomic Rinriesta Burst Hurricaaane~!
Ako: Oh...!
Rinko: Another... strong wind...
Rinko: A-Ako-chan, look up...!
Ako: ...!
