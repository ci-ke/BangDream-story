<Love Letter Etude>

(Character: Rimi Ushigome, Himari Uehara, Kaoru Seta)

[Haneoka Girls' Academy - Front Gate]

Himari: It's finally here, Rimi... Kaoru-senpai's Christmas play...!
Rimi: Y-yes...!
Himari: Are you alright? Did you bring your letter?
Rimi: I-I did! Um... here...!
Rimi: After we went and bought extras, I still made a ton of mistakes because my hand kept shaking... But I managed somehow!
Himari: Me too! We've poured our hearts into writing them! I'm sure our feelings will reach Kaoru-senpai!
Himari: We can be proud of the letters we're giving her...! Let's do this!
Rimi: Okay...!
Rimi & Himari: ...
Rimi: ... Ahh, w-wait~! I'm still nervous~!
Himari: S-so am I! My legs won't move at all...!
Himari: Oh no... The idea that Kaoru-senpai will read my letter is making my hands tremble...
Rimi: I-I completely understand why! Ngh... Himari-chan! Do you think I could maybe do a quick practice run...?!
Himari: You really are such a genius...! Yeah! We should practice first!
Rimi: Thank you...! That'll be a super big help...!
Rimi: (Okay, so, um... I have to imagine that Himari-chan is Kaoru-san...)
(White cut)
(White cut)
Rimi: (Ahh, I can't do it...! I'm shaking so hard that the words aren't coming out...!)
Himari: Are you okay...?
Rimi: Y-yes... I'll try my best...
Rimi: Sigh... I can't believe I'm so nervous, even though I know this is just practice...
Rimi: (But... I can't give up now. Not after working so hard to write out all the feelings I want Kaoru-san to know... I need to be brave...)
Rimi: (... I was able to express how grateful I am. I wrote about how special and wonderful her plays and she herself are to me.)
Rimi: (Kaoru-san has left me with so many incredible things...! I want her to know...! On this day...! On the day of her final play...!)
Rimi: Um...! Kaoru-san, please accept this letter...!
Kaoru: Fufu... Are you certain I may receive this?
Rimi: Ahh! K-Kaoru-san...?! What are you doing here...?!
Kaoru: I caught sight of the two of you and thought to offer my greetings...
Kaoru: However, you began such an adorable étude. Such was the joy I felt that I could not help but watch.
Rimi: Adorable...?! B-but this was just, um...!
Kaoru: Fufu, you need not explain. You were merely practicing giving the letter, yes?
Kaoru: Therefore, to accept it now would be improper. I shall wait until you are ready.
Himari: Kaoru-senpai...!
Rimi: Thank you so much... Um... after the play... I'll come and give you this letter for sure!
Rimi: And it won't be to an imaginary Kaoru-san like just now... It'll be to the real "Kaoru Seta-san..." and I'll be the one to talk to you first...!
Kaoru: My thanks. You have inspired me to try even harder.
Kaoru: First, however, it would please me greatly if you could enjoy today's play. Take as much time as you need afterward to give me your letter.
Kaoru: I shall wait until you do. Farewell for now then!
Himari: ... She's so...
Himari & Rimi: Amazing~!
Himari: Oh no! I-it's about to start!
Rimi: Ah, you're right! We have to hurry...!
Himari: I know thinking about the letter is stressful... but we have a play to enjoy first!
Rimi: Mm-hmm! Let's make sure we engrave Kaoru-san... into our memory!
(Black cut)
