<Rain-Shelter Groove Pt. 1>

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Rimi Ushigome, Arisa Ichigaya)

[Shopping Mall]

Kasumi: Mmm~... It’s been so long since we hung out after school~. Thanks for inviting me, Rimi-rin.
Rimi: You’ve been busy with shifts at RiNG lately, right?
Kasumi: Yeah, totally~. We’ve had events one after another, so Saya and I have been super busy. Today was our first day off in a while.
Rimi: Good work. It would’ve been nice if Saya-chan joined us too.
Kasumi: Yeah, but she said she had to help out at home, so it can’t be helped.
Rimi: Everyone’s been busy lately... O-Tae-chan’s working at the music store, and Arisa-chan said she’s swamped with student council stuff too.
Kasumi: You’ve been busy too, haven’t you, Rimi-rin?
Rimi: Yeah. But today cram school’s air conditioner broke, so class suddenly got canceled. I guess I got lucky.
Kasumi: Then let’s have as much fun as we can today!
Kasumi: Oh! Rimi-rin! They’ve got a limited-time Danish coronet!
Rimi: What!? Coronets!?
Kasumi: They look so good~! Let’s try one!
Rimi: Yeah!
(Black cut)
Kasumi: Mmm~...! So good!
Rimi: The pastry’s so crispy—just like a croissant! And it’s filled with so much chocolate cream... it’s sooo good~...! Kasumi-chan, you ordered the non-chocolate one, right?
Kasumi: Yeah! I got a custard cream one! But I wanted to try azuki cream too~.
Rimi: Fufu, I couldn’t decide between this and the whipped cream one either.
Kasumi: Then next time, let’s get azuki cream and whipped cream!
Rimi: Yeah. Looks like this shop’s around until next month, so next time let’s come with everyone.
Kasumi: That’s an awesome idea, Rimi-rin! In that case, I’ll message the group chat right now—
Rimi: ...Huh? Isn’t that Arisa-chan over there?
Kasumi: Huh? You’re right! Hey, Arisa~!
Arisa: Oh, Kasumi and Rimi... you two taking a detour?
Kasumi: Yeah, I was shopping with Rimi-rin~.
Rimi: Are you done with student council work, Arisa-chan?
Arisa: Nah, I’m still on duty. I just came out to grab some supplies.
Rimi: That’s a lot of stuff. What did you buy?
Arisa: Nothing special. Cleaning rags, detergent, work gloves... You know how we’ve got that school-wide cleanup next week, right? We didn’t have enough supplies for it.
Arisa: The old stuff was all rusty or had holes, so we figured we’d replace it while we’re at it.
Kasumi: I see. Then let us help!
Arisa: No, it’s fine. I’d feel bad dragging you into student council work.
Kasumi: But we want to help! Right, Rimi-rin?
Rimi: Yeah!
Kasumi: Here, let me carry some of that, Arisa!
Arisa: Ah—hey, Kasumi!
Rimi: I’ll take some too. Um, let’s see~...
Arisa: Alright, I’ll split it up...! Here, Rimi, you take this one. Thanks, both of you.
Kasumi & Rimi: You're welcome!
Kasumi: So, where to next?
Arisa: I’m thinking of going to the home-improvement store since I need a bamboo broom and some fire tongs.
Kasumi: Okay!
Rimi: The home-improvement store’s stuff is in the back of the first floor, right? If that’s where you’re going, taking this path might be quicker.
Arisa: Yeah.
Kasumi: Arisa~! Rimi-rin~! I’m going on ahead~!
Arisa: Huh—when did she...!?
Rimi: W-wait up, Kasumi-chan~!
(Black cut)
(Background change)


<Rain-Shelter Groove Pt. 2>
