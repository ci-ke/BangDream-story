1961_Rimi Ushigome_R5 Rimi's Resolve

Gacha phrase: I want to become a bigger version of myself than I am now.

<Learning from Predecessors>

(Character: Rimi Ushigome, Kaoru Seta, Misaki Okusawa)

[Shopping Mall - Bookstore]

Rimi: (...Oh, this magazine...!)
Rimi: (A special feature on studying abroad... Maybe I’ll give it a read?)
Kaoru: Hey there. Hello, little kitten.
Rimi: K-Kaoru-san...!? Hello...! Are you shopping?
Kaoru: Oh, today I’m escorting Misaki. While waiting for her to finish checking out, I’m searching for a fleeting encounter with a good book...
Kaoru: Hehe... To think I’d have the chance to meet you, little kitten. Ah, what incredible fortune...!
Rimi: Oh, I’m the one who’s lucky...!
Rimi: (Just getting to see Kaoru-san after so long was already wonderful, but to be able to have a conversation like this...!)
Kaoru: So, the magazine you were looking at... was this one? Here, allow me.
Rimi: Thank you so much!
Rimi: (That's right...! It's a rare chance to meet Kaoru-san, so I want to tell her I’m considering studying abroad...! If I tell her, I might even get some courage from her...!)
Rimi: U-um! Kaoru-san!
Kaoru: What’s the matter?
Rimi: Uh... well... um...
Rimi: (Ugh... my heart’s racing so much that I can’t speak properly... I managed to tell everyone in Poppin'Party just fine, though...)
Kaoru: ...Studying abroad is indeed a fleeting thing. I’ve experienced it myself, so I understand how you feel.
Rimi: Eh, Kaoru-san, you’ve studied abroad before?
Kaoru: Ah, just a little, at Broadway.
Rimi: (I didn’t know that... When did she go there? Oh, maybe it was before she became a high school student...!)
Rimi: Broadway is the place where they perform lots of musicals and plays, right?
Kaoru: Indeed. It’s a place where countless artists and countless audiences gather. A city of dreams and entertainment that feels like one big stage!
Rimi: Wow...! To think you studied abroad in a place like that... as expected of Kaoru-san! You must have had so many incredible experiences, right?
Kaoru: Truly, everything that happened there was an irreplaceable experience.
Kaoru: There were language barriers, but what truly matters is your heart. As long as you have sincerity, your feelings will be conveyed—that's something I came to understand firsthand.
Rimi: Um... but wasn’t it hard being apart from everyone?
Kaoru: Of course, being separated from my friends was painful. The pain of feeling like my heart was being torn apart is something I’ll never forget.
Kaoru: But all of them supported me wholeheartedly. It was because I didn’t want to waste their support that I was able to spread my wings and soar, even in a foreign land.
Rimi: I see... so that's how it was...
Kaoru: There were mornings when loneliness shook me, and nights when it felt like I might be swallowed by fierce waves. But living through those fleeting days filled with entirely new encounters became an irreplaceable experience in my life.
Kaoru: Through my journey in a foreign land, I was able to meet a new version of myself, allowing Kaoru Seta to evolve more than ever before.
Rimi: (That's amazing...! I really want to study abroad too and meet a new version of myself!)
Rimi: (Even though I feel anxious... I still want to grow more and more...!)
Rimi: (...I want Kaoru-san to hear these feelings too... But to share this with someone like Kaoru-san, who's already achieved so much, I feel like I haven’t taken even the first step yet... So...)
Rimi: Um, excuse me!
Kaoru: What’s the matter?
Rimi: ...If I ever figure out the path I want to take in life, would you be willing to listen to my story?
Kaoru: Of course. I’ll be waiting eagerly to hear what you have to share, little kitten.
Rimi: Thank you so much!
Misaki: Sorry to keep you waiting, Kaoru-san. I thought I overheard something about studying abroad? ...Huh? Rimi?
Rimi: Misaki-chan! Kaoru-san really is incredible, isn’t she...!
Misaki: Eh? Uh... yeah?
Rimi: Like Kaoru-san, who confidently accomplished studying abroad on her own, I want to become someone with that kind of determination and initiative... No, I’ve decided—I will!
Rimi: Kaoru-san, thank you so much for sharing your valuable experiences! I’ll do my best! Well, see you later!
Misaki: Huh...? Accomplished studying abroad...?
Misaki: No, no, her 'studying abroad' was just a one-week trip! Hey, Rimi! Wait—!
Kaoru: Oh dear, she’s already gone so far away. Haha, such an energetic little kitten.
Misaki: ...She definitely didn’t hear that, did she?
Misaki: So, what now? Looks like Rimi misunderstood something, though.
Kaoru: Hehe, as Shakespeare once said: 'Send your adorable children off to see the world.'
Misaki: Wait, isn’t that not a Shakespeare quote? Kaoru-san, next time you meet her, make sure to explain properly!
Kaoru: Hehe... The one who’ll be talking next time isn’t me. It’ll definitely be... the kitten herself.
(Black cut)

Rimi: Uu~... I still feel a bit fidgety...
