Rimi: A place to come home to... you mean, like home?
Kaoru: In a way, yes. For me, that’s Hello, Happy World!. And for you, that’s Poppin'Party. A precious, irreplaceable place.
Kaoru: When you’re abroad, you might have nights where you feel like you’ve learned nothing at all... or feel lonely in an unfamiliar land.
Kaoru: But in those moments, just knowing “I have a special place to return to, where my friends are waiting for me”... that thought alone will warm your heart.
Kaoru: And when you think about what you want to bring back to that place... your doubts will fade away.
Rimi: Wow, Kaoru-san...! You’re so cool~!!
Misaki: Ugh, there you go again saying all that vague stuff...
Rimi: Misaki-chan! You’re with Kaoru-san today?

Rimi: Fufu. Hello, Happy World! really is full of energy as always.

Rimi: ...? Why not?
Misaki: You probably haven’t heard this before, but... she only studied abroad for a week. At that point, it’s basically just a vacation.
Rimi: Wait, really!?
Kaoru: Fufu...
Misaki: Ah, so you really didn’t know. So yeah, just keep that in mind...
Rimi: Even so, Kaoru-san’s still amazing!
Misaki: Huh...? How did you come to that conclusion?
Rimi: Because... to think such deep thoughts in just one week is really impressive!
Kaoru: Fufu, it seems you truly understood, Rimi-chan. The fleeting charm of Kaoru Seta cannot be concealed... even if I tried!
Rimi: Yes!!
Misaki: ...Seriously...?
Misaki: Well, if it helped Rimi, then I guess it’s fine...
Rimi: Yeah. Kaoru-san really taught me something important. I’m glad I worked up the courage to tell her.
Rimi: (A place to come home to, huh...)
