Ran: (Climbing the stairs to the rooftop. When I push open the heavy door with my weight, there’s a new—)
(Background change)
Ran: ...Sunset...

Ran: You messed up too, didn’t you?
Tomoe: Hey, that’s not relevant now!

Tomoe: That was just because you threw me off. That won’t happen next time.
Ran: I won’t mess up next time either.
Ran & Tomoe: ...Pfft, ahaha!

Ran: ......

Ran: Okay, here we go. ...One, two, three...
Moca: Four, five...
Tomoe: Six... seven...
Tsugumi: Eight, nine...
Himari: Ten...
Himari: Ah! It’s true! I can see the shadows in the sky!
Moca: Five figures floating in the sunset... now that’s something special~.
Tomoe: I guess this is what our shadows look like on our way home too.
Tsugumi: Yeah...! We're always too caught up in chatting to notice.

Ran: (...Thank you, everyone)


<The Walk Home Before Sunset>

(Character: Ran Mitake)

[CiRCLE Café]

Marina: It's hot again today. Now that work's settled down, want to take a break inside?
Ran: Hello, Marina-san, {{userName}}-san.
Marina: Oh, hi Ran-chan! What brings you here today?
Ran: Um... do you two have a moment right now?
Marina: Yeah, we just finished up, so it's fine.
Ran: I'm glad... um...
Ran: I heard from Himari and the others that you said you'd be waiting for us, and I wanted to thank you properly.
Ran: I'm sorry for making you worry about so many things.
Marina: No, there's nothing to apologize for! We're really happy—and relieved too.
Marina: During GBT, you were so fired up, Ran-chan... almost burning out. But now, you seem so much warmer.
Ran: Back then... we were all giving everything we had to reach the dream we wanted to fulfill as Afterglow.
Ran: I felt like I had to make that dream come true... I was so desperate, I couldn’t see anything else.
Marina: I see... was there a reason why you felt so desperate?
Ran: ...Maybe it's because I started thinking about the future after graduation when I became a third-year.
Ran: The future that used to feel far away suddenly felt so close.
Ran: And I started losing confidence... wondering if we could keep going as a band the way we always had.
Marina: I see... and the choice you made was...
Ran: To bring Afterglow to a pause.
Ran: I thought if we kept going half-heartedly, Afterglow would start to lose its shine.
Ran: So I made the decision to step away, focusing on the time we had left and letting go.
Ran: I told myself we’d done an unforgettable live, and now it was time to focus on flower arrangement. That even without the band, we’d still be together.
Ran: But in the end...
Marina: You realized you still needed the band, didn’t you?
Ran: Yes. Tomoe said it—our band activities are like a diary. That idea really stuck with me.
Ran: Afterglow is about us sharing the experiences and emotions we gain together and turning them into music.
Ran: Afterglow is our place to belong. It's part of my life. I’d forgotten something so obvious.
Ran: Honestly... I don’t know what I was thinking.
Marina: Looking back, maybe so. But I think that kind of struggle is totally normal.
Marina: The fear you had—that the band might start to fade—is something every band probably faces at some point.
Ran: ...Really?
Marina: Yeah... when hard times start to outweigh the fun, the atmosphere gets heavy. And once that happens, it's hard to get back to how it was.
Marina: But you all are childhood friends and bandmates. I think that’s why you were able to decide to keep going with Afterglow, like writing a diary.
Ran: You're right... if we were just childhood friends, I might not have felt this way.
Ran: But through music, I can express things I can’t put into words.
Ran: I’m really glad we formed a band.
Marina: Me too—seeing you like this makes me happy, Ran-chan.
Ran: Sorry for talking so much and taking up your time.
Marina: Not at all—thank you for coming to talk. And come hang out at CiRCLE again soon, okay?
Ran: I will... see you again soon.
