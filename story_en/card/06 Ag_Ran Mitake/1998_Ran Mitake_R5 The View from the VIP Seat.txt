1998_Ran Mitake_R5 The View from the VIP Seat

Gacha phrase: If you don’t sit—whoa!?

<Sunset from Here Pt. 1>

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa)

[Downtown]

Himari: I'm so tired~!
Ran: Yeah... It happens every week, but for some reason, this day always feels extra exhausting after class.
Tomoe: Yeah... It’s not right after the weekend, and not right before it either. Must be the schedule.
Tomoe: All the teachers are strict, so if you doze off even a little...
Tsugumi: Come on, Tomoe-chan. You shouldn't be sleeping in any class, you know?
Moca: Speaking of dozing off~... Ran, you totally fell asleep during the fifth-period math class, didn’t you~?
Ran: Huh?
Ran: ...No, I didn’t.
Moca: I can see your seat really well from mine~. I’m pretty sure you were totally asleep.
Ran: You should focus on class instead...
Moca: But I was curious about what you were doing~. So? You were sleeping, right~?
Ran: ...Just a little.
Himari: Wait, really!? That’s so brave of you, Ran~!
Ran: It's not like I meant to fall asleep...
Ran: That seat is right by the window, and the sunlight hits just right, so it’s impossible not to feel drowsy...
Moca: And it’s towards the back, so even if you doze off a little, the teacher won’t notice~.
Ran: It’s not like I sleep every time...
Himari: Plus, being by the window means you can look outside during class—so freeing! I’m so jealous~!
Ran: Yeah, I guess... Being able to see outside is actually kind of nice.
Tsugumi: Oh, that reminds me, I’ve seen you looking out the window from your seat after school before, Ran-chan.
Himari: Ughhh, I’m so jealous~! That’s not fair, Ran~!
Ran: Then why don’t you aim for that seat in the next seating change?
Moca: No, no, no, this Moca-chan is the one who’s gonna claim Ran’s seat~.
Himari: Whaaat!? Moca, you’re my rival now!? I won’t lose...!
Tsugumi: Hearing you all talk about it is making me want to sit in Ran-chan’s seat too...
Ran: Tsugumi, your seat is by the hallway, huh?
Tsugumi: Yeah. It’s not like I dislike it, but...
Tsugumi: If I could sit in Ran-chan’s seat, I feel like I’d soak up the sunlight and have a much brighter mood in class.
Moca: Sounds nice~ You’d be able to photosynthesize happily every day~.
Moca: Ran, swap seats with me so Moca-chan can photosynthesize~.
Himari: Me too! I wanna swap too~!
Ran: No way. That’s my seat right now.
Himari: Come on~! Just for a day... No, even just the morning is fine!
Ran: Nope.
Moca: Please~! At least just for math class tomorrow~.
Ran: ...If the teacher catches us swapping seats in math, I’m definitely getting in trouble too.
Tomoe: Yeah, that’s true.
Tsugumi: Ahaha...
Himari: Ran~ Pleeease~... Oh, wait! Math! Tomorrow’s first period, right? Did we have homework?
Tsugumi: Nope, I don’t think we had any for tomorrow.
Himari: Phew~...
Tsugumi: Oh! Speaking of tomorrow, we have P.E. too, don’t we?
Tomoe: Ahh, I hope it’s sunny. If it rains, we’ll have to do strength training in the gym.
Himari: What!? No way! It has to be sunny~!
Ran: According to the weather forecast, it should be sunny tomorrow. Perfect napping weather.
Moca: Which means... Ran’s seat is gonna feel extra nice tomorrow~.
Ran: Ugh, here we go again.
Moca: Moca-chan won’t give up~. Ran, please have mercy~.
Ran: So persistent. I’m never giving it up.
Moca: So mean~... Boohoo...
Tsugumi: Come on, Moca-chan...
Tsugumi: Oh, this is where we part ways. See you all tomorrow!
Himari: Yeah! See you~!
Moca: Bye-bye~!
Tomoe: Ran, don’t fall asleep in class tomorrow~.
Ran: I won’t! Geez...
Ran: ...See you all tomorrow.
