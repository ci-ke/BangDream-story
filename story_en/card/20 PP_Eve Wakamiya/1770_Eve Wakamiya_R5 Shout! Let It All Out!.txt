<President Wakamiya’s Tea>

(Character: Kanon Matsubara, Chisato Shirasagi, Eve Wakamiya, Sayo Hikawa)

[Hanasakigawa Girls' Academy - Tea Room]

Eve: Welcome to the Tea Ceremony Club! Well then, everyone. Please take a seat.
Kanon: We'll be in your care today, Eve-chan.
Chisato: I'm already aware of tea ceremony etiquette, but it's still a little nerve-wracking to be in an actual tea room...
Sayo: Me too. Despite having some experience, it doesn't change the fact that I'm unfamiliar with it... I hope I don't do anything rude.
Eve: There is no need to worry! You may leave everything to me, Eve Wakamiya!
Eve: If you wish, you can make the tea yourselves. However, I would like to do that for you all today!
Eve: So, please relax and enjoy yourselves!
Sayo: Even so, there should be a certain etiquette for those receiving the tea, shouldn't there...?
Chisato: Also, I'm not used to... sitting on my heels for a long time...
Kanon: You're scared your legs might go numb, right? It's been a while for me too, so I'm a bit worried.
Eve: Please rest assured! Mashiro-san and the others were here earlier...
Eve: And Ako-san's legs were numb immediately, so instead of sitting on her heels, she enjoyed her tea while sitting comfortably!
Eve: So please relax as well, everyone!
Sayo: Sigh... Udagawa-san, really...?
Kanon: Fufu, I think it's a lot better than having to endure something and not have fun in the end.
Chisato: Ah... I suppose you're right.
Eve: Well then, I shall tell you about the tools required to make tea!
Chisato: That reminds me, the tools change depending on the seasons, don't they?
Kanon: Not only that, it changes based on the event too.
Kanon: The flowers that are displayed, the hanging scroll, the ingredients... Everything is used based on different situations.
Eve: Exactly! For example, the shape of this bowl will be different according to the season. Today, I shall be using a summer bowl that cools the tea down faster!
Eve: This container is called a tea caddy, and it's where matcha is stored. And this is a tea scoop that is used to scoop tea. When we mix the tea, we use this tea whisk!
Eve: Now then, I shall make some tea using these tools!
Sayo: We look forward to it.
Eve: I shall begin.
Eve: ...
Sayo: Even though the process is complicated, she's doing it without hesitation.
Kanon: Yeah. She's more composed now compared to when I was in the Tea Ceremony Club. Her movements are refined, too.
Chisato: I'm even more excited to have some tea now.
(Black cut)
Kanon: That was a great performance.
Chisato: It was delicious. Thank you, Eve-chan.
Chisato: Enjoying tea in a café is nice, but drinking matcha peacefully in a tea room like this is wonderful too.
Eve: I am delighted you think so!
Eve: However, I was actually quite nervous. I almost had the order confused while I was doing it.
Sayo: You were nervous?
Eve: Yes! I wanted you all to have wonderful memories and to enjoy your time here...
Eve: Those thoughts made my heart race...! But I am glad you all seemed to have fun!
Kanon: Your feelings definitely reached us, Eve-chan.
Chisato: You wanted us to relax and have fun, so you avoided using technical terms to tell us about the tools, didn't you?
Chisato: And when you were making tea, you faced it more seriously than anyone else did...
Sayo: It was as if I could hear a voice saying, "I want them to have a wonderful moment with this delicious tea."
Sayo: Every word you say, and every movement you make, makes us feel incredibly cared for... We had a lovely time.
Kanon: Eve-chan, you're doing a great job as the president of the Tea Ceremony Club.
Eve: Everyone...
Eve: Hearing you all say that makes my heart feel so very full!
Chisato: Oh my, you were so elegant until just moments ago, but now you're your usual self.
Kanon: Thank you for taking such good care of us, Eve-chan!
Eve: No, not at all...!
Eve: It is thanks to all of you that I had a wonderful time as well!
Eve: And in turn, this year's culture festival might just become an amazing memory! Thank you so much!
Chisato: Fufu... But I think it's a little early for the culture festival to be a memory just yet.
Sayo: Yes. This is just the beginning, Wakamiya-san.
Kanon: Let's enjoy it fully, Eve-chan.
Eve: I shall!
