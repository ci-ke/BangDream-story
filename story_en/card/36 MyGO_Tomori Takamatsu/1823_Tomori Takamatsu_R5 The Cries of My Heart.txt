Gacha phrase: … Keep moving forward, even if you're lost!

<Successor to the Astronomy Club>

(Character: Tomori Takamatsu, Anon Chihaya)

[Haneoka Girls' Academy Astronomy Club Room]

Anon: Tomori-n, it's about time for practice!
Tomori: ............
Anon: Tomorin?
Tomori: ............
Anon: She's writing something intensely again. I wonder what she's writing, I'll take a look.
Anon: Let me see...? "The List of Constellations Seen in Summer"...
Anon: Deneb in Cygnus, Altair in the constellation of the Eagle, and Vega in the constellation of Lyra... Oh, they are connected to form the Summer Triangle!
Tomori: What...!? Ano-chan...?
Anon: You just noticed me now? I've been calling you for a long time.
Tomori: Sorry...
Anon: Okay, but... what were you writing?
Tomori: Eh...
Anon: Oh, you don't have to hide it.
Tomori: ... Well, it's a picture of the constellations.
Tomori: I copied it... from an illustrated book.
Anon: Oh, wow. Sounds like a lot of work.
Tomori: It's my club activity.
Anon: Oh, so that's one of them. You do a lot of things, don't you?
Anon: ... I mean, I don't even know what the Astronomy Club usually does.
Tomori: Let's see...
Tomori: Polishing rocks...
Tomori: They're in the shape of stars.
Tomori: And then we collect... Band-aids.
Tomori: Plasters from the constellations series.
Anon: ... Hmm, it's more like Tomorin than an astronomy club.
Anon: Well, seems like the previous heads of the club were also eccentric, so I feel like you're inheriting from them.
Anon: It's really a free club, isn't it?
Tomori: ... Yes. It's kind of cozy.
Tomori: That's why I decided to join this club.
Anon: ... Well, I think it's a good fit for Tomorin.
Anon: But I had an image of an astronomy club as something like astronomical observation.
Anon: Don't you do that kind of thing?
Tomori: Oh... We do!
Anon: Wow! On the rooftop of the school? They have a great telescope there, don't they?
Tomori: Yeah... And I also go to the planetarium.
Tomori: I also stargaze... at home.
Anon: What, you have a telescope at home? That's amazing!
Anon: You must really like stars! What about them do you like, Tomorin?
Tomori: Eh... What about...?
Tomori: Well... there are various colors and shapes...
Tomori: So they're... interesting...?
Anon: Ahaha, you're starting to question yourself.
Anon: If you like it, you should be more confident!
Tomori: ... Yes.
Anon: But I see. Hearing you talk about it like this, makes it sound kinda fun.
Anon: Especially astronomical observation at school would be nice! A school building at night sounds exciting!
Tomori: ......!
Tomori: Ano-chan... do you want to join us?
Tomori: Astronomy club...!
Anon: Huh? Oh, no, I'll think about it some more.
Anon: I just thought it would be fun... Sorry for getting your hopes up.
Tomori: Oh... No. I'm sorry too...
Anon: ... Ah jeez, let's put an end to that! Come on, let's get going to practice!
Tomori: ... Yeah!


<Walking with a Band-aid>

[RiNG Cafeteria]

Tomori: ............
Ririko: I knew it's you, Tomori-chan. Hi there.
Tomori: ............
Ririko: Tomori-chan? What's up with all the band-aids on the table?
Tomori: ... Ano-chan gave them to me.
Ririko: Oh, from Ano-chan?
Tomori: ... Hehe.
Ririko: Tomori-chan?
Tomori: ...? Oh, uh...!?
Tomori: Uh, since when...!?
Ririko: ... Did you only just notice, Tomori-chan?
Tomori: ... I'm sorry.
Ririko: Huh, you must have been very focused. What were you thinking?
Tomori: Oh... About doing my best...?
Ririko: Whoa, that's good. You mean the band?
Tomori: ... Yes. I was afraid of failing... But.
Tomori: But if everyone is here... and if I have bandages...
Tomori: I'll be okay even if I fall down.
Ririko: ... Tomori-chan, you've changed somehow.
Tomori: ......?
Ririko: When you performed live here for the first time, you looked so nervous and anxious.
Ririko: Hmm when did it start? From that time when you asked to perform solo, you've gradually looked less and less like that.
Tomori: ............
Ririko: When I watch the live performances of "MyGO" now, I guess I can feel it.
Ririko: Tomori-chan's feelings... That the band is important, so you'll do your best...
Tomori: ......!
Ririko: I'm sorry if I'm saying something weird.
Tomori: Oh, umm... It's not weird. Rather... I'm happy.
Ririko: I see, that's great! I'll be looking forward to your growth from now on too!
Tomori: ... I'll do my best.
