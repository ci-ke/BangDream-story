<Effort, Grit, and Friendship>

(Character: Nanami Hiromachi)

[CiRCLE Cafeteria]

Nanami: Hmm, I see...
Marina: Oh, it’s Nanami-chan. ...She’s reading a book. Maybe I shouldn’t bother her right now.
Nanami: Hm? Oh, Marina-san. Hello~
Marina: Hey, Nanami-chan. Sorry, did I interrupt your reading?
Nanami: No, not at all~ I was just about to take a break.
Marina: I see. So, what are you reading?
Nanami: This here. It's Hiromachi’s latest recommendation~
Marina: Let’s see... ‘Break Through Your Limits! The Path Paved by Effort and Grit’ Wow, that’s quite an intense title!
Marina: So, what made you pick this book up?
Nanami: Well, you remember the pop-up book we made for the wedding, right? That experience left a big impression on me...
Marina: Ah, yes! The book that told their love story. It was such a wonderful gift. I thought it was beautiful.
Nanami: Thank you~
Nanami: Actually, it was during the preparation for that book that I learned the importance of effort and grit.
Marina: Oh? How so?
Nanami: Well... The night before the wedding, the book got taken away by mistake. I thought, ‘There’s no way we’ll finish it in time.’ But then Hagu-senpai said to me—
Nanami: Don’t give up! If we put in the effort and grit, we can make it!
Marina: Haha, that sounds just like Hagumi-chan.
Nanami: Even though Shiro-chan and the others were completely exhausted, they kept pushing through without giving up.
Nanami: Seeing them, I thought, ‘I’ve got to push past my own limits, too.’
Nanami: We ended up pulling an all-nighter, and it was so exhausting... But when we finished it, the sense of accomplishment was incredible. And more than anything— seeing Yuka-san and everyone so happy made it all worth it.
Marina: Yuka-chan was here at CiRCLE the other day— she said she’s been re-reading that book over and over!
Nanami: Really? Fufu, I’m so glad we gave it our all...
Nanami: Sure, the wedding day was full of great memories, but for me, even the preparations became a cherished memory~
Marina: Fufu, that’s so lovely.
Marina: Hearing your passion, you know what? I’m feeling inspired to give today my all, too!
Nanami: Yeah! That’s the spirit~
Nanami: Our motto is: Effort and Grit!
