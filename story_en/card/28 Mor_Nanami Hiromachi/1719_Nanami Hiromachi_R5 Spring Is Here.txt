<Spring Is Here>

Anime story


<The Spring and Youth beside Me>

(Character: Mashiro Kurata, Toko Kirigaya, Nanami Hiromachi, Tsukushi Futaba, Rui Yashio)

[Mountain Summit]

Nanami: Mmm~! I love feeling the spring breeze~!
Tsukushi: Me too! Ahh~. The air is so fresh here.
Nanami: It's the perfect weather to have a picnic~.
Tsukushi: I'm glad we all found a day to hang out. It's good to relax every now and then.
Nanami: Exactly~. We've been so busy lately with our part-time jobs, school, and...
Tsukushi: Practicing for the GBT too, right? We may have worked a bit too hard.
Nanami: Fufufu. Let's take it easy today~.
Tsukushi: Oh, is this the spot that your father was talking about?
Nanami: Yeah! He said we'd have the place all to ourselves, and that the view is amazing here~.
Tsukushi: I see what he meant. The mountains around us are beautiful, and it's so quiet that you can hear the birds singing! Let's spread the picnic blanket here, then.
Nanami: Do you think our voices will echo if we shout? There are so many mountains here.
Tsukushi: Hmm, I'm not sure.
Nanami: Yoohoo~!
Nanami: ...! It was really faint, but I could hear it! Did you? Tsu-chan, you should shout too~.
Tsukushi: I-I'm alright~.
Nanami: Ahaha, there's nothing to be embarrassed about~.
Tsukushi: ... By the way, everyone sure is late. They said they were getting some drinks, but...
Nanami: They're fine. The convenience store is probably a little far, that's all~.
Nanami: Or maybe Toko-chan's wild side awakened again!
Tsukushi: Fufu, that sounds possible. She's probably exploring.
Nanami: Exactly. Rui-rui is there too, so there's nothing to worry about. All we have to do is wait~.
Tsukushi: Let's do that then!
(Black cut)
Nanami: Whoa, a bush warbler chirped~! It really feels like spring now~.
Tsukushi: You're right! I wonder where it is.
Nanami: It's funny. We don't realize how the signs of spring are all around us.
Nanami: Oh, I see some signs of spring~!
Tsukushi: Don't go out too far, okay~?
Nanami: I won't~♪
Nanami: Tsu-chan, here! A spring delivery~!
Tsukushi: A horsetail! Like my name!
Nanami: I found more. Here you go~.
Tsukushi: Aww, a dandelion fluff!
Nanami: Huff!
Tsukushi: Kya...!
Nanami: Ahaha, you have signs of spring all over you~.
Tsukushi: Hey~, you gave me a fright. It's not good to blow stuff towards people.
Nanami: Okay. I'm sorry~...
Tsukushi: Fufufu. Thank you, though. I got to enjoy the beauty of spring. In return, here...
(White cut)
(Background change)
Nanami: Huh...? Wow, a flower crown~! It's so cute~!
Nanami: Fufu, thank you, Tsu-chan!
Tsukushi: I wanted you to enjoy the beauty of spring, too.
Nanami: Oh~! Fufufu. I wanna wear this flower crown all day~!
Tsukushi: I-I didn't think you'd like it that much. It's easy to make. Do you want me to show you how?
Nanami: Yes, please~! I've gotta gather lots of white clovers.
(White cut)
(Background change)
Toko: We're back~!
Tsukushi: Oh, welcome back, guys! What took you all so long...? Wait, Mashiro-chan, are you okay...?
Mashiro: I wanted to go the normal way...
Toko: Sorry, Shiro~! But hey, we got to explore the area 'cause we used that shortcut, right? Well, I guess it wasn't really a shortcut...
Rui: If we had walked down the determined path, we would not have wasted so much time.
Toko: Oh, Rui~. You don't get the fun in going off the beaten path, do you? It's the best part of going out for a picnic!
Nanami: Fufufu, so you did go exploring.
Toko: Anyway, what's that on your head, Nanami? I love it!
Mashiro: You look like a flower fairy...!
Nanami: Ehehe~, I know, right~? Tsu-chan showed me the beauty of spring♪
Nanami: She's about to teach me how to make a flower crown.
Toko: Fusuke, you made that?
Tsukushi: Hehe. I could teach you too if you want.
Toko: I just know I'll make one that's totally amazing~!
Nanami: Shiro-chan, do you wanna try it with us?
Mashiro: S-sure... I don't know if it will look good, though...
Rui: I'll watch you while I rest.
Toko: I see~! So you're just gonna run away from how amazing I am, huh~?
Rui: I am exhausted because you decided to use that so-called "shortcut".
Tsukushi: Now, now. Anyway, Rui-san, just let me know if you want to join us later.
Toko: Alrighty~. Let's gather as many flowers as we can! C'mon, Shiro!
Mashiro: W-wait, Toko-chan!
Nanami: (Having fun with everyone like this is making my heart spring to life~.)
Nanami: (Oh, I found another sign of spring♪)
(Black cut)
(Background change)
