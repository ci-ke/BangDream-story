<In My Own Words>

(Character: Maya Yamato)

[Residential Area]

Maya: Oh, {{userName}}-san, hello! Are you on your way home from work? Enjoy your rest.
Maya: Yes, I just finished a magazine shoot, and I'm on my way home too.
Maya: It ended earlier than I expected, so I decided to take a bit of a detour first. That's why I'm taking a stroll along the path leading to Haneoka.
Maya: By the way, I found the message you left on the board with messages at Haneoka! Thank you for the really wonderful words.
Maya: ... You know, walking down this path to school brings back memories of the last three years so gradually and vividly.
Maya: It feels really funny.
Maya: ... Back when I first started high school, I enjoyed supporting people, whether it involved being a backup drummer or a theater stagehand.
Maya: Of course, I still like working behind the scenes... but I never imagined I would end up taking center stage as an idol.
Maya: Looking back, though... I'm genuinely glad this is the path I've walked.
Maya: You see, after the graduation ceremony, the members of the theater club also congratulated me.
Maya: They said things like "Thank you for everything" and "Please don't graduate" while crying and giving me a bouquet...
Maya: It wasn't my first time receiving a bouquet... but feelings I'd never felt before came welling up... and I ended up crying too.
Maya: Yes, I also offered them some words.
Maya: It's not exactly my forte... but I did so using my own words and in my own way.
Maya: How grateful I was that I got to know them... The incredible works of theater we had brought into the world...
Maya: So many memories came back one after the other, and I ended up talking for a while...
Maya: It was nowhere near Hina-san's valedictory speech, but I think I did a reasonable job getting my message across.
Maya: ... I see... Well, thank you very much.
Maya: ... Those were some great years, alright.
Maya: Oh, I'm sorry. I became a bit sentimental... Anyway, I have to go this way, so...
Maya: There'll likely still be times when I'll need to be in your care. I hope you don't mind too much!
