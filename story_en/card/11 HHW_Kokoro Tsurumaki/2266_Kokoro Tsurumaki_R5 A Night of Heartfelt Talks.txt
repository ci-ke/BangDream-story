Hagumi: Wow, you really do! There are so many different sizes!

Hagumi: Oh, no! That’s not it...

Kokoro: Oh? Of course it is!
Hagumi: R-really...?

[2 Hour Later]

Hagumi: Okay, let’s draw the goal here... and done! It’s the Hello, Happy World! board game with all of us in it!
Kokoro: It’s finished! It looks super fun!
Hagumi: I even made the game pieces with everyone’s faces! It’ll be so much fun if we all play together!
Kokoro: Yes! Next time, let’s invite everyone and have a sugoroku tournament!
Hagumi: Oh, and maybe we can do it at the treehouse with a sleepover there too!
Kokoro: Wow, what a wonderful idea!
Kokoro: Fufu. I’m really glad you like the treehouse, Hagumi!
Hagumi: Yeah! I totally love it! You said you had it built because you wanted to live there when you were little, right?
Kokoro: Yes! I saw it in a cartoon when I was little. The main character lived in a treehouse, and I thought, “I want to do that too!”
Hagumi: Wait... I think I know that cartoon too...
Hagumi: So the main character’s a cheerful girl, right? She gets separated from her family, but she builds a treehouse where her friends all gather... and she always keeps smiling and doing her best!
Kokoro: Yes, that’s the one! That’s the cartoon I meant!
Hagumi: Wow, I watched that too! I loved it—it was so fun!
Hagumi: So it was that show... Now that I think about it, it really does feel kind of similar!
Kokoro: Similar... You know, Hagumi, you’re just like that protagonist!
Hagumi: Huh? Really?
Kokoro: That girl always forged her own path, even in tough times, and overcame every obstacle in her way.
Kokoro: Just like you, don’t you think?
Hagumi: But... she built her own treehouse and solved everything by herself.
Hagumi: I only get to live in the treehouse because of you and the People in Black, and I get food thanks to the downtown folks and Mii-kun and everyone...
Kokoro: Oh? And what’s wrong with that?
Hagumi: Huh?
Kokoro: Those people helped you, and because of that, you could smile again, right? Then I’m sure they’re happy too!
Kokoro: And besides, even the girl in the cartoon was helped by lots of people!
Hagumi: Kokoron...
Kokoro: And I bet you’ve helped others too, haven’t you? That’s how the circle of helping one another grows!
Hagumi: A circle of helping...?
Kokoro: Exactly! You and I are both part of that circle!
Hagumi: Yeah...! I’ll help lots of people in return for all the help I got!
Kokoro: Yes! And then the people you help will smile, and their smiles will keep spreading and spreading!
Hagumi: Wow, that’s amazing! Now I feel like going out and helping people right this second!
Kokoro: Then tomorrow will be our mission to find people who need help and help them out!
Kokoro: A special edition of the Smile Patrol!
Hagumi: Yay, I’m in~! The Kokoron & Hagumi Help Squad!
Kokoro: Then let’s go to bed early tonight and wake up nice and early tomorrow!
Hagumi: Yeah, let’s! I can’t wait for tomorrow now~.
Hagumi: I just know tomorrow’s gonna be a fun day!
Kokoro: Of course it will!
