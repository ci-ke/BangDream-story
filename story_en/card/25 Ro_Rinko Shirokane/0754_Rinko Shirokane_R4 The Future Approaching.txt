[An Hour Later]

Sayo: This just about ends the patrol, Shirokane-san... Were we walking a little too fast?
Rinko: No. I'm glad... we were able to see so many different clubs...
Rinko: Those first clubs... Tea Ceremony Club, Tennis Club, Kendo Club... I think starting with the clubs I've tried out... helped me feel less nervous...
Sayo: That is good to hear.
Rinko: So Wakamiya-san... was at Kendo Club today instead of Tea Ceremony Club.
Sayo: Correct. There are no tournaments for tea ceremony, as it isn't a competitive skill.
Rinko: And the girls playing tennis... were so into it... Even Okusawa-san... seemed more focused than usual...
Sayo: She was. And there wasn't an air of nervousness. It appeared to me that they felt a positive sense of urgency.
Rinko: Yes... The sports clubs... and the culture clubs... were all very serious...
Rinko: Thank you... Hikawa-san. This was a good learning experience...
Sayo: You're welcome. If you ever want to join a patrol again, don't hesitate to ask. I'm happy to carry out that duty together whenever.
Rinko: Okay. Thanks. Fufu...
Sayo: Shirokane-san? Is something the matter?
Rinko: No... It's just that walking around observing club activities like this... reminded me of when we... tried them out together. What a fun memory...
Rinko: Other students... were passionate on that day too. About teaching me... It was a great experience.
Rinko: I didn't join any clubs... in the end, but... I think that day... is what led me... to join the student council.
Sayo: Fufufu. You never know where you'll find motivation.
Rinko: "Perfect form, perfect shot"... An arrow shot perfectly will certainly hit its mark...
Sayo: You finally hit the target after trying so many times on that day. Like you were the embodiment of the phrase.
Rinko: Yes... And thanks to that, I've held... "perfect form, perfect shot"... as one of my most valued ways of thinking since then.
Rinko: If I perform the right behaviors, I will reach the right goal... If we move forward... in the way that makes us Roselia... we will eventually find ourselves... walking the appropriate path...
Rinko: That's the reason... I didn't feel nervous about Roselia's future...
Rinko: I believe I was able to think the way I did... because I learned about "perfect form, perfect shot." Hikawa-san... Thank you...
Sayo: So that's what got you through. In my opinion, you should be thanking and complimenting yourself, then.
Sayo: Words are like charms. You can hold the phrase "perfect form, perfect shot" all you want, but whether you can do something with it or not comes down to you as a person.
Sayo: You are who you are now not because of that mindset but because you held onto that mindset and used it to try and make positive change. The result comes from your efforts.
Sayo: There are times when we understand the ideal behavior but cannot push ourselves to want to take those actions.
Rinko: I suppose you're right... Still... I want to thank you, Hikawa-san.
Rinko: When I hesitate or am lost, when I'm ready to turn back and walk away... I have you, and I have your words...
Rinko: Just having that is reassuring... I find courage.
Sayo: No... I should be the one thanking you.
Sayo: Shirokane-san. I have the utmost respect for your will to move forward and try to make progress.
Sayo: With you around, we can change for the better... That is what I believe.
Sayo: Let us continue to work our hardest and have a positive influence on each other.
Rinko: Yes... I would love to...
