<About My Costume>

(Character: Yukina Minato, Rinko Shirokane)

Yukina: ......
Rinko: (Wh-what should I do...?  Yukina-san... has been silent...  for some time, now...)
Rinko: (Maybe... she doesn't like my outfits... after all...)
Yukina: Rinko. Did you make all of these yourself?
Rinko: Y... y...
Rinko: (T-too nervous... My voice...)
Yukina: ... I take it that nod means yes, then.  Yes...
Rinko: U-umm...!  I... can make... changes...  Any... changes... you want...
Yukina: ......?  Why would I want changes?  I think they're incredible as they are.
Rinko: ... Huh?
Yukina: The thing is, you use those fingers a great deal for playing the piano.
Rinko: Y-yes...  B-but... I still make sure to... practice really hard, too...
Yukina: I'd expect nothing less from a Roselia member.  But that's the problem, you see...
Yukina: The stitching, these silhouettes... it must take an enormous amount of skill to do all of this. Are you sure your fingers are up to it?
Rinko: I... Yes... No problem...
Yukina: For now, sure.  But what about down the line?
Rinko: Huh...?
Yukina: Rinko. I can see you have great skill as a keyboardist. But now I also see your talents for design and crafts, too.
Yukina: ... I want you to take care of all of Roselia's costume needs, and possibly also artwork needs, going forward as well.
Yukina: Your designs encapsulate the very essence of my music, of this entire band. There is no one else I can count on to do that.
Yukina: However... I realize this is asking a lot of you, beyond just the music. I just wonder if it is the right decision to make...
Rinko: I-I... I...  I really... really want to do it...!
Rinko: The whole time... I was making these outfits, I... I was thinking about Roselia... about your singing...
Rinko: The more I thought about it... the more I came to like it... and the more I enjoyed... making the clothes...
Rinko: When I think about... all of us practicing our music... performing our songs... while wearing these outfits...
Rinko: Just the thought... makes me feel so excited...
Yukina: Rinko...
Yukina: Okay. Just promise me you'll tell me as soon as you feel it start to get tough.
Yukina: I'll be the one to blame if this all starts to have an impact on your performance.
Yukina: ......
Yukina: They say there's only two types of true artist out there.
Yukina: One type is infatuated with just one single aspect. They spend all their time on it, and can do it and it alone. They cannot live without the music.
Yukina: The other type has abilities in all kinds of creative fields, and brings them together with their music to create their own unique world.
Yukina: Rinko. I believe you are the latter.
Rinko: Th-that's... I'm...!  I'm... just...
Yukina: It's rude to bring yourself down in front of people who truly recognize your ability, you know.
Rinko: I... I'm sorry...
Yukina: I hear you're quite the reader, too. You might well be the most creative mind in our band, you know.
Rinko: (I'm happy to receive these compliments, but... I'm just not sure it's true... But if I say that, she'll get mad again...)
Yukina: ... All I have is singing.  There's no other way for me to express myself...
Yukina: To be honest... I felt a little envious when I saw these outfits. I was completely speechless.
Yukina: I'm sorry if... that gave you the wrong impression.
Rinko: N-not at all...
Rinko: (Even our perfect Yukina-san... has some issues of her own...)
Rinko: (... I think I like getting compliments...)
