<A Fresh Challenge>

(Character: Sayo Hikawa, Rinko Shirokane)

[After Practice]

[Walking Home]

Rinko: It's already... rather dark outside... We may have practiced separately today, but... Hikawa-san... you stayed a pretty long time, as well...
Sayo: By the time I noticed, it had already gotten rather late.
Rinko: The same thing... happened to me... I couldn't believe... how much time had passed...
Sayo: Being able to focus on your training is a good thing. Spending so much time in there must be a sign that things are progressing smoothly, yes?
Rinko: They are... I even surprised myself... by just how long I was able to go... Is everything... going well for you, too...?
Sayo: Indeed. I've been able to bunker down and get a lot of work done. It would seem we're both able to make the most of our time as we approach the performance date.
Rinko: Yes... we are...
Rinko: Speaking of which... I feel as though... no amount of practice time... feels like it's enough...
Sayo: Certainly. We need to nail down the arrangements and take care of last-minute adjustments, among other things. All quite time-consuming... Or is something else on your mind?
Rinko: Well... we're doing a joint... performance... That means it will... be longer... than our usual ones... right?
Sayo: Yes, I do believe that to be correct.
Rinko: If so... then our set list... will need to be... equally as long... and should differ... from our typical routine...
Rinko: So it may be best to... arrange our songs... as well as give care to the different timbre... to match that sort of unique set list...
Rinko: And I know the stage lighting... is not something that we... normally pay much mind to...
Rinko: But it may be a good idea... to give it our own special touch... this time around... and see how that works out...
Rinko: We don't do... joint concerts very often... so it may be a good time... to try something new... like using the lights... to help show everyone how Roselia... sees the world...
Rinko: Having all of these things... running through my head is... exciting, of course... but time also... passes so quickly...
Sayo: Fufu. You seem to be enjoying yourself, Shirokane-san.
Rinko: Ah... I apologize...! I've... been rambling on by myself... this entire time...
Sayo: There's no need to apologize. In fact, hearing your opinion has been rather useful.
Rinko: It has...?
Sayo: The point regarding the set list is an especially important matter that we must resolve immediately. If we are to approach it as you have stated, it may take more time than usual to make our decisions.
Sayo: Each song is associated with a different image and approach. We should begin the discussions immediately.
Sayo: Let's gather everyone and consult on the matter. Nailing down a meeting date comes first, of course...
Sayo: Please allow me to arrange the dates and times. Meanwhile, would you be so kind as to create an agenda of items to be discussed?
Rinko: (W-wow... This is typical of Hikawa-san... She knows how to... quickly take charge... She's very reliable...)
Sayo: Shirokane-san? Are you alright?
Rinko: Y-yes... I'm fine... Please leave it to me...
Rinko: Uhm... Hikawa-san? Let's try our best... to make this an amazing show...
Sayo: Yes, of course. With everything we have.
