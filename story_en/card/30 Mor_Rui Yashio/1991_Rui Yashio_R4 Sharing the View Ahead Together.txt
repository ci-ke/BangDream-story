<The Duet of Emotion And Reason>

(Character: Mashiro Kurata, Toko Kirigaya, Nanami Hiromachi, Tsukushi Futaba, Rui Yashio)

[Tsukinomori Girls' Academy – Garden]

Tsukushi: Ugh, why do you make promises like that on your own?!
Mashiro: Yeah... I wonder if everyone participating in the Tsukinomori Music Festival can actually perform together...
Toko: It’s fine, we’ve got this!
Toko: Since it’s already decided that we’re performing on stage, we just need to play Morfonica's song together!
Rui: It’s not that simple. Morfonica songs were made for five people to perform, you know?
Rui: If we’re going to have everyone perform, we’ll need to rearrange it and rewrite the score.
Nanami: Yeah... and since there are so many of us, it’s going to take time for everyone to synchronize.
Rui: Exactly, we’ll need quite a bit of time. It’s going to be nearly impossible to get in sync in the short time before the Tsukinomori Music Festival.
Mashiro: What should we do...? Maybe we should just say we can’t do it now...?
Tsukushi: Yeah, I’m sorry to everyone, but I think that might be the best option.
Toko: No way! I’m against this!
Rui: ............
Nanami: I understand how you feel, but... this time, it’s just not realistic.
Toko: Why, why not? If we do it, it’s going to be so exciting!
Toko: We’ll be performing on the stage we created together, playing with everyone!
Toko: It’ll be the best Tsukinomori Music Festival ever, there’s no way we shouldn’t do it!
Tsukushi: Even if you say that...
Rui: You still have that unrealistic way of thinking, as always.
Rui: But... for this time, I agree with that thought. Let’s do it.
Mashiro, Tsukushi & Nanami: Eh?!
Tsukushi: Is... is it okay, Rui-san!? I mean, can we even do it...?
Rui: Realistically speaking, it’s a reckless challenge.
Rui: But, just like Kirigaya-san said, this Tsukinomori Music Festival was something created by all Tsukinomori students working together.
Rui: If that’s the case, there’s no reason we shouldn’t have a song we can all perform together. That’s just how I feel.
Mashiro: Rui-san...
Toko: Rui~~~~! I knew you’d understand!
Toko: Even though you’re stubborn, you surprisingly get it! I’m impressed!
Nanami: Toko-chan, I don’t think that was a compliment...
Toko: Well then, what song should we do? For me, ‘Flame of Hope’ would be great!
Rui: No, we won’t use an existing song. We’ll make a new one. This time, that’ll be quicker.
Nanami: Ah... I see. Sometimes it’s harder to redo something than to make it from scratch.
Nanami: Plus, this time there are a lot of people performing, so reworking it will probably take a lot of time...
Rui: But whether we really do this or not can’t be decided just by me and Kirigaya-san.
Rui: If we’re making a new song, it’ll put a burden on Kurata-san too, and practicing with limited time will put pressure on everyone.
Rui: So, if we really do this, I want it to be something everyone agrees on, not just me and Kirigaya-san.
Mashiro: ...I might want to do it.
Mashiro: Even though I still feel like it might be impossible, we were able to make the Tsukinomori Music Festival happen.
Mashiro: So, if we do it together, making a new song and performing it, maybe we really can do it.
Tsukushi: Mashiro-chan... well, I guess it can’t be helped.
Tsukushi: Okay, let’s do it! We can decide to give up only after we’ve tried everything we can.
Nanami: Yeah... I’m starting to feel like it might be good for Hiromachi to try too.
Rui: Then, let’s hurry up. By the start of next week, no—by the end of this week, I’ll have the song ready.
Rui: After that, I’ll leave it to you, Kurata-san.
Mashiro: Yeah, I’ll do my best with the lyrics.
Toko: Alright! Let’s make this the greatest, the best music festival ever!
Nanami & Tsukushi: Yesss!!!
Mashiro: Hehe...
Rui: Kurata-san? What is it?
Mashiro: I don’t know, I just feel so happy. Even though we might not complete the new song, I still feel this way...
Mashiro: If that happens, I might get hurt, but...
Mashiro: Watching Rui-san, who isn’t afraid and values her feelings, made my heart feel warm.
Rui: ――――
Rui: ......I just didn’t want to deny these feelings.
Rui: And if we’re going to do it, we’ll make sure it’s a success. Kurata-san, please be ready for that too.
Mashiro: Yeah.
