1833_Rui Yashio_R5 Lured Into a Sweet Sleep

<Lulled to Sweet Slumber>

Anime story


<Third Place>

(Character: Rinko Shirokane, Rui Yashio)

[Café]

Rinko: ...
Rui: ...
Rinko: This café... has a lovely atmosphere. It's nice...
Rinko: Do you come here often, Yashio-san...?
Rui: ... No. I rarely visit this area.
Rinko: ...Oh. I see...! Sorry for making you shop with me...
Rinko: But thanks to you... I bought the book I wanted...
Rui: The Dark Dragon and the Apprentice Knight... Correct? The fantasy novel written by a foreign author that has become popular lately.
Rinko: ...! You're familiar with it...?
Rui: No. I do not usually read that genre of books.
Rui: However... Kurata-san has read it and said it was interesting. I simply remembered that.
Rui: She is Morfonica's songwriter, so it is important to understand how she sees the world, after all.
Rinko: I see... Kurata-san does seem to like that type of story...
Rinko: ...
Rinko: Would you like... to read it too, Yashio-san...?
Rui: Me...? I thought you wanted to buy that book.
Rinko: Yes, but... I haven't finished the book I'm currently reading...
Rinko: We're in a café after all... If you're interested, I could lend it to you...
Rui: ... I will borrow it for a moment, then.
Rinko: Of course... Here you are.
Rinko: I'll be... reading my book beside you...
(Black cut)

[After Some Time]

Rinko: ...
Rui: ...
Rinko: ... Oh. It's getting late...
Rinko: Umm, Yashio-san... How is the time for you...?
Rui: ...
Rinko: Yashio-san...?
Rui: ...
Rinko: Fufu... She fell asleep... I'll leave her be...
Rinko: ...? Oh...!
Rinko: Her book will fal-
(Black cut)
Rui: ...
Rui: ...? What was that sound...?
(Background change)
Rinko: Thank goodness... I made it...!
Rui: (Shirokane-san...? Why are you so close...?)
Rui: (... She is propping my book up... Oh, it was probably about to fall.
Rui: (But... to think I fell asleep in public...)
Rui: How unusual for me...
Rinko: Oh...! Did I wake you up...?
(White cut)
(Background change)
Rui: ... Please do not worry. My carelessness is to blame.
Rui: Usually, I would not do such a thing... Thank you.
Rinko: Fufu... Your exhaustion was probably building up... without you realizing...
Rinko: Doing student council president duties... and band activities at the same time... must be difficult...
Rui: ... Not at all. I should be managing my tasks properly. This does not happen at school or during band practice.
Rui: I do not understand why I fell asleep only now.
Rui: Sleep usually happens when the brain needs to rest. How active you are during the day, your body temperature, your hormonal balance...
Rui: What is different between my usual situation and now...?
Rinko: Umm... Yashio-san...?
Rui: ...
Rui: ... I have a theory.
Rui: Perhaps I feel comfortable around you, Shirokane-san.
Rinko: ... Huh...?
Rui: ... The desire to sleep rarely occurs when one is tense. So the most plausible explanation is that I feel more relaxed than usual when I am with you.
Rinko: ... O-oh, I see...
Rui: ...? What is the matter?
Rinko: N-no, it's nothing... We should get going.
Rui: ...? Understood.
(White cut)
(Background change)
