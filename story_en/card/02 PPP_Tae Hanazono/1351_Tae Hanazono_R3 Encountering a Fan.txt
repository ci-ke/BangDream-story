<Say "Hoot Hoot!">

(Character: Tae Hanazono, Rimi Ushigome, Kokoro Tsurumaki)

[Hanasakigawa Girls' Academy - Class 2B]

Rimi: Phew... Class is finally over... Umm, I've got math next so...
Tae: Rimi, good morning!
Rimi: Ah, O-Tae-chan, good morning.
Tae: I had fun doing vocal exercises in the park with you yesterday.
Rimi: Yes. Thanks for helping me practice. I feel a little better about speaking in public.
Tae: Glad to hear it. Anyway, my senior at work taught me a really good way to train your voice yesterday. I wanted to tell you about it.
Rimi: Huh? You asked them just for me...?
Tae: Yep. You're giving this your best shot so I want to be here for you.
Rimi: O-Tae-chan... Thank you! So what do I have to do?
Tae: Be an owl.
Rimi: An owl...?
Tae: If you copy an owl's hoots in falsetto, it strengthens your throat muscles and improves your voice.
Tae: This will help you be an even better emcee, Rimi. Let's give it a try!
Rimi: Huh...? Right now in this classroom?
Tae: Yeah, obviously! Okay, I'll go first.
Tae: Hoot hoot... Hoot hoot. How'd that sound?
Rimi: Wow, O-Tae-chan, you're really good at this.
Tae: Really? Yay! Now it's your turn, Rimi.
Rimi: U-umm... This is a little embarrassing... However, I'll give it a try.
Rimi: I-is it something like this? H-hoot, hoot... Hoot hoot...
Tae: It sounds good, Rimi. But you need to really tap into an owl's emotions.
Rimi: Wh-what do you mean?
Tae: Picture this. We're in the middle of the forest. It's nighttime. You're perched on top of a big tree.
Rimi: Umm... This is a forest... It's nighttime... I'm an owl in a tall tree...
Rimi: Hoot. Hoo-hoot...
Tae: That's it, Rimi! You were just like an owl that time.
Rimi: Y-you think so...?
Tae: Yep. You sounded like an owl that caught its prey. Try it again.
Rimi: O-okay, sure...! Hoot hoot... Hoot hoot...
Tae: Great! That one sounded like an owlet! You're happy your mom came back to the nest, huh? You sure have a knack for being an owl, Rimi!
Rimi: R-really...? I actually tried to imagine I was one for a moment. Fufu.
Kokoro: Oh my, you two sure are having fun! What are you up to?
Tae: Ah, Kokoro! We're imitating owls.
Kokoro: Wow! That's wonder-tastic! I'll join you! Hoot hoot! Hoot hoot!
Tae: ...! That's amazing, Kokoro...! You're almost the perfect owl! You sound like an owl that's visiting their friend's house!
Tae: Well then, I'll be your owl friend. Hoot, hoo-hoot. Hoot, hoo-hoot!
Kokoro: This is so exciting. It feels like we're out in the forest! Hoot hoot! Hoot hoot! Hoo, hoo-hoot!
Tae: Ho-hoo-hoot. Hoo-hoot?
Kokoro: Ho-hoot! Hoot, hoo-hoot!
Tae: Hoo-hoot! Hoo-hoo-hoot! Hoot!
Hanasakigawa Student A: Hey, what are Hanazono-san and the others doing?
Hanasakigawa Student B: I think... they're playing make-believe owls. Fufu, it's pretty cute.
Tae: Rimi, what's wrong? Join us! Hoot, hoo-hoo. Hoo-hoot, hoot! Hoot!
Rimi: E-excuse me, O-Tae-chan, Kokoro-chan. Everyone's looking at us~.
Kokoro: Oh, then we can all play in the forest together! Ho-hoo-hoot! Hoot, hoot, hoot!
Tae: Fufu, this is fun! Hoot, hoo-hoot. Hoot, hoo-ho-hoot!
