<To the Rumored Coffee Shop>

(Character: Moca Aoba, Tsugumi Hazawa)

[A Certain Station]

Tsugumi: We're here~! I'm pretty sure this is the place...
Moca: Wow~. So this is where the coffee shop everyone was talking about is~.
Tsugumi: Seems so! Thanks for coming with me today, Moca-chan!
Moca: Don't sweat it~. I'll go with you anywhere around the country if it's for something yummy~.
Moca: The curry is supposed to be good too, right~? I can't wait~.
Tsugumi: M-mhm! The curry and the coffee!
Tsugumi: With their blends, I'll be able to drink mine black... Or at least, I should be able to!
Moca: I see~. I'm not really seeing anything that looks like a coffee shop though...
Tsugumi: Y-yeah...
Moca: It's supposed to be named after a famous novelist, right? Maybe it closed down...?
Tsugumi: I-I don't think so... The place was really popular, after all...
Moca: Hmm~, which means... they might have moved?
Tsugumi: Yeah, maybe! Moca-chan, let's try to find it!
Moca: Okay~. Let's do it~.

(Background change)

[Residential Area]

Tsugumi: We've already done quite a bit of walking, but there haven't been any buildings that look like it might be it...

Tsugumi: Huh? Batting cages?! I can't believe they'd have those around here...
Moca: Now I really wanna know what's going on inside. Let's stop by for a bit~.
Tsugumi: What?! Uh, yeah. I guess I do kind of wanna know...
(Black cut)

[An Hour Later]

Moca: Hmm, to think you'd hit a home run at that very moment...
Tsugumi: Ehehe, that was so fun! But now I'm covered in sweat after moving around so much.
Moca: Then do I have good news for you~. Take a look over there~.
Tsugumi: Ah, a bathhouse! Fufu, I like the ones that are old-fashioned like this~.
Moca: They're having mugwort baths today~. I'm sure soaking in that is extra refreshing after you've worn yourself out.
Tsugumi: Ngh... It's hard to say no, but I feel like this isn't the time to be taking it easy…
Moca: Aww~, c'mon~. It'll be fine~. Check it out. You can even borrow towels and stuff.
Tsugumi: Mmm~...! O-only for a little while!
Moca: Okay~. Right this way, ma'am~.
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[An Hour Later]

[Residential Area]

Tsugumi: W-we really took it easy in there...
Moca: It was nice and relaxing~. I kind of feel like my charm improved~.
Moca: And the coffee milk was great. What a satisfying experience~.
Tsugumi: Here I was, planning to drink mine black, and I ended up getting that instead...
Tsugumi: It's already late. I'm sure the coffee shop is closed by now...
Moca: That's alright~. You may not have found that shop, but that's just a sign that you should practice so that you're able to make black coffee that even you can drink~.
Tsugumi: I-it feels like you're trying to pull a fast one on me here, but... you're right! I'll do my best!
Moca: Wow~. Now that's the Tsugu I know. And while you're at it, make some yummy curry, would ya~?
