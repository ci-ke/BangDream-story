963_Masuki Sato_R2 Exciting Performance

<My Buddy Snarey>

(Character: Masuki Sato, Reona Nyubara)

[CHU²'s Penthouse - Studio]

MASKING (Masuki): Hey.
PAREO (Reona): Oh, good morning, Massu-san! You are the first one here!
MASKING (Masuki): You say that, but you were here before me, PAREO.
PAREO (Reona): Naturally. It is my duty to look after CHU²-sama from morning till evening♪
MASKING (Masuki): ... So, where is CHU²? You woke her up, right?
PAREO (Reona): She is still sleeping. She requested "just ten more minutes" ever so cutely~.
PAREO (Reona): I believe she will awaken by the time everyone has arrived♪
MASKING (Masuki): Sigh... She is so spoiled. Well, I've got things to do, so whatever.
PAREO (Reona): Things to do...? Ahh, do you mean drum maintenance?
MASKING (Masuki): Yep. I just did it the other day, but there ain't no harm in doin' it again.
MASKING (Masuki): I should probably polish my cymbals soon too.
PAREO (Reona): Hmm, cymbals. That reminds me, Massu-san, you often clean your drums but leave the cymbals as they are, don't you?
PAREO (Reona): I am not too knowledgeable about drums, but... is there a reason for this?
MASKING (Masuki): Ahh... Well, there kinda is. It all depends on the sound you want.
MASKING (Masuki): Basically, the sound changes dependin' on how much you polish 'em. I wanna keep the sound as is, so I just check 'em every now and then.
MASKING (Masuki): But every drummer has their own preferences.
PAREO (Reona): Oh, I see! I learned something new today! Thank you very much!
PAREO (Reona): Cymbal maintenance is very complex~.
MASKING (Masuki): Yeah. But the drums themselves are way easier, y'know? You just have to keep 'em clean.
MASKING (Masuki): You have to wipe the bolts after you hit 'em. And you gotta do it every time so they don't get rusty.
PAREO (Reona): I see. I did notice that you always wipe your drums with a cloth after practice!
PAREO (Reona): Huh... So that's how the drum sound of RAS is created... I am moved!
MASKING (Masuki): But ain't it the same with other instruments? You have to wipe the strings on guitars and basses, and I'm sure there are things you gotta do for the keyboard too.
PAREO (Reona): Yes, I do wipe the keys, but... it's an electronic instrument, so I take it into a store for more complicated maintenance.
PAREO (Reona): Truthfully, I would love to look after my cute partner myself, but-
MASKING (Masuki): The inside of a keyboard ain't a place for amateurs to mess around in.
PAREO (Reona): Exactly~... And it would void the manufacturer's warranty~...
MASKING (Masuki): That's the difference between acoustic and electric. Although ya can't really say which is better.
PAREO (Reona): No, you cannot♪ But that is why I think it is wonderful how you properly take care of your instrument, Massu-san.
PAREO (Reona): I am sure Drum-chan is very happy too~♪
MASKING (Masuki): Drum-chan...? What're you talkin' 'bout?
PAREO (Reona): What?! Don't tell me you haven't named your instrument, Massu-san! It's your partner that's been with you since the start of RAS!
MASKING (Masuki): No... I haven't... Which one would I even name...?
PAREO (Reona): What~?! Then I will help you name them! I will think of some extremely cute names!
MASKING (Masuki): My drums don't need names... But I guess you're right about the partner part.
MASKING (Masuki): I don't wanna play 'em half-heartedly either. I guess that means I'm pretty attached to 'em.
PAREO (Reona): That's great~! Then let's name them, right here and now! This round, flat drum can be-
MASKING (Masuki): That's the snare drum.
PAREO (Reona): Then... Snarey! How is that?
MASKING (Masuki): ... It's cute.
