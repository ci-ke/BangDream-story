event02-03 Chapter 2: A Singer's Worth

Ako and Rinko want to perform the song that Yukina played for them. What will her answer be...?

(Character: Yukina Minato, Lisa Imai, Ako Udagawa, Rinko Shirokane)

[Walking Home]

Ako: That song Yukina-san played us was so cool~! But...
Rinko: She said it's beyond our level... didn't she...?
Ako: Yeah... but if we practice really hard, I'm sure we'll be able to play it. I wanna reach the level of that song!
Rinko: I feel the same way...
Ako: Oh... that's Lisa-nee and Yukina-san over there, isn't it? Rin-rin, let's go and ask Yukina-san about it one more time!
Rinko: A-Ako-chan...!
Ako: Yukina-san~! Lisa-nee!
(Black cut)
Lisa: Hm? That sounds like...
Yukina: ... Ako? What's the matter?
Ako: *huff* *puff* ... I finally caught up...  U-umm! That song you played us... I think we should do it!
Yukina: Huh...?
Ako: I thought it was really cool! It'll be awesome if we play it live, I just know it!
Yukina: That song... it's...
Rinko: *huff* *huff* ...! Ako-chan, wait for me...!
Ako: Rin-rin! You wanna play that song too, right?
Rinko: Y-yeah... I do. I'm not sure whose song it is, but...
Rinko: I thought it was an amazing song that would go perfectly with your voice, Yukina-san...!
Yukina: My voice...?
Rinko: I like your voice, Yukina-san...! It's delicate but powerful... And there is a yearning restlessness in it because of your passion for music...
Rinko: That's the kind of voice you have. When I heard that song... I was overcome by the same emotion that I felt when I first heard you sing.
Yukina: ...
Rinko: So... umm... I want you to sing that song, Yukina-san...
Ako: If we're not good enough to play it yet, we'll just practice harder and harder until we are...!
Yukina: ... My voice isn't that pure.
Rinko: Huh...?!
Lisa: Yukina? What do you mean...?
Yukina: I'm... I'm not worthy of singing that song yet...
Rinko: Yukina... san...?
Yukina: ... I'm sorry. I appreciate how passionately you feel about this. I need more time to think.
Ako: Okay, I'll be waiting! We'll all be waiting!
Yukina: ...
Lisa: Ah, Yukina! ... Sorry guys, I'm going to go check on Yukina!
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Lisa: Yukina... what on earth is going on? And what do you mean by not being worthy?
Yukina: Lisa... you've realized, haven't you? That the song I played you was one of my father's.
Lisa: ... Yeah. I thought that was the case.
Yukina: It's a song from when he was with an indie label. Which means...
Lisa: Ahh, it's from the time when he was still playing the music he wanted to play...
Yukina: That's right. His passion for music used to be so pure... I couldn't possibly sing one of his songs from that time.
Yukina: I mean, I...
Lisa: It's okay, you don't have to say it. I know where you're going with this, Yukina.
Lisa: Your decision is what's important, I'll respect it no matter what Ako or Rinko say.
Lisa: ... Haha, just don't tell them I said that, okay?
Yukina: Lisa...
Lisa: I'll be waiting for your answer like the others. You can take your time to think about it, but...
Lisa: Those feelings you're painstakingly trying to come to terms with. I just don't want you to forget the whole reason you feel that way is because your passion towards music is so pure.
Yukina: ... The feelings I have to come to terms with...
