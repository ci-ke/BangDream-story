event95-04 Chapter 3: Surrounded by Red

The day continues with some kayaking. The five struggle with their vessels as they view the autumn scenery.

(Character: Rimi Ushigome, Ran Mitake, Hagumi Kitazawa, Yukina Minato, Sayo Hikawa)

[Forest Park]

Hagumi: Next up is kayaking! Woohoo~!
Ran: Hagumi, have you been kayaking before?
Hagumi: Nope, never! That's why I'm so excited~! It'll be my first time looking at the fall leaves from the water too. I can't wait to see what it looks like!
Yukina: It seems they have both two-seaters and three-seaters. How should we distribute ourselves?
Ran: How about we use our leaves from before? Whoever had yellow can be in the three-seater, and the ones who had red can go in the two-seater.
Rimi: Yeah! I think that's a good idea!
Sayo: That means... Ushigome-san and I will be in the two-seater, and Minato-san, Mitake-san, and Kitazawa-san will be in the three-seater.
Hagumi: Alright~! Ran-ran, Minato-san, let's do this!
Ran: Minato-san, this kind of activity is all about teamwork. I hope I can count on you.
Yukina: You'll see what I can do.
Sayo: Ushigome-san, we can do this. I am not sure if I can paddle very well, but let's try our best to show your sister this lovely scenery.
Rimi: Y-yes!
Hagumi: Alright, now let's go~!
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Rimi: Ah...! These paddles are quite heavy, aren't they?
Sayo: Yes. Please be careful not to lose balance.
Hagumi: Okay, I'll start a chant, and we can paddle in time to it! Ready?! Happy! Lucky! Smile! Yay!
Ran: Hagumi...! That's too complicated!
Yukina: Agreed. I'd appreciate it if you did a different chant.
Hagumi: Really? Okay then. One, two! One, two!
Ran: Oh, now you do a normal one.
Hagumi: Ran-ran, Minato-san, count with me! One, two! One, two!
Yukina & Ran: One, two. One, two...
Hagumi: Wow! We're moving guys! Looking good~! One, two. One, two.
Rimi: Forward, back. Forward, back...
Sayo: Ushigome-san, let's correct our posture. That way we'll be able to keep our balance without the paddles interfering.
Rimi: O-Okay!
Hagumi: One, two. One, two! ... Whoa, look! The leaves over there are so pretty!
Ran: They are...! Minato-san, Hagumi, let's quickly paddle over that way.
Hagumi: Okay! All together... One, two!
Yukina & Ran: One, two. One, two...!
(White cut)
(Background change)
Rimi: Huff... huff... We've finally arrived... That was harder than I thought it would be... Hikawa-senpai, sorry I'm so slow at paddling.
Sayo: Don't worry. The water was pushing back at us, so we needed much more strength than even I had anticipated. Now please, look at this breath-taking scenery spreading out in front of us.
Rimi: Wow... You're right...! It's so beautiful...
Ran: The reflections are nice too.
Yukina: Yes. It really is a wonderful sight to see.
Hagumi: Yeah! It's so pretty~...
Rimi: Oh, right! I have to take a video for Sis.
Sayo: Ushigome-san, please be careful not to capsize the boat...!
Rimi: Ah...! S-sorry, I didn't mean to do that...!
Hagumi: I'm gonna take a photo too!
Rimi: Sis, the reflection of the trees on the water is really beautiful too. Look, it's moving~.
Hagumi: Rimi-rin, look this way!
Rimi: Towards you, Hagumi-chan...? Ah...! I shook the boat again... Sorry.
Hagumi: You might be shaking, but one thing's for sure! Fall leaves sure are beautiful~.
Rimi: Yes! They are! And thanks to everyone here, you can see them too now, Sis♪
