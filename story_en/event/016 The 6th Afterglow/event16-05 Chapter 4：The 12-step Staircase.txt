event16-05 Chapter 4: The 12-step Staircase

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa)

[Haneoka Girls' Academy - Hallway]

Himari: Three, four...
