event276-04 Chapter 3: Ghosts Don’t Exist, Right?

As the shoot progresses, the horror vibes intensify. Kasumi heads into the dark forest to set up the camera, but then...

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Tae Hanazono, Rimi Ushigome, Saya Yamabuki, Arisa Ichigaya)

[A Few Days Later]

[Forest]

Kasumi: Okay, today’s shoot is... found it! Scene 9, “Night Forest”!
Kasumi: Director Rimi-rin~! Where should I place the camera!?
Rimi: Hmm, I want a bit of a wide shot~... maybe around here. Kasumi-chan, can you handle it?
Kasumi: Got it. Leave it to me!
Saya: Rimi-rin, sorry! Which prop is O-Tae supposed to hold?
Rimi: Oh, I’ll come over! They’re similar, so I’ll explain.
Kasumi: Let’s see... right about here should do. Make sure it doesn’t fall over... Okay, the phone for filming is set—perfect! Now to power it on before Rimi-rin gets back...
Kasumi: Hya!? Wh-what was that noise!? A bug!?
Kasumi: ...It’s a bug... right...? Uuugh~, this place really gives off a creepy vibe... Rimi-rin found a perfect spooky spot...
Kasumi: N-no, stop thinking scary thoughts! They say that attracts bad energy...! I’ll turn on the power quickly and head back...!
Rimi: Kasumi-chan!
Kasumi: Eeeeek!!??
Rimi: Eh!? Wh-what’s wrong? Are you okay?
Kasumi: Sigh~... Oh, Rimi-rin! You scared me~...! I thought it might’ve been a ghost...!
Rimi: Fufu, don’t worry. I scattered salt just in case.
Kasumi: Eh, really? That’s a relief!
Rimi: Yeah. Thanks for the camera, Kasumi-chan. Let’s see... for this scene, the camera angle should be~...
Kasumi: (...Wait. If she put down salt, does that mean there might’ve been ghosts here before...?)
Rimi: Okay, that’s perfect! Kasumi-chan, can you also prep the sound effects from the script? ...Kasumi-chan?
Kasumi: Ah, yep! The sound effect for this scene is~... this one!
Tae: The one with azuki beans in a bottle! I love that one. When you tilt it, it sounds like waves.
Saya: You’ve been using that sound a lot—what’s it for?
Rimi: Actually, the ghost girl who had the accident comes from a seaside town. So whenever she appears, I always add that sound!
Saya: Oh, I see!
Rimi: Even in regular movies, there’s lots of subtle details like that.
Rimi: With BGM, sound effects, lighting, and color grading—if you pay attention, it’s really fun to notice!
Saya: Wow~... I don’t think I’ve ever watched movies that closely.
Arisa: Me neither. Like they say, “love makes you better.” That’s totally you, Rimi.
Kasumi: Yeah yeah! Rimi-rin sparkles when she’s filming!
Rimi: Ehehe... Alright, let’s get back to shooting. O-Tae-chan, Saya-chan, please get ready!
(Black cut)
Tae: Hah... hah... I... I can’t do this anymore...! She’s angry, I know it!
Saya: L-let’s head to the studio! It should still be open—we can get help!
Tae: Y-yeah!
(Black cut)
(Background change)
