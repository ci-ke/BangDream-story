event276-02 Chapter 1: Who’s the Lead?

An audition begins with just one comment from Rimi. And the honored lead role goes to—

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Tae Hanazono, Rimi Ushigome, Saya Yamabuki, Arisa Ichigaya)

[Ichigaya Residence - Entrance]

Tae: Kya~~~~~.
Rimi: Cut-----!!!
Rimi: O-Tae-chan, can you act a bit more scared? That realism’s pretty important, you know...!
Arisa: Rimi, why are you calling cut? This is just the audition, right?
Rimi: Ehehe... sorry, I got carried away. Watching everyone act made me feel like we were already shooting.
Tae: Should we run it again?
Rimi: No, I’ve seen everyone’s acting, so I’m ready to make my decision.
Kasumi: Eh, already!? I’m nervous~...!
Rimi: Let’s start with the lead role.
Saya: Being the lead comes with a lot of responsibility, huh?
Arisa: Please don’t pick me for acting...!
Rimi: Hmm...
Rimi: Kasumi-chan’s scream really popped and would stand out well on camera. Saya-chan did great delivering lines. Arisa-chan could be good too if she relaxed a bit more...
Rimi: But I think... The lead role goes to O-Tae-chan!
Tae: Huh, me?
Kasumi: Wow~! Congrats, O-Tae!
Arisa: Huh. Honestly, didn’t see that coming.
Tae: Me neither.
Rimi: Yeah... but since this horror movie will be just 5 minutes, I want to focus more on visual presence than acting skill.
Rimi: O-Tae-chan’s tall and stands out on screen, so she’ll probably catch the judges’ eyes!
Arisa: Wow~... not that I get it completely, but with that much passion, it sounds convincing.
Kasumi: Yeah yeah! Rimi-rin, you’re totally in director mode now!
Rimi: Ehehe, you think so?
Rimi: Oh, and for the best friend role, I’d love to have Saya-chan play it.
Rimi: The story has two main characters—the protagonist and her friend. The friend is a classmate who gently supports the main character. Actually, I was originally hoping to ask Saya-chan to play that role.
Kasumi: Wow, you got a personal request, Saya!
Saya: Fufu, in that case, I’ll do my best. Let’s work together, O-Tae.
Tae: Same here!
Arisa: You’ve already figured out a lot of the details—don’t tell me you’ve got a script written already?
Rimi: Yep! I pulled an all-nighter writing it yesterday. I’ll hand out copies now.
Arisa: A-an all-nighter!? Wait, didn’t you only mention the audition yesterday!?
Rimi: Once I started writing, I just couldn’t stop...! Here’s your copy, Arisa-chan. And this one’s for Saya-chan.
Saya: Thanks, Rimi-rin. ...Wow, it looks like a real movie script!
Rimi: Since we’re doing this, I wanted to go all in...
Kasumi: As expected of Rimi-rin, you really put your heart into it!
Arisa: Hmm... skimming through it, it’s about a guitarist in a band who gets haunted by a ghost in their studio?
Rimi: Exactly! And as it turns out, the ghost is the band’s former guitarist who recently passed away in an accident—
Kasumi: Ugh... that’s already kinda scary...
Tae: Yeah. It’s a great setup though.
Arisa: Well, having a familiar setting makes it easier to grasp... huh?
Arisa: There’s a scene that specifies using CiRCLE’s studio for filming. But we haven’t actually asked Marina-san yet, have we?
Rimi: Good catch, Arisa-chan! That’s right... we haven’t!
Arisa: Don’t say that so proudly! We’d better contact her soon. If we can’t use CiRCLE, we’ll need to find another studio.
Rimi: Yeah, I’ll contact Marina-san today.
Rimi: Also, Arisa-chan... if possible, could you be my assistant director? I could really use your help managing the shooting schedule.
Arisa: Got it. Honestly, I prefer working behind the scenes anyway...
Rimi: Wow, thank you!
Kasumi: Yes, yes! What about me, Rimi-rin!?
Rimi: I’d like you to be the filming assistant, Kasumi-chan!
Kasumi: Got it! I’m in!
Arisa: You always say yes without thinking!
Kasumi: So, Rimi-rin, what exactly does 'filming assistant' mean? Is it like doing miscellaneous tasks?
Rimi: That’s a simple way to put it. But since we’re a small crew, you’ll be doing other jobs too.
Rimi: Like handling the lights, adding sound effects during certain scenes for more impact... very important tasks.
Tae: Whoa, Kasumi, that’s a big responsibility.
Kasumi: Ehehe, it’s no big deal~.
Rimi: Fufu. Now that everyone’s roles are set...
Rimi: The horror movie team is officially rolling!
