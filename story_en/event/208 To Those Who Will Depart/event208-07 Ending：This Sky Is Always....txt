event208-07 Ending: This Sky Is Always...

With the show over, Misaki speaks candidly to the person going far away.

(Character: Kokoro Tsurumaki, Kaoru Seta, Hagumi Kitazawa, Kanon Matsubara, Misaki Okusawa)

Misaki: ... Kanon-san, Nicolina-san. Thanks for giving me the encouragement I needed.
Kanon & Nicolina: ...!
Misaki: I... I've done what I wanted to do, so... Now, I'm going to say what I want to say, too!
Misaki: Kaoru-san!
Kaoru: Ah, Misaki. How may I be of service? You seem like there is something you wish to say.
Kanon: (You can do it, Misaki-chan.)
Misaki: ... I don't like that you're studying abroad.
Kaoru: Huh...? Misaki, what do-
Misaki: But if that's what you want to do, then I can't stop you. I'm sure you thought over it a lot before deciding, after all.
Misaki: Still, though...
Misaki: Even if we may be far away from each other, we're still Hello, Happy World!. Though we'll be separated, our hearts are one.
Misaki: So please, don't forget about us.
Kaoru: Misaki...! You thought of me that dearly...!
Kaoru: I am the same way. Just the thought of being unable to see all of you is enough to tear my heart asunder.
Kaoru: But even so, there are greater heights to which I aspire as an actress.
Kaoru: So, even though it may be for an entire week that we shan't be able to see each other's faces, I swear to you that I shall return more fleeting than ever before...!
Misaki: Kaoru-san- Hm?
Misaki: ... A week?
Misaki: Wait, hold on, Kaoru-san. Did you just say a week?
Kokoro: We feel the same way, Kaoru! Even if we're apart, we'll still be together in spirit!
Hagumi: Yeah! Good luck overseas, Kaoru-kun!
Kaoru: My thanks, everyone...! I am blessed with the greatest of friends...!
Misaki: W-wait, wait... You all... knew...?
Kanon: M-Misaki-chan... Did... you not ask how long her program lasts...?
Kaoru: Ahh, I will take all of your thoughts and carry them close to my heart as I travel across the sea into the distant beyond...!
Misaki: ...
Misaki: ... You should've told me that sooner~...
(Black cut)

Misaki: It's only a week for you, though~.
