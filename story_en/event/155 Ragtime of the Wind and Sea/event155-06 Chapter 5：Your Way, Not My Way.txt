event155-06 Chapter 5: Your Way, Not My Way

The sun is starting to set when the group is about to give up looking for the sunflower field. However...

(Character: Rimi Ushigome, Saya Yamabuki, Moca Aoba, Kokoro Tsurumaki, Sayo Hikawa)

[Hillside]

Rimi: Hmm... I don't see a sunflower field...
Saya: We've found most of the places from the photo book, and now we're at the tree that's in the picture with the sunflower field. It has to be close...
Sayo: We've walked around for a while now... but it just feels like we're going in circles...
Saya: I guess... we didn't think this through enough after all...
Moca: ...
Rimi: We should... probably begin heading back... It's, um, almost time for us to catch our train...
Sayo: ... When the sun starts setting, it'll get dark, and we might not be able to find our way back... I'm sorry I wasn't much help, Yamabuki-san.
Saya: What? Please don't worry about this! It was really fun having you come with us this far. I've been having a great time!
Moca: ... Um...
Saya: Yeah, Moca?
Moca: ... Hmm...
Sayo: ... So, should we start leaving?
Kokoro: Ah! A seagull!
Moca: K-Kokoro-chan?
Kokoro: Let's go, Moca!
Moca: Wha- Ahhh!
Saya: H-hey, you two! Where are you going?!
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Forest]

Moca: Huff... Huff... Kokoro-chan, you're too fast~.
Kokoro: Mmm~, something's not right. Where'd that seagull fly off to?
Moca: Hey, why are you chasing after a seagull anyway?
Kokoro: There were seagulls in Saya's photo book, right?
Kokoro: So I was thinking the seagulls might know where the sunflower field is.
Moca: Ah, now that you mention it... there might've been seagulls in the picture with the sunflower field...
Moca: Wherever the seagulls are, the sunflower field could be there too... Hmm~, even I didn't think of that.
Moca: But we're by the sea. Seagulls could be in places other than near the sunflower field...
Moca: ...
Kokoro: ...? Why are you quiet all of the sudden?
Moca: You know what, forget what I just said. A lot of the clues we've found so far are things I never would've noticed on my own.
Moca: So I'm just gonna trust your instincts.
Kokoro: Really?! Thanks, Moca!
Kokoro: Ah, look above us! There's the seagull! Let's follow it!
Moca: Chasing after a seagull, huh~? This is a first for me~.
Kokoro: Ahh~, we lost it again! Where did you go, Seagull?
Moca: It's flying, so it could've gone anywhere~.
Kokoro: Mmm~... Ah, I know!
Moca: What?
Kokoro: Hey, Moca. How would you chase after a seagull?
Moca: Huh? No one's ever asked me that before...
Kokoro: It's just like you said earlier.
Kokoro: You said you wouldn't have been able to find some of the clues we've found today all by yourself.
Kokoro: When I chase after the seagull, it just wants to play hide-and-seek.
Kokoro: That's why if we do it your way, we might be able to become friends with the seagull.
Kokoro: This time I'll trust in you, Moca!
Moca: Oh, I get it~. If it were me, well, I guess I'd just do nothing.
Moca: I've got my way of doing things, and you've got yours.
Moca: And I think seagulls have their own way of doing things too. The seagull is flying around how it wants, so we could wait for it to show up again.
Kokoro: We could wait...! That's amazing, Moca! I never would've thought of that plan!
Moca: Something tells me you're not too good at waiting~.
Kokoro: Alright! We're gonna try your plan!
(Black cut)
Kokoro: ...
Kokoro: I think we've waited pretty long. We should see it again soon, right?
Moca: Kokoro-chan, it hasn't even been thirty seconds yet~.
Moca: Let's wait just a bit longer. We can relax and enjoy looking at the sea.
Kokoro: Right. We'll wait and relax like you would.
Moca & Kokoro: ...
Kokoro: ... Ah, the seagull! I saw it poke its head out from that branch!
Moca: Wait just a little longer... There, it flew off~.
Moca: It went that way. Let's go~.
Kokoro: Gotcha!
(Black cut)
Moca: ... Hmm~? This smell of the sea in the air, the sound of waves... Are we...
Moca & Kokoro: Ah...!
(White cut)
