event155-04 Chapter 3: Beyond the Old Train Tracks

The group discovers a long-forgotten station in the woods. They spot an old tunnel at the end of the tracks.

(Character: Rimi Ushigome, Saya Yamabuki, Moca Aoba, Kokoro Tsurumaki, Sayo Hikawa)

[Forest]

Saya: Wow... This place is amazing...! There's an abandoned train station in the middle of the forest...
Rimi: It's like time stopped here.
Sayo: Sunlight sprinkles down through the trees on a long-forgotten station deep in the forest... This is a very mysterious place...
Moca: Hey, Saya, wasn't this station in the photo book?
Saya: You know... it was! There was a photograph of this station! Good eye, Moca!
Moca: Ehehe~. Lay that praise on thick~.
Rimi: ... Could that mean the field of sunflowers is near here?
Kokoro: Mmm~! I'm getting excited!
Kokoro: Huh? Hey, Moca, there's a hole through those bushes up ahead of the tracks. I wonder what's on the other side!
Moca: Oh~. Yes, ma'am, we've got ourselves one suspicious-looking tunnel~.
Sayo: The bricks that make up the outside of the tunnel are hidden by ivy... It looks like these tracks haven't been used for a very long time. I wonder where they lead...
Rimi: Hm? I feel like I've seen that tunnel before... Now I remember. It was in a horror movie I saw!
Moca: For real?
Rimi: Yeah. Fans of the movie really wanted to know where this was. I'd been curious for a long time too, since this place has such a unique feel. So this is where it was~.
Saya: I don't know if I'm brave enough to go in.
Moca: Yeah~. It's dark in there, and there's no telling what it's like inside~...
Rimi: Ah, um, it would take us out of the way, so it's okay if we don't go inside. I'm fine with just seeing the tunnel from here.
Sayo: Yes, let's keep moving. There seems to be a path right next to the station...
Kokoro: Everyone~! I think there's something amazing down this way! It's really cool in here too! Hurry up!
Moca: Huh? Did I just hear Kokoro-chan's voice come from inside the tunnel~?
Saya: Wh-when did she go in the tunnel?
Rimi: K-Kokoro-chan~!
Sayo: ... No reply. She might have gone deeper into the tunnel.
Saya: Y-yeah... Alright, I guess we're going after her.
Sayo: ... Yes, let's go.
Saya: Ugh... We're really going in there, huh...?
Rimi: I-I suppose it's dark and a little scary-looking, isn't it...?
Saya: Rimi-rin, you say that, but you kind of want to go in, don't you...?
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Inside the Tunnel]

Kokoro: Guys! This way~!
Saya: Argh~. Kokoro, you shouldn't have gone in here without saying something.
Sayo: ... It's a good thing we found you so quickly.
Rimi: ...! Ah, wait! This is...!
Saya: Wh-what is it, Rimi-rin?!
Rimi: Wow. The black stains on this wall...! They were in the movie too~!
Saya: O-oh. Sigh, don't scare me like that~...
Rimi: Ah, sorry. I couldn't help it...
Moca: For a fan, being here is like being on the set of the movie~. Of course she's gonna geek out~.
Saya: No wonder they picked this place. It's got the perfect look for a movie...
Saya: It's leaky in spots, and it feels kind of cold in here...
Sayo: That's probably because it's a tunnel. Sunlight doesn't come in here.
Moca: Nah, I think there's another reason why it feels cold in here.
Saya: What do you mean?
Moca: Before I went to sleep last night, I looked up some stories about this area. Some said there was a tunnel here that was haunted~.
Saya: H-haunted...?!
Sayo: D-don't talk about that while we're in here!
Kokoro: Wow! I've never met a ghost before! Come out, ghost~! Let's be friends~!
Sayo: For goodness' sake... There are no such things as ghosts...
Saya: Th-that's right... Ahaha...
Sayo: Eep...
Saya: Wh-what's wrong, Sayo-senpai?!
Sayo: J-just now, it felt like something soft and warm grazed my neck...!
Moca: Ah, that was my bun.
Sayo: Why on earth are you eating bread while you're walking through a tunnel?!
Moca: Well~, eating buns isn't limited by a time or place. I've always got buns on me. That's just how I roll.
Rimi: Moca-chan, what was the rest of that story...?
Rimi: There are horror movies about haunted tunnels... Maybe they're based on this place...
Saya: R-Rimi-rin, why are you so interested in things like that~...?
Moca: It's cool. Just relax and listen to my tale~.
Moca: Once there was a person who always carried a rolling pin. They left the world with regret, so now they walk this tunnel. You can still hear their rolling pin dragging behind them...
Saya: A ghost that drags a rolling pin... Is that story...?
Sayo: It sounds like something Aoba-san made up. Seriously...
Saya: Kyaaa~!
Rimi: That was the sound of something rattling just now, wasn't it...?
Moca: Huh? Really...?
Kokoro: You finally came out! Hi there~, ghost!
Saya: D-don't call it to us! Let's just get out of this tunnel already...!
Sayo: C-calm down! It was just the sound of some rocks outside the tunnel!
Moca: Huh? Where's my bun...?
Rimi: S-Saya-chan, relax~...!
Sayo: C-come on! Let's get to the exit quickly!
(White cut)
