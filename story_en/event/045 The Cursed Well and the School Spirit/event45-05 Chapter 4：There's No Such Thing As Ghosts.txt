event45-05 Chapter 4: There's No Such Thing As Ghosts

Having met up with Kaoru and Hina, Ran and Lisa notice that Maya is taking too long!

(Character: Ran Mitake, Kaoru Seta, Hina Hikawa, Lisa Imai)

[Haneoka Girls' Academy - Roof]

Ran: Maybe she went to check it out by herself...

Lisa: Y-yeah! That's impossible! Impossible!
Lisa: ... Right? Let me try calling her!

Hina: Ahaha, I'm just kidding~!
Hina: This is Maya-chan we're talking about. She probably just got distracted cleaning.
Lisa: I hope so...
Hina: Come on~. If you're so worried, why don't we just go back to the well?
Lisa & Ran: Huh?!
Hina: You'll calm down once you see there's nothing there, right?
Hina: Alright, next stop: the cursed well! Let's go~♪
Ran: W-wait, Hina-san...!

Kaoru: Then that leaves us no choice! Follow her...!
