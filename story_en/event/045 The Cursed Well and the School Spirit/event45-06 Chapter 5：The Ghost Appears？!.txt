event45-06 Chapter 5: The Ghost Appears?!

Kaoru and the others head to the well in search of Maya, but will they be able to overcome their fear...?

(Character: Ran Mitake, Kaoru Seta, Hina Hikawa, Maya Yamato, Lisa Imai)

[Haneoka Girls' Academy - Well]

Kaoru: It appears that no one is here.
Lisa: O-okay, then what about the inside of the well?
Kaoru: I don't see anything peculiar from here. Well then, there you have it. We have nothing to worry about.
Ran: W-wait, we need to actually check inside...
Kaoru: Whatever for? If there were anyone inside, we would surely see their protruding figure.
Kaoru: Isn't that right, Lisa?
Lisa: Sorry, but I'm not taking one step closer to that well!
Kaoru: Huh...?
Lisa: I can't do this! I can't look inside the well! Hina, Kaoru! You have to go check it out! Please...!
Kaoru: Huh...?!
Hina: I went and looked inside last time. Kaoru-kun, you go.
Kaoru: What are you saying, Hina...?
Kaoru: All you have to do is go over there and look inside, correct? It's as simple as taking in the scent of a beautiful flower.
Hina: Yup, so it shouldn't be a problem for you, right?
Ran: There's no way I'm going to. It's up to you.
Lisa: Please, Kaoru~! I'll give you cookies later!
Kaoru: ...!
Kaoru: ... Oh, what's this? How can flowers bloom here, with so little sunlight to aid them? Little blossoms, I admire your strength...
Ran: What do flowers have to do with anything...?
Lisa: Yeah, they're very strong. Come on, Kaoru. To the well...
Kaoru: Of course~, worry not. I understand your concerns about the well.
Kaoru: However, do you not hear a soft chirping amongst the trees? I can't help but wonder what bird could make such a lovely sound...
Hina: I'm pretty sure it's a sparrow.
Kaoru: Ah, yes, indeed it is...
Ran & Lisa: ...
Kaoru: ... I hear you loud and clear. For you, my precious little kittens, I shall take a peek.
Hina: ... Kaoru-kun's sure taking a long time.
Ran: I've never seen someone take such tiny steps before...
Lisa: Hurry up, Kaoru~!
Kaoru: There's no need to panic. The well isn't going anywhere.
Lisa: But I don't want to be here anymore~! Something's gonna pop out of there, I just know it!
Kaoru: That's absurd, nothing's going to-
？？？ (Maya): There you are...!
Kaoru, Lisa & Ran: ...!!
Lisa & Ran: Waaah~!!!
Ran: It's here!!
Lisa: Go away! Go away!!
Maya: W-wait! It's me!
Lisa: ... Huh? Maya?
Ran: I thought you were the ghost...
Maya: Hey, that's not very nice~! I'm still alive!
Ran: I mean, you kinda look like you drowned...
Hina: She's right. What happened? Did you spill something?
Maya: Yes. I tripped over a bucket of water while I was helping out the Theater Club...
Hina: R-really? Be careful. You might catch a cold.
Maya: Someone brought me a towel, so I think I'll be fine...
Lisa: Phew~. Don't scare me like that~...
Maya: I was just calling out to you, that's all...
Kaoru: Fufu, my little kittens scare so easily.
Ran: ...? Seta-san? Why are you all the way over there?
Kaoru: ... Ah, what's this? How strange indeed.
Hina: Weren't you just running away with Lisa-chi and Ran-chan?
Kaoru: Hahaha... Of course not. Clearly, my body moved on its own in order to protect these two. Yes, that's it.
Hina: Ah, okay. What a Kaoru-kun thing to do.
Kaoru: Hahaha...
Ran: Maya-san, you sure took your time. You didn't even pick up your phone.
Maya: Huh? You called me? Ah! I'm sorry, I didn't even notice!
Maya: There was something I wanted to look into. That's why I'm late.
Hina: What's that...?
Maya: I've solved the mystery of the cursed well...!
