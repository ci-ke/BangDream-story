event283-03 Chapter 2: [Request] Livestream [Plan]

After much discussion, Hello, Happy World! decides on a livestream. But what kind of request did they receive...?

(Character: Kokoro Tsurumaki, Kaoru Seta, Hagumi Kitazawa, Kanon Matsubara, Misaki Okusawa)

[Tsurumaki Residence - Courtyard]

Kokoro: One... two...
Kokoro, Kaoru, Hagumi, Michelle & Kanon: Hello, Happy Channel~!
Kokoro: Hey everyone watching the stream! It’s Hello, Happy World!
Hagumi: This time, we’re doing a request stream where we answer the requests you all sent in!
Kaoru: Let us wholeheartedly fulfill our little kittens’ requests...!
Kanon: Yeah, we’ll all do our best...!
Michelle (Misaki): Thanks to everyone who sent in requests ahead of time~ We printed them all out and put them in this box~.
Michelle (Misaki): This one's what everyone in the band was asked to do~ And these are requests for each member~!
Michelle (Misaki): We’ll still be taking requests, so if there’s anything you want us to do, be sure to leave a comment~.
Kokoro: Alright then, let’s get started right away!
Kaoru: Then allow me the honor of going first. The request box for me is... this one.
Kaoru: “To Kaoru-san, lately I haven’t been sleeping well at night. I think I could sleep soundly if you sang a lullaby for me.”
Hagumi: Whoa, Kaoru-kun’s lullaby! What do you think, Kaoru-kun?
Kaoru: Fufu, to the little kitten who can’t sleep, I shall deliver the sweetest slumber. Now, little kittens, sleep in my arms.
Kaoru: Sleeep, sleeeep, my dearest little ones~♪
Michelle (Misaki): Wow... I bet Rimi and Uehara-san are freaking out right now.
Kanon: Fufu, I hope Kaoru-san’s fans are enjoying this.
Kaoru: ...Oh dear, still awake? What a naughty little kitten.
Kaoru: Then let me tell you a bedtime story.
Michelle (Misaki): Huh? Bedtime story? What’s that?
Kaoru: This is a tale from a world far from here—
Michelle (Misaki): Wait, Kaoru-san, are you going to perform something now!?
Hagumi: Ah, Kaoru-kun drew another request from the request box!
Kaoru: “I’d love to see the great Kaoru-sama in a fantasy world. If possible, I’d like to see you play a hero.” ...I see. In that case—
Kaoru: “I am the legendary hero! I shall save those in need...!”
Hagumi: Whoa! Kaoru-kun, that’s so cool~! Hey, hey, I want to join in too!
Michelle (Misaki): Huh?
Hagumi: So I just pick one from this box, right? Here I go!
Michelle (Misaki): No no, that’s not how this works...!
Hagumi: Let’s see... “I want to see a cool Hagumi-chan”?
Hagumi: Cool... what does that even look like?
Kokoro: Hagumi, if Kaoru’s the hero, how about being the demon lord?
Hagumi: That’s a great idea, Kokoron! Alright then... “Fuhahaha~! I am the demon lord~!”
Kaoru: “What an adorable yet fearsome demon lord. But as the hero, I cannot back down...!”
Hagumi: Hey, hey, Kano-chan-senpai, you should pick one too!
Kanon: Eh... m-me? But I don’t know if I can do it well...
Hagumi: It’s okay! It’ll be fun, promise!
Kanon: Th-then... here I go!
Kanon: ...“Kanon-chan, you look like a princess, so I want you to play one”?
Michelle (Misaki): Eh~, another one of those kinds of requests~!? Why~!?
Kaoru: Fufu, as the great Shakespeare once said, all the world’s a stage.... and so on.
Michelle (Misaki): No way that’s what he meant~.
Michelle (Misaki): Still~, with a hero, demon lord, and princess, that means~...
Hagumi: “Fuhahahaha~! The adorable princess is mine now, claimed by the demon lord~!”
Kanon: “F-fuee...! H-help me...!”
Michelle (Misaki): Well~, I figured this is how it’d go~.
Kaoru: “Princess! Ah, I shall surely rescue you...!”
Kokoro: Fufu, looks like fun! Michelle, let’s draw one too!
Michelle (Misaki): Eh~? Us too~? Well, it’s already chaos anyway, so sure~...
Kokoro: Here I go! Here’s mine! “I want everyone to stay best friends forever”!
Michelle (Misaki): Um, let’s see~... “I still want everyone to play in a band together.”
Michelle (Misaki): Ah, looks like a request for all of us got mixed in~.
Kaoru: Fufu, I see... “Demon lord! Why commit such wicked deeds!?”
Hagumi: “U-um, the demon lord... the demon lord... just wanted friends!”
Hagumi: “So I kidnapped the princess in hopes of becoming friends with her...”
Kanon: “...You don’t need to do something like that to make friends.”
Hagumi: “Eh...? Then how...?”
Kokoro: “You just need to play in a band together!”
Kaoru: “Yes, playing in a band unites our hearts, and fills the world with wonderful music.”
Hagumi: “...! Okay! The demon lord will join the band!”
Kaoru: “...And thus, the hero, demon lord, and princess formed a band.”
Kaoru: “Of course, joining them were a girl as bright as the sun and a gentle-hearted bear.”
Michelle (Misaki): Oh, we’re included too~.
Kaoru: “Their band’s music was so joyful, it brought smiles to everyone in the land.”
Kokoro: Since we’ve come this far, let’s do a live show! Let’s stream our concert to everyone!
Kaoru: “And so, the band members lived happily ever after.”
Kaoru: “...So fleeting, so fleeting.”
