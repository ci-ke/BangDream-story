event283-05 Chapter 4: A Grand Stage Just for You

Among the comments sent to Hello, Happy World!—one of them was from “her”.

(Character: Kokoro Tsurumaki, Kaoru Seta, Hagumi Kitazawa, Kanon Matsubara, Misaki Okusawa)

[The Next Day]

[Tsurumaki Residence]

Kaoru: Misaki! Is that message true?
Kaoru: You said she left a comment...
Misaki: Yeah, I thought it'd be better to wait until we were all together before saying anything...
Misaki: Look, the name on this comment—it's the same as the girl from the letter. I'll read it out loud.
Misaki: “Hello, thank you for the request stream. I'm so happy to see Kaoru-san and everyone having so much fun.”
Hagumi: Wow! She watched Hello, Happy Channel too!
Kanon: That means we know what kind of request she'd want, right?
Misaki: Well, about that... it's not exactly a request, more like...
Misaki: “I tried to think of something to request, but I couldn’t come up with anything, so I didn’t write anything.”
Misaki: “But then I realized, there’s just one thing I’d like to ask.”
Misaki: “Kaoru-san.”
Misaki: “I want to see you perform on a big stage.”
Misaki: ...That’s what she wrote.
Kaoru: Yeah, her feelings definitely reached me.
Kaoru: Then let’s make sure we live up to her hopes.
Hagumi: Hey, what if we stream Kaoru-kun performing on a big stage for her to see?
Kokoro: Yeah, I’m sure it’ll make her smile!
Kanon: Ah... but Kaoru-san’s last stage just ended, and it’ll be a while before the next one.
Hagumi: Oh, right...
Kokoro: Then why don’t we just prepare a stage ourselves?
Misaki: Wait, us?
Kanon: So like... you mean we’d rent a venue ourselves?
Kokoro: Yeah, that could work, but I feel like there might be an even more wonderful way...
Kokoro: I’ve got it!
Kokoro: I just thought of a much, much bigger stage! Come on, everyone—let’s go!
Kaoru: Kokoro... what exactly do you mean...?
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Misaki: Sure, she said she wanted to see Kaoru on a big stage, but...
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Misaki: She definitely didn’t say she wanted her under the northern lights!
Kanon: Ahaha... I was surprised enough when we got dragged onto Kokoro-chan’s private jet, but when she said “Let’s stream under the aurora,” I was even more shocked.
Hagumi: Yeah yeah! We just zoomed here through the skies!
Kaoru: Ah, what a thrilling experience it was...
Kokoro: Well, she said she wanted to see you on a big stage, right? So unless it's this big, she won’t be happy!
Kaoru: True, that snowy field we saw earlier was huge... but there are other wide-open places too. Why this one?
Kokoro: Oh, Kaoru, you’re the one who said it! That the aurora was like a stage curtain!
Kokoro: So then, under a real aurora, it’s bound to be the perfect stage!
Misaki: Wait wait, Kokoro, that was just a metaphor in a story...
Kaoru: ...Fufu.
Kaoru: That truly is a beautiful, fleeting, and grand stage.
Kaoru: Thank you, Kokoro. I’m sure our little kitten will be delighted too.
Misaki: Kaoru-san...
Hagumi: At night, we’ll be able to see the aurora! Isn’t that exciting, Kano-chan-senpai?
Kanon: Yeah, it is. One of the People in Black said the sun’s activity has been strong lately, so you can even see the aurora from Japan now...
Kanon: But whether we actually see it depends on luck.
Kaoru: It’s fine. If we believe, it’ll happen.
Kanon: Yeah, you’re right.
Hagumi: If we all say “Come on out~!” together, I bet the aurora will show up for sure!
Kokoro: That’s right! Let’s make the wish together—not just us, but everyone watching the stream too!
Misaki: Will that really work? Well, I guess we’ll find out when night falls.
Misaki: We’ve still got a bit of time before then. Maybe we should all rest up in the rooms for a bit.
Misaki: I want to get everything ready for filming too.
Kaoru: Yeah, good idea. Let’s meet up again later.
