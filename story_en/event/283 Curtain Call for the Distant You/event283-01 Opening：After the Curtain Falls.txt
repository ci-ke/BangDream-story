event283-01 Opening: After the Curtain Falls

After finishing her stage performance, Kaoru receives a letter—sent by someone unexpected...

(Character: Kokoro Tsurumaki, Kaoru Seta, Hagumi Kitazawa, Kanon Matsubara, Misaki Okusawa)

[Tsurumaki Residence]

Hagumi: One... two...
Kokoro, Hagumi, Kanon & Misaki: Kaoru, great job on the performance~! Kaoru-kun, great job on the performance~! Kaoru-san, great job on the performance~!
Kaoru: Thanks, everyone...! It’s thanks to all your support that we made it through the final show.
Kaoru: And on top of that, you even prepared this wonderful party... ah, how fleeting...!
Misaki: Jeez, you’re so dramatic~.
Kanon: We should be the ones thanking you. You gave us such a lovely show.
Kokoro: That’s right! Especially the final scene—it was so beautiful!
Kanon: You mean the one with the legendary aurora appearing? I was really moved.
Hagumi: Me too! It felt just like I was really in that faraway northern country from the story!
Misaki: Even though it was a fantasy set in another world, I felt like I was really there.
Kaoru: Fufu, that scene is one of my favorites too.
Kaoru: It’s said the story was inspired by how the stage curtain resembled the aurora.
Kaoru: From there, through the crew’s stagecraft and the actors’ performances, the aurora was brought to life upon the stage.
Misaki: Wow... people who make stage productions really are amazing.
Kaoru: It’s true that creating something can be difficult. But if it brings a smile to the audience, then perhaps it's a fleeting challenge worth taking on.
Kanon: Fufu, that’s so like you, Kaoru-san. The other audience members looked like they had a great time too.
Hagumi: Yeah yeah! Oh, and look at this! These are letters from fans for you, Kaoru-kun!
Misaki: You were already getting a crazy amount, but now it feels like there’s even more...
Kaoru: Fufu, ever since starting college and joining different theater groups, I’ve had more chances to meet little kittens like them.
Hagumi: Everyone must already be excited for your next performance!
Kokoro: Yeah, I wonder what kind of Kaoru we’ll get to see next!
Kanon: We’re excited too. We’ll definitely go watch again.
Kaoru: Fufu, thank you. Actually—
Kaoru: ...Oh? This letter is...?
Kanon: What’s the matter with that one...?
Kaoru: Ah, this envelope—it’s the kind a little kitten who used to write often always used.
Kaoru: She mentioned in a past letter that she was in poor health, so I’d been a little worried.
Kaoru: When I saw this, I just had to pick it up. The name on it really is hers.
Hagumi: Oh wow! So she wrote to you again?
Kaoru: Yes. But throughout this performance’s curtain calls, I never saw her in the audience.
Misaki: Wait, you actually checked every time? That’s so you, Kaoru-san...
Kanon: Even so, she still sent you a letter. She must’ve really wanted to say something to you.
Kaoru: You're right... Let me read it for a moment.
(Black cut)
Kaoru: ............
Misaki: So? What did it say...?
Kaoru: As I suspected, she wasn’t able to attend the performance.
Kaoru: She wrote that she asked someone to deliver the letter on her behalf.
Kaoru: She’s apparently in the hospital now due to poor health.
Kanon: I see...
Kaoru: And yet, without a single complaint, they wrote so cheerfully about our last performance and the Hello, Happy World! live.
Kaoru: And the last line was—
Kaoru: “I want to get better so I can see Kaoru-san again.”
Kanon: I see... I hope she gets well soon.
Misaki: Yeah. I hope she can see lots more of Kaoru-san.
Misaki: I’m sure that’ll help her feel better too.
Hagumi: Hey hey, do you think we can do something to help cheer her up?
Kaoru: You mean us?
Hagumi: Yeah! If we can make her smile and have fun, she’ll feel more energetic, and maybe she’ll get better faster!
Kokoro: Oh my! Hagumi, what a lovely idea!
Misaki: Hold on, it’s not that simple. I mean, I want to cheer her up too, but...
Kanon: Yeah, I agree. If there’s something we can do, I want to help.
Kaoru: Same here. I feel the same way.
Kokoro: Then it’s settled! Operation Smile and Cheer starts now!
