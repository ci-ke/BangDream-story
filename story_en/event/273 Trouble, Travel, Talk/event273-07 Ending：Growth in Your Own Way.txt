event273-07 Ending: Growth in Your Own Way

They finally make the train. But something weighs on Ako’s mind.

(Character: Yukina Minato, Sayo Hikawa, Lisa Imai, Ako Udagawa, Rinko Shirokane)

[Inside the Train]

Ako: ......
Yukina: That old woman was a local, huh. What are the odds we’d run into her in that spot?
Ako: Yeah...
Yukina: In any case, I was relying on you right up until the end. Thank you so much.
Ako: It’s nothing!
Yukina: Ako...?
Ako: Ah, sorry... But really, it’s nothing...
Ako: I mean, you were counting on me, and I just kept messing up... I’m really sorry.
Yukina: Ako...
Ako: It’s just... I’m kind of embarrassed... I’m already a second-year like you were when we first met, but I’m still so immature.
Ako: You were always so calm and grown-up, but I’m still the same kid as ever...
Yukina: ...So that’s how you felt... But, Ako, that’s not true.
Ako: Yukina-san...?
Yukina: Back then, I wasn’t mature at all. And even now, there are still so many things I can’t do.
Yukina: Even today, I wouldn’t have made it without your help... You say you messed up, but I found you really dependable.
Ako: You really think so...?
Yukina: Yes. And you’re not still a child. When that old lady needed help, you noticed before any of us and acted.
Yukina: You asked locals for directions when we got lost, and kept the mood light while we traveled.
Yukina: You even encouraged me when I was about to give up. I could really feel how much you’ve grown.
Ako: Yukina-san...
Yukina: Your drive to act is something I could never imitate. That’s what makes you so special. You should be proud of how you’ve grown in your own way.
Ako: Thank you so much! Hearing that from you means everything...
Ako: But you know, what really let me try my best today was that you relied on me, Yukina-san!
Yukina: What do you mean by that?
Ako: You’re just so cool! Even today, you were calm and composed, and that helped me stay calm too.
Ako: And because someone as cool as you was counting on me, I really wanted to do my best!
Yukina: I’m not sure I did anything that deserving of such praise... But if I was able to support you, I’m glad.
Ako: Yeah! You definitely did!
Ako: Someday, I want to become someone with your composure, plus the initiative you said was my charm—perfectly cool in every way!
Yukina: Yes. And I still have a lot to learn too, so let’s keep growing together.
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Inside the Station]

Ako: We... we’re finally here~!
Yukina: Fufu, it was quite the long journey. Now then, where are Lisa and the others...
Lisa: Yukina~~~! Ako~~~!
Ako & Yukina: Lisa-nee! Lisa.
Lisa: Thank goodness you’re okay~!
Yukina: Sorry for worrying you.
Ako: I’m sorry too~!
Rinko: But really, I’m glad nothing happened...
Sayo: Yes. It’s a relief we were able to reunite safely.
Yukina: Yeah. I feel like I can finally breathe.
Rinko: Ako-chan, that must’ve been tough... You did great...
Ako: Thanks, Rin-rin. But even though there were some rough parts, it was really fun!
Rinko: It was...?
Ako: Because I got to go on tons of adventures with Yukina-san! Oh, and something amazing happened—
Announcer: Attention please: The 3:31 PM bullet train No. 28 bound for Tokyo will soon arrive on Track 13, in a 17-car formation—
Lisa: Ah, that’s our train, right?
Rinko: Yes... Can you tell us the rest of the story on the bullet train...?
Ako: Of course!
Sayo: Alright, shall we go then?
Yukina: Looks like it wasn’t time to relax just yet. Let’s hurry so we don’t miss it again.
Ako: Right! Let’s keep our guard up until we complete the quest!
