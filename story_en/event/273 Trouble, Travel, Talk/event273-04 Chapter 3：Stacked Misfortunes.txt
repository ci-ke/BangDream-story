event273-04 Chapter 3: Stacked Misfortunes

The two escape trouble thanks to Ako’s quick thinking, but encounter new problems while trying to board a train—

(Character: Yukina Minato, Sayo Hikawa, Lisa Imai, Ako Udagawa, Rinko Shirokane)

[Station Entrance]

Ako: Phew~ We finally made it! This is the place!
Yukina: Yeah, looks like we made it just in time.
Ako: We really cut it close in the end. Let’s hurry!
Yukina: This is a problem...
Ako: What’s wrong, Yukina-san?
Yukina: I usually use my phone to get through the ticket gate, but... I don’t have it with me now.
Ako: Ah, right! You gave it to Lisa-nee... In that case, use the ticket machine over there!
Yukina: Yes, I’ll do that. ...Now, the fare to our destination is—
Announcer: An express train is arriving on platform 1 shortly—
Ako: Wah, Yukina-san! Let’s just buy the cheapest ticket and settle the fare later!

[Station - Train Platform]

Ako: This way, Yukina-san!

[Inside the Train]

Ako: Phew... I’m just glad we made it!
Yukina: ......
Ako: Yukina-san?
Yukina: Ako, I hate to say this, but... I think this train is going the wrong direction.
Ako: Huh?
Announcer: Thank you for boarding. This train will stop at—
Ako: Ehhh~~~!?
Ako: I-I’m so sorry!
Yukina: No, it’s really my fault for getting held up at the entrance.
Ako: Still... if I’d just double-checked...
Yukina: I should’ve stopped you with more confidence...
Yukina: But, what’s done is done. Now we need to focus on fixing it.
Ako: Right... Um... The train we boarded is an express, so it won’t stop for a while...
Yukina: I see...
Ako: Ah! But if we turn back at the next stop, we should still make it!
Yukina: Is that so? That’s a relief.
Ako: We’ll be a little late meeting the others... but I feel better now!
Yukina: Yes. It looks like it’s not crowded, so let’s sit down for now.
Ako: Good idea.
Yukina: Phew...
Ako: (...Huh? Now that I think about it, it’s pretty rare to be alone with Yukina-san like this...)
Yukina: Ako, is something wrong?
Ako: Ah, um... I was just thinking it’d be nice to talk about stuff while we have the chance!
Yukina: Sure, I don’t mind.
Ako: Okay then... Do you hang out with Lisa-nee a lot in college too?
Yukina: Yeah... not as often as in high school, but we still spend our breaks and lunch together often.
Ako: I thought so! What have you talked about lately?
Yukina: Usually it’s Lisa who brings up topics.
Yukina: Oh, and she got a little mad the other day because I didn’t notice her new hairstyle.
Ako: Ah, Lisa-nee told me about that too! She’s gotten even more stylish since starting college!
Yukina: She has. Honestly, I preferred high school when we had uniforms. That was easier.
Ako: Then why not let Lisa-nee plan your outfits for you?
Yukina: If I did that... she’d get way too into it, and that’s kind of scary.
Ako: Ahaha, yeah, true!
Ako: (Fufu. It’s really refreshing and fun chatting with Yukina-san like this...!)
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Inside the Station]

Lisa: Hey, Rinko, aren’t you cold? Want to borrow my cardigan?

Sayo: Eh? I still have some left, so I’m fine...
Lisa: I see... hmm...
Sayo: (...Is Imai-san maybe overflowing with the urge to take care of people...?)
