event273-02 Chapter 1: Unexpected Trouble

Ako and Yukina help an elderly lady search for something, but time ticks down toward their bus departure...

(Character: Yukina Minato, Sayo Hikawa, Lisa Imai, Ako Udagawa, Rinko Shirokane)

[Bus - Inside]

Lisa: I wonder what's keeping Yukina and Ako. They’ve been gone a while.
Rinko: Ah... I just got a message from Ako-chan...
Lisa: Really? What’s it say?
Rinko: It says she was helping an elderly woman look for her lost wallet...
Lisa: Ah, so that’s what happened. I would've helped too if I’d known.
Sayo: Knowing Udagawa-san, she probably acted before thinking to ask us.
Rinko: Fufu... Yeah, that sounds like her...
Lisa: Ako’s super good at jumping into action like that~.
Sayo: Indeed. Not many people can do that.
Rinko: Yes... It’s definitely one of Ako-chan’s best qualities...
Rinko: That reminds me, just the other day—
Lisa: Huh?
Rinko: ...The bus... is moving, right...?
Lisa: Whaaat!?
Sayo: Don’t tell me it’s already departure time!?
Lisa: W-Wait, hold on a sec!
Sayo: No, Imai-san! It’s dangerous to stand up while the bus is in motion!
Lisa: Y-Yeah, I know that, but...!
Rinko: Ahh... the bus stop’s getting further and further away...
Lisa: Ako~! Yukina~~!
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Souvenir Shop]

Ako: I’m glad we found her wallet!
Yukina: Yes, me too.
Ako: Thanks so much for helping look for it, Yukina-san!
Yukina: Oh, it was nothing. Anyway, let’s head back to the bus stop.
Ako: Okay!
(Black cut)

[Bus Stop]

Yukina: ...Huh?
Ako: This is the bus stop, right?
Ako: Ah, a message from Rin-rin.
Ako: ...Eh----!?
Yukina: What’s wrong?
Ako: Rin-rin says the bus... already left...
Yukina: Oh, I see.
Ako: W-What do we do!? We’re totally separated now, aren’t we!?
Yukina: Looks like it. In that case, let’s catch the next bus.
Ako: Right! As expected of Yukina-san! Let’s see... when’s the next one~...?
Ako: ......
Yukina: Ako, something wrong?
Ako: Y-Yukina-san, the next bus... doesn’t come until this evening...
Yukina: Evening?
Ako: If we wait for the bus, we’ll miss our bullet train home.
Ako: We’re in serious trouble now, aren’t we~...
Ako: —Ah, another message from Rin-rin.
Yukina: What does it say this time?
Ako: Um... it looks like there’s a station about a 30-minute walk from here!
Ako: If we take the train from there, we can still catch the bullet train!
Yukina: She went out of her way to look it up for us.
Ako: Phew~, that’s a relief~. Looks like we’ll make it after all.
Yukina: Yeah. I’ll send her a thank-you message too.
Yukina: ...Huh?
Ako: What is it, Yukina-san?
Yukina: Looks like I accidentally left my phone with Lisa along with my bags...
Yukina: Sorry, but from here on, I’ll have to rely on your phone for contact and navigation.
Ako: Got it. Leave it to me! I’ll guide you all the way, Yukina-san!
Yukina: Good. I’m counting on you.
Ako: You bet! All right, quest start—let’s head for our destination!
