event133-05 Chapter 4: Let's Fight

LAYER figures out what she needs to do. To ease her worries, she visits a certain someone.

(Character: Tae Hanazono, Rei Wakana, Chiyu Tamade)

[Station Entrance]

Tae: Huh? Hey, Rei~.
LAYER (Rei): Hana-chan. On your way home?
Tae: Yeah, I stayed a little late today. Hm? Were you waiting for me?
LAYER (Rei): Huh? How could you tell?
Tae: That's the face of someone who was waiting.
LAYER (Rei): Fufu. Hana-chan... A lot happens when you're in a band, huh?
Tae: Of course! Bands are full of more fun than you can imagine! There are so many feelings that you could never experience on your own.
LAYER (Rei): In that case, I've also discovered a lot of new things. Like how much of a coward I am, for starters...
LAYER (Rei): I thought I was ready to fight for what I truly believe in... but when the time came, I got scared...
Tae: Rei, did something happen with RAS? Your solo concert is coming up soon, right?
LAYER (Rei): No, we're fine. Just seeing your face has given me the boost I needed.
Tae: Oh, I never knew my face had that kind of effect on people~. I'm a living power spot.
LAYER (Rei): Fufu. If word gets out, I'll never get the chance to see you anymore.
Tae: Don't worry. I would seek you out on my own. I'd be a mobile power spot.
LAYER & Tae: Fufu.
LAYER (Rei): See you later, Hana-chan. I'm glad I could see you today.
Tae: Wait, you're already leaving?
LAYER (Rei): Uh-huh, I got the boost I wanted. There's another matter that needs my attention.
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[LAYER's Room]

LAYER (Rei): ... Here goes.
PAREO: Hello. This is PAREO, the keyboardist maid♪
LAYER (Rei): Oh, PAREO? It's LAYER. Sorry for calling out of nowhere like this.
PAREO: No problem, LAYER-san~. It is rather rare for you to call me, though.
LAYER (Rei): Well, I've got a favor to ask.
PAREO: Okay~♪ I will do anything I can to help~♪
LAYER (Rei): Tomorrow, would you mind coming thirty minutes later than usual?
PAREO: Could this be about... wanting to resolve things with CHU²-sama?
LAYER (Rei): Yeah, I have something I want to tell her. And when I do, things might get awkward again... However, that's something we both need right now.
PAREO: I understand. I will wake up CHU²-sama first thing in the morning.
LAYER (Rei): Thanks, PAREO...
PAREO: Think nothing of it. I am both CHU²-sama's keyboardist maid and a fellow member of RAS♪
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[CHU²'s Penthouse - Living Room]

CHU² (Chiyu): Morning...
LAYER (Rei): Morning, CHU².
CHU² (Chiyu): What did you want that couldn't wait until later?
LAYER (Rei): I came to fight with you, CHU².
CHU² (Chiyu): Pardon?
LAYER (Rei): Let's fight.
CHU² (Chiyu): Wait, wait. Hold on. What's this all about? LAYER, do you have a fever or something?
LAYER (Rei): A fever... Maybe I am a little heated. It's probably my passion for the band...
CHU² (Chiyu): For the band...?
LAYER (Rei): CHU², do you remember what I said when PAREO went missing? I said we should argue if necessary.
CHU² (Chiyu): Alright, then it's not necessary. Done. Remember, our solo concert is right around the corner.
LAYER (Rei): Which is why this fight needs to happen.
LAYER (Rei): You're not your usual self, and neither am I. Do you truly think things are fine the way they are now?
LAYER (Rei): You once said that you don't want a sound that's trying to fit in with everyone else, but that's exactly what you're doing right now, CHU².
CHU² (Chiyu): ... ! I...
LAYER (Rei): I think I get why you've slammed on the brakes. That's why I'm going to help you hit the gas again.
CHU² (Chiyu): You've done nothing but talk nonsense since you got here. Maybe you should direct some of that spare energy towards our music.
LAYER (Rei): This is about our music. And our band.
LAYER (Rei): CHU², I want you to know how serious I am. I want you to see the side of LAYER that won't blindly follow orders like a servant.
LAYER (Rei): So I want you to come at me with all you've got. Don't worry about my feelings. Say whatever it takes to convince me. Try to crush me.
CHU² (Chiyu): LAYER... You...
LAYER (Rei): A producer that can't fight with her fellow members has no place taking on the rest of the world. That is a band that can never be on top.
LAYER (Rei): Was the so-called greatest band you've been rambling on about just an act? Was it all talk?
CHU² (Chiyu): No!
CHU² (Chiyu): You've a lot of nerve... talking to me like that! Fine! If it's a fight you want, then it's a fight you're gonna get!
CHU² (Chiyu): I'll give you that 100% you wanted... No, I'll give you 200%! And don't start crying if it gets too heated for you!
LAYER (Rei): Of course not. I wouldn't dream of it. Fufu... Come to think of it, this might be the first argument I've ever had with someone.
CHU² (Chiyu): Y-you think this is a joke?! They'll be picking up the pieces when I'm done with you, LAYER!
LAYER (Rei): Good. That's just what I wanted.
