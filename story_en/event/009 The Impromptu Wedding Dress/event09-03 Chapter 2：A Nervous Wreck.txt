event09-03 Chapter 2: A Nervous Wreck

A doubtful Saya gets encouragement from Rimi and runs into two familiar faces on the day of the photoshoot...

(Character: Rimi Ushigome, Saya Yamabuki, Tomoe Udagawa, Ako Udagawa)

[Hazawa Coffee]

Saya: I was talking with that person we saw earlier... And I was asked to model as a newlywed.
Rimi: Saya-chan... You're gonna be a model?!
Saya: R-Rimi-rin! Keep it down!
Rimi: Ah, sorry. I just didn't expect that...
Rimi: But how did that happen?
Saya: Apparently, they're having the shoot tomorrow, but the model who was supposed to play the bride canceled last minute.
Saya: I heard what happened and talked for a bit... Before I knew it, I agreed to be the replacement.
Saya: I just thought, I can't ignore someone who I see regularly downtown and at my store, so I said I'd help, but…
Saya: I don't know if I can do it~... Sigh...
Saya: I've never done any modeling, and I haven't even worn a dress before... The more I think about it, the more unsure I am...
Rimi: ... I think you'll be okay.
Saya: Huh...?
Rimi: You always have everything in order. And I think it's amazing that you'd try something you've never done, no matter what the reason is!
Rimi: With that attitude, I think you can do this modeling work, no problem!
Saya: Rimi-rin...
Saya: ... Thanks. Hearing you say that makes me feel like I really can do it.
Saya: Yeah, just a new challenge...! I told them I would, so I'll give it my best shot.
Rimi: Mhm! And I'll be cheering you on!
Saya: Oh, right. They gave me something to look that...
Rimi: A dress pamphlet? Wow, they're all so pretty.
Rimi: Which one do you want to try on?
Saya: Hmm... Wait, we just had this conversation.
Rimi: Ah, yeah. I just got carried away...
Rimi: But I wonder what you'll have to wear tomorrow.
Saya: Hmm, I was told that the person from the wedding venue would choose, so we won't know until then.
Rimi: Oh, okay. So it'll be like a surprise. How fun.
Saya: Fun... Yeah. I think it makes my heart race at the very least.
Rimi: Saya-chan, you can do it!
Saya: Ahaha, I'll try... Alright, Rimi-rin. Why don't we get down to business?
Rimi: Business? What do you mean...? Ah...
Saya: Let's put all that other stuff aside. We have to focus on studying.
Rimi: Right...
Rimi: I have to do this for the band, too.
Rimi: Okay, Saya-chan. I'm ready to learn!
