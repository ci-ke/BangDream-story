event09-06 Chapter 5: An Abrupt Entrance

Tomoe and Ako are filled with confusion after seeing Saya in a wedding dress. What's their next move...?

(Character: Saya Yamabuki, Tomoe Udagawa, Kaoru Seta, Ako Udagawa)

[Wedding Hall]

Ako: Oh, Sis! Isn't that...?
Tomoe: It's definitely Saya... What is she doing wearing a dress?
Ako: Not just any dress. It's a wedding dress! Maybe she's getting married.
Tomoe: No way. She's still in high school.
Ako: But as long as you're 16, you can still get married in high school, right? Maybe once her birthday passes...?
Tomoe: Haha, no no... Wait, hold on...
Tomoe: ... She was acting weird the last time we saw her, wasn't she?
Tomoe: Could this be what they call "pre-wedding jitters"...?
Ako: Wah~!! Sis! Look!!
Tomoe: What now?!
Ako: That person there...
(Black cut)
Kaoru: Saya-chan, break time's almost over.
Saya: Thanks for coming all the way out here to get me. We can go back inside.
Kaoru: That won't be necessary, for the next shots will take place outside. Would you allow me to be your escort?
Saya: Okay. Please lead.
(Black cut)
Tomoe: The person next to Saya is... Seta-senpai?!
Ako: Huh? What? Saya-chan and Kaoru are a couple...? Are they gonna get married...?
Tomoe: N-no, that can't be right... What is going on here...?
Ako: I-I have no idea! Uhh, uhm, this calls for...
Tomoe: (Saya and Seta-senpai getting married? No no no, if I just take a second to think, there has to be another explanation.)
Tomoe: ... Calm down, Ako. We both just need to try and keep cool.
Ako: O-okay...
Tomoe: We can't just sit here guessing... The only way to know what's really happening is to ask!
Tomoe: Let's check it out, Ako!
Ako: Yeah!
(Black cut)
Kaoru: Saya-chan, your hands are exquisite.
Saya: Not really.
Saya: The drumsticks give me blisters, and I help out a lot at home, so the skin gets rough sometimes, too...
Kaoru: That's why they're beautiful. In the words of the great bard, "He jests at scars that never felt a wound"... This is... exactly what he meant.
Saya: Fufu. Kaoru-san, you're so interesting.
Ako: S-Sis, Kaoru and Saya-chan look like they're having such a good time!
Tomoe: ... This is no good, looks like we're gonna have to jump in.
Tomoe: Hold everything!!
Saya: T-Tomoe?! And Ako...?
Tomoe: Saya, what is the meaning of this...? Is there something going on between you and Seta-senpai...?!
Saya: What?!
Ako: Are you getting married?!
Saya: Married?! What are you guys talking about...?
Tomoe: We were just watching you two talking and being all friendly.
Tomoe: And this morning... You were acting strange. Is this what you were worried about...?!
Saya: (They must have the wrong idea!)
Tomoe: Saya, what's going on?! There's no way we can ignore all of this now! Can you at least explain what's happening?!
Saya: C'mon, you two. Do you guys hear yourself? There's a perfectly good expl-
Kaoru: Saya-chan, stand back.
Saya: Kaoru-san...?
Kaoru: To try and tear us apart like this cannot be forgiven, no matter who it is. Not even you two.
Saya: Wh-what are you...? Kaoru-san?!
Kaoru: Saya-chan, I won't let them lay a finger on you. You stay behind me and don't worry about a thing.
Tomoe & Ako: ...?!
Saya: This is bad... Kaoru-san has completely sunk into her role... Why is this happening~?!
