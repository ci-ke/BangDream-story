event292-02 Chapter 1: University Explorers, Go!

Hello, Happy World! visits Keiho Women’s University, with Kanon taking the lead as their mentor and guide.

(Character: Kokoro Tsurumaki, Kaoru Seta, Hagumi Kitazawa, Kanon Matsubara, Misaki Okusawa)

[Keiho Women's University]

Kanon: Everyone, welcome to Keiho Women's University!
Kanon: Today I’ll be your mentor and guide you around.
Kokoro: Yes! Lead the way, Kanon!
Hagumi: What’s a mentor?
Kaoru: It’s like someone who guides and teaches those who come after them.
Misaki: Wow... but Kaoru-san, is it okay for a college student to join an open campus?
Kaoru: The gates of learning welcome all... that’s what it means.
Misaki: I don’t get it at all... well, if it’s fine then okay.
Hagumi: Hey hey, where’s Kano-chan-senpai’s classroom? I wanna see where you study!
Kokoro: Yes! I’d love to meet Kanon’s classmates too!
Kanon: Um, my university doesn’t have fixed classrooms or classmates.
Hagumi: Eh? What do you mean?
Kanon: For each class, you go to the teacher’s classroom—kind of like how you’d move to different rooms for music or home ec.
Kanon: And not everyone takes the same classes—everyone has different schedules.
Kanon: So you end up studying with different people in each class.
Hagumi: I see! So that’s why you don’t have a set classroom or classmates!
Kokoro: That sounds... wonderful!
Kokoro: It means you get to meet all kinds of people and take classes together!
Kaoru: Fufu, exactly. Everyone chooses classes that interest them and deepen their own learning.
Kaoru: For example, even though Chisato and I are in different faculties, she sometimes takes classes outside her own.
Misaki: Whoa... picking classes sounds tough...
Kanon: Yeah, I didn’t know which to take either and agonized over it.
Kanon: Required classes are fixed, but for others, as long as you get the credits, you can choose freely...
Kanon: So I started by taking classes that seemed fun to me.
Misaki: I see, so that works too.
Kanon: Today you can try out classes yourselves, so enjoy yourselves.
Kokoro, Misaki & Hagumi: Okay~.
