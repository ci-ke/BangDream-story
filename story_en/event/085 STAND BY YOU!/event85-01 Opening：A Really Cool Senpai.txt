event85-01 Opening: A Really Cool Senpai

On their way home from shopping, Moca and Himari witness a small incident...

(Character: Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Lisa Imai)

[Recess]

[Haneoka Girls' Academy - Hallway]

Himari: Hmhmhmmm~♪
Moca: Oh-hoh~? Hii-chan, you're in a good mood today~.
Himari: Ah, Moca! Get a load of this~!
Himari: A first-year student came up to me at the school commissary! She said that she came to see our show the other day!
Moca: Ah, the one on Sunday~?
Himari: "Himari-senpai, you were so totally cool!" She really said that! "You guys have my support!"
Moca: I see~. That explains the goofy grin on your face~.
Himari: Anyone would smile after hearing that~! I mean, it's usually Ran or Tomoe who are dubbed the cool ones!
Himari: Hehehe, I'm glad to know that someone out there appreciates my good points~!
Moca: Yes, how wonderful for you~. Anyway, we should go find the others~.
Moca: I'm so hungry that... I may wind up eating Hii-chan for lunch...!
Himari: Don't you dare!
Himari: Jeez~! You're not listening to a word I say~!
Moca: That's not true. Besides, I already know about your good points~.
Himari: Really~?
Himari: Oh well, whatever. Ran is going to make a fuss if we keep her waiting, so let's go...
Himari: Huh? Wh-what was that?!
Moca: Goodness, what did you break this time, Hii-chan~?
Himari: It wasn't me~!
Himari: Ah, over there. That first-year student dropped a flower pot...!
Moca: Uh-oh, that's bad~... Ah, someone came to help.
Himari: Isn't that... Lisa-senpai?
Lisa: What happened?! Are you alright?
First-Year Student: I-I'm fine, but the flower pot isn't...
Lisa: Hahaha, yeah, it looks like a goner~.
Lisa: But hey, don't sweat it. Let's clean this up before someone steps on it.
Lisa: ... Whoa, stop right there! Don't use your hands. We'll be in a mess if you cut yourself.
First-Year Student: Ah, s-sorry...!
Lisa: I'll go grab a broom and dustpan. Wait right here.
(Black cut)
Lisa: ... That should do it☆
First-Year Student: Uhm, th-thank you very much...!
Lisa: Hahaha, you're welcome.
Lisa: Ah, if you're going to look for the teacher, let me join you. Try not to worry. I can explain everything.
Moca: ... Lisa-san sure is amazing, cleaning up a mess like that so quickly.
Himari: Sigh... Kind, reliable... Lisa-senpai is just way too cool~.
Himari: I can't help but look up to her. All I want is to become a cool senpai, just like her.
Moca: Really?
Himari: Yeah. She's the kind of mentor you can count on, the kind who can look after those that need help. I'm already in my second year, too, so it's about time I step up to the plate!
Moca: A super cool Hii-chan... Nope, I can't really imagine that~.
Himari: M-maybe your brain is busted~! Come on, I'm a lot like Lisa-senpai!
Moca: Really...? How?
Himari: Well, we're both into fashion! And we both like baking sweets!
Moca: Uh, Hii-chan... There are about a trillion people who fit that description.
Moca: And if you ask me, you're lacking a lot of the qualities that make Lisa-san who she is~.
Himari: Hmph...! Are you telling me that I can never be like her~?
Moca: I wouldn't go that far, but maybe your goal shouldn't be trying to become like her.
Moca: Hii-chan, you have plenty of good traits that are unique to only you~.
Himari: Jeez~! You think I can't do it, don't you~?!
Himari: If I put my mind to it, I can become exactly like Lisa-senpai!
Himari: You're talking to the girl who was 'totally cool' on stage. I'll show you just how exceptional I can be!
Moca: ... Bravo, bravo. I applaud your initiative~. Now, let's go find the others~.
Himari: I-I knew it~! You're not taking me seriously at all~!
