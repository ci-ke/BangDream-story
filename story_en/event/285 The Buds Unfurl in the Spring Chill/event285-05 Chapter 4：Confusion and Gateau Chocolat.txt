event285-05 Chapter 4: Confusion and Gateau Chocolat

Still unable to shake her unease, Tsugumi bumps into Tomoe by chance at the supermarket.

(Character: Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa)

Clerk: —Here’s 200 yen in change. Thank you very much.
Tsugumi: ...Alright, here we go.
Tsugumi: (Cocoa powder, powdered sugar, and dark chocolate... Yep, looks like I didn’t forget anything.)
Tsugumi: (...I hope Ran-chan likes it...)
Tomoe: —Tsugu?
Tsugumi: Tomoe-chan...! Are you out shopping?
Tomoe: Bingo. We’re having French toast for breakfast tomorrow. You shopping for dinner?
Tsugumi: Ah, no. I’m thinking of making a bittersweet gâteau au chocolat for Ran-chan.
Tomoe: That sounds awesome! With Tsugu’s homemade cake, Ran’s promotion is practically guaranteed.
Tsugumi: ...Um... I was thinking of giving it to her after the exam.
Tomoe: Ah... I see.
Tsugumi: Yeah...
Tomoe: O-oh, right... Tsugu, wanna have something warm to drink...!?
Tsugumi: Huh? S-sure...?
Tomoe: I just really had a craving for a hot latte~! And I was hoping maybe you'd come with me, Tsugu~...!
Tsugumi: Ah... fufu, yeah. I could go for a little detour myself.
Tomoe: I see, then that works out perfectly! Why don't we stop by the park?
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Tomoe: ...Sigh~, this really warms you up~.
Tsugumi: Yeah, it’s comforting...
Tsugumi: Thanks, Tomoe-chan.
Tomoe: Huh?
Tsugumi: When I’m by myself, I end up overthinking things. So... I’m glad you’re here with me.
Tomoe: ...So you're still hung up on that after all.
Tsugumi: Yeah... but I think once I bake this gâteau au chocolat, I’ll feel better.
Tsugumi: Since I couldn’t bring myself to just say “Good luck on your exam,” I made this cake to tell Ran-chan “Good job” once it’s all over.
Tsugumi: I haven’t been able to do anything yet, but at least when it’s over, I want to celebrate with her.
Tomoe: ...Tsugu, are you really okay with that?
Tsugumi: Eh...
Tomoe: I’m not trying to put down your decision. If you’re sure about it, then that’s fine.
Tomoe: But seeing you now... I don’t think even giving her that cake would really make you smile.
Tsugumi: ...You think so...?
Tomoe: Tsugu, you’re the kind of person who can say “I’m fine” and take action no matter how hard things get. This gâteau au chocolat proves it.
Tomoe: But this time... maybe you should face your feelings before you say “I’m fine.”
Tsugumi: Face my feelings...
Tomoe: Yeah. Like earlier during lunch.
Tomoe: When we started talking about Ran’s assistant instructor exam, you kind of checked out of the conversation, right? I figured something was up.
Tsugumi: ...Back then, I was just thinking how amazing Ran-chan is, working so hard on flower arrangement...
Tsugumi: ...Even though I still think that way, my feelings just... can’t keep up...
Tsugumi: Before, seeing Ran-chan being so cool always made me want to work harder too.
Tsugumi: But now... I find myself wanting to look away.
Tomoe: Tsugu...
Tsugumi: You know... back then, I kind of started to hate myself.
Tsugumi: I know how hard Ran-chan’s worked for this exam, and still I can’t support her... It made me feel like—maybe I wasn’t being a real friend.
Tsugumi: So I thought I had to go back to being my usual self. I had to do something... anything...
Tomoe: I see... I think I get it.
Tomoe: It’s because she’s so important to you. You want to cheer her on with a smile... but something just kind of churns inside your chest.
Tsugumi: Ah, yeah. That’s exactly how I’ve been feeling.
Tsugumi: Lately, every time I think “Ran-chan is amazing,” I feel... distant from her.
Tsugumi: Even though we’ve restarted the band and we’re together again... it feels weird, right?
Tomoe: No, I get it. I felt the same way when I wasn’t sure if I was being the cool big sister Ako looked up to.
Tomoe: It’s like... if I cheer her on too hard, she’ll just take off into a world I can’t follow.
Tomoe: And that thought felt... a little lonely.
Tsugumi: Ah...
Tomoe: Feeling lonely while watching someone chase their goals—even I think that’s kinda lame.
Tomoe: But still, that loneliness was how I really felt. And I’m glad I managed to realize it at the time.
Tsugumi: ...I see... So that’s it... I guess I’ve been feeling lonely...
Tomoe: Huh?
Tsugumi: Tomoe-chan... I think I was feeling lonely too...!
Tsugumi: Watching Ran-chan move forward toward her future—I felt this little ache deep in my chest, and I think that’s why...
Tsugumi: Now that I think about it, I felt the same way when we broke up the band. I had a lot on my mind, but underneath it all was just... that lonely feeling.
Tomoe: That's true. Out of all of us, Ran was the first to really start moving toward her future once we became third-years.
Tomoe: Even though we’ve always been together... it’s natural to feel a bit left behind.
Tsugumi: Yeah... Even if there’s nothing I can do about it, I still feel lonely.
Tomoe: I think it’s okay to tell Ran how you feel. Supporting her doesn’t have to be the only right answer, you know.
Tsugumi: What!? But it’s right before her big exam! Wouldn’t saying something like that just make her feel awkward...?
Tomoe: Is that so? Wouldn’t it actually trouble Ran more if you kept those feelings to yourself?
Tomoe: If it were me, I’d rather hear an honest “I’m lonely” than a fake “You’ve got this.” That’d mean a lot more.
Tsugumi: ......
Tsugumi: (Maybe this gâteau au chocolat was just a way for me to run away.)
Tsugumi: (Maybe I wanted to make it so I’d have an excuse for not facing how I really feel—or for not facing Ran-chan.)
Tsugumi: (Giving this to her doesn’t magically mean we’ll be able to smile and talk like nothing happened.)
Tsugumi: ...I’ll tell her how I feel properly, with words. I’ve decided not to give her the gâteau au chocolat.
Tomoe: I see. Good luck!
Tsugumi: Thanks, Tomoe-chan.
Tomoe: Thanks for tagging along with my little detour. It’s gotten pretty chilly—let’s head home!
Tsugumi: Yeah! ...Ah! But then the ingredients I bought will go to waste...!
Tomoe: Ah~, you’re right!
Tomoe: Alright, then let’s just bake it together and eat the whole thing ourselves!
Tsugumi: What!? That’s a lot, you know!
Tomoe: No worries, no worries! You just gotta power through with pure spirit!
Tsugumi: Fufu... if you say so.
Tomoe: Ain't that right? Ahahaha!
