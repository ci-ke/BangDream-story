event285-01 Opening: The Assistant Instructor Exam

Ran prepares for her upcoming flower arrangement exam. Tsugumi, watching her, begins to feel something...

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa)

(Background change)

Tsugumi: Oh, those ones wilted already? They lasted a pretty long time.
Tsugumi's Mother: Yeah, and they looked great with the driftwood vase too.
Tsugumi: Okay, then I’ll put the vase away until we get new flowers.

Ran: Um... On the day, they give you flowers and a vase to work with, and then my dad—the headmaster—and other judges evaluate what you make.
Tsugumi: So you don’t know what you’ll be working with until the day of.
Ran: Right. That’s why I’ve been trying to come up with ways to express myself no matter what I’m given.
Moca: Training at home and visualizing at school, huh~.

Ran: No, there are still more ranking tests after that.
Tomoe: Huh, really?
Ran: Yeah. In our style, once you're promoted from assistant instructor to full instructor, that's when you're officially allowed to start teaching.
Ran: But even after that, there are still several higher-level promotion tests... but those are probably years away for me.
Tsugumi: Wow...! So it’s not like getting a certification where you’re done after one test.
Moca: I see~, so when you finally take over for your dad, we’ll be a grandma band or something~.
Ran: Maybe so.
Himari: Ah~~~!! I totally forgot—I was supposed to help get the materials ready for the next class in a different room!
Tomoe: Himari... Don’t yell out of nowhere. You scared me.
Himari: Sorry! Hey, can someone help me out? I’m begging you—just this once!
Moca: Hii-chan, you say “just this once” way too often. It’s lost all its power~.
Himari: Maybe so... but please!! Even just one or two of you!
Tomoe: Jeez, fine. Rock-paper-scissors, let’s go.
Tsugumi: Okay, ready? Rock, paper...
All: Shoot!
Moca & Tomoe: Aahh~~~...!!
Tomoe: Should’ve done my pre-game victory ritual~...!
Ran: Okay, off you go.
Himari: Thanks, Moca, Tomoe~! Okay, grab your textbooks, let’s go!
Moca: Ugh~, being the loser sucks~.
Tsugumi: Ah, no running in the hallways, okay~?
Ran: Fufu. Let’s take our time heading over.
