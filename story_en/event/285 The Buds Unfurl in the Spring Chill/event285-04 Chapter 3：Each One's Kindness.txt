After school, Moca approaches Tomoe and Himari. The topic, of course, turns to Ran and Tsugumi...

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa)

Moca: ...Oh, there you are. Hii-chan, Tomo-chin~.
Tomoe: Huh? Moca, didn’t you have work today?
Moca: Yeah, it’s still a bit early, so I’m hanging around for a bit~.
Moca: You two have club today, right~? Wanna have a little chat with Moca-chan before that~?
Himari: Wait, we don’t really have that much time.
Moca: C’mon, c’mon. So? How are our dear Mitake-kun and Hazawa-kun doing lately~?
Tomoe: ...You heard something about Ran and Tsugu too, didn’t you?
Moca: Yeah, I just heard a bit from Ran this morning~.
Tomoe: What did she say?
Moca: She said she wants to hear the real feelings Tsugu kept bottled up. She had that serious look on her face~.
Himari: I see... Tsugu told us about it yesterday too.
Himari: She said she couldn’t find the words to support Ran, and didn’t know why, which left her feeling all muddled inside.
Tomoe: She said she'd feel better after sleeping on it, but judging from lunch today...
Moca: So it really is about the flower arrangement stuff~?
Himari: Well, Tsugu said it wasn’t, but I kinda think it still plays a part.
Tomoe: Yeah. Even today, after we talked about Ran’s promotion exam, she seemed a little off.
Himari: She used to talk so excitedly about flower arrangement and flowers...
Tomoe: Yeah, she really does know a lot about things like flower meanings and seasonal blooms. She even talks with Ran about what flowers to display at the shop.
Moca: And Ran always looked happy during those chats~ I bet it meant a lot to her to share that with Tsugu~.
Tomoe: ...It’d be nice if they could smile and talk about flowers together again.
Himari: Yeah. I wish we could help Tsugu with how she’s feeling...
Moca: Still, only Tsugu can find her own true feelings~.
Tomoe: Yeah. Still, I’m glad we talked with you about it, Moca. Thanks.
Moca: Nah, it was just a little chat~.
Moca: Well, we all know what kind of sunset today brings, so it’s all good~.
Tomoe: Yeah, you’re right!
Tomoe: Next time I see Tsugu, I’ll talk with her again. Maybe it’ll help her feel a bit better.
Himari: Moca, if Ran seems down again, can you talk with her too?
Moca: You got it~.
