event285-02 Chapter 1: This Fuzzy Feeling Inside

After club activities, Tsugumi runs into Tomoe and Himari and tries to share the uneasy feelings she’s been carrying.

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa)

Tsugumi: ...Phew...
Himari: Tsugu~!
Tsugumi: Ah, Himari-chan, Tomoe-chan! Nice job at club today.
Tomoe: You too, Tsugu. Seems like you're still busy with the student council.
Tsugumi: Yeah, but I’ve pretty much wrapped everything up today.
Himari: That’s a relief. Once Ran finishes her exams successfully, we might be able to fit in more band practice~
Tomoe: Yeah, let’s go all in on practice once everyone’s free!
Himari: Yup. Until then, we’ll be cheering Ran on!
Tsugumi: Yeah...
Tsugumi: ...Hey. Speaking of support, it reminded me of something. During today’s class while we were changing rooms—
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Tsugumi: —So yeah, I really wanted to say “Good luck on the exam,” but... I just couldn’t get the words out.
Tomoe: I see... That’s kinda rare for you.
Himari: Yeah. You’re always the first one to cheer us on.
Tsugumi: I thought it was weird too. Like, why couldn’t I say something so simple?
Himari: Hmm... I mean, you were just talking about flower arrangement, right?
Tsugumi: Yeah. She said she'd just keep facing the flowers in front of her, like she always does. Cherishing those quiet moments, one at a time... And when I heard that—
Tsugumi: ...I just couldn’t stop thinking about it... how amazing Ran-chan is.
Himari & Tomoe: ......
Himari: Tsugu... do you still get a little uneasy when Ran gets all focused on flower arrangement?
Tsugumi: Huh?
Himari: Like, maybe seeing Ran like that makes you worry the band might drift apart again...?
Tsugumi: Ah, no! It’s not like that at all! I know we’re okay now. I’m not anxious about the band or anything...!
Tsugumi: Sorry...! I don’t really know how to explain it myself.
Tomoe: It’s fine. I get like that too, when there’s this foggy feeling I just can’t put into words.
Tsugumi: A foggy feeling... yeah. Maybe I’m feeling all muddled up because I don’t even understand how I really feel.
Tsugumi: But talking to you two helped clear my head a little. I think once I sleep on it, this feeling will go away.
Himari: Eh... really?
Tsugumi: Yeah. Now that I realized I’m just a little stuck in my head, I think overthinking it won’t help.
Tsugumi: So I’ll be fine tomorrow, for sure!
Tomoe: ...I see. But hey, if something’s bothering you again, tell us, okay? I’m always down to listen.
Tsugumi: Yeah, thanks. See you tomorrow!
Himari: Oh, yeah... Bye bye!
Himari: ...Tsugu said she was fine, but I wonder if she really is~.
Tomoe: Hmm... She’s super positive, so I guess she’s not the best at facing her more negative feelings~.
Himari: And she still says “I’m fine” right away, like always. It’s been her catchphrase for better or worse.
Tomoe: Well, for now, all we can do is watch over her. If anything happens, we’ll be right there to help.
Himari: Yeah... you’re right.
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[The Next Morning]

Moca: —I see~. So that’s why you’re hung up on how Tsugu seemed like she wanted to say something~.
Ran: Yeah... I was worried maybe talking about flower arrangement was a bad idea and wanted to change the subject...
Moca: Anyway, it’s kinda rare for Ran to be the one scrambling to change the topic~.
Ran: That’s...
Ran: ...All that stuff that happened with Afterglow—it all started because of my flower arrangement.
Moca: ...Ran...
Ran: Tsugumi’s still smiling like always, but I wondered if deep down she’s holding onto some feelings toward me...
Moca: Like... she might be angry at you?
Ran: ...Maybe something like that...
Moca: ...Hmm.
Moca: It’s quiz time~. Mitake-kun, what’s inside this paper bag~?
Ran: Huh? Where did that come from?
Moca: Come on, come on~. If you don’t answer, you’ll get zero points~.
Ran: ...It’s bread from Yamabuki Bakery, right?
Moca: Correct~. Now, question two... What’s inside this bread~?
Ran: Eh... then, cream bun?
Moca: Let’s break it open and see~ The answer is... a two-flavor bun with red bean paste and cream~.
Ran: Sigh... You really can’t hold a serious conversation, can you, Moca?
Moca: You just don’t get it, Mitake-kun~. Bread is like life.
Moca: You won’t know what’s inside until you break it open~.
Ran: What even is that?
Ran: But... yeah. You’re right. I won’t know unless I ask.
Ran: Even if hearing Tsugumi’s true feelings hurts, that’s something I brought on myself—and I want to face it head-on.
Ran: This time, I’ll really listen to what she has to say.
Moca: Very good~. Since you’re being such a good girl, Mitake-kun, I’ll give you half of the bread~.
Ran: Yeah yeah, thanks.
(Black cut)
(Background change)
