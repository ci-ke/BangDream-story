The five decide to try their hand at a shooting game. The prize they agreed to aim for is...?

(Character: Kaoru Seta, Aya Maruyama, Hina Hikawa, Rinko Shirokane, Rei Wakana)

[Fireworks Festival - Shooting Gallery Booth]
