event304-02 Chapter 1: The thrill before the festival

LAYER visits Rinko’s house to borrow a yukata. But she seems a little nervous...

(Character: Rinko Shirokane, Rei Wakana)

[Fireworks Festival Day]

[Shirokane Residence - Rinko's Room]

LAYER (Rei): Excuse me for intruding.
Rinko: Please, come in... Sorry, it's a bit messy...
LAYER (Rei): I don't mind at all! Are you sure it's really okay to borrow a yukata?
Rinko: Of course... I wanted you to wear one too...
Rinko: I've laid out two over there... Could you take a look...?
LAYER (Rei): Wow...! They're both so stylish and cute.
Rinko: LAYER-san, which do you prefer...?
LAYER (Rei): Um, I... I'll take whichever one you don't choose...!
Rinko: Is that so...? In that case...
Rinko: This one's a bit understated... So I think this vibrant pattern would suit you better, LAYER-san...
LAYER (Rei): Huh! Um, Shirokane-san...!
Rinko: ... Do you not like vibrant patterns...?
LAYER (Rei): No, that's not it...!
Rinko: Fufu... Then I'll have you wear this one, LAYER-san...
Rinko: As for the obi and accessories... is it alright if I choose them to match the yukata?
LAYER (Rei): Y-yes. Please do...
Rinko: Then... please wait a moment...
Rinko: I think there's a matching obi... around here somewhere...
LAYER (Rei): (I feel bad leaving everything to her. I wish I could help with something... No, wandering around would probably just be a bother.)
LAYER (Rei): (Still, Shirokane-san's room has more stuff in it than I expected.)
LAYER (Rei): (Especially this big grand piano. Having one in your room is amazing... She must still love it.)
Rinko: I've gathered the accessories, so let's get you dressed... LAYER-san...?
LAYER (Rei): ...Oh! Sorry, I was staring...!
Rinko: No... Is the piano unusual...?
LAYER (Rei): Yes. This is the first time I've seen a grand piano in someone's home.
LAYER (Rei): There's sheet music open. Were you playing something?
Rinko: Ah... this is...
Rinko: Um... when I was getting the yukata... I found sheet music I used as a child... I was feeling nostalgic, so I played a little...
LAYER (Rei): I see. May I take a look?
Rinko: Please...
LAYER (Rei): Oh, this song...
Rinko: Do you know it too, LAYER-san...?
LAYER (Rei): Yes. I learned it at music school when I was little. I remember singing along to the teacher's grand piano.
LAYER (Rei): We went to sing at a church instead of having a recital... That's nostalgic.
Rinko: So... it's a special song for you too, LAYER-san...
LAYER (Rei): That's right. Though I haven't sung it in quite a while.
LAYER (Rei): ...I wonder if it would sound completely different from back then if I sang it now...
Rinko: Oh... in that case, I could...
LAYER (Rei): ...Hm? A message in the group chat...
LAYER (Rei): It's from Hikawa-san. “I'm about to head out”...?
LAYER (Rei): We still have time before we're supposed to meet, right?
Rinko: Y-yes... There's still an hour and a half...
Rinko: But we shouldn't keep Hina-san waiting... Let's hurry a bit... LAYER-san, could you stand over here...?
LAYER (Rei): Got it, thank you.
