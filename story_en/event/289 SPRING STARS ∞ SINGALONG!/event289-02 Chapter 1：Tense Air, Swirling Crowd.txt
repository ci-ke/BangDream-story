event289-02 Chapter 1: Tense Air, Swirling Crowd

MyGO!!!!! takes the opening slot. As nerves rise, someone comes to visit—

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa, Tomori Takamatsu, Anon Chihaya, Rana Kaname, Soyo Nagasaki, Taki Shina)

[Waiting Room]

Taki: ...She's late.
Soyo: Even if it's Anon-chan, she should be back before we go on, right? MyGO!!!!! is the first to go on, so I doubt she’d actually be late.
Taki: But she left to buy a drink and it's already been ten minutes. We still have things I want to check in “Hekitenbansou.”
Soyo: Taki-chan, we already checked that in the last practice—
Anon: I’m back~...
Tomori: Ah... Ano-chan...
Taki: Don't just say "I’m back." What were you doing?
Anon: Eh? I told you I was gonna grab a drink when I left the waiting room.
Soyo: There’s a vending machine right nearby, though.
Anon: W-well~... come on, a little walk to clear my head isn't so bad, right?
Taki: Huh? Don't tell me, you're actually nervous?
Anon: Of course I am! We’re the opening act, you know! The opening act!
Anon: Hey~, maybe we can still get them to switch the order~?
Taki: No. What are you even saying right now?
Anon: Because everyone was like, “What kind of band gets to be the first act at a fest with pros performing?” People were seriously buzzing about it~...!
Tomori: ...! I... see...
Taki: Hey, don’t say things that'll make Tomori more nervous.
Tomori: .........
Anon: By the way, Tomorin, you okay? Your face looks stiffer than usual.
Tomori: Eh...
Taki: I said quit it with those comments. Besides, it’s our first time at a big festival. It’s only natural to be nervous.
Anon: Oh come on~! Then I’m in the same boat, aren’t I!?
Tomori: U-Um... uh...
Taki: What is it, Tomori?
Tomori: I mean... it is my first big festival, but...
Tomori: Before I got to the waiting room, someone talked to me...
Taki: Who? Did they say something to you?
Tomori: They said, “Good luck”... Kasumi-san and... the other vocalists...
Taki: That’s it?
Tomori: Yeah... but they were all so... bright...
Tomori: And then...
Tomori: .........
Taki: Tomori...
Himari: Hey, MyGO!!!!! Are you all in here?
Taki: U-Uehara-senpai!? What are you doing here...!?
Moca: Not just Hii-chan—Afterglow’s all here too~.
Ran: Excuse us. Sorry to bother you right before the show.
Taki: N-No, not at all—please don’t worry about it...!
Tsugumi: We weren’t sure if it’d be a bother, but we saw you all nearby so...
Rana: Yawn~...
Soyo: Rana-chan...!?
Taki: You were literally just napping over there a second ago...!
Tomoe: Knew you were wandering off again. Glad we brought you with us.
Taki: Stray cat! I’m really sorry about our member!
Himari: Don’t worry about it! More importantly, your show’s coming up soon, right? Are you nervous?
Taki: Well, um...
Anon: I'm super nervous~!
Taki: Hey!
Tsugumi: Ahaha, big fests do make you nervous. We actually brought a little something to help you relax.
Rana: Matcha.
Tsugumi: Whoa, you startled me!
Moca: Oh, sharp nose there~.
Himari: The treat is matcha cookies from Tsugu’s place!
Tsugumi: And we brought some warm tea too. It should help you calm down.
Soyo: Wow, thank you so much. We’ll all have some.
Himari: And if you're nervous, doing a team huddle helps! It gets everyone pumped and brings you closer—highly recommended!
Anon: A team huddle, huh...
Soyo: .........
Tomoe: Don’t push yourself too hard. Just do things the way you always do, and you’ll be fine. You’ve got this!
Taki: Yes, thank you!
(Black cut)
Anon: Sigh~, that really surprised me. I never thought Afterglow would actually come here~.
Rana: Matcha is tasty.
Tomori: Yeah... it’s really good...
Anon: Even if they say “Just do what you always do,” it’s not that easy, you know~?
Taki: They were just trying to encourage us. We should accept it gratefully.
Anon: Yeah, but even you, Rikki, aren’t acting like your usual self.
Taki: Huh? How so...?
Soyo: Taki-chan, you already checked the parts you were worried about in "Hekitenbansou" during practice this morning, right?
Soyo: So why are you still obsessively checking that same part over and over?
Taki: T-That’s...!
Soyo: Since we’ve got it, I think I’ll have some tea.
Soyo: Here you go, Tomori-chan.
Tomori: Th-Thanks...
Tomori: ......It's warm.
Anon: Sigh~... yeah, I guess it is kind of relaxing~.
Anon: Wanna try doing a team huddle while we’re at it?
Soyo: Anon-chan, if you don’t mean it, maybe don’t say it.
Anon: You’re one to talk, Soyorin...
Tomori: (This tea... is good...)
Tomori: (It’s warming me up... and loosening everything inside...)
Staff: MyGO!!!!!, please head to standby!
Anon: It’s already showtime, huh~...! Well, guess we’ve got no choice now.
Anon: Alright, let’s go, everyone!
Rana: Mm.
Taki: Stop acting like you're the leader.
Tomori: Deep breath in... and out...
Tomori: Okay... let’s go...!
