event289-01 Opening: Girls’ Bands Assembled

The girls’ bands of youth gather at Astrock. The curtain rises now!

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Ran Mitake, Kokoro Tsurumaki, Aya Maruyama, Yukina Minato, Mashiro Kurata, Rei Wakana, Tomori Takamatsu)

[“SPRINGTIME∞ASTROCK” - Venue]

Marina: —Yes, this is Tsukishima.
Staff: Sound check is ready on our end. You can start anytime!
Marina: Thank you. I’ll guide the bands to the stage as soon as they arrive!
Marina: Well, stage setup’s done, got the load-in report... Okay, looks like everything’s on track for now.
Marina: ...Phew... It’s finally here—today’s the day...!
Ririko: Yeah, the day of SPRINGTIME∞ASTROCK has finally arrived~.
Marina: Ririko-san! Don’t sneak up on me like that.
Ririko: Fufu, sorry, sorry. But you’re really fired up, Marina-chan.
Marina: Of course I am—so is the whole staff. After all, it’s an outdoor rock festival co-hosted by CiRCLE and RiNG!
Ririko: Totally! And the lineup couldn’t be a better fit for the theme, “Meeting bands, and running through youth with them.”
Marina: Yes. It took a lot of time to get here from the planning stage, but we’re totally prepared to make sure everyone who comes has a blast.
Marina: Still... I was so nervous last night I barely got any sleep.
Ririko: You too? I couldn’t sleep either. It felt like the night before a field trip.
Marina: Fufu, totally get that. I was nervous, but I also just really wanted today to come already...
Ririko: Yeah, yeah, it’s been a while since I felt like this.
Kokoro: Oh, it’s Marina and the others! Hey~~!!
Kasumi: Marina-san~~, Ririko-san~~!! Good morning!
Marina: Good morning. Kasumi-chan, Kokoro-chan, you’re full of energy this morning.
Kasumi: Yup! I was so excited for “Astrock”, I woke up before my alarm went off!
Kokoro: With weather this perfect and a day full of live shows ahead, how could I sit still!?
Ririko: So dazzling~...! Where’s everyone else?
Kasumi: Hmm? They were with us until just a moment ago...
Marina: Oh, looks like they just got here. Good morning, everyone!
Yukina: Good morning.
Ran: Kasumi and Kokoro bolted the second we arrived... way too much energy for the morning.
Aya: But I get how they feel though. Just seeing the venue totally pumped me up too!
LAYER (Rei): Yeah. Singing on a stage this wide feels like it’s going to be amazing.
Tomori: ...
Mashiro: Are you nervous, Tomori-chan...?
Tomori: Eh...! Um...
LAYER (Rei): It’s only natural to feel nervous with so many bands gathered like this.
Mashiro: I know what you mean... My stomach’s all twisty too.
Kasumi: It’s okay. We’re all nervous, but once the live starts, those nerves will turn into sparkles!
Kokoro: Exactly! Let’s all have tons of fun and fill today with smiles!
Tomori: Y-Yes...!
Kasumi: Oh, Marina-san! What does “Astrock” mean?
Marina: Ah, that’s actually a made-up word. It combines “astro,” as in stars or space, with “rock.”
Marina: Since it’s an outdoor stage, I thought we could perform under the sun by day and the stars by night.
Ririko: All of us on the staff worked really hard to come up with the name~.
Kasumi: It sounds super cool!! Like we’re doing the live with the sun and stars themselves!
Marina: It’s a totally different vibe from CiRCLE or RiNG lives, so make the most of it and have fun.
Ririko: Also, we’ve got fun stuff outside of the live performances too, right Marina-chan?
Marina: Yep! We’ve got a food area with kitchen trucks, photo spots, and merch booths—make sure to check them out during your breaks.
Aya: Oh, I saw that on the fest’s official social media! I was really looking forward to taking cute photos with those stylish drinks!
Yukina: There were tons of food options too. Lisa told me there’s a cupcake with a cat face on it.
LAYER (Rei): Really? I’ll let my bandmates know too.
Ririko: If you get a good photo, we’d love it if you could share it with the fest’s official hashtag.
Ririko: Post it with the fest’s name and “#CrankUpYourYouth”.
Ran: Um... what’s this white board? It’s not a photo spot, right?
Marina: Oh, that’s the message board. It’s one of our special features to hype up the fest.
Kasumi: The fest logo smack in the middle looks so cool!
Mashiro: A message board? So we’re supposed to write something around the logo?
Marina: Exactly. It’s open for everyone—attendees and performers alike—to write anything they want.
LAYER (Rei): Now that you mention it, I’m not sure what to write.
Kokoro: Oh, just jot down whatever pops into your head! I’ll draw the shining sun and flying Michelle!
Ran: Wait, are you only drawing...?
Marina: Ahaha, that’s totally fine! Whether it’s something you felt today or your band’s future goals—write whatever you like.
Yukina: Band’s future goals, huh...
Aya: I bet it’d look cute if we added stickers or photo booth pictures around the messages!
Mashiro: There are so many kinds of pens...! It’s going to turn out super colorful.
Kasumi: I see... It’s all blank now, but by the time the fest ends, it'll be full of everyone’s messages~.
Kasumi: Everyone’s sparkling, heart-pounding moments will be gathered on this board!
Kasumi: I’m getting really excited now! I can’t wait to see how it turns out~!
Aya: Yeah, yeah. I’d love to see what kind of fun stuff the attendees discover too.
Ran: I’m kinda curious what each band will write too.
Kokoro: Mmm~~! So much fun stuff happening today!
Ririko: Fufu, hearing that makes all the hard work feel totally worth it~.
Marina: No, no, the real show starts here! Let’s give it everything we’ve got so we won’t lose to anyone else!
Ririko: Oh, you’ve got the same look on your face as Kasumi-chan now, Marina-chan. Well then, shall we take everyone to the stage?
Marina: Yes!
Marina: Alright everyone, let’s have an amazing day together!
All: Let’s do our best!
