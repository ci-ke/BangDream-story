event207-04 Chapter 3: If Only Time Would Stop

Sadness causes Eve to break out in tears. Seeing that, Hina makes a suggestion.

(Character: Aya Maruyama, Hina Hikawa, Chisato Shirasagi, Maya Yamato, Eve Wakamiya)

[Shibuya - Station Entrance]
