event280-05 Chapter 4: Even in the Cold, You All

They’ve made it to the café. Which latte art did they end up picking after all that indecision...?

(Character: Rei Wakana, Rokka Asahi, Masuki Sato, Reona Nyubara, Chiyu Tamade)

[Inoshima - Café Terrace]

LAYER (Rei): Sigh~... It's pretty cold out on the terrace.
LOCK (Rokka): S-sorry, I didn’t think it would be this crowded...!
PAREO (Reona): We should’ve reserved the café too. CHU²-sama, is your hand warmer still working?
CHU² (Chiyu): No need to worry so much. We’ve got hot drinks, after all.
MASKING (Masuki): So, what did everyone end up ordering?
LAYER (Rei): CHU² and I got milk tea with honey. Did you all get café lattes? They’ve got art on them.
LOCK (Rokka): Masuki-san and I got hot cocoa, and PAREO-san got a café latte!
LAYER (Rei): Wow, they even do art on cocoa?
CHU² (Chiyu): LOCK’s is the bear she mentioned earlier. And MASKING’s... what is that? A rabbit...?
MASKING (Masuki): Yeah! Cute, right?
PAREO (Reona): CHU²-sama, take a look at mine too. They drew a heart on it for me~♪
CHU² (Chiyu): Yes yes, that’s nice.
LAYER (Rei): Still, this is such a nice café. I get why Tsugumi-chan recommended it.
LOCK (Rokka): When Afterglow came to Inoshima, they stopped by a different café, but apparently they were curious about this one too!
MASKING (Masuki): I remember asking Tomoe a lot about Inoshima too—how pretty the ocean was, how fun the aquarium was...
MASKING (Masuki): It sounded like so much fun, I was kinda jealous.
LAYER (Rei): Afterglow always seems to have fun no matter where they go.
PAREO (Reona): Right. I can totally picture them excitedly sightseeing around Inoshima.
MASKING (Masuki): Well, they’ve been like that since they were little. Just getting all five of them together would get them hyped up...
LOCK (Rokka): Masuki-san, you knew the Afterglow members when they were little?
MASKING (Masuki): I forget how old they were, but I’d occasionally see a group of girls who seemed really close playing together at the neighborhood park.
MASKING (Masuki): Thinking back now, I’m pretty sure that was Afterglow.
MASKING (Masuki): I’d been going to Shirayuki since kindergarten, so I never really got to play with them or anything.
LAYER (Rei): I see...
PAREO (Reona): It’s kind of amazing how you've managed to end up being close through both band stuff and work now, considering how things used to be.
MASKING (Masuki): Haha, yeah, it really is.
LAYER (Rei): ...Looks like Afterglow didn’t break up after all.
CHU² (Chiyu): Seems that way. At the GBT finals, it felt like they were ready to burn out right there on stage...
LOCK (Rokka): After GBT, Tsugumi-senpai was acting all cheerful, but it felt forced... I couldn’t really ask her about it.
MASKING (Masuki): Tomoe was kinda the same. But she seemed brighter after a while, so I figured things settled.
PAREO (Reona): I don’t know what happened, but I’m glad they didn’t break up.
LAYER (Rei): Yeah. If a band that close broke up, even we'd end up feeling sad over it.
MASKING (Masuki): Especially CHU². You were kind of rooting for Afterglow, after all.
CHU² (Chiyu): Not in particular. ...But I'm glad we'll get to see Afterglow perform again.
LOCK (Rokka): Right.
CHU² (Chiyu): C-cold!?
LOCK (Rokka): It’s obvious, but there’s nothing blocking the sea breeze at this seat...!
LAYER (Rei): And the drinks have already gone lukewarm.
CHU² (Chiyu): Ugh, maybe I’ll order another warm one...
MASKING (Masuki): CHU², you’re shaking!?
PAREO (Reona): Oh no, CHU²-sama, your hands are freezing...! Here, please take PAREO’s scarf!
CHU² (Chiyu): Thanks...
LOCK (Rokka): I brought extra hand warmers—please use one! I’ll pop it into your coat pocket.
LAYER (Rei): All I’ve got to lend is my scarf, I guess... But if we wrap it together with PAREO’s, it might be warmer.
CHU² (Chiyu): Th-thank you.
MASKING (Masuki): CHU², you can use mine too. Here.
CHU² (Chiyu): Jeez~~~, this is way too much! I’m not that cold!!
