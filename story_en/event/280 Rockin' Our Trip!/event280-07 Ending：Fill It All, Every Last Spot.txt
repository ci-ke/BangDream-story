event280-07 Ending: Fill It All, Every Last Spot

On the train ride home, as everyone sleeps... what quiet words does MASKING share with LOCK...?

(Character: Rei Wakana, Rokka Asahi, Masuki Sato, Reona Nyubara, Chiyu Tamade)

[Train]

MASKING (Masuki): Maybe I should’ve bought a few more souvenirs after all.
LOCK (Rokka): Yeah, maybe. We did get a bunch to hand out, though...
MASKING (Masuki): Yeah...
MASKING (Masuki): ............
Announcer: —Doors will open on the left... Next stop...
MASKING (Masuki): Looks like we’ll be there in about 10 minutes. Rei and the others are...
LAYER, PAREO & CHU²: Zzz... Zzz...
MASKING (Masuki): I’ll wake them up one station before ours...
LOCK (Rokka): Yeah.
MASKING (Masuki): ...Today was fun.
LOCK (Rokka): It was.
MASKING (Masuki): ...Wish it didn’t have to end yet.
LOCK (Rokka): Huh...?
MASKING (Masuki): It’s just... the more fun I have, the more I hate that it has to end.
MASKING (Masuki): I’ve been at Shirayuki since kindergarten, and I’ve never had people around who I clicked with like this.
MASKING (Masuki): There are so many firsts when I’m with you guys, and every one of them’s been amazing.
MASKING (Masuki): That’s why... every time we go out as RAS, the ride back always hits me with this huge wave of loneliness.
LOCK (Rokka): Masuki-san...
MASKING (Masuki): I’ve always looked up to my dad’s band. Everyone was serious about music, but also knew how to let loose and be stupid when it was time for that.
MASKING (Masuki): But maybe what I really wanted... was someone who’d accept me for who I am, and go all out having fun with me.
MASKING (Masuki): That’s probably why... I’m really glad I met everyone in RAS.
MASKING (Masuki): So let’s keep chasing more of those firsts together.
LOCK (Rokka): Of course! Let’s go on all kinds of trips together, play lots of music... and keep doing band things.
LOCK (Rokka): Let’s make tons of RAS memories together.
LOCK (Rokka): All the happy times, the tough times—when we look back, I’m sure they’ll all be precious memories we can laugh about.
LOCK (Rokka): Let’s fill up that lonely feeling you have, Masuki-san, with RAS memories! Fill it to the brim!
MASKING (Masuki): ...!
MASKING (Masuki): W-Wait...
LOCK (Rokka): Eh? Are those... tears!?
LOCK (Rokka): D-Did I say something awful, Masuki-san!?
MASKING (Masuki): No, it’s just, uh...

[Station Entrance]

CHU² (Chiyu): Yawn~... I slept so well.
PAREO (Reona): Good thing we got seats on the train. Let’s head home.
LAYER (Rei): You left your bike at CHU²’s place, right? ...Masuki?
MASKING (Masuki): Hm?
LAYER (Rei): ...What’s wrong? Were you... crying?
PAREO (Reona): Huh?
CHU² (Chiyu): W-Wait, what happened?
MASKING (Masuki): Ah~, uh, it’s nothing, really...
LOCK (Rokka): Um! Masuki-san and I just got kind of lonely!
MASKING (Masuki): H-Hey, LOCK!
LAYER (Rei): Lonely...? That’s why you were crying?
CHU² (Chiyu): Oh, come on. I was worried for nothing.
PAREO (Reona): Massu-san’s especially sensitive to that kind of thing, after all.
MASKING (Masuki): What, is that a crime?
LAYER (Rei): Fufu... but I get how you and LOCK feel. Saying goodbye right after the ticket gate feels kind of sad.
MASKING (Masuki): Right? Going home now just feels like a waste.
CHU² (Chiyu): Here we go. Lemme guess, you wanna start a jam session or something?
MASKING (Masuki): Oh, you know me too well.
PAREO (Reona): I was planning to sleep over at CHU²-sama’s place anyway, so it’s fine.
LAYER (Rei): I don’t have anything going on tomorrow besides RAS practice, so I’m good too.
LOCK (Rokka): Me too!
CHU² (Chiyu): Then why don’t you all just stay over at my place? It’s already getting late anyway.
MASKING (Masuki): Seriously!? That’s gonna be insanely fun! Come on, let’s go already!
LOCK (Rokka): Yeah!
CHU² (Chiyu): Hey, wait...! You two, hold it right there!!
CHU² (Chiyu): Sigh~... Maybe I really do need to put leashes on those two.
