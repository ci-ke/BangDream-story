event151-05 Chapter 4: The Tenacity in My Fingertips

Despite LAYER's perfect performance, MASKING suddenly stops her.

(Character: Rei Wakana, Masuki Sato)

[Galaxy - Studio]

LAYER (Rei): Alright, Masuki. Let's do this. And thanks.
MASKING (Masuki): Hey, you sure about this? You were just practicin' our new song at CHU²'s place.
LAYER (Rei): I'll be fine. You were practicing too.
MASKING (Masuki): Yeah, but you were a sweaty mess by the time I arrived.
MASKING (Masuki): ... You've practically been spendin' all your free time in CHU²'s studio and Galaxy.
MASKING (Masuki): LOCK and PAREO are worried you might be overdoin' it.
LAYER (Rei): I know they're worried...
LAYER (Rei): But I don't have much time until the show, and I want to do everything I can.
LAYER (Rei): I need to be perfect on stage so that I can be RAS' LAYER.
MASKING (Masuki): Fine... but get some sleep at night, at least. You've got eye bags.
LAYER (Rei): Oof, really? I'll go look in a mirror later...
LAYER (Rei): Anyway, let's get started. We don't have much time. From the top okay with you?
MASKING (Masuki): Sure thing, but we're stoppin' practice the moment anythin' feels off.
LAYER (Rei): Okay. Don't worry.
MASKING (Masuki): ... Alright, you ready?
(Black cut)
MASKING (Masuki): (She's a real piece of work... The way she's playin' now would be good enough for the actual concert.)
MASKING (Masuki): (Nothin' sounds outta the ordinary either... Maybe I'm just overthinkin' things. We should be able to keep goin' like this until the end then...!)
LAYER (Rei): ...
MASKING (Masuki): ... ?!
LAYER (Rei): ... What is it? Why did you suddenly stop playing...?
MASKING (Masuki): ... Show me your fingers.
LAYER (Rei): Hm...? Why?
MASKING (Masuki): I said show me your damn fingers!
LAYER (Rei): Ah, what are you doing...?!
MASKING (Masuki): I knew it...! Your fingers... they're all busted up!
LAYER (Rei): ... It's nothing. Come on, could we keep practicing?
MASKING (Masuki): What do you mean nothin'?! This ain't friggin' nothin'!!
LAYER (Rei): ...!
MASKING (Masuki): There was hesitation in your playin' right before we hit the chorus. It may have been an unconscious decision, but you were probably tryin' not to hurt them.
MASKING (Masuki): We're the drummer and bassist. You think you can keep somethin' like this hidden?!
LAYER (Rei): ... Sorry.
LAYER (Rei): But I don't want to stop practicing yet. I need to be perfect on stage as RAS' LAYER.
MASKING (Masuki): ... I don't give a crap about RAS' LAYER! What's wrong with just bein' Rei Wakana?!
MASKING (Masuki): The Rei Wakana I know may try to act all tough even though she isn't...
MASKING (Masuki): She ain't perfect... She'll try to endure things when she shouldn't have to, and she can be stubborn as hell...
MASKING (Masuki): But she's got a lotta good qualities too. I know it and so does everyone else in the band.
LAYER (Rei): Masuki...
MASKING (Masuki): So stop tryin' to be all cool so that you can become this RAS' LAYER.
MASKING (Masuki): It's alright to be the Rei Wakana that can't drink black coffee and likes sweet things, you know...?!
LAYER (Rei): ... I didn't know that's how you felt.
LAYER (Rei): I see... I get it now.
LAYER (Rei): After hearing what you just said, I feel a lot better.
LAYER (Rei): You guys know who the real Rei Wakana is...
LAYER (Rei): That's why I don't need to be afraid of becoming RAS' LAYER!
LAYER (Rei): Thanks, Masuki.
MASKING (Masuki): Rei... You...
