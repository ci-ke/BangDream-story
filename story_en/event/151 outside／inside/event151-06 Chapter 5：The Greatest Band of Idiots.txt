event151-06 Chapter 5: The Greatest Band of Idiots

LAYER and MASKING finish their last practice. Which drink will LAYER take...?

(Character: Rei Wakana, Masuki Sato)

[A Few Days Later]

[Studio - Large Room]

Staff: This will be our final rehearsal before the concert. If there are any issues, let's be sure to work them out today!
LAYER & MASKING: Okay.
(Black cut)
LAYER (Rei): I didn't make any mistakes, and I played through all of our songs without losing focus... I'd say I did pretty okay.
MASKING (Masuki): Heh, can't believe you just said that about your own performance.
LAYER (Rei): You say it all the time, Masuki.
MASKING (Masuki): You ain't wrong there.
Staff: Wakana-san, Sato-san, thank you so much! All that remains is the rehearsal on the day of the concert and the concert itself. Let's do our best.
LAYER (Rei): I intend to.
Staff: I have to say I'm surprised by how much the two of you improve with every performance. I suppose that's what it means to be talented, huh?
LAYER (Rei): ... Maybe. Fufufu.
Staff: W-well, I'm certainly impressed... Ah, we received some canned drinks. They're on the table, so please feel free to pick a drink of your choice.
MASKING (Masuki): Got it. Much appreciated.
MASKING (Masuki): Hey, there's a pretty decent selection here, Rei.
MASKING (Masuki): Milk tea, orange juice, soda... Think I'll have the milk tea.
Staff: What would you like, Wakana-san?
LAYER (Rei): Let's see...
LAYER (Rei): I'll have the black coffee.
MASKING (Masuki): ... Sigh~...
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Station Entrance]

LAYER (Rei): RAS got a lot of praise during practice, huh?
MASKING (Masuki): So did RAS' LAYER... You know, I knew you wouldn't be able to drink that black coffee.
LAYER (Rei): That's because it's so bitter.
MASKING (Masuki): Wanna swap? My old man would probably drink it.
LAYER (Rei): Hm~... No, it's alright. I'll take it home with me and go through it a sip at a time.
LAYER (Rei): ... Hey. Thanks for everything.
MASKING (Masuki): Hm?
LAYER (Rei): I know I've made you and everyone else worry a lot.
MASKING (Masuki): I don't mind... but LOCK and PAREO have been pretty worried.
LAYER (Rei): I know. I'll be sure to apologize to them.
LAYER (Rei): ... Even I also know that wanting to become RAS' LAYER is just me being stubborn.
MASKING (Masuki): ...
LAYER (Rei): If it weren't until recently, I probably would've stopped if it meant causing the rest of you trouble.
LAYER (Rei): But RAS' LAYER is the best front man of the best band.
LAYER (Rei): As long as there are people who know who Rei Wakana really is, that's good enough for me.
MASKING (Masuki): You seriously are one hell of an idiot, you know?
LAYER (Rei): You think so...?
MASKING (Masuki): Well, it's not like it's news. I've known since before we joined RAS.
MASKING (Masuki): When we'd work together as backups back then, you were even more obedient. You said yes to practically everything.
MASKING (Masuki): But there was this fire in your music.
MASKING (Masuki): And that's how I knew you were a hopeless idiot just tryin' to tackle music head-on.
LAYER (Rei): A hopeless idiot tackling music head-on...? Really? You think you're one to talk? Fufu... Ahaha.
MASKING (Masuki): What are you laughin' for...? Jeez.
MASKING (Masuki): Anyway, we should probably head to Galaxy for some practice. Put your helmet on.
LAYER (Rei): Um... You don't mind if we go to Galaxy?
MASKING (Masuki): Why would I? It doesn't mean I agree with your decision, though...
MASKING (Masuki): But I gave you my word that I'd help you out till the concert's over.
LAYER (Rei): I know I've said this before...
LAYER (Rei): But you are pretty nice.
MASKING (Masuki): That's the dumbest crap I've ever heard~... Keep talkin' and I'm leavin' you behind!
LAYER (Rei): Fufu, okay, okay, I'm sorry.
LAYER (Rei): ... Masuki.
MASKING (Masuki): Huh?
LAYER (Rei): Thanks.
