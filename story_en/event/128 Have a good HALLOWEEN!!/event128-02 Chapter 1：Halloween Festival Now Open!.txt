event128-02 Chapter 1: Halloween Festival Now Open!

LOCK visits MASKING and tells her about the menu her class is planning.

(Character: Ako Udagawa, Rokka Asahi, Masuki Sato)

[Galaxy Ramen]

MASKING (Masuki): A Halloween festival?
LOCK (Rokka): Yes! All the classes are going to be doing something special, and everyone will go around school in costumes!
MASKING (Masuki): So you're celebratin' Halloween at school, huh? Sounds interestin'.
MASKING (Masuki): My school's uptight as hell, so no fun for us.
LOCK (Rokka): I didn't know high schools in Tokyo did this kind of thing either!
MASKING (Masuki): So, what's your class gonna be doin'?
LOCK (Rokka): We're going to do a Halloween café! Now we just have to figure out what to serve! Ah, and we still need a menu board too!
LOCK (Rokka): We also need to do decorations, make sure we have plenty of snacks... Ah, it might be good to offer takeout too!
MASKING (Masuki): ... You look like you're havin' fun.
LOCK (Rokka): I am! I'm actually really excited!
MASKING (Masuki): If you're doin' a Halloween café, you can offer pumpkin cake and purple sweet potato tart... Good stuff.
LOCK (Rokka): Actually~, we're not serving cake...
MASKING (Masuki): Huh? Why're you callin' yourselves a café if you ain't gonna be servin' cake?
LOCK (Rokka): Well... We don't have anyone who's good at baking cakes, so it looks like we can't.
LOCK (Rokka): It might just be cookies and other simple snacks...
MASKING (Masuki): Gotcha. That blows...
LOCK (Rokka): If only I could bake cakes like you, Masuki-san...
LOCK (Rokka): ... Ah!
MASKING (Masuki): Hm?
LOCK (Rokka): Masuki-san! Could you come teach us how to bake?
MASKING (Masuki): S-seriously...? Well, yeah, guess I could when there's no school, but...
LOCK (Rokka): How about coming after school on days when you have no band practice or work?
MASKING (Masuki): I probably could... You sure it's all right for me to come?
LOCK (Rokka): I think so... But I'll ask our teacher just in case!
LOCK (Rokka): Your cakes are so good! I'm sure everyone in class would really appreciate your help!
MASKING (Masuki): Y-you think?
LOCK (Rokka): Please, Masuki-san! You're the only person I can ask to do this!
MASKING (Masuki): Hmm...
MASKING (Masuki): How can I say no when you're practically beggin'...? All right, I'll do it.
LOCK (Rokka): Yay~! Thank you so much!
MASKING (Masuki): ... You better know I'm not gonna go easy on your guys or tolerate any slackin' off.
LOCK (Rokka): I-I'm glad you won't!
MASKING (Masuki): Good! I'm getting fired up! Eat up before the noodles get soggy!
LOCK (Rokka): Yes! Thank you!
