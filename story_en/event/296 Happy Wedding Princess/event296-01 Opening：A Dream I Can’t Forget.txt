event296-01 Opening: A Dream I Can’t Forget

One day at Hazawa Coffee, Himari learns about an upcoming event and...

(Character: Himari Uehara, Tsugumi Hazawa, Toko Kirigaya, Rokka Asahi)

[Hazawa Coffee]

Himari: Eh~! A bridal photo contest~~?! That sounds so nice~...!!
Tsugumi: Himari-chan, keep it down a little...!
LOCK (Rokka): H-hello~. May I ask what that loud voice just now was...?
Tsugumi: Welcome, Rokka-chan. We were just talking about this poster.
LOCK (Rokka): Wow, what a beautiful wedding dress. The model’s smile is gorgeous too...!
Tsugumi: It’s for a photo contest being organized by one of our regulars. Seems like anyone can enter.
Himari: Sorry for causing a scene...! The poster was just so beautiful, I couldn’t help myself.
LOCK (Rokka): Fufu, I see. I heard you say “That sounds so nice!”—are you thinking of entering, Himari-senpai?
Himari: Hmm... I want to, but getting ready sounds like a lot, and the deadline’s in two weeks...
Himari: Oh, but we’re just standing here chatting. Rokka, did you come to have some tea?
LOCK (Rokka): Yeah, I came by after RAS practice. Would it be okay if I joined you?
Himari: Fufu, I was thinking the same thing! Tsugu, a table for two, please.
Tsugumi: Sure. Right this way, please.
(Black cut)
Tsugumi: —Here you go. One seasonal cake set and two hot teas. Enjoy.
LOCK (Rokka): Thanks so much! ...So, about that contest you mentioned—
Himari: Yeah. I mean, wedding dresses~... I just can’t help but admire them.
Himari: I actually love looking at bridal photos online and in ads. You can find tons if you search for them.
Himari: Like... this one! It’s a collection of bridal photos, and every single one is just so beautiful~...!
LOCK (Rokka): Wow~, those dresses are gorgeous! Oh, and the garden in the background here is so lovely...!
LOCK (Rokka): They even shoot in the middle of the city. It’s amazing how much the setting changes the mood.
Himari: Right?! That’s one of the coolest parts of bridal photos!
Himari: Sure, the bride in the dress is the star, but the props and location really bring out her personality.
Himari: “Like a candlelit church at sunset, or a private café with all the decorations handmade... And someone playing the piano in a wedding dress—that’s so lovely too...
LOCK (Rokka): Fufu...
Himari: Ah, sorry! I’m totally hogging the conversation...!
LOCK (Rokka): No, please keep going!
LOCK (Rokka): Himari-senpai, your eyes were sparkling so much—it really showed how much you love this, and it made me happy just listening.
Himari: Rokka~...! Thank you!
Himari: You know, I’ve always admired fairy-tale princesses.
Himari: Those happily-ever-after scenes at the end of stories of princesses and princes—there are usually wedding scenes, right? Every time I saw one, I’d get totally dreamy...
Himari: I used to tell the others in Afterglow, “Someday I’m gonna wear a beautiful wedding dress in a magical place!”
LOCK (Rokka): Oh, I see...! I think I get what you mean.
Himari: Wait—Rokka, do you have a story like that too?
LOCK (Rokka): Yeah. Not about being a princess, but... it was guitar for me.
LOCK (Rokka): When I was little, I saw a guitar displayed outside a store and totally fell in love with it... But there was no way I could afford one with my allowance, so I made a guitar out of cardboard and pretended to play it.
Himari: I used to do that kind of thing too! One time I got to wear a princess-like dress for my birthday, and I kept spinning around in it and everything.
Himari: It was all just pretend for me, but you actually made it happen, Rokka. That’s amazing.
LOCK (Rokka): Huh?
Himari: I mean, you saved up your allowance little by little, bought a guitar, moved to Tokyo, and even formed a band.
Himari: It’s seriously amazing how you didn’t just let your dream stay a dream.
LOCK (Rokka): Um... has your dream already ended, Himari-senpai?
Himari: Eh...
LOCK (Rokka): S-Sorry if that sounded rude! It’s just... when you looked at that photo contest poster earlier, you said “how nice,” so I kind of assumed...!
Himari: My dream...
Himari: ...It’s not over!
Himari: Okay, I’ve made up my mind...! I’m entering that bridal photo contest!
Himari: It’s been fun just dreaming about it, but talking to you made me want to actually go for it—make my childhood dream come true!
LOCK (Rokka): Wow! I think you’d look amazing in a wedding dress, Himari-senpai!
LOCK (Rokka): Um, if it’s okay... please let me help too!
Himari: Really? Ah, but you don’t have to feel obligated or anything!
LOCK (Rokka): That’s not it. Watching you talk about your dream with those sparkling eyes made me want to help!
Himari: Ugh, thank you~...! I’ll be counting on you then!
Himari: Then we’d better get moving! Let’s see... what should we do first? Get a dress?
LOCK (Rokka): Yeah. I wonder if there are places that rent them?
Himari: Probably... but I’ve heard that rentals don’t offer much variety in design.
Tsugumi: Ah, Toko-chan! Welcome. Working on your designs again today?
Toko: Yes! ...Wait, huh? Himari-san? LOCK?
Himari: Toko-chan! You’re here alone? Wanna sit with us?
Toko: Can I? Yay, thanks! ...Huh? What were you two talking about?
Himari: Actually, I’ve decided to enter the bridal photo contest. We were just starting to plan it out. You know, the one on that poster?
Toko: Bridal photo contest...
Toko: Oh my gosh, that’s it!
Himari & LOCK: Huh?
Toko: Himari-san! If you haven’t picked out a dress yet, would you let me design one for you?!
Himari: Ehh, really?!
Toko: Man~, I’ve been designing dresses for my brand, but I still haven’t decided on one of the main highlights for this collection.
Toko: But then it hit me—if it’s a wedding dress, it’s perfect~.
Toko: So how about it, Himari-san? I’ll design a fully custom wedding dress packed with 10,000% of what you want!
Toko: Plus, I get to be inspired by you too, so it’s totally win-win!
Himari: E-Eh? Really...?! I was just thinking about renting one, but a custom dress~...?!
LOCK (Rokka): This timing is incredible...! I’m so happy for you, Himari-senpai!
Himari: Y-yeah...! But are you really sure, Toko-chan...?
Toko: Of course! I’ll make you the cutest dress ever, so you can totally leave it to me!
Himari: Thank you so much~~~~! I can’t believe it, I’m so happy...!
Himari: Okay, the dress is all set—now what else do I need for the entry~...?
Toko: A photographer, right? You’re gonna be in a wedding dress, so it’s gotta be shot beautifully!
LOCK (Rokka): Oh! I actually know someone really reliable—let’s ask them!
