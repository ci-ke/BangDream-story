event296-02 Chapter 1: Friends I Can Rely On

LOCK boldly claims she knows a great photographer. And the person she brings is...

(Character: Saya Yamabuki, Himari Uehara, Aya Maruyama, Toko Kirigaya, Rokka Asahi)

[The Next Day]

[CiRCLE Café]

LOCK (Rokka): Let me introduce you—our super reliable photographer, Saya-senpai!
Saya: H-hi there~...!
Himari & Toko: Wow~~!
Toko: ...But why Saya-san?
LOCK (Rokka): Glad you asked...! Turns out, Saya-senpai is really into photography, and she even entered the New Year photo contest!
Himari: I saw that! There was a photo of Rinko-san that Saya took, hanging in that photo shop!
Toko: No way! That means it got super high marks!? That makes Saya-san basically a pro!
Saya: No, no, it just turned out nice because Rinko-senpai was such a good model... They only displayed it because I submitted it.
LOCK (Rokka): Still, I think being able to capture that expression from Rinko-senpai is all thanks to Saya-senpai’s skills!
Himari: Yeah, yeah. I really loved that photo! Saya, please... lend us your talent!
Saya: Fufu, of course! If you’re okay with me, I’ll give it my all.
Himari: Thank you, Saya~!
Toko: Alright, now the photographer’s set! So, what’s next?
Himari: Ah, actually... there’s one more person helping out. I just got a message—they’ll be here any second...
Aya: Himari-chan~. Hey everyone~! Sorry I’m late~!
LOCK (Rokka): Aya-senpai!?
Aya: Sorry I’m late. Work ran longer than I expected.
Himari: No, thank you so much for coming even though you’re busy!
Toko: So it’s me, Aya-san, Saya-san, LOCK, and Himari-san...? This is a pretty unique group!
Aya: Fufu, yeah, it really is.
Himari: So, I posted on social media that I was entering a bridal photo contest... and she messaged me right away!
Aya: I sent her a message saying, “I just know it’s going to be a gorgeous shoot! I’m cheering for you!” And then we totally got carried away talking about dresses...
Aya: And I asked if I could help out!
LOCK (Rokka): I see. Thank you so much, Aya-senpai!
Aya: Yeah. I’m not sure how helpful I’ll be, but I’ll do everything I can!
Himari: Thank you so much, Aya-san...!
Saya: Alright, shall we get started then? First up is the dress, right? I heard Toko’s in charge of that...
Aya: Wait, Toko-chan, you can make wedding dresses too!?
Toko: Well, not exactly make—I design them! It’s my first time, but I think I can pull it off!
Aya: Wow, that’s amazing... wait, your first time!?
Toko: Yup! Just watch—I’m gonna make the cutest, most gorgeous, absolutely perfect wedding dress ever!
LOCK (Rokka): She’s never done this before, and yet she’s this confident... She’s on another level...!
Toko: Of course! It’s me we’re talking about! Trying something for the first time gets me totally fired up!
LOCK (Rokka): That’s so cool...! I’ve gotta do my best too so I can be helpful for you, Himari-senpai!
Himari: You guys are way too dependable~...! Thank you so much!
Himari: I've gotta pull all I got too—I can’t leave it all to you! Okay, the deadline’s in two weeks, so~...
Toko: Wait, two weeks!?
LOCK (Rokka): K-Kirigaya-san...!? We looked at the poster together, remember...!?
Toko: Uhh... I think we did...? Maybe...? Kinda~...?
Toko: Ahaha, well, we’ll figure it out! I mean, it’s me!
LOCK (Rokka): W-wait, for sure? Are you sure we’ll be okay!?
Toko: Totally fine! LOCK, you worry too much~.
Aya: U-Um... I’m starting to get nervous... in all kinds of ways...
Saya: Me too...
Toko: It’s gonna be fine, really! The word “impossible” doesn’t exist in my dictionary!
Toko: But I will need to switch into express mode, so let’s do your measurements right away!
Himari: Huh, measurements!? Right now!?
Toko: Yup! Alright, let’s go!
Himari: W-wait, Toko-chan! I’m not mentally ready~~~...!
