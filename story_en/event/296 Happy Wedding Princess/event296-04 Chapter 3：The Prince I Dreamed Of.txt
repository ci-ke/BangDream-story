event296-04 Chapter 3: The Prince I Dreamed Of

With the dress decided, Himari struggles to find a location—until Aya gives her an idea.

(Character: Saya Yamabuki, Himari Uehara, Aya Maruyama, Toko Kirigaya, Rokka Asahi)

[Kirigaya Residence - Kimono shop]

Toko: —All right, I’ll move forward with the design like this!
Himari: Yup! I’m counting on you! I’m just so excited—it’s really starting to feel real!
Aya: Fufu, Himari-chan, you looked like you were having fun even when you were stressing over it.
Himari: Ehehe, it’s all just so new and exciting for me.
LOCK (Rokka): Then let’s move on to the next step—picking a photoshoot location!
Saya: Yeah. Himari, do you have somewhere in mind?
Himari: Hmm, I’m still not sure~... I’ve been looking at a bunch of places, and they’re all just so beautiful.
Aya: Then maybe you could start by imagining the kind of scene you want in the photo?
Himari: Like, the situation?
Aya: Yeah. When I was doing a magazine shoot, they gave us a little setup—like, “You’re waiting to meet a friend after school,” that kind of thing.
Aya: The props and set were all tailored to that story, so it helped me really get into character during the shoot.
Toko: Ah, we’ve done that kind of thing in our brand’s shoots too! When there’s a story behind it, everything just comes together so nicely.
Aya: Exactly! If you imagine a story filled with all your dreams, I bet the perfect location will come to you.
Himari: As expected of you, Aya-san! I’ll give it a try!
Toko: Alright then, Himari-san’s ultimate dream scenario—let’s hear it! Three, two, one...
Himari: Wait—so sudden!? Uhh... Maybe... a sparkling prince on a white horse comes to sweep me away!?
LOCK, Saya & Toko: Huh?
Himari: Ah! No, wait, forget I said that!! That’s not what you meant, right!?
Aya: It’s okay! You said your dream started with fairytales, right?
Himari: Still, saying something that unrealistic feels kind of silly...!
LOCK (Rokka): It’s fine! Keep going!
LOCK (Rokka): Even if we can’t do everything exactly like that, I’m sure we can make parts of it come true!
Himari: Y-you think so...?
LOCK (Rokka): Absolutely! I’ll take notes! Let’s see... “white horse,” “prince”... Got anything else in mind?
Himari: Hmm, what else... Maybe I trip on my dress and the prince catches me...?
Himari: Or, a prince gracefully walks down a spiral staircase and says, “You’re the one of my life...!” and found me from the crowd...!
Himari: I-I’m starting to get embarrassed just talking about it!
Toko: I see, so a real princess fairytale kind of scene!
Aya: I used to love those too—like when the prince takes the princess’s hand!
Himari: Right~!? Even just imagining it makes your heart race!
LOCK (Rokka): A spiral staircase... the prince taking her hand...
Saya: LOCK, you don’t have to take that many notes...
LOCK (Rokka): No, you never know what might be a helpful hint!
Aya: Thinking about Himari-chan’s dream, maybe somewhere like a castle would be nice? How about a Western-style mansion with a balcony?
Himari: Wow, that would go perfectly with a princess-line dress! And a rose garden would be amazing too~...!
Himari: Oh! But maybe a chapel with an ocean view would be even better...!
Saya: Come to think of it, a lot of the bridal photos you like have the ocean in the background.
Himari: Yeah. In the picture book I loved as a kid, the final scene was a wedding at a seaside chapel.
Himari: Looking back, it was kind of a typical story, but... The princess faced all sorts of hardships waiting for the prince she admired, and she never gave up.
Himari: With the help of a wizzard and all the people around her, she finally made her dream come true.
Himari: So whenever I read that picture book, I’d always cheer the princess on like, “You can do it!”—with all my heart.
Saya: Fufu. That’s so you, Himari.
Aya: I totally get it. I’d always root for the princess too in stories like that.
Himari: When she finally gets her happy ending after giving it her all, it just makes you feel so happy too.
LOCK (Rokka): Yeah. The magic helped her reach her happiness, but I think it only worked because she worked hard to begin with...
Toko: You’re totally right...! Himari-san, you’re crazy good with words, huh!?
Himari: N-No I don’t~!
LOCK (Rokka): I’m honestly kind of moved! I never thought about why princesses get magic in fairytales before...
LOCK (Rokka): But now I realize—it’s because they earn it through effort. That’s why they get to wear the beautiful dress and meet the prince at a chapel by the sea...!
LOCK (Rokka): This story totally fits you, Himari-senpai! You’re entering the bridal photo contest to make your childhood dream come true...
LOCK (Rokka): Himari-senpai! Let’s absolutely get a gorgeous dress and take photos at a seaside chapel!
Himari: Rokka~...! Yeah! I’ll work hard to find the perfect chapel!
Saya: I’ll help too. I’m sure finding it and getting permission won’t be easy.
Toko: Honestly, we should all work together! Let’s search all at once and find it in no time!
Aya: Yeah! Don’t you think we make an amazing team?
Toko: Right!? We’ve basically got the dress and the location figured out today—we’re all geniuses!
LOCK (Rokka): Wait, what!? Kirigaya-san, you still have to design the dress, and we haven’t even picked the photo location yet...
Toko: C’mon, don’t sweat the small stuff!
Himari: Micron by micron, right? Fufufu, we really are the best team!
Himari: All right—let’s keep up the momentum and find the perfect chapel! Ready, set—go~!
