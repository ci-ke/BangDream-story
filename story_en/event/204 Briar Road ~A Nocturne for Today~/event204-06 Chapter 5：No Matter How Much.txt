event204-06 Chapter 5: No Matter How Much

After shopping with Sayo, something in the lobby catches Rinko's eye...

(Character: Yukina Minato, Sayo Hikawa, Lisa Imai, Ako Udagawa, Rinko Shirokane)

[Hotel - Lobby]

Sayo: Thank you for accompanying me, Shirokane-san.
Rinko: It was nothing... After getting some fresh air with you... I'm more ready to focus...
Sayo: Likewise... Ah, I need to contact Harumi-san.
Rinko: She did ask us... to let her know once we were back...
Sayo: Yes. Could you wait for a bit?
Rinko: Of course...
Rinko: (Ah... The grand piano's lid is still up...)
Rinko: (It feels like it's popping out of its surroundings... because it's under the spotlight...)
Rinko: (Oh, ivory keys... With a hint of yellow too... Such a gentle color...)
Sayo: I'm sorry I kept you waiting. Now that Harumi-san has been informed, we should return to our room and...
Sayo: ... Shirokane-san?
Rinko: Ah... I-I'm sorry... I couldn't help it after seeing the piano...
Sayo: ... If you're going to be playing, I think I'll stay and listen. There's still some time until the meeting, after all.
Rinko: I-if you do that, though... you won't be able to rest... It's probably better... for you to return ahead...
Sayo: I happen to be in the mood for listening to the soothing sound of a grand piano now.
Rinko: Hikawa-san...
Rinko: Okay... Maybe just a little then...
Rinko: (Now even Hikawa-san... has to deal with what I've been feeling...)
Rinko: (Things can't go on like this... Just like everyone... I need to hurry up and start focusing on tomorrow...)
Sayo: (For the people who came today, today was all they had... This is quite a painful fact for us to have to face in our current position.)
Sayo: (We have six more concerts over the course of this tour, and the next one is already taking place tomorrow. However...)
Sayo: (If we don't deal with "today" by the time tomorrow comes... We've already seen what will likely happen...)
Rinko: (I can't do it... I can't stop seeing the faces... of the people in the audience...)
Rinko: (How did today's Roselia... appear to them...? How did our music sound...?)
Rinko: (... These thoughts must be coming to me because I haven't come to terms yet with how I did today...)
Rinko: (I never want to feel this way again... I never want to have to apologize... to myself... or the audience... for a performance I've given... Never...!)
Sayo: ...! This piece sounds familiar...
Yukina: ... When Rinko re-entered the piano competition she had entered once before when she was little, she played this very piece.
Sayo: Minato-san...?! Imai-san and Udagawa-san too...?!
Lisa: You were taking a bit longer than expected, so we got worried and asked Harumi-san. She said you'd be here.
Ako: Rin-rin's sound... Just listening to it gives me this tight feeling in my chest... It's painful, but that's not all...
Lisa: Yeah... Each note hits hard, but I feel like I'm getting courage from them too...
Yukina: ...
(Black cut)
Rinko: Phew...
Ako: Rin-rin~! The way you play the piano really is amazing!
Ako: Listening made me think of today, and my heart felt like it was being squeezed, and...
Rinko: A-Ako-chan...?! You're all here too...?! That must mean I've kept all of you waiting for a while then...
Sayo: No, it's not time for the meeting yet.
Lisa: We're a bunch of worrywarts, that's all. We thought something might've happened to our two little cuties since you were taking longer than expected.
Yukina: Anyhow, Rinko, that piece you played was the same one you had to play for the competition, wasn't it?
Rinko: Y-yes...
Yukina: Why were you playing it just now?
Rinko: Why... I'm not sure myself...
Rinko: I started playing the piano... to work through my feelings... but the same thoughts kept going around and around... in my head...
Yukina: ... What do you mean?
Rinko: I spoke about it... with Hikawa-san already, but...
(Black cut)
Yukina: I see... "For the people who came today, today was all they had..."
Lisa: Of course... People even wrote online about how they weren't sure when they'd get another chance if they missed this one.
Ako: But we still couldn't give our best performance for them...
Ako: ... I wish we could redo today's show again.
Rinko: ... Ah...
Yukina: That feeling... the regret we have over today's show, is something I'm sure we all share.
Yukina: This regret is also something that will never go away.
Sayo: Could you explain?
Yukina: The same exact people who were there today can never be together at the same venue again.
Yukina: Even if tomorrow's concert and every subsequent concert goes well, nothing will change today's outcome.
Yukina: That's why this regret will always be a part of us, and we can never start over, either.
Rinko: ... Of course... It all makes sense...
Ako: What is it, Rin-rin?
Rinko: I finally understand... why I played the competition piece earlier...
Rinko: That piece... is from when I wanted to stop running from my weakness... and I made the decision to start all over again instead...
Rinko: Maybe... a part of me has wanted to redo today's concert...
Rinko: Even though, as Yukina-san said... that can't be done...
Sayo: You probably already knew, Shirokane-san.
Sayo: You didn't want this regret to simply be a bitter memory. You wanted to face yourself like you did back then and find a way to come to terms with everything.
Sayo: And that's likely why you chose that particular piece to play for yourself.
Rinko: ... I believe... you may be right...
Yukina: Rinko, thank you for playing the piano. Although you did it for your own sake, it has led us to the answer.
Yukina: I finally see what we must do today... and what we must do tomorrow.
