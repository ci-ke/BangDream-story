event245-07 Ending: Best Wishes for Another Year

On the way home after the sun completely set, Lisa asked Moca to take a small side trip…

(Character: Moca Aoba, Lisa Imai)

[A Few Hours Later]

[Residential Area]

Lisa: Moca, sorry to keep you waiting. Thanks for coming with me all the way to return the car.
Moca: No sweat. We're the ultimate tag team, this much is a given~
Lisa: You say that, but at the end, you really had a blast with Toko and the rest, huh.
Moca: Well that's that, and this is this~ Be flexible and switch gears as needed~
Lisa: You're on a roll, huh… Ah.
Moca: What's wrong~?
Lisa: Look, the shrine over there.  It looks like they're still serving amazake, so why don't we stop by for a bit? I'm getting cold from all the walking.
Moca: Absolutely~ Let's go, let's go~

Lisa: … Right. I'm glad you enjoyed it, Moca.
