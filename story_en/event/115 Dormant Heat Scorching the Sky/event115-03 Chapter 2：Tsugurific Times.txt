event115-03 Chapter 2: Tsugurific Times

The Afterglow members meet for practice for the first time in a while. But something is amiss...

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa)

[Studio]

Tsugumi: It's been a while since we practiced in a studio. The last time was before our concert, wasn't it?
Ran: ... Yeah. It's been a bit.
Moca: Are we going to work on the songs we already have or write something new~?
Himari: I-I want to work on our current lineup! I think my pick flew off because I wasn't used to the new song!
Tomoe: Yeah, I feel like going over our old ones, too.
Tomoe: How about we play through the songs that were on our set list last time?
Moca: Sounds good~.
Ran: Okay. Are you okay with that, Tsugumi?
Tsugumi: Of course! I'm ready when you are!
Ran: Let's do this... First up... "Tied to the Skies."
(Black cut)
Ran: ... Phew. That's about all of them.
Moca: Any thoughts, Hii-chan~?
Himari: Huh?! Me?!
Moca: You're the one who made the request~.
Himari: Oh, yeah, you're right~! Umm, I think we played good and sounded pretty cool!
Tomoe: Y-yeah, you said it, Himari! I feel the same way! It sounded like us!
Himari: Yeah! What did you think, Moca?
Moca: Hmm~? I guess I feel the same way as you two~.
Himari: What kind of an answer is that~? Ran, what about you? What did you think?
Ran: ... Yeah. The way we played was the same as always...
Himari: Ohh~! It looks like we all feel the same way! And you, Tsugu?
Tsugumi: ...
Himari: Ts-Tsugu...? Hey~...
Moca: Looks like she's lost in thought again~.
Ran: ... That's a pretty scary face for Tsugumi.
Tomoe: Sh-she mad or something...?
Himari: Sh-she wouldn't be~...
Tsugumi: ...
Himari: G-guys, huddle around.
Ran: ... Why are we whispering in this corner?
Tomoe: Come on, what else are we gonna do? This is an emergency.
Moca: Jeez, Hii-chan, what did you do to Tsugu?
Himari: Why are you assuming it's my fault?!
Moca: Because it's you, Hii-chan~. Does anything come to mind~? There's no need to hide anything from us~.
Himari: I-I am not hiding anything... What about you guys?
Moca: I've just been myself, the same as always~.
Tomoe: S-same. I haven't done anything...
Ran: ... She's not the type to get mad in the first place, no matter what happens.
Himari: Y-yeah. I mean, this is the kindhearted Tsugu we're talking about. She helps me with my homework and even listens to me ramble... She can't be angry...
Tsugumi: ...
Tomoe: I've never seen Tsugu like this before, though...
Ran: ... She might really be mad...
Moca: Hii-chan. I think you should apologize just in case~.
Himari: Huh?! Why just me?! Shouldn't we all apologize~?
Tsugumi: (We're playing our old songs and the new song really well, but... I feel like we can do even better...)
Tsugumi: (Yes, I should bring it up with everyone. It concerns the band, so it's better if we all think about it!)
Tsugumi: (That leaves our newest song...)
Himari: Ts-Tsugu!
Tsugumi: Ah! Oh, Himari-chan. Sorry, gu-
Himari: We're sorry! We're not sure what we did, but whatever it was, please don't be mad~!
Tsugumi: ...? Umm, mad...? Me?
Tomoe: Yeah. You haven't said a word, and you've had that frown on your face for a while now...
Moca: So we thought you were furious with us~... Were we wrong~?
Tsugumi: ... Pfft. Fufu, ahahaha! I'm not angry at all~!
Himari: ...! Wh-what a relief~!
Moca: Jeez~. This is all your fault for making us freak out, Tomo-chin~.
Tomoe: I mean, well... I've never seen Tsugu like that... Right, Ran?
Ran: I-I wasn't freaking out... What was with that look anyway, Tsugumi?
Tsugumi: Fufu, I've been thinking about the songs we just played...
Ran & Moca & Himari & Tomoe: ...
Tsugumi: There are a few things I want us to try out. So... Will you hear me out?
Himari: ... Tsugu...?
Tomoe: Y-you're really fired up...
Moca: That's some highly Tsugurific stuff~.
Tsugumi: Umm, did I say something that weird...?
Himari: N-no, it wasn't weird or anything... It's just that you don't usually have suggestions for how we should do songs...
Ran: ... Which makes me all the more curious. I want to hear what you have to say, Tsugumi.
Tsugumi: Thank you! Then I want to talk about "Tied to the Skies" first...
