event18-02 Chapter 1: Emergency Meeting

Feeling uncomfortable after listening to her classmate, Misaki calls an emergency meeting...

(Character: Kokoro Tsurumaki, Kaoru Seta, Hagumi Kitazawa, Kanon Matsubara, Misaki Okusawa)

[Hanasakigawa Girls' Academy - Class 1C]

Misaki: I mean, she's not a bad person or anything. It wouldn't be fair to just brush her off...
Misaki's Classmate A: Hmm, I guess you're right... Oh, Tsurumaki-san is in a band, right?
Misaki: Ah, umm...
Misaki: (It'll just sound suspicious if I said I didn't know that...)
Misaki: Y-yeah, now that you mention it, she said something about it~. I think they're called Hello, Happy... something...
Misaki's Classmate B: Yeah, Hello, Happy World! What a weird name. Kaoru-sama from Haneoka is in the band too, right?
Misaki's Classmate C: I heard one of the members wears a giant-bear suit too!
Misaki: O-ohh~, really? How interesting!
Misaki: So... uh, have you guys ever seen one of their concerts...?
Misaki's Classmate A: No, I haven't. I'm too scared.
Misaki's Classmate B: I know what you mean. It's Tsurumaki-san's band, after all. Who knows what she'll do on stage... I'm scared of the idea too. I'm a fan of Kaoru-sama, but...
Misaki: ...
Misaki: (I don't know why, but... it kinda bugs me when they say bad things about Hello, Happy World!)
Misaki: (They haven't even seen us play... Isn't this a bit unfair?)
Misaki's Classmate C: I bet it's going to be a really strange band~. I mean, they have a person in a bear suit. It's not funny, it's just weird.
Misaki's Classmate A: Right? Tsurumaki-san is weird in general, after all.
Misaki: ... U-umm!
Misaki: Apparently, they're not bad, you know? Uh... one of my friends said their shows are kinda fun...
Misaki: M-maybe we shouldn't pass judgment until we see them for ourselves...
Misaki: Oh! I think she said something about a show two weeks from now~. Why don't you guys go and check it out? The bear might not be as weird as you think when you see it for yourself...
Misaki's Classmate A: O-oh, really...?
Misaki's Classmate C: I-if Okusawa-san says so, then... wanna give it a try?
Misaki's Classmate B: If I get to see Kaoru-sama on the guitar, sure, why not...?
Misaki: Y-yeah, you guys totally should!  I'll find the details for you!
Misaki: Okay, I'm gonna get going!
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Hanasakigawa Girls' Academy - School Gate]

Misaki: *sigh*...
Misaki: Emergency! Meet at Hazawa Coffee... That should do it.
Misaki: Okay, sent.
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Hazawa Coffee]

Misaki: Sorry for suddenly calling everyone here.
Kanon: What's the matter, Misaki-chan? I thought we had rehearsal tomorrow...
Hagumi: You said it was an emergency, so I said sorry to my dad and ran out of the shop!
Kokoro: I know! You have an important message from Michelle!
Kaoru: Misaki, stop being dramatic and tell us. Uh... what is that Michelle needs to tell us...?
Kanon: A-ahaha...
Hagumi: C'mon, tell us, Mii-kun~!
Misaki: Okay, okay, calm down.
Misaki: ... Ahem. Listen up, everyone.
Kokoro & Kaoru: ...
Hagumi & Kanon: ...
Misaki: We're having a performance, and it's going to be in two weeks from!
