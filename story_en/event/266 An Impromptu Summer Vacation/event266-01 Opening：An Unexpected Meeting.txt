event266-01 Opening: An Unexpected Meeting

Sayo and Moca run into each other at a convenience store in the early morning. Just then, someone calls out to the two of them, still dazed from an all-nighter—

(Character: Moca Aoba, Kokoro Tsurumaki, Sayo Hikawa)

Sayo: (Blue skies... white clouds... a sea that stretches out as far as one can see...)
Sayo: (And now we’re surrounded by a feast, being treated like honored guests...)
Sayo: (Why... is this happening...)
(White cut)
(Background change)

[Convenience Store]

Sayo: (I know things were hectic, but still... I can't believe I ended up pulling an all-nighter...)
Sayo: (Maybe it just means that request is weighing on my mind more than I realized...)
Sayo: (A roundtable with several other band guitarists... What could I possibly contribute, still being so new to the professional scene...?)
Sayo: ...Sigh. Sitting here brooding won’t help. Maybe some coffee will—
Moca: Oh?~ Isn’t that Sayo-san?
Sayo: ! Aoba-san...?
Moca: Fancy seeing you at this hour. You’re up early~...
Sayo: Not really, I pulled an all-nighter... What about you, Aoba-san?
Moca: Man~, I started reading a manga and couldn't stop. Thought I'd take a walk to clear my head~.
Sayo: I see. Life is full of strange coincidences...
Moca: For sure. Oh, I think I’ll grab some coffee too.
(Black cut)
Moca: Still working, Sayo-san~?
Sayo: No, I’ve finished everything urgent, so nothing pressing for now.
Moca: Oh~, then you’re officially on summer break~.
Sayo: Well, I still have practice and plenty to do, so not exactly carefree.
Moca: In that case, you really can't afford to stay up all night~...
Sayo: Right, we both need to be careful...
Moca & Sayo: Yawn...
Sayo: Oh, excuse me...
Moca: No no, pulling an all-nighter really isn’t a good idea after all~...
Sayo: I know... Hm? Is that person in the car waving at us...?
Kokoro: I knew it! That’s Moca and Sayo!
Moca & Sayo: Kokoro-chan...? Tsurumaki-san...!?
Kokoro: Were you two heading somewhere?
Sayo: No, I was just on my way home...
Kokoro: I see! In that case, let me give you a ride home!
Sayo: That’s kind of you, but... weren’t you on your way somewhere too...?
Kokoro: I was! But a little detour won't hurt!
Moca: If you’re offering, Kokoro-chan, then I’ll happily take you up on that~...
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Tsurumaki Vehicle]

Sayo: Tsurumaki-san, what were you doing out at this hour...?
Kokoro: I was heading to the airport for a trip!
Moca: A trip, huh... Sounds like summer vacation to me~...
Kokoro: That’s right! Do you two have any plans for summer break?
Moca: Hmm... Not really, I guess~... Just taking it easy, like always...
Sayo: Same here... I don’t have anything in particular planned...
Sayo: (Though I still haven’t responded to that interview request...)
Kokoro: Oh, I know! If you don’t have any plans yet, why not go on a vacation together?
Moca: A vacation, huh~... That could be nice~...
Sayo: Yeah... Somewhere quiet, to unwind...
Kokoro: A quiet getaway, then? In that case, I think the beach would be perfect!
Moca: Sounds great~. Just relaxing on a beach chair, listening to the waves...
Kokoro: Exactly! And a beach barbecue would be so much fun!
Sayo: A barbecue... That does sound nice once in a while...
Moca: Meat, fish... I’d want some bread too~...
Sayo: Then we should definitely have fries on the side...
Kokoro: That sounds perfect!
Sayo: A beach barbecue... Brings back memories of last year...
Moca: Ah, yeah, it really does~...
Kokoro: You two had a barbecue together before?
Sayo: Yes, last year with our respective bands at the beach...
Kokoro: Oh wow! That sounds absolutely wonderful!
Moca: Stuff like fireworks really feel like youth huh~...
Kokoro: Fireworks! That’s a fantastic idea too!
Moca & Sayo: Yawn...
Kokoro: Oh, feeling sleepy? It’s okay, you can take a nap if you need to.
Moca: Thanks~ I think I will...
Sayo: Me too, if you don’t mind...
Kokoro: Of course! Just look forward to it!
(Background change)
Kokoro: Alright, we’re here!
Moca & Sayo: ......What?
