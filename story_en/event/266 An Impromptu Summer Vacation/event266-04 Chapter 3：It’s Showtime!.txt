event266-04 Chapter 3: It’s Showtime!

Led by Kokoro, the group finds themselves out at sea, where they meet some unexpected “friends”...

(Character: Moca Aoba, Kokoro Tsurumaki, Sayo Hikawa, Mashiro Kurata, Toko Kirigaya)

[On the Sea]

Kokoro: We've arrived!
Toko: Wait, arrived...? We're still out at sea, right?
Sayo: Tsurumaki-san, weren't we going to see an animal show? I assumed we were headed to an aquarium or something...
Kokoro: Oh, did I forget to mention? I’ve got some friends here!
Mashiro: Friends?
Moca: Hmm, I see something over there... Could it be...
Toko: Whoa! The dolphins are gathering!
Sayo: No way, your friends are—
Kokoro: It's been a while, everyone!
Dolphins: Eee!
Mashiro: Wow, they're actually greeting her...
Kokoro: I brought my friends with me today! I’d be so happy if you gave them a warm welcome!
Dolphins: Eee-eee!
Mashiro: Ah, the dolphins are forming a circle...!
Moca: I can feel it... A real dolphin show’s about to start~.
Toko: Whoa, that jump was insane!
Moca: Beautiful~.
Sayo: Such precise, coordinated movements... Truly impressive.
Mashiro: So sleek and cool...
Toko: Ah, they're all coming this way!
Kokoro: Go ahead, give them a handshake if you'd like!
Mashiro: Really...?
Kokoro: Of course! I’m sure they’ll love it!
Moca: Oh~, They’re even lifting their fins to make it easy~. Well then, excuse me...
Toko: Wow, they're all such sweethearts! Let's take some selfies!
Sayo: Please be careful not to fall in.
Mashiro: Fufu, they’re so cute...
Sayo: Yes, absolutely adorable.
Kokoro: Thank you so much, everyone! Let’s play again sometime!
Dolphins: Eee!
Moca: Bye-bye~.
Mashiro: I can’t believe wild dolphins put on a show for us...
Sayo: Yeah, that was something special.
Kokoro: Fufu, but the show’s not over yet.
Sayo & Mashiro: Huh?
Moca: Hmm...? Something’s swimming this way...
Toko: Wait, isn’t that way bigger than a dolphin!?
Moca: And that splash...
Sayo: Could it be... a whale!?
Toko: No way, that really is a pod of whales!
Moca: Don’t tell me... these whales too?
Kokoro: That’s right! They’re my friends too! They came to put on a show!
Sayo: Whales... doing a show!?
Mashiro: Ah, they’re lining up...
Moca: Oh~, The one on the far right just spouted water~.
Mashiro: Now the next one... Wow, they’re taking turns perfectly...
Sayo: Does this mean they’re communicating somehow...?
Toko: Whoa, are they forming a circle now!?
Mashiro: Wah!?
Toko: Whoa! They all spouted at the same time!
Moca: Oh~, Totally in sync~. What a performance~.
Sayo: You're right, this really is a whale show...
Mashiro: It’s like something out of a picture book...
Kokoro: Fufu. Mashiro, your eyes are sparkling!
Mashiro: It’s just... so beautiful...
Mashiro: The way the spray from the whales catches the sunlight... It’s like they’re dancing with fairies made of light...
Mashiro: —Ah, sorry, that was weird of me to say.
Sayo: Not at all. That was a beautiful image. I was genuinely moved.
Moca: Shiro-chan, you’ve got quite the poet in you~.
Kokoro: Yes, I could see it too!
Mashiro: N-no, really, it’s nothing—
Toko: Right!? Shiro’s got a great sense for this kind of thing!
Sayo: Fufu. Why are you the one sounding proud, Kirigaya-san?
Mashiro: Y-you’re embarrassing me, Toko-chan...
