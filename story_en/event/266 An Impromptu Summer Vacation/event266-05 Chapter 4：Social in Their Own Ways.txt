event266-05 Chapter 4: Social in Their Own Ways

Just when Mashiro starts to calm down, her nerves return. But their vacation only grows more extravagant...

(Character: Moca Aoba, Kokoro Tsurumaki, Sayo Hikawa, Mashiro Kurata, Toko Kirigaya)

[The Next Morning]

[A Hotel Room]

Mashiro: (Everything that happened yesterday was so overwhelming that I went numb... But thinking about it now, I’ve barely ever talked to Sayo-san or Moca-san before...)
Mashiro: (Did I say anything rude...? Will I be able to talk to them properly today...?)
Mashiro: (...Ugh, now I’m getting nervous just thinking about it... Maybe I should pretend to be sick...)
Sayo: This is Hikawa. Kurata-san, are you awake?
Mashiro: Eh!? S-Sayo-san? I’ll open up right now!
Sayo: Good morning. Sorry for the sudden visit. You weren’t in the lounge, so I was a bit concerned.
Mashiro: Ah, um, that’s...
Mashiro: (Sayo-san came to check on me... B-But... I can’t tell her I didn’t want to go because I was too nervous... O-Oh! I’ve got it!)
Mashiro: A-Actually, I’m having some stomach troubles...
Sayo: I see. That explains it.
Mashiro: So I hope everyone else can just enjoy the day without me—
Sayo: In that case, please take this.
Mashiro: ...Um, what’s this...?
Sayo: I too, embarrassingly enough, overate yesterday... My stomach wasn’t feeling well, and someone local gave me this secret remedy.
Mashiro: A s-secret remedy...!?
Sayo: As the saying goes, “Good medicine tastes bitter.” It has a very unique flavor and an indescribable smell, but I guarantee it works!
Mashiro: N-No! I suddenly feel totally fine!
Sayo: That can’t be true. You look even paler than before.
Mashiro: W-Well, that’s because...
Sayo: When it comes to these things, early treatment is key. Now, drink up!
Mashiro: U-Ugh...
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Hotel Lounge]

Toko: Oh, Shiro, you finally woke up! ...Huh? You don’t look too hot. Come to think of it, didn’t Sayo-san mention some secret remedy—
Mashiro: Eh!? N-No, I’m totally fine...!
Kokoro: Everyone’s here! Let’s have a party at my villa today!
Sayo: A party at your villa? Are you sure it’s okay for us to join?
Kokoro: Of course! You’re all my friends, so don’t worry!
Moca: So we’re all Kokoro-chan’s friends, huh... I feel like some of them might be crazy important people.
Mashiro: I-Is it really okay for us to be somewhere like this...?
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Tsurumaki Residence - Villa]

Mashiro: E-Eek~...
Sayo: It’s somehow just as outrageous as I imagined... which is what I imagined, I guess.
Moca: This is less a villa and more a castle~. And there’s a ton of VIP-looking people here too.
Kokoro: I’m going to chat with my friends, so everyone, enjoy yourselves however you like!
Moca: I don’t really know how you’re supposed to enjoy a place like this, but let’s start with the buffet~.
Sayo: That sounds good. It seems Tsurumaki-san has some greetings to attend to.
Mashiro: Hey, Toko-chan... I don’t think I can eat anything in a place like this...
Toko: Huh, Shiro, you nervous? Just act normal and you’ll be fine, right?
Participant: Oh my, could that be Kirigaya-san?
Toko: Eh? Oh, long time no see! What a crazy coincidence, running into you here!
Participant: The fact that you’re here too... does that mean you’re acquainted with Tsurumaki-san?
Toko: Yes, Kokoro-san and I are best friends!
Mashiro: Toko-chan, you’re amazing...
Sayo: She’s holding her own with such poise...
Moca: Easy to forget, but Toko-chan’s also pretty high-class herself~. She’s totally in her element here~.
Toko: Man, I’m still shocked.
Mashiro: Was that someone you knew just now?
Toko: Yeah, she’s an acquaintance of my grandma. I once went with her to a company founding party as her guest.
Moca: Now that sounds super VIP~.
Sayo: As expected, Kirigaya-san is very comfortable in this kind of place.
Moca: Totally. She fit right in~.
Toko: Oh, really? Maybe that’s thanks to my grandma being super strict with etiquette!
Sayo: Lately I’ve been meeting more and more people through work... I should learn from you, Kirigaya-san.
Toko: In that case, let me teach you! I’ll be your sensei next time!
Moca & Mashiro: Ahaha~. Fufu.
Kokoro: Oh my, you all look like you’re having fun!
Moca: Nice work, Kokoro-chan~. You’ve been talking to people nonstop.
Kokoro: Yes, there are people I can only see on occasions like this— Ah! It’s Smiley Nick!
Mashiro: She ran off again...
Toko: Wait, is Smiley Nick, like, a relative?
Moca: Hmm, doesn’t look like a relative. But man, he’s got an air of authority, huh~
Sayo: That face... I’ve seen it somewhere before... Ah!
Toko: Sayo-san!?
Sayo: There’s no mistake! That man is... the King of the Kingdom of Happinelle! I saw him on the news before!
Mashiro & Toko: The kiiiing!?
Toko: Ahaha! Kokoro-san’s friends with a king. She’s really something else!
Sayo: So this is the kind of grand stuff the Hello, Happy World! crew always gets caught up in...
Moca: Man~, Even Moca-chan’s blown away by this one.
