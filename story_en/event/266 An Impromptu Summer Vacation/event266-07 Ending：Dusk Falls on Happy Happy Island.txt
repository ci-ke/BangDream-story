event266-07 Ending: Dusk Falls on Happy Happy Island

As their dreamlike vacation nears its end, Toko suggests they finish it off with fireworks.

(Character: Moca Aoba, Kokoro Tsurumaki, Sayo Hikawa, Mashiro Kurata, Toko Kirigaya)

[Happy Happy Island]

Sayo: It’s getting pretty late. We should start getting ready to head back soon.
Moca: Time really flies when you're having fun, huh~.
Mashiro: Yeah... It feels kind of sad...
Toko: Why’s everyone getting all sentimental? Our vacation’s not over yet!
Sayo: Kirigaya-san?
Toko: Let’s go out with a bang and light up some fireworks!
Moca: Ooh, nice idea, Toko-chan. Fireworks on the beach really take me back~.
Sayo: You're right. Does that mean you’ve brought fireworks with you?
Mashiro: But I don't think there's any around here...
Toko: What? I already took care of that! Look, everyone, over there!
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Mashiro: Wow...!
Sayo: So you meant those kind of fireworks... launch fireworks.
Moca: They're beautiful~.
Sayo: Kirigaya-san, you arranged all this...?
Toko: Yes! I called in a favor from my family and asked a fireworks pro to help out!
Kokoro: Yeah! You all said you wanted to try fireworks, so I thought this would be perfect!
Moca: Man~, this is seriously professional-grade stuff~.
Mashiro: The stars over the island and the fireworks are blending together... It’s like flower petals floating through the sky...!
Kokoro: It’s such a wonderful sight! I bet everyone on Happy Happy Island is smiling too!
Toko: Ahaha, I hope so! Think of it as our way of saying thanks for this amazing vacation!
Sayo: Fufu. In that case, we should be the ones saying thank you.
Moca: For sure~. Thanks for inviting us on such an incredible vacation.
Kokoro: No, thank you! Seeing you and Sayo smiling made me so happy!
Moca: And Toko-chan, Shiro-chan, thank you for hanging out with us too~.
Sayo: Yes, thanks to you both, I was able to enjoy this vacation even more.
Toko: Ahaha! Hey, the feeling’s mutual! I had a total blast too!
Mashiro: I’d like to say thank you as well. Thanks to all of you, I’ve made some truly wonderful memories...!
Kokoro: You’re very welcome! Thank you too, Toko and Mashiro, for enjoying Happy Happy Island with us!
Mashiro: Ehehe...
Toko: See? Aren’t you glad you came? These things are all about jumping in when the mood hits.
Moca: Well then, for our grand finale, should we do that classic launch firework thing?
Mashiro: That...?
Toko: Oh, I got it! You mean shouting “Tamaya!” right?
Moca: Bingo~.
Kokoro: Sounds great! Let’s shout loud enough to reach the fireworks in the sky!
Mashiro: Loud enough to reach the sky...? I-I’ll try...
Toko: Shiro, show them the power of Morfonica’s vocalist! Not that I’m gonna lose, though!
Sayo: Honestly... When did this become a competition?
Kokoro: Here we go! Ready, and...!
All: Taaamaaaayaaaa!!
(Black cut)
Sayo: ...What a truly beautiful sight...
Moca: Oh~, So this is where you were, Sayo-san.
Sayo: Aoba-san. Yes, I just came to get a drink.
Moca: Then I’ll join you~.
Sayo: Of course, go ahead.
Moca: ...Man~, Looking back, these two days were really something else.
Sayo: Fufu, they really were...
Sayo: At first, I felt nothing but confusion. But before I knew it, I found myself truly enjoying it.
Moca: That’s probably thanks to Kokoro-chan and Toko-chan’s ability to drag us all into the fun~.
Sayo: Yes. And I’m glad Kurata-san, who was in a similar position to ours, ended up enjoying herself too.
Moca: Totally~. I mean, without this trip, there’s no way this group would’ve ever spent so much time together.
Moca: If someone had told me ahead of time what we’d be doing, I probably wouldn’t have come. I guess we really owe Kokoro-chan for her wild ideas~.
Sayo: True. Though... I do hope she goes a little easier on us next time.
Sayo: Still... Maybe it’s because this whole experience was so unexpected that I got to spend such meaningful time with you.
Moca: Oh? Did our bond level just go up~?
Sayo: Fufu. Teasing me like that... It feels like such an “Aoba-san” thing to do now.
Moca: Oh no, I’ve been outplayed~.
Sayo: —Ah, that’s right.
Moca: Something up~?
Sayo: There was something I’d been unsure about trying... But now, I think I’m ready to give it a shot.
Moca: Oh~. Sounds like someone’s had a change of heart?
Sayo: Yes. After everything that happened, I thought... maybe diving into the unknown isn’t so bad after all.
Moca: I get that~. Though, this time it was less diving in and more getting thrown in before you noticed.
Sayo: Fufu, you might be right about that.
Toko: You two~! Next up is the grand finale starmine!
Sayo: Kirigaya-san... Okay, we’re coming!
Moca: Let’s go then~.
Sayo: Yes, it’s our vacation, after all. Let’s enjoy it all the way to the end.
(White cut)
