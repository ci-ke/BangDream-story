event260-03 Chapter 2: Let’s Go Shopping!

On a supply run, the group's bond grows with every shared task…

(Character: Kaoru Seta, Hagumi Kitazawa, Mashiro Kurata, Nanami Hiromachi, Rei Wakana)

[Shopping Mall Food Court]

LAYER (Rei): Oh, everyone’s already here. Did I keep you waiting?
Mashiro: N-no, we just got here too...
Nanami: Kaoru-senpai isn’t here yet either, so no worries~
Hagumi: ReiRei, let’s do our best with the shopping today!
LAYER (Rei): U-uh... wait, ReiRei...? Are you calling me that?
Hagumi: Yup! Oh, unless... Would you prefer Reiyan, like how I call Mashiron and Nanamin?
LAYER (Rei): N-no, it’s not that, but... why so sudden?
Hagumi: Because ReiRei sounds cuter than LAYER-san!
LAYER (Rei): I-I see...?
Nanami: Maybe this means Hagumi-senpai feels closer to LAYER-senpai now~
LAYER (Rei): S-so that’s it... Then... should I call her by a nickname too?
Hagumi: Now we just need Kaoru-kun, and we’re good to go!
LAYER (Rei): Ah, um... Hagumi-chan, didn’t you come here with Seta-san?
Hagumi: Nope! I asked her to come with me, but she said she had something to take care of first and would meet us later!
Mashiro: Something to take care of... Well, Kaoru-san’s performing on so many stages— she must be super busy... Ugh, shopping with a celebrity... I’m getting nervous...
Hagumi: Mashiron, you okay?
LAYER (Rei): Oh, it’s just... Come to think of it, you looked really nervous the last time you talked to Seta-san too, didn’t you?
Nanami: Shiro-chan didn’t really know much about Kaoru-senpai before, but recently she realized how incredible she is from seeing her perform on big stages, and now she’s all nervous.
Mashiro: Of course I’m nervous... I’ve heard so many stories about Kaoru-san from Tsukinomori fans...
LAYER (Rei): Stories...?
Mashiro: Yeah, like... People fainting from her wink during a live show,  or crowds forming around her just from walking down the street...
LAYER (Rei): Th-that’s gotta be just rumors... right?
Hagumi: Both of those are true! I saw it happen with my own eyes!
LAYER (Rei): Wha...?
Mashiro: S-so... it’s all real...!
LAYER (Rei): Hagumi-chan, don’t tease the juniors too much—
Nanami: ...? Hey, it’s getting noisy over by the entrance.
Mashiro: M-maybe it’s Kaoru-san...!
LAYER (Rei): N-no way... It couldn’t be—
Hagumi: Ah! It really is Kaoru-kun! Kaoru-kuuun! Over here, over here~!
Kaoru: Ah, Hagumi. So this is where you were. Sorry, I got delayed— I was surrounded by some little kittens on my way here. I'll be right over.
Hagumi: Okay! We’ll wait for you here~
LAYER (Rei): S-She’s really surrounded by fans...
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Shopping Mall – General Goods Store]

Mashiro: We got the cellophane for the lights, and the flower reservation is done... That’s everything Marina-san asked us to get.
Kaoru: Now we just need to pick up the things for our own preparations... Oh?
LAYER (Rei): Nanami-chan, Hagumi... What are you two looking at?
Hagumi: Oh, ReiRei! Look at what we just found!
LAYER (Rei): A picture book?
Nanami: Not just any picture book— look, if you pull this tab on the side...
LAYER (Rei): Whoa, the pictures pop up! Hehe, these are so fun, aren’t they?
Nanami: Wouldn’t it be fun to make something like this?
Hagumi: ReiRei, did you have books like this when you were a kid?
LAYER (Rei): Me? Not really... W-what about you, Hagumin...-chan?
Hagumi: Hagumi did~!
Mashiro: Hey, Nanami-chan... Did you notice?
Nanami: Yeah, LAYER-senpai’s been trying to call her Hagumin this whole time. Hehe, she always seems so cool, but she has an adorable side too.
Mashiro: Hehe, totally.
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[After Shopping]

Nanami: Oh, LAYER-senpai, let me carry that bag for you~
LAYER (Rei): Thanks, Nanami-chan. Where’s everyone else?
Nanami: They went ahead to the next store. Shiro-chan shared her idea for some decorations, and Kaoru-senpai and Hagumi-senpai got all excited about it...
LAYER (Rei): I see. Thanks for waiting for me. Well then, shall we head over too?
Nanami: We bought quite a lot, huh? Good thing there are five of us.
LAYER (Rei): Yeah. Plus, shopping together like this... It’s kinda exciting. Feels a bit like preparing for a school festival, doesn’t it?
Nanami: Oh! Shiro-chan said the same thing the other day.
LAYER (Rei): Really? I’ve always admired that... I never really got to experience a proper school festival myself, so doing something like this with everyone feels so special.
Nanami: Wait... you’ve never participated properly? How come?
LAYER (Rei): Well... The timing just never worked out for me.
LAYER (Rei): A lot of times, my support gigs would clash with the festival, so I couldn’t join the preparations or even attend on the day.
LAYER (Rei): When you’re only half-involved like that, it’s hard to share that ‘We did it!’ feeling with the classmates who worked their hearts out. You can’t help but feel a bit apart from them.
LAYER (Rei): I mean, I chose to do support work, and I don’t regret that. But... Not being able to share that same feeling with my classmates— That part... felt a little lonely.
Nanami: Ah... I think... I know how that feels.
(White cut)

(Memory cut-in)

(Background change)
Middle School Classmate A: Huh? Hiromachi-san, you already finished your assigned tasks?
Middle School Classmate B: We’re doing the same job, but I’m nowhere near finished yet! Even though I stayed late yesterday too...
Nanami: (I wasn’t trying to rush— I was just working at my own pace... But somehow, I finished faster than everyone else.)
Nanami: (Even at the school festival... I felt out of sync with everyone.)
Middle School Classmate A: Haaah—! The festival’s finally over! Great job, everyone!
Middle School Classmate B: It was tough, but totally worth it! We really gave it our all, didn’t we?
Nanami: (The festival ended... before I ever felt like I’d truly given it my all.)
Nanami: (When everyone said, ‘We did it!’— I couldn’t quite share that feeling. And that... left me feeling a little lonely.)
(White cut)

(Memory cut-out)

(Background change)
LAYER (Rei): Nanami-chan...?
Nanami: ...Ah, sorry. I kind of spaced out for a moment.
LAYER (Rei): You okay?
Nanami: Yeah.
LAYER (Rei): I see... But you know, I wasn’t expecting you to relate to what I said, so that made me a little happy.
Nanami: Hehe, me too~. Plus, I realized LAYER-senpai has a surprisingly cute side.
LAYER (Rei): Eh? What do you mean...?
Nanami: Well, since Hagumi-senpai calls everyone by nicknames, LAYER-senpai was trying to call her by a nickname too, right?
LAYER (Rei): Ah...! You noticed that...?
Nanami: Yup! Shiro-chan noticed it too~. We were both saying how it’s cute because you’re usually so cool.
LAYER (Rei): E-even Mashiro-chan...? Ugh, now it’s going to feel a bit awkward the next time I see them...
