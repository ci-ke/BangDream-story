event260-05 Chapter 4: Ready to Go…?

On the day before the wedding, excitement fills the air—will their efforts bring joy?

(Character: Kaoru Seta, Hagumi Kitazawa, Mashiro Kurata, Nanami Hiromachi, Rei Wakana)

[The Day Before the Wedding]

[Hiromachi Residence – Atelier]

Nanami: ...Alright! With this, we’ve got everything we need for tomorrow’s preparations.
Hagumi: Yay! Great job, everyone!
LAYER (Rei): Hehe, it took us right up to the day before the wedding, but we made it in time. That’s what counts.
Kaoru: Fufu, I just finished my speech draft yesterday, too. I can’t wait to share it with everyone.
LAYER (Rei): Now all that’s left is to finish decorating at CiRCLE tomorrow, and we’ll be ready.
Mashiro: We have to get there pretty early tomorrow, don’t we...? Ugh, I hope I can wake up on time...
Hagumi: Don’t worry, Mashiron! If you can’t wake up, I’ll come get you!
Mashiro: I-I’ll be fine, really...
LAYER (Rei): Oh yeah, did everyone see the photo of the wedding cake?
Nanami: You mean the one Yuka-san sent? It was huge! I was so surprised!
Kaoru: Yeah! It was about as tall as we are. The cake-cutting is definitely going to be a showstopper.
Hagumi: I actually suggested decorating the cake with croquettes... But they told me, ‘Maybe next time.’ Too bad...
Mashiro: Ahaha...
Nanami: Hehe, I hope it’ll be a wonderful wedding.
Hagumi: It totally will be! Our gifts for the couple are absolutely perfect!
Kaoru: For this special occasion, I’ve prepared a poetry collection titled ‘Fleetingly Forever: For Wedding’, filled with poems wishing them eternal happiness.
LAYER (Rei): Hehe, since you all held poetry readings together, I’m sure they’ll be thrilled with your original poetry collection.
Mashiro: LAYER-san’s photo frame is absolutely stunning too. And I hope they’ll like the pair of glasses I picked out~.
Mashiro: (Though... my mom helped me choose them...)
Nanami: Hagumi-senpai’s gift was croquettes, right? You said you’d have them delivered to CiRCLE, didn’t you?
Hagumi: Yep! I asked my dad to make a special batch! Yuka-senpai told me she loved them when she visited our place before!
Hagumi: But... the real masterpiece is definitely Nanamin’s pop-up storybook!
Kaoru: Truly, even my poetry collection couldn't hold a candle in comparison.
Mashiro: Is the book already finished?
Nanami: Yep, it’s all done~ I left it in the storage room.
Kaoru: Great! Let’s take everything to CiRCLE and start decorating. We should definitely bring the book too— it’s the highlight of our gifts.
LAYER (Rei): Absolutely. Nanamin worked so hard on it. We can’t forget it!
Nanami: Okay, I’ll go grab it now~
Nanami: ...Huh?
Hagumi: Nanamin, what’s wrong?
Nanami: The book... It’s gone.
Nanami: And the room’s been tidied up a lot...
Nanami: D-don’t tell me...!
(Black cut)
Nanami: ...It seems like the delivery workers accidentally took the storybook along with my mom’s artwork.
Mashiro: What...?
Kaoru: Can’t we ask them to send it back right away?
Nanami: They said... even if they rush it, it won’t arrive until the day after tomorrow...
All: ............
Hagumi: N-Nanamin worked so hard on that book...
Nanami: ...Please, don’t worry about it. Even without my gift, we still have everyone else’s gifts.
Mashiro: But... weren’t they planning to use it during the wedding?
Nanami: I think it’ll be okay. Yuka-san mentioned they were planning to show a video anyway.
Nanami: So... I’ll just let it go...
Hagumi: No—Don’t give up, Nanamin!!
Nanami: ...! B-but... I spent days working on that book... There’s no way I can finish it again overnight...
Hagumi: Then we’ll help you!! If we all work together— I promise we can finish it in time!
Nanami: But... I can’t trouble you all with this...
Kaoru: No one here would ever see it as trouble.
LAYER (Rei): Yeah! We’ve come this far together. A problem like this? We’ll overcome it together!
Mashiro: I... I don’t know how much help I can be... But I’ll do everything I can.
Nanami: ...!
Hagumi: Nanamin! Let’s finish it together—one more time! I want us all to celebrate the wedding with smiles!
Nanami: Y-yeah...! Thank you... so much...!
Hagumi: Alright! Let’s all give it our best to the very end!
All: Yeah—let’s do it!
