event260-04 Chapter 3: A Hint of Bitterness

Working in the atelier, things go smoothly, but Nanami is worried about something...

(Character: Kaoru Seta, Hagumi Kitazawa, Mashiro Kurata, Nanami Hiromachi, Rei Wakana)

[Hiromachi Residence – Atelier]

Hagumi: Excuse us!
Nanami: Welcome, welcome~
LAYER (Rei): Wow, so this is where Morfonica practices usually. Are you sure it’s okay for us to use it?
Nanami: Of course, no problem~! Next week, Mom’s holding an exhibition overseas, so the storage room’s a bit messy, though...
Mashiro: Yeah, if we were doing the decorations at CiRCLE, it’d probably get in the way of their regular business.
Kaoru: Hehe, surrounded by such delicate works of art... I bet it’ll inspire us to create some truly beautiful wedding decorations.
Hagumi: Yuka-senpai loved the venue concept we all came up with, so let’s give it our best during the preparations!
All: Yeah—let’s do this!
(Black cut)

[During Break]

Nanami: We've made quite a lot of progress in just a single day~ It's going a lot better than I thought it would.
LAYER (Rei): Hehe, it’s all thanks to Hagumi-chan. She's always the first to jump in whenever something came up.
Kaoru: Truly, from running out to buy supplies to double-checking things with Marina-san— you’ve been absolutely everywhere today.
Hagumi: Ehehe, I’m still full of energy! If there’s anything you need, just let me know!
Mashiro: Hagumi-san, you’re amazing... You’re still so energetic, and I’m already completely wiped out...
Hagumi: At times like this, it’s all about effort and grit!
Nanami: Effort and... grit?
Hagumi: Yup! Even when you feel totally exhausted, as long as you have effort and grit, you’ll find your energy again!
Mashiro: Ugh... I don’t think I could ever manage to think like that...
Hagumi: You can do it too, Mashiron! I know you can!
Hagumi: And you know what— when you push through your limits with effort and grit, you get this incredible feeling of accomplishment— like, ‘I did it!’
LAYER (Rei): Hehe, I think... I kind of understand what Hagumi-chan means.
Kaoru: Yeah. Whether it’s acting on stage or performing live— that sense of achievement is the same.
Nanami: (So... everyone has felt that before...)
Nanami: Hey, hey... Shiro-chan. Do you get what they’re talking about?
Mashiro: Huh? W-well... I kinda feel like I hit my limit really easily...
Mashiro: But... I do get that feeling of accomplishment— like when you think, ‘There’s no way I can do this,’ and then... somehow, you actually pull it off.
Nanami: ...I see.
Nanami: (So, it’s like during the school festival preparations... There’s something everyone feels that I just couldn’t fully relate to back then.)
Nanami: (I wonder... what it really feels like to push past your limits. Would I finally understand that ‘I did it!’ feeling then...?)
Hagumi: ...Huh? Nanamin— Is that a picture book over there?
Kaoru: It looks like it’s still unfinished... Is it your parents’ work?
Nanami: Ah, actually... I’m preparing it for the wedding day. I’m planning to use it during the ceremony.
LAYER (Rei): Really?
Nanami: I got this idea from that pop-up book we saw when we went shopping together.
Nanami: I asked Yuka-san about how they met and their special memories together, and I thought... why not turn it into a storybook?
Hagumi: Wow! That’s such a wonderful idea!
Nanami: I’m making it as a gift, but when I told Yuka-san about it, she said she’d love to use it to introduce their love story during the wedding.
Mashiro: Hehe, The couple in the story looks so happy together.
Nanami: Yeah, it’s not finished yet, but I’m planning to work on it little by little and have it ready by the big day.
Hagumi: Oh! I know! I should prepare something too!
Nanami: Hey, how about we give our gifts together? We could all prepare something as a group!
LAYER (Rei): That’s a lovely idea. In that case, I’ll think about what I can prepare.
Kaoru: Hmm... I’ll need to find a gift that’s just as special as this beautiful storybook.
Mashiro: Ugh... But what do you even give for something like this...?
Hagumi: As long as it comes from the heart, I’m sure anything will be perfect, Mashiron!
Mashiro: R-right... I’ll do my best...
Nanami: Alright then, once we’ve all decided what to give, let’s bring everything together here. I’ll let Marina-san know about the plan too.
Hagumi: Alright! Break time’s over— let’s get back to work!
