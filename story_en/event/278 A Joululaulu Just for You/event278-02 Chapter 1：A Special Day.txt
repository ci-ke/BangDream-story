event278-02 Chapter 1: A Special Day

As rehearsals begin, Hina and Eve talk about their Christmas memories.

(Character: Hina Hikawa, Eve Wakamiya)

[Rehearsal Studio]

Hina: Good morning~ ...Huh?
Eve: Good morning, Hina-san!
Hina: You're early, Eve-chan. I thought I’d be the first one here.
Eve: Ehehe, the event details, plan, and setlist are all decided, so...
Eve: Now that our lessons for the Christmas event are starting, I got too excited to stay home and came early!
Eve: This is where the real work for the Christmas event begins!
Hina: Eve-chan, you're super fired up, huh~?
Eve: Yes! It's a very special Christmas that we get to spend with our fans, after all.
Hina: Christmas, huh... I love it too.
Hina: Because I have some really precious memories of it with my sister.
Eve: With Sayo-san... I’m sure they’re beautiful memories!
Hina: Ehehe, they really are~.
Eve: Christmas is a special day for me too. After all, I was born in Finland!
Hina: Oh right, Finland’s where Santa lives, isn't it?
Hina: I remember seeing a segment on TV showing Santa Claus Village and all that.
Eve: Yes! For people in Finland, Christmas is one of the most important days of the year.
Eve: On Christmas Day, most stores close and everyone relaxes at home with their families.
Hina: Wow~ That kind of sounds like Japanese New Year~.
Eve: Exactly. About a month before the holiday season starts, people take their time decorating and preparing food while looking forward to the day.
Hina: A whole month!? You start prepping that far ahead?
Eve: Since we don’t rush and take it slow, that timing works out just right.
Hina: I see~ Oh, but in Japan too, once Halloween's over, it’s immediately “Next up—Christmas!” so maybe it’s kind of similar.
Eve: That might be true. But the atmosphere of Christmas in Japan is quite different from Finland.
Eve: Even during the hectic end-of-year rush, people here try to make it a festive time...
Hina: Yeah, Japan’s Christmas feels more like a big citywide celebration~.
Eve: Exactly! Whether you spend it quietly or party with friends, it’s a fun time...
Eve: I really love Christmas for that!
Hina: Come to think of it, Eve-chan always seems a little extra excited around this time of year...
Eve: As expected from you, Hina-san! You noticed! I can’t help but get excited during the Christmas season!
Eve: That’s why I want our upcoming Christmas event to be the most fun, unforgettable day ever!
Hina: Wow~ Eve-chan’s totally fired up~.
Eve: Of course! Our fans chose to spend their special Christmas day with us.
Eve: I want to make it a Christmas event full of smiles and memories they’ll never forget...!
Hina: ...Fufu, yeah.
Hina: And I hope you smile a lot too, Eve-chan.
Eve: I will! Let’s all smile together!
Hina: ...Yeah, we will~.
Hina: By the way, Eve-chan, tell me more about Christmas in Finland.
Hina: What else do people do? What kind of preparations are there?
Eve: Let’s see... In addition to familiar foods from Japan, there are also traditional Finnish dishes.
Eve: For example, there’s Joulukinkku and Piparkakku...
Hina: Joulu? Pipar? Those names are so fun~!
Eve: And then! There’s also a drink called Glögi!
Hina: Whoa~ That sounds really interesting too~.
Hina: Sounds nice~. I’d love to try the Christmas food from Eve-chan’s hometown.
Eve: I'd love for everyone to taste it someday too!
Hina: Hey hey, is there anything else?
Eve: Let’s see... Oh, I’ve never done it myself, but I’ve heard that some people go to the sauna on Christmas...
Hina: A sauna!? Like the ones at bathhouses!? That’s wild~!
(Black cut)
