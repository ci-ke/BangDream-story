event299-07 Ending: With the Waves Behind

Their worries turn out to be needless, but Ran and Arisa’s expressions are bright and refreshed—

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Arisa Ichigaya, Ran Mitake, Himari Uehara, Hagumi Kitazawa)

[Evening]

[Ocean]

Himari: Today was so much fun~!
Hagumi: Yeah! I got to do everything I wanted to at the beach!
Kasumi: Same here! Okay, let’s start cleaning up!
Himari: Oh, Ran, Arisa—you two must be exhausted. You can just take it easy, okay?
Ran: Yeah, thanks...
Arisa: I’ll take you up on that...
Ran: Here, Arisa. Sports drink.
Arisa: Oh, thanks.
Ran: ...You know, thinking back on it now, the whole thing was kinda hilarious.
Arisa: Seriously. If we’d stayed calm, we could’ve called the lifeguards or something...
Ran: They probably would’ve helped us inflate the float at the beach hut too...
Arisa: Oh yeah, totally! ...Didn’t even think of that in the moment.
Ran: ...Sigh. You made it sound like such a brilliant idea, saying we should go rescue them.
Arisa: Wait, you’re blaming me!? You were super into it too, you know!
Ran: Only because you were freaking out...
Arisa: You were freaking out too!
Ran: I was not.
Arisa: Oh yes you were!
Ran & Arisa: ...Ahaha.
Himari: Hey, Ran~! Arisa~!
Ran: What is it?
Himari: You two bought that float together, right? So figure out who's taking it home!
Ran & Arisa: Ah...
Ran: Uh... Arisa can take it home.
Arisa: What!?
Ran: Well, you were the one who wanted to buy it in the first place...
Arisa: No way! We both paid for it in the end!
Arisa: Plus, um~... oh! You should take it, just in case Himari-chan says she wants to go to the beach again.
Ran: If you're saying that, you know Kasumi will probably want to go too.
Ran & Arisa: ............
Arisa: Wait a sec, Ran-chan... Are you trying to dump this on me?
Ran: Ah, you just said “dump it”!
Arisa: No, no, that was just a figure of speech... Okay then, let me ask you—you gonna take it home?
Ran: Absolutely not.
Arisa: Ah! You just said no!
Ran: Sigh... Fine. Let’s settle this with rock-paper-scissors.
Arisa: Got it! Just one round, no take-backs! Don’t you dare say “best of three” later!
Ran: Same goes for you. No whining if you lose.
Arisa: Alright, ready... rock, paper, scissors...
Ran & Arisa: Shoot!
Arisa: ...Yes! I won~! That means you're taking it home, Ran-chan.
Ran: Wait, you totally threw yours late! That doesn’t count!
Arisa: Did not~. Ran-chan, making excuses isn’t cool, you know?
Ran: It’s not an excuse! That match was totally invalid! I mean—
Hagumi: Oh, hey, hey! Hii-chan, Kaa-kun, look over there! Those two are totally fighting about something!
Himari: Huh!? Where, where...?
Himari: Ah, it’s fine! That’s just how Ran gets when she’s with the Afterglow crew!
Kasumi: Arisa’s always like that in the basement too! Look at her, she’s having fun!
Hagumi: I see~! So that means... they’ve gotten super close?
Kasumi & Himari: Exactly!
(Black cut)
