event299-05 Chapter 4: Trouble on the Horizon...?

Ran and Arisa finally relax around each other—only to realize the other three are nowhere to be found...?

(Character: Arisa Ichigaya, Ran Mitake, Himari Uehara)

[Ocean]

Arisa: Huh...? Where did Kasumi and the others go? Ran-chan, did you get in touch with Himari-chan?
Ran: No, I messaged her, but she hasn’t read it yet...
Arisa: I hope they’re just too busy having fun to notice...
Ran: They did say they were gonna play with the floatie, right?
Arisa: Yeah. That thing’s super noticeable—if we look around, we might spot it quickly.
Ran: Right. I see some similar ones, but not Himari’s...
Arisa: Ran-chan! That float washed up on the beach over there...!
Ran: Wait...! That’s Himari’s float...!?
Arisa: It looks just like the one she brought, right...?
Ran: Yeah. That bright pink—no mistaking it. And look at the back...
Arisa: There’s a hole in it...!? You don’t think something happened to the three of them...?
Ran: I-I’m gonna try contacting Himari again!
Arisa: Y-yeah...!
Ran: ...Ah, just got a reply!
Arisa: What’d she say!?
Himari: “Fu, fu, fu~ Guess where we are right now!? Ta-da~!”
Ran & Arisa: ......
Arisa: Sigh~... I’m just glad everything turned out okay~... And Kasumi and the others are in it, too...
Ran: ...Yeah. But where are Himari and the others in this photo?
Arisa: From the background, I don’t think it’s anywhere around here...
Ran: ...Ah.
Arisa: Ran-chan, is something wrong?
Ran: I mean... it probably isn’t, but... Could that tiny island over there be where they are...?
Arisa: Huh? ...Whoa, those rocks are exactly like the ones in the picture.
Ran: Well, at least we know where they are—that’s a relief.
Arisa: ...No, wait.
Ran: What is it now?
Arisa: How do you think they got out there...?
Ran: Uh, I mean, on Himari’s float, right...?
Ran: ...Oh!
Arisa: That’s what I’m saying! If Himari-chan’s float is here now, then...!
Ran: They used the float to get over there—but now it’s drifted back here!?
Arisa: Probably...
Ran: Then how are they gonna get back!?
Arisa: Right!? What should we do—I've gotta contact Himari-chan...!
Ran: I’ll try calling her!
Ran & Arisa: ......
Ran: ...No answer.
Arisa: Maybe she just doesn’t hear it while they’re playing...?
Ran: Let’s hope that’s all. But if her phone’s out of battery, that’s not good. And once Himari notices the float is gone, she’s definitely gonna panic.
Arisa: If that happens, what do you think those three would do...?
Ran: ...Try to swim back.
Arisa: Exactly! I mean, those three are totally capable of doing something like that...
Ran: Yeah, when Himari panics, you never know what she’ll do... And Kasumi tends to just go for it without thinking...
Arisa: And Kitazawa-san is actually capable of swimming back and be like, “Nice idea!”...
Ran: Eh, what do we do...? If it gets dark, it’ll be even more dangerous...
Arisa: ...L-let’s go get them!
Ran: Yeah...! Oh, but how...?
Arisa: Uh... ah! Ran-chan, we have that!
Ran: That?
Arisa: We can explain later! As long as we’ve got that, we should be fine!
Ran: ...Got it. Let’s go, Arisa!
