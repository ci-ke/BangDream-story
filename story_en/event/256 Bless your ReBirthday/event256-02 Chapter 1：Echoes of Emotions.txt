event256-02 Chapter 1: Echoes of Emotions

PAREO struggles with CHU²'s proposal.

(Character: Rei Wakana, Rokka Asahi, Masuki Sato, Reona Nyubara, Chiyu Tamade)

[CHU²’s Apartment Studio]

MASKING (Masuki): What’s that supposed to mean, PAREO?! Do you even understand what you’re saying?!
PAREO (Reona): Y-Yes... Until the Japan Rock Fest practice starts, I was thinking of contributing to RAS not as a keyboardist, but as a manager...
LOCK (Rokka): You mean it’s just temporary, right?!
PAREO (Reona): Well, yes, for now...! As for what comes after, I’m still not sure...
MASKING (Masuki): CHU², PAREO, why are you two deciding something this important all on your own?! We’re supposed to be a team, aren’t we?!
LAYER (Rei): Masuki, calm down for a second...!
MASKING (Masuki): Calm down?! How can I stay calm?!
MASKING (Masuki): This is just... it’s just so sad...!
CHU² (Chiyu): ............
PAREO (Reona): I’m sorry...! But PAREO just can’t keep playing in the band with everyone like this... and that’s why...!
LOCK (Rokka): PAREO-san...
LOCK (Rokka): I think I understand, at least a little, how you feel, PAREO-san. You’ve seemed so troubled during practice lately.
LOCK (Rokka): Deciding to spend some time as a manager... I’m sure it’s a decision you really struggled to make, right?
PAREO (Reona): Yes...
LOCK (Rokka): Then I’ll wait! I’ll wait for you, properly...!
CHU² (Chiyu): For now, let’s start practice. We’ll use programmed keyboard tracks for the time being.
LAYER (Rei): Okay... Got it.
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Residential Area]

MASKING (Masuki): Ugh... I still can’t wrap my head around it.
LAYER (Rei): Masuki, you had that frown on your face the entire practice.
LOCK (Rokka): I could tell PAREO was struggling, but this all feels so sudden...
LAYER (Rei): Yeah, I didn’t expect it to turn out like this either. I think... maybe something shifted for her back then.
LOCK (Rokka): Back then...?
LAYER (Rei): On our way back from hanging out together... Actually, it was on the train—
(Black cut)
LOCK (Rokka): So that’s what happened...
MASKING (Masuki): She wants to stay with everyone, even though she sees things differently from us, huh...?
MASKING (Masuki): She’s always getting caught up in these nitpicky, annoying things to worry about~!
LAYER (Rei): No matter her reason for doing music, I don’t think there’s any reason she doesn’t deserve to be in RAS.
LOCK (Rokka): Exactly! Our goal of ‘Changing the World’ is something we all share...!
LAYER (Rei): But PAREO is seriously worrying about her future too. It’s something only she can figure out...
LAYER (Rei): I’ve been thinking about it ever since that day on the way home, but I just couldn’t come up with anything I could do for her.
MASKING (Masuki): Yeah. No matter what we say, in the end, it’s up to PAREO to decide if she wants to keep doing music or take a different path.
LAYER (Rei): Yeah...
LOCK (Rokka): It’s about her future, so it’s natural for her to worry about it. Especially for someone as serious as PAREO...
LOCK (Rokka): But... if, by any chance, PAREO leaves, what will happen to RAS’s music...?
MASKING (Masuki): LOCK...
LOCK (Rokka): The music I want to make is RAS’s music. Everyone clashes with their sound, refuses to back down, and together it all resonates to create the strongest performance...
LOCK (Rokka): I really believe only this group of members can make that happen. I don’t think we’ll ever come across a miracle like this again...!
LOCK (Rokka): Is there really nothing we can do but just stand by and watch...?
LAYER (Rei): ...We won’t give up.
LOCK (Rokka): Huh?
LAYER (Rei): I said I couldn’t think of anything, but that doesn’t mean I’m ready to give up.
LAYER (Rei): Just like you, I want to keep making music with this group of members. Because RAS’s music is the strongest and the best.
LAYER (Rei): I won’t give up on PAREO. I’m going to fight until the very end.
LOCK (Rokka): LAYER-san... sniff. I’ll do the same!
MASKING (Masuki): Alright... Now I’m fired up!
MASKING (Masuki): You guys, give me your hands.
LAYER (Rei): Our hands?
MASKING (Masuki): Just put them on top of mine, okay?
LOCK (Rokka): Like this?
MASKING (Masuki): Yeah.
MASKING (Masuki): Let’s show PAREO just how stubborn the three of us can be!
LAYER & LOCK: Yeah! Got it!
