event256-06 Chapter 5: The Weight of Tears

The bike heads to Kamogawa. CHU² and PAREO face off with their own thoughts in mind--

(Character: Masuki Sato, Reona Nyubara, Chiyu Tamade)

[Port Town]

MASKING (Masuki): Is here okay for you to get off?
CHU² (Chiyu): Thanks. This is perfect.
MASKING (Masuki): Take care of PAREO... Producer.
CHU² (Chiyu): I will.
PAREO (Reona): CHU²-sama...!
CHU² (Chiyu): PAREO. You’re right on time.
PAREO (Reona): I couldn’t possibly keep CHU²-sama waiting. Um, but... why did you go out of your way to come to Kamogawa...?
CHU² (Chiyu): I wanted to tell you how I feel right now.
PAREO (Reona): !
CHU² (Chiyu): Thank you for letting me hear your performance. No matter what small feelings you might have, it came across clearly that you want to make music with RAS.
CHU² (Chiyu): And that fiery passion just now... For the first time in a while, I felt power fitting for RAS.
PAREO (Reona): Thank you so much...
CHU² (Chiyu): But... I can’t tell you to come back.
PAREO (Reona): Huh...?
CHU² (Chiyu): Whether you walk the path of being a member of RAS, or choose a different path entirely... It’s a decision only you can make.
CHU² (Chiyu): If you walk a path someone else shows you, you’ll eventually regret it somewhere along the way. That’s why... think carefully and make your choice.
PAREO (Reona): ............
PAREO (Reona): (What should I do...? I haven’t even explained RAS to my parents yet, is it okay to give my answer now?)
PAREO (Reona): (But if I don’t answer now while I have this momentum, I’ll end up...!)
CHU² (Chiyu): What are you thinking about right now?
PAREO (Reona): Huh...?
CHU² (Chiyu): Say everything you’re thinking, clearly and completely. It doesn’t matter what it is, no matter how foolish it might seem!
CHU² (Chiyu): If you can’t do that... then leave RAS.
CHU² (Chiyu): RAS is a band that aims to change the world through music. But to reach that goal, we’re bound to face countless obstacles. There’ll be so many times when you’ll want to run away...
CHU² (Chiyu): And every time, you might struggle like this. You might get hurt.
CHU² (Chiyu): So this is a parting of the ways, Reona...!
PAREO (Reona): ...CHU²-sama...!
PAREO (Reona): It’s true, I’m still struggling now... There’s no guarantee I can convince my parents, and being told to decide my future right now... honestly, it’s scary.
CHU² (Chiyu): I thought so. In that case...
PAREO (Reona): I also really want to keep going as part of RAS!
PAREO (Reona): I want to keep making the best music with everyone, and I want to keep dreaming big by your side, CHU²-sama...!
CHU² (Chiyu): Then make your choice! Come with me. Just take one step forward, that’s all!
PAREO (Reona): D-Don’t say it like it’s that simple!
CHU² (Chiyu): It’s not simple at all!!
PAREO (Reona): !!
CHU² (Chiyu): It wasn’t simple for me either...!
CHU² (Chiyu): I’ve agonized over it so much! Both as a producer wrestling with the decision of whether to cut PAREO loose, and also as someone who wants to stay by your side...
CHU² (Chiyu): If it had been back when I first formed RAISE A SUILEN, I wouldn’t have hesitated for a second. And yet...!
PAREO (Reona): CHU²-sama...!
CHU² (Chiyu): I don’t want you to leave RAS! But I hate even more the idea of just pretending everything’s fine and moving on!
CHU² (Chiyu): I hate myself for throwing a tantrum like this, and I hate how selfish and unreasonable you’re being too!!
PAREO (Reona): S-Selfishness is fine, isn’t it?! Throwing a tantrum is fine too, isn’t it?! You’re the one who told me to be honest and open about myself in the first place, CHU²-sama!
CHU² (Chiyu): That’s true, but that’s not what I’m saying right now! And I don’t want to see you struggling any more than this!!
CHU² (Chiyu): So if you can’t make your own decision, then quit being RAS’s keyboardist...!
PAREO (Reona): No way...
PAREO (Reona): That’s... that’s so irresponsible...!
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Reona: It’s because you’re the one who brought me into the light, Chiyu!!
CHU² (Chiyu): ...!
Reona: I was so happy...! When you called me to audition, it was the first time I felt like someone had really found the real me...!
Reona: It felt like the world was shining... You’re the one who pulled me out of the dark depths of the sea, Chiyu...!
Reona: So please, don’t let go halfway through...! Take responsibility... you idiot, Chiyu!! Waaahhhhhhh...!!
(Black cut)
(Background change)
CHU² (Chiyu): H-Hey, there’s no need to cry so much, is there?!
Reona: Of course I’m crying...! This is my whole life we’re talking about...!
Reona: I hate this...! I really hate this...! I hate how indecisive I am, and I hate that I ended up hurting you, Chiyu...!
CHU² (Chiyu): Reona...
Reona: Sob... sniff... I... I don’t know what to do anymore...! Waaaahhhhh...!!
CHU² (Chiyu): C-Calm down already! If you start crying, no one can rely on you...!
(Black cut)
Reona: Sniff... sniff... Sob... sob...
CHU² (Chiyu): Are you feeling better now? Your eyes are all red.
Reona: Yeah... sniff... I’m okay now...
Reona: Haah... after crying this much, I think I finally understand... If I don’t do it, I’ll regret it for sure...
CHU² (Chiyu): Huh...?
Reona: I’ll keep walking forward as “PAREO” from now on... Even if Chiyu... even if CHU²-sama says she doesn’t want me, I’ll follow you to the ends of the earth.
Reona: That’s one thing that will never change, because it’s how both PAREO and I feel.
CHU² (Chiyu): ......！
CHU² (Chiyu): ...What’s with that? I’ve thought this before, but the things you say are always so heavy.
Reona: Hehehe...
PAREO (Reona): That’s right, CHU²-sama. PAREO’s love for you is heavier than the entire planet. And that will never, ever change.
PAREO (Reona): Do you regret finding PAREO?
CHU² (Chiyu): Haha... hahaha... ahahaha!
CHU² (Chiyu): Perfect. That’s exactly how PAREO should be!
CHU² (Chiyu): You’re the first piece of my dream that I found. Even if you try to take it back later and say you spoke without thinking, I’ll never let you go.
CHU² (Chiyu): You’re the one who convinced me, so you’d better take responsibility, PAREO...!
PAREO (Reona): CHU²-sama... Yes, of course!
