event256-01 Opening: Sound Shaken Off

One day, CHU² stops PAREO after tidying up.

(Character: Rei Wakana, Rokka Asahi, Masuki Sato, Reona Nyubara, Chiyu Tamade)

[CHU²’s Apartment Studio]

PAREO (Reona): CHU²-sama, how was my performance just now...?
CHU² (Chiyu): ...No issues. Keep going.
PAREO (Reona): Y-Yes...
MASKING (Masuki): Hey, CHU²! You can’t tell me there ain't nothing wrong with PAREO's playing just now!
LOCK (Rokka): M-Masuki-san, that’s too harsh!
MASKING (Masuki): Even PAREO knows it wasn’t great! But you, as the producer, saying nothing? That’s just weird!
CHU² (Chiyu): Huh!? I didn’t...!
PAREO (Reona): S-Sorry! I’ll do better next time! I’ll try harder to match everyone...!
LAYER (Rei): Phew... Let’s take a quick break. Everyone, take a deep breath. We're short on time, so we’ll start again in five minutes.
PAREO & LOCK: Yes, yes!
(Black cut)

[1 hour later]

PAREO (Reona): Well, I have a train to catch, so I’ll excuse myself. See you at the next practice...
CHU² (Chiyu): Alright, take care.
LAYER (Rei): ...PAREO seems to be having a tough time.
CHU² (Chiyu): LAYER, aren’t you heading home?
LAYER (Rei): No, there’s no support practice today. Besides... I feel like talking to CHU² for a bit.
CHU² (Chiyu): Is it about PAREO?
LAYER (Rei): ...Yeah.
CHU² (Chiyu): Do you really think us talking will solve anything? PAREO’s worries are probably about her future... or her career path.
LAYER (Rei): Eh, how did you know...?!
CHU² (Chiyu): Thought so. That’s pretty much what I figured.
LAYER (Rei): ...! CHU², that’s a bit unfair!
CHU² (Chiyu): Unfair? That's fine by me. Still, her future, huh...
CHU² (Chiyu): (So, does this mean PAREO isn’t confident about staying in RAS... or maybe she doesn’t want to continue at all...?)
CHU² & LAYER: ............
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[A few days later]

PAREO (Reona): CHU²-sama~! I’ve finished cleaning up the kitchen! Well then, I’ll take my leave for today—
CHU² (Chiyu): Wait. ...I have to talk to you about something. Do you have time?
PAREO (Reona): Y-Yes. I can catch a later train, so it’s fine, but... um, what do you need to talk about...?
CHU² (Chiyu): I’ve been avoiding this because it felt like something you didn’t want to discuss, but... PAREO, do you plan to keep staying in RAS?
PAREO (Reona): !! Th-that’s...
PAREO (Reona): ............
CHU² (Chiyu): You’re not answering right away.
CHU² (Chiyu): Let me be blunt. If PAREO is going to stay like this... I think you should leave RAS.
PAREO (Reona): Wha...?
CHU² (Chiyu): You must’ve known I’d say something like this, right?
CHU² (Chiyu): We don’t have time to just go through the motions. RAS is a band meant to change the world...!
PAREO (Reona): ............
CHU² (Chiyu): So, what’s making you so stubborn about this?
PAREO (Reona): Stubborn? I don’t know... I just... I’m not sure anymore.
CHU² (Chiyu): ?
PAREO (Reona): I’ve been asking myself, why am I even doing music? When I look back, I realize... I was doing music because I wanted to be with CHU² and everyone in RAS.
PAREO (Reona): But that’s not the same as heading in the same direction as everyone else.
PAREO (Reona): It feels like I’m the only one... PAREO’s the only one looking at something different. While chasing after everyone’s backs, I kept doing music.
PAREO (Reona): Right now, I don’t have that unshakable feeling of wanting to do music no matter what... At this rate, someday, I’m sure I’ll end up falling out of sync with everyone...
PAREO (Reona): That’s what makes me so... uneasy...
CHU² (Chiyu): What are you even saying...? We’ve always worked together toward the goal of ‘Changing the World’!
CHU² (Chiyu): You weren’t lying about how you felt, were you!?
PAREO (Reona): You’re absolutely right, CHU²-sama! I wasn’t lying! But...!
CHU² (Chiyu): ...What is this even about...?
CHU² (Chiyu): Anyway, the options I can offer you are limited.
CHU² (Chiyu): First, you can get back in shape before we start practicing for Japan Rock Fest and stay in RAS. Second, you can leave RAS as you are now.
PAREO (Reona): ............
CHU² (Chiyu): ...And the third option is leaving RAS but staying involved as a manager.
PAREO (Reona): What...!? A manager...?
CHU² (Chiyu): Yes... It’s just another possibility, that’s all.
CHU² (Chiyu): Give me your answer before we start practicing for Japan Rock Fest.
CHU² (Chiyu): Until then, you can keep practicing with RAS as usual, or try out being a manager for a bit if you want.
CHU² (Chiyu): Do whatever feels right to you... Just make sure it’s a decision you won’t regret.
PAREO (Reona): Y-Yes...!
CHU² (Chiyu): Well then, I’m heading to the studio to work. Bye.
PAREO (Reona): Thanks for the hard work...! I’ll contact you again once I get home.
(Black cut)
(Background change)
