event158-03 Chapter 2: A Toconut Park Adventure

CHU² and the others finally enter a pool and ask LOCK what they should be doing in one...

(Character: Rei Wakana, Rokka Asahi, Masuki Sato, Reona Nyubara, Chiyu Tamade)

[Toconut Park]

CHU² (Chiyu): ... I didn't think floats would shake this much...!
LOCK (Rokka): It must be the waves being made when people move around.
PAREO (Reona): Do not fear, CHU²-sama! I shall hold on to your ring so that you do not tip over.
CHU² (Chiyu): You better not let go, okay, PAREO?
CHU² (Chiyu): So, now what we're in the water, what are we supposed to be doing for fun?
MASKING (Masuki): It's a pool. We'll swim.
CHU² (Chiyu): When it's this crowded? It doesn't look like there's any space to swim.
LAYER (Rei): True. Everyone around us seems to be walking instead of swimming.
MASKING (Masuki): Well, what else is there to do in a damn pool?
PAREO (Reona): Simply being able to float alongside CHU²-sama is enough for me♪
LAYER (Rei): LOCK, what did you do the last time you came with Ako-chan?
LOCK (Rokka): Um, we played with a beach ball and the floats, and we splashed water on each other! But other than that, I don't think we did much else...
MASKING (Masuki): Yeah, that's pretty much all there really is to do in a pool.
LOCK (Rokka): Oh! The three of us also talked and ate together!
CHU² (Chiyu): Those aren't things you have to go to a pool to do.
LOCK (Rokka): But we still had a lot of fun~! It might be because Ako-chan showed us around, though...!
MASKING (Masuki): She know the park well?
LOCK (Rokka): Yes, she knew about the types of pools and where we could go to eat! She was a huge help.
LOCK (Rokka): Everyone in Roselia came here before too.
CHU² (Chiyu): Roselia came here? The Roselia?!
LOCK (Rokka): Yes. Ako-chan said the nighttime show was amazing.
LOCK (Rokka): I wanted to see it too, but we had to leave early because the weather was going to be bad at night.
LOCK (Rokka): She was so disappointed we never got to see the show.
LAYER (Rei): Interesting. It must be quite a show then.
CHU² (Chiyu): It didn't look all that impressive on TV... But maybe there's still something to be learned from it.
CHU² (Chiyu): Roselia must have come to get ideas for their concerts. I should check who the directors and producers are.
CHU² (Chiyu): Okay... I've decided I'm going to watch the nighttime show.
LOCK (Rokka): You will?! Yay~!
PAREO (Reona): Ohhh~! I did not say anything, but I have been wanting to see it as well~♪
PAREO (Reona): The music will be accompanied by fireworks going up into the air, and the swimming pools will be lit up!
LAYER (Rei): Not bad. I can't wait for evening to come.
MASKING (Masuki): Great! Let's go try out all the swimmin' pools before the show starts then!
CHU² (Chiyu): And why do we have to do that?! It has nothing to do with the show!
LAYER (Rei): But it might not be a bad idea. Who knows? Maybe you'll even find a pool that you can enjoy, CHU².
CHU² (Chiyu): But a pool's still a pool, isn't it?
LOCK (Rokka): Wave pools are very different from lazy rivers, CHU²-san.
MASKING (Masuki): Anyway, we still haven't even fully experienced this one. Why don't we start by ferrying CHU² over to the other side?
CHU² (Chiyu): Ah! Don't just put your weight on my float so suddenly! I thought I was going to turn over!
LOCK (Rokka): Masuki-san, please be more gentle! We don't want to lose balance!
MASKING (Masuki): What? I am bein' gentle.
PAREO (Reona): Please use about one-fiftieth of your strength then!
MASKING (Masuki): So... like this?
CHU² (Chiyu): Ahhh! Pfft, I got water on my face~!
PAREO (Reona): CH-CHU²-sama! Massu-san, I forbid you from laying a single finger on CHU²-sama's swim ring from now on~!
MASKING (Masuki): What did I do?!
LAYER (Rei): That's what you get for being too rough...
MASKING (Masuki): I wasn't! C'mon, I wanna push CHU²'s float too.
PAREO & CHU²: No! Absolutely not!
MASKING (Masuki): No need for y'all to say it at the same time...
LOCK & LAYER: Ahahahaha.
