event158-06 Chapter 5: Moment of Bliss

It is finally time for the nighttime show to begin. PAREO shares her feelings with CHU²...

(Character: Rei Wakana, Rokka Asahi, Masuki Sato, Reona Nyubara, Chiyu Tamade)

MASKING (Masuki): Guess I can come with. What about you two, PAREO, CHU²...?

PAREO (Reona): Ah, CHU²-sama! You are welcome to lean towards me if you have trouble sitting up...! You must be very tired. Would you like to return home?
CHU² (Chiyu): No... I'm going to watch the show... with everyone...
PAREO (Reona): Everyone... Fufu, thank you very much.
CHU² (Chiyu): ...? Why are you thanking me...?
PAREO (Reona): ... That is because today has been a most wonderful day, thanks to you and everyone else.
PAREO (Reona): I have never had such fun at a pool before.
PAREO (Reona): CHU²-sama... Thank you for making me PAREO.
CHU² (Chiyu): Wh-what's with all the thank you's all of a sudden...?!
PAREO (Reona): I merely wished to express the gratitude that I always feel♪
CHU² (Chiyu): ... If you're going to thank someone, why not thank yourself? You're the one who made the decision to change.
PAREO (Reona): If that is the case, it must mean you also decided to change, CHU²-sama♪
CHU² (Chiyu): What do you mean?
PAREO (Reona): I do not think the old you would have ever come to Toconut Park, CHU²-sama.
CHU² (Chiyu): I-I only came because all of you begged...!
PAREO (Reona): Fufu, oh yes♪
CHU² (Chiyu): Pools aren't special for me because there's one at the penthouse. And even now, I can't say I'm the slightest bit more interested in them either.
CHU² (Chiyu): ... But I've seen that the world can sometimes be very different when you experience it directly. I had no idea the water in a pool could be so cold even in summer.
CHU² (Chiyu): I never knew that something so simple as getting on a raft and going down a slide could be so fun or that the way the sunlight reflects off a snow cone could be so beautiful...
CHU² (Chiyu): There's a lot that you can't truly know from knowledge alone.
PAREO (Reona): ... You really have changed too, CHU²-sama.
PAREO (Reona): I feel the CHU²-sama I knew when we first met would have said something else entirely.
CHU² (Chiyu): You might be right...
CHU² (Chiyu): You see, today reminded me of the very first moment that someone ever played a song I wrote.
CHU² (Chiyu): Their performance was imprecise, but that was what gave the music color, texture, and warmth. That was my first encounter with the sound of RAS' music.
CHU² (Chiyu): Of course, back when I was playing the violin, I wasn't capable of recognizing the existence of imperfections...
PAREO (Reona): But... Well, in any case, all of your experiences are what have made your music what it is today, has it not?
CHU² (Chiyu): Of course. And the music of a true musician is guaranteed to captivate people.
PAREO (Reona): Yes! I believe that to be the case as well!
CHU² (Chiyu): PAREO... When we get home, I want you to help me compose.
PAREO (Reona): ... Excuse me?
CHU² (Chiyu): I want to write something different. It might be fun to add a little imprecision, don't you think?
CHU² (Chiyu): Lend me your sound, PAREO.
PAREO (Reona): Gasp...! Of course, CHU²-sama~!
CHU² (Chiyu): Ah! Wh-what are you doing?! You're choking me!
PAREO (Reona): I am just so happy that you have asked me to assist you, CHU²-sama...! It does not matter when, where, or how, I shall always be at your side to serve you, CHU²-sama!
CHU² (Chiyu): Get a hold of yourself! PAREO... Sit!
