event291-03 Chapter 2: Our “Ako Udagawa”

At the office, Rinko and the others wait for Ako. Each of them holds their own thoughts about her...

(Character: Tomoe Udagawa, Yukina Minato, Sayo Hikawa, Lisa Imai, Ako Udagawa, Rinko Shirokane)

(Memory cut-in)

Ako: Tan, tan, ta-tan, tan...
Tomoe: Ako, what are you doing?
Ako: Practicing drums! Mom bought me sticks!
Tomoe: Oh, I see. But if you want to get better...
Tomoe: Here, hold the sticks like this. Hold them more gently.
Ako: Wow! They move so easily! Thanks, sis!
Ako: I’m gonna become a cool drummer just like you!
Tomoe: Yeah, do your best!
(White cut)
(Background change)
Ako: (At first, I just wanted to imitate sis, even if it was only on the surface.)
Ako: (Because I thought that way, I could get just a little closer to her coolness.)

(Memory cut-out)

(Background change)

[Music Agency - Staff Room]

Lisa: Good work—oh, not everyone’s here yet. Where’s Ako?
Rinko: She had school stuff... She’ll be a little late...
Lisa: I see. No need to rush her, let’s not mention that we’re already gathered.
Rinko: Yeah... Running and falling would be dangerous...
Lisa: Ahaha, you sound like a mom. Ako’s been fired up lately, hasn’t she?
Rinko: Yes... Ako-chan’s so full of energy... but...
Rinko: ......
Sayo: But...? Did something happen with Udagawa-san?
Rinko: Eh, um, no, nothing...
Sayo: If that’s the case, then that's good...
Rinko: ...... Um, please don’t tell Ako-chan about this...
Yukina: What is it?
Rinko: ...After reading that magazine interview the other day... I think Ako-chan’s been thinking about a lot...
Rinko: About Roselia... and about Tomoe-san...
Lisa: Ah, the world’s second-best drummer thing.
Lisa: It was too bad it got cut, but... Honestly, if that part went out to people who didn’t know Ako yet, it might have come across the wrong way.
Sayo: Yes, I think the magazine’s decision wasn’t wrong.
Lisa: We know that’s just the kind of girl Ako is~.
Sayo: Yes, that’s what makes Udagawa-san who she is. She can honestly recognize when someone’s amazing and admire them.
Sayo: But, looked at another way, it means she accepts being second. Maybe she realized that contradiction.
Yukina: ...When we first met, Ako told me, “I’m the second-best drummer in the world.”
Sayo: Ah, that’s right. And you brushed her off, saying, “I won’t team up with someone who brags about being second.”
Yukina: Yes. Back then, I didn’t know the real Ako, so I just took her words literally.
Yukina: But now, Ako is Roselia’s number one drummer. Without her, we wouldn’t be who we are.
Lisa: Yeah... that’s true.
Lisa: But in the end, that’s Ako, isn’t it. Because she admired Tomoe like that, she became who she is now.
Lisa: So I don’t think denying Ako’s way of thinking is right.
Rinko: Yes... I feel the same...
Yukina: ...Sayo.
Sayo: Yes.
Yukina: You have a little sister, so don’t you understand Ako’s feelings better than the rest of us?
Sayo: M-me...?
Lisa: Yukina~. Even if you say that, Sayo and Ako’s situations are different.
Yukina: Still, it might be a hint.
Sayo: That’s true... Like Imai-san said, my relationship with Hina and Udagawa-san’s with Tomoe-san are different.
Sayo: And I’m the older sister, while Udagawa-san is the younger. What we see is completely different.
Sayo: But still... Sisters are a special bond, more than close friends, spending time together regardless of compatibility or likes and dislikes.
Rinko: A special bond...
Sayo: No matter how much builds up between you, you can’t just part ways like friends—you can only keep stacking emotions on each other...
Sayo: That’s why there are things only they can understand. ...Ah, no, not that Hina and I are like that, but...
Sayo: Perhaps all we can do for them is to just watch over them.
Rinko: Just watching over her... huh...
Rinko: .........
Lisa: You’re still worried about Ako?
Rinko: ...Yes...
Rinko: I am worried... But I want to believe she’ll be okay...
Rinko: Because Ako-chan and Tomoe-san are... a very close pair of sisters...
