event291-06 Chapter 5: What Has Been, and What Will Be

Rinko shares her feelings with Ako. Ako receives them, and shares her own feelings in turn—

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa, Ako Udagawa, Rinko Shirokane)

[A Few Days Later]

Ako: Ah, there you are! Rin-rin~!
Rinko: Ako-chan... sorry for calling you out so suddenly...
Ako: Nope, it’s fine. We're off work today, and practice isn’t until evening anyway.
Ako: But wow, this place is nostalgic~! When you said to meet here, I was so surprised.
Rinko: Yeah... for me too... it feels nostalgic...
Rinko: Here... I feel like I can be brave...
Ako: Brave? Rin-rin, did something happen?
Rinko: Th-that’s... um... well...
Ako: ...?
Rinko: U-um... Ako-chan, I...
Ako: ...Rin-rin. You have something you want to tell me, right?
Rinko: ......Yeah...
Ako: Then let’s talk slowly. Just like all the times we talked in NFO, and in real life too.
Rinko: Like before...
Rinko: ...... ..........
Rinko: ...Tomoe-san is the coolest drummer in the world to you, right?
Ako: Eh? Yeah, she is.
Ako: I’ve always wanted to be a cool drummer like sis... no, a cool person.
Ako: I’ve always been searching for my own kind of cool. But, if I could...
Ako: —I want to be a cool drummer like sis.
Rinko: ............
Ako: ...But today isn’t about me, it’s about you, Rin-rin.
Rinko: ............
Ako: Rin-rin...?
Rinko: ...You don’t have to, you know.
Ako: ...Eh? What do you mean...?
Rinko: ...... ..........
Rinko: Ako-chan... you’ve admired Tomoe-san for so long... and I’ve been watching you admire her all this time...
Rinko: I... I really love you, Ako-chan... the way you love Tomoe-san, the way you admire her... I love that about you...
Rinko: Because you admired Tomoe-san... that’s why the current Ako-chan exists...
Rinko: But... but I...
Ako: Rin-rin...
Rinko: I don’t want you to become anyone else but you, Ako-chan...
Rinko: The drummer Roselia needs isn’t one like Tomoe-san...
Rinko: What Roselia needs... is a drummer like you, Ako-chan...
Rinko: To Yukina-san, to Imai-san, to Hikawa-san...
Rinko: To Roselia... and to me... the world’s best drummer is you, Ako-chan...
Ako: ............
Rinko: Sorry... for suddenly saying all that...
Ako: ...Ah. N-no, I’m the one spacing out.
Ako: Sorry... you surprised me saying that so suddenly...
Ako: Because sis has always been my idol... I’ve always wanted to be like her...
Ako: So when you say I don’t have to be like her, I don’t know what to do...
Rinko: ...... ..........
Ako: But...!
Rinko: Eh...?
Ako: My head’s all jumbled, I don’t know what I should do at all...
Ako: But I know you were brave, and said that for me.
Ako: So...
Ako: Let’s talk about it together, okay? About me, about you, and about what’s ahead.
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[The Day of the Show]

Himari: Wow~! This really feels like Roselia’s live venue!
Ran: Yeah. The atmosphere’s different from our lives.
Tsugumi: Tomoe-chan, you were invited by Ako-chan, right?
Tomoe: Yeah. She said we could all come if we’d like.
Moca: Tomo-chin, guess she wants you to see her shining on stage? Man~, what a thoughtful little sis~.
Tomoe: R-really...?
Himari: I’m sure that’s it! Ako-chan sees you as her idol, Tomoe!
Tomoe: W-when you say it like that, it’s embarrassing. But...
Tomoe: Ako started drumming because of me... so I want to keep showing her a strong example.
Moca: Wow~ Tomo-chin, so cool~.
Tomoe: Cut it out, you’re making me blush.
Himari: I wonder what Ako-chan’s doing right now~ Ah, maybe she’s nervous since you’re here, Tomoe!
Tomoe: Ako will be just the same as always.
Tomoe: —I’m sure, same as always, she’ll give the best performance.
