event272-01 Opening: Everyone’s Own Kind of Fun

While practicing, the girls chat about Halloween. As the excitement builds, Tsukushi shares an idea.

(Character: Mashiro Kurata, Toko Kirigaya, Nanami Hiromachi, Tsukushi Futaba, Rui Yashio)

[Hiromachi Residence - Atelier]

Tsukushi: Yeah! That last performance felt pretty solid, didn’t it?
Toko: We’re totally unstoppable! Next show’s gonna be legendary!
Nanami: Toko-chan, you're totally on fire today. Your sound was amazing~. And Shiro-chan too.
Mashiro: Huh? Really...? I’ve actually been practicing at home...
Rui: Your practice is paying off. You sounded more confident too.
Nanami: Whoa~, Rui-rui’s seal of approval.
Mashiro: Ehehe...
Tsukushi: Oh wow, it’s already that late...? Let’s take a break.
Nanami: Good idea. I’ll go make some tea.
Tsukushi: I’ll help, Nanami-chan. I actually brought some snacks today.
Nanami: Really? Then I’ll let you pick the tea leaves to go with them~.
Tsukushi: Sure, leave it to me!
(Black cut)
Toko: What’s this? Munch... Oh, pumpkin cookies! Yum~, these are great!
Tsukushi: Glad they turned out well~. I wanted to bake some since Halloween’s coming up.
Mashiro: You really like Halloween, Tsukushi-chan?
Tsukushi: Yeah, it’s just kind of exciting, don’t you think?
Nanami: Totally! The town gets all decked out with pumpkins and black cats—it’s such a fun vibe.
Toko: I know, right?! And it’s awesome that we’ve got more events where we can all go wild together!
Mashiro: Really? I’ve never really gone to those kinds of events...
Tsukushi: Actually, I go to Halloween events pretty often too.
Mashiro: Wait, really?
Tsukushi: Yeah. I mean, it’s mostly family-friendly stuff with my little sisters.
Tsukushi: And I dress up a bit every year. Last year I wore a witch’s hat and a cape.
Nanami: Oh~, that sounds cute. Got any pics?
Tsukushi: Um, I took some with my sisters on my phone... Ah, here!
Nanami: Wow, all three of you look adorable~! Rui-rui, come see~.
Rui: ...
Tsukushi: Their costumes were so cute, I took tons of photos. Here’s one where they’re getting candy from a staff member, and this one’s at the photo spot, and...ah!
Rui: Looks like there’s one of just you, Futaba-san.
Toko: Fusuke, you’re totally into it! That selfie’s super hyped!
Tsukushi: T-That one’s different! I just got really excited, that’s all...!
Nanami: Fufu, no need to hide it. So you really do love Halloween~, huh?
Mashiro: Kind of surprising... I didn’t think you were the type to get that into dressing up.
Tsukushi: Y-Yeah, it’s not really like me, is it...?
Mashiro: N-No, that’s not what I meant...! You just looked way more into it than I expected...!
Tsukushi: I know... but I’ve always liked it.
Tsukushi: When I dress up and look at myself in the mirror, it’s like I become someone else.
Mashiro: Ah... I kinda get that. Like when you wear a witch costume and think maybe you could really use magic.
Nanami: Or when dressing as a black cat makes you feel like you’ve actually turned into one~.
Toko: I don’t really get that, but I totally get the part about getting hyped—it’s the best!
Rui: I feel like you’re missing the point, Kirigaya-san...
Toko: Whatever, it's fine! You're so nitpicky~.
Nanami: Okay okay okay~. Hey, how about we all dress up as a group for Halloween this year?
Rui: I’ll pass.
Nanami: Too fast~, Rui-rui.
Toko: Well, we’ll just drag Rui along anyway. So where should we go?
Tsukushi: Um... in that case, how about going to an amusement park? We can dress up, and there's one that puts a lot of effort into its Halloween events.
Tsukushi: I’m pretty sure they had an official website... Look, here it is!
Nanami: Whoa~... So they even have a Halloween-only parade called Halloween Night. Sounds like fun~.
Mashiro: The amusement park sounds fun, but dressing up might be a bit embarrassing...
Toko: Come on, Shiro—this kind of thing is all about jumping in and having fun! And at a Halloween amusement park, most people are in costume anyway. You’d stand out more in normal clothes.
Mashiro: R-Really? Then maybe I’ll give dressing up a try too...!
Toko: Okay! Now all that’s left is Rui~.
Rui: ...
Tsukushi: What’s wrong, Rui-san? You’ve been staring at the park’s website for a while now.
Rui: No... It's just that the composer for the parade is one of my favorite overseas artists, so I was reading their comments, that's all.
Tsukushi: Wait, seriously!? ...You're right—it says they’re a world-renowned violinist!
Toko: Well, that settles it! We want to go to the amusement park in costume, and Rui wants to hear the parade music. Perfect match!
Rui: I don’t follow your logic. I can just listen to the music without going to the park.
Toko: You don’t get it~. It’s way better with the full performance experience! Right, Fusuke?
Tsukushi: Y-Yeah! That’s right! So let’s go together, Rui-san!
Tsukushi & Toko: ......!
Rui: Sigh...
