event272-02 Chapter 1: Girl in Transformation

The Morfonica members plan their Halloween costumes. On the big day, each of them steps out in a look all their own—

(Character: Mashiro Kurata, Toko Kirigaya, Nanami Hiromachi, Tsukushi Futaba, Rui Yashio)

[Tsukinomori Girls' Academy - Garden Gazebo]

Toko: That’s why Halloween costumes should be flashy and as extra as possible!
Tsukushi: There’s a limit, you know! We don’t want to bother other guests, and we’ll be walking all day, so heavy costumes are a no-go.
Tsukushi: If we wear ourselves out halfway through, it’d be such a waste, right?
Toko: Well, that’s true... but if the design’s too simple, it’s just not enough~...!
Nanami: Fufu, here they go again today.
Rui: They never seem to get tired of it.
Mashiro: Lately they always talk about costume designs for the amusement park at lunchtime.
Tsukushi: Oh, right. I wanted to ask everyone something today. When we go to the amusement park, is there anything you want to do?
Toko: I wanna dress up!
Tsukushi: Besides dressing up! Like, any attractions you want to ride or foods you want to try... Let me know and I’ll plan out a schedule!
Nanami: Oh~, Tsu-chan’s in full organizer mode~.
Tsukushi: Rui-san, for you it’s the night parade, right?
Rui: Yes. Nothing else in particular.
Toko: Sure, sure! Then I want to ride a roller coaster!
Mashiro: Huh!? I’m not really good with roller coasters...
Toko: Wait, Shiro, you’re not a roller coaster person?
Tsukushi: Can you handle the smaller ones? Or are they a total no-go?
Mashiro: If it’s for kids, I can barely manage...
Tsukushi: Then how about the boat ride where you go on a pirate adventure? There's a small drop at the start, but the rest is just watching stuff.
Toko: Huh. Sounds kinda fun. I’m fine with that! What about you, Shiro?
Mashiro: Depends on how steep the drop is...
Tsukushi: It’s an attraction even little kids can ride, so I think you’ll be fine!
Mashiro: Hmm... okay, I’ll give it a try...!
Toko: Alright, it’s settled then!
Nanami: Tsu-chan, you know a lot about the rides. Have you been there before?
Tsukushi: Nope! I’ve just been really looking forward to going with everyone, so I looked up a bunch of stuff. I even made notes, so I’ve got most of it covered!
Nanami: Whoa~, Tsu-chan is seriously prepared~
Rui: You must be really looking forward to it.
Toko: Geez, making notes and everything... Fusuke, you’re so serious~.
Tsukushi: It’s fine! It’s just easier for me to understand when I write it all down!
Nanami: Hey hey, Tsu-chan, I want to take photos with everyone~. Taking pics with friends at the amusement park feels super nostalgic and fun.
Tsukushi: Then the square in front of the castle in the center of the park would be perfect. I heard it’s the cutest decorated spot!
Tsukushi: If we start from the back area of the park when it opens, we can probably take photos there early in the afternoon.
Mashiro: Tsukushi-chan, can I suggest something too...? I want to visit the town area where all the character houses are...!
Tsukushi: Sure, leave it to me! I’ll work hard on the schedule so we can do everything everyone wants!
