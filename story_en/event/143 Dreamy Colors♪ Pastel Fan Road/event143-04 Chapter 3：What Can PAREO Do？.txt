event143-04 Chapter 3: What Can PAREO Do?

PAREO attends the release event again to show her support for Eve. What does she learn there...?

(Character: Hina Hikawa, Maya Yamato, Eve Wakamiya, Reona Nyubara)

[Event Hall]

PAREO (Reona): (I didn't intend on coming to the meet and greet part of the event... But I came nonetheless...!)
PAREO (Reona): (Instead of just posting about the radio show, I shall tell them what I thought in person! I must tell Eve-chan that there are people who are looking forward to it...!)
Fan A: Hey, hey, did you listen to All Day＊Bushido?
Fan B: Yeah, I did... But you know, Eve-chan sounded a bit down.
PAREO (Reona): ...
Fan A: I'm sure she was just nervous since it was her first show!
Fan B: Yeah! Okay, I'll send her lots of letters for next time too!
PAREO (Reona): (Yes, yes! That is the spirit! As her fans, that is what we should do!)
PAREO (Reona): (Let's do our best!)
Fan A: Um... Did you need something?
PAREO (Reona): Ah- s-sorry! I did not mean to stare... Please excuse me!
PAREO (Reona): (How improper of me to eavesdrop on their conversation!)
PAREO (Reona): (However, I am a little envious that she has a fellow fan as a friend who she can talk to about Pastel＊Palettes... Oh well, I have enough fun on my own anyhow♪)
(Black cut)

[10 Minutes Later]

Eve: Ah, PAREO-san! Hello~!
PAREO (Reona): Ahhh~! Eve-chan, you look so cute today~♪
Eve: Fufu, thank you!
PAREO (Reona): Good job on your first radio show. Erm... I am looking forward to the next one!
Eve: ... Thank you! I will exert myself to the utmost! Please look forward to it!
PAREO (Reona): Erm, I am a really, really big fan of yours, Eve-chan! I love your soft, warm smile, and the shimmering sound of your keyboard!
PAREO (Reona): And the ninja pose you do on All Fun and Games is so adorable!
Eve: You mean the Kuji-kiri.
PAREO (Reona): Ahhh~! That's it~! Thank you very much~! The rare Hasso-no-kamae pose you do is also very cool and cute-
Staff: Your time is almost up.
PAREO (Reona): What, already?! E-Eve-chan! I will send you lots of letters!
PAREO (Reona): I will put 1,000 times more of my love into them!
Eve: 1,000 times more...? Fufu, thank you! I cannot wait!
PAREO (Reona): (Sigh~... Why do meet and greets always end at the speed of light? I was so excited... I hope my message was conveyed.)
PAREO (Reona): (There is no use in being down about it. I came all this way, so I must greet everybody! Alright, time to line up in Hina-chan's lane!)
(Black cut)

[10 Minutes Later]

Hina: Ahh, PARE-chan♪ Hey!
PAREO (Reona): Wah~, Hina-chan~!
Hina: Thanks for always coming to our events! Hey, hey, did you listen to Eve-chan's radio show?
PAREO (Reona): Yes, of course I did! I cannot wait for the next one!
Hina: Right? I knew you'd say that. It'd be so much better without a script though.
PAREO (Reona): Huh...? A script?
Hina: Yeah, yeah. Apparently, the staff make her say every single word as it is in the script.
PAREO (Reona): Wh-what? But then Eve-chan must feel... That must be horrible for Eve-chan!
Hina: I know, right? I knew you'd get it, PARE-chan♪ That's why Eve-chan is trying to negotiate with the staff.
Maya: H-Hina-san! What are you talking about?! Stop, stop!
Hina: Ah, Maya-chan. Is your break over already? That means it's time for my break next.
Maya: Ah, yes. Feel free to- Wait, that's not what I was going to say! You can't talk to the fans about things like this.
Hina: Ahh, right. Sorry, PARE-chan. Could you forget what I said just now? Like, whoosh.
Maya: I'm sorry, PAREO-san. Could you please keep what Hina-san said a secret?
PAREO (Reona): Of course! I will not tell a soul...!
Maya: Great... I'm relieved you said that. Um... Eve-san may be going through that situation right now, but she's doing her best.
PAREO (Reona): I understand that her work can be very challenging. However... I believe that Eve-chan will do what she believes is right!
Maya: I think so too. Please keep cheering us on, PAREO-san.
PAREO (Reona): I will!
(Black cut)
(Background change)
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Walking Home]

PAREO (Reona): (Being an idol is much more difficult than I'd ever imagined...)
PAREO (Reona): (Despite everything, Eve-chan was still smiling like always. She was laughing and chatting with each fan with her usual radiance...)
PAREO (Reona): (There must be something more I can do for her...)
PAREO (Reona): (Hmm~, but even so, a script?! They don't understand even a fraction of Eve-chan's appeal! Has that staff member even seen Pastel＊Palettes before?)
PAREO (Reona): (She plays the keyboard so well in this video! And her lines in the All Fun and Games ninja episode were so cute...)
PAREO (Reona): (Ahh, I cannot stand it! I want to educate the radio staff by showing them all of my favorite videos!)
PAREO (Reona): ...! That's it!
PAREO (Reona): Now is the time to show the power of the fans. Eve-chan, I will support you with all of my strength!
