event50-03 Chapter 2: Let's Have Some Fun!

As each girl finds their own enjoyment at the beach, Kokoro stumbles upon something...!

(Character: Tae Hanazono, Arisa Ichigaya, Kokoro Tsurumaki, Hagumi Kitazawa, Eve Wakamiya)

[In The Ocean]

Kokoro: Look, everyone! Have you ever seen such lovely fish?! They totally wanna play with us!
Hagumi: Kokoron, they're here, too! And there! ... They're everywhere! It's just like an aquarium!
Kokoro: Hey, this one just smiled at us! It totally did!
Arisa: Oh, please! Did it flash its pearly whites, too?
Tae: Arisa, have a look at these cute green fish here. They're just swaying back and forth, back and forth... I wonder what they're called...
Arisa: Green, swaying fish...? ... O-Tae, how about we stop confusing seaweed for animals, alright?
Tae: ... Huh? But... they were fish just a second ago...
Arisa: The only fishy thing here is your brain!
Hagumi: You better watch out, fish! The mighty Hagumi is here to race ya! Yahoo~!
Eve: Hagumi-san, wait for me! I wish to participate, too!
Eve: Now is the time for me to unleash the forgotten swimming arts bestowed upon me by Hina-san herself!
Eve: Let's see... The legs go "ton~"... and the arms "pi"...  And then, a final "sui-sui"... Right?! "Ton, pi, sui-sui~!"
Kokoro: Eve, that's amazing! You're already an expert swimmer!
Eve: I could not have done it without Hina-san's outstanding instruction! Please join me, Kokoro-san!
Kokoro: Here I come! Hmm, how about we swim to that spot over there? See it? The one with the shark?
Arisa: Hold it! Sh-shark?! Did I hear that right?!
Eve: Ah... I believe I can make out its dorsal fin...
Arisa: A shark fin...? ... Uwaaah! I see it, too!
Tae: And look, it's getting closer and closer.
Kokoro: Even the sharks wanna play with us today! Who wants to race to meet it first?!
Arisa: Stop, no...! Tsurumaki-san, this isn't a joke! Get back here!
Kokoro: Don't sweat it! Just come on, already! Why, hello there, Mister Shark! ... M-Mister Shark?
Tae: ... Is that a...?
Hagumi: A plank of wood...?
Eve: W-what a relief, Kokoro-san...
Arisa: C-come to think of it, a shark being this far inland is absurd...  ... Ugh, don't scare me like that...
Arisa: N-nearly gave me a heart attack... Let's get out and take a breather...
Hagumi: Aa-chan, you're getting out already? In that case, let's play us some beach volleyball!
Kokoro: Some volleyball sounds fantastic! Let's do it!
Arisa: Gimme a break... I said to take a breather, not take a turn at the net...
(Black cut)

[The Beach]

Hagumi: Looks like you're the referee, Aa-chan! Stay sharp!
Arisa: Roger that...
Arisa: (... Why'd I get stuck on referee duty...? ... Tsurumaki-san and Kitazawa-san versus an O-Tae and Eve combo... Talk about stacked odds...)
Hagumi: How 'bout a taste of my Hagumi Serve! Ya better not blink!  ... Hiiiyah!
Tae: Ah, I've got it...! ... Aaand catch!
Arisa: Don't catch it, genius!
Tae: Huh? Why not?
Arisa: This isn't baseball! The basic rules of volleyball are to hit it back! Tsurumaki-san and Kitazawa-san are up one point!
Hagumi: Service ace~! There's more where that came from!
Kokoro: Nice serve, Hagumi! Keep it up!
Eve: Tae-san! The match has only just begun! We must tap into our inner bushido to triumph!
(Black cut)

[10 Minutes Later]

Kokoro: Okay, my turn to serve! Here it comes! ... Hah!
Tae: Eve, it's heading your way!
Eve: Okay! Bushido~!
Arisa: She actually got it...!
Eve: Tae-san! It's time to bring forth our finishing move!
Tae: Right!
Arisa: Finishing move?! No way!  Is that what they were whispering about before?!
Eve & Tae: Hanazono Bushido... Attack~!!
Arisa: Hanazono's what now?! ... ... That's just a normal feint, you clowns!
Kokoro: Not on my watch!
Arisa: So much for being finished off!
Hagumi: Nice one, Kokoron! Here I come~! Hagumi Attack!
Tae: S-so fast...! No choice but to... catch it!
Arisa: There she goes again... And that's game set.  Tsurumaki-san and Kitazawa-san are the winners!
