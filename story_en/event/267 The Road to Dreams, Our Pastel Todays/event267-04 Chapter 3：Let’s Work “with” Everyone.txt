event267-04 Chapter 3: Let’s Work “with” Everyone

Reading fan letters boosts everyone’s spirits. But Hina still doesn’t quite get it...

(Character: Aya Maruyama, Hina Hikawa, Chisato Shirasagi, Maya Yamato, Eve Wakamiya)

[Talent Agency - Hallway]

Hina: Yawn... Morning~.
Eve: That's a big yawn, Hina-san!
Hina: Yeah... I ended up staying up late last night.
Maya: I get it. Even after I got into bed, I kept thinking about everything we talked about at the café.
Eve: Yeah... It feels like I’m walking through a maze with no exit, all by myself.
Maya: Just like Aya-san said in her message, I hope we can find something in the words from our fans.
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Aya: Ah! Everyone’s already here, Chisato-chan.
Maya: Good morning, Aya-san, Chisato-san.
Aya: Good morning! Look at all these boxes... They’re full of fan letters addressed to Pastel＊Palettes!
Chisato: They’re filled with so many kind words. Look, this letter here.
Eve: Wow... The floral stationery is so cute.
Maya: Maybe they picked it out while thinking of Pastel＊Palettes. Let’s see...
Maya: “To everyone in Pastel＊Palettes, great job in TIS Third Round. Your dance performance was so amazing, even my little sister danced along in front of the TV.”
Maya: Looks like all that hard work paid off.
Aya: This letter talks about the street live Hina-chan and I did.
Hina: “I’ve watched the video posted on social media over and over. A street live is so rare—it totally got me boppin’♪” That was such a fun time.
Eve: Come to think of it... Ever since TIS started, I’ve seen people use the hashtag ‘#CheerForPastel＊Palettes’ on social media.
Aya: About that—apparently it’s gotten really popular lately.
Hina: Wow~. Oh, found it! This hashtag, right?
Hina: “Pastel＊Palettes can win this!” “I want to see Pastel＊Palettes perform at Budokan! You’ve got this!”
Hina: Here’s another one.  “Got my ticket to the TIS audience seats, booked my salon appointment for the day too!”
Aya: I totally get that~... I always dressed up whenever I went to a Marmalade concert.
Eve: “Studying for my tests now so I can attend! I’ll work just as hard as Pastel＊Palettes!”
Eve: Someone our age, maybe? Good luck!
Chisato: Fufu, even if you respond to a letter, they won’t be able to hear you.
Eve: R-right! Then I’ll send my feelings out with all my heart!
Maya: Looking at all these again... there really are so many messages of thanks to Pastel＊Palettes.
Aya: Yeah. I feel like we’re the ones who should be thanking everyone...
Hina: Hey, listen to this one—“I’m gonna dress in Hina-chan colors from head to toe on the day of the show!” Think we’ll see them from the stage?
Maya: This one says, “I’ve been struggling with school, but Maya-chan’s courage at TIS gave me strength...”
Aya: That really warms my heart.
Maya: Yeah. The fans’ support really is amazing. Even when we’re struggling or feeling down, it gives us so much strength.
Aya: ...Everyone, let’s do our best at TIS.
Eve: Yeah!
Maya: Absolutely.
Chisato: Right. For everyone who sends us their words and support like this—
Hina: Still doesn’t sit right with me.
Aya: Huh?
Chisato: Hina-chan...?
Hina: Personally, I don’t think it’s “for them”... I think it’s more like “together with them.”
Eve: “Together with them”...?
Chisato: What do you mean, Hina-chan?
Hina: I’ve been thinking about it for a while now. I don’t know if I’ve ever thought like you all do—like “for the fans” or “we have to live up to their expectations.”
Hina: Because when I’m having fun doing what I love, the fans have fun too.
Hina: I don’t think I’ve ever had that mindset of “this is for the fans!”
Maya: That does sound like you, Hina-san.
Hina: And when I read the fan letters and scroll through social media, I feel like a lot of fans are kind of the same way.
Hina: It’s not so much “for Pastel＊Palettes” as it is “I want to support them because it makes me happy.”
Chisato: Now that you say it...
Eve: The fan letters and the ‘#CheerForPastel＊Palettes’ tag we just read—they were full of joy and excitement!
Hina: Right? And we love our fans just as much as they love us.
Hina: That’s why when something good happens, we celebrate together, and when things are tough, we cheer each other on.
Hina: So instead of “for the fans,” I think “working together with them” fits us better, don’t you?
Aya: Not “for the fans” but “working together”...
Maya: You might be right.
Maya: Just like the fans say they can do their best because Pastel＊Palettes exists, I think we’re able to keep going because they’re there for us too.
Aya: So... fans and idols... it’s not a one-way street.
Hina: Exactly! Maybe we’re not “to” the fans, we’re “with” the fans, you know?
Eve: Not “to” but “with”...?
Aya: Wow, You’re totally right, Hina-chan...!
Aya: Being a fan and being an idol... it’s not about doing things “to” each other. It’s about doing our best “with” each other!
Hina: Yes! Knew you’d get it, Aya-chan♪
Chisato: That’s such a perfect way to put it—for all of us.
Eve: A “with” kind of bond with our fans... It really is beautiful!
Aya: Alright... Let’s give it our all next time too. Together with our fans!
All: Yeah!
(Black cut)
(Background change)
