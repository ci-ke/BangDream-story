event100-02 Chapter 1: Chii-chan's So Cool

During elementary school, Chisato and Kaoru's relationship was completely different.

[A Few Years Earlier...]

[Elementary School]

Chisato: "If you want the vaccine, listen to everything I have to say. That is the rule of this city."
Kaoru: Chii-chan!
Chisato: Kao-chan. Hello. What's wrong?
Kaoru: Ah... Sorry. Were you practicing?
Chisato: No, it's fine.
Kaoru: You know, I watched it yesterday! "The City of Solitude"!
Chisato: Thanks. What did you think?
Kaoru: Chii-chan, you were so cool~...
Kaoru: You were so powerful when you confronted the main character, it was a little bit scary~...
Chisato: I never ever speak to my friends like that, so it was a really difficult scene to do.
Chisato: But I am an actor. I have to act out things I've never done before all the time.
Kaoru: That's so cool~...!
Classmate A: Chisato-chan! I watched yesterday's episode of "The City of Solitude"!
Chisato: Really? Thanks!
Classmate B: Chisato-chan, you're actually very scary~.
Chisato: Huh? What makes you say that?
Classmate A: You were fighting in the show. I didn't know you were so scary!
Kaoru: Ah...
Chisato: That was just acting. I wasn't actually fighting.
Classmate A: Kao-chan, did you watch it?
Kaoru: Ch-Chii-chan... isn't like that, you know...
Classmate B: Whatever! I'm gonna keep watching anyway!
Chisato: Thank you.
Kaoru: ...
Chisato: It was all just an act. I'm not bothered by what they said.
Kaoru: But...! Chii-chan, they have the wrong idea about you!
Chisato: Yes, but I can't tone down my acting just because of that. There's nothing I can do about it.
Kaoru: But you're a nice person in real life...
Chisato: Kao-chan...
Chisato: Thanks for saying that. But I'm okay, I promise.
Kaoru: Alright...
Chisato: I don't have any jobs tomorrow. Why don't you come over to my house?
Kaoru: Really? I would love to!
Chisato: Let's walk Leon together.
Kaoru: Y-yeah...! I'll try.
Chisato: Wait, what's wrong?
Kaoru: Leon jumped on me last time. I was so surprised that I fell over...
Chisato: You did?
Kaoru: Y-yeah~! That's why, this time, I want to try harder to stay upright when I get jumped on!
Chisato: Haha! Leon really loves you Kao-chan.
Kaoru: That's what I thought! Okay, I will not fall over...!
Chisato: How about I let you hold Leon's leash?
Kaoru: B-but Leon will pull me over again.
Chisato: Ahaha! Mama will come with us, so it'll be fine.
Kaoru: You think so~? That sounds scary though~!
Chisato: It'll be fine! And I'll help you hold it if you need me to.
Kaoru: O-okay. Yes, please...!
Chisato: Leave it to me. I'm looking forward to it already♪
