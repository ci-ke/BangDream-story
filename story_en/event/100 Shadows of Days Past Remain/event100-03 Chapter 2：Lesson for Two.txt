event100-03 Chapter 2: Lesson for Two

On one of their days off, Kaoru finds Chisato's script while visiting her at home.

[The Next Day]

[Shirasagi Residence - Living Room]

Kaoru: Hello. Sorry to intrude.
Chisato: Come in! I got some tasty treats at a shoot. Let's eat them together!
Kaoru: Wow, thanks!
Kaoru: Yum! They're so sweet and fluffy~...!
Chisato: Yes, they are delicious. They're called dacquoise.
Kaoru: D-duck... waz... I see! It does look like a duck.
Chisato: Huh...?!
Kaoru: If it's called a duck-waz, then it must be because it looks like a duck, right? See, if you look closely, it looks like a duck's bill!
Chisato: D-does it...?
Kaoru: But I wonder what "waz" means. Waz... maybe it means...
Chisato: Means...?
Kaoru: Y-you know... It is but that!
Chisato: Fufu...! What does that even mean?
Kaoru: Maybe I'm wrong. Anyhow, where did you get them again?
Chisato: I got them from one of the older actors on the show. I love how sweet these are.
Kaoru: Chii-chan, you're amazing~...! You can talk to adults the right way, and you know about sweets that I've never even heard of!
Chisato: Fufu. I didn't know what dacquoise were at first either.
Kaoru: ... What's that over there? A script? Can I read it?
Chisato: Of course. But you can't tell anyone I let you, okay?
Kaoru: Sure! Thanks.
Kaoru: Hmm... "Hey, you! What're you doing out here?"
Chisato: "My name is Akane, not 'you'."
Kaoru: Whoa! Chii-chan, did you memorize all of these lines?
Chisato: Sure did. Try another line, then I'll say the one after it.
Kaoru: "Let's make a deal."
Chisato: "Fine. However, I have one condition. You must give all of the people living here free access to food, clothing, and shelter."
Kaoru: You really do remember them all~...!
Chisato: It's honestly nothing.
Kaoru: That's amazing. You memorize hard lines like these, all while still going to school... and you get good grades.
Kaoru: Chii-chan, why do you want to become an actor anyway?
Chisato: I don't want to become an actor, I am one!
Kaoru: Ah, you're right. Sorry.
Chisato: Why did I become an actor...? Um...
Kaoru: Oh? Was I not supposed to ask? Sorry!
Chisato: No, that's not it. I became an actor because I think that the more you can express yourself, the richer your life will be.
Kaoru: Your life will be richer...? That's kind of hard to understand.
Chisato: Right? Being an actor is a difficult job.
Chisato: That's why I want to become an actor who can play any role she's given. Because then I'll become a person who can live a great life.
Kaoru: I see...
Chisato: And if you're an actor, you get the chance to turn into many different people.
Chisato: You can be a cool person or a cute person... a kind person or a cold person... There are all sorts of people you could be.
Chisato: You can even become your favorite princess or prince charming. Don't you think that's just wonderful?
Kaoru: All sorts of people... Yeah, that sure is wonderful!
Chisato: Exactly. That's why I became an actor.
Chisato: You know what? Since you're here, won't you practice my lines with me? You can read the ones that aren't mine.
Kaoru: Are you sure? I do want to try...!
Chisato: Okay then, let's go from the beginning.
Kaoru: "You- How dare you say that?!!"
Kaoru: ...
Chisato: What's wrong?
Kaoru: I can't possibly say these kinds of words to you, Chii-chan~...!
Chisato: Fufu... Ahaha! We're just acting, so it's fine.
Kaoru: But...
Chisato: Kao-chan, you're so nice. Should we try a different scene instead?
Kaoru: S-sorry, Chii-chan... Thanks.
Chisato: Don't worry about it. Alright, let's try this scene on page ten. Are you ready?
