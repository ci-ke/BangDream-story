event88-07 Ending: A Haiku After My Own Heart

While Misaki is the only one struggling to come up with a haiku, everyone sets off handheld fireworks by the river.

(Character: Kokoro Tsurumaki, Kaoru Seta, Hagumi Kitazawa, Kanon Matsubara, Misaki Okusawa)

[Fireworks Festival - Event Location]

Misaki: I'm the only one who couldn't come up with anything...
Hagumi: No sweat, Mii-kun! You'll think of something soon enough!
Kokoro: Exactly! Don't give up, Misaki!
Misaki: All this encouragement makes me feel even more useless.
Misaki: Oh well, no sense stressing over it. I'll give it some more thought once I get home.
Misaki: The fireworks should be ending soon, so... Hmmm...?
Child 1: Hahaha! Look, look! So pretty!
Child 2: Hey, I want one like that~.
Kaoru: Handheld fireworks. Fufu, how sweet.
Kanon: The big ones haven't even stopped yet... I guess those kids couldn't wait.
Misaki: Probably not. It does look like fun, though...
Kokoro: We should play with our own!
Kanon: Huh...?!
Kokoro: That store is selling them! I'll buy some for us!
Hagumi: Wait, Kokoron! I'm going with you~!
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Fireworks Festival Event Location - Riverside]

Kokoro: Look, Hagumi! This one changed from blue to yellow!
Hagumi: Cool~! I wonder what color this one is~.
Kaoru: To light this one or this one. That is the question...!
Misaki: It's hard to choose. Hm, what color should I choose?
Kanon: You all seem to be having fun. Ah, I-I am too, of course! They aren't as fancy as the bigger ones, but handheld fireworks have their own charm...
Misaki: Yes, we know~.
Misaki: ... Hello, Happy World! is kinda like fireworks, isn't it?
Kanon: Huh? We are...?
Misaki: Kokoro, Hagumi, and even Kaoru-san are like sky fireworks. Kokoro especially feels like a massive eye-catching explosion, wouldn't you say?
Kanon: Ah... Yeah, I can see that...! So, does that mean we're the handheld kind?
Misaki: Pretty much. I don't think we're ones to make as flashy of a display as the others...
Kokoro: What a wonderful discussion! Misaki, Kanon!
Misaki: Huh?!
Kanon: Kokoro-chan...?!
Kokoro: Alright, you two. Come this way. Your conversation has given me an idea.
Kanon: Where? By the river? Why...?
Kokoro: Telling you would ruin the surprise!
Kanon & Misaki: ...?
(Black cut)
Kanon: Um, is this... the right spot...? Ah...
Kanon: Both the sky and handheld fireworks are reflecting on the water's surface...
Kokoro: I got the idea from what Misaki said earlier.
Kokoro: Whether big or small, each and every firework can make people smile.
Kokoro: So, seeing both types together would make for an even more lovely show!
Misaki: Together, huh...? I think I see what you mean...
Misaki: ... Ah.
Kanon: ...? Misaki-chan?
Kokoro: Have you finally come up with your haiku? Tell us!
Hagumi: Really? Did it finally come to you, Mii-kun?
Kaoru: What glorious tidings! My ears hunger for it.
Misaki: Hm~. I did, but it's a secret for now.
Misaki: I'll share it once summer break is over and our homework is turned in.
Hagumi: Huh~?! I can't wait that long~!
Kokoro: Don't worry, Hagumi. Misaki is not one to break a promise. Now we just have to wait for the end of our vacation!
Kanon: We'll listen to it whenever you're ready.
Kaoru: I await your haiku with bated breath.
Misaki: Alright, but... try not to get your hopes up...
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Walking Home]

Misaki: Okay, then. Bye-bye.
Misaki: Phew. Man, I'm beat.
Misaki: ... Whether big or small, fireworks are fireworks. Viewing them together makes for a more beautiful display. Hm...
Misaki: "On riverside winds... Five flashes closely nestled... Kindred fireworks..."
Misaki: Yep. My magnum opus. Just kidding... Hahaha.
(Black cut)
