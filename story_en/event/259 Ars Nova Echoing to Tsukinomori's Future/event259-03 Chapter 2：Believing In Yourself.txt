event259-03 Chapter 2: Believing In Yourself

Even though it’s time to gather, Mashiro doesn’t show up. Worried, Tsukushi runs out of the atelier...

(Character: Mashiro Kurata, Toko Kirigaya, Nanami Hiromachi, Tsukushi Futaba, Rui Yashio)

[Hiromachi Residence - Atelier]

Toko: She’s late! What’s Shiro even doing!? I’ve been messaging her, but she hasn’t read anything!
Toko: Ugh, seriously! We need to have a meeting about what to do since everyone’s scared of the chairwoman!
Nanami: Toko-chan, calm down. Getting frustrated won’t change anything, you know?
Toko: Sorry... Yeah, I guess I was getting a little worked up.
Rui: Let’s start without her. Every minute counts right now.
Nanami: Yeah... you’re right.
Tsukushi: ............
Rui: Futaba-san? Were you even listening?
Tsukushi: Oh, s-sorry. I was just thinking about Mashiro-chan...
Tsukushi: Being told we were making trouble... it really seemed to hit her hard.
Nanami: Yeah... even during lunch afterward, she barely touched her food.
Tsukushi: (The look in Mashiro-chan’s eyes back then... I can’t stop thinking about it...)
(White cut)

(Memory cut-in)

(Background change)
(White cut)

(Memory cut-out)

(Background change)
Tsukushi: (It’s like looking at the old me from not too long ago...)
Tsukushi: ...Alright, I’m going to go find Mashiro-chan!
Nanami: What? Tsuu-chan!? Where are you going~~~?
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Park]

Mashiro: (What am I even doing...? Everyone’s waiting for me. But still...)
Mashiro: I don’t even know why I’m doing this anymore...
Tsukushi: Mashiro-chan~...!
Tsukushi: Haah... haah... I finally found you...
Toko: I caught up! Wait, Shiro!?
Mashiro: Everyone...
Tsukushi: What are you doing out here? Come on, let’s go. You’ll catch a cold if you stay here.
Mashiro: ........................I can’t go.
Mashiro: Let’s stop this... the signature campaign... it’s meaningless.
Mashiro: I don’t want to keep doing unnecessary things and end up hurting everyone anymore...
Toko: So, does that mean you’re giving up on the Tsukinomori Music Festival?
Mashiro: ............
Rui: Kurata-san. Time may be short, but we still have some left. I think it’s too early to give up.
Nanami: Yeah, exactly! If we all put our heads together, we’ll come up with a great idea~!
Mashiro: But... no one wants it.
Toko: So what? Just because no one wants it, you’re going to give up?
Toko: Weren’t you the one who said you wanted to bring energy back to Tsukinomori?
Mashiro: ...! That... that was a mistake...!
Mashiro: I thought holding the Tsukinomori Music Festival would make everyone feel better...!
Mashiro: But that wasn’t the case. Everyone just found it a bother...!
Mashiro: If it’s going to turn out like this, then I wish I’d never thought about bringing energy back to Tsukinomori...!
Tsukushi: ............That’s not true.
Tsukushi: It wasn’t a mistake... The feelings you had back then, Mashiro-chan, they weren’t wrong!
Mashiro: ...Tsukushi-chan...?
Tsukushi: It must’ve been scary... it must’ve been so hard... I understand how you feel.
Tsukushi: When the things you believed in can’t be trusted anymore... You feel lost, like you don’t know what to do or where to go.
Tsukushi: But even so, the feeling of wanting to bring energy back to Tsukinomori— I don’t think that feeling is wrong.
Mashiro: Why...? Why can you say that...?
Tsukushi: Because that’s a feeling Mashiro-chan found precisely because of your time at Tsukinomori!
Tsukushi: When you joined Tsukinomori, so much happened, right?
Tsukushi: We were depressed after our first performance, couldn’t write lyrics, and Morfonica almost fell apart...
Tsukushi: But we also went shopping, went camping, had Christmas parties together...
Tsukushi: All the tough times and the fun times, everything you’ve been through— that’s why you grew to love Tsukinomori, right?
Tsukushi: Because of those memories, Mashiro-chan, you wanted to bring energy back to Tsukinomori.
Tsukushi: That’s why you can’t deny those feelings.
Tsukushi: If you deny them, it’s like denying everything you experienced at Tsukinomori.
Mashiro: But... but it’s causing trouble for everyone...
Tsukushi: It’s okay! We can do this!
Tsukushi: And Mashiro-chan, you haven't given up yet. Because you’re not running away.
Tsukushi: Even if you’re lost or scared, you were still trying to come to the atelier, and that’s why you’re here in this park along the way, right?
Tsukushi: The fact that you’re here shows that you haven’t given up!
Mashiro: ――――！
Tsukushi: Let’s hold our heads high, together. For our past selves.
Tsukushi: Let’s see this through to the end and be the kind of people we can be proud of.
Toko: Hey, Shiro. To be honest, I’m feeling a little nervous too.
Toko: But at the same time, I also feel like it’s going to be okay. Because we’re Morfonica, you know!
Nanami: Yeah, yeah. We’ve messed up plenty of times before, but we’ve always ended up smiling in the end.
Nanami: When it comes to not giving up, we're always so straightforward~
Rui: Kurata-san, you once said, ‘The one thing I can do is believe in myself.’
Rui: And just like you said, no matter what happened, you always believed in yourself and overcame so many challenges.
Rui: So this time, why not trust yourself again?
Mashiro: Everyone...
(White cut)
(Background change)
Mashiro: (This light... I know it... The light that pushed me forward and guided me to the stage...)
Mashiro: (The light that Kasumi-san showed me, a sky full of shining stars... warm, courageous light...)
(White cut)
(Background change)
Mashiro: ............
Mashiro: Everyone, I’m sorry... I ended up saying a bunch of weak stuff again.
Mashiro: But... I want to try again. Because I want to hold the Tsukinomori Music Festival. Because I want to cheer everyone up.
Tsukushi: Mashiro-chan...!
Mashiro: Thank you, Tsukushi-chan. I’m going to hold my head high for my past self too.
(Black cut)
(Background change)
