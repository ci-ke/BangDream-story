[Residential Area]

Misaki: Huh~. So the fortunetelling thing was that incredible?
Kanon: Yeah. The set was really well made, too. Even the fortuneteller... It was almost like she was actually a witch...
Misaki: Right. Well, in any case, going someplace you normally wouldn't does sound like it'd help you come up with good ideas.

Kanon: Ahaha... After trying to do this all on my own, I feel like I understand a lot better how amazing of a thing it is you're always doing, Misaki-chan.
Misaki: Nah, I just take everyone's ideas and fit the pieces together, that's all.
Misaki: ... Hey, Kanon-san. If you ever feel like you need help, just let me know, alright?
Misaki: I can't give you advice, but... I can probably give you a little bit of a hand.
Kanon: O-okay... Thanks, Misaki-chan.
Misaki: Man, we've got to go to school tomorrow, huh...? Kokoro and the others have definitely forgot. I'll have to remind them at practice.
Kanon: Ahaha... They've been having all sorts of fun this summer break, haven't they?
Kaoru: Ahh...! What a glorious day it is...! The sky shimmers, the birds chirp, and do mine ears deceive me, or is that the sound of fortuitous bells ringing from above...?!
Misaki: Huh...? Over there... That's Kaoru-san, right?
Kanon: Yeah... I wonder what she's doing. She looks like she's having the time of her life.
Kaoru: Oh...? If it isn't Misaki and Kanon. For the three of us to meet here, this must be fate...!
Misaki: C'mon, we've run into each other here a few times at least... So? What's up? Did something good happen?
Kaoru: Indeed. I was struck by inspiration just moments ago, you see. A line I have toiled over endlessly was suddenly transformed into a thing of beauty.
Kaoru: Near the end of the play, the protagonist calls out to the heroine. What was originally, "Welcome home, Andrée," I have dramatically changed into... "Welcome home, Andrée."
Misaki: I... literally couldn't tell if there was any difference...
Kaoru: The delivery of the former was filled with joy at their reunion, while the latter was filled with both that, their love towards Andrée, and the deep sorrow of decades spent apart.
Misaki: W-wow~... Could you tell, Kanon-san?
Kanon: Ah. Ummm~... Sort of... I guess...? Maybe...?
Kaoru: With this, next week's performance will undoubtedly be a monumental success. Forsooth, inspiration do be a fickle little kitten.
Misaki: Well... I mean, if you're happy with it, then good for you, I guess...
Kanon: ... Oh. Wait, could this be...?
Kaoru: ... ? What is it, Kanon?
Kanon: What the fortuneteller said. How you're going to find something you've been searching for. She must have been talking about this, right...?
Kaoru: I see... Indeed, the little fortunetelling kitten did say as such.
Kaoru: Which means that she foresaw this coming, then.
Misaki: Whoa, that's actually incredible...
Kaoru: Fufu, so, even the goddess of fate herself was looking forward to my exploits. Then I must continue to polish my performance so that I may live up to her expectations on the day of!
Kanon: Good for you, Kaoru-san! I'll come see your show, okay?
Kaoru: And I shall provide to you the utmost of entertainment on that day. Look forward to it.
