event196-07 Ending: A Place to Return To

Kanon encounters a girl while wandering around the city...

(Character: Kokoro Tsurumaki, Kaoru Seta, Hagumi Kitazawa, Kanon Matsubara, Misaki Okusawa)

Kanon: ... I ran out of the club room...
Kanon: ... Is it really... not too late...? That's what Eve-chan said, but... but...
Kanon: ... What am I supposed to do...?
Little Girl: ...
Kanon: Huh...? That girl...
Kanon: Is something wrong? Why are you crouching here...? Oh, is that...a drum case?
Little Girl: Yeah... I heard there's an instrument shop around here, so... I thought I'd go sell my snare there...
Kanon: Huh...?
Little Girl: But I've gotten lost... and I've been carrying this thing around, so I'm all worn out...
Kanon: ...
Kanon: ... You're... giving up...?
Little Girl: ... Yeah, I... I was thinking, I should...
Kanon: Oh... Is that... really okay with you?
Little Girl: ... What?
Kanon: You'll definitely regret it if you give up now.
Little Girl: ...
Kanon: ... Didn't you want to play in a band? Isn't that why you picked up the drums?
Little Girl: ... But I couldn't find anybody to play with... Anyone who seemed like they'd start a band with me...
Kanon: ... Have you ever actually told someone, "Let's start a band together?"
Little Girl: ... That's... No, but...
Kanon: You figured that someone would reach out to you... Hold their hand out and lead you to happiness. Isn't that what you thought?
Little Girl: ... But I'm so bad at drumming... How could I ask someone else to play with me...? I was so scared...
Kanon: Whether you're good or not isn't something you decide.
Kanon: And everyone gets scared. Anyone would be scared, at first.
Kanon: ... Why did you want to be in a band?
Little Girl: Because it looked like fun... Because then I wouldn't be alone anymore...
Kanon: Yeah. You're right. True fun isn't something you can create all on your own, after all.
Little Girl: ...
Kanon: I'm sure that there are people out there who are waiting, just like you. Waiting for you.
Kanon: So why not try find a little bit of courage, and... take the first step?
Kanon: There's no such thing as being too late to start something. As long as you don't give up, you can always try again!
Kanon: (You're right, Eve-chan...)
Little Girl: ... But I already sold my sticks...
Kanon: ... Then, here.
Little Girl: ... A... stick case...?
Kanon: Take my drumsticks. I've always had them on me. You can start drumming again with these, right?
Little Girl: Huh? But...
Kanon: Take them. Use them, and start again.
Little Girl: ...
Kanon: Now come on. Stand up. I know you can do it. Take the first step, and never look back.
Little Girl: I... I'll try sticking to it... a little more... Thank you.
Kanon: ... That's a good smile. Take care of yourself.
(White cut)
(Background change)

[Tsurumaki Residence]

Kanon: Pant... Pant...! S-sorry I'm late, everyone!
Kokoro, Hagumi, Kaoru & Misaki: Kanon! Kano-chan-senpai! Kanon-san!
Kokoro: You're finally here! Alright, let's start practice!
Kaoru: I see you were slightly delayed in your arrival... Did you happen to perhaps lose your way?
Kanon: I stayed up really late last night working on my song, so I overslept... I'm sorry to make you all have to wait for me.
Hagumi: Oh, that's right! How's the song going, Kano-chan-senpai?!
Kanon: Th-that's right, um, the score...
Kanon: H-huh...?
Misaki: Kanon-san? What's wrong?
Kanon: That's weird... My stick case is usually in my bag, but...
Misaki: What?! Wait, did you lose it?! The score was in there, wasn't it...?
Kanon: Y-yeah... Wh-what should I do? Did I forget it somewhere...?
Kaoru: Gasp...! Could this be? If I were to guess, this may very well be the "something very precious to you" that the soothsayer foretold you would lose...
Kaoru: Which means... that fortune did indeed come to pass...
Kanon: ... No, it didn't! That fortune was wrong. Could someone lend me some paper and a pen?
Kokoro: I've got both of those here! Here you go, Kanon!
Misaki: Kanon-san...?
Kanon: I realized it on the way here, actually. How lucky I am to have been able to meet all of you.
Kanon: And after I reminded myself of that fact, the words suddenly started flowing in my head.
Kanon: (Word after word, all connecting together... If I can put my feelings right now into song, I know...!)
(Black cut)
Kanon: ... Done. Please, take a look, everyone.
Kokoro, Misaki, Hagumi & Kaoru: ...!
Kokoro: This is amazing, Kanon! It's so, so, sooo full of all of your love!
Hagumi: Yeah! I sorta feel all warm and fuzzy inside now!
Kaoru: Ah, what passion, and on top of that... such overflowing kindness, do these lyrics hold.
Misaki: I... think we've got a hit on our hands, here. No, not "think." I know.
Kanon: ... Thanks, everyone.
Kokoro: Oooh~!! Come on, guys! Let's hurry up and play Kanon's song!
Hagumi: Yeah, yeah!
Kanon: Oh, hold on a second, everybody.
Misaki: What's up?
Kanon: Well, actually... I still haven't figured out the melody yet, so...
Kanon: ... Could you all help me work on it?
Kokoro: Of course we will! Doing things you can't do alone is what bands are for! Our summer break's just getting started!
Hagumi: Ah! I just came up with something! Hm-hmhm-hmmm~♪
Kokoro: That's great, Hagumi! Then I'll do this! La-lala-lala~♪
Misaki: Ahhh, wait, you two! I've gotta write this stuff down!
Kanon: ...
Kaoru: Fufu... It seems to me you have quite the fleeting piece on your hands.
Kanon: Yeah. I may have lost my score, but... it's transformed into something even more precious to me.
Kaoru: I see... In other words, you've faced your future that might have been.
Kanon: I don't know about that, but... I do have this feeling that I had a strange dream last night.
Kanon: And when I woke up today, I felt really refreshed.
Kaoru: Then perhaps in your subconscious, you faced your fate and rewrote it a fraction, no? Nay, I'm sure of it.
Kanon: Oh... So that's why the fortune didn't come true.
Kaoru: No... For the fortuneteller also spake unto you thus... "I'm quite confident you're capable of overcoming this trial."
Kanon: Huh...? So it did come true, then? Or did it not...? Which is it...?
Kaoru: It is...
Kaoru: But that.
Kanon: Fufu... Right!
Kokoro: What are you doing, you two? Come over here so we can make a fun song together!
Kanon & Kaoru: Okay! Indeed.
