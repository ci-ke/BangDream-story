event257-04 Chapter 3: Shining, Radiant Love

Aya and Maya dedicate themselves to extra practice. Aya’s meaningful words deeply resonate with Maya and Nao.

(Character: Aya Maruyama, Maya Yamato)

[A few days later]

[In the studio]

Nao: Maruyama, Yamato— it’s getting late. Let’s call it a day for now.
Aya: Huh? It’s already this late? We were just planning to do a bit of practice, but we really got into it!
Maya: Yeah. But thanks to that, both the dancing and the playing have improved a lot.
Nao: Oh, right, Maruyama— how’s your private lessons with Fuyumi-sensei going?
Aya: She’s super strict, as expected. But I’m learning so much.
Nao: I see. Fuyumi-sensei even said, ‘I wish you had this much determination back in your idol days,’ and gave me a little jab about it. You really do love lessons, don’t you, Maruyama?
Aya: Ehehe... I just want to make our performance as good as it can be.
Maya: Yeah. If we keep this up, I’m sure the judges and the audience will vote for us, right?
Nao: Yeah, I think they’ll really appreciate what we’ve done. We can definitely win the third round.
Aya: Exactly! But... it’s not just that...
Aya: I can’t wait to see everyone’s smiling faces!
Aya: This performance is so much fun, there’s no way they won’t enjoy it, right?
Maya & Nao: ............
Aya: ...Huh? What’s wrong, you two?
Nao: No, it’s just... that really hit me, you know?
Maya: Yeah. Same here.
Aya: Huh? What do you mean?
Maya: How do I put it... I think I got so caught up in wanting the judges and audience to rate us highly, that I forgot something important.
Nao: Yeah. The people watching us just want to enjoy the stage, pure and simple.
Maya: It’s like Aya-san reminded us of what really matters most as idols.
Maya: Aya-san, you’re amazing!
Aya: W-Wait, I’m not amazing or anything!
Nao: Haha, Maruyama, you’re always like this, aren’t you? Oh—don’t tell me you forgot this is still a competition?
Aya: N-No way! Of course I want to win, okay?!
Aya: But thinking about how the audience will definitely enjoy it... it’s just so exciting, you know?
Aya: That feeling kind of came out first, I guess...
Nao: Ahaha, Maruyama, you’re like... it’s like you were born to be an idol.
Maya: That’s exactly it! Aya-san, you’re a natural-born idol!
Nao: I get that. If I had to say more... you’re kind of an idol nerd?
Aya: W-Wait, that’s not a compliment, is it~?
Nao: No, no, it is! I’m serious.
Nao: Oh, by the way, Maruyama— the office staff said they need to check your schedule before you leave.
Aya: Really? Okay, I’ll go get changed first!
Nao: You know, Maruyama really is something else.
Nao: She’s not the most technically skilled, and she’s not the fastest at picking things up. She even trips up during the most important moments...
Nao: But as an idol, her love for it is unmatched. She’s always looking out—not just for the members, but for the fans, the staff, and everyone involved. She never leaves anyone behind.
Nao: It’s idols like her who are truly loved. That’s what I think.
Maya: ...I think so too! Aya-san is the idol I really look up to.
Nao: ...I used to have people like that, too. No matter how down I got, they were always there to cheer me up, never letting go of my hand.
Maya: Nao-san...
Nao: Haha, they were such meddling, endlessly cheerful people. But honestly, that kind of stubbornness is something I really respect.
Maya: (She says that, but... )
Maya: (Nao-san’s eyes were sparkling just now. Those people must mean so much to her.)
Maya: (Maybe it’s the same for me, with Pastel*Palettes.)
Maya: (Even though what Nao-san said earlier about Aya-san still stung a little...)
Maya: (She only said it because she cares about Pastel*Palettes.)
Nao: Alright then, great job today. Make sure you show up to the studio tomorrow with that same energy!
Maya: ...Got it! Thank you so much for today!
Nao: Yeah, I’m counting on you, Yamato~.
Maya: ...Huh? Isn’t that your phone on the table, Nao-san? It’s ringing.
Nao: Oh, you’re right. A message. Huh, looks like it’s a media interview request.
Maya: Whoa, that’s great news!
Nao: Let’s see here... It’s from an entertainment news site, and...
Nao: ......！
Maya: What is it?
Nao: Yeah, it was just a follow-up email about the interview. We did it—yay~☆!
Maya: I see... Um, Nao-san, you don’t look so well...
Nao: Huh? Really? Oh no— I might’ve overdone it a little lately.
Nao: But more importantly, Yamato, you should get changed too. The studio’s about to close, right?
Maya: Okay...
Maya: Um, Nao-san?
Nao: Hm?
Maya: If something’s wrong, you can tell us, okay?We’re here for you.
Nao: Huh? What’s with you all of a sudden? I’m just a little tired, that’s all.
Maya: R-Right... Sorry for bringing it up out of nowhere...
Maya: But seriously, if you’re ever really in trouble, please don’t hesitate to tell us.
Maya: We’re a team, after all.
Nao: ......
Nao: Haha, Yamato, you’re such a good kid. If something happens, I promise I’ll rely on you.
Nao: Alright, alright, now head on home already
Maya: Got it. Well then, see you tomorrow. Good work today!
Nao: ...They really are such good kids... Which is exactly why I need to figure something out...
(Black cut)
