event257-03 Chapter 2: Honestly, You Are

Each person does what they can. Surprisingly, the one who acts alongside Nao is...

(Character: Aya Maruyama, Hina Hikawa, Chisato Shirasagi, Maya Yamato, Eve Wakamiya)

[In the producer’s car]

Chisato: It’s lucky we were able to meet them now. The timing worked out perfectly, didn’t it?
Nao: ...Yeah.
Chisato: Even though they’ve stepped back from the spotlight, I heard they’re still very busy... Nao-san? Is something wrong?
Nao: ...Now that we’re actually going to meet them, I’m starting to get really nervous!
Chisato: Nao-san...
Nao: I know, I know! I get it! Man... Shirasagi, you’re so tough on me sometimes!
Chisato: I’m just surprised. I didn’t think Nao-san got nervous about anything.
Nao: Shirasagi?! I’m human too, you know! Every time I see the TIS results, my heart’s practically pounding out of my chest!
Chisato: Haha, I suppose that’s true. Sorry about that.
Nao: ...To be honest, I didn’t think you’d come along with me.
Chisato: Huh...? But why?
Nao: Well, I mean, it’s not like you’re super fond of me, right?
Chisato: ...That’s true.
Nao: Ahaha! Wow, you’re so honest for once!
Chisato: This isn’t the time for that, though. I just want to do whatever I can to help.
Nao: Yeah, you’re right.
Nao: Honestly, as the producer, I should be the one to nail this perfectly... but...
Chisato: ......
Nao: —If we’ve got time to talk about this, then let’s just focus on what we need to do!
Nao: Alright! I’ll keep driving safely from here on out!
Chisato: ...To be honest, I still think you’re a bit hard to figure out, Nao-san. But... I do trust you, to a certain extent.
Chisato: We’ve had our ups and downs, sure. But over time, it’s become clear to me that you really care about us.
Nao: Shirasagi...
Chisato: And, if I could borrow someone’s words— ‘We’re a team, all of us together.’
Chisato: Performers, staff—we all have our roles to fulfill. I think working together to give it our all is what makes us a team.
Chisato: Excuses have no place on the stage, and I don’t want to make any. That’s the responsibility of an idol.
Chisato: So, if we’re going to keep moving forward, let’s each focus on doing our part to the very best of our abilities.
Nao: ...Yeah, you’re right.
Nao: Alright then, let’s hurry up! Hold on tight!
Chisato: W-Wait, please drive safely— Eek, aaaaahh!!
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[A few days later]

[In the rehearsal studio]

Aya: One, two, three, four, five, six, seven, eight...! And finish with a ta-da pose!
Aya: ...S-So? How was it?
Eve: It’s super cute choreography! I think it’ll really stick in the audience’s hearts!
Aya: Right?! That’s Fuyumi-sensei for you...!
Hina: It looks so fun! I can’t wait to do it! This level of moves is totally doable while playing!
Chisato: It matches the song perfectly. All the ideas we shared really came together in a good way.
Eve: Yeah! It was totally worth checking out the Idol Research Club!
Aya: And the feedback from the staging team was really helpful too! Right, Maya-chan?
Maya: ......
Nao: What’s up, Yamato? Something on your mind?
Maya: Well, the choreography is super cute and catchy. If all the members do it together, it’ll definitely leave a big impact on the audience. It’s just...
Chisato: If I had to say whether it’s enough to guarantee we’ll win... I think we’re still missing something—just a little push.
Maya: So you think so too, Chisato-san?
Nao: Of course, Fuyumi-sensei noticed it too. But with the time we have left and what we’re capable of right now, this was the best she could come up with.
Aya: ...Just one more step, huh.
Hina: I dunno, I kinda like this dance. Look—spin and turn, like this!
Aya: W-Wait, what?! Hina-chan, how are you already perfect at this!?
Hina: Huh? But Aya-chan showed it to us just now, right? It’s easy to remember—just like that!
Chisato: I think that’s just Hina-chan, though.
Eve: As expected of Hina-san!
Hina: But you know, this kind of dancing— we haven’t really done it that much, but dancing together as a team feels so fun and exciting!
Aya: I totally get that! I’m so happy I get to dance together with everyone!
Aya: I wasn’t very good at it back during my trainee days, but dancing as a team was so much fun back then!
Eve: Aya-san, so you’ve loved dancing ever since your trainee days?
Aya: Nope, I started loving it way before that. It all started with a Marmalade concert.
Aya: They had this signature dance song, you know? And when it started, everyone in the audience would...
Aya: Oh!
Maya: What is it, Aya-san?
Aya: I just had a great idea! What do you think about this?
Aya: So here’s what I’m thinking—
(Black cut)
