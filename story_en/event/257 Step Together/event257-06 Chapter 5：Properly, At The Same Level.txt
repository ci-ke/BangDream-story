event257-06 Chapter 5: Properly, At The Same Level

After the celebration, Pastel✽Palettes rejoice over the positive reactions to their third-round performance. Watching them, Nao narrows her eyes with a gentle smile...

(Character: Aya Maruyama, Hina Hikawa, Chisato Shirasagi, Maya Yamato, Eve Wakamiya)

[After the celebration]

[On the way home]

Hina: Mmm~ I’m so full! Nao-san’s recommendation for Chinese food was so good!
Aya: I know, right? I’m so satisfied! The xiaolongbao was amazing... Though, I think I might’ve eaten a little too much.
Chisato: It’s fine, just for today. This was probably the toughest round we’ve ever faced, after all.
Aya: Exactly! Let’s keep working hard starting tomorrow, Chisato-chan!
Maya: Oh, look! There’s already so much buzz on social media!
Aya: Whoa, really? Let me see, let me see!
Hina: ‘The dance was so much fun! I hope they do it at a live concert too!’ ‘Can’t believe Pastel＊Palettes made it through... They’re this round’s dark horse!’ ‘Could they beat the favorites and take the championship? I’m rooting for them!’
Eve: Oh, even the ViVidCanvas team sent a message! ‘Congratulations on clearing the third round! We’re cheering for Pastel*Palettes on your road to the Budokan!’ ...!
Aya: Ugh... Seeing so many people cheering for us... I’m just so happy~!
Hina: Oh! Aya-chan’s full-on emotional moment—it’s been a while since we’ve seen that!
Chisato: Haha, well, it’s been a tough road to get here. I guess she finally let her guard down a little.
Aya: Ugh, sorry. I told myself I’d save the tears for the very end~.
Nao: It’s okay to cry when you need to. That’s just the kind of idol you are, Maruyama.
Nao: You know, Ayumi was like that too. She’d cry all the time, but every time she did, she’d come back stronger.
Nao: Haha... That really brings back memories...
Aya: Nao-san?
Nao: You know, watching everyone face this challenge head-on... it reminded me of when I was in Marmalade.
Nao: Back then, we hit so many walls, over and over again. But no matter what, we were determined to make it to the Budokan— together, with our members and our staff.
Nao: I used to have that same feeling too. But as I grew up and became a producer, I guess I forgot about it.
Nao: When I was performing, what meant the most to me was having adults around us who would give their all and run alongside us. That’s what made me happiest...
Nao: But somewhere along the line, I forgot something so simple.
Nao: I thought that, as someone who quit being an idol, I had to be the one pointing you toward the right path, guiding you away from anything risky. I got so stubborn about it... I kept pointing, but I never really stood beside you.
Nao: I’ve said unreasonable things, and I’ve been pushy at times. I know I haven’t been the best producer.
Nao: Even now, I don’t think being an idol is all sunshine and roses.
Nao: I still believe you shouldn’t carry too many expectations on your shoulders. And I know we’ll probably clash again in the future... but still...
Nao: I want to be someone you can rely on. That feeling hasn’t changed. So... let me keep doing my best alongside you.
Aya: Nao-san?
Maya: Um... There were times when I was surprised by your decisions, Nao-san, and even felt uncertain about them. But now, I understand they were things we needed.
Eve: Yes! Even when your words were harsh, I know they came from a place of care for us.
Hina: There were definitely moments when I thought, 'Wow, this doesn’t feel fun at all,' but... there were just as many fun moments too!
Chisato: Exactly. Without you, Nao-san, there are so many sights we never would’ve seen.
Chisato: And, I don’t think trust is only about relying on someone completely. It comes in different forms, after all.
Aya: We’re a ‘team’ with you too, Nao-san. So please, keep working with us moving forward!
Nao: Everyone...
Nao: Thank you. I’ll keep doing my best with you all.
Pastel＊Palettes: Please take care of us!
Eve: Oh, I’ve got an idea! How about we celebrate our win with a toast?
Hina: Good idea! And hey, we just bought some juice earlier!
Aya: Here you go, Nao-san. This one’s for you!
Nao: Oh, thanks.
Hina: ...Wait, isn’t that the soda you dropped earlier—?
Aya & Nao: Huh?
Aya & Nao: Wahhhh!
Maya: A-Are you okay?
Nao: No, I’m not okay! I’m completely soaked—this is ridiculous~!
Hina: Haha, you two got covered in soda together—it’s like a celebratory toast!
Aya: Nao-san, I’m so sorry! I didn’t think it would turn out like this!
Nao: ...Unforgivable.
Aya: Huh?
Nao: Unforgivable, Maruyama~! Come here—you’re not getting away~!
Aya: Wahhh! It’s not my fault, I swear~!
Eve: Aya-san, you’re incredible! After all that dancing, you still have so much energy!
Hina: Go, Aya-chan! Nao-san doesn’t have much stamina, so drag this out—it’s your win!
Nao: Hey, Hikawa...! Who said I don’t have stamina...?!
Maya: Huh, are you already getting tired...? Alright, both of you, do your best~!
Chisato: Geez, don’t overdo it too much, or you might trip and fall.
Aya: Ughhh, come on, someone help me~!
Hina, Eve & Maya: Hahaha!
