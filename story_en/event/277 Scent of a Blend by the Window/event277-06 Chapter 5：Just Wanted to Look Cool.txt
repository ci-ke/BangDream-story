event277-06 Chapter 5: Just Wanted to Look Cool

With a large group of guests expected, things grow hectic. Taki takes the plunge and heads out to the floor—

(Character: Tsugumi Hazawa, Tsukushi Futaba, Taki Shina)

[Hazawa Coffee]

Tsugumi: Repeating your order: two seasonal cake sets—
Tsukushi: Next customer, this way please!
Taki: ...
Taki: (It’s getting harder for Hazawa-senpai and Futaba-san to handle things on their own.)
Taki: (The kitchen’s still holding up okay, but...)
Café customer B: Excuse me, can I order~?
Tsugumi: P-please wait a moment! Okay, that’s 3000 yen—
Tsukushi: Thanks for waiting. Here’s your pão de ló and—
Taki: ...
Taki: (Guess I have no choice.)
Taki: I’m stepping out of the kitchen for a bit.
Café customer B: Excuse me...
Taki: May I take your order?
Café customer B: Oh, thank goodness. It’s busy today, huh~?
Taki: ...Yeah.
Café customer B: Huh, are you new here?
Taki: Yes, uh... just for today.
Café customer B: I see. With it this packed, I get not wanting to smile. Or maybe you’re just nervous? I’m a regular, so feel free to use me for practice.
Taki: ...!
Taki: ...Thank you...
Tsugumi: ...
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Shopping Mall - Back Alley]

Taki: ...Sigh.
Taki: (The crowd’s thinned out, and I’ve been given a break, but...)
Taki: ...What am I supposed to do now?
Tsugumi: Oh, there you are, Taki-chan!
Taki: Huh... Hazawa-senpai...?
Tsugumi: I’m on break too. Thought maybe we could talk, if that’s okay.
Taki: ...
Tsugumi: Sorry about today. Looks like it’s going to stay busy.
Taki: ...No, Hazawa-senpai, you don’t need to apologize...
Taki: And I’m sorry too. I couldn’t handle the front properly.
Taki: I wanted to try my best, since Afterglow asked me...
Tsugumi: Did I make you feel pressured...? I’m sorry. Don’t worry about Afterglow. Seriously, it’s fine!
Taki: No... it’s just...
Taki: I wanted to look cool. That’s all.
Tsugumi: ...!
Tsugumi: Wanting to look cool, huh... That actually makes me happy.
Taki: Huh?
Tsugumi: By the way, Taki-chan, when did you start supporting us?
Taki: Huh? Uh... since middle school, I guess. It’s like... your raw, honest vibe really hit me.
Taki: You felt like you were fighting as your real selves... It was just... cool. Like you were unbeatable.
Tsugumi: ...I see. So that’s how we looked to you.
Taki: ...Huh? “Looked to me”?
Tsugumi: Yeah. We actually...
Tsugumi: ...
Taki: ...Hazawa-senpai?
Tsugumi: It’s nothing. Never mind! Forget I said anything!
Taki: Huh?
Tsugumi: If that’s how you see us, then we want to look cool too.
Taki: What do you mean by that...?
Tsugumi: Well, we should head back soon. This is where the real work begins.
Taki: R-right.
Tsugumi: Oh... just one more thing.
Tsugumi: Taki-chan, I think you tend to focus on what you can’t do. But you’ve got a lot of things you can do too.
Tsugumi: You make coffee really carefully. And as a girl raised in a café, trust me on this—that’s something to be proud of!
Taki: Thank you...?
Taki: But, I mean... that doesn’t really have to do with customer service...
Taki: (Still... I wonder why...)
Taki: (When she said that... it didn’t feel bad at all.)
(Black cut)
(Background change)
