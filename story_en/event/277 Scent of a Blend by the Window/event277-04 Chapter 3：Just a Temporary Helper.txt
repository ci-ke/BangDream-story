event277-04 Chapter 3: Just a Temporary Helper

Taki is asked to handle a regular customer’s bill, but it turns out things aren’t quite the same this time—

(Character: Ran Mitake, Tsugumi Hazawa, Tsukushi Futaba, Taki Shina)

[Hazawa Coffee]

Café customer A: Oh? I don’t think I’ve seen you before. Are you new here?
Taki: Ah... no, I’m just helping out temporarily...
Café customer A: I see. I come here often, so I was curious.
Taki: ...I see. Um... that’s one seasonal cake set, so your total comes to...—
Tsukushi: Huh? Taki-chan, you look kinda tense. She was doing so well just now, too...
Tsugumi: Yeah. She said she usually works in the kitchen, so maybe she's not used to customer service...?
Taki: Here’s your change.
Café customer A: Thanks. By the way, did you change the recipe for today’s cake a little?
Taki: ...Huh?
Café customer A: You offered this cake last winter too, right? But it tastes a little different today.
Taki: ...Ah... um...
Tsugumi: That’s right! We tweaked the baking time a bit. I’m impressed you noticed.
Café customer A: I knew it! Guess my taste buds are still sharp, ufufu.
Tsugumi: Actually, we’ve also updated the cake for the next set, so please look forward to that.
Café customer A: Oh, really? Then I guess I’ll be coming back again soon.
Tsugumi: We’ll be waiting for you!
Café customer A: Fufu. Well then, thanks for the treat.
Tsukushi & Tsugumi: Thank you very much!
Taki: ...Thank you very much.
Taki: .........
Taki: ...Sorry, Hazawa-senpai. Thanks for stepping in.
Tsugumi: No... I should be the one apologizing. There’s no way you’d know everything right off the bat.
Tsukushi: Maybe we should hold off on the customer service and start you in the kitchen after all.
Taki: ...Okay.
Taki: (...Ugh. I hate this feeling.)
Taki: (Like I’m being quietly judged for not doing as well as expected...)
Tsugumi: Welcome... oh!
Ran: Hey. I just stopped by for a bit before heading to my flower arrangement class... Y'all doing okay?
Tsugumi: You mean Taki-chan? Fufu, she’s doing great. Right, Taki-chan?
Taki: Ah, yes...
Ran: ...Taki, are you nervous or something?
Taki: No, not really...
Tsugumi: She’s amazing, honestly. She remembers everything right away.
Taki: .........
Ran: I see... yeah. Not bad.
Taki: ...Huh?
Ran: Anyway, I’m heading out.
Tsugumi: Okay. Good luck at your lesson, Ran-chan!
Taki: Thanks for your hard work. ......
Tsugumi: Fufu. I think Ran-chan came by because she was worried about you.
Taki: Sh-she did...?
Taki: .........
Tsugumi: ...How about we take a little break? Tsukushi-chan, would you mind going with Taki-chan?
Tsukushi: All right, we’ll take our break now! Taki-chan, we take our breaks in this room over here. Come on!
Taki: Ah, okay...
Tsugumi: .........
