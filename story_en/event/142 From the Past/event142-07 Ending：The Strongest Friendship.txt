event142-07 Ending: The Strongest Friendship

RAS gathers for practice. Are their sounds finally in harmony? LOCK answers the burning question...

(Character: Rei Wakana, Rokka Asahi, Masuki Sato, Reona Nyubara, Chiyu Tamade)

[CHU²'s Penthouse - Studio]

LOCK (Rokka): Good morning.
PAREO (Reona): Good morning, LOCK-san!
MASKING (Masuki): Yo... Yawn~...
PAREO (Reona): ... And Massu-san♪ Good morning~.
LAYER (Rei): Morning, you two... Hm? Are you guys still doing things together?
LOCK (Rokka): No, we just happened to run into each other below.
LOCK (Rokka): Being together all the time turned out to be a bit hard in terms of scheduling...
LAYER (Rei): I would imagine. I'm sure you have plans even when you have no school.
CHU² (Chiyu): So... Does this mean your performance will be impeccable?
MASKING (Masuki): Oh, wanna find out?
CHU² (Chiyu): N-not really...! I was just asking as your producer. How you play is my business!
LAYER (Rei): You sure are being coy, Masuki.
PAREO (Reona): Does this mean you are now in perfect harmony...?
LOCK (Rokka): U-umm... Actually...
MASKING (Masuki): Nothin' really changed.
PAREO (Reona): Aww~, really?
CHU² (Chiyu): So it was all just a waste?
LOCK (Rokka): Ah, but we both got plenty of practice for the new song!
CHU² (Chiyu): That's a given. I wouldn't expect any less from RAS' guitarist and drummer.
LOCK (Rokka): ... Of course!
PAREO (Reona): However, it is such a shame that nothing came of it~. If being together really did have an effect, I would have a reason to forever be at CHU²-sama's side.
MASKING (Masuki): Reason or not, you're always with her anyway.
PAREO (Reona): That is true♪ I am CHU²-sama's keyboardist maid, after all!
CHU² (Chiyu): Call yourself whatever you want, but practice begins in half an hour. I want each of you to be ready by then.
LAYER & PAREO: Okay. As you command~♪
MASKING (Masuki): Thirty minutes, huh...? Hey, LOCK.
LOCK (Rokka): Okay... Shall we?
MASKING (Masuki): Yeah, let's do a bit of warming up.
PAREO (Reona): Are you both practicing already? Something appears to have motivated you.
MASKING (Masuki): Not really. I just feel like playin'. That's all.
LOCK (Rokka): Uhhh... Would all of you like to join us?
LAYER (Rei): Hm? But we're practicing together later.
MASKING (Masuki): So what? C'mon.
PAREO (Reona): This could be interesting. I would love to join♪
LAYER (Rei): Okay... I'll do it, but let's make sure it doesn't affect practice later on.
LOCK (Rokka): Yay! What about you, CHU²-san?
CHU² (Chiyu): Me?!
MASKING (Masuki): Why are you lookin' so surprised? You're just as much a part of RAS as we are.
CHU² (Chiyu): But... it's not like I can play any instruments though...
LOCK (Rokka): It's only because of your arrangements that RAS' music can be what it is!
PAREO (Reona): CHU²-sama, you should join us♪
LAYER (Rei): CHU².
CHU² (Chiyu): F-fine, I suppose I can join...! It'll make for a good warmup at least!
MASKING (Masuki): Looks like it's settled then.
LOCK (Rokka): What should we play?
LAYER (Rei): I think we should try our new song.
PAREO (Reona): I agree~♪
CHU² (Chiyu): Okay.
MASKING (Masuki): Alright, let's do this, LOCK!
LOCK (Rokka): Yes.
LOCK (Rokka): Here we go!
