event195-03 Chapter 2: This Feeling Is Probably...

The interview succeeds thanks to Aya, but Chisato's feelings are complicated.

(Character: Aya Maruyama, Hina Hikawa, Chisato Shirasagi, Maya Yamato, Eve Wakamiya)

[After Filming]

[Fireworks Festival - Organizers' Tent]

Eve: Aya-san, you were incredible today! I was overwhelmed with emotion!
Maya: You helped the head organizer feel less nervous and got the interview back on track smoothly... You handled things so well!
Aya: Thanks, guys.
Hina: You nailed it, Aya-chan! What gives? You got so good super fast!
Aya: Ehehe. I am getting better at things, you know! I'm not going to be making the same old mistakes forever.
Aya: Chisato-chan... How was I? Did I do okay?
Chisato: Yes. I think you did well.
Aya: Really? Alright~!
Hina: But Aya-chan, earlier, you almost fell again~.
Aya: Argh~, Hina-chan! Why do you always notice those things~?!
Chisato: Fufu. Still... I think you did really well. I prepared for today and visualized many ways the interview might go, but I guess I didn't need to.
Hina: ...
Aya: Hrm? The staff over there is calling us.
Eve: It looks like they have prepared drinks for us. I will go and bring them all back to the tent!
Maya: I'll go with you. It'll be hard for you to carry all the drinks by yourself.
Aya: I'll help too! You two relax here.
Hina: Thanks~. I'll be here~.
Chisato: Thank you.
Hina: It's evening, but it's still so hot.
Chisato: Yes. I wish the nights were a little cooler.
Hina: You're a little sad, aren't you?
Chisato: ...? What do you mean?
Hina: Oh, did I guess wrong?
Hina: I was talking about today's shoot. You didn't have to swoop in and save Aya-chan, right?
Hina: I thought maybe you felt sad because you didn't get to do that.
Chisato: Sad...? Me?
Hina: Yep.
Chisato: I'm not s-
Chisato: ... Well, perhaps. I guess I do have mixed feelings about it. Maybe to you, it looks like I'm sad, though.
Chisato: But improving is a good thing. I'm happier when things go like they did today rather than having to help so much.
Hina: Oh, I guess you've got a point. We can't just stay the way we are now forever.
Chisato: Right. We need to keep growing as idols.
Hina: Yeah! Things will be way more fun that way♪
Chisato: Yes, they will.
Maya: Chisato-san, Hina-san, we're back~!
Eve: We brought cold ramune! Please have some!
Hina: Thanks! Mmm~, it's so cold~!
Aya: We have one for you too, Chisato-chan. Here.
Chisato: Thank you.
Aya: What were you two talking about? You looked kind of serious.
Chisato: Well...
Chisato: It's a secret.
Aya: Whaaat~?!
Hina: It's our little secret~. Right, Chisato-chan?
Aya: Saying that just makes me want to know even more~.
Chisato: Fufu. I'm sorry. Seriously though, it was nothing. Don't worry about it.
Maya: Why don't we make a toast? We can celebrate a job well done.
Hina: Yeah~! Aya-chan, lead us in the toast!
Aya: Huh? Me?
Eve: Yes! You are today's MVP!
Aya: Ehehe~. Really? Okay then, I humbly accept the honor... Ahem.
Aya: Great work today, everyone! Let's enjoy the fireworks too! Now raise your drinks. Ready~...?
All: Cheers~!
