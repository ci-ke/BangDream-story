event241-05 Chapter 4: A Perfect Day for the Dentist

Tae overcomes the drill sounds and triumphantly heads for the dentist, but...

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Tae Hanazono, Rimi Ushigome, Saya Yamabuki, Arisa Ichigaya)

[The Next Day]

[Residential Area]

Kasumi: Hm-hm~♪ The sun feels so warm and nice~, huh?
Tae: Yeah. It's the perfect weather for a dentist appointment!
Tae: Hey, look at that cloud! It looks like a bunny that's waving this way. Thanks for wishing me luck~!
Arisa: Compared to yesterday, O-Tae's so cheerful now...
Rimi: Fufu, I don't blame her. Overcoming something you've struggled with makes you feel like a new person. It's so uplifting.
Arisa: True, I guess... Hey, that person walking towards us...
Candy Lady: Well, well, if it isn't Saya-chan and a bunch of familiar faces. Are you girls going somewhere?
Saya: Oh, hello. We're on our way to the dentist.
Candy Lady: I see, I see. Ah, have a little something from me then. I ordered too many snacks, so I've been going around handing them out to the kids downtown.
Kasumi: Ohh~, a bunch of snacks! Thank you so much!
Saya: Fufu, she gave us so much~. I wonder if we'll be able to finish them all.
Kasumi: I don't see why not! It's the perfect amount for five people to eat! Right, O-Tae?
Tae: Yeah! Although, I also wanna eat lots of Hamburg steaks and do lots of band practice, of course!
Rimi: Fufu, we have to for sure... Oh, why don't we perform a concert, too, then?
Kasumi: Great idea! It can be to celebrate the fact that O-Tae has conquered her fear of dentists!
Arisa: Not everything has to be a cause for celebration, you know...?
Saya: Well, we've celebrated lots of things with a show. One more won't hurt, right?
Kasumi: Oh, we'll almost be at the dentist. Did you bring your earplugs, O-Tae?
Tae: Mm-hmm. They're right in my pocket...
Kid: Wahhh~! You liar! You lied to me, Mom! You said the dentist wouldn't be scary~!
Tae: ...!
Mother: There, there. On the way home, how about I get you that picture book you want?
Kasumi: O-O-Tae, are you okay...?
Tae: ... I don't think I can do this after all.
Arisa: Whoa, whoa, whoa, you've made it this far! It'll be over real soon! C'mon, don't give up now.
Rimi: E-exactly! We're right here with you too. Let's have your cavity fixed so that we can fully enjoy that concert together.
Saya: Oh... Take my hand, O-Tae. We can try going while holding hands.
Tae: Guys...
Tae: Thanks, but I can't...
Kasumi: Hey, O-Tae, wait...!
Arisa: Whoa, where are you guys going?!

[Park]

Tae: ...

Tae: ... I don't understand why I can't just go...
Tae: I'm so frustrated with myself...! I'm frustrated for being unable to change... and I'm frustrated for letting you all down...
Kasumi: D-don't be so hard on yourself! The way you've tried so hard even though it scares you so much is amazing! You're amazing!
Kasumi: And it's not like today is your only chance to see a dentist. If you can't do it today, maybe you can try again tomorrow... How does that sound?
Tae: ... I wonder if this is what things are going to be like forever...

Tae: I don't want that... I want us all to do band stuff together...
Kasumi: O-Tae...

Tae: Kasumi...? Why are you opening the bag of snacks we got from the candy lady...?
Kasumi: I'm getting cavities too...! Bite, chew, chew!
Tae: Huh? What are you doing?!
Kasumi: If I eat lots of snacks, I might get cavities. Hold on. I'll join you real soon...!
Tae: S-stop it! You can't give yourself cavities!

Tae: No! Stop, Kasumi! They really hurt, you know?

Kasumi: O-okay...! Is there anything I can do...?
