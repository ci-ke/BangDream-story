event241-04 Chapter 3: Hamburg Steak and Eggs

The girls look for ways to overcome a fear of dentists, but Tae remains afraid.

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Tae Hanazono, Rimi Ushigome, Saya Yamabuki, Arisa Ichigaya)

[A Few Hours Later]

[Ichigaya Residence - Basement]

Kasumi: Wh-whirrr~, zzzzt...
Tae: Ahhh~! Make it stop, make it stop~! Whimper... I can't! I can't take it anymore...!
Kasumi: Okay, let's take a break!
Rimi: I'm sorry we haven't been able to help, O-Tae-chan...
Saya: According to the internet, relaxing before an appointment is supposed to help. I was so sure this was going to work...
Arisa: Rimi's idea of trying to evoke happy thoughts by thinking of delicious food didn't help either...
Tae: Gasp... Gasp... Dentist... Scary...
Kasumi: ...
Arisa: Hey, how many things have we tried so far...?
Saya: Um... I think that was around the tenth... We've tried out just about everything we've come across...
Rimi: Poor O-Tae-chan... No matter what we try, the moment Kasumi-chan makes drilling noises, she becomes scared...
Tae: Fufu... So that's where you've been hiding, Oddie... Why don't you join us so that we can enjoy these cookies together?
Tae: You're all so lucky, you know? No matter how much you eat, your teeth will never hurt in Hanazono Land because Hanazono Land is a happy place without cavities...
Arisa: Uh-oh~... She's talking to the wall... That's not a good sign.
Rimi: I hope she can go back to being her usual self...
Kasumi: ... Guys. Do you really think we need to take her to the dentist...?
Kasumi: I can't stand seeing her be so terrified...
Arisa: I get you... but if we stop here, it's not gonna do her any good, you know?
Kasumi: I-I know, but...
Rimi: Kasumi-chan, it's painful for me, as well, but if the cavity stays untreated, O-Tae-chan will be the one who suffers more...
Kasumi: ...
Saya: Hmm, we're starting to run low on internet tips. Which one do you think we should try next...?
Tae: Guys... It's alright. I'm prepared to accept my fate.
Arisa: And what fate is that...?
Tae: The fate of having to live with this pain while I continue on as a member of the band.
Tae: So don't worry about me anymore. Thanks for trying though.
Arisa: Hey, stop trying to weasel out of it.
Rimi: Arisa-chan's right. I know it's difficult, but don't give up.
Tae: It's no use. I always end up getting flashbacks of when I had my cavity fixed...
Saya: Yeah, and that's the problem. I wonder what we can do...
Arisa: The thing is, O-Tae almost cries just from hearing Kasumi's drill noises~...
Arisa: ... Hmm? Wait a sec.
Kasumi: What is it, Arisa?
Arisa: Remember what Rimi said earlier? She also said the drill noises are what triggers O-Tae's fear...
Arisa: So, what if we tried earplugs...?
Rimi: Oh... I think that could work.
Arisa: I should still have the ones I used while studying the other day... Here you go, O-Tae. Think you could try them out a bit?
Tae: ... I doubt it'll help, but I guess I can... Okay, I'm ready.
Saya: Alright, Kasumi, could you give it another go?
Kasumi: S-sure... Okay, O-Tae, lie down on the sofa, will you? As soon as you get scared, just lift your hand, okay?
Kasumi: H-here I go... Whirrr~!
Tae: ...
Arisa: Huh...? O-Tae isn't freaking out.
Saya: Y-yeah. The spoon's in her mouth too...!
Rimi: Kasumi-chan, try being louder.
Kasumi: Okay! Zzzzzzt!
Tae: ...
Arisa & Saya & Rimi: Wh-whoa~!
Tae: ... This... This is incredible.
Tae: The old man with the drill didn't show up in my head!
Arisa & Saya & Rimi & Kasumi: H-hooray~!
Tae: I can't believe this! With these earplugs, I can take anything! I'm as unbeatable as a Hamburg steak topped with a sunny-side up!
Tae: I think I can do this now. Thanks so much, guys!
Saya: I'm so happy for you, O-Tae!
Arisa: Alright! You wanna try scheduling an appointment now?
Tae: Yeah! Let me go make that call real quick!
Rimi: Fufu. It's been so long since we last saw O-Tae-chan look this cheerful.
Saya: Like, seriously. I'm so glad we all kept at it!
Kasumi: Alright, then! When O-Tae goes to the dentist, let's go with her and show our support!
Saya & Rimi & Arisa: Yeah~!
