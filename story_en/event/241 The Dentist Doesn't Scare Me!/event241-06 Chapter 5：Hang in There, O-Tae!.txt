event241-06 Chapter 5: Hang in There, O-Tae!

The girls have a sleepover the night before Tae goes to get her cavity treated.

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Tae Hanazono, Rimi Ushigome, Saya Yamabuki, Arisa Ichigaya)

[Night]

[Hanazono Residence - Living Room]

Tae: Patient ID, check, handkerchief, check... Alright, I have everything I need for tomorrow.
Rimi: Good luck, O-Tae-chan...!
Kasumi: Go~, go~, O-Tae~!
Saya: I know you must be scared, but stay strong...!
Tae: Thanks, guys...! I'll see things through for sure!
Kasumi & Saya & Rimi: Great...!
Arisa: That's the nth time you guys've done that routine, and the last one was right after eating, too, wasn't it...?
Saya: O-Tae's decided to go, so we just want to make sure she gets all the encouragement she needs.
Arisa: I guess... You know, though, O-Tae, I couldn't believe it when we were at the park and you said you were gonna go to the dentist.
Arisa: I had my doubts 'cause it was right after you ran away.
Tae: Trust me, I won't run tomorrow!
Kasumi: Yeah! You even promised the dentist you'd go!
Saya: Fufu, none of us doubt you.
Rimi: Mm-hmm! Especially after seeing how peaceful you looked when we found you.
Arisa: Well, in any case, I'm just glad you're feeling better now.
Arisa: Anyway, we should get ready for tomorrow. You're scheduled to go in pretty early, after all.
Tae: Yeah. We should take a bath once the water's ready.
Tae: Oh, before that though... Oddie, Shiroppi, Doro-chan, everyone... Gather around. I have something really important to say, so listen good.
Tae: Tomorrow, I'm going to the dentist. It'll probably be a difficult fight, and I may even end up doing a lot of screaming.
Tae: But I'm going to emerge victorious over this cavity! I want all of you to send me lots of support from here, okay?
Arisa: That was a pretty impassioned speech... but I dunno if the rabbits got the message...
Rimi: I'm sure they have. They seem to be gazing at O-Tae-chan more seriously than usual.
Tae's Mom: Girls, I made you some tea~.
Kasumi: Wow~, thanks so much!
Tae's Mom: I'm really glad Tae-chan has decided to go to the dentist~. No matter how many times I told her to go, she kept refusing.
Tae's Mom: I'm so grateful to all of you. Thank you so much.
Kasumi: No, we didn't do anything...! We just wanted O-Tae to feel better as soon as possible...!
Tae's Mom: Fufufu. Well, help yourselves to the cookies I baked. It's a little early, but consider it a celebration.
Tae: Wow, they're my favorite kind too! In you go~...
Saya: Hold on, O-Tae, you still have a cavity...
Tae's Mom: Oh dear, I completely forgot. No cookies for you until you're better, then, O-Tae-chan.
Tae: Aww~.
Kasumi & Arisa & Saya & Rimi: Ahahaha.
