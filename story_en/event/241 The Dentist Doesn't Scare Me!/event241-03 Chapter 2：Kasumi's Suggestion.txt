event241-03 Chapter 2: Kasumi's Suggestion

When Tae says she's afraid to go to the dentist, the girls leap into action.

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Tae Hanazono, Rimi Ushigome, Saya Yamabuki, Arisa Ichigaya)

[After School]

[Ichigaya Residence - Basement]

Kasumi: Alright, O-Tae! Could you lie down on the sofa face-up?
Tae: O-okay... Done...
Rimi: Kasumi-chan, I brought that cup of water...
Saya: I brought the spoon. Are you sure this'll work though? It really is just a plain old spoon...
Kasumi: Thanks~, you two! I've got everything I need now!
Kasumi: The spoon's going to be used as a drill, so it goes here... And the water is for O-Tae to rinse her mouth, so it goes here...
Arisa: ... Hey, are you seriously going to pretend play a dental appointment?
Arisa: I don't really see this doing much to help O-Tae overcome her fear of dentists...
Kasumi: Don't worry! It really works!
Kasumi: When I tried this with Aa-chan way back, it helped me become less scared.
Arisa: You're talking about when you were in elementary school or something, though, right? Expecting it to work on a high schooler might be a bit of a stretch.
Kasumi: Hmm~, it should still work... I think, anyway...
Saya: Maybe these'll help then. I checked online for ways to overcome dental anxiety.
Kasumi: Can I take a look...? Whoa~, this is a lot~!
Saya: I'm thinking we can combine them with your idea, Kasumi. What do you think?
Kasumi: You're so smart, Saya! O-Tae will get over her fear of the dentist for sure now!
Arisa: I'm still not sure about the whole pretend play thing, but maybe the other stuff makes it worth trying.
Kasumi: Okay, which one do we want to try first?
Tae: ... Ngh, the ceiling light is so scary...
Tae: Lying here on the sofa brings back memories of the dental chair...
Arisa: F-for real...? If this is all it takes to start making you freak out, just how afraid of the dentist are you...?
Rimi: Are you okay, O-Tae-chan...? Is there anything we can do for you?
Tae: ... Ngh, hold my hand...
Tae: The last time I went to the dentist, my mom held my hand for me...
Saya: Now that you mention it... holding Sana's hand while she was in the chair helped her relax a bit. Here you go, O-Tae. Hold away.
Tae: Thanks... Wow... Your hand's so warm...
Saya: There, there, O-Tae. Everything's going to be just fine, okay?
Tae: Really? Are you sure everything's going to be okay?
Saya: Absolutely. You have nothing to be afraid of.
Arisa: Huh~, looks like she's starting to calm down.
Kasumi: Alright, let's see how this goes then...! Rimi-rin, could you point the light toward her mouth?
Rimi: O-okay...! O-Tae-chan, I'm going to shine my phone's light on you a bit.
Kasumi: Arisa, drill, please.
Arisa: S-sure...
Kasumi: I'm beginning the procedure. If it hurts, lift your hand, okay?
Tae: Whimper...!
Saya: O-Tae, you don't have to just yet, you know...?
Kasumi: Okay...! Open your mouth wide, please~.
Kasumi: Whirrr~, zzzzt...
Tae: ...! Ahhh~! Stop~!
Kasumi: Whoa! S-sorry, O-Tae! It's alright now! I've stopped...
Tae: Gasp... Gasp... Ngh...
Rimi: Here, have some water, O-Tae-chan... Do you feel better now?
Tae: ... The whole time... there was this dancing old man in my head... with a drill...
Tae: Whimper... There's no way I can do it~! I'll never be able to go to the dentist~!
Kasumi: Don't give up, O-Tae! Think of all the fun things there'll be to do once your tooth is better!
Kasumi: Let's get through this together!
Tae: Together...
Rimi: Hang in there, O-Tae-chan! We can eat lots of Hamburg steaks!
Tae: I want Hamburg steaks...!
Saya: Once you're all better, let's do tons of band practice too.
Tae: I wanna practice...!
Arisa: ... Alright, let's try everything we can. For O-Tae's sake, we can't leave her like this.
Saya: Yeah! Let's all help O-Tae!
