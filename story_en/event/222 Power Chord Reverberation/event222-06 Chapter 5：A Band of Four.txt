event222-06 Chapter 5: A Band of Four

Mirror Dreams has started acting like a proper band. How will the concert go?

(Character: Rokka Asahi, Masuki Sato)

[Studio]

LOCK (Rokka): Let's play that part one more time, please! If we arrange it a little more, I think my sound will blend in wonderfully with everyone else's!
Yuka: Huff, huff... You've got to be kidding. You want to go again~...?! Rokka-chan, you're a beast...!
LOCK (Rokka): But we're sounding so good now!
Maki: Don't give me that... Huff... But we'll go one last time.
LOCK (Rokka): Thank you! Let's do it!
Aoi: Maki~...
Maki: Like we've got a choice. See her face? No way she's backing down... And, hey, Rokka-chan, could you play the phrase before the refrain a little harder?
LOCK (Rokka): Huh? S-sure!
Maki: Aoi, try to add more notes where you can, but see how it goes. I want the bass to have more of an edge. Yuka, can you give me that?
Yuka: Y-yeah... It's been a while since you've talked like that.
Maki: It's not a big deal... We might as well since we're playing again. I'm going to try to sing harder than usual too, so-
LOCK (Rokka): ...!
Maki: Wh-what?
LOCK (Rokka): Nothing! I was just thinking that we're playing like a band!

Maki: ... Fufu. Alright, guys, here we go.
LOCK (Rokka): Okay!
(Black cut)

[A Few Hours Later]

[Galaxy Ramen]

LOCK (Rokka): Hi~!

MASKING (Masuki): Okay, here's your ramen! And your Ladies Meals.

LOCK (Rokka): J-just a minute, Masuki-san! You guys are pretty bad too~.

LOCK (Rokka): (... The four of us?)
MASKING (Masuki): ...
