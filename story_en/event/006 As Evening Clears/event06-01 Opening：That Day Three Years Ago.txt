event06-01 Opening: That Day Three Years Ago

The band has gathered as usual, but Himari arrives late and is acting very strange...

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa)

[Studio]

Moca: Alrighty~, time for another exciting day of rehearsal~.
Tsugumi: Mhm! ... Wait, where's Himari-chan?
Tomoe: Huh, that's weird. Himari's never late.
Ran: ... Yeah...
Himari: Heh. Fufu. Fufufufufu...
Moca: Not only is she late, but she's laughing uncontrollably... You think Hii-chan's gone mad?
Ran: Definitely mad.
Tsugumi: H-Himari-chan, are you okay?
Himari: Fufu, fufufu. I've uncovered something awesome. Look!
Tomoe: Uhm... Is this...?
Tsugumi: It's the first CD we ever recorded, right?!
Himari: Bingo! And~, I also found some notes for the lyrics.
Ran: ...!! No, don't look at that!
Moca: Aww~, why not~? It's probably fine~. Besides, it's a song we're still performing now.
Ran: Y-yeah, but... it's kinda embarrassing, so no.
Moca: Wow~. There's nothing to be embarrassed about. It's not like I'm saying to everyone let's get that book Ran writes in and read it together~.
Ran: ... Moca, say anymore, and I'm really gonna get angry.
Moca: Don't be angry with me~.
Tomoe: Hey, you two. Knock it off, will ya? ... Anyway, this sure brings back memories.
Himari: Right? I was cleaning my room, and there it was. I couldn't help but laugh remembering everything♪
Ran: And that's when you became a mad woman.
Himari: Hey~, I'm not a mad woman! Hmph! ... So, uhh, since we have it, why don't we listen to it?
Ran: What...? You don't feel... embarrassed at all...?
Tomoe: I mean, of course we do. We're definitely a lot better now than we were.
Tsugumi: B-but, you know, if we listen to it now, we might learn something from it! Maybe we'll feel something new...
Himari: Yeah, yeah! It could be nice to go back every now and then to see how we were, right?
Tomoe: If Himari and Tsugu feel that way... we should just play it.
Moca: Mhm. I don't mind at all~.
Ran: Sigh... Fine.
Himari: Alright! Here we go~!
(Black cut)
Moca: Uhm... Who's that on the guitar?
Tomoe: That's you.
Himari: ... Actually, this is kinda embarrassing...
Ran: That's what I was saying...!
Tsugumi: Ahh~... The keyboard sounds awful... Mmm, I'm so sorry...
Tomoe: There's no point in apologizing to the CD... But the nostalgia is definitely there. A bunch of different memories comes to mind.
Himari: Ahh, memories~. Afterglow is three years old now.
Ran: Three years, huh...?
Moca: We started the band during our second year of middle school, right~? Sigh, time sure does fly~.
Himari: Yeah. It doesn't seem like it was that long ago~.
Ran: I remember that day like it was yesterday.
Tomoe: Yeah... I'd say it's cause it all started off in a very memorable way.
Ran: Mhm... I can never forget it. What happened on that day and everything that led up to it...
