event06-05 Chapter 4: Desire to Be Together

The girls have finally gathered. Despite being in different classes, they find a new way to spend time together...

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa)

[Restaurant]

Tsugumi: It's been a while since we've all been together like this.
Tomoe: Sure has. About a week, right?
Himari: So we're all here, but what are we gonna do today? Karaoke?
Moca: Uhh, things are a little tight until my next allowance.
Ran: I think I'm gonna have to pass, too... Wait, isn't that what we did last time?
Moca: Hii-chan, you really like karaoke, don't you~?
Himari: Yeah! It has kind of a refreshing feeling! And doesn't it make you want to pretend you're performing?
Himari: Like, "Yay! Thanks for coming!" And you play the air guitar or something!
Ran: ... Not really.
Moca: Yeah~, "not really~."
Himari: Aww~, but it's so fun~.
Tsugumi: I think I know what you mean. If I'm in the tub and singing and it feels really nice, I'll close my eyes, and suddenly, I'm performing at a concert...!
Tomoe: Yep! It's cool when you get that echo while singing in the bathtub! I do a lot of humming myself.
Himari: That's it! That's what I mean~! Sigh... I bet performing at an actual concert would be tons of fun~.
Moca: Hii-chan's imagination is going wild again~.
Himari: C'mon~, nothing wrong with a bit of day dreaming~. Himari's Band! That sounds super awesome.
Tsugumi: A band...
Moca: Hii-chan's Band, huh? Alright, maybe I can play the guitar~.
Tomoe: Haha. And I guess with my taiko experience, I'd be the drummer?
Himari: What were the other instruments in a band...?!
Tomoe: Uhm... The bass?
Himari: Okay, then maybe I can play the bass~. Hehe, this is so much fun! Tsugu, what about you...?
Tsugumi: A band... Let's start a band!!
Himari: Tsugu...?!
Tsugumi: We should start a band! No, we have to!
Ran: Huh...? Tsugumi, what's going on?
Tsugumi: Uhm, well. Uhh... Ever since you were put in a different class, we haven't been able to see you very often, so I've been thinking...
Tsugumi: Even if we all have club activities... If we find something that the five of us could all do, then we could spend a lot more time together.
Ran: Tsugumi, you've really been thinking about that...?
Tomoe: It's not just her. We've all been wondering what to do. Especially since you've seemed kinda down lately.
Ran: Tomoe...
Moca: A band, huh~...? That could work considering Ran's talent for writing.
Ran: Wha-?! Moca...!
Tomoe: Oh, really? Is that true, Ran?
Ran: ... It's nothing to brag about...
Moca: Ran~, show them the poem you wrote. I think it's great.
Himari: I wanna read it!
Ran: ... Fine, alright.
(Black cut)
Tsugumi: Wow~... Ran-chan, you actually wrote this...?
Himari: Hm... I can't really put it into words, but I think it's cool and powerful!
Ran: You think so...?
Himari: Yeah! You're definitely talented!
Tomoe: Say, Ran... If you sang and wrote the lyrics, we could make some pretty good songs, don't you think?
Ran: If I sing...?
Moca: Yeah~. The person who wrote the lyrics would be the best one to sing them. They could really get into it.
Tsugumi: Mhm, I think so too! We can start a band, and it'll be something new for us to try out.
Himari: Yeah! ... Wait, what instrument are you gonna play, Tsugu?
Tsugumi: M-me...? Uhh... I know! The keyboard! I used to take piano lessons, so I think I could do it.
Ran: I don't only want to sing. I want to play the guitar, too. I kinda think if I were just singing, my hands would feel too free...
Moca: That's fine. We can work on our guitar skills together~.
Himari: A band... I'm starting to get excited!
Tomoe: Just like being in a band, I like that we can all work together as a team...
Tsugumi: Mhm.
Ran: Uhm... Guys... So, uhh... You know... Thanks for worrying about me.
Tsugumi: How nice...! Ran-chan is smiling!
Himari: Mhm. I think it's been a while since the last time we've seen that!
Moca: Yeah~.
Ran: I-I can smile too, you know... When I'm happy.
