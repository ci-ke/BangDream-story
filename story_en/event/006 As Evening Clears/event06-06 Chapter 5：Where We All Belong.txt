event06-06 Chapter 5: Where We All Belong

Now that they have a band, the girls need to come up with a name. The search for cool words begins.

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa)

[A Few Days Later]

[After School]

[Roof]

Ran: The pointer finger goes here... and the middle finger...
Himari: Ohh, you played a note~!! Wow! So cool!
Ran: ...!
Ran: Moca! I did it! I played a note...!
Moca: Ni~ce.
Tomoe: Good stuff! I know it seems kinda fast, but I brought a musical score I think even beginners could play. Wanna try it out?
Tsugumi: Musical score...?
Tomoe: Like sheet music. Here, look.
Himari: Oh, I know this song!
Tsugumi: Me too!
Tomoe: Great. I figured if everyone knew it, it'd be easy to practice. That's why I chose it. Let's start with this piece, then.
Himari: This is gonna be our first song~. Wow, we're really starting to feel like a band.
Moca: Now that you said that, what are we gonna do about the band name? If we don't think about it, we'll end up as Hii-chan's Band.
Ran: That's... not gonna work...
Tomoe: Yeah, we definitely want something that's a bit cooler.
Tsugumi: Hmm, what would be a good name...? I'm really bad at this sort of thing...
Himari: How about RMHTT? It's the first letter of all of our names.
Ran: ... Himari, you're no longer allowed to participate.
Himari: What?! That's so mean~!
Moca: Okay, what if we look up some cool-sounding vocab? Something like that could work.
Tsugumi: I think I remember having an electronic dictionary in my bag...
Tomoe: Alright, let's all take time to find some words.
(Black cut)

[20 Minutes Later]

Himari: Okay, guys~. Find anything~?
Moca: UltraViolet!
Ran: What's that?
Moca: Like ultraviolet rays!
All: ...
Tsugumi: How's this? Stratosphere. It's like... a part of the atmosphere.
Ran: Omni... It means "all" or "everything."
Moca: That's cool, but doesn't that seem like a bit much? Like "all-powerful?"
Himari: Hmm... I don't know if just having a cool word is good enough... It just doesn't click since the words don't have a lot of meaning behind it.
Tomoe: Yeah, even if we called our band UltraViolet, it doesn't really make sense to us...
Tsugumi: A word that has meaning for the five of us...
Ran: How about... something related to the sunset?
Ran: We always practice after school... when the sun is going down, right? And... Take a look.
Moca: Ooo, so pretty~! It does look really nice when you watch from here, so that might be good~.
Ran: And if we use the sunset to name our band, we'll always be able to look back on this day.
Himari: Nice! Sunset... Uhm~... If we want cool vocab, then...
Moca: If we look at the synonyms for it in the dictionary, a bunch of different options comes up~. Hmm...
Tsugumi: Evening...?
Tomoe: Afterglow... What do you guys think of that?
Moca: Afterglow... That sounds cool.
Himari: Yeah, I think so too!
Tomoe: Then, it's decided! That will be our band name!
Tsugumi: Yeah!
Moca: Afterglow... I think that makes things official.
Tomoe: If we ever fight or argue... I get the sense that we'll be able to look at the sunset, remember today and make things better.
Himari: That's pretty cool, isn't it...? Looking at the sunset and thinking back...? Wait, we're not gonna fight! Because we're all such good friends!
Tomoe: Haha, yeah. You're right. This sunset is a symbol of our friendship!
Tsugumi: Mhm! We'll become even closer. Closer than before!
(White cut)
(Background change)

[Present Day]

[Studio]

Moca: ... And Moca-chan lived happily ever after.
Himari: Wha-?! Were you even paying attention?!
Tomoe: You know, that's a pretty good story for us, though.
Himari: Yeah. Ahh~! We are in the prime of our lives!
