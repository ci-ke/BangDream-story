event06-03 Chapter 2: Where Ran Belongs

Tomoe and the others brainstorm ideas for spending time with Ran. Meanwhile, it seems like Moca has noticed something...

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa)

[Haneoka Girls' Academy - Middle School Class 2B]

Himari: Hmm... Even after talking to Ran, we don't know anything about where she's going during class or what's on her mind...
Tomoe: ... Yeah. She does tend to keep everything to herself...
Tsugumi: Moca-chan, you haven't heard anything from her?
Moca: Hmm... Not really.
Himari: Grr, ahh!! What are we supposed to do when we don't know anything~?
Tsugumi: Ah! Isn't our next class in the science room?!
Tomoe: Oh crap! We gotta go!
Moca: Sorry, you guys go on ahead~. I have to find something~.
Himari: Okay! We'll distract the teacher and buy you some time, so hurry up~.
Moca: ... Alrighty. I wonder if I'll find what I'm looking for~.
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Hallway]

Moca: ...
Moca: (She's not in her classroom... Looks like she's really not going to class...)
Moca: Hmm... Oh?
Moca: ... Target sighted. Objective: tail the suspect. Mission start~.
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Roof]

Ran: ...
Moca: Ra~n~.
Ran: ... Moca?
Ran: Uhh, wh-what are you doing here? Don't you have class...?
Moca: I could be asking you the same thing, Ran~.
Ran: I-I'm just...!
Moca: Everyone's been worried about you~. Of course, that includes me.
Moca: It would seem you've stopped showing up to class after we had our schedules changed. But you can't fool the great detective Moca-chan~.
Ran: ... That... has nothing to do with it.
Moca: Oh really~? Ever since we've been separated from you, we've all been pretty sad about it~. Are you saying you don't feel sad, Ran?
Ran: ... I... kinda... feel sad... maybe...
Moca: Mhm, so everyone feels the same~.
Ran: ... Moca, shouldn't you go to class?
Moca: Of course, I should... However~, I think it'd be more interesting to stay here and see what you're up to.
Ran: ... I see.
Moca: I can just relax~, kinda like this. Do you mind me being next to you?
Ran: ... No...
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[School Path]

Himari: Moca! You were supposed come to class~! Do you know how hard it was to buy time when you didn't even show up?!
Moca: Alright, alright, I'm sorry.
Tomoe: Geez... If you were gonna go searching for Ran, you could've just said that in the first place, you know.
Moca: I said, I'm sorry~.
Tsugumi: I'm just happy we know what's been going on with Ran-chan.
Tomoe: Yeah, that's for sure. But what are we supposed to do now?
Himari: We've been spending a lot less time with her. I guess it was obvious that would happen since we're no longer in the same class.
Tsugumi: And we've all been together for so long. I know if I was put into a different class, I would feel really lonely.
Himari: Hmm, yeah... If only we could make more time to spend together... Like, maybe there's something we could do as the five of us...
Moca: Like what?
Himari: Uhh, hmm... Maybe we could learn something together...?
Tomoe: No, that would work better if we were in elementary school...
Tsugumi: We could promise to always walk home together!
Himari: Sorry, I can't do that every day. I have club meetings I have to go to...
Tomoe: Moca, does anything come to mind? Something Ran would want to do...?
Moca: Hmm... We could all skip class together.
Himari: N-no we can't!!
Moca: I guess not~...
Himari: Oh! I know! We can plan to always hang out on Saturdays!
Moca, Tomoe & Tsugumi: And the other days...?
Himari: Oh...
All (Himari): Sigh...
Himari: Is it really this hard for us to find something to do together?
