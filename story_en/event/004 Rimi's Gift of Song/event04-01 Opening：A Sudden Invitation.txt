event04-01 Opening: A Sudden Invitation

Rimi is looking for a present for Yuri when she gets a phone call from Nana, the student council president.

(Character: Rimi Ushigome, Saya Yamabuki)

[Shopping Mall]

Rimi: Hmm... Saya-chan, what about this? Do you think my sister would like it?
Saya: Ooo, that cardigan is great! Yuri-senpai would definitely love it.
Rimi: ... But you know, it's kind of hard to tell. We like such different things...
Saya: I'm sure she'll be happy whatever you give her, Rimi-rin.
Rimi: You think so...? Either way, I want to make sure I choose something I'm really sure about...
Rimi: ... Sorry, Saya-chan. I just can't decide.
Saya: It's okay, don't worry about it. Let's just take our time and find something you know she'll like.
Rimi: Okay. Thank you, Saya-chan. I'm glad I brought you with me.
Saya: You said her birthday is next week? So there's no need to rush. Let's make sure to take a good look.
Rimi: Be careful. I might actually hold on to you until the week is over...
Saya: Fufufu. That's alright, I can do that.
Rimi: Hehehe.
Saya: Rimi-rin, you really love your sister, don't you?
Rimi: Huh...? Uhh, I-I guess, you could say love...
Saya: It's okay. I get it. If I had an older sister like that, I would love her too.
Saya: I mean, Yuri-senpai is the guitarist and singer for Glitter*Green.
Saya: If I was that cool, I'm sure my little brother and sister would brag about me, too.
Rimi: B-but you are cool, Saya-chan. I'm sure Sana-chan and Jun-kun are proud to have you as a sister...
Rimi: ... Oh, I know.
Rimi: Saya-chan, if you were to get a present from Sana-chan, what would you like?
Rimi: Or from Jun-kun, either is fine. What would be a good present to get from them?
Saya: Uhh... It's kinda hard to say. They're still pretty young...
Saya: I guess if they gave me a present... it would be a portrait of me? Or a coupon to get them to do whatever I want? Something like that.
Rimi: Hmm... I see.
Saya: ... But it's really the thought that counts, so I think I would be happy with whatever they give me.
Rimi: I guess you're right. It is the thought that counts...
Rimi: ... Hm? It's my phone. Sorry, Saya-chan. Give me a second.
Rimi: What?! ... It's from Nana-chan!
Saya: You mean... the student body president?!
Rimi: I wonder if everything is okay...
Rimi: ... Huh?! Oh... I see. Okay. I'll ask them. Mhm. Bye.
Saya: ... What's up?
Rimi: Uh, well... She said Glitter*Green is gonna do a performance for my sister's birthday.
Saya: What...? Oh yeah! Wanibe-senpai plays the keyboard for Glitter*Green. What else did she say?
Rimi: She asked if Poppin'Party wanted to perform with them...
Saya: What?! Us?! At a concert with Glitter*Green?!
Rimi: Well, she didn't really say concert. She said it's supposed to have more of an at-home feeling with just family and classmates...
Rimi: And that... since they're already having a show, why not have Poppin'Party join as well.
Saya: B-but even if it's just family and classmates, that's still a lot of people.
Rimi: ... Probably.
Saya: Wow, the big leagues.
Rimi: I told Nana-chan that I would ask you guys first...
Saya: When is it?
Rimi: It's the same day as my sister's birthday, so... about a week from now. They need an answer by that morning.
Saya: If we perform there, that'd mean we're gonna be on the same stage as Glitter*Green.
Saya: Makes you kinda nervous, doesn't it?
Rimi: I'm not sure we can do it...
Rimi: Ahh... Maybe we should tell them no...
Saya: Rimi-rin, hold on. No need to decide so quickly.
Saya: This is pretty important... It's your sister's birthday. Just like buying the present, let's take our time to think about it.
Saya: I told you, it's no problem for me. I'll help you out.
Rimi: S-Saya-chan... Thank you...
Saya: Let's start by asking the others tomorrow. We've still got plenty of time before we need to reply, right?
Rimi: ... Yeah, you're right! Okay, I'll make sure to talk to them tomorrow!
