event04-04 Chapter 3: That Which Lifts the Spirit

Rimi shuts herself in the studio to make the new song. What will the others do to support her...?

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Tae Hanazono, Rimi Ushigome, Saya Yamabuki, Arisa Ichigaya)

[Yamabuki Bakery]

Kasumi: ... Alright! Everyone here?! Saya~, we're ready!
Saya: Okay~. It's a bit of a tight fit, but... in ya go.
Arisa: Ugh~, this is so annoying... Why do we have to do this~?
Kasumi: C'mon~. Arisa, is something wrong?
Arisa: Of course something's wrong! I'm told we have band rehearsal... and I show up to this!
Arisa: I mean, I had a bad feeling when I heard we're meeting at Saya's house, but...
Kasumi: We have to do this! For the show!
Arisa: For the... What are you...?
Arisa: O-Tae, even you have to think this is weird, no?
Tae: ... What's weird?
Arisa: Wh-?! Are you holding an apron...?!
Kasumi: Now for today's band activity!
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Studio]

Rimi: Hmm... Mmm...
Rimi: Something doesn't seem right. Maybe if I change the lyrics here...
Rimi: ... No. Still no good... Sigh... I have no idea what to do next...
Rimi: And there's so little time until the show. What to do... Ahh...
Rimi: ... I should probably take a little break.
Kasumi: Ri~mi-rin♪ How's everything?
Rimi: Ah! Kasumi-chan! Saya-chan! O-Tae-chan! Arisa-chan!
Saya: O-Tae said that you were having a rough time, so we came to see how you were doing.
Tae: The studio workers told me. They said they could sometimes hear a strange growling coming from inside the room...
Arisa: What kind of growling makes it through soundproof walls?!
Saya: Feeling stuck?
Rimi: ... Y-yeah. The song is almost done, but I'm having a hard time with the lyrics.
Rimi: ... Maybe I should have asked Kasumi-chan for help after all.
Kasumi: That wouldn't work! This is gonna be a song that only you could make! There'd be no point in me writing the lyrics!
Arisa: Look who's talking! Before you were begging to write the lyrics yourself!
Kasumi: ... And Arisa was nice enough to explain why I was wrong!
Arisa: That's better.
Rimi: ... Haha. Hahaha.
Rimi: ... Yeah. You know, I think I feel better just seeing your faces. Thank you.
Saya: Great. With a smile like that, you'll definitely be okay.
Rimi: ... Wait. Speaking of... Your faces seem a bit lighter...
Kasumi: Ehehe, Ri~mi-rin. What if you could feel even better than you do now? Wouldn't you like that?!
Rimi: Y-yeah... Of course I would...
Kasumi: Okay~! Then how about some pastries to lift your spirit~?
Rimi: ... Huh? P-pastries? Whoa, this bag is so warm...!
Saya: We baked these especially for you, to help cheer you up, Rimi-rin. Take a look.
Rimi: Ah! Chocolate cornets~! Oh, how I missed you!
Rimi: Th-this is amazing! You guys made these for me?! They look delicious, just like the ones you sell!
Saya: Well, we did ask my dad to help us make them. But we're the ones who put the chocolate inside.
Saya: When we told him we would be making them for you, he was happy to give us a hand. Said it's because you're our best customer.
Rimi: Th-thank you...
Kasumi: Alrighty, see ya later!
Rimi: What? You're leaving already?
Kasumi: Rimi-rin! We're looking forward to hearing something great!
Rimi: O-okay!
Saya: It's okay. I know you can do it, Rimi-rin.
Rimi: Thank you! I'll do my best!
Tae: Make sure the door is closed when you're making weird nois-
Arisa: Alright, that's enough! Let's go, O-Tae!
Rimi: ... Thank you so much. ... Sniff.
Rimi: No. No crying... Okay! I'll eat a chocolate cornet and get back to work!
Rimi: ... Hm? There's still more at the bottom of the bag. What is it? The shape is kinda strange...
Rimi: Is this... like the letter G? Here's an... R? And this one...
Rimi: Ah! If you take these... and put it like this...
Rimi: G-O R-I-M-I. Go Rimi...
Rimi: Thanks, guys~... Okay, here I go!
