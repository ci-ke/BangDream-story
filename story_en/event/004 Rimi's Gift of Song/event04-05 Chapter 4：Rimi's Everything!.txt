event04-05 Chapter 4: Rimi's Everything!

Rimi plays her new song for the rest of Poppin'Party. What will they think...?

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Tae Hanazono, Rimi Ushigome, Saya Yamabuki, Arisa Ichigaya)

[Studio]

Kasumi: ... It's almost time!
Saya: Yep! Rimi-rin's worked so hard for this!
Kasumi: She really has!
Arisa: And... where is she, exactly?
Saya: She said she's putting on the final touches.
Arisa: When it comes to music, she sure is specific about every last detail.
Tae: Ah, here she is.
Rimi: H-hey, sorry to make you all wait.
Kasumi: Congrats, Rimi-rin! Good job! Yay~!
Kasumi: Okay! In honor of Rimi-rin finishing a new song, let's cheer! All together... Ready, set-!
Rimi: Ah! Hold on! Wait, Kasumi-chan!
Kasumi: Huh? What happened?!
Rimi: I'm not really sure if it's good, so I wanted you guys to listen to it once...
Rimi: I put it on this recorder. Could I play it for you? I want to hear your honest opinions...
Kasumi: Does that mean... we'll be the first ones to ever hear the song?! Woo~! Awesome~!
Saya: Yeah, we can do that. Ahaha, now I'm getting nervous, too.
Rimi: O-okay. I'll start it...
(Black cut)
Kasumi: ...
Rimi: ...W-well, what do you think?
Kasumi: A-amazing! That was totally awesome!
Rimi: R-really...?
Kasumi: Look, I've still got goosebumps. I'm so impressed... Aren't you Arisa?
Arisa: Yeah, it's good I guess. Nice job, Rimi.
Saya: I really like the lyrics!
Saya: I could tell how you feel about Yuri-senpai just from listening to them.
Saya: No one would think you made this song in such a short time.
Rimi: ... O-Tae-chan, what about you?
Tae: ... Rad.
Rimi: Are you all being honest? Is that what you really think...?
Rimi: I want you to be straightforward, without trying to be nice because we're friends...
Kasumi: We told you what we think! If we played this song at the birthday party, it'd be great!
Rimi: ... I see. I hope so...
Saya: ... Uhm, Rimi-rin? What's up? Something's been bothering you for a while now.
Rimi: I-it's just, while I was making the song, I started to feel like I wasn't sure...
Rimi: Will this really make my sister happy...?
Rimi: Saya-chan, you understand because it was the same when we went shopping. Making decisions is really hard for me...
Saya: Rimi-rin...
Tae: Uhm... I don't really follow, but...
Tae: Rimi, in the end, who do you want to be happy after you play this song?
Arisa: Have you been paying attention at all?! She made the song for her sister-
Tae: I got that part, but Rimi, is your sister... the only person you wrote this song for?
Rimi: ... Huh? W-well...
Tae: Putting on a show for only one person, even though there's gonna be such a huge crowd...
Tae: Would you call that a fun show?
Rimi: ...Oh. I see... I get it.
Rimi: O-Tae-chan is right. That kind of show wouldn't be fun at all!
Saya: Yeah... I also hadn't thought about that until now. We can't put on a show if we're not playing for everyone watching.
Arisa: O-Tae... You know, every now and then you know exactly what to say... I'm surprised.
Tae: ... Did I say the right thing?
Kasumi: Alrighty! Then let's put on a show for not just Yuri-senpai, but for everyone!
Kasumi: There's only one thing left to do!
Rimi: ... Yeah! Just practice!
Kasumi: Yes!! Time to get down to business!
Arisa: Yeah, but there are only three days left. There's nowhere to practice with the studio being booked...
Kasumi: ... But there is. A place where only we can practice! Starting today, let's go there and play our hearts out!
Arisa: When you say, "a place where only we can practice..." you don't mean... my place, do you?
Kasumi & Saya: Yes!!
Tae & Rimi: Yes!!
Arisa: Ugh!! You gotta be kidding!!
