event04-06 Chapter 5: Rimi's Present

The girls practice their new song in Arisa's basement... long enough for a sleepover.

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Tae Hanazono, Rimi Ushigome, Saya Yamabuki, Arisa Ichigaya)

[Ichigaya Residence - Basement]

Rimi: Kasumi-chan, it feels like you're coming in a bit late at the start of the chorus. You have to be in sync with everyone or you'll get left behind.
Kasumi: Gotcha!
Rimi: Saya-chan, the syncopation in the second verse is a little weak. Try to make the beats you're supposed to stress a bit more clear.
Saya: Okay, I'll give it a shot.
Arisa: (Wh-when did Rimi get so intense...?)
Rimi: And Arisa-chan.
Arisa: Y-yeah?!
Rimi: Can you be just a little more gentle during the intro? Right now, it sounds like we're playing in a marching band...
Arisa: G-gently? ... I-I'll try.
Kasumi: ... Ahh~. Can we take a quick break~?! We've been playing three hours straight. I'm beat...
Rimi: ... Ah, r-right. Sorry! I didn't even realize!
Kasumi: Okay then, let's rest for 10 minutes!
Saya: That means it's a good time for... Here you go, guys! I brought some pastries for you to eat if anyone wants.
Kasumi: Me! Yay~! Itadakima~su!
Arisa: That was fast.
Tae: ... Hey, what about me? What should I work on?
Rimi: S-something for you to improve on? Hm, uhh... Okay. O-Tae-chan... You can try to smile more.
Tae: More... smiling? ...Like this?
Tae: ...
Rimi: Y-yeah... Try like that, please.
Kasumi: Nom nom... I guess tomorrow's the day!
Arisa: Hey, don't talk with food in your mouth!
Saya: Only one more day... The week went by so fast.
Arisa: Hold on. Isn't it a little too early to start relaxing? We still got a long way to go to play that song in front of a crowd.
Saya: Well, we managed to get this far, we just gotta put in a little more effort. That's why we're all prepared to stay for the night.
Arisa: ... I'm letting you know right now. Once we're done with rehearsal, you guys have to leave. Th-there will be no sleepovers... Got it?!
Kasumi: How can you say that when you're really happy that we're staying over~?
Arisa: Wh-what?! No way!
Saya: Hehe. Alright, either way, we've gotta keep practicing until the last minute. Then Rimi-rin can decide whether or not we'll perform!
Kasumi: That's right! Yeah! The night is young! Let's do this!
Kasumi: Ehehe, I'm so excited!
Arisa: To tell you the truth, you're all just making my life harder! ... Thinking you can just stay at my house...
Saya: ... Oh, yeah. That reminds me, Rimi-rin. What did you decide to buy for Yuri-senpai?
Rimi: Buy...? What do yo- Ah! Wh-what do I do?! I was so focused on the song, I completely forgot...!
Kasumi: So... Why don't you make this song her present?!
Saya: Oh, yeah. I think that's a great idea. There's no way you could find a present with more thought put into it.
Tae: And then... a bit more of a smile...
Tae: ... Kinda like this.
Arisa: Ew, no! Anyway, I guess if you say that, we have no choice but to give it our all...
Rimi: Th-thank you... all. Okay, could you guys bear with me a little bit longer, then...?
Kasumi: Don't act like we're strangers! We'll be with you all night!
Kasumi: Alright, let's play!
(Black cut)
Kasumi: Haa~, so sleepy~... I think it's about time we get to bed.
Arisa: ... A-are you all really planning on staying here?
Kasumi: Of course! We have to try it one more time in the morning.
Tae: Uhh, Rimi... There's something I wanted to ask you about.
Rimi: Really? What's that?
Tae: During the performance, if I smile the whole time, I won't be able to see anything, so can I just make a normal face every now and then?
Rimi: ... Y-yeah. I think... that's fine.
Tae: Great... I was worried for a second. Good night.
Rimi: ... G-good night.
(Black cut)
