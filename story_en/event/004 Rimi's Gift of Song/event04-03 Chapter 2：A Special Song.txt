event04-03 Chapter 2: A Special Song

Rimi has begun writing a new song for Yuri. She requests to write the lyrics herself...

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Tae Hanazono, Rimi Ushigome, Saya Yamabuki, Arisa Ichigaya)

[A Few Days Later]

[Hanasakigawa Girls' Academy - Class 1A]

Kasumi: Rimi-rin, how's the new song?
Rimi: Uhh... I'm kind of having trouble.
Saya: I can tell. You were staring at the sheet music all through class.
Rimi: Huh? You saw that?!
Saya: Yeah, sorry. I did.
Tae: And I could hear you thinking out loud, too. Like, "Hmm, mmm..."
Rimi: No way! Really...?! I didn't even realize. Y-you think the teacher noticed?
Saya: No, I don't think so. You're fine.
Rimi: Th-thank goodness...
Kasumi: Fufu. I know the feeling, though. Whenever I can't think up good lyrics for a song, I make the exact same sounds!
Arisa: And even then, her lyrics are still not that great. It just sounds like groaning to me.
Kasumi: Hmph~! Arisa, you're such a meanie!
Saya: It makes sense that you'd try so hard, though. It's a song for Yuri-senpai's birthday, and she's special to you.
Rimi: Yeah... I just have this feeling like I have to create a song that will make her happy...
Tae: What've you got so far?
Rimi: Uhm, so far I have the melody up to the second verse. I also have an idea of what chords I want for the chorus...
Kasumi: So you're almost done!
Rimi: But... I'm not sure if what I have is good enough...
Kasumi: It's fine! What you have will work! I guess it's about time I started writing the lyrics, right?!
Rimi: Oh, uhm. Kasumi-chan...
Kasumi: Hm? Something wrong, Rimi-rin?
Rimi: Do you think, maybe, I could write the lyrics for this one...?
Kasumi: Huh? You want to do the lyrics too?! Are you sure, Rimi-rin? Do you want me to help?
Rimi: Yeah... Maybe, but...
Kasumi: Rimi-rin, I want to give you a hand~! Isn't there something I can do?
Rimi: I'm glad you want to help, but... How do I say this...?
Arisa: ... Kasumi, come with me for a sec.
Kasumi: ... What? Wh-what's the matter?
Arisa: Just come!
Kasumi: Ack! Don't pull so hard~!
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Arisa: I know you want to help, but Rimi's putting in all this effort for a very good reason, you know. Shouldn't we let her do this on her own?
Arisa: It is a song for her sister, after all.
Kasumi: Oh... I see. Yeah, you're right! Thanks, Arisa!
Arisa: Yeah, yeah. As long as you understand.
Arisa: Sigh... What am I, your mother?
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Kasumi: Rimi-rin! About those lyrics... I'm sorry. I wasn't thinking right... You want to try writing for Yuri-senpai yourself, right?
Rimi: Mhm, yeah...! I know I'm asking for a lot, but...
Rimi: Thank you for letting me do this, Kasumi-chan.
Saya: Rimi-rin, looks like you've got a lot on your plate.
Rimi: Y-yeah... But it's okay. I have you guys supporting me...!
Arisa: ... Well, if you ever need anything, we'll help you out, okay? You just focus on that song.
Arisa: I mean, we do have until the very last minute to decide whether we'll perform or not. Wanibe-san and the others must know it won't be easy.
Rimi: Arisa-chan... Thank you! I'll keep working on making the best song I can!
Tae: Oh, yeah. Rimi, I reserved a slot at the studio for you.
Rimi: The studio...?
Tae: Yeah. You can make all the noises you want there without worrying about anyone else, and you can practice the song as you write it, too.
Rimi: Th-thank you, O-Tae-chan!
Arisa: You know, you could have mentioned that earlier!
Saya: We're here for you, Rimi-rin.
Kasumi: Rimi-rin! We'll be looking forward to hearing it when it's done!
Rimi: Okay! Thanks guys!
Kasumi: ... I wonder what the song will be like. I can't wait to hear it!
Arisa: Forget about that. You sure made it sound like you're this great songwriter, so we better see something amazing from you next time.
Saya: Fufu, fufufu... Hey, Arisa...
Arisa: ... Wh-why are you laughing?! What's so funny?!
Saya: You really care about your friends, don’t you?
Arisa: ... Wh-what?! What are you saying?! I have no idea what you're talking about... Wh-what?!
Saya: Think about it. At first, you didn't even want to perform, but now you're the one supporting Rimi-rin the most.
Arisa: Th-that's stupid! Look, I had to say something! Kasumi was being so pushy...!
Saya: Yeah, yeah. I know. I know. And that's why I said, you really care about your friends, Arisa~.
Arisa: A-are you even listening to me?!
