event04-02 Chapter 1: I Want to Make a New Song!

Poppin'Party is invited to play with Glitter*Green at Yuri's birthday concert. Rimi has an idea...!

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Tae Hanazono, Rimi Ushigome, Saya Yamabuki, Arisa Ichigaya)

[Hanasakigawa Girls' Academy - Class 1A]

Kasumi: ... What? Us... With Glitter*Green...
Arisa: As guest performers?!
Rimi: Y-yeah. Nana-chan called me yesterday to ask...
Kasumi: Yeah~! Yes yes yes! That's amazing~!! Let's do it~!!
Kasumi: Ahh~! This is crazy! It's like we're one step closer to our dream already!
Arisa: W-wait wait wait! There's no way we could pull this off!
Saya: We were thinking we should all talk about it, together. Right, Rimi-rin?
Rimi: Yeah. We want to hear what you guys think...
Kasumi: We have no reason not to do it!
Saya: ... I'd like to agree, but...
Kasumi: What do you mean?
Arisa: Kasumi... Do you even know how many days are left until the performance? It's in a week.
Kasumi: But... we won't know unless we try, Arisa!
Kasumi: We're talking about a birthday performance with Glitter*Green! Wouldn't it be awesome if we could be on the same stage as them?!
Arisa: ... I can see where you're coming from, but let's just calm down and think about it for a second.
Kasumi: Everyone starts off as beginners, don't they? And they get better by rehearsing and doing performances like these!
Saya: Kasumi, just relax. Let's hear what everyone else thinks.
Saya: O-Tae, what about you?
Tae: I'd like to give it a shot. It's hard to give up a chance to perform. I feel like that'd be a waste.
Kasumi: O-Tae~!! I knew you would understand~!
Rimi: I-I'm sorry everyone... This is my fault...
Kasumi: Why apologize?! There's no need! This is a great chance for us, and it's thanks to you that we found it!
Kasumi: We should be thanking you!
Arisa: So? What do you think, Rimi?
Rimi: I-I... Uhm... I was thinking about it all night...
Rimi: I want to do it. And...
Rimi: I want to make a new song... for my sister...
Arisa: Excuse me?!
Rimi: There's so much effort going into this birthday party, I thought I'd like to try and make a new song for her, too...
Kasumi: Why not? That's an awesome idea! I'm with Rimi-rin!
Tae: Yeah. Me too.
Arisa: S-stop, hold on! It's hard enough being a guest performer for Glitter*Green, but on top of that you want to make a new song?! Nope. No way!
Arisa: We only have one week!
Rimi: ... Yeah, I know. I know it's going to be hard.
Rimi: But... I want to be able to play on the same stage as my sister.
Kasumi: Yeah! Of course!
Arisa: But still... Rimi, you know-
Kasumi: We won't know unless we try!
Saya: Alright! How about this?
Saya: We'll practice for the next week, and then we can think about whether or not we should play in the show.
Saya: What do you think?
Kasumi: ... Huh? But doesn't that mean there's a chance we won't perform?
Saya: I mean, think about it. If we go up there and we're unprepared, we could end up ruining the party they worked so hard on.
Saya: Wouldn't that make things worse for Glitter*Green, Yuri-senpai and everyone else there?
Arisa: ... Who's gonna decide whether we should do it after our week of practice?
Saya: Well... We can have Rimi-rin make the call, right?
Rimi: M-me...?!
Saya: I think Rimi-rin is the one person who most needs be convinced that we're ready to play at the show.
Kasumi: I'm with Saya! We're gonna make Yuri-senpai's birthday celebration the best we can, Rimi-rin!
Rimi: Saya-chan, Kasumi-chan... Thank you!
Saya: Arisa, what do you think?
Arisa: Well, Saya's plan is the best so far, so... Fine. I'm in.
Tae: ... Alright, then let's get rehearsing. We only have 6 days, 3 hours, 45 minutes and 56 seconds... 55... 54... 53...
Rimi: D-don't count down like that! It's too stressful...!
Saya: Hahaha... Alright guys, we have a week. Let's do our best!
