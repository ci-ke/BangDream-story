Hagumi says she wants to participate in some winter sports. That's when Hina arrives, and...

(Character: Hagumi Kitazawa, Misaki Okusawa, Hina Hikawa, Ako Udagawa)

[Downtown]

Hagumi: Brr~... It's so cold, Mii-kun...

Hagumi: I am, but... I get cold too, you know... I hope it at least snows soon...

Misaki: That's cool. I don't.
