event33-03 Chapter 2: Snow Michelle

After arriving at the slopes, the group decides they're going to make a snow Michelle...

(Character: Hagumi Kitazawa, Misaki Okusawa, Hina Hikawa, Maya Yamato, Ako Udagawa)

[Ski Resort]

Ako: We're here~!

Misaki: Yeah, no kidding... It's still a little cold for me though.
Maya: Hey, but taste that clean mountain air! Every breath of it is refreshing.
Misaki: Yeah. To be honest, I wasn't too excited about today, but now that I'm here, I think this might be okay.

[Ski Resort - Play Area]

Maya: So this is the play area...? Ah, you can even borrow tools to use in the snow.
Misaki: Looks like they have everything you need. Buckets, toy shovels... the works.
Ako: We're gonna make a snowman, right?! What kind should we make?
Hina: Let's do something awesome! Like one that moves on its own!
Misaki: There's no way to make that not sound ridiculous...
Hagumi: Ah! How about Michelle?! Let's make a snow Michelle!
Misaki: That does fall within the realm of reality... Isn't that pretty hard, though?
Maya: Ah, actually, that sounds doable. Michelle-san is mostly made up of round parts, so she might make for the perfect snowman...
Misaki: Wait, really?
Maya: If we break it up into parts, we should be able to pull it off...
Hagumi: Then let's do it! Where should I start, Maya-san?
Maya: Okay, Kitazawa-san and Ako-san, you make a big snowball and a slightly smaller snowball... Those will be her body and head!
Hagumi & Ako: Okay~!
Hina: What about me~?
Maya: Hina-san, you're in charge of the arms! Start making cylinder shapes using a bucket and some water, please~.
Hina: Arms, huh...? Wait, does she have claws?
Maya: W-we'll worry about detail work after we have the base done.
Hina: Hmm, Okay. I'll get on it!
Misaki: ... Yamato-san, you're amazing... You were able to deal with those three so efficiently...
Maya: H-huh?! No, I'm sure anyone could have done that...
Misaki: No, really. That was impressive. Ah, so what should I do?
Maya: Well, you're probably the most familiar with Michelle-san's smaller details, right? If you could make the smaller parts, that'd be pretty helpful.
Misaki: Oh, uhm, actually... Not really. I'm not that familiar with what she looks like... You know, since I'm always looking out from the inside...
Maya: O-oh, really? Then I guess we have a problem. If only we had Michelle-san here...
Black Suit: Okusawa-sama, please use this.
Maya & Misaki: Huh?
Maya: Th-That's...!
Misaki: It's Michelle... She's even got a new winter outfit.
Black Suit: Kokoro-sama expressed a desire to play in the snow with Michelle, so we had this insulated, water-proof prototype made for such an occasion. We would like for you to test it out.
Misaki: ... Sure. Okay, got it.
Black Suit: Thank you for your cooperation.
Misaki: ... Alright, looks like I'm gonna get changed. Be right back.
Maya: Uhm, okay... That really surprised me. They just appeared out of nowhere... But you barely even flinched... That's pretty cool.
Misaki: Nah, I've just gotten used to it, I guess. Not really sure how to feel about that though...
Hagumi: Maya-san~! We're finished!
Maya: Okay! Put that smaller snowball on top of the bigger one, and then we'll pack snow where the two meet to smooth out the edges.
Ako: I-it's too heavy~! We can't do it ourselves!
Maya: Hang on, I'll help...! Okay, ready? Heave!
Hina: Maya-chan~! The arms are ready~! How do they look?
Maya: Great! Stick them on, and we'll use snow to attach them!
Hina: Okay~! Hiyah~!
Hagumi: Wow~! It's starting to look like Michelle! Maya-san, you're awesome! Super duper awesome!!
Ako: But, what now? What about the face and everything else?
Maya: Well~...
Michelle (Misaki): Hello, everyone~!
Hagumi: Yay~! It's Michelle!!
Ako: What are you doing here, Michelle?!
Michelle (Misaki): I heard you were making a snowman of me, so I came to help you all~!

Hina: Aye, aye, captain! So the eyes are like this... Here's the hat... Then the clothes♪
Michelle: (She's so fast! And it looks great, too! Hina-san, what are you?!)
