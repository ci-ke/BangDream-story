event33-06 Chapter 5: Hagumi's Advice

Unfamiliar with skiing, Maya falls, again and again. Unable to stand by and watch, Hagumi tries to teach her the right way to do it...

(Character: Hagumi Kitazawa, Misaki Okusawa, Hina Hikawa, Maya Yamato, Ako Udagawa)

[Ski Resort - Beginner's Course]

Maya: Urgh... Skiing sure is hard... M-my legs feel so unstabl- Uwah~! My skis fell off...!
Ako: Maya-san's down again!
Maya: Sigh...
Hagumi: ... Uhm, Maya-san!
Maya: Yes?
Hagumi: T-try going hrmph!
Maya: ... 'Hrmph'?
Hagumi: Uhm, uhh... Like this. Bend your knees and crouch a little... Hrmph...
Maya: ...? ... Ah! So lower my center of gravity?
Hagumi: Yeah, that's it! Probably!
Maya: Okay... Let's see... Oh, this feels a bit steadier!
Michelle: (Hagumi...?)
Maya: ... Wh-whoa~!! Now I can't stop sliding!
Hagumi: If you wanna stop, go hup with your knees!
Maya: What?! 'Hup'?!
Hagumi: Mhm! Bend your knees inward! Hup!
Maya: Hup! ... Ohh~! I did it, Kitazawa-san!
Ako: Wow, awesome~! Maya-san's skiing!
Hina: That's it, Maya-chan! You got this!
Michelle (Misaki): Hagumi... She's actually trying to teach her... using actual words...
Maya: Kitazawa-san, what should I do to turn?!
Hagumi: Don't look at your feet! Face forward!
Maya: Face 'forward'?!
Hagumi: Yeah! Uhm... Uhh, ah! Look in the direction you want to go!
Maya: Got it! The direction I want to go... The direction I want to go...
Michelle (Misaki): Wow... She's turning...
Maya: I see... If you face the direction you want to go, your body naturally turns that way as well, causing you to turn... I think I'm starting to get it!
Hina: Oh~! Maya-chan's skiing! She's really skiing!
Ako: You're doing great! Keep doing that all the way to the bottom!
Maya: Alright! Here I go!
Ako: Wait for me~!
Hagumi: Ah! Hold on~! I'm going too~!
Michelle (Misaki): Guess we should get going too, Hina-san.
Hina: Yup! It's been so long since I've done this! I'm so excited!
(Black cut)

[Ski Resort - Lift]

Hina: Maya-chan~! This way~!
Maya: R-right!
Hagumi: The hup part is right, but relax your body a little, like haaa~!
Maya: Haaa~...
Hagumi: Yeah! That's it! Perfect!
Ako: Maya-san! Just a little more!
Hagumi: And... goal~!
Maya: Phew~...
Ako: Nice job, Maya-san! Congratulations!
Michelle (Misaki): Yamato-san, you hardly fell at all on the bottom half of the course.
Maya: Y-yeah! All thanks to Kitazawa-san!
Hagumi: I didn't do anything! That was all your doing, Maya-san!
Maya: No, I could only do it because you took the time to teach me how. Thank you so much!
Hagumi: R-really?! Ehehe, I'm glad it made sense!
Hina: What do you think, Maya-chan? Wanna go down again?
Maya: I want to be able to ski down this course without falling!
Hagumi: Oh~! That's the spirit, Maya-san!
Maya: Alright! Today, I'm going to ski to my heart's content!
Ako: Me too! Me too! Let's keep going!
Hina: Okay, should we have a race next? Loser will be punished!
Michelle (Misaki): Where'd that come? Yet another crazy idea from Hina-san, huh?
Hagumi: Sounds like fun! I accept your challenge!
Maya: Wh-what?! I'm not ready to race yet~!
