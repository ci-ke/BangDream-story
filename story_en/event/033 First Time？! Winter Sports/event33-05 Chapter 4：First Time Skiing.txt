event33-05 Chapter 4: First Time Skiing

Maya tries skiing for the first time. Will she really be able to pull it off...?

(Character: Hagumi Kitazawa, Misaki Okusawa, Hina Hikawa, Maya Yamato, Ako Udagawa)

[Ski Resort]

Hina: Oh man~! It's been so long since I've gone snowboarding!
Ako: Me too! Me too!
Maya: So you're all snowboarding? Should I do that too? I feel like skiing is a little less scary...
Michelle (Misaki): Yeah, that's true. You can't really move your legs snowboarding, so it can be a bit more intimidating.
Hagumi: Plus your body is sideways while you're going down the hill. But with skiing, you face forward, so it feels a bit safer!
Ako: I'm definitely a ski person!
Hina: I can do both, so~... maybe I'll just change halfway through the day!
Maya: You always amaze me, Hina-san... Alright, I'll go with skiing!
Hagumi: Everybody ready?
All (ex. Hagumi): Yeah~!
Hagumi: Okay~! Ski slopes, here we come!
Maya: U-uhm... I was able to get my skis on, but... How do I walk in these? I keep sliding backwards...
Hagumi: Oh, you just go like, grah ha ha ha!
Maya: H-huh?! What is 'grah ha ha ha'...?
Hagumi: You know, grah ha ha ha! Maya-san, what you're doing is more of a whoosh~!
Maya: ... I have no idea what you're talking about.
Ako: I think it's actually like, swoosh, swoosh!
Hina: No way, Ako-chan! Isn't it like, swish swish swish~?
Maya: Ngh... What is going on...?
Michelle (Misaki): J-just point your toes outward so that the skis are facing diagonally. Then you can walk forward like that, a little bit at a time...
Maya: Okay, got it... Oh! You're right! I can definitely move like this!
Ako: Alright! To the lift, we go~!
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Ski Slope - Lift]

Hagumi: Maya-san, we're about to get on!
Maya: Okay! I'm a little nervous, this being my first time and all, but I'm ready!
Hina: You sure about that, Maya-chan? The lift doesn't stop, so once the people in front of us get on, we gotta rush in and get on quickly. Like, swoosh, then bam!
Maya: ... 'Swoosh', then 'bam'...? We're really just gonna get on while it's moving like this?
Ako: Yup! That's right!
Maya: Th-there's no way I can do that... Isn't there some way to slow it down, just a little bit...?
Hagumi: It's okay! Once you try it out, it's actually really easy! If you need me to, I can let you know the timing too!
Michelle (Misaki): Uhm... If you're worried, you can just take off your skis and ride the lift that way, so~...
Maya: Wait, really?
Michelle (Misaki): Yeah... Once you've done a bit of practice up there, I think you should be able to get on the lift, no problem.
Maya: That makes sense... Okay, I'll do it that way for now. I'm sorry, Kitazawa-san. After you offered to help me and everything...
Hagumi: No problem! Either way's fine by me!
(Black cut)

[Ski Resort - Beginner's Course]

Ako: We're here~!
Maya: Th-this is the beginner's course? It seems pretty steep...
Hina: I think you'll see that it's not too bad once you try it out.
Hagumi: Yeah! You won't pick up too much speed either, so there's nothing to worry about!
Michelle (Misaki): By the way... I know it's kinda late to be saying this, but I'm not sure I can even snowboard with this on...
Hina: As long as you can see what's in front of you, it's fine~♪
Michelle (Misaki): I don't think it's that simple...
Ako: Come on! Let's go already~!
Maya: U-uhm... What should I do?
Hagumi: It's okay, Maya-san! We can slide down together!
Maya: O-okay! Lead the way!
Hagumi: You got it! When you start, first you wanna slide by going bahhh~!
Maya: Uhm, 'bahhh~'? Okay... What about stopping?
Hagumi: Ah! You just gotta go skreee to stop!
Maya: Skreee...
Hagumi: Alrighty! Let's give it a shot!
Maya: Y-yeah...
Michelle (Misaki): Y-Yamato-san, you alright? Your legs are shaking...
Maya: I-I'm fine! H-here I go!
Maya: Whoa~!!
Hagumi: Maya-san?!
Ako: Oh no~! ... Maya-san, are you hurt?!
Hina: Awesome, Maya-chan! How many spins was that? Was that a triple axel?
Misaki: That was not 'awesome'... Seriously, Yamato-san, are you okay?
Maya: Yeah... Somehow...
Hagumi: ... I'm sorry... It's because I didn't explain it right...
Maya: N-no... It's not your fault. It's mine. Sorry you had to teach me for nothing, but I can't seem to get the hang of the 'skreee' part...
Michelle (Misaki): What do you think? Should we walk down together?
Maya: No... That'd just be more of a burden to you all... I'll keep trying!
Ako: Really? Don't push yourself too much!
Hina: Yeah, there's no point if you end up getting hurt.
Maya: Ahahaha... You're right... I'll be more careful...
Hagumi: ...
