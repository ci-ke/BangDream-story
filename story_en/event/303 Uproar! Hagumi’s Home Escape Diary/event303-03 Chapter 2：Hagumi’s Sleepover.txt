event303-03 Chapter 2: Hagumi’s Sleepover

Hagumi invites Kokoro to the treehouse. A sleepover is held at Hagumi’s place.

(Character: Kokoro Tsurumaki, Hagumi Kitazawa)

[Treehouse]

Hagumi: I got this huge shiitake mushroom today, and I bet it’d be amazing grilled with soy sauce!
Hagumi: Alright, once I finish the laundry, I’ll start cooking... I’m home~! ...Wait, huh?
Hagumi: There’s a letter stuck in the door... Who’s it from?
Hagumi: ...Oh! This is...
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Kokoro: Hagumi, I’m here!
Hagumi: Kokoron! Welcome~!
Kokoro: Fufu, thanks for having me over today!
Kokoro: I brought cake as a little gift. Let’s eat it together later!
Hagumi: Wow...! Um, in times like this... Oh! “No need to fuss,” right?
Hagumi: Hey hey, Kokoron, did you bring your pajamas?
Kokoro: Of course! I’m staying over at your place tonight, after all!
Hagumi: Yay~! Let’s do all kinds of fun stuff!
Hagumi: We can play cards, have a pillow fight, chat a bunch, and then...
Kokoro: Yes, let’s do it all. And if we can’t finish tonight, there’s always tomorrow and the next day!
Hagumi: Yeah! You’re right!
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Hagumi: Wow, it’s so warm when we’re in the futon together. Are you warm too, Kokoron?
Kokoro: I am! Super warm!
Kokoro: ...Hey, Hagumi. Do you enjoy living alone?
Hagumi: Huh? Umm...
Hagumi: It’s fun right now ‘cause you’re here, Kokoron... But I’m not sure if living alone itself is fun yet.
Kokoro: Oh really?
Hagumi: Yeah. But...
Hagumi: I wanna try doing lots more things.
Hagumi: I feel like if I learn and experience more, I’ll become an even cooler grown-up.
Hagumi: Then maybe I’ll be able to make even more amazing croquettes!
Kokoro: Fufu, I’m sure you will. You’ve got this, Hagumi!
Hagumi: Thanks, Kokoron! ...Yaaawn~.
Kokoro: Oh my, getting sleepy already?
Hagumi: Yeah... But it feels like such a waste to fall asleep just yet...
Hagumi: I still... wanna keep talking with you, Kokoron...
Hagumi: So... I’m staying awake...
Kokoro: It’s okay, Hagumi! Even if tonight ends, we can always have another sleepover!
Kokoro: Because you’re my “next door neighbor” now!
Hagumi: I’m... your “next door neighbor”...?
Kokoro: Yup!
Hagumi: ...Yeah! Sounds good, “next door neighbor”!
(Background change)
Kokoro: Now then, let’s chat until we fall asleep. What should we talk about?
Hagumi: Hmm... Oh, yeah, the other day Mii-kun was...
Kokoro: Then next time we’ll have Kanon and Kaoru too...
Kokoro & Hagumi: ......... ...............
