event303-01 Opening: Hagumi Runs Away

Hagumi has run away from home! Hello, Happy World! is shocked, but Hagumi’s resolve is firm...

(Character: Kokoro Tsurumaki, Kaoru Seta, Hagumi Kitazawa, Kanon Matsubara, Misaki Okusawa)

Hagumi: Dad, you're such an... idiot~~!!!
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Tsurumaki Residence]

Misaki: Hagumi, did you run away from home!?
Hagumi: Yeah! I'm not going back home ever again!
Kaoru: Why so suddenly...? Did something happen at home?
Kanon: If you want, I’d love to hear about it.
Hagumi: Well... This morning while I was eating breakfast with my dad, we started talking about the open campus we went to the other day.
Kokoro: You mean when we went on a trip at Kanon’s university!
Misaki: It wasn’t just for fun—it was a school visit...
Hagumi: It was so much fun, and it made me wonder if there are other fun-looking colleges out there too...
Hagumi: So I told my mom and dad I wanted to visit a different school’s open campus next.
Hagumi: I said I wanted to explore different paths and think more about my future.
Hagumi: Like, maybe after graduation, I could live with someone or even on my own like Kano-chan-senpai! That sounds so fun!
Kanon: Yeah. Living with Chisato-chan is really fun.
Hagumi: Right!? But then, Dad...
Misaki: What did he say?
Hagumi: He said there's no way I could live alone. That he’d never let me do something like that...
Hagumi: I told him I totally could! But he just kept saying no way, over and over...
Hagumi: It made me so mad... and sad... so I yelled “idiot!” at him and then...
Kokoro: That’s when you ran away!
Hagumi: Yeah...
Kanon: I'm sure your dad only said that because he’s worried about you...
Kaoru: Ah, as Shakespeare once said, “Parents know not the hearts of their children...”
Misaki: Um, that’s not Shakespeare... but whatever, we’ve got bigger problems.
Hagumi: So yeah, I decided to move out and live on my own—to prove to my dad that I can handle living by myself!
Misaki: You “decided” ... What exactly are you planning to do?
Hagumi: Umm... well...
Kanon: Why not stay at our place? Chisato-chan’s away for a few days on work.
Kaoru: You’re always welcome at my place too, Hagumi.
Misaki: Same here, though I’d have to ask my parents first... but still... I get why you’d want to run away from home...
Kokoro: Me too! You're always welcome!
Hagumi: Everyone... thank you!
Hagumi: But if I stay at someone else’s house, I don’t think that proves I can live alone!
Hagumi: So I’ll do this on my own!
Misaki: No way. You can’t do this by yourself. Even if you tried staying at a hotel, you probably don’t have the money for it...
Kanon: And sleeping outside would be really dangerous. I don’t think you can rent a place by yourself either...
Hagumi: What!? Really!? Ugh... what should I do...
Kokoro: I know! Then you can just live at my house!
Kaoru: Wait, at Kokoro’s house?
Misaki: Kokoro, were you listening? Staying with someone else isn’t living alone.
Hagumi: I appreciate the thought, Kokoron, but I want to live by myself!
Kokoro: Yes, exactly! You want to live on your own!
Kokoro: Then just live alone... inside my house!
Hagumi: Live alone... inside your house? What does that mean...?
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[A Few Hours Later]

(White cut)
(Background change)
Kokoro: Come on, Hagumi! Let’s go look!
Hagumi: Okay! Wait for me, Kokoron~!
Misaki: “Living alone inside Kokoro’s house,” huh...
Misaki: Don’t tell me... she’s building Hagumi a house in her backyard or something...
Kanon: What a lovely place... Is this called a treehouse?
Kaoru: It’s like a scene from a movie...! Ah, so fleetingly beautiful...
Misaki: The People in Black built this, but I can’t believe they made something so fancy so fast...
Black Suit: We are honored by your praise, Okusawa-sama.
Black Suit: This treehouse is a renovated version of one originally built per Kokoro-sama’s childhood request.
Black Suit: Inside, you’ll find all the basic necessities for daily life, along with supervision from our staff, a security alarm, and communication devices.
Misaki: Wow... they really thought of everything...
Kanon: Yeah. If the People in Black are watching over her, this might be safer than staying somewhere random.
Hagumi: Whoa! Look! There’s a ladder leading up to the house!
Kokoro: Fufu, let’s go check it out together!
Kaoru: Looks like Hagumi really likes this little castle.
Misaki: Yeah. She might’ve already forgotten she ran away from home...
Misaki: Sigh... Well, I guess we’ll just see how this plays out.
