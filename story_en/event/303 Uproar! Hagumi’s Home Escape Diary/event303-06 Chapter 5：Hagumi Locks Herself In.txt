event303-06 Chapter 5: Hagumi Locks Herself In

Hagumi barricades herself in the treehouse. And at a time like this, who does she call...?

(Character: Kokoro Tsurumaki, Kaoru Seta, Hagumi Kitazawa, Kanon Matsubara, Misaki Okusawa)

Misaki: Hagumi~! Kokoro~! Come on, stop barricading yourselves in and come out already~!
Hagumi: No way~!!!
Hagumi: I'm not coming out—no matter what~!!
Kokoro: I'm staying here with Hagumi too~!
Misaki: They even brought a megaphone...
Kaoru: Looks like they're seriously trying to barricade themselves in this treehouse.
Misaki: No way... There's no way they could actually...
Kanon: They might be able to...
Misaki: Huh?
Kanon: This treehouse has everything they'd need... and if Kokoro-chan's here, the People in Black would help too...
Kanon: If it's just for a while... they might really be able to do it...
Misaki: No way... So then, what are we supposed to do...?
Kaoru: I have an idea.
Misaki: Kaoru-san!?
Kaoru: As the great Shakespeare once said... when a princess shuts herself away and won’t come out...
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Hagumi: Mii-kun and the others got really quiet all of a sudden.
Kokoro: Yeah! So, what's next for our pretend barricade game?
Hagumi: Umm, next we just stay shut in... and stay here forever...
Kokoro: Forever? So we won't see Misaki or Kaoru or Kanon anymore... and we're not going home either?
Hagumi: Huh!? I don’t think I like that...
Kokoro: .........? Hagumi, do you not actually want to do this pretend barricade thing?
Hagumi: Well, um... I just... I just wanted dad to listen to me...
Kokoro: Oh, I see! Then let's tell Misaki and the others how you feel!
Hagumi: W-wait, Kokoron!
Kokoro: ? What is it?
Hagumi: If I talk to dad... will he understand how I feel...?
Hagumi: What if he doesn’t get it... what then...?
Kokoro: Hmm. It's really hard to get your feelings across sometimes.
Kokoro: But if you stay shut in, no one will know how you feel!
Hagumi: Huh?
Kokoro: Words reach people better when you actually say them, instead of just keeping them inside!
Kokoro: That’s why I think you should say what’s in your heart, Hagumi!
Hagumi: Kokoron...
Hagumi: ...Yeah, you're right!
Hagumi: I really want dad to understand. I want him to know that I have things I want to do... that I want to find what I want to do.
Hagumi: But if he doesn’t understand... then I want to know why. I want to know what he's thinking too.
Hagumi: And for that—
Misaki: Hagumi~! Kokoro~! Listen~!
Hagumi: ! That’s Mii-kun’s voice...!
(Background change)
Misaki: Hagumi, listen! I brought your dad here!
Hagumi's Father: Hagumi...!
Hagumi: Dad...!?
Misaki: I get why you're being stubborn, but at least hear him out...!
Hagumi: ...Okay~! Dad, let's talk~!
Misaki: Huh? That’s... not what I expected...
Kanon: Did something happen with Kokoro-chan inside the treehouse...?
Kaoru: Then, let’s do as Hagumi wishes and let her hear her father's voice. Please go ahead, sir.
Hagumi's Father: Y-yeah...
Hagumi: ...............
Hagumi's Father: Hagumi... I'm sorry~!!!!
Hagumi: Huh?
Hagumi's Father: Misaki-chan, Kanon-chan, and Kaoru-chan came to visit... and they told me how much you love our croquettes.
Hagumi's Father: And hearing that... I was... I was so moved! It made me think about your future too!
Hagumi's Father: So, Hagumi, let’s talk!
Kanon: I didn't think that would get to him that much...
Misaki: That’s... so like Hagumi’s dad...
Hagumi: Dad wants to talk to me...
Kokoro: Hagumi. Now’s your chance. Say what’s been in your heart!
Hagumi: ...Yeah!
Hagumi: Dad~~!!
Hagumi's Father: !
Hagumi: Dad, there’s so much I want to tell you~! Like how I tried living on my own here, and how I became Kokoron’s “next door neighbor”...
