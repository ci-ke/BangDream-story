event303-04 Chapter 3: Meeting Hagumi’s Father

Misaki and the others act to reconcile Hagumi and her father. What does he think about Hagumi running away?

(Character: Kaoru Seta, Kanon Matsubara, Misaki Okusawa)

[The Next Day]

[Downtown]

Misaki: Hmm... Kanon-san’s not here yet...
Kaoru: The star of the show always arrives fashionably late. Let’s just wait with confidence.
Misaki: No, I mean... knowing Kanon-san... I bet she got lost...
Kanon: S-sorry to keep you waiting...!
Kanon: Sorry, I got lost on the way here...
Misaki: Ahaha, I knew it... But it’s okay, we didn’t wait that long.
Kanon: I’m glad. So, um... today we’re going to talk to Hagumi-chan’s dad, right?
Misaki: Yeah. Up until now, we’ve only heard Hagumi’s side of the story...
Misaki: I figured her dad probably has his own perspective, too.
Kaoru: Exactly. First we’ll hear his side, and then we can figure out why they’re not seeing eye to eye.
Kanon: Yeah, let’s do that...! ...Oh, look. That’s the shop.
(Background change)
Misaki: Looks like Hagumi’s dad is helping a customer right now.
Kaoru: Right. He seems to be busy with them at the moment.
Kanon: Hagumi-chan’s croquettes are always super popular, after all.
Misaki, Kanon & Kaoru: ...... ............
Misaki: ...Oh, there’s a break in the customers. Now’s our chance...!
Misaki: Uh, excuse me!
Hagumi's Father: Hey, welcome! What can I get ya...!?
Hagumi's Father: You girls friends of Hagumi!? Hagumi, she—uh, never mind. What’ll it be?
Kanon: Sorry, Hagumi-chan’s dad. We didn’t come to buy croquettes or meat today.
Misaki: We actually wanted to talk about Hagumi... Do you have a moment?
Hagumi's Father: ...Ain’t nothin’ to talk about. The day she left, someone from Kokoro-chan’s place came by to let me know she’d be staying there.
Misaki: I’m glad they let you know...
Kanon: The People in Black do their job, huh...
Kaoru: Please, would you consider sitting down and talking with Hagumi?
Kaoru: If you talk things through, I’m sure you’ll both understand each other better.
Hagumi's Father: Not a chance. There’s no way she can live on her own.
Misaki: But... Hagumi’s doing her best, even now...!
Kanon: Misaki-chan. We’ve got to stay calm and talk things through.
Hagumi's Father: That girl grew up eatin’ our croquettes. No way she can handle living alone.
Kanon: ...That’s true. Hagumi-chan really loves this shop—and your croquettes, too.
Kanon: She always said eating one could cheer her up, no matter how sad she was feeling.
Hagumi's Father: See? That’s why she can’t—
Kaoru: Just like Kanon said, Hagumi always told me she wanted to make croquettes as good as her dad’s one day.
Kaoru: Maybe she didn’t run away because you said no to her living alone... Maybe it was because the dad she looks up to so much wouldn’t even hear her out.
Hagumi's Father: Ugh... But still, she—
Misaki: (...Wait. Could it be...?)
Misaki: ...Your real reason for saying no to her living alone... isn’t because she can’t handle it, is it? It’s because you don’t want her to leave, right?
Hagumi's Father: Guh...! T-that’s not true...!
Misaki: Hagumi’s dad...
Kaoru: ...Misaki. Let’s leave it at that for today.
Kanon: Yeah. Thank you for your time, and sorry for interrupting your work.
Hagumi's Father: Hold it!
Misaki, Kanon & Kaoru: !
Hagumi's Father: I ain’t sayin’ I’m okay with her living on her own. But—
Hagumi's Father: ...Could you do me a favor and deliver something to her?
