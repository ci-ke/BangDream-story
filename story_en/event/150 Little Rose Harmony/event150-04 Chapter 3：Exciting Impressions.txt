event150-04 Chapter 3: Exciting Impressions

With the Poppin'Party concert over, the girls share their thoughts on the show.

(Character: Ako Udagawa, Mashiro Kurata, Rui Yashio, Rokka Asahi, Chiyu Tamade)

[Station Entrance]

LOCK (Rokka): Sigh~, they're always so amazing! I can't believe it's over now...♪
Ako: I know~! I wish I could turn back time so I could watch it again!
LOCK (Rokka): Tae-senpai was incredible on the guitar... I need to practice more!
Mashiro: I-I got to make eye contact with Kasumi-san, and she even smiled at me when I did...!
LOCK (Rokka): Really?! She did?! Ah, but she waved at me...!
Mashiro: ...
CHU² (Chiyu): You two seem pretty excited... but if you ask me, their set list could've used some work. I would've done better if I was in charge of it.
Ako: But CHU², it looked like you were really into their performance the whole time.
CHU² (Chiyu): Silence! I was not!
Rui: I sensed they were able to captivate the audience with their very first note.
LOCK (Rokka): Their shows are like that! When I saw them for the first time, I was instantly sucked in too.
Mashiro: M-me too.
Rui: It would seem to be impractical to apply their methods to Morfonica.
CHU² (Chiyu): ... It's interesting that you should focus on that earlier point, Rui.
CHU² (Chiyu): So, which of their songs did you think was the best?
Rui: Best? Well, what constitutes a good song for you? I cannot answer your question when you fail to clarify what it is you mean.
CHU² (Chiyu): What?! Good means good! Nothing more, nothing less!
Rui: Please formulate your question properly before asking me.
CHU² (Chiyu): Argh~! Who does she think she is, LOCK!
LOCK (Rokka): CH-CHU²-san, please calm down! Here, have some water!
Ako: ...
Ako: (I've been in a band the longest... I should do something!)
Ako: Ah! H-hey, Mashiro, you started Morfonica after seeing a Poppin'Party concert, right?
Mashiro: Yes... The performance I saw that day changed everything.
LOCK (Rokka): That's what happened to me too! They're so amazing, aren't they?
Ako: Didn't they ask you a few times to join their band, Rokka?
Mashiro: ... They did?
LOCK (Rokka): Th-that's just because they're so nice~. It did make me super happy, though~.
Mashiro: O-oh...
Mashiro: But did you know Kasumi-san spoke to me at CiRCLE even before I started Morfonica?
LOCK (Rokka): ... Really?
Mashiro: And I was even invited to go see sakura blossoms with them!
LOCK (Rokka): Sakura blossoms?! Was Kasumi-senpai the one who invited you...?
Mashiro: Yes, of course. She was so nice~...
LOCK (Rokka): I-I may not have seen sakura blossoms with her, but I also-
Ako: S-stop it, guys, stop it!
Ako: You're both getting too heated~.
Ako: We just saw a concert together, so shouldn't we be trying to have a fun conversation instead~?
LOCK (Rokka): Ah, y-you're right... I got a bit carried away because we were talking about Poppin'Party...
Mashiro: S-so did I... I'm sorry...
LOCK (Rokka): Oh, I just remembered... I wanted to give you this, Kurata-san. Take a look...
Mashiro: Hm? What is this...?
LOCK (Rokka): It's a flyer I saw by the entrance for "Fairy Legends II: Another Story." It's coming to theaters soon.
Mashiro: Oh... thank you. I had no idea! I didn't even realize...
Ako: Look~, there's the marshmallow rabbit that you like so much, Mashiro! It's as cute as ever~♪
Mashiro: I think I'll add it to my marshmallow rabbit collection and put it on display... Thank you, Asahi-san...
LOCK (Rokka): Ah! I just thought of a great idea!
