event113-07 Ending: The First Step Towards Our Dream

Morfonica feels troubled that they don't have a clear goal. What advice does Yukina give...?

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Ran Mitake, Kokoro Tsurumaki, Aya Maruyama, Yukina Minato, Mashiro Kurata, Toko Kirigaya, Nanami Hiromachi, Tsukushi Futaba, Rui Yashio)

[Café]

Mashiro: Sigh...
Kasumi: Mashiro-chan, what's wrong? You and the rest of Morfonica seem kinda down!
Mashiro: N-no, we're not! Umm... We're...
Nanami: We were just thinking about how amazing everyone is.
Toko: You've all got clear visions of what you wanna do. It's amazing.
Tsukushi: I hate to say it, but we don't have a clear idea for what we want to do yet.
Mashiro: Yeah. But... I wonder if we ever will have a clear dream like you all have...
Yukina: There's no need to rush things.
Mashiro: Huh...?
Yukina: Kurata-san, you want to have a clear vision of what you want to do, don't you?
Mashiro: Huh? W-well... Yes...
Yukina: In that case, you have nothing to worry about. Your desire for a vision is the first step in helping you decide what kind of future you'd like to have.
Aya: I agree with Yukina-chan.
Aya: Morfonica should take its time and figure out its future together.
Mashiro: Figure it out... together...? But I don't know if we can...
Yukina: Instead of asking yourselves if you can, how about asking yourselves if you want to?
Yukina: Kurata-san... No. Everyone in Morfonica, what sort of future would you like for your band?
Mashiro: I... I want...
Tsukushi: ... I want our band to grow!
Nanami: Ah. You beat me to it!
Toko: Ahaha. Me too, me too~!
Rui: Yes, we certainly need to do that.
Toko: And? What about you, Shiro?
Mashiro: M-me too...! I want to grow too...!
Yukina: I see. Well then, I expect you to work hard.
Aya: Ehehe. This is so nice... Ah! It's time!
Kasumi: You're right! The seating section opened a little bit ago...!
Kasumi: It's time to move!
Mashiro: O-okay...
Kasumi: So we're all going to keep growing together~. Ehehe.
Yukina: ...? Toyama-san, what are you so happy about?
Kasumi: I was thinking about what you just said and how wonderful it is!
Kasumi: Look! See that? Everyone in Morfonica has such a sparkling face now!
Yukina: ... Do they really look that different?
Kasumi: They do!
Kasumi: Shows... Bands... They're both so amazing, aren't they? You know, the more sparkling and heart-pounding excitement there is, the harder I wanna work.
Kasumi: I'm so happy I started a band!
Yukina: As usual, you're being quite melodramatic. But... I'm glad I started one as well.
Aya: Kasumi-chan! Yukina-chan! We're leaving~!
Kasumi: Alright~! C'mon, Yukina-senpai!
Yukina: Right.
(Black cut)
(Background change)
