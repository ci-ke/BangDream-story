event139-06 Chapter 5: The Visitor

The group hears a pounding on the door. Who could it possibly be...?

(Character: Yukina Minato, Sayo Hikawa, Lisa Imai, Ako Udagawa, Rinko Shirokane)

[Imai Residence - Front Entrance]

Ako: Yukina-san. Is the circuit breaker actually tripped...?
Lisa: If we reset it, everything will go back to normal... right?
Yukina: It is too dark for me to see.
Sayo: I will shine the light from my smartphone on it.
Rinko: How... is it...?
Yukina: ... It does not seem like the circuit breaker is the cause of the power outage. The circuit breaker lever is in the same position as when Ako and I fixed it.
Rinko: ...
Sayo: Then why did the lights in the house suddenly turn off? You aren't actually implying... that it was the actions of a gh-ghost, are you...?!
Rinko: The ghost... bassist... that follows you home and tries to take your place...
Lisa: Oh~, I hate this~!
Ako: L-Lisa-nee, it's okay. You're going to be fine...
Rinko: Eek...!
Sayo: Wh-who is knocking on the door at a time like this?!
Ako: Wh-what do we do...? Yukina-san...!
Yukina: We just need to look through the peephole to see who it is.
Lisa: No~! If you're attacked too, I really won't be able to handle it...!
Lisa: Eek!
Ako: A-are you okay, Lisa-nee?!
Ako: (Lisa-nee is shaking so much... Why wouldn't she be? Even I'm about to cry just because I think there really is a ghost...)
Ako: (What would Sis do at a time like this...? She'd probably be really scared too.)
Ako: (But, if it were my sister, I'm sure... she would do this...!)
Ako: I-it's okay now, Lisa-nee! I'll go yell at the ghost that's scaring you!
Lisa: W-wait, Ako! What are you trying to do?! Your hands are shaking!
Sayo: Are you actually planning on opening the door...?!
Rinko: Ako-chan, no-
Ako: I-I don't know if you're a ghost or what...! B-but don't be mean to Lisa-nee anymore~!
Lisa: ...!
Yukina: Oh...? Why are you here?
Yukina's Dad: Good evening. Were you in the middle of something?
Ako: Yukina-san's dad?!
Lisa: Huh...? Wh-what's going on...?
Yukina's Dad: Apparently, there's a power outage in this area due to the lightning. The power has not been restored, so I came to check on you all.
Sayo: So that is why the lights in the house went off despite the circuit breaker not being tripped...
Lisa: Th-thank goodness~...
Yukina: Oh, the lights... It would seem the power has been restored.
Ako: It was just a power outage... That was really scary~. Right, Rin-rin?
Rinko: ...
Ako: Huh? Rin-rin? Hey, Rin-rin!
Rinko: Ah... S-sorry, Ako-chan... The moment you opened the door... I sort of passed out... Wh-what happened...?
Sayo: Are you saying you fainted while still standing...?
Rinko: It... would seem so...
Ako: Wah, I'm sorry, Rin-rin! I didn't notice at all!
Yukina's Dad: It would appear you all had a terrible fright. Would you like to come over to our house?
Lisa: No, it wasn't just because of the power outage... But if it was just that, then I'm fine. Thank you so much.
Yukina's Dad: I see. If you have any trouble, come over any time you like. That goes for you as well, Yukina.
Yukina: I know. We're fine here, so go back home.
(Black cut)
Lisa: I can't believe I was that scared when the cause was a simple power outage~...
Ako: But thank goodness it wasn't a ghost! And now we know that your room's air conditioner was the reason the circuit breaker tripped.
Lisa: Hm... I was certain that I had turned it off though.
Rinko: U-um... it might be that... the air conditioner reacted to... your new TV's remote control...
Lisa: Does that happen?!
Rinko: Yes... apparently it happens sometimes... I've previously... read an article about it online...
Sayo: So, piecing together all the facts up until now... It seems that ultimately, the reason we saw everything as the actions of a ghost was because we were already scared... Correct?
Rinko: That would... seem to be the case...
Sayo: I-I see...
Yukina: What's wrong, Sayo? Your face is red.
Lisa: Could it be~... that you were scared too, Sayo?
Sayo: That is not the case!
Lisa: Right~, ahaha... Sigh... Now that I have that peace of mind, I feel so tired...
Ako: Me too... Yawn~...
Yukina: What a big yawn.
Lisa: For now, let's go back to the living room and take a breather.
(Black cut)
