event139-02 Chapter 1: Help Me

After Rinko and Ako receive a message from Lisa, they rush to her house...

(Character: Yukina Minato, Sayo Hikawa, Lisa Imai, Ako Udagawa, Rinko Shirokane)

[Shirokane Residence/Udagawa Residence]

Ako: Sigh~...
Rinko: Ako-chan... what's wrong...? Ever since you logged into NFO... you've just been sighing... We just got home from practice, so... are you tired...?
Ako: Nuh-uh. I was just wondering if Lisa-nee is okay at home alone...
Rinko: She was saying over the Roselia group chat... that her house was too quiet...
Ako: I don't like staying home alone either, so I'm starting to worry~.
Rinko: Hmm, should we message her more...? It's just text, but... it might make her feel like we're talking to her...
Ako: Good idea! Let's message her right away over the group chat... Huh? There's something from Lisa-nee.
Rinko: You're right... Huh...? Ako-chan, is this...?
Ako: It says "Help." ... I wonder what's wrong.
Rinko: Could it be... the ghost bassist...?
Ako: Th-that's just a rumor! For now, let's send her a message asking what's wrong!
Rinko: Y-yeah... you're right...
(Black cut)
Rinko: It's been a while... since you sent your message, but... she hasn't replied...
Ako: What if Lisa-nee is in so much trouble that she can't reply?!
Rinko: What...?
Ako: I'm worried about Lisa-nee, so I'm gonna go to her house for a bit. I'm logging out for now!
Rinko: W-wait, Ako-chan... I'll... go with you...
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Residential Area]

Ako: Lisa-nee's house is~... over there, right?
Rinko: Yeah... It should be... Huh...? Isn't that person in front of her house...?
Sayo: Udagawa-san, Shirokane-san...?!

Sayo: ... Also, the ghost story we heard at CiRCLE has me concerned.
Rinko: Imai-san was... really frightened... by that story...
Ako: Anyway, I'm gonna press the intercom button! Hello~!
Lisa: Coming~! Be right there!

Ako: Wh-what do you mean...? You sounded like you were in trouble.

Lisa: I'm sorry, guys~. Why don't you come in for now?
Ako & Rinko: ...?
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Imai Residence - Living Room]

Ako: We waited awhile and didn't get an answer, so we got scared, Lisa-nee~.
Lisa: Ahaha, sorry, my bad. To tell you the truth, after I got home from practice, some stuff happened~...
Lisa: When I got home, it was just so quiet, so I tried playing my bass to fill the silence. Then I heard a loud clatter from the kitchen!
Lisa: No one besides me should've been home, so I panicked and sent a message to Yukina... Or so I thought...
Sayo: Instead, you accidentally sent the message to our group chat.
Lisa: Ahaha, I'm sorry~.

Ako: So that's all it was! But I'm glad you weren't in trouble, Lisa-nee~.
Lisa: You reassured me that it was just a rumor, but after hearing about the ghost bassist, I couldn't get it off my mind. I mean, if they were gonna target someone, it'd be me.
Ako: It's fine. Hauntings don't happen that oft-
Lisa: Eek! What was that?! I heard a weird cracking sound...!
Rinko: That... sounded like... a knock...
Lisa: Seriously~, what gives? Why is this happening on the one day no one else is home~...?!
Ako: Lisa-nee... if you're scared, I'll stay over at your house today! If I let my mom know, I think it should be okay!
Lisa: R-really...? Honestly, it'd be reassuring to have someone with me...! As a matter of fact, why don't you all stay? With this many people, I can distract myself...
Sayo: I understand Minato-san, but us as well? Although, as long as I receive permission from my parents, it should be possible...
Rinko: I-I can stay too... Although, I need to go back home... to get my things...
Yukina: I do not mind.
Ako: Whoa! In that case, it'll be a Roselia sleepover! Yay~!
