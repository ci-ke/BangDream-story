event31-01 Opening: Join Me Online!

Upon finishing rehearsal, Ako has an unexpected request for the other members of Roselia.

(Character: Yukina Minato, Sayo Hikawa, Lisa Imai, Ako Udagawa, Rinko Shirokane)

[CiRCLE Café]

Lisa: I'm back~! I made the reservation for our next rehearsal~.
Rinko: Thank you... As always...
Lisa: You know you don't have to thank me. Just doing the usual☆
Sayo: ... Hey, Udagawa-san.
Ako: Hm? Something wrong?
Sayo: Not really. Just that you were playing ahead of tempo most of rehearsal.
Ako: Ah...
Lisa: Ako~. You should tell us if something is bothering you, you know.
Ako: Really?!
Lisa: Really really. Right, Yukina?
Yukina: ... Yeah. It affects the entire band when you're having an off day. If there's something wrong, we should try to deal with it sooner rather than later.
Ako: O-okay... Then I have a favor to ask, if that's alright...
Yukina: A favor...? Like what...?
Ako: I want you all to play NFO with me!
Sayo: NFO...?
Rinko: ... Neo Fantasy Online... It's a computer game... Ako and I play it together.
Ako: Right now they're having a new player campaign. If you clear a quest with people who are playing for the first time, you get a super cool weapon as a reward!
Yukina: Quest? Weapon? Ako, you've completely lost me.
Lisa: Uhhh~, so in other words, if we play this game with you, you'll get something good?
Sayo: ... What does that have to do with the way you were playing today?
Ako: It's just... I've really wanted that weapon... for a while now... so...
Rinko: That's why you were squirming around so much...
Ako: Yeah... Please, can you guys do this for me?!
Yukina: No.
Ako: Huh?!
Sayo: I agree with Minato-san. Video games are completely unrelated to the band. During rehearsal, you're supposed to block that out and focus on your playing.
Ako: Y-yeah, I know... But still...
Lisa: Hold on now. I totally get where Ako's coming from. There are times when you have something on your mind and you just can't focus. Which is why I was thinking I wouldn't mind giving you a hand.
Ako: Lisa-nee~.
Lisa: What about you two? Are you completely against the idea?
Sayo: ... What do you think, Minato-san?
Rinko: The quest... It's short. I don't think it would take that long...
Ako: Yukina-san...!
Yukina: ... Fine.
Ako: R-really?! Yay~!
Rinko: That's great, Ako-chan...
Yukina: However, once that's done, it's back to rehearsal.
Ako: Okay~! Alright, guys! Tomorrow! Internet café! Let's do this~!
Sayo: ... Well, you gave in to her request pretty easily, Minato-san. Are you sure that was the right choice?
Yukina: I learned recently that putting up a fight against something like this is just a waste of time... This is still for the sake of the band and our goal to be the greatest, though, so I plan to take this seriously.
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Internet Café]

Lisa: Alright, Ako. What should we do?
Ako: So basically, we'll all be sitting at our own booth, and we'll log into the same place so we can play together. All of these computers already have NFO installed, so it'll be easy!
Yukina: We can play together on individual computers?
Rinko: We'll be online... So our journey will take place in the same space.
Ako: Let's start up the game... Oh, what should we do about classes?

Rinko: They're roles... We have to decide on them. I'm a wizard. Ako-chan is... a necromancer...
Ako: Lisa-nee seems kinda like a healer, doesn't she?

Lisa: I see~... So that's your image of me, huh? Okay! I'll give it a try!
Rinko: Yukina-san... Is there a class... you'd like to try out?
Yukina: There are too many to choose from... Which one is a good one?

Sayo: It's not that... It's just that its ability to defend might give me peace of mind.
Lisa: Ahhaha☆ What a Sayo thing to say~.

Ako: Done! Is everyone ready?
Yukina: Yes.
Lisa: Hm~, I'm kinda nervous!

Sayo: Understood.
Ako: Okay! Let's play some NFO!
