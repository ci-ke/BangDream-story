event38-03 Chapter 2: Try it Out! Tennis

Rinko gives tennis a try. She teams up with Sayo and practices against Misaki.

(Character: Kanon Matsubara, Misaki Okusawa, Eve Wakamiya, Sayo Hikawa, Rinko Shirokane)

[Hanasakigawa Girls' Academy - Club Gathering]

Eve: Oh... That's too bad. I was really looking forward to having you join our club...
Rinko: I'm... sorry...
Kanon: No, we're sorry for asking you so suddenly.
Kanon: You don't have to think about joining or not. As long as you enjoyed yourself, even if it was just a little, we'd be happy to have you join us again.
Rinko: Y-yes... I-I mean, no... I mean... I-I'm... sorry...
Kanon & Eve: ...
Sayo: Shirokane-san, this is only the first club you've talked to, so why not wait to give your reply?
Rinko: Yeah, that's... probably for the best... but what do you mean... 'only the first'...?
Sayo: You've only been to the Tea Ceremony club so far, correct? You shouldn't just stop at that. What about trying out a sports club next?
Rinko: S-sports...? N-no... I-I couldn't possibly...
Sayo: You'll be fine. I'll go with you to the next one as well... Thing is, I have no idea which sports club we should talk to.
Kanon: Sports club, huh? I think Misaki-chan is a member of the Tennis Club... Should I go ask her about it?
Sayo: That'd be great. Do you think you can come with us? Of course, I'll explain the situation... Could you help us?
Kanon: Of course! I'd love to.
Sayo: Thank you. Well, let's head over.
Rinko: Huh...? Uhm, Hikawa-sa-...
Rinko: ... Too late... Wh-what should I do...? A sports club...? There's no way I...
Rinko: (Hikawa-san seems more... forceful today... than usual... Or am I just... imagining things...?)
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Hanasakigawa Girls' Academy - Tennis Court]

Misaki: ... And that's all the exercises we do before practice. Pretty par for the course, really. Not much special going on here.
Sayo: Yes, it felt like your standard warm-up.
Misaki: Sometimes we run a few laps, but looks like we're skipping that today. Uhm... You doing okay, Rinko-senpai?
Rinko: ...! Y-yes, I'm fine...
Misaki: O-okay, just don't push yourself too hard... How about we do some light hitting?
Rinko: H-huh...?! Y-you mean like... back and forth?! Do you think... I'll be able to do that...?
Misaki: Remember how I taught you to swing and hold the racket? We'll just be practicing those two things. And even if you mess up, it's fine. This is just a casual lesson.
Sayo: Thank you again for agreeing to this.
Misaki: Alright. Since both of you are new to tennis, you two will both defend on that side. That way, you'll only have to worry about your half of the court, and you don't need to move as much.
Sayo: In other words... it'll be myself and Shirokane-san versus you, correct?
Misaki: To put it simply, yeah.
Misaki: Rinko-senpai, you said you're not very athletic, so you stand to the front. Then, even if you miss, Sayo-senpai can give you a hand. Just concentrate on hitting the ball when you can.
Rinko: ... O-okay... Hikawa-san... I'm sorry... I'm sure having me on your team... will make things a lot harder for you...
Sayo: Don't worry about it. Everyone has their strengths and weaknesses. Let's just do our best.
Rinko: Y-yes!
Rinko: (Just as I thought... Today, Hikawa-san is... looking out for me.. more than usual...)
Rinko: (But... this is so sudden... Why...?)

Rinko: Ah... The b-ball... What should I do...? I have to hit it...
Sayo: I got it.
Misaki: Whoa!
Misaki: Wh-what was that?! That speed! I didn't even see it coming.
Misaki: Wait a minute. No need to get so worked up. I'm up against beginners, so that was just dumb luck. Yeah, that's it.
Sayo: Okusawa-san, we're ready when you are!
Misaki: R-right! ... We're here today for Rinko-senpai, so I should try hitting the ball in her direction... Alright. Rinko-senpai! Here it... comes!

Misaki: Nothing against Rinko-senpai, but what a relief~. I really am up against amateurs...

Misaki: Uwah! Huh?! She got to the ball?! But she was on the other side of the court!
Misaki: And that speed again! So last time wasn't a fluke after all!
Sayo: Shirokane-san, I've got your back, so relax. Just try to hit any balls headed your way.
Rinko: ... O-okay...
Misaki: ... They're supposed to be doing this for Shirokane-san... Right?
