event38-02 Chapter 1: Try it Out! Tea Ceremony

Rinko goes to see what the tea ceremony club is like. What happens when she tries following along with the other members...?

(Character: Kanon Matsubara, Eve Wakamiya, Sayo Hikawa, Rinko Shirokane)

[Hanasakigawa Girls' Academy - Tea Room]

Tea Ceremony Club Advisor: ... And that's the general flow of a tea ceremony. Usually, only the tea master would make the tea, but since we have guests, why don't we all give it a shot?
Rinko: I... didn't realize we would be... doing more than just watching... Uhm... I-I've never made tea like this before... I don't think I'll be able... to do it right... Maybe I shouldn't...
Kanon: Don't be so nervous, Rinko-chan. I'll be doing a demonstration first, so just watch what I do and copy that the best you can.
Rinko: ... Just copy what you do...? I-is that really... okay?
Eve: Of course. Today is about learning the joys of tea ceremony, not the detailed process.
Eve: We want to show our guests a good time during the short period we have together. That hospitality is what tea ceremony is all about... Right, Kanon-san?
Kanon: Yes. I couldn't have said it better myself. Don't worry about the complicated stuff, we just want you to have fun.
Sayo: But... Actually, never mind. I'm a guest here. It'd be rude to refuse any further. Matsubara-san, Wakamiya-san, thank you for this opportunity.
Kanon: Thank you. I'll do my best to be a good example for you two.
Eve: And I'll be making tea alongside Kanon-san! We'll all be here to help each other out.
Rinko: I-If that's the case... then... I will also... do my best...
Kanon: That's all we ask. Okay, I'll explain as Eve-chan and I make the tea.
Kanon: First, take the hot water from the kettle and pour it into the tea bowl to heat it up. Once warm, we dispose of the water like so...
(Black cut)
Kanon: ... And that's how you make the tea. I don't think it's as complicated as people usually think, but... do you think you can do it?
Sayo: Yes. Seems doable to me. How about you, Shirokane-san...? Hm? Shirokane-san?
Rinko: ... Huh? Ah, I-I'm sorry! ... I just got... so caught up in the moment...
Sayo: No, it's fine. No need to apologize.
Sayo: (What concentration... She barely even blinked while watching them.)
Kanon: Now it's your turn. Take it slow, and if you're not sure about something, please feel free to ask. Relax and take your time.
Rinko: O-okay...
Sayo: Thank you for the lesson. I'll do my best.
Rinko: Uhm... Y-yes, I will... too.
Eve: Great! I can't wait to see how you do.
Rinko: ... First we need to... warm up the tea bowl... Slowly, slowly... Now that it's warm... slowly add the matcha powder...
Rinko: Next comes... the water... Scoop it with the ladle... As for the amount... I'm pretty sure... it was about... one-fourth...
Sayo: First, we warm the tea bowl... I wonder if this amount of matcha works... Now that I'm doing it myself, I'm not so sure...
Rinko: ...
Rinko: (Matsubara-san... held the tea whisk with all five fingers... Then she bent her wrist slightly... but held it firm...)
Sayo: (... Shirokane-san's movements are so fluid. I need to be just as precise... Maybe about this much...?)
Sayo & Rinko: ...
Kanon: I knew you two would be good at this. After only seeing us do it once, you have a handle on the most important points.
Eve: Yes, you both are amazing.
Kanon: (Rinko-chan's speed and the amount of force she applied were spot on. Even her finger placement was right... She really paid attention before.)
Kanon: Great job. Watching you two was a pleasure.
Tea Ceremony Club Advisor: You both managed to remain calm and collected while making the tea. Especially you, Shirokane-san. Splendid job.
Rinko: ... Th-thank you very much...!
Sayo: I agree with everyone. You did great. There were actually times when I looked over at you for reference.
Rinko: That's not... Even you, Hikawa-san...? All I did was... copy Matsubara-san... I'm just glad I did it right...
Kanon: Fufu. You don't have to be so modest, Rinko-chan. All of those compliments just go to show how well you did.
Tea Ceremony Club Advisor: She's right. You even took note of the differences between Matsubara-san and Wakamiya-san's tea-making, such as how much pressure they apply.
Eve: What?! You noticed something that specific?!
Kanon: Have you maybe done tea ceremony before this?
Rinko: N-no... This is... my first time...
Kanon: So you really did learn all that from watching me and Eve-chan? That's incredible!
Eve: Yeah! So impressive! You should join the Tea Ceremony Club! I'm sure you'd make a great host or tea master in no time!
Eve: The host leads the guests through the ceremony while the tea master makes the tea! They're both super important and being selected for the role is a big deal!
Rinko: L-lead... guests...?
Rinko: (What should I do...? This might be my chance... to change... Plus... if it's the Tea Ceremony Club... Matsubara-san and Wakamiya-san... will also be there...)
Rinko: U-uhm... I-I-...!
Tea Ceremony Club Member A: Hello~... Huh? Are these two... new members?!
Tea Ceremony Club Member B: What?! Really?! That's great~! We don't have very many members. This is perfect!
Kanon: W-wait, everyone... Rinko-chan's just here to observe...
Tea Ceremony Club Member C: Is that so~? It's been a while since anyone's shown any interest in our club! We're always looking for members, and we'd love to have you! Please join our club!
Tea Ceremony Club Member A: How about we welcome you to our club with a tea party? I brought some delicious treats with me today!
