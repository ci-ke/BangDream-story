event38-01 Opening: Do I Have to Change?

Rinko wonders whether it's okay to be bad at trying new things. She consults Ako, and...

(Character: Eve Wakamiya, Sayo Hikawa, Ako Udagawa, Rinko Shirokane)

[After Practice]

[Studio]

Ako: Hey, Rin-rin, did you hear about that new game coming out?
Rinko: Uhm... The one being released next month...? I did read a few... reviews online...
Ako: That's the one! I have a lot of friends who play that series, so I was wondering what you thought of it.
Rinko: I-I... don't really... have any interest...
Ako: Really? Now that I think about it, you've told me before that what we play now has been your main game for a while now.
Rinko: That's... right... Finding new friends and stuff... is hard... when starting a new game... If it's single-player... maybe it'd be okay... but...
Rinko: ...
Rinko: ... Do you think... I have to change the way I am...?
Ako: What do you mean, 'the way I am'?
Rinko: Ever since... I was little... I've never... been very good at... trying new things... I'm terrible with... anything unfamiliar...
Rinko: ... Even with piano... I never wanted to do competitions... Th-there's always... a lot of people... and I never know what to do... It's scary...
Rinko: So I always... avoided doing them... But...
Ako: Hmm... You're cooler than you give yourself credit for, Rin-rin. You just have to go about things with a bigger bang!
Rinko: B-bang...?
Ako: Yeah, for example, uhm... Oh! How about starting a part-time job? My sister is always telling me how they can help you discover new things!
Rinko: But... uhm... Going r-right into a part-time job...means... I'd have to do things like interact with customers...
Ako: Ah, you're right. Jumping into a job where you deal with people might be a bit hard.
Ako: Hmm~, how about joining a club? No customers, and most clubs let you sit in on meetings before officially joining, so if you end up not liking it, no biggie!
Rinko: Sitting in on a club meeting...? I don't know... Am I really-...
Sayo: Are you two ready to go? The next band's reservation is coming up. Take care of whatever you need to so we can leave.
