event13-03 Chapter 2: Tempestuous Theater

The other four members of Pastel＊Palettes have come to observe the rehearsal and seem quite cheerful. How will Chisato react...?

(Character: Aya Maruyama, Hina Hikawa, Chisato Shirasagi, Maya Yamato, Eve Wakamiya)

[Theater Area]

Chisato: "Cough, cough... I know there's not much time left for me..."
Bell's Actress: "Marie, just a little longer. I'll figure something out... You have to hang on."
Chisato: "I don't want to cause you any more trouble, Bell..."
Chisato: "... Wait, look at this newspaper... They're holding an audition for new actresses! The winner gets to appear in a show, and there's prize money..."
Bell's Actress: "This... This is it! Marie, I'll do it! I'll audition, and I'll win, just for you!"
Chisato: "Bell..."
Miyagawa: Stop!! You're killing me here, Shirasagi-kun! What were you even trying to do with that last line? Do you know the word "passion" by any chance?!
Chisato: What do you mean...? What was I doing?
Chisato: (I'm doing my best!)
Miyagawa: Your acting is one-dimensional. It's like an attempt at the bare minimum. Did you think for a second what your character might be feeling?
Chisato: ...
Miyagawa: You're just doing what the words on that paper tell you to. If you can't think for yourself, you can just go home!
Aya: (H-he's so scary... Too scary!)
Eve: (Even when I was modeling, no one was ever this mean...)
Maya: (The pros sure are strict...)
Hina: (They said working with him is really stressful, but this is much more intense than I imagined it would be...)
Chisato: I don't mean to be rude, but could you tell me exactly what I'm doing wrong? It's hard to fix the problem when I'm not sure what it is...
Miyagawa: That's your job, not mine. There's no point if I'm doing your work for you. We'll have a break soon, so take that time to use your brain a little bit.
Chisato: Y-yes sir...
(Black cut)

[Intermission]

Aya: Chisato-chan, over here!
Chisato: ... Ah, Aya-chan. You guys came.
Aya: We wanted to see your rehearsal. Things are a bit more intense than I thought they would be, though...
Eve: He scares me... I felt like he was getting mad at me too...
Maya: Yeah... If I was in your shoes, I think the pressure to do well would make me perform a lot worse...
Aya: Are you okay, Chisato-chan? It doesn't bother you?
Chisato: No, I'm alright. I knew he would be like this when I took the job.
Aya: I see. Well, if it doesn't bother you, then it's fine, I guess... It might be tough, but I know that you can do it, Chisato-chan! Just keep doing your best!
Aya: You've been doing this for a long time. You're a pro too, so I'm sure you can get through this!
Maya: That's right. I'm sure that he's being really hard on you because he knows that you're so talented. Try not to let it get to you.
Eve: I think so too! Show them your fighting spirit!
Chisato: ... Thanks, guys...
Chisato: (I'm having a hard time because I have no idea what he's asking me to do! It's pathetic that I can't live up to these expectations...)
Hina: Hmm... Uhm, Chisato-chan. You're not acting like your normal self. Is everything okay? Are you feeling sick?
Eve: You do seem a bit unwell. Are you sure there's nothing wrong?
Aya: If you're not feeling well, I have medicine. There's headache medicine, stomach medicine...
Chisato: ... Don't worry about it. I'll be alright.
Hina: Really? Well, if you say so. Looks like you have a hard time at work sometimes too, huh?
Hina: That's so weird~. I thought that you could do anything.
Chisato: ...
Chisato: (You all have no idea how I'm feeling...)
Chisato: ... Don't... I can... anything...
Hina: Huh? What'd you say?
Chisato: Don't just assume I can do anything!! Get outta here!!
Aya: Huh...?
Maya: Chisato-san...?!
Eve: H-hold on! What happened? This is so unlike you.
Aya: D-did we say something wrong? We're sorry if we did...
Chisato: Just leave... Please...
Hina: Guys, you heard her. Why don't we head back?
Chisato: ...
(Black cut)
(Background change)

Eve: Oh, Chisato-san. I wonder what's wrong... That was so out of character for her…
Maya: She didn't want to talk to us at all...
Eve: I don't understand why she got so upset…
Hina: Me neither. Hmm... Was it something I said?
