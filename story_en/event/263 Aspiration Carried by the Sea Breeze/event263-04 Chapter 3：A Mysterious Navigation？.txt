event263-04 Chapter 3: A Mysterious Navigation?

Tsukushi leads everyone to the perfect spot for a beach picnic.

(Character: Tae Hanazono, Tsugumi Hazawa, Kanon Matsubara, Eve Wakamiya, Tsukushi Futaba)

[Beach]

Tsukushi: Hmm, according to the app, it should be around here...
Eve: You got this, Tsukushi-san!
Tsukushi: ...Ah! That beach over there! I’m so glad we made it!
Tae: Wow, there’s barely anyone here compared to earlier. I can’t believe you found a place like this, Tsukushi.

Kanon: You worked so hard on this. Thank you, Tsukushi-chan.

Eve: Then let’s go borrow one! Beach umbrellas just scream summer to me!
