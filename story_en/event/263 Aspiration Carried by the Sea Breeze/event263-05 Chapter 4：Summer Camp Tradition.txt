event263-05 Chapter 4: Summer Camp Tradition

After hearing about summer training camp from her former tea ceremony club, Eve proposes an idea—but...

(Character: Tae Hanazono, Tsugumi Hazawa, Kanon Matsubara, Eve Wakamiya, Tsukushi Futaba)

[Hanasakigawa Girls' Academy - Tea Room]

Eve: Excuse me! I’m looking forward to today as well!
Tea Ceremony Club Member B: You’ve got so much energy, even in the summer, Eve-chan. Your master’s already wilting over there.
Kanon: It’s just... really hot today...
Tea Ceremony Club Member B: Yeah, but what are you going to do when summer camp rolls around?
Eve: Summer camp...? The tea ceremony club has training camps too?
Tea Ceremony Club Member B: Yup. Usually we go somewhere cooler and invite a special instructor to learn advanced techniques.
Tea Ceremony Club Member A: Last year we did nodate, an outdoor tea ceremony. It was hot, and the cicadas were so loud.
Kanon: Fufu, I remember. Since kimono were too hot, we got to wear light yukata.
Eve: That sounds wonderful! I’d love to do a nodate by the sea!
Tea Ceremony Club Member A: By the sea...?
Tea Ceremony Club Member B: We wouldn't normally do it by the sea~
Eve: O-Oh, I see... I’m sorry...
Kanon: ...Why do you want to do nodate by the sea, Eve-chan?
Eve: Well, my senpai and the club advisor taught me that the essence of nodate is to enjoy tea while taking in the scenery and refreshing yourself.
Eve: From my own experience, I think nodate is a wonderful tradition that lets you feel the changing of the seasons with your whole body.
Kanon: ...!
Kanon: Yeah, now that you mention it, when we have tea in the courtyard sometimes, everyone talks about the flowers nearby or the temperature.
Tea Ceremony Club Member A: We were all experiencing the changing seasons together... Maybe a nodate at the beach isn’t such a bad idea?
Tea Ceremony Club Member B: Nah, there’s no precedent. It’d be hard to pull off.
Eve: I see... But I’m still looking forward to training with everyone at summer camp!
Kanon: (Eve-chan is so genuine. She’s not bound by assumptions and sees things from so many different angles...)
Kanon: (She’s really a wonderful person.)
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Beach]

Tsugumi: ...Kanon-san...?
Kanon: Hmm... Huh? Did I fall asleep...?
Tsugumi: Yes. Did you stay up late last night?
Kanon: Yeah, I might’ve gone a little overboard getting everything ready... Yawn... Wait, where is everyone?
Tsugumi: They said they were going to shower before starting the picnic.
Kanon: I see, sorry.
Kanon: (It’s been a while since I remembered that talk about nodate... In the end, we went to the usual place that year...)
Tae: We’re back~
Tsugumi: Welcome back! Feel refreshed?
Eve: Yes! Now then, let’s begin the beach picnic!
Kanon: Okay. So what I brought is... ta-da~! Detox water, wrap sandwiches... and a lemon peel pound cake!
Tsukushi: Wow, did you make all of that? It looks so good I thought it was store-bought...!
Kanon: Well, my mom actually helped me prepare it back home. What about you, Tsukushi-chan?
Tsukushi: I baked coconut muffins!
Tsugumi: Ooh, nice. You always make such tasty treats. I’m looking forward to them.
Eve: Tae-san, are those tiny hamburg steaks in your container?
Tae: Yup! And I brought some buns too, so if you put them together... Mini hamburgers!
Kanon: So cute~! That’s so picnic-y, it’s getting me excited!
Tsugumi: I brought coffee and black tea. The tea is a tropical flavored blend.
Tsukushi: Wow, it smells amazing! Let’s see... mango, pineapple... and maybe hibiscus?
Tsugumi: Impressive, Tsukushi-chan! You really know your stuff!
Tsukushi: Hehe, I’ve been studying a lot about sweets and drinks since I started working at Hazawa Coffee.
Eve: That’s so Tsukushi-san—banri ikku!
Tsukushi: Ba... what? What does that mean?
Kanon: I think it means something like “a hard worker”? If so, then Eve-chan is banri ikku too.
Kanon: You’re juggling three clubs and still work so hard at tea ceremony... I think you’re incredible.
Eve: Kanon-san...! Thank you so much!
Tsugumi: Hehe... Hey, Eve-chan. Isn’t it time to bring that out?
Eve: Y-Yes, I think so!
Kanon: Bring what out?
Eve: Actually, today I have...
Eve: A special surprise for you, Kanon-san!
(Black cut)
(Background change)
