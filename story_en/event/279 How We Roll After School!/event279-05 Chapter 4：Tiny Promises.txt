event279-05 Chapter 4: Tiny Promises

While browsing some quirky items at the shopping center, the five of them make a promise together.

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa)

[Shopping Center in Tokyo]

Himari: Wow~, this necklace is super cute!
Tomoe: Himari, don’t you already have something like that?
Himari: Ugh... I haven’t even said I want to buy it yet! No need to shut me down already~!
Tomoe: Ahaha, my bad, my bad.
Tsugumi: Whoa, this bracelet is beautiful...!
Ran: You’re right. That delicate design would really suit you, Tsugumi.
Tsugumi: Eh, y-you think so...? Isn’t it a little too mature for me...?
Moca: You’re good, you’re good~. Tsugu’s a seasoned student council president now~.
Tomoe: Hey, Ran! This one would look great on you!
Ran: I’m not wearing flashy sunglasses like that.
Himari: But don’t you want some for summer? Let’s all get matching ones and hit the beach together!
Moca: There it is~ Hii-chan’s yearly tradition.
Tsugumi: Once summer gets close, Himari-chan always invites us to the beach.
Ran: It’s still winter though...
Himari: Yeah, but making little plans like this ahead of time is exciting, right?
Tomoe: You’ve got a point. I don’t mind the beach, and sure, let’s go with sunglasses next summer!
Himari: Yay~! These colored sunglasses are super trendy lately, and...
Moca: Eh~, the beach is a done deal already~?
Ran: I mean, isn’t it way too early to be picking them out right now?
Tsugumi: Fufufu... Oh, Himari-chan. Is that shop the one from the video you shared?
Himari: Oh yeah, it’s the candied fruit place! It’s super popular right now! Let’s go check it out!
(Black cut)
Tsugumi: Strawberries, grapes, mandarin oranges, pineapples... They all look so shiny and delicious!
Tomoe: Do they taste like the candied apples from festivals?
Moca: Probably~? I think I’ll go for the grape one~.
Himari: Each skewer has a bunch on it. Want to get a couple and share with everyone?
Ran: Let’s do that. I was just thinking I wouldn’t be able to finish a whole one myself.
Himari: Alright, time to take a poll! Who wants strawberry? Hands up—
(Black cut)
Tsugumi: Thanks for waiting, I got them!
Himari: Strawberry candy and grape candy! Hey, Moca! Don’t start eating them already!
Tomoe: Yeah, come on. We gotta take photos first, obviously.
Moca: Eh~? Tomo-chin, did you switch sides too~?
Tomoe: I didn’t expect to get festival vibes in a place like this, y’know.
Ran: There it is—Tomoe’s festival mode.
Tomoe: ...Alright, got the shot.
Himari: Hey, Tomoe! At least say something before you take the picture!
Tomoe: But it feels more natural this way, right?
Himari: This kind of thing looks super stylish if you just get a shot of our hands like this. Come on, Ran, you guys put your hands in too.
Tsugumi: Uh... like this?
Ran: You and Tsugumi are holding candy, but what about us?
Moca: Peace signs, let’s go—peace~.
Tomoe: Like this? ...Oh, yeah, I’ve seen pics like this before. Alright, here goes~.
Moca: Grape candy, here I come~ ...Mmm~, sweet~.
Himari: Ah~, you’re eating two at once!?
Tsugumi: Here, Ran-chan, strawberry candy for you!
Ran: Thanks. ...Mmm, it’s good.
Tomoe: So Himari, how’s the long-awaited candied fruit?
Himari: It’s super tasty! Want a bite, Tomoe?
Moca: Hii-chan, Tomo-chin’s waiting on a food review~.
Tomoe: Oh, Moca, you caught on quick.
Himari: Not a chance! I know you two are just gonna tease me!
Tomoe: Hahaha! Then maybe Ran can do the food review... Hm? What’re you looking at, Ran?
Ran: Eh? Oh, that sign. There’s an aquarium and planetarium here too.
Tomoe: Oh yeah, that was in the video Himari sent~. You wanna check it out?
Ran: We haven’t gone to a planetarium together yet, so it sounds nice... but maybe another time.
Tomoe: That so? Then let’s make it another one of our little promises!
Ran: Yeah.
Ran: Actually, isn’t it getting kinda late? If we don’t hurry, we’ll miss the sunset.
Tomoe: Eh? ...Seriously!? Everyone, finish up and let’s head to the observatory!
Himari: What!? It’s that late already!? I haven’t even gone to all the shops I wanted yet!
Moca: We can come back later~.
Tsugumi: Tomoe-chan, you haven’t eaten yet, right!? Here, last strawberry!
Tomoe: Oh, thanks Tsugu. Nom... Mmm! So good!
Ran: The observatory—we’re taking that elevator over there, right?
Tsugumi: Yeah, let’s hurry!
Himari: Come on~, why are we always like this~!?
Tomoe: Ahaha, come on—just run already!
