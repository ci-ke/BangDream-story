event294-04 Chapter 3: The Lame “Me”

Afterglow gathers on the rooftop. Tsugumi speaks to the worn-out Tomoe...

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa)

[Recess]

Tomoe: —and yeah, I was too out of it to even bring up the whole MC thing.
Himari: I get that... I’m the same. If I fought with my sister, I probably couldn’t talk to her normally for a while either.
Tsugumi: That’s the hard part about family... you have to see each other every day.
Moca: But you and Ako-chin aren’t avoiding each other, right~?
Ran: That’s pretty amazing... I don’t think I could sit down to eat together if it were me...
Tomoe: Still, in the end, I made Ako look like that...
Tsugumi: Look like what?
Tomoe: How do I put it... Back then, when Ako called me “cool,” her eyes would sparkle.
Tomoe: Just seeing that always gave me strength.
Tomoe: ...but today, when I had my shirt buttoned wrong, she tried to say it again—called me “cool” while clearly trying to comfort me.
Tomoe: It sounded more like, “It’s okay, you’re still kind of cool,” and I thought... maybe it’s become just something she has to say now, not something she feels.
Tomoe: ...Could I be any lamer?
All: ......
Tsugumi: You’re cool, Tomoe-chan.
Tomoe: Eh...
Tsugumi: You’ve always been confident, even since we were kids. And when you decide to do something, you see it through to the end—that’s cool.
Tsugumi: The way you can say what you love without hesitation, the way you get your courage up to take action for the band—that’s all super cool.
Himari: Yeah, exactly! Even when you waver, once you’ve made up your mind, you always follow through!
Ran: Yeah. I think that’s why we all end up relying on you, deep down.
Moca: As expected from someone whose catchphrase is “oomph”—when it counts, she gets things done, our very own Udagawa~.
Tomoe: H-hey, what’s with you guys all saying this stuff...! I’m not cool at all right now...
Moca: Beep beep! Talking bad about yourself—yellow card~.
Tomoe: E-eh...?
Moca: Now, now, listen up, Udagawa-kun.
Moca: Once upon a time, there was a super beautiful girl who struggled because she couldn’t live up to her own idea of “cool.” One day, a hero appeared before her.
Moca: The hero said, “Here, have some croquette and bread. Put them together and it’ll taste amazing...!”
Moca: Bathed in the setting sun, that hero looked cooler and more dazzling than anyone else~ The end~.
Tomoe: Wait, that’s... that time...
Moca: I’ve always loved that hero~. She’s so cool~.
Himari: Wait... is that story about you and Tomoe?
Ran: Pretty sure it is.
Tomoe: ...I see... Maybe I actually did have some cool moments after all.
Tsugumi: Way more than just once in a while. You're always cool, Tomoe-chan!
Tomoe: T-Tsugu... you’re being awfully direct today...
Himari: Fufu, because right now, Tomoe needs words that hit straight-on!
Tomoe: Haha... yeah, maybe you’re right. Guess if I could just fix this gloomy side, I’d be perfect, huh.
Himari: But wasn’t this time kind of unavoidable?
Ran: Yeah. It’s just how big this was for you.
Moca: Actually, why can’t you be gloomy sometimes~?
Tomoe: It's not that it's bad... I just can't stand how lame I feel when I do...
Moca: I see~ but I think that’s part of what makes Tomo-chin who she is, too~.
Moca: See, you might look like a bold and scary senior at first glance, but deep down, she’s got a heart of glass~.
Tomoe: ...Well, that’s not exactly wrong...
Moca: But that’s why you’re so kind, Tomo-chin. You think things through carefully and always come to your own answer.
Tsugumi: Yeah... I think so too. That’s the root of your kindness, Tomoe-chan.
Tomoe: You think so...? Maybe trying to force myself to change isn't the right move after all...
Himari: That’s right. I told you before, didn’t I? You don’t have to change yourself.
Himari: The cool Tomoe and the gloomy Tomoe—they’re both you.
Tomoe: ......
Tomoe: Yeah, you’re right. They’re both... precious parts of me.
Tomoe: But thinking everything’s a failure just because of one thing, and brooding over it... Yeah, that’s not right. I want to take my time and work on changing that.
Tomoe: I doubt I can change overnight... but I’ll try, little by little.
Moca: You're looking good~ That’s our hero.
Tomoe: Hey, you’re teasing me now, aren’t you?
All: Ahahaha...!
