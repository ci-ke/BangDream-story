event294-06 Chapter 5: As “Tomoe Udagawa”

With the sunset glowing over the ocean, Tomoe finally opens up about her feelings.

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa)

Tomoe: Whoa~~... the sea and the sunset are so beautiful...!
Tsugumi: Haa... haa... They really are...! The wind’s a bit chilly, but it feels nice...!
Himari: Haa... haa~... But seriously, did we have to run all the way from the station...!?
Ran: Yeah... I think I took this more seriously than PE class...
Moca: Guess I’ll have to eat fifty pieces of bread after this~...
Tomoe: Ahaha... sorry. I just didn’t want to miss the sunset.
Himari: Well, that makes sense. So? How’s the ocean you wanted to see?
Tomoe: Man~... I just feel so small right now.
Tsugumi: Tomoe-chan, did something good happen?
Ran: I kinda get that. You’ve got that look on your face today.
Tomoe: Yeah... actually, I finally talked with Ako yesterday.
Tomoe: She told me what she really meant with that MC... She’s grown so much.
Tomoe: She chose her words carefully so I wouldn’t worry. They were honest, but kind.
Tomoe: That’s the kind of thing I was always the one doing for Ako... But it felt like we’d switched places—as if she were the big sister.
Moca: Did that... hurt?
Tomoe: Hmm... I guess I just thought, “What am I even doing?”
Tomoe: Ako’s charging ahead full speed, and all I was hung up on was being the “cool big sister.” It just felt... kinda small.
Ran: That whole “being the cool sister” thing... I always wondered why you cared about it so much.
Tomoe: Huh? You did?
Ran: Yeah... well, I figured it wasn’t my place to ask, so I kept quiet. I like to think I understand how much resolve Tomoe had.
Himari: That’s true...
Himari: It’s because of Ako-chan that you’ve pushed yourself so hard—and the same goes the other way too... That’s just who you and Ako-chan are.
Tomoe: Yeah. But, I think I’ve been relying on that.
Tomoe: Ever since we were little... whenever Ako—or anyone—called me “cool,” it made me feel like I could be an invincible hero.
Tomoe: Even the things that scared me... if I was doing it “for someone,” that gave me the courage to take the first step.
Tomoe: But I never tried going anywhere without someone waiting there. Meanwhile, Ako kept challenging herself in a much bigger world.
Tsugumi: I see...
Tsugumi: You know, Tomoe-chan—you really are cool.
Tomoe: Huh? But I was just talking about how Ako’s the cool one—
Tsugumi: Of course Ako-chan’s cool too. But I’ve been thinking about your feelings ever since that live.
Tsugumi: You’ve been Ako-chan’s big sister since before we even met, right?
Tsugumi: I bet you’ve worked really hard behind the scenes to be that sister.
Tsugumi: Maybe the reason I can face my own weakness instead of running from it... is because there’s someone important I want to be strong for.
Tomoe: Tsugu...
Moca: Yeah, that makes sense~. “Being someone’s Tomo-chin” is kinda the core of who you are.
Moca: Whether it's because you're Ako-chin's big sister, or because you want to protect what's special about Afterglow... Tomo-chin just naturally thinks that way, y'know~.
Tsugumi: Yeah. But I think the reason you can think that way so naturally is because you’ve been trying so hard ever since you were little.
Tsugumi: So maybe now, you don’t have to keep pushing yourself to be “Tomoe-chan for someone else” all the time.
Tsugumi: Because Tomoe-chan is Tomoe-chan.
Tomoe: ――......
Tsugumi: You know, maybe coming to the beach today, and saying you “never went anywhere without someone waiting” ...maybe that shows how you’re feeling now.
Tsugumi: That maybe you do want to try going somewhere, even if no one’s there waiting.
Tomoe: Somewhere... no one’s waiting...
Tomoe: (...I don’t really get games, but maybe when Ako talked about the map expanding, this is what she meant.)
Tomoe: (What kind of world lies beyond this ocean? How far can I go?)
Tomoe: (If I could just start walking toward wherever my heart leads––)
Tomoe: ...Yeah.
Tomoe: From here on out, I want to step into a world I’ve never known. Even if it's scary going alone, I want to become someone who can be strong—for myself.
Tsugumi: Yeah...! But remember, you’re not alone, Tomoe-chan.
Moca: That’s right~ You’ve got the power of friendship backing you up~.
Tomoe: Haha, thanks.
Tomoe: ...Alright!
Himari: Eh, why are you taking off your shoes...!? Don’t tell me you’re going into the water...!?
Tomoe: Just my feet! I’ll be right back!
Himari: Eh~... it’s still gotta be freezing though...
Tsugumi: Fufufu, but it’s been getting warmer lately.
Moca: And stopping her now would totally ruin the moment~.
Moca: Man, Tomo-chin’s silhouette against the sunset looks so cool~.
Ran: ...Yeah. Not bad at all.
Tomoe: (...I wonder if the water’s cold.)
Tomoe: (Maybe I’ll step on something sharp and it’ll hurt.)
Tomoe: (But this step I’m taking today—it’s not the same as yesterday’s me!)
Tomoe: —That’s cold~~~~!! Ahahaha!!
Himari: Fufufu, I knew that’d happen!
