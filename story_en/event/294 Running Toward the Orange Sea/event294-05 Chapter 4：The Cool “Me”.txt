event294-05 Chapter 4: The Cool “Me”

Returning home, Tomoe reflects on what she’s seen through her talk with Ako.

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa, Ako Udagawa)

Tomoe: I’m home~...
Tomoe: (Whether I’m cool or unsure, every part of me is still me. No matter what kind of person I am, I’ll always be Ako’s big sister.)
Tomoe: (Then, what kind of sister do I want to be from now on? If I can figure that out, maybe I’ll finally be able to face Ako head-on...)
Ako: ...Sis.
Tomoe: Wah! A-Ako...
Ako: Sorry, I didn’t mean to scare you.
Tomoe: No, I should be the one apologizing... uh, um...
Tomoe: (I have to say something... I can’t let Ako get hurt like this again—)
Ako: Sis, I will always, always love you.
Tomoe: Huh...?
Tomoe: (Ako... her hands are trembling.)
Ako: It’s because you’re the coolest person I know, sis. That’s why I thought I couldn’t just “admire” you anymore.
Ako: ‘Cause, you know... I have my own “cool.” My own place I want to reach... So, to keep depending on you while calling it admiration just didn’t feel right.
Tomoe: (Ah... those words—they’re for me.)
Tomoe: (It’s just like what I always did for Ako. So she wouldn’t be sad, so she could smile without worry—and still, I tried to stay true to myself.)
Tomoe: (Words that sit right between honesty and kindness.)
Tomoe: (When did she become able to do that...? She used to cry so easily, always calling for me. Now she’s so much braver and kinder than I am.)
Ako: ...S-sis...?
Tomoe: Ah, no... I was just thinking how cool you are, Ako.
Tomoe: Thanks for telling me that. ...I love you too, Ako.
Ako: Yeah... yeah...!
Tomoe: Ako. Is drumming... and being in the band still fun?
Ako: Yeah, it’s super fun.
Ako: Ever since you taught me drums, and since I joined Roselia... I’ve had hard times and painful ones too.
Ako: But because of all that, I found my “cool,” and I’ve gotten to see so many new places I’d never imagined before...!
Tomoe: Yeah. I never imagined you’d end up going pro, honestly.
Ako: Yeah... If I told the old me who just started drumming, she’d never believe it.
Ako: But I’m still not there yet.
Ako: I wanna become way, way cooler than I am now—cool enough to proudly say that me and Roselia are the best in the world...!!
Tomoe: ...The best in the world, huh... Hearing it out loud, it’s so big I can’t even picture it.
Ako: Yeah... I don’t think I can really picture it either. But right now, I kinda think that’s okay.
Ako: Um... So, like, when you clear a dungeon, the map expands, and a new dungeon appears... You’ve gotta level up, but you also need new skills and items.
Ako: When I think about what kind of map lies ahead, and what kind of me I might become... I just get super excited!
Ako: It feels like that excitement alone is what keeps me moving forward into new worlds!
Ako: ...I don’t know if I’m explaining it right, but, like... that’s the kind of feeling I mean...!
Tomoe: Yeah... I think I get it, sort of.
Tomoe: (Ako’s still chasing something far beyond, and she’s enjoying every part of that journey.)
Tomoe: (While I was fixated on being a “cool sister,” Ako was already out there fighting for her own version of “cool” in a bigger world.)
Tomoe: (So then... what about me...?)
Tomoe: (...What kind of “cool me” do I want to be?)
(Black cut)
