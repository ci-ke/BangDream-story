event290-01 Opening: Let’s Go! European Park!

With the rare chance of everyone having the day off, Aya suggests they all go out together.

(Character: Aya Maruyama, Hina Hikawa, Chisato Shirasagi, Maya Yamato, Eve Wakamiya)

[Talent Agency]

[Before the Meeting]

Aya: Oh right, the location shoot for “All Fun and Games” this weekend got moved, didn’t it?
Chisato: Yeah, they just said it was due to the other party’s schedule. We should hear more in the meeting later.
Maya: When you suddenly get a whole day off on the weekend, it's hard to decide what to do, right?
Eve: Totally! I haven’t figured out any plans yet either.
Aya: Then, if everyone’s free, how about we all go hang out together?
Eve: Eh, really!?
Maya: I’m all for it. We haven’t had the chance to do something like that in a while.
Chisato: I don’t mind either. Aya-chan, did you have anything in mind?
Aya: I hadn’t thought that far... Oh! Remember when we talked about wanting to go camping as Pastel＊Palettes?
Maya: Camping, huh... I’d like to do it too, but if it’s all five of us, getting the gear ready and reserving a campsite now might be a bit tough.
Aya: Ah, right. We don’t have as much time as when we went with Himari-chan and the others. In that case, maybe something we could do just for a day—
Hina: Good morning~. What were you all talking about?
Aya: Oh, good morning, Hina-chan!
Aya: We were just talking about how the shoot this weekend got moved, so if we all have the day off, maybe we could go somewhere together.
Hina: I see~. Well then, that worked out perfectly!
Hina: Here, these are tickets to European Park. A staff member gave me five of them and said, “Why not go with Pastel＊Palettes?”
Chisato: European Park?
Eve: That’s the theme park modeled after Europe, right?
Hina: Yep, that’s the one. It’s a huge place with attractions and photo spots everywhere. Have you been, Eve-chan?
Eve: No, but I heard about it from a friend in class. When I heard the name “European,” I thought it might help if I was ever feeling homesick for Finland.
Hina: Huh? I saw some photos of the park, but it didn’t really look like Finland~.
Eve: Yes. I looked it up, and it seems the theme is Central Europe, not Finland.
Aya: That makes sense. Finland’s part of Northern Europe, so the vibe must be totally different.
Eve: Yes, the climate and culture are very different. But it still looked like a really fun place, so I’ve always wanted to go!
Hina: Then let’s go there for our day off! The staff said the spring flowers are at their peak right now.
Aya: That sounds amazing! I'm in!
Maya: Me too. And if we need a driver, I’m happy to help.
Eve: Wow! I finally get to ride in Maya-san’s car!
Chisato: Fufu, sounds like our day off is going to be lively..
Aya: It’s been a while since we all got to hang out—I'm really looking forward to this. I’ll make a little itinerary for us!
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[On the Day Off]

[Rental Car – Inside]

Eve: I still can’t believe I get to ride in Maya-san’s car! It feels like a dream!
Hina: Ahaha, Eve-chan, you’ve been hyped since this morning~.
Chisato: Fufu, I never imagined we’d be going out somewhere in a car one of us is driving.
Hina: Yeah. It’s kind of surreal, but fun~.
Maya: Well, it’s not my first time driving people around, but it does feel different—and a lot more nerve-wracking—when I’m driving my own bandmates.
Aya: Even when we went camping the other day, everyone praised your driving. You can be confident!
Maya: Thank you. I’ll stay focused and make sure we all get there safely.
Hina: We’re counting on you~! So, what should we do once we get to European Park?
Chisato: Let’s check Aya-chan’s itinerary.
Eve: I want to visit the fishing pond that’s listed on page 4!
Chisato: You’re interested in fishing, Eve-chan?
Eve: Yes! I recently rewatched an old samurai drama, and the fishing scene with the main character was so cool and calm!
Hina: Oh, that’s neat. I thought this athletic obstacle course looked fun.
Chisato: There’s so much to do, it’s hard to choose. But Aya-chan’s itinerary is so nicely organized—it really helps.
Aya: Ehehe, really? I’m glad I made it~.
Maya: Even just reading it got me excited.
Aya: Honestly, while I was looking things up, I ended up with way too many things I want to do.
Aya: First, I definitely want to take pictures with the flowers, ride the Ferris wheel, and try all the yummy-looking food...
Chisato: Yeah, I wonder how much we can fit into one day.
Hina: Well, it’s our day off, so let’s just each do what we want freely.
Chisato: Yes, I’d be happy to take it easy without worrying too much about sticking to a schedule.
Maya: Ah, look everyone! There’s the sign for European Park!
Aya: You’re right! “European Park ahead”!
Aya: Alright everyone, ready to have some fun~? Let’s make the most of European Park~~~!
Eve & Hina: Yeah~~!!
Chisato: Fufu. Aya-chan, you sound like you’re starting a live show.
