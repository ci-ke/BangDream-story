event290-06 Chapter 5: The Feelings of a Supporter

Learning that an idol live is being held, they make their way to the stage area.

(Character: Aya Maruyama, Hina Hikawa, Chisato Shirasagi, Maya Yamato, Eve Wakamiya)

[European Park - Stage Area]

Hina: Come on, hurry, hurry! Looks like it already started!
Aya: You're right, it’s already pretty lively!
Chisato: Yeah. We shouldn’t draw too much attention, so let’s stay back here to watch.
Eve: So this “Fresh Idol Special Live” means they’re rookie idols, right?
Aya: Yeah. I checked their website earlier—they only debuted about six months ago.
Chisato: But the people cheering up front already have their calls perfectly in sync. They must be really dedicated fans.
Maya: Even though this venue isn’t that easy to get to, that’s some serious passion.
Aya: Fufu, watching this kind of thing gets me all excited!
Aya: You got this~!
Chisato: There are still some rough spots in their performance, but you can really feel their effort.
Eve: I wonder... did we look like that too when we had just debuted?
Maya: Maybe so. It kind of makes me feel ticklish thinking about it.
Aya: Hey, hey! The girl in the center just made eye contact and gave us fan service!
Eve: Really!? I won’t miss it next time!
Maya: Huh? I don’t see Hina-san anywhere...
Chisato: She was just next to me a second ago...
Hina: Sorry, everyone~. They were handing out glow sticks over there, so I grabbed some.
Maya: Oh, these are the kind you crack to make them light up.
Hina: The staff said, “Please join in and cheer them on!”
Chisato: But if we get too excited...
Hina: You're worried we might be recognized as idols, right? But I think we’re okay for now.
Aya: Yeah, I think so too. Right now, everyone here is just cheering for the idols on stage.
Chisato: That’s true... fufu, you’re right.
Eve: Everyone looks like they’re having so much fun cheering. It’s such a heartwarming sight...!
Hina: Then let’s join in and cheer them on too!
Eve: Yes! We may be newcomers to this, but let’s cheer with all our hearts!
Maya: I'm a little nervous... but cheers really do give the performers strength!
Chisato: Exactly. We know that feeling better than anyone—so we have to give it our all.
Chisato: But, let’s not go too overboard, okay?
Aya: Yeah! Let’s make sure they can feel our support loud and clear!
Rookie Idol: Everyone, wave your glow sticks with all your might! Hai! Hai!
Aya: Hai! Hai! Hai! Hai!
Hina: You’re really going for it, Aya-chan! I’m not gonna lose to you!
Hina: Hai! Hai! Hai! Hai!
Eve: Hina-san, you’ve got some sharp moves!
Hina: I am a proud member of the Idol Research Club, after all~!
Chisato: Fufu, guess that means we have to step it up too.
Maya: Right! I’ll do my best too!
All: Hai! Hai! Hai! Hai!
Rookie Idol: Looking good~!!
All: Hai! Hai! Hai! Hai!
Rookie Idol: Come on, let’s keep it going~!!
Aya: (Cheering together with everyone is so much fun...! And the more we cheer, the more the stage shines...)
Rookie Idol: Thank you all for your amazing support~!!
Aya: (I can feel it...! Every cheer, every emotion—it’s all reaching the stage...)
Aya: Idols really are the best...!
(Black cut)
Hina: Sigh~. That was so much fun~!
Eve: Yeah! Waving all those glow sticks and shouting out loud really cleared my head!
Maya: It’s a different kind of fulfillment than what we feel after performing ourselves.
Chisato: When we watched ViVidCanvas’ show, we were their seniors, so we didn’t really cheer from the audience like this. This was really refreshing.
Hina: Oh, look over there! That little kid is dancing to one of their songs.
Maya: Fufu, they’re dancing to their mom’s clapping rhythm.
Hina: Maybe that kid will become an idol too someday~.
Aya: I see...
Chisato: Aya-chan?
Aya: “The story of idols” really is being written in so many different places...!
Aya: Idols all around the world show people their dreams, and those dreams are born in someone’s heart... Uuuh... Idols really are amazing...!
Eve: Aya-san’s getting emotional!
Maya: But she’s right...! This really brought me back to my roots.
Chisato: Yes. I feel like I’ve been reminded once again of the true charm of idols.
