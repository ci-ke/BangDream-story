event290-04 Chapter 3: Indulging in Theme Park Gourmet!

Around lunchtime, they each grab their favorite dishes from the food court to share.

(Character: Aya Maruyama, Hina Hikawa, Chisato Shirasagi, Maya Yamato, Eve Wakamiya)

[European Park - Terrace Area]

Aya: Hey everyone, want to break for lunch? I’m getting pretty hungry.
Maya: Sounds good, I’m in favor too.
Chisato: You added lots of details in the trip guide, but it looks like this whole area is basically a food court. Anyone have something they want to try?

Eve: I agree! And I’d love to try these sausages over here too!
Maya: Being "European Park" and all, they’ve really got dishes from all sorts of countries. With this much variety, it’s hard to decide.

Hina: Yeah! Okay then, let’s meet back here after we’ve all bought our food!

Maya: Ahaha, we ended up with a lot more food than I expected.
