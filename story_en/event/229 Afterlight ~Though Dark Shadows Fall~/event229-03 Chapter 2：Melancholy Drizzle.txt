event229-03 Chapter 2: Melancholy Drizzle

Tsugumi is devastated. She has found out something she wasn't supposed to know.

(Character: Saya Yamabuki, Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa)

[Walking Home]

Himari: Whaaat~?! We can't have band practice this day either, then! Well, how about the next day?!
Moca: I've gotta work~.
Himari: Argh~, why is it so hard for us to be free on the same day~?!
Ran, Moca & Tomoe: Ahahaha.
Tsugumi: ...
Himari: Alright, now it's just you, Tsugu! You're free this day, right?! Please, please say you are!
Tsugumi: ... Huh?
Ran: Himari, you're way too worked up. You're scaring her.
Tsugumi: I-I'm sorry! I didn't hear you...
Himari: I'm scheduling our next band practice... Are you tired? You seem kind of down.
Tsugumi: No, I'm alright! Um, I don't have student council that day, so I can go.
Tsugumi: Ah, I just remembered! My mom asked me to run some errands for her! I'll see you all later!
Ran, Moca, Himari & Tomoe: ...
Moca: Her emotions seemed a little all over the place, didn't they~?
Himari: They sure did... I wonder what's going on with her...
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Park]

Tsugumi: Sigh...
Tsugumi: (I ran away again...)
Tsugumi: (I don't want to, but I can't stop thinking about... what Ran-chan said earlier...)
(White cut)
(Background change)
Ran: ... I think we'll end the band.
(White cut)
(Background change)
Tsugumi: (... Just the thought of it makes my heart start racing. I'm so... scared...)
Tsugumi: (I wonder why Ran-chan is thinking about that...)
Tsugumi: (Maybe it's because things aren't going well with her flower-arrangement meetings and her dad again...)
Tsugumi: (... No, that's not it. Ran-chan chose to change so her relationship with us wouldn't have to.)
Tsugumi: But then why...?
Tsugumi: (...! It's a message... from Himari-chan...)
Himari: Thanks for practicing hard today! You seemed tired, so take a nice long bath tonight and then get some rest!
Himari: If something's bothering you, you can always talk to me, okay?
Tsugumi: ... Himari-chan...
Tsugumi: (... "There's something I'd like to talk to you about the band," ... That's not right.)
Tsugumi: ("Have you heard anything from Ran-chan?" ... That's not right either...)
Tsugumi: (I can't tell... Himari-chan or anyone else about this...)
Saya: ... Tsugu?
Tsugumi: S-Saya-chan...
Saya: I was about to take a shortcut home after running an errand when I thought I saw you~. Are you on your way home from band practice?
Tsugumi: Ah... Sob... I, um, well...
Saya: Huh? Wh-what's wrong? Why are you crying?
Tsugumi: Ah, haha... I'm sorry, I'm sorry... I'm just...
Saya: You don't need to apologize. You don't have to hold back the tears either.
Tsugumi: Sob~...
(Black cut)
Tsugumi: Sniff... Sigh... Saya-chan, I'm really sorry for bursting into tears like that.
Saya: It's okay. Are you feeling a little better now?
Tsugumi: Yes... Thank you. I'm alright.
Saya: Good... But you're still not really alright, are you?
Tsugumi: Ahaha... I don't have a choice. I have to be...
Tsugumi: ... Saya-chan... have you decided how long you're going to play in your band?
Saya: Huh? ... Well, I've never even thought about it before.
Saya: I bet Kasumi and the others haven't either. I guess I've only ever assumed we'd keep playing...
Tsugumi: I know... how you feel...
Tsugumi: I'm the same. I assumed my band would keep playing together, the same as always.
Tsugumi: But... keeping a band together is hard, isn't it? When I think that my band could suddenly call it quits... I feel just awful...
Tsugumi: I wish I could tell myself I was just overthinking things, but...
Saya: ... I see...
Saya: Hey... can I tell you something?
Tsugumi: S-sure...
Saya: I've thought keeping a band together is hard too. It seems like a miracle when one to stay together forever.
Saya: Even if all the members like music just as much and want to play together, there's no guarantee they'll always feel that way.
Saya: No matter how much they care for each other, there are still going to be times when things don't go well between them.
Tsugumi: ...
Saya: When I chose to quit a band, it was... extremely painful.
Saya: That's why this time, I won't do that again. I'm trying to stay enthusiastic and not give up.
Tsugumi: Saya-chan...
Tsugumi: ... I don't want to give up either.
Saya: Good... I'm rooting for you. You'll be alright. I know you will.
Tsugumi: Thank you... When you say I'm going to be alright, it's filled with so much kindness.
Tsugumi: (I'll talk to Ran-chan. No matter what happens in the end, the first thing I need to do is face this.)
(Black cut)
