event14-01 Opening: A New Idea

It's summer vacation, and the members of Afterglow are focused on band practice. However, Himari wants to try out something different...

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa)

[CiRCLE - Studio]

[Walking Home]

Moca: Ahh... It's so hot~... I'm melting~.
Tsugumi: The air-conditioner isn't working properly, huh...? There's a little air coming out, but...
Tomoe: At least it died when it did~. I really don't want to play the drums in this heat.
Ran: Yeah. We're lucky it didn't happen in the middle of a song. Why don't we use the rest of our time to have a meeting?
Himari: We have to plan some things for our next gig, huh~? As far as location goes... I guess we'll just use the same live house as always?
Tsugumi: Hmm, that's the most realistic option, but... I kind of feel like it'd be nice to play somewhere different for a change.
Ran: That's true. Our heavier songs would be even more exciting outside...
Tomoe: Ooh, an outdoor gig? I've always dreamed of doing something like that~!
Moca: Nice~. It'd feel good being on stage outside with all those lights~. Ah, but it'd be hot too~.
Tsugumi: Ahaha... I'm sure it'd be really fun though! We could organize a stage at a sports ground... Oh, in this season a pool or the beach could be good too!
Ran: A beach gig... That sounds good.
Himari: Done! Let's all hit the beach this weekend and scope things out!
Ran: ... Huh?
Himari: Oh... uh... it's just... We've been practicing every day during our summer break... We haven't gone out and had any fun, you know?
Himari: I mean, it's fun practicing with everyone, but... it's our summer vacation, you guys?!
Himari: It's summer! We're in high school! We absolutely have to go to the beach! C'mon, you guys~!
Moca: Like a summer memories thing?
Himari: Exactly! We only have one summer vacation in the year! Let's make some memories together~!
Moca: Hrm, sorry Hii-chan, but I go outside as little as humanly possible in the summer. I'll melt if I go to the beach~.
Himari: Wh-wh-whaaat~?! C'mon, let's go~! White sand and the blue ocean! We can play beach volleyball too~!
Moca: No~, my beautiful white skin will get all toasty~.
Himari: B-but...! ... Oh, what about you, Tsugu? You'll go with me, won't you~?
Tsugumi: Sorry... I've got plans next weekend...
Himari: Can't you cancel them~?! Let's eat yakisoba at one of the beach huts~! Oh, Tomoe! The beach! What do you say?!
Tomoe: The beach, huh... It sounds like fun, but I've got meetings for the Downtown summer festival.
Himari: I thought you'd say yes, Tomoe...! C'mon, we can have a swimming race~! Wh-what about you, Ran~?
Ran: I can't, I've got my part-time job.
Moca: Well, there you have it, Hii-chan. Just go by yourself~.
Himari: Th-that's the last thing I wanna do~! C'mon, you guys, it's the beach! Let's go~!
Himari: We're only going to have one summer vacation while we're in the first grade, you know?! We have to enjoy it~!
Ran: More importantly, have you done your homework yet, Himari?
Himari: Ugh...! W-well~...
Ran: You better not end up repeating first grade.
Himari: N-no, I won't! I promise I won't!
Tomoe: Ahaha. Maybe you should try for a study session instead of a day at the beach.
Tsugumi: I haven't finished all of my homework yet either~. I think we'll get a lot done if we work on it together.
Himari: Hmph! I don't wanna study, I wanna go to the beach! I can't believe you guys are so against going with me~!
Moca: C'mon, that's not true. We love you, Hii-chan. Not wanting you to repeat the first grade is... a symbol of our love~.
Himari: Grr~! I thought we were better friends than this!
Tomoe: Okay, chill out, Himari. We can't go to the beach, but we can at least go get something to eat tonight.
Himari: Wah~... I wanna go to the beach... *sniff*
Tsugumi: D-don't cry, Himari-chan~. C'mon, let's go get some ice cream at the station!
Himari: But... no one will go to the beach with me...
Moca: Just let it go, Hii-chan. I'd rather have a cream puff than ice cream~.
Ran: C'mon guys, let's pack up. I feel like a cream puff too.
Tsugumi: Let's go to the cake shop then. Okay, Himari-chan?
Himari: You guys are so mean... Wahhh! I don't care! I'm going to the beach, just you wait and see~!!
