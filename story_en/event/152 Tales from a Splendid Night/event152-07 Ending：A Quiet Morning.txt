event152-07 Ending: A Quiet Morning

Once the dream has ended, morning comes.

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa)

[Morning]

[Riverside]

Ran: Tomoe.
Tomoe: Oh, heya, Ran. Great weather this morning, huh?
Ran: Yeah, the weather's nice.
Ran: ...
Tomoe: What's up? Did you forget something?
Ran: No, it's not that. The other day when we stayed over at your place...
Tomoe: Yeah?
Ran: I had a dream. I don't remember much, though. I just know it was strange and bizarre.
Tomoe: I see... Was it a nightmare?
Ran: No, I don't think so...
Ran: We were together, and something odd happened...
Ran: Also, we watched the sunset and...
Ran: It's no use, I've forgotten it. Sorry, never mind.
Tomoe: No worries. Actually, I had a weird dream that night as well.
Ran: Really?
Tomoe: Yeah. A weird and mysterious dream.
Tomoe: Whatever you wished for in the dream came true.
Tomoe: So you, Moca, Himari, Tsugu and I could always be together. Having fun without a care in the world.
Tomoe: And those wishes you guys made... were all granted by me.
Ran: I see. Does that mean it was a nice dream then?
Tomoe: Hmmm...
Tomoe: Nah, it wasn't!
Ran: Huh?
Tomoe: Well, think about it. Imagine if your wishes came true without any effort.
Tomoe: Wouldn't that feel lousy? There's nothing comfortable about sitting on a throne you didn't earn yourself.
Tomoe: Am I wrong?
Ran: ... No, you're not.
Ran: We've paved our path all by ourselves. We started a band, wrote our own songs, and sang them.
Ran: Nobody handed any of this to us. We've accomplished it all with hard work.
Ran: ... Besides, if you granted everyone's wishes, Tomoe, where would that leave the rest of us?
Tomoe: ... Mhm.
Ran: In fact, if you called that a good dream, I might've been angry. I'd wanna know what you take us for.
Tomoe: Haha. Right?
Tomoe: Yeah. You're just the type to say that, Ran.
Ran: Huh...?
Tomoe: You wouldn't be satisfied with living in a dream. You're pretty greedy, after all.
Ran: Hey, so you do get me.
Tomoe: A childhood friend should know these things.
Tomoe: I've been your friend for ages, Ran... That's why I feel the same.
Ran: Really?
Tomoe: Uh-huh. Dreams that stay in your head are pointless.
Tomoe: Our dreams are there to be achieved.
Tomoe: That's why we work hard to make them come true.
Ran: ... Yeah. You're right.
Tomoe: We're competing with girl bands from all around the world.
Tomoe: I hope that one day we can go meet them, play against them, and become friends.
Ran: ... Yeah.
Ran: But what would you do if we're still dreaming right now? Imagine if you woke up the moment you fulfilled that dream.
Tomoe: Then I'll work toward achieving it again after I wake up. No matter how many times it takes.
Ran: Fufu, yeah. That's just like you, Tomoe.
Ran: We better get going, or we'll be late for school.
Tomoe: Okay!
(White cut)
(Background change)
Himari: Ah, Ran! Tomoe!
Tsugumi: Good morning, you two.
Tomoe: Oh, morning.
Ran: Morning, guys... Fufu.
Moca: Ran, what's up?
Ran: Well, Tomoe was telling me about her weird dream...
(Black cut)
