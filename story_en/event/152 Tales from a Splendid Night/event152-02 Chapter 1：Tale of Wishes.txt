event152-02 Chapter 1: Tale of Wishes

One morning, Himari shows up with a mysterious lamp. One by one, the girls make their wishes on the lamp.

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa)

[?]

Moca: For today's lunch, I've brought a wiener sausage bun and a minced meat cutlet sandwich~.
Moca: Oh, I can hardly wait~.
Tsugumi: Make sure not to wolf it down before lunchtime, Moca-chan.
Moca: Nah, I won't do that today~. Then there'd be less to eat for lunch.
Ran: What kind of reasoning is that...?
Himari: You guys~!
Tsugumi: Good morning, Himari-chan.
Himari: Morning! Hey, look what I've got!
Moca: Th-this is...!
Ran: ... A lamp?
Himari: Yes! A magic lamp! I went hunting around for one after our chat, and found this at the knick-knack shop!
Tsugumi: Will a genie really pop out if you rub it?
Ran: No, there's no way...
Himari: You don't know that~. I haven't actually tried it yet!
Moca: Maybe it will, maybe it won't. We won't know for sure unless we give it a shot.
Moca: Basically, there's a fifty-fifty chance...!
Tsugumi: I don't think that's quite right...
Himari: Hey, Ran! Try rubbing the lamp.
Ran: Huh? Why me? You bought it, Himari, so you do it.
Himari: I can't. I'm too nervous! Come on, give it a whirl!
Ran: If you insist...
Ran & Moca & Himari & Tsugumi: ...
Tsugumi: Ahaha... Nothing's happening.
Ran: Well, of course not.
Himari: Awww, what a letdown~.
Moca: Hii-chan, you need to be more realistic.
Himari: You're the one who said we had a fifty-fifty chance, Moca!
Tomoe: Hiya. What's got you guys so excited this morning?
Moca: Ah, Tomo-chin has appeared in place of the genie~.
Tsugumi: Himari-chan got her hands on a lamp that looks like it houses a genie.
Himari: It's this thing here... But Ran rubbed it, and there's no genie in sight...
Tomoe: Ahaha. That lamp certainly looks magical.
Tomoe: Hey, Ran. If a genie really did appear, what would you have wished for?
Ran: Huh? How should I know...? Besides, there's no genie anyway.
Tomoe: Oh, who cares? I'm just asking, "What if?"
Ran: Well, when it comes to wishes...
Ran: ... All I really want is for us to be together forever.
Tsugumi: Ran-chan...!
Moca: Oh~. That's so selfless of you, Ran~.
Ran: I don't know about that...
Tomoe: Haha, I see. Well, I'm sure we all wish for that too.
Ran: Um... Yeah.
Himari: Alright, I'm gonna make a wish as well!
Himari: Umm... I think I'd like... to take a trip around the world!
Tsugumi: Since we're sharing our wishes...
Tsugumi: Mine is... to fly through the sky.
Moca: Then I want~...
Moca: All-you-can-eat buns at Yamabuki Bakery~.
Ran: Is that all, Moca?
Moca: Excuse you. I'll have you know that this is my life's dream.
Tomoe: I guess I'm up next. Hmm, well...
Tomoe: Someday, in a massive sold-out venue... I wanna hold our very own solo concert.
Ran: Tomoe...
Tsugumi: Yes, that's something we all want... It's our band's dream.
Himari: We'll make that dream come true even without the help of a genie, though!
Tomoe: Haha, true that.
Moca: Better yet, I want that to happen as soon as possible, not someday in the future.
(White cut)
(Background change)
Ran: Huh...?
Tsugumi: Wh-what's happening?!
Audience Member: Afterglow! Afterglow!
Himari: Is this our concert...?
Moca: Whoa, the place is packed. Looks like Tomo-chin's wish has become reality already~.
Himari: What?! Wait, does that mean everyone else's wish will be granted next?!
Tsugumi: Th-that's not possible... I-is it...?!
Tomoe: ... Yo, Ran, what should we do?
Ran: Err, w-well...
Ran: I don't get what's happening... But if a band stands on a stage, there's only one thing to do... Right?
Tomoe: Gotcha. Okay, let's do this!
Moca & Ran & Himari & Tsugumi: Yeah!
(White cut)
