event05-01 Opening: A Compelling Poster

Kasumi looks for someone to join her on a stargazing tour. She finds Ran and Tsugumi on their way to rehearsal...

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Ran Mitake, Tsugumi Hazawa)

[Downtown]

Kasumi: Hm? What's this poster about...?
Kasumi: Stargazing tour?
Kasumi: Whoa~, that sounds awesome!
Kasumi: Stargazing, huh?
Kasumi: That reminds me. It was when I was looking at the night sky as a kid that I felt the beating of the stars... Sparkling and heart-pounding...
Kasumi: I wanna go~! I want to feel it again~!
Kasumi: Let's see, looks like... Yep, I'll be free on that day! Okay, I have to register!
Kasumi: But since I'm already planning to go, maybe I should invite someone else, too...
Kasumi: O-Tae and the others? Would they want to go if they had time?
Kasumi: Ah! ... But it looks like today is the last day to register... Hm, I don't have time to check with them, so I guess I'll sign up by myself...
Kasumi: But, but...! I want someone to go with me~!
Ran: ... So wouldn't doing a performance like that be good?
Tsugumi: Yeah, I was thinking that too. I'm glad you thought the same.
Kasumi: Hm?! Is that...?!
Tsugumi: Oh, Kasumi-chan. Hello.
Kasumi: Tsugu~! Ran-chan! Just in time♪
Ran: Wh-what...?
Tsugumi: For what?
Kasumi: Do you guys have a sec'?! I swear it won't take long!
Ran: ... Sounds suspicious...
Tsugumi: Ahaha, Ran-chan. Don't say that...
Tsugumi: Sorry, Kasumi-chan. What were you saying? We're okay for now...
Tsugumi: We're going to the studio in a bit, so we still have some time. Right, Ran-chan?
Ran: Maybe, but not for anything shady...
Kasumi: Then, we're okay! This is totally not shady, believe me! So, I'll get right to it. Are you guys free at the end of the month?
Tsugumi: Uhm... Yes, I will be, since I haven't been asked to help out at our café.
Ran: I don't really have anything planned... So?
Kasumi: Yay! Then it's decided!
Ran: S-stop! No deciding...! What are you talking about?
Tsugumi: Kasumi-chan, could you give us a few more details?
Kasumi: Just look! This poster!
Kasumi: Bam!!
Tsugumi: Stargazing tour...?
Ran: ...
Kasumi: Wouldn't you wanna go? You do, right?! You totally wanna go, don't you?!
Kasumi: You can feel the beat of the stars under the endless, glittering sky! I just know it'll be heart-pounding!
Kasumi: The participation fee is so cheap, you can't miss this chance!
Ran: What is "the beat of the stars?"
Tsugumi: Ohh... That sounds fun. I think I might be interested... A stargazing tour is not something you can go on whenever you want.
Kasumi: Right, right?! This is just the right season to go! It'll be the best starlit sky you get to see!
Kasumi: Something we would never be able to see here! You just have to go!
Ran: Even with all that being said, it's kind of short notice...
Kasumi: C'mon~!! I'm telling you, it'll be more fun to go as a group!
Tsugumi: I think I would like to go... Ran-chan, what do you think?
Kasumi: What do you think? What do you think?
Ran: ... Hmm...
