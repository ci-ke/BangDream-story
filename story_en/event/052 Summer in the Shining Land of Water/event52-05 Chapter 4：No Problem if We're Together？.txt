event52-05 Chapter 4: No Problem if We're Together?

The girls walk to the water slide. Rinko finds the courage to challenge herself...

(Character: Yukina Minato, Sayo Hikawa, Lisa Imai, Ako Udagawa, Rinko Shirokane)

[Toconut Park - Water Slide]

Yukina: This is the water slide...?
Rinko: I-it's so tall... I didn't think it'd be this big...
Ako: This ride puts you on a rubber raft and then sends you zooming down the water slide!
Ako: Each raft holds up to five people, so we can all ride together~!
Lisa: ... Wait, we got five fast passes for this ride, but is that what everyone wanted?!
Ako: I-I didn't think about that! Rin-rin, you don't like these kinds of rides, do you? You sat out during the river rafting and the wave pool!
Sayo: Shirokane-san, there's no need to ride this if you don't want to.
Ako: Yeah! Don't force yourself!
Rinko: ...
Rinko: (I've been avoiding scary-looking rides all day... Am I really okay with that...?)
Rinko: (If I want to change... I can't keep running away from these little things... If I go on this, it may bring me one step closer to my goal...)
Rinko: Uhm, I think... I-I'll give it a shot...!
Yukina: Are you sure?
Rinko: Y-yes. It's a little scary, but... everyone's here with me...
Ako: Rin-rin...! Yeah, you'll see. It'll be tons of fun!
Sayo: Well then, shall we get going?
Yukina: Yes, this looks like it'll be rather thrilling.
Lisa: ... Wait, you and Sayo are coming too?!
Yukina: ...? Is there a problem with that?
Lisa: No, I just didn't think you two would want to do this sort of thing.
Sayo: It's a much better use of time than sitting around waiting.
Yukina: I usually don't... But right now I do. Let's go.
Lisa: O-okay, if that's what you want~...
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Staff: Is everyone ready? Have fun~!
Ako: Here we go~! Go! Go!
Yukina: ... This is a lot slower than I thought it would be.
Sayo: Agreed. Honestly, I imagined a much faster ride.
Lisa: If this is as speedy as it gets, you should be okay, right, Rinko?
Rinko: Y-yes... This is fine...
Ako: Fufufu, but we're just getting started~! One of this ride's best features is all the swerves and curves!
Sayo: Swerves and curves...?
Rinko: Kyaaa!!
Lisa: Whoa! These turns are a lot more intense than I thought!
Sayo: Too intense if you ask me! It feels like we're going to fall out!
Yukina: And they just keep coming... How entertaining. Rinko, grab hold of the handles and don't let go.
Rinko: O-okay...!
Ako: Yay~! I'm getting dizzy~!  Ehehe, this is fun~!
Sayo: ... Looks like we got through all the curves.
Lisa: Is everyone okay? Those twists and turns were no joke~.
Rinko: Am I... still... on the raft...?
Lisa: Ahaha, yup, you sure are! I think we're through the worst of it. You can open your eyes now!
Yukina: Rinko, if you're going to cling to something, cling to the raft. I can't have you hanging on to me...
Rinko: I-I'm sorry... I was just so scared...
Lisa: No kidding. Even I freaked out a little~.
Sayo: ... What a long ride. I wonder if we're close to the end.
Ako: Not yet! We haven't even gotten to the other special part!
Sayo: What do you mean...? Is there something else coming up?
Ako: You bet! There's a super-fast, super-steep drop right at the very end!
Lisa & Sayo: A super-steep...
Rinko: Drop...?!
Ako: Ah, look! Here it comes!
Lisa: Whoa! Everyone, grab onto the handles!
Sayo: R-right!
Rinko: W-we're going to die...!
Yukina: Rinko, I don't mean to repeat myself, but cling to the raft, not me.
Rinko, Sayo & Lisa: Kyaaa~!!!
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Ako: Phew~, that was a blast~!!
Yukina: Agreed. It was quite the thrilling experience.
Yukina: How did you all feel about it? I didn't think it was so bad...
Rinko: ...
Ako: Rin-rin? Hey~, Earth to Rin-rin!
Rinko: ... I-I'm sorry. My mind went completely blank...
Lisa: Ooo, that was way, way, way too scary~!!
Yukina: You too, Lisa? You were so excited before.
Lisa: I didn't know it was gonna be that intense!
Sayo: Unbelievable! What is such a dangerous attraction doing here?! Has it actually received official approval to operate?!
Yukina: Looks like Sayo was frightened as well.
Ako: Who would have thought...?
Yukina: ...
Ako: Is something wrong, Yukina-san?
Yukina: No, it's just interesting seeing everyone's reactions.
Ako: ...? You're so weird, Yukina-san...
