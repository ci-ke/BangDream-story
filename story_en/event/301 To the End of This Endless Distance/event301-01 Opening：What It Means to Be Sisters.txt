event301-01 Opening: What It Means to Be Sisters

After the incident with Tomoe, Ako is more fired up than ever. Seeing her dependable figure, Sayo and Rinko...

(Character: Yukina Minato, Sayo Hikawa, Lisa Imai, Ako Udagawa, Rinko Shirokane)

Sayo: (“To stand on the same stage as Hina”... It’s been quite a while since I told her that.)
Sayo: (Dreaming of the day I can stand proudly by her side, I’m still taking this long, winding detour.)
Sayo: (...I wonder how close I am to the end of that detour.)
(White cut)
(Background change)
Yukina: —Alright, let’s go with this arrangement.
Lisa: Okay. So we’ll start locking it in from here?
Rinko: Yes... I think so...
Ako: ...Hmm...
Sayo: Is something bothering you, Udagawa-san?
Ako: Ah, no! I mean, I think it sounds really cool!
Ako: But I guess it just feels a little lacking somehow...
Yukina: Which part do you feel is lacking?
Ako: Hmm, maybe the latter half? It doesn’t really go “bam!” enough to feel like “the coolest in the world” yet...
Yukina: ...I see.
Ako: Oh, I know! Can we try a different version real quick?
Lisa: Ah~ I’d love to, but if we start tweaking things now, won’t we run out of time?
Sayo: You’re right—we have a meeting with Harumi-san coming up. We need to wrap up in 30 minutes or we’ll be late.
Ako: Oh, right~...! Okay, then! How about we try it out during our next practice instead!?
Yukina: No, let’s try it now.
Lisa: Wait, are you sure?
Yukina: Yes. I want to hear Ako’s idea at least once. Even if we can’t finish today, we might come up with a plan by next time.
Sayo: Understood. Udagawa-san, could you show us what you had in mind?
Ako: Yes! So, right after the second chorus—
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Ako: Sigh~ Time’s up already, huh~.
Sayo: But I think we made good progress. Even in that short time, we were able to share a clear image.
Rinko: Yes... We also identified what we each need to work on... so we can fine-tune everything at the next rehearsal...
Ako: Yeah! I’ll come up with even cooler ideas before then too!
Lisa: We’ve really been trying more arrangement patterns lately. Especially with all the ideas Ako’s been throwing out.
Yukina: Yeah. You can tell she truly believes in Roselia’s potential and keeps chasing new ways to express it.
Ako: Of course I do! Because I seriously believe Roselia can become the coolest band in the world!
Ako: And to make that happen, I’ve decided I’ll become the coolest drummer in the world... So I just want to do everything I can!
Lisa: Oh, look at you go~, Ako! Guess I better step up my game too~.
Ako: Oh, right, Lisa-nee! About that part that was hard to sync up earlier—
Sayo: Fufu...
Yukina: What is it?
Sayo: Ah, it’s nothing... I just realized once again how strong Udagawa-san really is.
Sayo: Both Udagawa-san and Tomoe-san... After what they went through, they seem to shine even brighter now.
Rinko: ......
Yukina: Yeah... I remember the day after Ako said she’d stop admiring and start aiming for the top—she came to the studio with puffy eyes from crying. That really surprised me.
Sayo: I was a little worried that their relationship might fall apart.
Rinko: You were...?
Sayo: Yes. As someone with a little sister myself, I couldn’t help but see myself and Tomoe-san in their situation.
Sayo: Even if you understand your little sister’s decision logically, it’s a whole other thing to catch up emotionally.
Sayo: Especially between sisters... there’s always something complicated there.
Rinko: ...Sisters... Yes, that’s true...
Yukina: I see... So that’s why you were so concerned about Ako back then.
Sayo: ...Maybe so.
Sayo: Even if things get messy, sisters are bound together for life.
Sayo: That’s why I told her—before things truly fall apart, at the very least, be honest with your feelings and share them with the other person... That’s what I told her.
Yukina: ...Coming from you, those words probably reached her more deeply than anyone else’s could.
Sayo: Even so, it was Udagawa-san who chose to move forward in the end.
Sayo: And Tomoe-san didn’t run away either—she faced it and gave her answer. ...If it were me, I think it would’ve taken a lot longer.
Yukina: You might be right.
Yukina: But now, you’re no longer fixated on Hina. You’re simply enjoying honing your own sound... That’s how I see it, so I’m not too worried about you.
Sayo: —......
Ako: Hey—Yukina-san, everyone~! Harumi-san’s already in the meeting room!
Yukina: We're coming.
Sayo: (No longer fixated on Hina...)
Sayo: (From the “me who could only think in comparison to Hina,” I wonder... am I truly growing beyond that now?)
Ako: Sayo-san~, Rin-rin too! Let’s start the meeting already~!
Sayo: (If, on the day I could stand on the same stage as Hina... if I could shine the way Udagawa-san does—)
Sayo: Fufu... Alright. I’ll be right there.
