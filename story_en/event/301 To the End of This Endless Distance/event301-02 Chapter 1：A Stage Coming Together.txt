event301-02 Chapter 1: A Stage Coming Together

Roselia’s numbers are steadily improving. For Sayo, however, this progress carries a deeper meaning...

(Character: Hina Hikawa, Yukina Minato, Sayo Hikawa, Lisa Imai, Ako Udagawa, Rinko Shirokane)

[Music Agency - Meeting Room]

Harumi: —And here’s the sales report for your 2nd album!
Lisa & Ako: Wow~!
Sayo: It looks like it’s selling better than the previous one.
Harumi: That’s right! CD sales and streaming numbers are both through the roof! It’s pretty rare for a rookie artist to keep up this level of performance—people inside the company are really impressed!
Yukina: I see.
Lisa: Hey, Yukina, don’t you think you could show just a little more excitement?
Ako: Yeah! It means our music is reaching so many people!
Yukina: Of course I’m happy. The better the results, the more opportunities we’ll have moving forward.
Rinko: If we can broaden the scope of our activities... then it should also bring us closer... to our goal of taking on All Music Fes once again...
Sayo: Yes. These results are proof that we’re getting closer to our goal... no more, no less.
Yukina: Exactly.
Lisa: Well, sure, but still~... I feel really happy about it. Like, relieved, even...
Ako: Yes, you wrote lyrics for this album, Lisa-nee? That means people loved your lyrics too~!
Lisa: I-I think you’re giving me too much credit~...! But thanks, Ako.
Lisa: I guess seeing these results just makes me feel like I’m really moving forward... and that makes me happy.
Sayo: Indeed. It’s thanks to your “challenge,” Imai-san.
Yukina: Yes. And our challenge is only just beginning. Let’s stay focused and keep going.
All: Yes! Yeah!
Harumi: Alright, shall we move on to today’s main topic?
Harumi: As you all know, we’ve been planning to release a single and hold a solo live following the 2nd album.
Harumi: Today, I’d love to hear your thoughts and ideas for the new single’s concept. Do you have any kind of image in mind?
Yukina: Actually, I brought a rough draft of a song I’ve been working on.
Ako: What!? You’ve already written a song, Yukina-san!?
Yukina: Yes. I’d like you all to listen to it first. I want to hear your impressions.
Lisa: Then let’s hear it.
(Black cut)
Yukina: —And that’s what I’ve got so far.
Ako: It’s suuuuper cool~!! I wanna drum to it already!
Rinko: It’s got power and momentum... It’s definitely difficult, but that makes it a fitting challenge...
Sayo: Yes. It steps a little outside of Roselia’s usual style, which makes it all the more worth exploring.
Lisa: Yeah, I think we could work in some cool new phrases too.
Ako: Totally! I’ve already got so many ideas popping into my head—I’m trying to figure out which one would fit best!
Yukina: I’m glad to hear that.
Yukina: I composed the foundation with the intent to capture Roselia’s current momentum, while giving everyone as much room as possible to express what they want to do.
Lisa: I see. But if we all throw in what we want to do, won’t it get a little messy?
Yukina: Given the Roselia we have become, we'll be fine.
Yukina: Lately, I’ve felt our mutual trust growing deeper. That’s why I want to show my trust in everyone too... That feeling is part of what’s in this song.
Lisa: I see... Hearing you say that, I can’t help but want to rise to the occasion.
Sayo: Indeed. Let’s live up to the trust you’ve placed in us—with the best performance possible.
Ako: Yes!! Rin-rin, let’s make it the coolest song in the world!
Rinko: Ah... Y-yeah...!
Ako: ......
Harumi: Then let’s move forward with this song for the single!
Yukina: Yes, please do.
Harumi: Also, I have one more piece of good news today...!
Lisa: Good news?
Harumi: Yes, about the solo live... Here’s the list of potential venues!
Ako: Let’s see~... Whoa! These are all huge halls!
Sayo: ...Oh my, this one...
Lisa: The last one on the list—it’s the biggest one, right? Just having it as a candidate is amazing!
Rinko: Yeah... The idea of doing a solo show at a place this size... it feels like it might actually happen...
Lisa: It really feels like we’re growing, huh? Is this partly because of how well the album’s doing?
Harumi: Yes. Not only because of the album, but also thanks to accomplishments like your All Music Fes appearance, we believe that with Roselia as it is now, you can fill a venue of this scale!
Sayo: ......
Ako: Huh? Sayo-san, is something wrong?
Sayo: Ah—no... It just doesn’t feel real yet...
Sayo: Actually, one of the venues on the list... the second-largest one... That’s where Pastel＊Palettes held a fan event.
Lisa: Oh wow...! Yeah, it’s kind of hard to believe we’re now talking about the same scale as Pastel＊Palettes.
Yukina: It’s not about comparing ourselves to other bands. Objectively, this is simply where Roselia stands now.
Sayo: Yes, you’re right...
Sayo: (Just like Minato-san said, objectively speaking, the fact that we can hold a live at the same scale as Pastel＊Palettes... that’s all it is.)
Sayo: (But... on the flip side, that means I, too, am a guitarist capable of performing in a venue equal to Hina’s...)
(White cut)
(Background change)

(Memory cut-in)

Sayo: ...Hina.
Hina: Hm~?
Sayo: ...Someday, I want to be able to hold my head high... and stand on the same stage as you.
Hina: I want you beside me too! I can’t wait for that someday to come♪
(White cut)

(Memory cut-out)

(Background change)
Sayo: (—The place I’ve been striving for... is almost within reach.)
Sayo: (...Is it really?)
