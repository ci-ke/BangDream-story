event301-07 Ending: Enjoying Even the Thorny Road

At last, Sayo finds her resolve. That determination, in turn, begins to inspire Yukina as well.

(Character: Yukina Minato, Sayo Hikawa, Lisa Imai, Ako Udagawa, Rinko Shirokane)

[A Few Days Later]

Harumi: Huff, huff... Thanks for waiting! Sorry I kept you waiting!
Lisa: You didn’t have to rush over, Harumi-san!
Rinko: Catch your breath... take it slow...
Harumi: Right... sorry...! Whew~... Here, please take a look at this first!
Yukina: This is... a proposal for a solo live show.
Sayo: ! Harumi-san, this venue...
Harumi: Yes, it’s the largest one on our candidate list!
Ako: Amazing~~!! Does this mean we’re gonna perform here?!
Sayo: Udagawa-san, please look more carefully. It says “Tentative,” doesn’t it?
Harumi: We’re still in talks with the venue, so it’s marked tentative, but it’s practically confirmed!
Lisa: Whoa~, I’m already starting to get nervous~...! Whether we can fill the venue really depends on us, you know.
Rinko: Right... I think it also shows how much faith they’re putting in us to succeed at something this scale...
Sayo: That just makes me want to step up even more.
Yukina: I agree. But I’m excited.
Sayo: That’s rare, coming from you, Minato-san.
Yukina: ...You’re right, it is.
Lisa: Ahaha, what’s with that~? You’re talking like it’s not even about you!
Harumi: But I’m really glad you feel that way!
Harumi: Now that we’ve got the timing and venue lined up, let’s start prepping things little by little after we finish the single!
All: Yeah! Yes!
Harumi: Ah, one more thing before we dive into the main topic. Based on Roselia’s recent achievements, there’s been some internal talk about revisiting your activity plans.
Harumi: If there’s anything you’d like—more releases, events you want to join, anything at all—please don’t hesitate to tell me.
Yukina & Sayo: ......
Sayo: Harumi-san, if I may get straight to the point... I have a request of my own.
Harumi: Yes, what is it?
Sayo: Actually, I heard there’s a girls’ band event that’s currently in the works.
Sayo: I heard it’s a pretty major event without public auditions, and that only bands affiliated with agencies will be invited.
Harumi: Could it be... “Cross×Point Fes.”? I’ve heard it’s going to be a large-scale festival featuring a variety of famous bands, though the format hasn’t been finalized yet.
Sayo: I’d like to talk with everyone first, but... I want to take part in that event.
Sayo: How about the rest of you?
Lisa: I think we’re all feeling the same way, but before you answer, just one thing... Sayo, where’d you hear that?
Sayo: ...I heard about it from Hina.
Lisa: ! I see... So that’s how it is.
Rinko: Then... there’s a chance that Pastel＊Palettes might be invited too...?
Sayo: I’m not sure, but... probably.
Rinko: I see...
Sayo: ...My entire life as a guitarist has always existed alongside the shadow cast by the brilliant light that is Hina.
Sayo: I struggled beneath that blinding radiance, kept fighting and pushing forward—until I finally came to believe that my own sound can shine just as brightly.
Sayo: To stand on the same stage as Hina, there’s nothing I’m still lacking anymore. And because of that, I want to fulfill my goal—and move beyond it...
Sayo: Before challenging All Music Fes once more, this is something I must overcome—right now.
All: ...!
Ako: Let’s do it!! You all feel the same, right?!
Rinko: Yeah... I’m in too...
Lisa: It’s Sayo’s important challenge, of course—but more than that, we’ve all seen the path she’s walked. That’s exactly why we want to be right there with her, supporting her as closely as we can!
Harumi: From what I’ve heard, if we do get invited, it’ll still be a while. Which means we’ve got plenty of time to prepare.
Harumi: And I’ll do everything I can to support you all in this challenge!
Sayo: Everyone... thank you so much.
Ako: Wah~~, I’m so pumped right now! This is totally peak Roselia—world’s coolest band!
Yukina: ...! That’s right. This is who we are...
Yukina: Harumi-san. Everyone. There’s something I want to discuss.
Lisa: What is it?
Yukina: The song for the single we’ve been working on... I want to rewrite it.
Ako: Ehh?! Wh-why...?!
Yukina: Looking at all of you now... I realized that song just won’t do.
Yukina: I was the one who asked whether you had the resolve to stake everything as “challengers”—but I never expected everyone’s determination to grow to this extent.
Yukina: From who you are now, I can feel a strength and resilience—an ability to face even the greatest challenges without fear, and to walk a thorny path while embracing it.
Yukina: For artists like you... no—for us—the song must be worthy of that resolve. Otherwise, there’s no meaning in releasing a single at this moment.
Rinko: Yukina-san...
Yukina: I already have the image in mind. So please, let me bring a new song tomorrow.
Sayo: ...I see. Understood. Harumi-san, will this cause any trouble for the other staff?
Harumi: That part should be fine, but... if we’re going back to square one, the production schedule for all of you will become quite tight. That’s my only concern.
Sayo: Then that won’t be a problem. If it’s Minato-san, I’m sure—
Lisa, Ako & Rinko: ...!
Yukina: Everyone...
Yukina: Thank you. Honestly... I didn’t think you’d accept it so quickly.
Sayo: That depends on what kind of song you bring us tomorrow.
Yukina: ! Fufu... fair enough.
Sayo: ...I believe in you, Minato-san.
Yukina: Of course. Leave it to me.
(White cut)
