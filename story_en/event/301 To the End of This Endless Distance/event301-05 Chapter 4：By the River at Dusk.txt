event301-05 Chapter 4: By the River at Dusk

Rinko, still unsure, decides to speak with Tomoe. The two begin to share their honest feelings...

(Character: Tomoe Udagawa, Yukina Minato, Sayo Hikawa, Lisa Imai, Ako Udagawa, Rinko Shirokane)

[The Next Day]

(Black cut)
(Background change)
Rinko: ......
Rinko: Suu... Haa...
Tomoe: Ah, there you are. Hey, Rinko-san~!
Rinko: T-Tomoe-san...
Tomoe: It’s been a while! Sorry for asking you to come all the way out here!
Rinko: No... I should be the one apologizing for the sudden request...
Tomoe: It’s totally fine. You said you wanted to talk about something, right?
Rinko: Y-yes...
Rinko: ...
Rinko: About that live show where we invited you all... Ako-chan declared on stage that she’s going to be the best drummer in the world...
Rinko: I was the one who told Ako-chan... That she’s fine just the way she is... that she doesn’t need to try to be anyone else...
Tomoe: ...!
Rinko: To Ako-chan, the “world’s greatest” is you, Tomoe-san... I know how much she’s treasured that admiration...
Rinko: It’s because of that admiration that Ako-chan always shone so bright... But ever since a certain interview, she’s been struggling...
Rinko: She kept saying how much she wished she could be like you, Tomoe-san... And hearing that... it just made me think, Ako-chan should just be herself...
Rinko: So... I’m the one who gave her that push... It was me...
Tomoe: I see... That’s why she said what she did during the MC...
Rinko: After that... I heard there was a period where you and Ako-chan couldn’t talk properly...
Tomoe: Ah, no, it wasn’t like that at all—...
Tomoe: ...Actually. To be honest, when she said she didn’t look up to me anymore, it hit me really hard... I just couldn’t keep being her “cool big sister” anymore.
Rinko: ...I see...
Rinko: (...Ah... no, this won’t do.)
Rinko: (The one who suffered the most—who was in the most pain—was Tomoe-san... I shouldn’t be making a face like this.)
Rinko: Um... Tomoe-san...! ...I’m really—
Tomoe: Rinko-san.
Tomoe: Sorry if I’m off base here... but how about we make it a rule that neither of us apologizes over this?
Rinko: ...Why...?
Tomoe: I kinda figured you’d say something like that.
Tomoe: Besides, this was something both Ako and I needed. I figured it would happen someday... This was just the time it did.
Rinko: ...
Tomoe: Rinko-san, do you remember when I told you before that I tend to try to act cool in front of Ako?
Rinko: Yes... But when it comes to you acting cool, Tomoe-san...
Tomoe: The first step toward becoming the version of yourself you want to be. That’s what you told me. But, I don’t think that’s enough anymore.
Tomoe: When I really think about who I want to be... it’s not someone who just fits in the role of the “cool big sister.”
Tomoe: I’ve made up my mind—I’m going to become strong for my own sake from now on. So... please don’t apologize.
Rinko: ...Tomoe-san...
Rinko: (“Sisters are bound together for their entire lives”... Hikawa-san said that too.)
Rinko: (When I encouraged Ako-chan... did I really understand everything it meant?)
Tomoe: ...Still, I don’t think I’ll be able to stop trying to look cool anytime soon.
Rinko: Thank you... for talking to me...
Rinko: Back then, when I gave her that push... I didn’t truly understand...
Rinko: That no matter what happens... siblings will always be siblings... I didn’t understand the weight of that... or the pain...
Tomoe: Rinko-san...
Tomoe: ...Maybe that goes for me too.
Tomoe: Because you’re someone who thinks so deeply about Ako—as her bandmate and as a dear friend—I feel like there are things only you can notice, and words only you can say to her.
Rinko: Maybe... that’s true...
Tomoe: ...Yeah. I think it is.
Tomoe: Rinko-san. As “Ako’s big sister,” I have a favor to ask.
Rinko: ? Yes...
Tomoe: You’re the one who lit that fire in Ako to aim for the world’s coolest drummer.
Tomoe: You’re the one who supported Ako through her struggles and her passion to grow as Roselia’s drummer.
Tomoe: Because you’re both her bandmate and her friend... I know you’ll be able to run alongside her as she aims for the top...
Tomoe: So... I’m counting on you to look after Ako.
Rinko: ――...!
Rinko: Yes...! I want this choice to be something we can look back on and truly say was the right one... So I’ll keep giving it my all with Ako-chan... from here on, too...!

Rinko: She asked me... to take care of you...

Rinko: Yeah... She said... the one who can run beside you, as you aim for the top... is me—the one who pushed you forward...
Rinko: Thanks to Tomoe-san’s words... I feel ready now...!
Rinko: I’m... going to keep striving for greater heights together with Ako-chan...
