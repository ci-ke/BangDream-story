event301-04 Chapter 3: Just a Little Further

Sayo is invited to lunch by Hina. There, she ends up hearing about a certain event...

(Character: Hina Hikawa, Sayo Hikawa)

[The Next Day]

Professor: —Alright, that’s it for this week.
Hina: Eh~, it’s already over? I was just enjoying watching my sis taking notes so seriously~.
Sayo: You really should pay proper attention to the class...
Hina: I am, I am! Ears on the professor, eyes on you!
Sayo: That means you’re not paying proper attention.
Classmate A: Oh, Sayo-chan! I bought Roselia’s new album~!
Classmate B: All the songs were super cool! I especially loved the last one!
Hina: Thanks~~! My sis’s guitar was awesome, right? But at a live show, it gets even more boppin’♪
Sayo: Why are you answering for me? Thank you both. I really appreciate you taking the time to share your thoughts.
Classmate A: No worries! But Hina-chan, you’re amazing too. You were just on TV!
Classmate B: Oh, the quiz show? That was really fun to watch~. Pastel＊Palettes’ pretty popular right now, huh?
Classmate A: Totally. It’s incredible that both twins are so talented~.
Sayo: ...Uh...
Hina: Ahaha, my sis is flustered! We got praised together, so just enjoy it~.
Classmate A: Ah, sorry...! We didn’t mean to make you uncomfortable. We just wanted to say we’re rooting for you!
Sayo: No, I should be the one apologizing. I’m happy, really... I’m just not used to it.
Classmate B: No problem. See you in the next class!
Hina: Bye-bye~!
Hina: Hey, sis, you don’t have any more classes now, right? Let’s grab lunch together.
Sayo: Alright...
Sayo: (...Hina and I, “both” talented, huh...)
Sayo: (If I really were, I wouldn’t have taken such a long detour.)
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Hina: Nom... Mmm~, so tasty~! I’m in heaven~.
Sayo: You’re being dramatic over a simple lunch.
Hina: Not dramatic at all. We go to the same school, take the same classes, and get to eat lunch together—of course I’m happy!
Sayo: Sigh... You should be used to it by now.
Hina: I’m still happy~!
Hina: Oh! That reminds me! I heard something good!
Sayo: Something good?
Hina: Yeah, I ran into a staff member I’m close with on set the other day, and they said there’s a big girls’ band event being planned right now.
Hina: I think it’s called “Cross×Point Fes.” They haven’t done casting yet, but they plan to reach out to lots of agencies!
Sayo: So that means... Hina’s agency, and mine as well...?
Hina: Exactly! Hey, doesn’t that make you feel super boppin’♪!?
Sayo: ...It’s kind of moving.
Sayo: There were times I used to think, “If Roselia was part of the major scene, maybe we could appear at events where Pastel＊Palettes performs.”
Hina: Right! It reminded me of that talk we had back then! And now I’m thinking... maybe this time, we can actually make it come true!
Sayo: Yeah...
Sayo: (This is also another chance—a real, objective opportunity to “stand on the same stage as Hina.”...)
Sayo: (And yet, I still feel like something’s missing—)
Hina: Sis? Sis!
Sayo: Eh?
Hina: You were totally lost in thought~. I thought you’d be way more excited about this~.
Sayo: Sorry. I didn’t mean to come off that way...
Hina: Then what were you thinking about?
Sayo: I’m... not even sure myself.
Sayo: Just as you said—back then, it was out of reach, but now... the chance to make it happen is right in front of us.
Sayo: But even though the conditions are finally lining up for it to happen... I feel like I’m not ready for it yet.
Hina: Really? Why not?
Sayo: That’s what I’ve been trying to figure out. I just... have this vague feeling that something’s still missing...
Hina: Hmm... I think you’re overthinking it. You’ve always been perfect, sis!
Sayo: ...Sigh...
Sayo: You know... for you, us standing on the same stage might feel like something that could happen anytime. But for me, it’s a huge deal.
Hina: It’s a huge deal for me too!
Hina: That’s why I’ve been waiting. All this time.
Sayo: Hina... You’re right.
Sayo: I’ll definitely find whatever it is I’m still lacking. Just a little longer... please wait for me.
Hina: Okay. But this time, you're almost there, right!
Sayo: Fufu... Yeah.
Hina: Y’know, you never used to talk to me like this, sis.
Sayo: ......
Hina: I hope you keep sharing stuff like this with me~. I wanna hear what you’re thinking.
Sayo: ...Yeah.
Sayo: (...Back then, I forced myself to talk to Hina like "I had to face her.” Like I had to close off any escape route.)
Sayo: ...Thank you, Hina.
Hina: Huh? For what?
Sayo: No reason.
