Sayo: (I tried to stop using Hina as a benchmark.)

Sayo: (Just being a guitarist capable of performing at a venue the same size as Pastel＊Palettes’... that alone isn’t a good enough reason to stand beside her.)

Sayo: ...In that case, I’ll just have to find it.

[Later That Evening]

Sayo: —This is a good stopping point. Shall we take a break? I believe it’s been about two hours.
Rinko: ......
Yukina: ...Yeah. Let’s take 20 minutes.
Lisa: Okay. I’m heading to the convenience store downstairs. Anyone else coming?
Ako: Oh, I wanna go! Gonna grab some rice balls and bread!
Lisa: You’re always hungry, huh~ Anyone else need anything? All right, I’ll go pick them up.
Yukina: ...Rinko. Has something been bothering you lately?
Rinko: Eh... N-no, nothing really...
Yukina: I see...
Yukina: ...But since that last meeting, I’ve had the feeling that your heart hasn’t been at ease.
Sayo: I’ve felt something as well. That said, your playing hasn’t dropped in quality, so I couldn’t be sure...
Sayo: Did something happen with Udagawa-san...?
Rinko: ...No, with Ako-chan... there’s nothing at all...
Yukina: So it’s not about Ako...
Rinko: ...Um... it’s just...
Rinko: ...After Ako-chan made her declaration, she cried while talking about it, right...? Saying that her decision—that MC—might’ve hurt Tomoe-san...
Rinko: Ever since then... I haven’t been able to stop thinking about it... I keep wondering if it was my fault they both got hurt... because I was the one who pushed Ako-chan forward...
Rinko: Maybe I’m the one who hurt Tomoe-san... not Ako-chan...
Sayo: Shirokane-san...
Yukina: ...In the end, it was Ako who made that decision. It’s not your fault.
Rinko: But................
Rinko: ...I know. I know that it’s something Ako-chan and Tomoe-san have already worked through between them... I know that, but...
Rinko: Wanting to apologize to Tomoe-san... is that... wrong of me...?
Yukina & Sayo: ......
Yukina: I think, if that’s how you feel, then you should. Whether it’s a mistake or not... you won’t know until you speak with her.
Yukina: Sayo, what about you?
Sayo: ...Um, I’m sorry if this sounds harsh. Shirokane-san...
Sayo: If you do apologize, what meaning will it have for Tomoe-san...?
Rinko: Huh...?
Sayo: As you said, the issue between Udagawa-san and Tomoe-san has already been resolved as sisters. “Who pushed her forward” doesn’t matter anymore.
Sayo: Even so, the desire to apologize... that’s coming from your feelings, not for Tomoe-san’s sake, isn’t it?
Rinko: —...!
Sayo: ...This is just my personal view, but... if you’re going to apologize, it’s important to be aware of that.
Rinko: ...You’re absolutely right...
Sayo: And after that... it all comes down to what you want to do. Because you’re the only one who can truly honor your own feelings.
Rinko: What I want to do...
Yukina: I feel the same. Unless you see her face to face, I don’t think you’ll find peace with how you feel.
Rinko: ...Thank you both...
Rinko: I think, deep down, I just wanted to talk to Tomoe-san...
Rinko: Whether I apologize or not... I’ll decide that after I see her face... and feel what’s in my heart in that moment...
Sayo: Yes... I’m sure it’ll be okay. Tomoe-san is someone who always faces things head-on.
