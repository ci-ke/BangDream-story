event21-01 Opening: Ahead of Tempo

Hina and Sayo watch Pastel＊Palettes' show on TV. Sayo can feel what makes Hina's playing special, but...

(Character: Hina Hikawa, Sayo Hikawa)

[Hikawa Residence]

Hina: So then, when I was in class...
Sayo: (Ever since writing "I wish Hina and I could be open and honest with each other" on that tanzaku...)
Sayo: (... I think little by little, we've been spending more time together.)
Sayo: (Hanging out with her is not all that bad. I also end up learning new things when we talk sometimes.)
Hina: Sis, you weren't even listening to me just now!
Sayo: ... Huh?
Hina: Whenever you're in thinking mode, you go all statue-y and it looks like your brain is saying, "Hmm~," so I notice right away!
Sayo: Statue-y...? Did I really stop blinking for that long again?
Hina: You did~! C'mon~, listen to me~!
Sayo: (... I feel like I've gotten a little better at understanding what Hina is trying to say with her strange word choice.)
Hina: ... Welp, whatever. Hey hey, so today, the Pastel＊Palettes' concert is gonna air on TV! Let's watch it together!
Sayo: What...?
Hina: They said part of our show from the other day is gonna be played on TV! It's just a local channel though! Sis, watch it with me!
Sayo: (... Now that I think about it, I've pretty much never seen one of Hina's performances. Actually, if I wanted to be more precise...)
Hina: ... Is that a no? Are you busy?
Sayo: No. I finished my student council work for the day, so I have time.
Sayo: (It's just that I've been avoiding it. But I have to try and change a little bit at a time...)
Hina: Yay! There are about five minutes until it starts! I'll go get some drinks for us!
(Black cut)

[Five Minutes Later]

Hina: Ah! It started!! Wow~, look at all the people in the crowd! It's amazing~ seeing it like this!!
Sayo: Hina, take it down a couple notches.
Hina: It's just that I was thinking, this is what we look like if you watch from the crowd's point of view! Ah! It started!
Sayo: Sigh, Hina...
Hina: So when we were playing, the lights were super duper bright! They were blinding and hot... Playing was really tough. And then, Aya-chan...
Sayo: ...
Sayo: (Hina's performance is... just as I expected. She's extremely skilled... However, her tempo tends to be too fast, and her playing is assertive.)
Hina: Ah, look! Look! This is my guitar solo!
Sayo: (Still...)
Sayo: (She has a sound that seems to convey enjoyment... Her expression, and even her melody...)
Sayo: ...
Hina: Sis? Are you okay?
Sayo: ...! Sorry, I just...
Hina: I know. Thinking about something?
Sayo: No, it's okay. I'm alright...
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Sayo's Room]

Sayo: ...
Sayo: (Not wanting Hina to copy me. Not wanting her to best me... Those are the entire reasons I worked so hard to hone my skills, and still...)
Sayo: (Not only does she have the skill, but her sound has appeal. Even when she was jumping ahead of tempo, I could feel her personality shine through.)
Sayo: (... But what about me? Compared to Hina, what kind of sound do I possess...?)
