event293-02 Chapter 1: Don’t Be Late

While scouting for their next venue, Tsukushi and Nanami recall a recent incident...

(Character: Mashiro Kurata, Toko Kirigaya, Nanami Hiromachi, Tsukushi Futaba, Rui Yashio)

[The Next Day]

[Station Entrance]

Tsukushi: Hmm... maybe I got here a little too early?
Nanami: Ah, Tsu-chan’s already here~. I really thought this time I’d be the first one.
Tsukushi: Fufu, there’s still plenty of time before we’re supposed to meet. I only just got here myself.
Nanami: Oh, really? Glad I didn’t keep you waiting then~.
Nanami: Still, next time I’m gonna get here even earlier, so I won’t lose to you, Tsu-chan~.
Tsukushi: Geez, that would make setting a meeting time totally pointless, you know.
Nanami: Ahaha, okay, fair enough.
Tsukushi: But... are you sure? We’re just checking out the live house.
Nanami: Don’t worry about it. I didn’t have any plans today anyway~.
Nanami: Plus, seeing how hard you’ve been researching, I wanted to do something to help too.
Tsukushi: Fufu, I see. Thanks, Nanami-chan!
Nanami: Still, maybe we really are a bit too early this time~. I came at my usual pace, but it’s just the two of us today.
Tsukushi: Ahaha, true. Last time Rui-san got really mad, remember?
Nanami: Oh, you mean that time we went to see a live at RiNG, and Toko-chan showed up late?
(White cut)

[Station Entrance]

Tsukushi: Jeez! Toko-chan’s still not here yet!!
Nanami: Good thing we told everyone to meet earlier than the opening time. Showing up late to a live would be a disaster.
Mashiro: Y-you’re right...
Rui: In that case, we can simply leave Kirigaya-san behind. ...I wouldn’t mind that today.
Tsukushi: R-Rui-san’s mad...
Nanami: Well, she did warn her over and over yesterday...
Mashiro: Ah, Toko-chan just texted. “Almost there! Running like crazy!” she says...
Rui: ...Is that so.
Tsukushi: T-Toko-chan, hurry up, please~...!
Toko: ...Gahh~! I’m so sorry for being late! So, the thing is—
Rui: ...Kirigaya-san.
Toko: ...!
Rui: I told you yesterday—today’s live is one Toyama-san invited us to. I said we absolutely couldn’t be late.
Toko: Y-yeah, but there’s actually a reason—
Tsukushi: Toko-chan...!
Toko: ...I’m seriously sorry.
Rui: ...Then I won’t say any more. But there won’t be a next time.
(White cut)
Nanami: Rui-rui was scary that day~.
Tsukushi: Since then, it really seems like Toko-chan’s learned her lesson. Even when she’s about to be late, she always sends a message now!
Nanami: I heard later it was a family issue that time, so Rui-rui apologized for scolding her without asking first, but...
Tsukushi: It must’ve really scared her anyway...
Nanami: Man~, Rui-rui was giving off this aura back then. Even we got nervous and she wasn’t even mad at us.
Tsukushi: Right? Mashiro-chan was shaking...
Nanami: Oh, looks like our train’s coming. It’s one earlier than planned, but let’s hop on.
Tsukushi: Yeah, being late isn’t good!
