event293-01 Opening: Full of Motivation!

Tsukushi is full of energy for the next live performance—thanks to Mashiro’s inspiring words...

(Character: Mashiro Kurata, Toko Kirigaya, Nanami Hiromachi, Tsukushi Futaba, Rui Yashio)

[After School]

[Tsukinomori Girls' Academy - Class 2B]

Tsukushi: Right now, I’m thinking either this venue... or this one. Size-wise, these seem just right for Morfonica right now.
Tsukushi: But you can’t tell the real atmosphere from flyers alone, so I’ll probably visit both before deciding.
Nanami: I see~. As expected from you, Tsu-chan. You’re so thorough~.
Tsukushi: Heh, of course! I’m Morfonica’s leader, after all!
Nanami: But the real reason is for Shiro-chan, right?
Tsukushi: Of course!
Tsukushi: We’re gonna make Mashiro-chan’s dream of doing a joint gig with Poppin'Party come true!

[A Few Days Earlier]

[Hiromachi Residence - Atelier]

Nanami: Practice went well today too~. Right, Rui-rui?
Rui: Yes, I’d say today was decent.
Tsukushi: Wow~...!
Toko: Well, rare for you to give praise, Rui! So? Think Shiro’s ready yet?
Mashiro: ? Ready for what...?
Toko: For asking Poppin'Party for that joint gig! You said you wanted to after “Astrock,” remember?
Mashiro: Uh, um... I still think it’s too soon...
Toko: Ehh~!? Why?
Mashiro: Well, it’s just... um~...
Tsukushi: Hey, Toko-chan! Don’t push Mashiro-chan like that!
Toko: I mean, sure, but waiting too long wouldn’t be great either, right?
Rui: Kirigaya-san does have a point.
Toko: Wait, Rui’s taking my side!?
Rui: Not exactly. ...Kurata-san.
Mashiro: Y-yes!?
Rui: You once agreed with Hiromachi-san about “standing side by side.” But I wonder—what does that mean to you?
Rui: It was left vague back then, so I’d like to hear your definition now.
Nanami: R-Rui-rui~. that sounds kinda like an interrogation.
Rui: ...? I didn’t mean it that way.
Mashiro: Ahaha, it’s okay. I know what she means.
Mashiro: Hmm... I’m not sure if I can explain it well, but...
Mashiro: The reason I want to do a joint gig with Poppin'Party is because I want to see the same scenery Kasumi-san sees.
Mashiro: The first time I watched a Poppin’Party live show, I was so moved.
Mashiro: The moment they started playing, the air in the venue changed... the sound itself looked like it was shining...
Mashiro: It made me realize—this kind of world exists. This is what it means to be in a band.
Tsukushi: Fufu, that’s what inspired you to form Morfonica, huh.
Mashiro: Yeah... and as I kept playing with the band, I started to wonder what the world looked like from Kasumi-san’s perspective back then.
Mashiro: If I could stand on stage with them... what kind of view would I see?
Rui: ...
Mashiro: Of course, we’ve shared stages at joint lives before, but—
Mashiro: I was just wondering what kind of brilliance we’d see when the sound that taught me what ‘shining’ means and the sound we create together come as one.
Mashiro: It’ll be a different kind of scenery...
Mashiro: And for that to happen, I think our sound needs to shine just as brightly as Poppin'Party’s does.
Mashiro: ...S-sorry! I guess that came out all messy...
Tsukushi: No, not at all! Your feelings came through loud and clear, Mashiro-chan!
Nanami: So basically, it’s about whether you feel that your sound carries that same shine, right?
Mashiro: Yeah, maybe... Sorry, I guess it really depends on how I feel.
Toko: Eh, that’s fine, isn’t it?
Toko: Personally, I think Morfonica’s sound is just as strong! But since it’s your goal, Shiro, it wouldn’t mean much if you aren’t ready yet.
Rui: Agreed. I understand your intent, Kurata-san. I have no objections.
Mashiro: Toko-chan, Rui-san... thank you both.
Nanami: Then we’ve gotta practice even more~. Until Rui-rui says, “Perfect. Flawless.”
Toko: Isn't that more of an impossible standard!?
Mashiro: Ahaha...
Tsukushi: Mashiro-chan...
Tsukushi: (Mashiro-chan’s dream, huh... I really want to help her somehow.)
