event293-07 Ending: Memories Between Just Us Two

The two report their findings from the scouting trip—how will Mashiro and the others react...?

(Character: Mashiro Kurata, Toko Kirigaya, Nanami Hiromachi, Tsukushi Futaba, Rui Yashio)

[Hiromachi Residence - Atelier]

Tsukushi: —And that’s why I think our next live should be around here!
Mashiro: Wow, you’ve made so many notes. Even about the atmosphere of each venue and things that stood out to you...
Rui: It’s all organized really well. Did you make this, Futaba-san?
Tsukushi: Yep! Actually, on our last day off from practice, Nanami-chan and I went to check out the places in person!
Toko: Wow~, you two are seriously on top of things!
Nanami: Fufu, I got permission from the staff, so I even took some photos inside~.
Mashiro: I see... thank you both so much!
Toko: You two are amazing! Oh, and I went to that event I mentioned—and I got tons of inspiration for new outfits!
Nanami: That’s the one where everyone designs clothes based on a theme, right?
Toko: Yeah, yeah! Everyone’s got such great taste! So yeah, you can seriously look forward to our next outfits!
Tsukushi: Hehe, I can’t wait to see the new costumes. Mashiro-chan, you went out with your family, right?
Mashiro: Yeah, we went on a little family trip out of town. On the way back, I found a songwriting book that caught my eye, and they bought it for me.
Toko: Oh nice, Shiro’s fired up too~! And Rui, you went to a classical concert, didn’t you?
Rui: Yes. My parents were invited by a family acquaintance.
Rui: They performed arrangements of famous band songs in a classical style—it gave me some ideas for future Morfonica compositions.
Nanami: Wow, I see~. ...Fufu.
Mashiro: Nanami-chan? What’s up?
Nanami: Oh, nothing. I was just thinking—it makes me happy that even when we’re on break, everyone’s still thinking about Morfonica~.
Mashiro: Fufu, you’re right.
Toko: Obviously! We’ve gotta be fired up for our two-man with Poppin’Party!
Rui: In that case, I trust that the phrases I pointed out in today’s practice will be improved by next time?
Toko: O-of course! Totally fine!
Toko: Anyway, Shiro! Let’s decide where we’re gonna play next!
Mashiro: Eh, r-right now?
Rui: I don’t mind deciding our next live show, but if we rush it and Kurata-san is not satisfied, it won’t mean much, will it?
Toko: Well, I know that! But still—having a goal keeps us more motivated, right?
Mashiro: Y-yeah, that’s true. But if we decide, I want us all to choose together. It’s not just my goal, after all.
Tsukushi: Fufu, then let’s all look over the options after this and decide which live house we’ll go with!
Toko: Okay!
Rui: Yeah, I have no objections.
Nanami: Fufu, then I’ll go grab some drinks for everyone. Be right back~.
Tsukushi: Thanks, Nanami-chan!
Toko: So, what do you think? Any place that really clicked for you, Shiro?
Mashiro: Hmm... I think it’s between these two for me right now...
Nanami: I’m back~. I got us some tea~.
Mashiro: Thanks, Nanami-chan. Wow, it smells so nice...
Toko: ...Hey, wait a sec—Nanami, is that a new coaster? It’s cute!
Tsukushi: Ah...!
Nanami: Fufu, actually, this one is~...
Toko: What? What? Don’t tell me there’s a funny story behind it!?
Nanami & Tsukushi: ......
Nanami: Nope! I’ve decided—it’s a secret!
