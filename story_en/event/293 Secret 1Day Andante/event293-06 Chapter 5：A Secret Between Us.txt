event293-06 Chapter 5: A Secret Between Us

On the way home after a full day out, Tsukushi lets slip what’s on her mind...

(Character: Nanami Hiromachi, Tsukushi Futaba)

[Main Street]

Tsukushi: I-I’m just glad it’s finally done...
Nanami: Fufu, good work.
Tsukushi: I ended up needing a ton of help from both the staff and you, Nanami-chan.
Tsukushi: You even finished making a little plushie before me... and I still took longer...
Nanami: Ahaha, well, the staff insisted, so I kinda went along with it... But hey, you finished it all the way through, Tsu-chan—that’s awesome~!
Tsukushi: Heh! Well, I did my best!
Tsukushi: ...But still.
Nanami: Hm?
Tsukushi: At times like this, I can’t help but feel really frustrated... or maybe a little jealous.
Nanami: Tsu-chan...?
Tsukushi: Like when Mashiro-chan or Rui-san makes an amazing song, or when Toko-chan designs those cool outfits.
Tsukushi: Even today with the patchwork—I just think, I wish I had some kind of special talent like that too.
Nanami: ......
Tsukushi: Just kidding! Sorry! You probably don’t know what to say to that, huh?
Tsukushi: I guess since it’s just been the two of us all day, I ended up talking about stuff I normally wouldn’t.
Nanami: No, not at all. You don’t usually talk about stuff like this, so I’m happy I got to hear it.
Tsukushi: Fufu, that’s good then. ...Nanami-chan, do you ever get jealous of someone?
Nanami: I think... I’m jealous of you, Tsu-chan.
Tsukushi: Eh, me?
Nanami: Yeah. You always give everything your all—and that’s why everyone naturally wants to cheer you on...
Nanami: Even that staff member earlier, remember?
Tsukushi: Y-you think so...?
Nanami: You know... I’ve never been very good at communicating with people around me.
Nanami: So when I see everyone—Tsu-chan included—being so good at it, there are times I wish I could do that too.
Tsukushi: I see. I never got that impression at all, though...
Tsukushi: But I guess you’ve had your own struggles too, huh, Nanami-chan.
Nanami: ...Yeah, maybe I have.
Tsukushi: ...Fufu.
Nanami: Tsu-chan?
Tsukushi: Nothing, I just think it feels kind of new—talking about stuff like this with you.
Nanami: Yeah. We don’t usually have chances to, huh?
Tsukushi: Fufu, it feels a little embarrassing somehow.
Nanami: Totally. So let’s keep what we just talked about a secret between us, okay?
Tsukushi: Yeah—our little secret!
Nanami & Tsukushi: ...Fufu.
Nanami: Hey hey, Tsu-chan. Want to trade coasters?
Tsukushi: Huh?
Nanami: Let’s make them a memento of our day together. What do you think?
Tsukushi: Hmm... but compared to yours, mine looks a little clumsy... Is that really okay?
Nanami: Of course. What matters to me is that we made them together.
Tsukushi: Fufu... okay then—here!
Nanami: Yay~. I’ll treasure this forever~.
Tsukushi: Eh, isn’t that kind of an exaggeration?
Nanami: Not at all~. It’s a precious keepsake full of memories with my friend~.
Tsukushi: ...Fufu! Then I’ll treasure mine too!
