event96-02 Chapter 1: Loosen Up

Aya remains nervous as the lesson begins, but Chisato and Maya rise to the challenge.

(Character: Aya Maruyama, Hina Hikawa, Chisato Shirasagi, Maya Yamato, Eve Wakamiya)

[Talent Agency - Rehearsal Studio]

Aya: Okay, now let's toe our touches next... Huh? Touch our toes? Toe touches? Wait, which was it?
Chisato: Aya-chan, don't you think you're a bit too nervous? Your body looks much stiffer than usual.
Aya: Y-you think so...?
Hina: Now that you mention it, you're right! Aya-chan's normally way more flexible!
Maya: Do you want some help stretching? I can give you a push.
Aya: Huh!? No, no, I'm goo-
Maya: Okay, here we go~. One, two, three!
Aya: Ngh~, It... hurts...
Hina: Oh~! See? You can do it if you try! Lemme help too!
Eve: I want to join in as well!
Aya: H-Hina-chan? Eve-chan too!? Like I said, I'm fin-
Chisato: Then I suppose I'll also lend a hand.
Aya: Chisato-chan, not you too!
Hina: Alright, everybody... All together now. Ready, set!
Hina, Maya, Eve & Chisato: Go!
Aya: Wah, stop! No, no, no~! I can't take it anymore~!
Hina: So? Does your body feel a little looser?
Chisato: Fufu. Never mind her body, I'd say her expression has softened up considerably. That's the smile I'm used to seeing from you, Aya-chan.
Aya: My expression...? Wait, was that all to help me relax...?
Chisato: Even if there's a camera rolling, we're still here for you. Don't worry so much and be yourself.
Aya: Chisato-chan... Everyone... Thank you~!
Chisato: Fufu. Now then, shall we continue the shoot?
All: Yeah!
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[A Few Days Later]

[Filming Studio]

Aya: Um... So today's photo shoot is just you and me, right, Maya-chan?
Maya: That's right. We need pictures of myself with my interviewee, Aya-san!
Aya: ... Meanwhile, you're going to record the whole thing, right...? There's so much going on. I-I don't know if I can wrap my head around all this...
Maya: Huhehe, don't think about it too hard. You'll be fine if you just be yourself. The video camera's hidden, anyway.
Aya: Wh-what do you mean by that...?
Maya: Actually, we've been recording this whole time.
Aya: Huh!? This whole time!? Say that sooner~.
Maya: It's just like Chisato-san said before, we're here for you. Today, I'll be by your side the whole time, so just be yourself and you'll be fine.
Aya: Okay... I'll do my best to act like I always do! Ah, the shoot's starting. Let's go!
(Black cut)

[After the Photo Shoot]

Maya: Phew~, It's finally over~. Aya-san, how do you feel about  the shoot today?
Aya: I-I don't know~... More importantly, I didn't do anything weird, did I?
Maya: No, but that's what I wanted to ask you. Did you notice anything about my performance today that I should work on?
Aya: Huh? Did I notice anything...? No, I don't think so...
Maya: Ah, looks like we can check the photos over there. Why don't we take a look?
(Black cut)
Maya: Ooh~! Yep, no matter which one you look at, these photos are gorgeous. That's our Aya-san for you!
Maya: Wow, you've got such a mature expression in this one! Oh, this one's nice too. How should I put this...? It's such an innocent smile...
Aya: Ehehe, really? Well, I'm happy to hear that.
Maya: What were you thinking about during the shoot? It's pretty hard for me to be expressive, so if you have any tips or tricks, I'd love to hear them...
Aya: Hmm~... I don't know if you could call it a trick, but during photo shoots, I like to think back on a lot of things.
Maya: What do you mean by that...?
Aya: For example, in this photo, I was remembering a concert that Pastel＊Palettes did. For this one, I thought about the time we went to the ocean.
Aya: A while ago, I realized that my face just naturally changes when I recall all those memories during photo shoots...
Maya: Ooh~! Now that's some good advice! I see! I've never heard of that before!
Maya: Um, by the way...  When we took this photo where your cheeks are puffed out... What were you thinking about...?
Aya: U-um... That was... my English test...
Maya: I-I see. That makes sense...
Aya: Wh-what do you mean "that makes sense!?"
Maya: Ah, no, I didn't mean anything by that!
Maya: But seriously, I think the fans are gonna love what you just said! It's not every day you get to hear stuff like this!
Aya: ... Ah! That's right! We were recording this whole time! Uh, if my teacher is watching, I was kidding just now! I love English~!
(Black cut)
