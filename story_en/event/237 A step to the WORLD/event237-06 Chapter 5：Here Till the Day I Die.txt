event237-06 Chapter 5: Here Till the Day I Die

RAS gathers at CHU²'s for rehearsals, but CHU²'s expression is clouded...

(Character: Rei Wakana, Rokka Asahi, Masuki Sato, Reona Nyubara, Chiyu Tamade)

[CHU²'s Penthouse - Living Room]

CHU² (Chiyu): Looks like you're all here. Let's get on with rehearsals then.
PAREO (Reona): CHU²-sama, perhaps you should rest a little. You look terribly exhausted...
CHU² (Chiyu): Not a problem.
LAYER (Rei): How did the negotiations to squeeze us into the event go...?
CHU² (Chiyu): ...
LOCK (Rokka): Oh... Well, I'm sure there are alternatives! Please feel free to let us know if there's anything we can do!
CHU² (Chiyu): ... There isn't.
LOCK (Rokka): B-but you never know! We'll do whatever we can!
CHU² (Chiyu): Like I said, there's nothing you can do.
LOCK (Rokka): If we work together, though, maybe-
CHU² (Chiyu): ... Just drop it! When I say there's nothing, I mean there's nothing!
LOCK (Rokka): ...!
CHU² (Chiyu): I hit up every last place I could think of... and still came up empty!
CHU² (Chiyu): Everyone looked at me with pity because they thought I was being an unreasonable child who didn't know any better... They didn't even let me go on...!
LAYER (Rei): CHU²...
MASKING (Masuki): I see... Thanks, CHU².
MASKING (Masuki): Looks like we might have to get ready to take this one on the chin. So what, right? It's not like a few empty seats will change how we play on stage.
LOCK (Rokka): Exactly! While you were trying your best, CHU²-san, we've been rehearsing even harder than usual for the show, so...
CHU² (Chiyu): "Change the world..."
LOCK (Rokka): What...?
CHU² (Chiyu): We're meant to become the kind of band that changes the world and goes down in history... That's what I've been aiming for, at least.
CHU² (Chiyu): To make that happen, though, we need lots of people to listen to our music and acknowledge our sound.
CHU² (Chiyu): We don't have the time to dawdle. That's why I've gone all out from the start. And now, finally, our big chance to take our next step is right in front of us.
CHU² (Chiyu): But instead of being able to go for it, we're about to stumble when we can least afford to fall!
PAREO (Reona): CHU²-sama...
CHU² (Chiyu): ... I'm going to go and get some fresh air.
LOCK (Rokka): CHU²-san, wait!
LAYER (Rei): LOCK... Let her be for now.
LOCK (Rokka): B-but...
MASKING (Masuki): ...
LAYER (Rei): Masuki...? Hey, now's probably not the best time...!
(Black cut)
(Background change)
CHU² (Chiyu): ... What?
MASKING (Masuki): Uh~... I don't really know either. I just kinda came.
CHU² (Chiyu): Huh? Sheesh. You sometimes act with incredibly little forethought, don't you?
MASKING (Masuki): If I wanted your opinion, I'd have asked for it.
CHU² (Chiyu): ...
MASKING (Masuki): ...
MASKING (Masuki): Hey... I know what you said earlier, but I actually kinda think we're on track to "change the world."
CHU² (Chiyu): Huh? On what basis?
MASKING (Masuki): I dunno if it counts as a basis, but...
MASKING (Masuki): ... Take a look at this. You've been so busy you haven't been checkin' any social media, have you?
CHU² (Chiyu): Yeah, I haven't. So?
MASKING (Masuki): These are the posts by some fans who grabbed some of our flyers at Galaxy.
CHU² (Chiyu): "Can't wait for RAS's solo concert..." "I handed out lots of flyers at school..."
MASKING (Masuki): And I'm guessin' these ones are by the people who became our fans as a result of the contests we've been in.
CHU² (Chiyu): ... "I've been curious about RAS, so I grabbed a ticket." "Was torn between Midnight-Peaky and RAS, but I picked RAS."
CHU² (Chiyu): ...
MASKING (Masuki): There's still more. See? I mean, sure, just about everyone's talkin' about Midnight-Peaky, but there are still plenty of folks who are talkin' about us.
MASKING (Masuki): This counts as pretty good proof that we're on track to change the world, don't you think?
MASKING (Masuki): I mean, just look at all these people who are sayin' they like our music.
MASKING (Masuki): It's also a whole lot more than when we started out, wouldn't you say?
CHU² (Chiyu): ...
MASKING (Masuki): ... I've always believed in your whole "Change the world" thing, you know?
CHU² (Chiyu): What...?
MASKING (Masuki): Although the first time you said it, it did come across as a childishly naive dream. After all, bein' a backup drummer, I'd seen how harsh the music industry could be.
MASKING (Masuki): But once I joined RAS and we finished puttin' the crew together... I seriously started believin' that with all of us together, we could change the world.
MASKING (Masuki): That's what made me decide to stick with you. Doesn't matter where we go, even to hell. I've made up my mind to play the drums here till I die.
MASKING (Masuki): You're the one who made me feel this way, so stop tryin' to throw in the towel!
CHU² (Chiyu): I-I'm not...
MASKING (Masuki): Then stop lookin' at the ground!
MASKING (Masuki): There ain't no way for someone who always looks down to change the world!
CHU² (Chiyu): ...!
