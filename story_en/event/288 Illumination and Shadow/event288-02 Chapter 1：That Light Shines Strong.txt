MASKING comes to help with a studio recording, where her drumming skills are praised—but...

(Character: Masuki Sato)

[Studio]

Band Member: Man~, you really nailed it. Didn't think the recording would wrap up this fast.
MASKING (Masuki): Glad I could be of help!
Band Member: I saw the video of your guerrilla live behind JRF and thought, "These girls are seriously amazing." Totally made the right call bringing you in.
MASKING (Masuki): Thank you so much! You saw that live?
