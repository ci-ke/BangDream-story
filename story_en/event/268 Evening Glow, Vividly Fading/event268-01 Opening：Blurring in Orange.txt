event268-01 Opening: Blurring in Orange

A few weeks after GBT, only Moca and Himari remain on the rooftop, bathed in the setting sun.

(Character: Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara)

Moca: Hmm hmm hmm~.
Moca: Hmm... maybe I’ll tweak this phrase after all~.
Moca: From Ran... Ooh, bold use of flowers... that’s a masterpiece.
Moca: And... sent~ Alright, time to focus on this too~.
Himari: Haa, haa, haa... Sorry I'm late...!
Moca: Nah, it's totally fine~ So, what did the tennis club advisor say?
Himari: They scheduled a practice match. We just discussed the pairings and how the day will go.
Moca: Ooh, Vice Captain of the tennis club—very cool~.
Himari: Hehe, right~.
Himari: Oh, and on the way here, I ran into Tsugu and Tomoe.
Moca: Wasn’t Tsugu super busy with student council stuff?
Himari: Yeah... she still looked swamped.
Himari: Tomoe's busy too—she’s started practicing for a new dance routine.
Moca: Ahh, the one she said she’d choreograph herself?
Himari: Yep, that’s the one. And I’m sure she’s got her hands full training juniors too.
Moca: .........
Moca: Oh yeah, Hii-chan. Did you check the group chat?
Himari: Ah, I think I saw a notification. Let me see... Wow, it’s beautiful...!!
Moca: The latest masterpiece from Artist Mitake~. Ever since we asked her to share them, she’s been sending us every one~.
Himari: Yeah... Ran and everyone, they’re all doing their best. Always looking forward and moving on.
Himari: Ughh, why am I like this? We’re all working hard in our own ways...
Himari: What should I reply... “I can feel your emotion through your work”... Sounds too much like song feedback.
Himari: “How you're doing your best, even if I don’t really get flower arranging”... That kinda sounds cold...
Himari: Ugh... hmm...
Moca: Why not just keep it same as always~? Like you said at first: “It’s super pretty!”
Himari: Yeah... you’re right!
Himari: ...Sent! So, what about you, Moca? What were you working on?
Moca: Great composer Moca-chan is in the middle of writing a new song~.
Himari: I see... and how’s the great composer’s progress going?
Moca: I’m waiting for the ultimate melody to descend~.
Moca: I mean, there’s no deadline, so there’s no need to rush~.
Himari: That’s true, but still...
Himari: How about setting a goal to get at least one chorus done by the end of this week?
Moca: Hmm... I guess I could. But why?
Himari: Well, we’re a band, right? So let’s write more songs and play more lives! Afterglow is all about live shows! So that’s why—
Moca: Hii-chan.
Himari: .........
Himari: ...I don’t know if this is really okay.
Himari: We used to find live shows we wanted to join and write new songs for them. We had goals.
Himari: But now we’re just sitting here, playing instruments and watching the sunset.
Himari: For the first week or so, that was enough. Just having this place still here.
Himari: But now... sometimes the sunset makes it harder. No matter how long we wait, it’s just the two of us. Everyone else is somewhere else.
Moca: Well, everyone’s fired up in their own way these days~
Himari: I know that. And I don’t want to get in the way of anyone working hard.
Himari: But... don’t you feel lonely, Moca?
Moca: ...Yeah. I do. But I decided not to let Afterglow fade away. So I’ll stay here—with the loneliness too.
Moca: Besides, you’re here, Hii-chan. We can share the loneliness~.
Himari: Moca...
Himari: Hey, Moca. Why don’t we play a live show?
Moca: Huh... just the two of us?
Himari: Two’s fine too, but let’s talk to Tsugu and Tomoe about it.
Himari: If we decide to do a show, I bet your new song will come together too. And then maybe...
Himari: We’ll finally be able to move forward...!
Moca: .........
Himari: I’m not good at putting it into words, but I feel like playing a live show will change something. If we don’t change... we can’t move forward.
Himari: Afterglow means challenging ourselves—always moving forward.
Moca: ...Yeah... you’re right. Looks like it’s time for us to get fired up too~
Himari: Then...!
Moca: Let’s do this live show~!
