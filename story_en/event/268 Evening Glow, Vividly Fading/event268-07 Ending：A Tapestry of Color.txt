event268-07 Ending: A Tapestry of Color

While looking back on the live show atop the rooftop, the sound of a door creaks open—

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa)

[The Next Day]

Tsugumi: Did you add an arrangement just now? Um, rewind ten seconds... here.
Moca: Nah, isn't it just Hii-chan messing up as usual~? It's all over your face~.
Himari: Ugh... sorry.
Tomoe: Right after finishing, I thought it went pretty well, but listening back calmly, the rough spots really stand out.
Moca: For our first live in a while, this was more than good enough~.
Ran: ...Good work.
Himari: Ran...!? What's up?
Ran: There's something I want you all to see.
Tomoe: ...This... these are lyrics written by Ran.
Tsugumi: Wow... these are new lyrics, right?
Ran: Yeah. I wrote them after watching your live yesterday.
Moca: ...So you kept writing lyrics, Ran.
Ran: No. After I started focusing on flower arrangement, I stopped... I couldn't write anymore.
Ran: But... the words I wanted to say to everyone overflowed, and this was the only way I could organize them...
Himari: I see, so that's why... I thought these lyrics felt like they were about us.
Ran: Yeah... I knew you'd understand.
Ran: The reason I'm who I am now, the reason I could turn my feelings into songs and perform them with you all... it's all thanks to you.
Ran: Because I got to become Afterglow with you... I finally realized how colorful my life has been.
Ran: I put everything in here—everything that's happened and how I feel now—so I want Ultra Violet to have it.
Ran: I want you to turn these lyrics into a song.
Moca: ...This feels nostalgic~. Your feelings come through completely in these lyrics.
Tomoe: Yeah. These are Ran’s words, but they're filled with “us.”
Tsugumi: Yeah... it’s kind of overwhelming...
Himari: Ran, thank you. Let's turn this into a song together and make it a treasure for all five of us.
Ran: Yeah...
Ran: .....
Ran: I know I'm being selfish. I want you to turn my personal feelings into a song to get closure.
Ran: But... if I don’t do this, I feel like I’ll fall apart...!
Tsugumi: Ran-chan...?
Ran: Writing lyrics and turning them into songs with everyone... that became a part of who I am.
Ran: Even though it’s something precious I can’t separate from, I tried to cut it clean and move on...
Ran: But I couldn't forget the days I spent with Afterglow, so I kept staring at my dusty guitar... ever since GBT ended... all this time...
Ran: While writing the lyrics, I kept thinking: why didn’t I realize this sooner? I missed everyone’s sound, kept remembering our lives together...
Ran: And I realized... I'm the one who's fading away...!
Moca: ...
Ran: I couldn’t see myself, or anyone else. I know saying sorry won’t fix anything.
Ran: Even so... I’m really sorry...! I took advantage of you, hurt you, did whatever I wanted—
Tomoe: Ran. We get it.
Ran: Tomoe...
Tomoe: We’ve been chasing all kinds of goals, like making legends and stuff.
Tomoe: But I don’t think becoming a legend is about being recognized or praised by others.
Tomoe: We didn’t win GBT, but we gave it everything we had. We burned so bright there’s nothing left, and even the disbandment—everything—will stay with us.
Tomoe: But what really matters is what we did in those moments. What we felt. Whether we shared those things as five people.
Tomoe: Our band activities are like a diary to prove we lived together.
Tomoe: So, Ran... how about we take it easy and just enjoy playing in a band again?
Moca, Himari & Tsugumi: ...!
Ran: Everyone...
Ran: I want to live alongside all of you... Please let me sing with Afterglow again... for as long as I can, always...!
(White cut)
(Background change)
Himari: Yeah... yeah...! Waaaahh, I’m so glad...!!
Moca: Ahhh, this is one of the biggest cries in recent years~.
Himari: I just... I was really lonely~...!! But... I'm so happy...!!
Ran: Really... I'm sorry. I don’t think even you cried like this since we were little.
Moca: Hima-leader, you did a great job~.
Tsugumi: Sniff... Himari-chan, you always said it, didn’t you...? “It’ll be okay,” “It’s definitely going to be okay”... and you really made it true...!
Tomoe: Haha... look at us all falling apart again. But Leader, we’ve gotta start turning our treasure into something real now, right?
(White cut)
(Background change)
Himari: Sniff... huh...?
Moca: We’ve got to put music to the lyrics Ran wrote~.
Himari: ...Right...! But I'm just so happy...!
Moca: Can’t be helped~. Then, to our Afterglow Ran-kun, a word from Master Composer Moca-chan...!
Ran: Eh... what?
Moca: These lyrics... “not bad”~.
Tsugumi: ! Yeah, not bad...! They’re beautiful, Ran-chan!
Ran: ...Thank you, everyone.
(White cut)
(Background change)

[Several Weeks Later]

Tsugumi: It's really hyped in here...!
Tomoe: But we’ll just do our own performance. Right, Ran?
Ran: ...Huh? Yeah.
Moca: Oh ho~? Ran, are you super nervous right now~?
Ran: Shut up. It’s been a while, so of course I’m nervous.
Himari: Ran, your greeting is “Good evening, we’re Afterglow.” Not Ultra Violet!
Moca: You’re not Hii-chan, so I’m sure you’ll be fine~.
Himari: Everyone acts like that when they’re nervous!
Tomoe: Come on, it’s our turn soon.
Himari: Ah, wait! We've really gotta do it today!
Himari: Then everyone... let’s play our best "same as always" tonight!
Himari: Hey, hey, hoh~!
Ran, Moca, Tomoe & Tsugumi: ......
Himari: Ehhh, I thought we were really gonna do it this time~! It’s Afterglow’s comeback live, you know!?
Ran: Hehe... isn't this what's “same as always”?
Tsugumi: Himari-chan, looks like Ran’s nerves are gone, so...!
Moca: Nice job, Hii-chan~.
Tomoe: Ran, don’t mess up the intro~.
Ran: I know, I know.
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Ran: Good evening, we’re Afterglow. Let’s start with our first song.
