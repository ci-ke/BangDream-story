event268-03 Chapter 2: Purple Sunset

The four begin preparing for a new challenge, but stumble on their very first step.

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa)

[The Next Day]

Himari: Here’s the entry form for Girl Jam.
Moca: Ooh, it’s been a while since I’ve actually looked at one of these~.
Tomoe: Last year we were invited, so we didn’t need to apply or go through screening.
Tsugumi: Then this is the first time since our debut show.
Tomoe: Yeah. Never thought we’d be filling it out with just four of us this time.
Himari & Tsugumi: ...
Moca: Hey now, Tomo-chin~. This is where you go all in~.
Tomoe: Ah, sorry, sorry...! You’re right!
Tomoe: Alright, then let’s go for it! First item... band name...
All: .........
Himari: Should we write “Afterglow”...?
Tomoe: ...That’s the only option, right? We’re still...
Tsugumi: Yeah, since we’re doing this to keep Afterglow going, it makes sense to use the name, but...
Himari: It does take some courage, huh...
Moca: C’mon, I think we all feel the same, but no matter what, I’m always Afterglow~.
Moca: But if everyone’s feeling unsure, we don’t have to force ourselves to use the name.
Moca: We could go with something different this time~ Like...
Moca: Like “Ultra Violet”~.
Tomoe: Pfft... haha! That takes me back! That was the word you found when we were choosing our band name.
Himari: Right! We were looking up cool English words in the dictionary.
Tsugumi: Ultra violet is UV rays, right? Should we go with that?
Moca: Let’s do it~ It feels easier not to overthink it.
Himari: Yeah...! Let’s enter as Ultra Violet this time!
Tsugumi: Even if we end up on the smallest stage at Girl Jam, I think that’ll be more than enough!
Tomoe: Honestly, that might be the perfect place for us. What about the song?
Himari: How about going instrumental? We’ve always been about our instruments—maybe we make a track without vocals.
Tsugumi: I like that. We’ve only done songs with vocals so far, so writing one like this will be a new challenge...!
Tomoe: Let’s give it a try. Time to take on something new.
Himari: Okay, then I’ll write it down. Band name: Ultra Violet—
Ran: Huh, you’re all still here?
Himari, Tomoe & Tsugumi: Ran... Ran-chan...
Moca: Heyyy, Ran~ Missed us, huh~?
Ran: No, I just forgot my textbook... What are you guys doing?
Tomoe: Ah... this. We’re thinking of performing at Girl Jam.
Ran: Wait...
Himari: After GBT, we hadn’t done anything band-related. So we looked for a live event and found this one.
Tomoe: We were just filling out the entry form. Not that we’re sure we’ll be accepted or anything.
Tsugumi: We were planning to talk to you once things were confirmed.
Ran: I see... That’s really great. Good luck.
Moca: Hey hey, Ran, don’t you notice anything~?
Ran: Huh? Notice what...?
Moca: C’mon—the band name~
Ran: Wait, Ultra Violet...!?
Himari: Right!? Isn’t that nostalgic!?
Ran: Haha, it really is...! But why that?
Moca: Well, I never really gave up on it~ I figured if it was ever gonna see the light of day, this was the time~.
Ran: That’s definitely not true.
Tsugumi: Haha... Honestly, we just couldn’t bring ourselves to write “Afterglow.” It didn’t feel quite right.
Tomoe: Plus, Ultra Violet’s the kind of name we’d never get to use otherwise. And it sounds cool—strong, even.
Ran: What, like a special attack or something?
Moca: Tomo-chin’s a natural-born hero~. On stage she’ll shout the attack name before we start playing~.
Tomoe: No way, that’d be so embarrassing...! Even if we’re Ultra Violet, I’m still counting in like normal.
Ran: Sounds good to me—because you are a hero... haha.
Himari: (I’m so glad... Ran’s smiling. When was the last time the five of us talked about the band and she smiled like that?)
Himari: (...I wonder if she’d come watch us if we invited her to the show... But she’s working hard on something else right now...)
Tsugumi: —Himari-chan?
Himari: Huh? Sorry, what?
Tomoe: You spaced out for a second.
Himari: Ah... just thinking about something! ............
Himari: Hey, hey Ran... if you’re okay with it...
Ran: ?
Himari: Um...
Tomoe: Hey, how about we all hit a family restaurant after this?
Tsugumi: Yeah, it’s been a while since we’ve done something like that! Ran-chan, do you have flower arrangement practice?
Ran: Not today. I was just planning to read through my textbook at home.
Moca: Then let’s go~. I’m getting hungry anyway~.
Tomoe: C’mon, Himari. We can finish the form later. Let’s go.
Himari: ...Yeah! Let me clean this up real quick—wait for me!
