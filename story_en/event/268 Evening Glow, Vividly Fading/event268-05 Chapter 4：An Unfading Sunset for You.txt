event268-05 Chapter 4: An Unfading Sunset for You

Himari and the others finally finish their song. What decision does Himari make after hearing Tsugumi’s words—?

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa)

[A Few Days Later]

Moca: Ooh~. Looks like we’ve got it~
Himari: Yeah, that one just now felt really good!
Tomoe: Yeah...! finally felt like it clicked.
Tsugumi: It really did...! Like when we used to toss around our favorite phrases and build a song together...
Moca: That was one of Moca-chan’s genius inventions too~. My own talent scares me sometimes~.
Tomoe: What scares me is how confident you are saying stuff like that.
Tsugumi: We really made a song...!
Himari: Tsugu...?
Tsugumi: Honestly, I was a bit nervous. We’ve never done an instrumental band, and now it’s just the four of us... I couldn’t see the final picture.
Tsugumi: But we did it...! I finally feel like I’m moving forward on my own two feet.
Tsugumi: Now I feel like I can finally keep my promise to Ran-chan...!
Moca: Yep. Not bad at all, Tsugu~.
Tsugumi: Hehe, thanks Moca-chan.
Tsugumi: From now on, I’ll keep doing what I can, step by step. I want to be someone Ran-chan can be proud of—someone I can be proud to be in front of her.
Himari: Someone Ran can be proud of, huh...
Himari: Hey everyone... Can I talk to you about something?
Tomoe: What’s up?
Himari: I want to invite Ran to the live show. But... I just couldn’t bring myself to say it.
Himari: I kept wondering if it was okay to talk about the band in front of her... if it would hurt both of us... so I hesitated.
Himari: But when we went to the family restaurant, I had such a great time! Just laughing and talking about band stuff with everyone—it made me so happy...!
Himari: It felt like our new version of “normal,” and I realized nothing has changed—we’re still close childhood friends.
Himari: That’s why I want to show her at the live that we haven’t faded. That we’re still us. What do you all think?
Tomoe: Yeah... I think you’re right. I want to invite Ran too.
Tsugumi: Me too. I don’t know if she’d even want to watch us now that she’s not in the band anymore...
Tomoe: But our feelings for each other haven’t changed, right?
Moca: Exactly~. And hey, there’s no harm in inviting her~
Moca: I think rather than overthinking it and not inviting her, we should just go for it~.
Tomoe: Right...! We’re all about charging forward, after all!
Himari: Thanks, everyone...! I’m going to invite her!
Tsugumi: If Ran really does come, then I’ll work even harder to give the best performance possible!
Moca: Tsugu’s being Tsugurific again—Hii-chan, it’s all on you now~.
Himari: Yep, leave it to me!
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Later That Night]

Himari: .........
Ran: “Hello?”
Himari: Sorry for calling out of the blue. Are you busy right now?
Ran: “I’m fine... but a call? That’s rare.”
Himari: Right? I just felt like hearing your voice today.
Ran: “We talk every day at school, though.”
Himari: Yeah, but sometimes you want to hear someone’s voice on the phone~. Also... I’m calling to invite you to something.
Himari: Ran, would you come to our upcoming live?
Ran: “...Huh?”
Himari: We just finished a new song today. It’s totally different from our usual Afterglow stuff—and we’re even using a different band name...
Himari: But I think this next live will be the first step in us continuing on as Afterglow. I want you to see us take that step.
Himari: I’m not asking you to cheer us on or anything like that—I just...
Himari: I want you to see our sunset that hasn’t faded.
Ran: .........
Himari: Of course, only if you’re up for it. But if you feel like it...
Ran: “I’ll go. And of course I’ll be cheering you on.”
Himari: Really!? I’m reserving your ticket right now, okay? No take-backs!
Ran: “Got it. Maybe I’ll send flowers to Ultra Violet, like my dad used to.”
Himari: Ahaha, the others would love that! But just having you there is more than enough!
Ran: “Alright, then just send me the details later.”

Ran: (And if it does... would that feel kind of lonely too?)
Ran: ......
