event268-04 Chapter 3: Dust-Covered Red

It’s been a while since the five of them spent time together after school. Every little word brings back memories...

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa)

Tomoe: Ooh, Moca’s looks good.
Moca: This is a new hot sandwich~. Not sharing though~.
Tsugumi: Here you go, Ran-chan. Fork.
Ran: Thanks. Himari, want a bite of my gâteau au chocolat?
Himari: Yay, thanks!  Munch... Huh, it’s kind of bitter?
Ran: Really? I like it that way—bitter.
Moca: Ran, you never waver on your food preferences~ I’ll take the whipped cream~
Ran: Hey—don’t just... well, I wasn’t gonna use it anyway.
Ran: It’s been a while since we’ve hung out like this.
Himari: Yeah... lately we haven’t really gotten together outside of school.
Ran: Yeah... how’s everyone been? Busy?
Himari: Not much has changed for me... I go to club, cram school, and practice with Moca on the rooftop.
Tsugumi: I fell behind on student council work, so I’ve been trying to catch up.
Tomoe: Club and my part-time job. Guess we’ll be adding band practice soon, too.
Himari: It’s gonna be tough getting back into the groove... we’ll have to practice a lot—
Himari: Ah... we’ve been talking all about ourselves.
Ran: No, it’s fine. What about the song for Girl Jam?
Himari: We decided to go instrumental this time. So Moca’s working on a track without vocals.
Ran: Oh yeah? What kind of vibe is it?
Moca: Hmm, I’m in the middle of receiving a genius phrase~.
Ran: Moca, make sure you write it in your notebook properly this time. You always lose your printouts.
Moca: Okaaay, I know I know~.
Tsugumi: Haha... If it’s too much to keep track of, I’ll help you organize it.
Tomoe: Ran, how’s flower arrangement going? You’ve been sending us pics.
Ran: Yeah. Thanks to you all, I’ve been really focused since GBT ended.
Ran: When I’m arranging flowers, it feels like my senses sharpen. It’s exhausting, though.
Tsugumi: The piece you sent yesterday was gorgeous. That kind of flower’s a rare pick, isn’t it?
Ran: Yeah, it was made using preselected flowers. You caught that, huh?
Moca: You’re underestimating the power of our friendship~.
Ran: Ugh, don’t say stuff like that—it’s embarrassing.
Tomoe: Haha, but you kind of get it, right? The meaning behind the flowers and all.
Himari: Yeah. We can tell what Ran would like.
Ran: I see...
Himari: C’mon, Ran~! If you’re happy, just say you’re happy!
Tsugumi: If you’re happy, we’re happy too!
Ran: I-I said stop, it’s embarrassing...!
All: Fufu...
Ran: ...Fufu...
