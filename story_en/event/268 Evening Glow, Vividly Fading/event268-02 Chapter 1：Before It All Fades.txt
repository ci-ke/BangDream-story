event268-02 Chapter 1: Before It All Fades

To talk to Tomoe and Tsugumi about joining the live show, Himari brings something with her—

(Character: Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa)

[A Few Days Later]

Himari: So after checking out all the options, I really think this one’s the best!
Moca: Ooh... Girl Jam, huh? That brings back memories~.
Himari: Yeah, it was the first big event we ever played! I thought maybe Tsugu and Tomoe would want to join if it’s this one.
Moca: I'm totally in too~.
Himari: Then let’s go talk to them right away.
Moca: So, what are their plans today~?
Himari: Tsugu’s doing document filing for the student council, and Tomoe’s probably at club.
Himari: I’ll message them to meet us on the rooftop once they’re done—let’s get some practice in until then!
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Two Hours Later]

Tsugumi: Sorry for being late, Moca, Himari.
Tomoe: You said you wanted to talk—what’s up?
Himari: Thanks for coming even though you’re both busy. It’s more of a proposal than a problem.
Moca: Take a look at this~.
Tomoe: Wait... an announcement for Girl Jam? Don’t tell me you two...
Himari: Yeah. Would you and Tomoe want to join us?
Tomoe & Tsugumi: .........
Tsugumi: So... you mean just the four of us?
Himari: Yeah, that’s what we’re thinking. Moca’s already working on a new song for the show.
Moca: And we’ll arrange it same as always—all of us together~.
Tomoe: ...A new song and a live show... that’s amazing...
Moca: Tomo-chin, are you not in the mood for band stuff right now?
Tomoe: ...I don’t know...
Tomoe: We made our decisions as five, and... I just can’t picture doing this without all five of us.
Tsugumi: Same here. I thought I’d sort out my feelings once things calmed down with student council, but...
Himari: I see...
Tsugumi: I’m sorry...! I hadn’t really thought about the band seriously until you two brought it up...
Moca: Nah, it’s okay. Sometimes you’re just not in the mood to think about that kind of stuff.
Tomoe & Tsugumi: .........
Tomoe: But yeah... maybe it is time to start thinking about what’s next.
Tomoe: I haven’t played drums at all lately. I just haven’t felt like it.
Tomoe: And instead of facing that, I kept focusing on other things.
Tomoe: Meanwhile, you two were already trying to move forward...
All: .........
Himari: ...It’s not like we’ve figured everything out either. We just want to take that first step.
Himari: We don’t really know what kind of show this’ll turn out to be, or what we want from it.
Himari: But if we don’t do anything now, I feel like the “us” who were in a band will just fade into the past...
Himari: If we don’t take that step, we’ll become the “fading versions of ourselves” Ran talked about...!
Himari: So why don’t we just try? And if, after this, it still feels wrong to perform with just the four of us, then just say it!
Tomoe & Tsugumi: .........
Tsugumi: ...You’re always the one who gives me the courage to take that first step, Himari.
Himari: Huh?
Tsugumi: I think I was scared of facing Afterglow without Ran.
Tsugumi: Even though we promised to keep running together... I can’t keep going like this.
Tsugumi: I want to be someone who can stand tall in front of Ran, who’s still doing her best. That’s why I’ll play at Girl Jam too.
Himari: Tsugu...!
Tomoe: Man... all my childhood friends are seriously cool.
Tomoe: I was scared too. That’s why I kept avoiding looking at how Afterglow’s changed...
Tomoe: I told myself I’d protect what shouldn’t change—but I’ve been holding us back.
Tomoe: I’ll play at Girl Jam too—so I can protect the sunsets we all watched together.
Moca: Tomo-chin, that was cool~.
Tomoe: Haha, not even. I just mean I’ll work hard so I can become someone who’s cool.
Tsugumi: Yeah...! If we all take the first step together, there’s nothing to be afraid of.
Himari: You’re right...! Let’s all give it our best—together!
