event11-05 Chapter 4: A Place Full of Memories

A bird steals Hina's tanzaku, and the girls chase after it. When they finally stop, they find themselves somewhere they've been to many times before...

(Character: Hina Hikawa, Sayo Hikawa)

[Park]

Hina: Pant... Pant...
Hina: Ohh~... I was on its tail all the way here, but now I have no idea where it went...
Sayo: Pant... Pant... That's why I told you, you can't chase a bird. There's no way you could catch it... Huh?
Sayo: Hina. Isn't that the tanzaku in the grass over there?
Hina: What...? Oh, it is! Yay~!!
Hina: I bet it heard how much I wanted this back in my voice and dropped it for me!
Sayo: I'm not so sure about that... Anyway, good thing you found it.
Hina: Yeah! ... Phew... But now I'm beat from running around.
Hina: Hey, Sis. Why don't we rest here for a bit?
Sayo: ... Makes sense. I haven't run like that in a while. I'm exhausted.
Hina: Yay! Okay, okay! Let's sit on that bench!
(Black cut)
Sayo: ...
Hina: ...
Sayo: ...
Hina: ...

Hina: Ah... Sorry...
Sayo: It's fine...

Sayo: Hina, since when do you get nervous...? Ever since we were kids, that's never been your thing.
Hina: Yeah, you're right. Usually, I don't... But for some reason, today... I am.
Sayo: (Since we were little, Hina has never been the type to get nervous. I don't know if it's because she's just really relaxed or she's fearless...)

Sayo: You always went higher. So high that everyone watching would start to worry...
Hina: Ehehe... Really?
Sayo: ... Sigh. That part you don't remember.
Sayo: (But I do because I was jealous that you could swing higher than I could.)
Hina: That's fine! We were just kids, anyway! I was happy because I could play with you every day.

Sayo: ... That's...

Sayo: ...? I see...

Hina: Aww, already...?
Sayo: Don't you want to hang that up?
