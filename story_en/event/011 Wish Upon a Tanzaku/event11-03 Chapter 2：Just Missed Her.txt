event11-03 Chapter 2: Just Missed Her

Hina heads downtown on her own. When it begins to rain, she takes cover in a nearby store and runs into a familiar face...

(Character: Kanon Matsubara, Aya Maruyama, Hina Hikawa, Sayo Hikawa)

[Downtown]

Hina: Wow~, it's so crowded! But it looks like a lot of fun! Where should I start...? I gotta go to the most boppin' place first...
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Hina: ... No no, not rain! Aw man~! And I don't even have an umbrella~!
Hina: Alright, gotta find a place where I can wait for it to stop!!
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Fast Food Store]

Aya: Welcome! ... Oh, Hina-chan!
Hina: Huh? Aya-chan! Whatcha doin'?
Aya: I work part-time here. I haven't been able to come recently though...
Hina: Oh, okay. Well, I'm a customer♪ Please let me take shelter from the rain~.
Aya: Yes, of course! Rain on the day of the Star Festival, though... That's unfortunate. Hina-chan, did you come with someone?
Hina: ... No. I invited my sister, but she said she wasn't interested.
Aya: Oh. That's too bad...
Hina: Well, nothing I can do, right~? Anyway~, do you have any recommendations, Aya-chan?
Aya: Hmm... Oh yeah, we're having an up-fries campaign! You can get a large fries for the price of the medium!
Hina: Sweet, that's a boppin' deal! Alright, I'll have that and a soda.
Aya: Got it~! One large fries and a soda!
Kanon: O-okay~! Coming right up!
Hina: Huh, is that Kanon-chan? She works here too, huh?
Aya: Yep, that's right! We'll have your fries done in no time, so please wait at your table and we'll bring it to you.
(Black cut)
Hina: ... Sigh, the rain stopped, but I can't get into the festival mood...
Hina: I guess I'll go home now...
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Downtown]

Sayo: ... The streets are really busy. I forgot today was the Star Festival... I should buy what I'm looking for and get back home.
Sayo: (Hina... I wonder if she's here with anybody.)
Sayo: (I knew this crowd would stress me out.)
Sayo: Sigh...
Sayo: Huh? That's Hina over there...
Sayo: Hina...! She can't hear me over all of these people...!
(Black cut)
Sayo: Sigh... Where did she run off to?
Aya: Oh, Sayo-chan!
Sayo: Maruyama-san. Matsubara-san.
Aya: Good afternoon. Is everything okay?
Sayo: I'm just looking for something my mom asked for... More importantly, did Hina invite you to the festival, Maruyama-san?
Aya: Me? No, I was scheduled to work. And, you know, Hina-chan was disappointed that she wasn't able to go with you, actually.
Sayo: She said that?
Aya: Mhm. She told me when she stopped by the store to get out of the rain.
Sayo: I see... I can't really deal with crowds or things like festivals, so I told her she should invite someone else, like you, but I guess not...
Aya: Sayo-chan. I'm sure Hina-chan just wanted to go with you, not me, or anyone else!
Kanon: M-mhm...! I think so too. She was looking kinda sad when we saw her earlier…
