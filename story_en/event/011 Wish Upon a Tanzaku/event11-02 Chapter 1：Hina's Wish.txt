event11-02 Chapter 1: Hina's Wish

Sayo comes home, and Hina is waiting to invite her to the Star Festival. What will Sayo say...?

(Character: Hina Hikawa, Sayo Hikawa)

[Hikawa Residence - Living Room]

Sayo: I'm home.
Sayo: (... Once I finish dinner I have to get to guitar practice. Then I'll go through the student council paperwork...)
Hina: Sis~, hi!!
Sayo: Hina... Hey.
Hina: So, uhm...! I've been waiting for you! There's something I wanted to ask you about!
Sayo: What's that?
Hina: Uh-uhm, well...! Will you go to the Star Festival downtown with me?
Sayo: (...! This is what Imai-san was talking about...)
Hina: It's this weekend! It's all everyone's been talking about at school.
Hina: Including me! I've been boppin' around waiting for it!
Sayo: ... R-really...?
Hina: S-so, uhh... I was thinking... Could we go together?
Sayo: There's gonna be a lot of people at this festival, right?
Hina: Uhm, well... Yeah, but...
Hina: Still! Th-there are gonna be these really cool lights, and the people downtown are working super hard! You'd be boppin' too~!
Sayo: I-I don't like crowded areas...
Hina: Uhh... B-but...!
Hina: I heard there are gonna be a bunch of booths! Like with candy apples and yakisoba and some shooting games... And, uhh...
Hina: Ah! And there's supposed to be a parade, too!
Sayo: A parade...?
Hina: Mhm! They said we'll see kids marching along dressed like Orihime and Hikoboshi!
Hina: It's gonna be so cute! Makes me feel all tingly~! I would love to see it...
Sayo: ... Sorry, but I think I'll pass. Why don't you invite someone else?
Hina: Sis...
Sayo: I'm sure it'd be more fun if you went with Imai-san or someone from your band, like Maruyama-san.
Sayo: Like I said, I don't like being around so many people, and I'm not much of a fan of food stands anyway.
Hina: ... Oh... Sis, I'm sorry for trying to pressure you...
Sayo: It's fine. Alright, I have stuff to do. I'll be in my room.
Hina: ...
Hina: But I really wanna go with her...
Hina: Sigh...
