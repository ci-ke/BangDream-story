event221-04 Chapter 3: Wavering

Yukina struggles with the meaning behind being "truly Roselia."

(Character: Yukina Minato, Rinko Shirokane)

[Minato Residence - Yukina's Room]

Yukina: If I give the melodic line more contrast, like in "BLACK SHOUT," this should bring this song closer to what we used to perform in our early days...
Yukina: ... No. This is just a collection of phrases. The song itself has no soul...
Yukina: Sigh...
Yukina: (Write, erase, write, erase... No matter how often I try, I can't turn this into a song I can be proud of...)
Yukina: (I can recall what I felt and thought back when Roselia was formed.)
Yukina: (But what good is that when my heart no longer screams the same things?)
Yukina: ... My hand... It won't move...
Yukina: ...
Yukina: (Even when I was all by myself and writing music for the sake of revenge, I was at least able to follow my emotions when writing songs...)
Yukina: (This, though, is unbearable... Compared to those days, trying to write this song now is beyond...)
Yukina: ...!
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Campus Street]

Yukina: ...
Rinko: ... -san... Yukina-san...?
Yukina: ... Rinko.
Rinko: You don't look so well... Are you maybe experiencing difficulty with the new song...?
Yukina: ...
Rinko: Please rest once you arrive home... There's still time until the deadline...
Yukina: ... That's not it. I finished the song yesterday.
Rinko: You... did...?
Yukina: However...
Yukina: ... Can I ask you to look at this score, Rinko? I want to hear your thoughts.
Rinko: O-okay... May I have the score...?
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Rinko: ...
Rinko: This song...
Yukina: ... What do you think?
Rinko: Um... It's not bad... but... take this bridge here...
Rinko: If the bass' part was more pronounced... it would make the phrase feel more fresh and catchy...
Yukina: ... Yes, I agree...
Rinko: Oh...
Rinko: Right... Of course you do...
Rinko & Yukina: ...
Rinko: ... And if we change the phrase that begins at this measure... it might make the song... more truly Roselia...
Yukina: ... That's a good point. I'll adjust it.
Rinko: Aside from those things... there's nothing in particular that I have to say... I'm sure this song will be seen... as being truly Roselia...
Yukina: ... Thank you. You've been very helpful.
Rinko: ... Um...
Rinko: I wonder what being Roselia means... for other people...
Yukina: ...
Rinko: Lately... that's what I've been thinking about all the time...
Rinko: The current Roselia... is the result of taking on new challenges and evolving...
Rinko: That willingness to take on new challenges... is what I used to think made us Roselia...
Rinko: But what matters most... is believing in whatever path we're walking as a result of having chosen what we believed to be right... and doing so with our heads high.
Rinko: That, for us... is what I feel it means to be "truly Roselia..."
Yukina: Rinko...
Rinko: I-I'm sorry... I know I shouldn't be saying this to you...
Rinko: Just from seeing the score... I can see so clearly what it cost you to write it...
Rinko: But even so... I can't bring myself to believe that this is the right choice...
Rinko: As a member of Roselia... I don't think I'll be able to play this song... I'm so sorry...
Yukina: ...
Yukina: ... You're right.
Rinko: What...?
Yukina: Thank you, Rinko. I feel the same way.
Yukina: As I was writing this song... I found myself questioning time and time again if this truly was a Roselia song.
Yukina: But as a professional, there was a job that needed to be done... and so, I ignored my inner voice.
Yukina: This, however... is not a Roselia song. At least not for who we are now...
Rinko: Yukina-san...
Yukina: It's not a matter of choosing between our responsibility as professionals or our pride as Roselia...
Yukina: To throw away our pride as Roselia now means we'll likely never be ourselves again.
Rinko: Th-then...
Yukina: Yes. I'll rewrite this song once more. It's the most Roselia thing to do...
Yukina: And it's the path that I believe is right.
(Black cut)
