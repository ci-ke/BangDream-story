event205-05 Chapter 4: Can’t Say Sorry

On the way to a concert, MASKING and PAREO try to find a way to make up...

(Character: Rei Wakana, Rokka Asahi, Masuki Sato, Reona Nyubara, Chiyu Tamade)

[Station - Train Platform]

LAYER (Rei): The train we'll be riding... is on this platform.
LOCK (Rokka): We're going to see a joint concert, right? I can't wait!
CHU² (Chiyu): Don't treat this like a field trip. Our target band is the first one up, so you better stay focused once it starts.
LOCK (Rokka): R-right!
LAYER (Rei): PAREO, that's a rather large bag. What's in it?
PAREO (Reona): I will return home after seeing the concert today, so I have various items I brought to CHU²-sama's house... such as my scores, writing tools, and homework.
PAREO (Reona): I also have a towel, a frozen PET bottle, and a change of clothes. I cannot allow CHU²-sama to become drenched in sweat from jumping around at the concert.
CHU² (Chiyu): I wouldn't do something like that!
PAREO (Reona): Aww~. The other day you were inconvenienced because you made no preparations at all~.
PAREO (Reona): ... Isn't that right, Massu-san?
MASKING (Masuki): Y-yeah. Right...
PAREO (Reona): ...
PAREO (Reona): (Massu-san's not taking part in my teasing... I was hoping to open a line of communication with her...)
MASKING (Masuki): (Agh~, what kinda response was that...?! There's gotta be a more thoughtful response I can give...!)
MASKING & PAREO: Sigh~...
CHU² (Chiyu): Those two have been looking at each other and sighing for a while now... What is wrong with them?
LAYER (Rei): I imagine they might be looking for an opportunity to make up with each other... Let's leave them be for now.
CHU² (Chiyu): Sigh~... Why am I being dragged into this mess?
LOCK (Rokka): It's like... the mood between them is unbearable...
MASKING (Masuki): Ah... I'm thirsty, so I'm gonna head to a vendin' machine.
PAREO (Reona): I-I am feeling thirsty as well, so I will go with you!
LAYER (Rei): You two~! The train is coming soon, so be careful!
(Black cut)
MASKING (Masuki): Hmm~... Should I get somethin' hot or somethin' cold?
PAREO (Reona): It is hard to choose between the two during this season. As we are going to a live house, I believe a cold drink would be best...
MASKING (Masuki): Yeah, you're right... Guess I'll go with orange juice. What about you, PAREO?
PAREO (Reona): I will have that as well!
MASKING (Masuki): Got it.
PAREO (Reona): (Hang in there, PAREO...! To make up with Massu-san, you merely need to do as LAYER-san said and say, "I'm sorry"...!)
PAREO (Reona): ... Massu-san, um...!
MASKING (Masuki): Ah, you've gotta be kiddin' me!
MASKING (Masuki): The orange juice just sold out... I managed to get one, so you can have it.
PAREO (Reona): No, I will have something else, so you may have it.
MASKING (Masuki): Don't worry about it. I'll have coffee or somethin'.
PAREO (Reona): Oh no, please do not worry about me!
MASKING (Masuki): C'mon, just take it!
Announcer: The local train bound for... is now departing from track three...
PAREO (Reona): I-it cannot be...!
MASKING (Masuki): Hm?
MASKING (Masuki): H-hey. The train is leavin'! We gotta go after it!
PAREO (Reona): It has already started moving, so we will no longer make it~!
MASKING (Masuki): It's because ya wouldn't take the orange juice!
PAREO (Reona): I told you that I would have something else!
MASKING (Masuki): Wait, this ain't the time to be arguin'!
PAREO (Reona): Th-that's right! I will search for another route on my phone!
PAREO (Reona): Umm, I'll set it to "walk quickly" and add buses into the scope...
MASKING (Masuki): How's it look?!
PAREO (Reona): It is no use~...! The next train arriving at this platform in twenty minutes is the quickest!
MASKING (Masuki): We won't make it if we wait for that! Considerin' how long it'd take to go home now, goin' by bike'll be impossible!
MASKING (Masuki): Even if we call for a taxi, we'll still end up havin' to wait...!
PAREO (Reona): ... It is too soon for us to give up!
PAREO (Reona): While there are no substitute trains, it would seem there is a bus stop nearby with a bus departing for the concert venue.
PAREO (Reona): We have three minutes until it leaves. The bus stop is five minutes away on foot, but if we run with all our might, we might make it...!
MASKING (Masuki): ... Alright! Let's hurry, PAREO! Gimme your bag!
PAREO (Reona): Huh? But...
MASKING (Masuki): I'm the one with more power. You just run and don't worry about anythin' else!
PAREO (Reona): Okay!
