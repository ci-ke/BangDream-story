event149-05 Chapter 4: Neither Is The Answer

Rinko and Lisa have come to a restaurant. Lisa can't decide what to order...

(Character: Lisa Imai, Rinko Shirokane)

[Restaurant]

Lisa: Ahhh, that was fun! We got in a ton of shopping~! And the best way to reward a tired body is with some sweets~♪
Lisa: Ah, and since you let me drag you along, I'll treat you to one thing of your choosing!
Rinko: I couldn't... If anything, it was you... who had to take care of me...
Lisa: No way, you're the reason I found such great items! I'd feel terrible if I didn't repay you somehow!
Rinko: I-if you insist... then I'll accept the offer...
Lisa: Hahaha, feel free to spoil yourself! Oh, here's the menu.
Rinko: Thank you very much...
Rinko: Um... "New Specials & Returning Favorites On Sale Now!" ... ?
Lisa: Oh, they've got a campaign going on!
Lisa: Looks like the new item is this honey fruit toast~. Whoa, get a load of how much fruit is on that thing~.
Rinko: The returning item is... this classic chocolate parfait. It's a brownie topped with chocolate ice cream and chocolate ganache...
Lisa: Oh~, that sounds amazing too!
Lisa: Which do you want, Rinko?
Rinko: This is... another difficult decision... Fufufu.
Lisa: Yeah, now there's a nice smile♪ Hitting a fork in the road can be fun sometimes.
Rinko: Yes, that's true... Which one would you prefer... Imai-san?
Lisa: Good question~... Ahhh, I can't decide~.
Lisa: We all know the classic chocolate parfait would taste incredible~.
Rinko: You... can't go wrong with that one...
Lisa: Having said that, the honey fruit toast looks like a winner too, don't you think?
Lisa: Oh man, which one do you feel destined for, Rinko?
Rinko: I'm not so sure... about destiny...
Rinko: But why don't we get one of each... and share...?
Lisa: Now there's an idea! Let's ask for some extra plates and split them right down the middle!
Lisa: When you think about it, chocolate ice cream on honey toast sounds nice, doesn't it?
Rinko: Ah... That does sound good...
Lisa: It's going to be amazing☆ I can't wait~.
Rinko: ...
Lisa: Hm? What's wrong, Rinko? Did you find something you want instead?
Rinko: N-no... Nothing like that. I just realized... that this mindset might work, as well...
Lisa: Huh...?
Rinko: It may seem like there are only two choices at first glance... but in actuality, there could be more than meets the eye...
Rinko: I'm beginning to wonder if... you brought me here to teach me that...
Lisa: No way! You're giving me too much credit~.
Lisa: All I wanted to do is treat you to something nice.
Rinko: Nevertheless... I can't thank you enough...
Lisa: But still, to have your frown turned upside-down so quickly... Sweets sure are amazing~♪
Rinko: Fufu, they certainly are...
Lisa: Anyway, now that things are looking brighter, let's make our order!
Lisa: Excuse me. May we order now?
Server: Yes, of course. What will you be having?
Lisa: One order of the honey fruit toast, and one order of the classic chocolate parfait.
Rinko: Um... and also...
Rinko: Two additional plates, please.
Lisa & Rinko: Hahaha... Fufufu...
