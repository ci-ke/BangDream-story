event144-03 Chapter 2: Marie's Struggles

Marie is as popular as ever with the children. But what happens when they ask her to do a backflip?!

(Character: Tae Hanazono, Misaki Okusawa)

[Downtown]

Marie: Have a balloon~! There's no need to rush. We have plenty~!
Michelle (Misaki): ... A lot more people than I imagined came to visit. Marie, are you okay?
Marie: Of course! It's hard but really fun!
Marie: I wonder if it's because everyone's smiling, waving, and greeting us.
Marie: That really makes me happy, and it really pumps me up~. At this rate, I might be fine doing this all day!
Michelle (Misaki): It's hard but fun, huh? ... Fufu.
Michelle (Misaki): I know how you feel, but... no matter how you look at it, I don't think we can keep this up all day.
Marie: What?! I actually think I can keep going though~.
Michelle (Misaki): Nope, there's no way.
Marie: Awww~.
Tae: Ah, they're here. Hey~, Michelle~, Marie~.
Michelle (Misaki): Ah, hello~.
Marie: O-Tae! ... I mean... H-hello~!
Tae: Hello. I heard you both were here, so I came to see you. Can I have a balloon too?
Marie: Of course! Here you go!
Child 1: Awww! You're so lucky! I want a balloon from Marie too!
Tae: Okay, then you can go next.
Child 1: Yay! Thanks, Marie!
Child 2: Me next! Marie, let's play tag! After that, show us a backflip! You always do them, right?!
Marie: Okay, sure. A balloon, tag, and a backflip... Wait, a backflip?!
Michelle (Misaki): ...?!
Michelle (Misaki): Uh, that's, ummm... She's holding a lot of balloons right now, so I think that might be difficult~.
Tae: In that case, I'll hold the balloons for you. I've not seen you do a backflip in a while, so I'd like to see it too.
Marie: Huh~?!
Marie: (No way, that's impossible! I've never done a backflip before!)
Tae: Okay, I've got the balloons. Good luck, Marie!
Marie: (Ngh... O-Tae's eyes are really sparkling. She really believes that Marie can do it.)
Marie: (I decided I would completely become Marie. If I tell them I can't do it, they'll get disappointed.)
Marie: (I... definitely can't let that happen!)
Michelle (Misaki): Everyone, Marie's not feeling that well today...
Marie: I-it's okay~. I'll try doing a backflip!
Michelle (Misaki): Huh?! A-are you serious...?!
Marie: O-of course! If Marie can do it, then I have to!
Marie: Here I go~... Yah!
Child 1: Ahaha. Marie, that's just a normal jump! You have to jump higher and spin around!
Marie: I-I see. Jump higher and spin... Okay. Yah!
Marie: ... There's no way I can do that so easily~!
Child 2: Ahaha! Marie, you're so funny!
Child 1: Hey, Marie, do that one more time!
Michelle (Misaki): Th-they think she's doing it on purpose.
Michelle (Misaki): ... Wait, this is not the time to relax. I need to stop them before it escalates any more... Hanazono-san, sorry, but could you help me get the kids-
Tae: It's okay, Marie. I'm sure you can do it on your next try. I'm rooting for you!
Michelle (Misaki): She's really piling it on...!
Child 1: Hey, Marie, pick me up next!
Child 2: Lucky! Marie, me too!
Marie: Huff, puff... I think I can handle that... Wait, both of you at once?!
Child 1 & 2: Marie, hurry up!
Marie: O-okay~! If this is what I need to do to completely become Marie... Here we go...!
Marie: They might be kids, but they're still heavy if there are two of them... My legs are shaking... I-I can't hold on anymore~!
Michelle (Misaki): Whoa! Marie, your head! Your head is wobbling!
Marie: Huh?! B-but I've got my hands full with the kids... Whoa!
(White cut)
Michelle (Misaki): J-just in the nick of time...
Marie: That was close. Thanks, Michelle.
Child 1: Ahaha! Michelle, why are you holding on to Marie's face? You're so weird!
Child 2: Hey, Marie, do that thing where you go splat again!
Marie: Huh?! Again?!
Marie: Ngh, these kids are way too powerful... Even if I'm Marie, I can't do more than this!
