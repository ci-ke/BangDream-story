event281-05 Chapter 4: A Kind Sister, A Kind Senpai

Rinko is invited over to Saya’s home, where her lively younger brothers give her a warm and energetic welcome.

(Character: Saya Yamabuki, Rinko Shirokane)

[Yamabuki Residence - Saya's Room]

Rinko: Excuse me...
Saya: It’s not that big, but feel free to sit wherever you like.
Saya: Oh, it must be hard to sit in a kimono. I’ll go grab a cushion.
Rinko: Thank you very much...
Jun: Sis, do we have a guest!?
Saya: Jun, Sana! I told you not to barge in when we have company!
Jun & Sana: Okay!
Rinko: Um... are they your siblings...?
Saya: Yes. They’re still small, so they’re always like this...
Jun: Sis, here’s the cushion!
Sana: This one’s for you, and this one’s for the guest!
Saya: Oh, you brought the cushions? Thanks, you two.
Sana: Mom told us to bring them! I did good, right?
Saya: Yeah, very good.
Sana: Ehehe...
Jun: Hey, I brought one too!!
Saya: You did great too, Jun! Could you give that cushion to Rinko-senpai?
Jun: Okay! Here you go!
Rinko: Th-thank you...
Sana: ......!
Rinko: (They’re... really staring at me...)
Sana: Rinko-senpai, you look so pretty~! Like a princess!
Rinko: A p-princess...? Um... I’m not really...
Saya: Jun, Sana. We’re hungry, so could you go ask Mom for some snacks?
Jun & Sana: Okay!
Saya: Hey, no running in the house! I always tell you that~!
Rinko: They’re so... energetic...
Saya: Sorry. It’s always like this at our place...
Rinko: ...I think I finally understand why you’re the way you are, Yamabuki-san...
Saya: Huh?
Rinko: I mean, you’re very much the big sister type...
Rinko: Spending today with you made me feel that way. Even during the shoot, you were always so considerate...
Rinko: You made sure I didn’t trip on the steps... You’re really kind and thoughtful...
Saya: Oh, no, not at all!
Rinko: Hehe... I know you're younger than me, but being with you gives me this sense of reassurance, like even if I mess up, you’d catch me...
Rinko: It’s a lot like how I feel when I’m with Imai-san...
Saya: Th-thank you...
Saya: But I think you’re the one who’s kind and dependable, Rinko-senpai!
Rinko: Me...?
Saya: Yeah. I’ve heard all about when you were student council president, and Arisa’s told me a lot, too.
Saya: No matter what kind of problem came up, you always gave it your all. Even when things didn’t go well, you stuck it out with everyone to the end... That’s why everyone says they want to work with you, Rinko-senpai.
Saya: And that’s why I really wanted to try my best today and take some great photos—with you.
Saya: To be honest, I was pretty nervous about even asking you.
Saya: But I was able to take that small step forward because it was you, Rinko-senpai.
Rinko: Ah...
Rinko: I’m really happy... Taking that first step takes a lot of courage...
Rinko: I’ve hesitated many times too... and stayed stuck in place...
Rinko: So if I could be the reason someone else took a step forward... that means a lot to me...
Saya & Rinko: Ehehe... Fufufu...
Saya: Hm? A message on my phone...? Excuse me a sec.
Saya: ...It’s from Kasumi. “I found something super New Year’s-y at the shopping street!” she says. Looks like she’s done greeting her relatives.
Rinko: I wonder what she found... Anyway, shall we go check it out...?
Saya: Yes, let’s!
