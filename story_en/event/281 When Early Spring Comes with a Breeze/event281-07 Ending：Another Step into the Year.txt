event281-07 Ending: Another Step into the Year

A few days into the new year, Saya receives a message from Rinko on her way to practice...

(Character: Saya Yamabuki, Rinko Shirokane)

[Yamabuki Residence - Living Room]

Saya: Want seconds on bread or juice, Jun, Sana?
Sana: I'm full!
Jun: Me too~.
Saya: Yeah? Then big sis is heading off to band practice now.
Jun: Okay! Let's clean up our plates~.
Sana: Me too, I’ll help!
Saya: Fufu. Good job, you two.
Saya: Let's see... sheet music, pens, drumsticks, and a bread snack packed... Yep, I think I’ve got everything.
Saya: Huh? A message from Rinko-senpai.
Saya: Well... “Thanks again for the other day.”
Rinko: “Thanks to the New Year’s experience, my photoshoot job yesterday went more smoothly than usual.”
Saya: Oh, really? I’m glad to hear that...!
Rinko: “—That said, I was still nervous... I guess I can’t change overnight.”
Saya: Fufu. “But you still made real progress and got great results, Rinko-senpai. You did awesome!”
Rinko: “Thank you. You really are like a big sister, Yamabuki-san.”
Saya: Big sister...? Ah! I just told a senpai she was “awesome”!
Saya: “Sorry, I’d just finished praising Jun and Sana, so it slipped out!”
Rinko: “No need to apologize. I haven’t been complimented like that in a while, so it actually made me happy.”
Saya: Sigh, that was kinda awkward... But Rinko-senpai really is the sweetest.
Saya: ...Oh, that’s right.
Saya: “By the way, the photo I entered in the contest is currently on display at the local photo shop.”
Saya: “They said it really brings out the subject’s charm—it got some lovely feedback.”
Saya: “Neighbors have been coming up to me saying it’s a wonderful photo.”
Saya: “Thank you again so much for being my model!”
Saya: ...I wonder if I’ve still got a little time.
Saya: Let’s see—
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Shirokane Residence - Rinko's Room]

Rinko: (Today’s got a magazine interview meeting and band practice... Gotta make sure I don’t forget anything...)
Rinko: (A reply from Yamabuki-san...?)
Rinko: (“They said it really brings out the subject’s charm”... “Neighbors have been calling it a wonderful photo”...)
Rinko: Fufu... I’m glad...
Rinko: (Oh? Another one from Yamabuki-san...)
Saya: “Since the New Year, I’ve felt more confident, like I might be able to try more new things this year.”
Saya: “Am I just getting carried away?”
Rinko: ...
Rinko: “You’re not getting carried away at all.”
Rinko: “I think the feelings you’re having now are really important.”
Rinko: “Just taking the tiniest step with a little courage can open up so many possibilities.”
Rinko: (I think that’s true for me too...)
Rinko: (Let’s keep moving forward this year, one step at a time... Because those little steps will lead to new scenery.)
Rinko: (Now it’s a message from Ako-chan...)
Ako: “Rin-rin, wanna head out a little early and stop by a café? They’ve got a special New Year parfait with mochi and mandarin oranges!”
Rinko: Fufu... “Sure, let’s go. I’m heading out now.”...
Rinko: Oh, right... I need to reply to Yamabuki-san too...
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Rinko: “I know it’s a bit late, but here’s to another great year.”
Saya: “Same to you! Looking forward to it!”
Saya & Rinko: Fufufu...
(White cut)
(Background change)
Saya & Rinko: I’m off! Heading out now...
