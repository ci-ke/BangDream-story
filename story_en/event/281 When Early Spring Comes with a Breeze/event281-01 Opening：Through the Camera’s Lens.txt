event281-01 Opening: Through the Camera’s Lens

To join a New Year’s photo contest, Saya heads to a shrine to take some pictures.

(Character: Saya Yamabuki, Rinko Shirokane)

[Downtown]

Saya: I'm heading out now~
Saya: Suuu... Mmm~, the chilly air feels so nice.

Saya: N-No way! It’s just a hobby, really!
Saya: ...But actually, I’ve been thinking I might enter something in a bigger contest this year.
