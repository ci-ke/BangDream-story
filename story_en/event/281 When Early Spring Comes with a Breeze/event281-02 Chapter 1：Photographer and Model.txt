Saya quickly starts her shoot with Rinko as the model—but Rinko’s expression is stiff and frozen...

Rinko: O-Okay...
Rinko: .........
Saya: (Huh? Rinko-senpai suddenly looks super stiff...)
Saya: ...Okay, I'm taking the photo now!
Rinko: H-How... does it look...?

Rinko: O-Okay...

[Shrine - Entry Path]

Saya: This time, could you stand in front of the torii gate and look up at the plum branches, please?
Rinko: L-Like this...?
Saya: (Hmm... her expression’s even stiffer than before...)
Saya: Sorry, let me try from a few different angles!
Rinko: Y-Yes...
Saya: ...Hmm...
Rinko: S-Sorry... None of them turned out very well, did they...?
Saya: Eh? No, it’s not that...
Saya & Rinko: .........
Saya: (What should I do? She seems troubled... Maybe this was a bother for her...)

Saya: What!? Really!? But you always look so cool in magazines and posters!
Rinko: Th-Thank you... It’s just, I usually get a lot of help from Imai-san and Ako-chan... That’s how I get through it...
Rinko: When you asked me, I thought... maybe this could be a chance to face something I struggle with... That’s why I said yes...
Rinko: I’m really sorry...
Saya: Rinko-senpai...
Saya: I’m sorry too. Part of the reason the photos aren’t turning out well is probably because I’m not skilled enough as the photographer.
Saya: I really want to enter a photo of you, Rinko-senpai, in the contest. So please, let me keep trying together with you! I’m counting on you!
Rinko: ...!
Rinko: Same here... I might end up causing you some trouble, but I’ll be in your care...
Saya: Th-Thank you so much!
Rinko: So, where should we take the next photo...?
Saya: Actually, how about we take a break first? I saw a bench over by the amazake stand!
