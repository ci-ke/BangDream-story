event269-03 Chapter 2: Escaping the Shadow

CHU² prepares for a guerrilla live, but the plan doesn’t go as smoothly as hoped...

(Character: Rei Wakana, Rokka Asahi, Masuki Sato, Reona Nyubara, Chiyu Tamade)

[Shopping District]

CHU² (Chiyu): (The stage planning is done... It’ll be tight, but we should be ready in time. Now for the equipment.)
CHU² (Chiyu): Hi. This is CHU², as I mentioned in my email... Yes, about the outdoor equipment rental.
CHU² (Chiyu): I've attached a list of the gear we need in the email I just sent. Could you check it?
Staff: Please hold... Ah, this file, yes.
Staff: ...I'm terribly sorry, but all of this equipment has already been booked for that day.
CHU² (Chiyu): (It’s JRF day... I guess getting ideal equipment is out of the question.)
CHU² (Chiyu): Thank you for checking. How about this next list? I’ll send it right now.
Staff: Thank you... I’m very sorry, but all of these items are also...
CHU² (Chiyu): What's!? Is that because of JRF...!?
Staff: No, this is separate from JRF. Apparently a major agency is hosting a private live that day...
CHU² (Chiyu): No way...!
CHU² (Chiyu): ...Thank you for your time. I’ll look elsewhere for equipment. Goodbye.
(Black cut)
(Background change)
CHU² (Chiyu): This is absolutely ridiculous...! There’s only one person who would pull something this absurd...!
Hayamiya: Hello, this is Hayamiya.
CHU² (Chiyu): Hi. I heard your agency is hosting a private concert on the day of JRF. Just checking if that’s true.
Hayamiya: Oh? You called sooner than expected.
CHU² (Chiyu): I should say the same. When did you catch wind of our guerrilla live?
Hayamiya: We’ve arranged to monitor your activities. Though I didn’t expect you to plan a guerrilla live right after being dropped from JRF.
CHU² (Chiyu): Sorry, but we don’t have time to sit around and sulk. ...So tell me, why are you so determined to interfere?
Hayamiya: Oh my...
CHU² (Chiyu): If you want RAS under your agency, this harassment is pointless.
CHU² (Chiyu): When you scheduled 'Midnight-Peaky' to clash with our solo live, I figured you were trying to make a statement.
CHU² (Chiyu): Using RAS to show others what happens to anyone who defies President Hayamiya...
CHU² (Chiyu): But forcing us off JRF, trying to crush our guerrilla show...
CHU² (Chiyu): If this is just payback, you’re putting in way too much effort. So what’s your real motive?
Hayamiya: I didn’t expect someone your age to analyze so calmly― That’s exactly why I’m interested in you.
CHU² (Chiyu): ...What?
Hayamiya: Do you understand if I say that it’s not RAISE A SUILEN that matters to me... but you?
CHU² (Chiyu): So... you're scouting me, not RAS?
Hayamiya: Correct. As I said before, you’re an exceptional producer. And you’re the daughter of the world-renowned violinist, Miu Tamade.
CHU² (Chiyu): ! ...
Hayamiya: You possess the talent and notoriety befitting this golden age of girl bands—a rare gem among countless stones. Under my guidance, you could shine even brighter.
Hayamiya: I knew from the start how invested you were in RAS, so I thought I might as well bring the whole band into the agency...
Hayamiya: Since that no longer seems possible, I’ve decided to take a different approach.
Hayamiya: It would be unfortunate if you found yourselves unable to continue making music, wouldn’t it? If RAS ends up disbanding... I’d be more than happy to buy your talents as a producer.
CHU² (Chiyu): What a joke...! You really think a threat like that is enough to make me bawl and come crawling to you?
CHU² (Chiyu): I’d rather die than join your agency. RAS is my hands, my feet—my voice. Like hell I’m letting them go.
CHU² (Chiyu): No matter what, I’ll protect RAS myself.
Hayamiya: Fufu, of course you’d say that now. But can a child like you truly protect RAS?
CHU² (Chiyu): What...!?
Hayamiya: Our agency is powerful, well-funded, and connected. We can’t be shaken easily. But you?
Hayamiya: Isn’t it true that with just a bit of pressure, you’re already scrambling—left without support and completely at a loss?
CHU² (Chiyu): That’s not true...!
Hayamiya: Surely someone as intelligent as you can see the benefits of joining a proper agency. I trust you’ll think it over carefully.
CHU² (Chiyu): ...Tch, how dare you just end it like that...! Don't mess with me!
