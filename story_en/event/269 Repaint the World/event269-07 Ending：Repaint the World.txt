event269-07 Ending: Repaint the World

After the live, CHU² goes to pay a “visit” to someone important.

(Character: Tae Hanazono, Lisa Imai, Rei Wakana, Rokka Asahi, Masuki Sato, Reona Nyubara, Chiyu Tamade)

[Guerrilla Live Venue]

LAYER (Rei): Haa... haa... Is everyone... okay...?
LOCK (Rokka): B-Barely~... There were so many people in the end... and the encore just wouldn’t stop...!
PAREO (Reona): Massu-san, are you alright...?
MASKING (Masuki): Ughh~... my whole body hurts from going wild... Now I get how CHU² feels after a workout...
CHU² (Chiyu): Stop dragging me into it...! Sigh~...
Lisa: Great job, everyone~! That show was seriously incredible! The crowd was on fire—I’m drenched!
Tae: Your music felt alive, explosive, and full of passion... It was just so cool!
LOCK (Rokka): Thank you so much...!
Lisa: A bunch of other bands shared the videos too. Afterglow, Pastel＊Palettes... even Poppin'Party!
CHU² (Chiyu): I see...
CHU² (Chiyu): I'll make sure to thank them later. And thank you too.
CHU² (Chiyu): Alright then...
LAYER (Rei): CHU², where are you going?
CHU² (Chiyu): I’ll go pay them a visit.
(Black cut)
Hayamiya: ......
CHU² (Chiyu): Hi. Ms. Hayamiya. What did you think of RAISE A SUILEN’s performance?
Hayamiya: Like a bunch of unruly children. Pretending to be clever while picking fights left and right... even with me.
Hayamiya: I waited until the day of to let you feel the weight of your mistake... but that was clearly a miscalculation.
CHU² (Chiyu): Glad to hear you understand. And hopefully, you also understand that I’ll never be yours.
CHU² (Chiyu): I won’t change my stance. I believe today’s success happened because RAS has stayed true to that stance.
CHU² (Chiyu): ...Though I will say, I do now understand what you meant.
Hayamiya: ...In what way?
CHU² (Chiyu): Things like money, connections, the environment... from a producer’s perspective, those things are inseparable from making music.
CHU² (Chiyu): I’ve only now come to realize that. A little late, maybe.
CHU² (Chiyu): But saying “music that doesn’t make money is meaningless” is total nonsense.
CHU² (Chiyu): If all you chase is marketable music, all you get is bland, crowd-pleasing junk. It’s the discoveries, the inventions—one spark of originality—that change the world.
CHU² (Chiyu): —So our goals still don’t align. Kindly back off.
Hayamiya: Your mouth never stops, does it? I have no interest in handling wild animals like you.
Hayamiya: Even if you joined my agency, who knows when you'd rebel again.
CHU² (Chiyu): Well then...!
Hayamiya: This is a small industry. I’m sure we’ll cross paths again. Try to behave next time.
CHU² (Chiyu): Yes... Right back at you.
CHU² (Chiyu): And if we do... we’ll crush you all over again.
(Black cut)
CHU² (Chiyu): ...Phew.
PAREO (Reona): CH-CHU²-sama~! Where are you!?
CHU² (Chiyu): I’m right here. Why are you in such a panic?
PAREO (Reona): CHU²-sama! Your phone’s been blowing up nonstop~!
LOCK (Rokka): And social media is going wild too! Our follower count is exploding!
CHU² (Chiyu): Of course it is. We’re the reckless band that picked a fight with JRF.
LAYER (Rei): Well... not like we didn’t expect this...
MASKING (Masuki): Now that I think about it, this really was kind of insane.
LAYER (Rei): Back when I was a support musician, I used to quietly dream of performing at JRF.
MASKING (Masuki): You too, Rei? Same here, actually.
LAYER & MASKING: Fufu... Ahahahaha!
LOCK (Rokka): You two, this is not the time to be laughing~.
CHU² (Chiyu): I’m sure we’ll be all over the news. So you better brace yourselves.
LOCK (Rokka): Eek~, When did this turn into something so huge!?
PAREO (Reona): Honestly, I think it’s been that way from the beginning~... But that laid-back side of you is very LOCK-san, and it’s super charming♪
LOCK (Rokka): I-I don’t really feel like that was a compliment~!
CHU² (Chiyu): Relax. One way or another, years from now they’ll say today’s performance was the true beginning of RAS.
CHU² (Chiyu): No, I will make them say it...!
CHU² (Chiyu): We’re all going to sprint full speed toward the future we believe in!
All: Yeah! Let’s go! Yes!
