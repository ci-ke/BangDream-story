event270-02 Chapter 1: Bonds We've Built

Gathered on stage for rehearsal, Kasumi and the others realize just how much support they’ve been receiving...

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Tae Hanazono, Rimi Ushigome, Saya Yamabuki, Arisa Ichigaya)

[Flower Bloom Rock Festival - Stage]

Kasumi: Marina-san! Ririko-san!
Marina: Oh, there you are. We just finished setting up here too.
Rimi: Wow, what a stage...!
Tae: Yeah. I think the live show here is gonna be amazing.
Ririko: We’ll be adjusting the monitors during the rehearsal, so let us know if anything feels off.
Kasumi: Thank you so much!
Saya: Still, is it really okay that we’re getting so much help from CiRCLE and RiNG?
Arisa: Yeah, seriously. Even tomorrow’s PA work is gonna be handled entirely by the staff.
Ririko: Fufu, don’t be so formal. We’re just happy to be invited to such a fun event.
Marina: Exactly. It's a festival meant to get the whole community excited. And besides, it’s a request from you girls.
Kasumi: Uuu... Marina-san, Ririko-san! Thank you so much!
Arisa: Hey, quit trying to hug everyone you see!
Marina: Ahaha... Still, everyone’s getting into it even though it’s just setup time.
Ririko: Totally~. And everyone looks like they’re having fun. That’s thanks to Poppin’Party’s energy, right?
Kasumi: Ours?
Ririko: Yeah. Poppin’Party, the host, looks like they’re having so much fun prepping...
Marina: And you can see it in the faces of everyone helping out. Like over there.
Kasumi: Ah, those are the band members we met at the Rocking Star Festival...!
Marina: They were happily talking about how they wanted to make the directional signs cuter and easier to understand.
Kasumi: Really...!
Arisa: Back when we first played Rocking Star Festival, so much happened...
Rimi: Yeah... like our flyers getting tossed out...
Kasumi: Ugh~...! Even now, it still stings just thinking about it~...!
Saya: But we decided we’d make people turn their heads with our music. I remember we did a team huddle with Marina-san back then.
Marina: Right. It was just me and Poppin’Party in that first huddle. But the next Rocking Star Festival, it was a massive circle.
Marina: And that’s because Poppin’Party got everyone’s attention with their music and kept growing afterward.
Arisa: It was rough back then... The owner didn’t acknowledge us at all...
Ririko: Speaking of which, the owner said Tsukishima was just like Toyama back then.
Marina: Ahaha... It’s a bit embarrassing now. But watching you girls try so hard made me feel like I had to do something too.
Marina: You’ve always powered through everything with your brightness and your stubbornness.
Saya: Stubbornness, huh... The city hall staff said something similar too.
Arisa: Yeah. But if we’re talking stubbornness, it’s really just Kasumi...
Ririko: But if you all stick with Kasumi-chan, then you’re just as stubborn as she is, aren’t you?
Arisa: Well... yeah, maybe you’re right...
Marina: Fufu, growing through that signature never-give-up spirit... I think a lot of people have seen that in Poppin’Party, and it makes them want to make things even more wonderful too.
Marina: Poppin’Party’s desire to “have fun together” draws people in, and they resonate with it... This whole fest feels filled with those beautiful bonds. That’s the feeling I get.
Ririko: Yeah. All the bonds you’ve built through your activities have led to this festival.
Saya: Everything we’ve done has led to this moment...
Marina: Everyone wants to feel that sparkling, heart-pounding, just like you all do. Us included.
Kasumi: Marina-san...!
Marina: So let’s make this a wonderful festival together.
Kasumi: Yes! We’re gonna make this a festival full of sparkling, heart-pounding for everyone!
Ririko: Alright, then. Let’s get rehearsal started. Everyone ready?
Poppin'Party: Yes!
(Black cut)

[After Rehearsal]

Arisa: Rehearsal’s done, and the setup’s finished... That means we’re all ready.
Tae: The stage looked amazing. Marina-san and the others really paid attention to every detail.
Rimi: There’s gonna be a lot of people coming to see the stage tomorrow, right?
Saya: I hope we get a big crowd tomorrow.
Kasumi: ......
Saya: Kasumi, something on your mind?
Kasumi: I was just thinking about what Ririko-san said earlier.
Arisa: Ah, about how everything we’ve done has led us to now?
Kasumi: Yeah. Everyone who’s supported us and helped us along the way... I want to treasure what we’ve built together.
Kasumi: ...Everyone, let’s make tomorrow the best festival ever!
Poppin'Party: Yeah!
