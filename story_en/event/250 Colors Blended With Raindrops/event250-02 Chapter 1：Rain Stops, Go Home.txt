event250-02 Chapter 1: Rain Stops, Go Home

They decide to take shelter from the rain at Soyo's house. She doesn't want them to stay there, but ....?

(Character: Tomori Takamatsu, Anon Chihaya, Rana Kaname, Soyo Nagasaki, Taki Shina)

[Nagasaki Residence - Living Room]

Anon: Haaah~, it's so warm~. It brings me back to life~.
Tomori: Thank you, Soyo-chan.
Soyo: Really, it's only until the rain stops.
Rana: ... Sniff... Ah--.
Tomori: Are you okay, Rana-chan?
Rana: It's a little chilly.
Taki: Can we borrow towels? We should dry ourselves and our instruments.
Soyo: They're in the bathroom, so feel free to use it--
Soyo's Mom: Oh, is that you, Soyo~? Are you home--?
Soyo: Mom!
Soyo: I thought you were working today.
Soyo's Mom: Yeah, I was. I had to work on my day off, but it was forecast to rain in the afternoon and work had calmed down so I came home early.
Soyo: Oh, I see. Welcome back home.
Soyo's Mom: Are those your friends over there?
Anon: Ah, yes! I'm--
Soyo: I've been getting along well with them lately. Anon-chan, Tomori-chan. Rana-chan and Taki-chan.
Soyo's Mom: Thank you all for being good friends with Soyo!
Soyo: It suddenly started raining, and I had them come inside because I didn't want them to catch a cold.
Anon: Eh...?
Soyo: Is something wrong, Anon-chan?
Anon: Ah, no! Well... I'm sorry to bother you!
Soyo: Mom, you've still got work to do, right? I'll take care of the rest, so you can go back to your room. Everyone else, please make yourselves at home too.
Soyo's Mom: That's a great help~ Thanks, Soyo. Call me anytime if you need anything!
Soyo: Yeah. Good luck with your work.
Tomori: ...
Taki: ...
Anon: Um…
Soyo: What?
Taki: That's our line. What was that?
Soyo: What is what?
Anon: Soyorin, you're totally different when you're with us only and when your mother is around!
Anon: It's the first time you said to us, "Make yourselves at home"!
Taki: You told us to go home as soon as the rain stopped.
Anon: That's right, that's right!
Soyo: Shh! Mom will hear you.
Anon: ...That means she shouldn't hear us.
Taki: Are you putting on an act?
Soyo: No, I'm not. Since Mom is working in her room, don't get in the way.
Tomori: When you were talking with your mom, Soyo-chan...
Tomori: You looked so sweet.
Soyo: ...
Soyo: Not in particular...
Rana: …Achoo!
Anon: Are you okay? You sneezed louder than just now.
Soyo: Let's go to the bathroom. I'll lend you a towel.
Anon: Let's do that. Rana-chan looks like she's catching a cold.
Tomori: Are you okay, Rana-chan?
Rana: Just barely.
Soyo: Everyone, this way, follow me.
Anon: Yeees.
(Black cut)
