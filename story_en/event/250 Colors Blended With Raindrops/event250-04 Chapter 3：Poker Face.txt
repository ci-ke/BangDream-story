event250-04 Chapter 3: Poker Face

The five of them start playing Old Maid to pass the time. What happens after the game's conclusion...?

(Character: Tomori Takamatsu, Anon Chihaya, Rana Kaname, Soyo Nagasaki, Taki Shina)

[Nagasaki Residence - Living Room]

Tomori: Something good?
Anon: Tada! This is it!
Taki: Playing cards...? Why do you have them?
Anon: I went to the Shopping Mall the other day and there was a lottery. I got this~. I remembered I had left it in my bag!
Anon: ... So let's all play cards together!
Soyo: I won't do it.
Taki: Me too.
Anon: What!? Why!? Aren't we bored out of our minds here?
Taki: I'm not interested.
Anon: Is this really the time to be saying whether you're interested or not~? What's important now is how to kill time, right?
Taki: I wasn't the one complaining...
Anon: Hey, hey, let's play~! Let's play cards! Tomorin, you'll play cards with us, won't you?
Tomori: Yeah, let's do it.
Taki: … Huh!
Anon: Yay~! Come on, let's do it, Rikki. Hey, hey~.
Taki: ... If Tomori's going to do it.
Anon: Good! Then, let's play Old Maid or something, okay? I'll hand out the cards for now.
Soyo: Why are you handing out cards to Rana-chan and me too?
Anon: What? Is there any choice not to do it?
Rana: I'll do it. Because I'm bored.
Soyo: … Haah.
(Black cut)
Anon: All right, I'm done! Only Tomorin and Soyorin are left~.
Taki: Soyo has only one card left in her hand... If she doesn't draw a joker from Tomori, she wins.
Anon: Tomorin, fight~!
Tomori: …
Soyo: ... Is it this way?
Tomori: …!
Soyo: ... Or this way?
Tomori: ~!
Soyo: …
Soyo: ... Yes. I win.
Tomori: ! I lost...
Anon: Oh~, Soyorin won! By the way, Soyorin, aren't you strong? You haven't lost yet, right?
Taki: Soyo, will you stop teasing Tomori?
Soyo: I'm not teasing her. You guys are just way too easy to read. Especially, Tomori-chan.
Tomori: Uh...
Taki: Let's change the order of drawing cards. I'll deal with Soyo, Tomori.
Anon: What about me? I'm in the position to draw cards from Soyorin.
Taki: You'll have to manage it yourself.
Anon: Well, I want to read each other's facial expressions too. But Soyorin doesn't let it show at all. Rana-chan, wanna swap places?
Rana: I like it here.
Soyo: I'm fine with whatever, but are we still playing?
Taki: I'll do it. I can't let it end with Tomori losing.
Soyo: Sigh. I don't have a choice then.
(Black cut)
Taki: I lost...
Soyo: Taki-chan, you're so easy to understand too. It's all showing on your face.
Taki: It's not.
Soyo: It is. When you think you drew the joker, you make a displeased face.
Taki: Ugh...
Anon: Soyorin hasn't lost yet. I'd like to see Soyorin lose, even if it's only once.
Anon: Oh, that's right. How about inviting Soyorin's mom to join and play with all six of us? If that's the case…
Soyo: Anon-chan.
Anon: I'm kidding~. Don't make such a scary face~.
Taki: Let's play another game. I can't beat Soyo by playing Old Maid.
Soyo: Do you really want to beat me that badly?
Taki: Shut up.
Anon: We'll get bored if we just play Old Maid all the time. What would be better?
Soyo: How about Concentration? You can do this without reading your opponent's expression or anything.
Soyo: As long as you can remember where the cards are, anyone should be able to win, right?
Taki: Why did you look at me just now?
Soyo: Did I?
Taki: ... Anon, hurry up and line up the cards. I'll definitely beat her.
Anon: Ahaha, Rikki's getting serious!
Taki: Just hurry up.
Anon: If you say so, why don't you help me, Rikki?
Taki: Shut up.
Anon: Ah--, she's mad again~.
(Black cut)
(Background change)
