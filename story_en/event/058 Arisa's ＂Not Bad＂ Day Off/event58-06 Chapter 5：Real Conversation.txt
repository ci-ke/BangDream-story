event58-06 Chapter 5: Real Conversation

As Lisa watches over them, Arisa and Rimi manage to make pleasant conversation, if not a little bit awkward...

(Character: Rimi Ushigome, Arisa Ichigaya, Ran Mitake, Himari Uehara, Lisa Imai)

[Candy Shop]

Himari: Since we're already here, why don't we look around together~?
Arisa: (Th-this is it...!)
Rimi: (This is where our hard work pays off...!)
Lisa: Come on, Arisa! Keep it going!
Arisa: Ra-...!
Ran: Rah?
Arisa: R-Ran-chan, Himari-chan, what are you doing here?!
Ran: Uh... Shopping.
Arisa: O-oh... Shopping, huh...? Yeah, that makes sense.
Rimi: (This is just like our conversation at the restaurant! I knew it. Ran-chan may be the silent type, but when she speaks, she gets right to the point!)
Lisa: ... Pfft!
Himari: ...? Is something wrong, Lisa-senpai?
Lisa: Nope! Nothing at all.
Rimi: Is there any place you two would like to go?
Himari: Actually, I was just thinking about how I wanted to check out this candy shop. Could we stay here a little longer?
Ran: Hold on, Arisa and the others have already been here for a while. We shouldn't force them to stick around just because we want to.
Rimi: Ran-chan's so nice... Yet so direct!
Arisa: R-Rimi! You're thinking out loud!
Arisa: ... Ah, uhm, we wanted to look around a little more anyway, so it's no big deal for us if we stay here.
Himari: Really?! Thank you~!! I've been so curious about this store~. Did you find anything good?
Rimi: Ah, well... I found this big chocolate bar...
Himari: Wow! It's huge~! I can't even see your face. Even for a chocolate lover like me, there's no way I could eat that by myself~!
Rimi: Ah! You're a fan too, Himari-chan?
Himari: Yep! Wait, does that mean you like chocolate as well?!
Rimi: Yes! I love chawcolate- Ah! I mean, I- Uhhh...
Himari: Ah! I almost forgot! You're from Kansai, right?! Fufu, your accent is so cute~!
Rimi: Yeah, every once in a while, it'll slip out... It's so embarrassing~.
Arisa: (What the heck, Rimi...? She's already getting along with Himari-chan so well.)
Ran: ...
Arisa: ...
Arisa: (Crap... I need to say something...! If I don't, I'll never get to know Ran-chan and Himari-chan!)
Arisa: D-do you have any candy you like?
Ran: Me...? Uhm, I'm not really into this kind of stuff...
Arisa: (Huh?! She doesn't like sweets?! Wait, maybe she thinks candy is for babies. In that case, she might be pretending to hate them...)
Arisa: ... So she can act tough...
Ran: Huh?
Arisa: N-never mind! So does that mean you don't like sweet things?
Ran: Well... I guess you could say that. How about you?
Arisa: I-I... actually prefer Japanese sweets.
Ran: Oh, really? We get a lot of those from my dad's students.
Arisa: That sounds amazing... Ah, that's right, your father teaches flower arrangement...
Ran: Right. Japanese sweets aren't something I could eat every day, but I like them enough.
Arisa: That's good...
Arisa: (Gah, another awkward pause... Th-think of something to talk about...! Like... Ah, perfect!)
Arisa: A-actually, you know... I like to raise bonsai trees as a hobby... I suppose that's a little different from flower arranging...
Lisa: (There you go! Arisa and Rimi are both doing great♪)
Lisa: (Looks like my idea worked out after all.)
Ran: Lisa-san, why are you staring at us from so far away? It's kinda creepy...
Lisa: Huh?! Th-that's a pretty mean thing to say~. I'm just watching over all my underclassmen.
Ran: You're acting really suspicious. Isn't that right, Arisa?
Arisa: Huh?! I-I don't know... Maybe...?
Ran: Hold on... Are you hiding something too?
Arisa: O-of course not! Why would I-
Ran: Something's up... Lisa-san, Arisa. Tell me what's going on.
Lisa: Ooo, you're getting a little intense there...
Himari: R-ran, what are you doing?! Don't corner Lisa-senpai like that~!
Ran: They're hiding something, I can feel it. Lisa-san's the one who asked us to keep quiet in the first place.
Rimi: ...?!
Himari: Rimi, is everything okay?
Ran: Don't tell me you're in on this too.
Rimi: N-naw! I-I mean... No!
Ran: Alright, something's definitely going on. Lisa-san, tell me right now!
Lisa: Alright, alright! I'll talk~!
