event58-03 Chapter 2: Conversation Simulation

The girls decide to hone their conversational skills through a simulation. Will this work...?

(Character: Rimi Ushigome, Arisa Ichigaya, Lisa Imai)

[Restaurant]

Arisa: We're going to have a conversation simulation...? What does that mean?
Lisa: Two of us will pretend to be Ran and Himari, and then we'll start talking! You never know, it might help~.
Lisa: Alright, why don't we start with you, Arisa? I'll be Himari, and you can be Ran, Rimi!
Rimi: Huh? Me?! Ran-chan?!
Arisa: Yeah, I can't even imagine...
Lisa: Don't worry about it! It'll be fine~! As for the setting~... How about we use CiRCLE?
Lisa: The biggest thing we all have in common is music, after all. If we do that, you should be able to come up with stuff to talk about.
Arisa: O-okay... Here I go!
Rimi: ...
Arisa: R-Ran-chan! H-hello...
Rimi: Yeah...?
Arisa: So, uhm... Th-the weather's pretty nice today, isn't it?
Rimi: Yeah... It's not bad...
Lisa: Hold on, stop right there!!
Lisa: Is that how Ran usually is? It seemed like way too stiff of an impression to me.
Rimi: Ooo... I don't know Ran-chan very well... But I've heard she's the cool, silent type...
Rimi: And I feel like she says 'not bad' a lot...
Arisa: Ahaha! I don't know, I kinda liked that Ran-chan.
Lisa: And you, Arisa!
Arisa: Y-yes?!
Lisa: What was with that talk about the weather? How exactly did you plan to follow that up?!
Arisa: Ngh...
Lisa: How is anyone supposed to learn about your interests with that?
Arisa: My interests...? Y-you're right... I wasn't really opening up there, was I?
Lisa: Exactly, you gotta tell them more about yourself!
Arisa: G-got it! Lisa-san, Rimi! Give me one more shot!
Arisa: Let's see... What are my interests...? I need to tell them more about myself... Ah! That's it!
Arisa: Ran-chan! I actually like bonsai gardening. You do flower-arranging, don't you? Wh-what do you think... of bonsai trees?
Rimi: ...
Rimi: Bonsai gardening's... not bad...
Arisa: Really...? Thank goodness... Hold on! Rimi, stop!
Rimi: That was wrong too, wasn't it~? I can't do this...
Lisa: Ahahaha! You did great, though, Arisa!
Lisa: Now I'll come in as Himari. Let's see if you can keep up, Arisa~.
Lisa: Arisa-chan, yahoo~!
Arisa: Huh?! 'Yahoo'?! Y-yahoo~...
Lisa: What are you up to, Arisa-chan? I was just practicing with the band~! And now I'm taking a break! Phew~, I'm so tired~.
Arisa: Talk about intense... Uhhh, I just had rehearsal too! What a coincidence~!
Lisa: No kidding~! It must be fate~♪Welp, I'm all rested up now. I feel like I'm ready for anything!
Arisa: Huh?! A-already?! You just got her-...
Lisa: Okay~! Back to practice I go! Hey, hey, hoh~!!
Arisa: ...
Lisa: Arisa, why are you so quiet?
Arisa: I know Himari-chan's pretty cheery, but is she really that extreme...?
Lisa: Huh? I thought I had her down pretty well.
Arisa: That was totally wr-... I think you exaggerated a little.
Lisa: A-ahaha... I suppose I did go a little overboard...
Arisa: Is this lesson... really going to work...?
