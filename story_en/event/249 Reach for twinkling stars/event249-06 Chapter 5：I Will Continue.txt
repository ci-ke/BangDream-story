event249-06 Chapter 5: I Will Continue

On the train home after playing together. LAYER and PAREO's shadows are cast in the light shining in from the train window…

(Character: Rei Wakana, Reona Nyubara)

[After the Session]

[Train]

LAYER (Rei): Whew... Feels like things have finally calmed down.
PAREO (Reona): Yes, we've been walking around since noon, and it's been very lively since everyone else joined.
PAREO (Reona): I had a lot of fun today. Thank you very much, LAYER-san.
LAYER (Rei): Why? I haven't done anything to deserve such a formal thank you.
PAREO (Reona): Really? I thought that you were being considerate of me and took me out today for some distraction…
LAYER (Rei): Was it a nice distraction?
PAREO (Reona): Yes, of course.
LAYER (Rei): I'm glad to hear it.
PAREO (Reona): Fufu... Thank you as always.
LAYER (Rei): Which station do you change trains at?
PAREO (Reona): It's a little further. What about you, LAYER-san?
LAYER (Rei): I'm about three stops away. There's a studio close to the station that the people I support often use.
PAREO (Reona): I see. Still, it must be tough to practice now after such a day...
LAYER (Rei): Yeah, but well, I like it.
PAREO (Reona): Eh...
LAYER (Rei): It's tiring, but music is always fun. I haven't felt like I don't want to play recently.
LAYER (Rei): Recently, or rather, since I joined RAS.
PAREO (Reona): ...Is it because you love it that you can always give your all, LAYER-san?
LAYER (Rei): I haven't really thought about it that way... But maybe you're right.
LAYER (Rei): I've been through a lot before I came here, but I still love music.
PAREO (Reona): ...
LAYER (Rei): I feel like there were a lot of hardships and sad memories while I was making music. But I've always loved music.
LAYER (Rei): When I was a little girl, I was more reclusive than I am now, and I worried about every little thing. But I was always happy when I was singing.
LAYER (Rei): To be honest, there were times when I wanted to quit. But in the end, music was all I had…
LAYER (Rei): I think I'll probably continue to do music from now on too.
PAREO (Reona): Is that... for the rest of your life...?
LAYER (Rei): For the rest of my life...
LAYER (Rei): Yes. I'm sure I'll do it until I die.
PAREO (Reona): Um... Do your parents know about this?
LAYER (Rei): Yes, they do. They're supporting me.
PAREO (Reona): I see... so that's the case.
PAREO (Reona): I see...
LAYER (Rei): PAREO…?
PAREO (Reona): Umm... one more thing... just one more thing, please let me ask you…!
PAREO (Reona): Even if you're no longer with everyone at RAS... will you still continue with your music?
LAYER (Rei): What…!?
LAYER (Rei): ... With everyone at RAS...
LAYER (Rei): ...
LAYER (Rei): ... I'll continue.
PAREO (Reona): …!
LAYER (Rei): Sorry... but I don't think I should lie about my feelings…
LAYER (Rei): I'll keep playing music... No matter what.
PAREO (Reona): That's right...That's why LAYER-san is part of RAS...!
PAREO (Reona): It's not just LAYER-san. I'm sure everyone else, except me, would say that...!
LAYER (Rei): What's wrong, PAREO...!?
PAREO (Reona): I...! I can't answer right away that I'm going to keep playing music until I die...!
PAREO (Reona): I'm the only one who can't answer that...!
LAYER (Rei): That's…
PAREO (Reona): I can't tell my mom and dad that I want to pursue a career in music..!
PAREO (Reona): I even wrote the name of some bland school or another on the school application form!
LAYER (Rei): PAREO...!
PAREO (Reona): LAYER-san, what should I do…!?
PAREO (Reona): As myself... As PAREO...! Even though I see things differently than you guys, I really want to be with you all...!!
PAREO (Reona): What should I do…!!
LAYER (Rei): You've been struggling with this for a long time...! I'm sorry I didn't notice...!
PAREO (Reona): Ugh... Ugh...!!
LAYER (Rei): It's okay...! No one is going to hate you, and no one's leaving you.
LAYER (Rei): It's okay... It's okay..!
PAREO (Reona): LAYER-san...! Ugh... Ugh... Sob~...!
(Black cut)
