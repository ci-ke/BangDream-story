The career planning surveys they've filled out. The futures they each envision...

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Tae Hanazono, Rimi Ushigome, Saya Yamabuki, Arisa Ichigaya)

[A few days later after school]

[Ichigaya Residence - Basement]

Kasumi: So yeah, everyone in the performing bands was super hyped about making this the best festival ever!

Saya: Yep, and I’ve also shared the performance durations with them.

Tae: By the way, what did everyone write on their career planning survey?
Arisa: ......

Arisa: Is that really what you wrote down!? I told you not to write it, didn’t I!?

Kasumi: Keiho seemed really great too... but when I visited Yotsuba the other day, it made my heart race like crazy!
Saya: So you'll be at Yotsuba with me then. What about you, Tae?
Tae: Studio musician.
Kasumi: Whoa! That’s so like you, O-Tae! Didn’t the people you worked with before say they'd reach out to you again?
Tae: That, and Yotsuba. I'm still deciding, but I think it’d be fun to go to university while also working as a studio musician—kind of like living a double life.
Arisa: Well, that’s what you call juggling two things at once...
Rimi: Hehe, but if it’s O-Tae-chan, I feel like she could totally pull it off!
Tae: And what about you, Arisa?

Saya: Wow, really? I thought you’d aim for a higher-ranking university, Arisa.
Arisa: Ah, well, Rinko-senpai told me all about how great Yotsuba is.
Arisa: Things like the curriculum and the atmosphere? I’ve been thinking for a while that it might suit me...
Saya: Yeah, academic ability is important, but so is finding a place that feels like a good fit for you.
Kasumi: So, there’s a chance we could end up at the same university, right...? Wow~~! If that happens, it’d be so much fun!
Arisa: Haha, Kasumi, you’re getting ahead of yourself as usual. You have to actually pass the exam first, right? Better study hard for it!
Kasumi: Alright, alright, I get it already~~!
Tae: What about you, Rimi?
Rimi: Well... I’ve been thinking... I’d like to study abroad.
Arisa: Huh...
Kasumi: Study abroad!? That’s amazing!! Rimi-rin, are you planning to go overseas!?
Rimi: I haven’t really made any preparations yet, but... I’ve been thinking for a while that it’s something I want to try.
Rimi: I want to grow into a bigger version of myself than I am now. That’s why I’m excited about studying abroad!
Saya: Rimi-rin studying abroad, huh... I hadn’t really imagined that.
Tae: As for me, I’ve thought for a while now that Rimi might study abroad someday.
Saya: Really? Is that so? But wait, why do you look so proud, O-Tae?
Rimi: O-Tae-chan... thank you for back then.
Tae: Huh? Did I do something?
Rimi: Hehe... still, thank you.
Arisa: ............
Rimi: Arisa-chan... Are you okay?
Arisa: Ah, no, I’m just a little surprised, like... Study abroad? That’s amazing, Rimi...!
Rimi: Hehe... I’ll have to study a lot harder than I am doing now, though.
Kasumi: Rimi-rin studying abroad, huh~. Oh! If that happens, maybe we could connect between here and where you’re studying and try doing a live stream concert!
Rimi: Like what Glitter*Green did at Rockin’ Star Fes?
Kasumi: Exactly!
Saya: Ohh, that sounds great! It’d be fun!
Tae: That’s a good idea too, but I’d love for us to go visit Rimi and do a live show locally.
Kasumi: Not just a livestream, but a Poppin'Party overseas live?! That would be so much fun!
Arisa: Haha, right? That’d really get everyone hyped up.
