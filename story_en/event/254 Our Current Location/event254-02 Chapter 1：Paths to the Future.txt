[The Next Day]

[Ichigaya Residence - Basement]

Kasumi: Flower Bloom Rock Festival— It’s officially happening!!
All: Yayyy!
Saya: With approval from the local revitalization department, the shopping district, and the live house, it’s all set now... Things are about to get really busy.
Tae: Yeah. But ever since Kasumi said, ‘Let’s do it,’ everything’s been moving so quickly. It feels like it’s all going so smoothly.
Rimi: Totally! It feels like everything went by so fast up until now.
Rimi: Back then, I never would’ve imagined this would turn into a music event involving all of Hanasakigawa.
Arisa: Yeah. When Kasumi said, 'Let’s do something big!' on the train ride back from Osaka, I just thought, 'Oh, here she goes again with another idea.
Saya: Hehe, and yet, wasn’t it you, Arisa, who got the most excited and helped push things forward the most?
Arisa: Huh? N-No way. I just thought, if we’re gonna do it, we need to be realistic about it, that’s all...
Kasumi: Yeah, yeah! We all thought a lot about it together. What does 'something big' even mean? What do we actually want to do?
Kasumi: And then, like that festival in Osaka, we wanted to show everyone in Hanasakigawa —who we love so much— a view of music spreading everywhere.
Kasumi: A festival where we set up multiple stages in the parks of Hanasakigawa, and tons of bands take turns performing live all day long...!
Rimi: And we even got the shopping district and all kinds of live houses to cooperate with us.
Rimi: It’d be so awesome if the venue is packed on the day, with food stands and food trucks making it super lively!
Saya: Dad’s already super fired up. He said he’s gonna make a special chocolate cornet just for the festival~.
Rimi: Really? I can’t wait to try Yamabuki Bakery’s special chocolate cornet—it sounds amazing~!
Tae: My part-time job at the music store is pitching in big time too. They’re planning to set up an instrument trial corner and even a live jam session corner. The manager’s super motivated about it.
Kasumi: Wowww! That’s awesome! Let’s do everything we can to make sure it’s a huge success on the day!
Rimi: Yeah!
Arisa: I mean, preparing for this and running it is gonna be way harder than just hosting a regular live show. Do you guys really understand that?
Kasumi: Of course we do! We absolutely want to make this a success! Let’s all do our best!
Arisa: No, that’s not what I meant...
Rimi: Arisa-chan?
Arisa: Everyone’s already so busy with part-time jobs and cram school as it is. And more importantly...
Arisa: We’re third years now. We need to seriously start thinking about stuff like that, don’t we?
Tae: Stuff like what?
Arisa: Well... the future, or like, what path we’re going to take.
Rimi: (Our future path, huh... I’ve been thinking about it a lot, but...)
Kasumi: Yeah, deciding your future path is super important... But there’s one thing I’ve already known for a long time! And that is—
Arisa: Enough with the sparkling heartpounding stuff! You need to think about something realistic that you can actually submit as your future plan to school.
Kasumi: Ughhh, Saya~~! Arisa’s being so strict!
Saya: Haha... Well, she’s not wrong. We’re third years now, so we really have to think about it seriously.
Saya: As for me, I’m planning to go to university. Right now, I’m thinking Yotsuba University might be good. I’ve been talking about it with my dad too.
Rimi: Saya-chan, you’ve been talking about your future plans with your dad?
Saya: Yeah. Ever since then, we’ve been having little talks about it more often... It feels like my whole family really understands my plans now... Thank you so much for back then, Rimi-rin.
Rimi: No, I didn’t really do anything worth thanking me for. But... I’m really glad for you, Saya-chan.
Rimi: (To be able to talk about it in front of everyone like that... Saya-chan is amazing. Me... I still don’t have that kind of courage at all.)
Kasumi: You know... Right now, Saya, you’re just so... sparkly, if that makes sense! You’re super cool!
Tae: Yeah, it feels like you’re facing your future head-on.
Saya: Hey, hey, don’t praise me so much. You’re making me feel embarrassed.
Kasumi: But it’s true that you’re super cool! Right, Arisa? You think so too, don’t you?
Arisa: (...Saya’s first choice is Yotsuba, huh...)
Arisa: (I wonder about everyone else... Are they all aiming for Yotsuba too...?)
Kasumi: Arisa? Hey, Arisa? What’s up? You’re spacing out.
Arisa: N-Nothing. It’s just that things are gonna get busy, so we need to keep our schedules in sync and make sure we’re properly prepared.
Kasumi: Of course!
Kasumi: Everyone, let’s make the Flower Bloom Rock Festival an absolute success! Let’s give it our all!
All: Yeah!
