event169-03 Chapter 2: Same as Always, But...

Ran studies Moca to understand the world she's seeing now, however...

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa)

[Residential Area]

Moca: Morning, everyone~... Yawn...
Ran: Oh, Moca. Morning.
Himari: ... Whoa, Moca... You stayed up late again, didn't you?
Tsugumi: Fufu, your eyes aren't even fully open yet.
Tomoe: I bet you probably got hooked on some manga or something again, right?
Moca: Got it in one, Tomo-chin~. I'm just too much of a bookworm~.
Himari: Gosh, make sure you don't fall asleep in class, got it?
Moca: Okaaay~, I'll try~.
Ran: (She's the same as always, right? Moca hasn't changed, but her guitar playing has...)
Ran: (In any case, I've got to keep a close eye on her today. I need to figure out what the world looks like from Moca's perspective.)
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[After School]

[Haneoka Girls' Academy - Class 2A]

Ran: (There's nothing different about her at all... I didn't notice her acting weird or anything.)
Ran: (Same as always, she's sleeping during class, doodling in her notebook, and eating bread. That's all.)
Ran: (But I know there's got to be something in that daily routine she sees that we can't.)
Himari: Hey, Ran? Did you know the due date for our modern lit. homework is tomorrow?!
Ran: Huh? Oh. Yeah, it is, huh?
Moca: We all talked about it, didn't we~? Hii-chan, you're such an airhead~.
Himari: Ugh, what am I gonna do? I haven't even started~... Moca...! You're good at Japanese, right? Help me write this report!
Moca: Ehhh~. Professor Aoba's courses are pretty expensive, you know~?
Himari: Then, Tomoe! And if that doesn't work, Tsugu!
Tomoe: So it doesn't matter who helps you...
Tsugumi: Sorry, Himari-chan! I'm not finished yet, either. We can work on it together, though.
Himari: Tsuguuu~! I knew I could count on you! C'mon everyone, you're all gonna help too, right?!
Moca: I guess if you insist~. I'll show you the ropes, so you better be prepared~.
Ran: Where are we going to do this? The restaurant?
Himari: That'll work! I was just in the mood for some of their new fall-themed desserts, anyway!
Moca: Don't forget what we're going there for, Hii-chan~. You can't write any reports on the stuff you eat, got it~?
Himari: I know, I know!
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Restaurant]

Tomoe: Yo, Himari. Your hand's stopped.
Himari: I just need a break! Tomoe, help me out once I'm ready again~.
Tsugumi: I think I'll take a break too. I'm still having trouble putting together a good conclusion.
Himari: Oh, that's where I'm stuck, too! Hey, Moca~, show me yours! Please!
Moca: Ah~, you're planning on stealing all my hard work~. Cheating's bad, Hii-chan.
Himari: I won't copy it, honest! I'll just reference it! Okay?!
Moca: Well~, I guess I can't say no if you're that desperate~. I'm super nice, after all~. Here.
Himari: Thanks~! ... Wait, what is this? You scribbled all over your homework!
Ran: Wow, you're right... Pfft. Look at this.
Tomoe: Ahaha, it's Himari! You look real happy surrounded by all those sweets~!
Himari: Hey, my face isn't this round, is it?! Wait, did I gain weight recently...?!
Tsugumi: No, I don't think so. But you do look like this when you eat something you like!
Ran: Fufu, yeah. Hey, hold onto this paper for a sec, Himari. I want to take a picture.
Himari: No way! And you've gotta erase all this before you turn it in, Moca.
Moca: Okaaay~. I'll do it later~.
Ran: Hey, Moca. You're always doodling this sort of stuff during class, aren't you?
Moca: Not always, but I guess I do doodle a lot...? Whenever I get a really good idea, my hand starts moving on its own, you know~.
Tsugumi: Huh? So when we saw you scribbling in your notebook after class, that was...?
Moca: Hm~? I don't really remember that well, but yeah, I was probably drawing something~.
Tomoe: What, that's what it was? I was shocked, girl. I thought you'd suddenly decided class was more important than bread.
Moca: Hey, that hurts, Tomo-chin~. That makes it sound like I don't take studying seriously~.
Tomoe: Ah, sorry, yea- Wait. You don't, though.
Tsugumi, Himari & Moca: Ahahaha!
Ran: (It's the same as always, but there's something here that only Moca can see...)
Ran: (That passion I can feel from Moca's guitar, it's...)
(Black cut)
