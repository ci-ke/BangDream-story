event51-01 Opening: Ako SOS

As the members of Afterglow finish up their practice, Ako sends a message to Tomoe, and it's an emergency!

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa, Ako Udagawa)

[Riverside]

Tomoe: Ah~, it really is hot out!
Himari: Why do you sound so happy saying that...?  This heat is exhausting~...
Moca: Hearing the cicadas only makes it worse~...  I can't take it~. I'm burning up~. Melting down~.
Ran: Moca. Too much talking. Chill out...
Tsugumi: Ahaha, the studio was nice and cool, so you can really feel the difference in temperature out here, can't you?
Tomoe: ... Oh? A message from Ako.
Tomoe: "SIS! HELP ME~!!"
Tsugumi: D-did something happen to Ako-chan?
Himari: She always messages Tomoe right away when she's in trouble.
Moca: She's really dependent on you, Tomo-chin~.
Tomoe: You... could say that, yeah. But, as a big sister, I'm happy to know she trusts me.  I want to be someone she can count on.
Himari: Ako-chan's so lucky~.
Tsugumi: Himari-chan, don't you have a big sister too?
Himari: Yeah, but you know what I mean~! The grass is always greener on the other side, right?
Moca: Ah~, kinda like how the buns other people eat always look so tasty~?
Himari: Well, yeah, but at the same time, no~!
Tomoe: Ahaha. Alright, since Ako's messaged me, I'm gonna head home. See you guys later!
Ran: Tomoe really has a soft spot for Ako, doesn't she?
Moca: Yeah~. I wonder what will happen when Ako-chin grows up~.
Ran: You know, I can't even imagine that.
Moca: Me either~.
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Udagawa Residence]

Tomoe: Ako, I'm home! What's up?
Ako: Sis~! Help me~!
Tomoe: Whoa! Ako, calm down!
(Black cut)
Tomoe: Gotcha. Homework, huh...? What part are you having trouble with?
Ako: Ooo... This one, and this one, and this one, and... this one.
Tomoe: Hahaha! So in other words, you don't understand any of it.
Ako: I'm sorry...
Tomoe: It's fine. I'm not mad.  Come on, we'll go through them one by one. Let's see...
(Black cut)
Ako: I'm finally done~!! Sis, thank you so, so, so much~!!
Tomoe: Is that everything? Good!  Next time, you're doing everything on your own, got it~?
Ako: I'll... try!
Tomoe: Isn't it a little early for you to be asking for my help, though? Usually you wait until the end of summer to send me an SOS text.
Ako: That's because Roselia has a concert next week! I want to have plenty of time to practice.
Tomoe: So that's it! Now that I think about it, everyone in Roselia seems like the type to finish their summer homework right away~.
Ako: Hey, Sis, you should come to our show! Please~!
Tomoe: Oh, good idea! I haven't been to one in a while, so why not?  I'm free next week, anyway.
Ako: Really?! Yay~!!  I'll make sure to give my coolest performance ever!
Tomoe: I'm looking forward to it, Ako!
Ako: Yeah. I have to tell Rin-rin right away~♪  Oh~! I can't wait~!!
