Tomoe: Heck, maybe even more than me!
Ako: More than you...?
Tomoe: For sure! You don't need to focus on being like me! You're already doing great as Roselia's drummer right now!
Tomoe: I mean, you didn't even ask for my help with that solo,  and it was perfect!
Ako: Really...? You think so?
Tomoe: Definitely. You're doing amazing stuff, Ako!  Man~, looks like you don't need me anymore~. Ahaha... haha...
Ako: ... Sis...

Tomoe: ... What the heck was I thinking...?
