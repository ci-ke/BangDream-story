event264-01 Opening: A Heart That’s Still Unhealed

A few days after the AMF show, Harumi comes to deliver unexpected news...

(Character: Yukina Minato, Sayo Hikawa, Lisa Imai, Ako Udagawa, Rinko Shirokane)

[Minato Residence - Yukina's Room]

Yukina: (I still can't shake off the shock of that day, even though it's been three days since All Music Fes...)
Yukina: (We prepared as best we could for one of Japan's top music festivals, "All Music Fes.")
Yukina: (And yet... our performance skills, the energy to excite the audience, even our resolve to stand on that stage... none of it was enough.)
Yukina: (Just thinking about it makes my chest tremble. Is this nerves... or something else?)
Yukina: A message... it's from Lisa.
Lisa: "Hey, I've got something to do at university today, so I’ll head straight to the office after that. I’ll make sure I’m not late."
Yukina: ...This will be my first time seeing everyone since that day.
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Music Agency - Meeting Room]

Harumi: —That covers this month’s schedule. If anyone has questions, feel free to ask.
Yukina: No, everything looks fine.
Harumi: Also, this might be a complicated topic for you all...
Yukina: ...Go ahead, let’s hear it.
Harumi: Ever since Roselia performed at All Music Fes, you've been attracting a lot of attention.
Lisa: ......
Rinko: We're attracting attention...?
Sayo: What exactly do you mean...?
Harumi: It means your appearance at All Music Fes is being recognized by the industry.
Sayo: Eh...?
Yukina: ......
Harumi: You're receiving a number of offers for interviews and discussions from various media outlets.
Harumi: As your agency, we believe this is an opportunity we shouldn’t miss.
Harumi: The schedule is still being adjusted, but things will likely be even busier starting next month.
Rinko: I-I think that’s... something we should be thankful for...
Ako: But why...?
Harumi: Huh...?
Ako: I mean, all the other performers at All Music Fes were way more skilled than us. We didn’t measure up at all...
Harumi: We know you weren't satisfied with your performance... but the fact you're being recognized like this is still incredible!
Sayo & Rinko: ......
Lisa: ...Harumi-san’s right! If we all stay down in the dumps, I feel like that’d be disrespectful...
Lisa: So... um...
Rinko: Imai-san...
Harumi: I-I’m sorry. I’m not trying to force you to cheer up... I just don’t want you to carry this all by yourselves...
Yukina: ...Thank you, Harumi-san. Anything else we should know?
Harumi: Let’s see... about the second album.
Harumi: We prioritized All Music Fes, but now it’s time to start focusing on our second album.
Yukina: Yes, you're right. As we discussed before, Lisa and I will be splitting the lyrics this time.
Lisa: ......
Harumi: Understood. You have band practice now, correct? I’ll check in again later to see how things are going.
Yukina: Yes, understood.
Roselia: ......
Ako: ...Sorry, I said too much.
Sayo: I understand how you feel, Udagawa-san. I felt conflicted as well.
Sayo: Having a performance we weren’t satisfied with receive praise... it’s such a frustrating feeling.
Yukina: ...Yeah. But for now, we have no choice but to accept it.
Ako, Sayo & Rinko: ...Right.
Lisa: ......
Yukina: Lisa, about the lyrics—
Lisa: Ah, yeah... what is it...?
Yukina: Until the lyrics are done, I want to increase the time we spend reviewing them. Is that okay with you?
Lisa: ...Um...
Yukina: ...Lisa?
Lisa: About that...
Lisa: Can we maybe forget about the lyric writing thing altogether...?
All: ...!?
(Black cut)
(Background change)
