event264-03 Chapter 2: Feelings Beyond Words

Yukina tries to understand Lisa’s true feelings, but Lisa’s reply is...

(Character: Yukina Minato, Sayo Hikawa, Lisa Imai, Ako Udagawa, Rinko Shirokane)

[Walking Home]

Lisa & Yukina: ...
Yukina: ...Hey, Lisa.
Lisa: Hm...?
Yukina: I wanted to talk to you about today...
Lisa: Ah—sorry. It's my fault practice ended early, huh...
Yukina: That’s not it. Everyone just needed time. That’s all.
Yukina: What I meant was... about the lyrics.
Lisa: ...!
Yukina: If you're blaming yourself for pushing us to perform at All Music Fes, don’t.
Yukina: We all made that decision together. There's no need to carry it alone.
Lisa: ...So you heard from Rinko, huh.
Lisa: Thanks. But... that's not the only reason I backed out of writing lyrics.
Yukina: What...?
Lisa: After our set at All Music Fes, you all went to watch the other bands right away, didn’t you?
Lisa: To move forward. Even though you knew it would hurt.
Lisa: When I saw your back that day... you felt so far away from me...
Yukina: ...That’s not true—
Lisa: It is...
Lisa: I couldn’t follow you right away.
Lisa: Rinko brought me to your side, but... I couldn’t bring myself to watch the stage.
Lisa: I barely even heard the music. All I could think was, "I want to leave. I don’t want to hear any more music."
Yukina: Lisa...
Lisa: I felt so ashamed of myself for thinking that...  When all of you were trying to move forward...!
Lisa: That’s why I don’t think I deserve to write a song for Roselia...!
Yukina: So that’s how you felt about that day...
Lisa & Yukina: ......
Lisa: Sorry for dropping all of this on you so suddenly...
Yukina: ......
Lisa: ......
Lisa: ...You know what? I think I’ll reconsider writing lyrics.
Yukina: Huh...?
Lisa: I knew it would trouble you, and still I said all that... I’m really sorry...!
Lisa: It’s just how I felt in that moment. I should’ve waited instead of blurting it out. I messed up...
Yukina: Lisa...
Lisa: Um... do you think I could have a little more time? I’ll think it through properly and give you my answer.
Yukina: That’s fine, but...
Lisa: I can’t keep dragging everyone down. I’ll do my best to move forward.
Lisa: I really will...
Yukina: Lisa, what I want to say is—
Lisa: Oh, looks like we’re home already. That was fast.
Lisa: Well then, see you later, Yukina.
Yukina: ...Yeah. See you.
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[A Few Days Later]

[Studio]

Lisa: Good morning, everyone!
Rinko: Imai-san...?
Ako: G-Good morning, Lisa-nee.
Lisa: Sorry about last time! Starting today, I’ll be back to normal!
Sayo: I don’t mind about last time, but... are you really okay?
Lisa: Yeah, I am. I mean, I know it might not look like it... but I’ve sorted out a lot.
Lisa: Alright then, shall we get started with practice? Right, Yukina?
Yukina: Yes... let’s.
Yukina: (She’s clearly forcing herself, but she’s trying to move forward...)
Yukina: (Lisa, you're stronger than you think. I know you’ll rise again...)
Yukina: (But something still doesn’t sit right... no, it’s more like a fog in my mind...)
Yukina: (Even if Lisa recovers with time and we all aim high again... that should be enough...)
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Yukina: (...Then why do I feel this unsettled?)
