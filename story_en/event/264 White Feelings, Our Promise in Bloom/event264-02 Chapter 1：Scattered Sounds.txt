event264-02 Chapter 1: Scattered Sounds

Lisa announces she’ll step down from writing lyrics—shocking the Roselia members...

(Character: Yukina Minato, Sayo Hikawa, Lisa Imai, Ako Udagawa, Rinko Shirokane)

[Music Agency - Meeting Room]

Ako: L-Lisa-nee...?
Sayo: I understand that it's not easy to switch gears, but...
Lisa: I'm sorry for saying something so selfish... But I've been thinking a lot these past few days...
Lisa: I just think... if I don’t write the lyrics, maybe the album will turn out better... That’s why...
Rinko: Imai-san... That’s not...
Yukina: Lisa... Why do you feel that way?
Lisa: Didn’t you hear what Harumi-san said earlier? That Roselia’s performance at All Music Fes brought us a lot of attention...
Lisa: If that’s the case, then our next songs need to be something we can be proud of as Roselia. And right now, I just... don’t think I can write those lyrics.
Ako: But Lisa-nee, you were really looking forward to it...
Lisa: ...Sorry.
Yukina: ...Lisa.
Lisa: ...What?
Yukina: Why do you think you can't do it right now?
Lisa: That’s...
Lisa: ......
Sayo: ...Shall we head to the studio? Our reservation time is approaching.
Ako: Y-Yeah! Lisa-nee, come on!
Lisa: Y-Yeah...
Yukina: (Lisa...)
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Studio]

Yukina: (...No matter how many times we try, something feels off... There's no point in pushing through like this.)
Yukina: ...Let’s take a break for now.
Sayo: Um... Could we try one more time before the break? I’m not satisfied with my performance yet...
Ako: Me too! I want to try again!
Yukina: Let’s not. I don’t think it’s right to keep going in this state.
Rinko: I-I think taking a break is best too... Our sound right now... it’s completely out of sync...
Sayo: But...
Yukina: You know it too. Normally, you would agree with me, wouldn’t you, Sayo?
Sayo: ...!
Yukina: ...We still haven’t recovered from the pain of that stage.
Yukina: That day, our pride was shattered completely. We realized just how far away our goal truly is.
Yukina: Sayo and Ako... You’ve both been practicing non-stop since then, haven’t you?
Yukina: Sayo, your guitar lacks its usual precision. And Ako, your drumming has lost its fire.
Sayo: ...I’m sorry.
Yukina: (And Lisa...)
Lisa: ......
Ako: Yeah... Maybe I’ve been pushing myself too much lately...
Ako: But it’s just been so frustrating... I keep thinking about it whenever I try to sleep...
Ako: I can’t help but wonder... if there was more we could’ve done...
Sayo: Yeah... Even during my university lectures, I kept feeling like I should be practising instead...
Yukina: I understand how strong that sense of urgency is. But we can’t move forward if everyone’s going in a different direction... Don’t you agree?
Lisa: ......!
Yukina: ...Lisa? What’s wrong? Why did you suddenly stand up...?
Lisa: Sorry... I’m just feeling a little tired. I’m gonna go buy a drink.
Ako: Lisa-nee, wait!
Sayo: Let her go for now.
Ako: B-But...
Sayo: I'm sure she just needs a moment alone. The way she played today... it was hard to even listen to.
Yukina: Yeah... You’re right.
Ako: And she said she couldn’t write lyrics anymore...
Ako: Lisa-nee... All Music Fes really shook her that badly...
Rinko: ......
Rinko: I think... Imai-san feels responsible for all of this...
Yukina: ...Rinko, do you know something?
Rinko: Actually...
(Black cut)
Sayo: ...I see. So she blames herself for encouraging us to participate in All Music Fes.
Ako: No way... But it’s not Lisa-nee’s fault...!
Sayo: Exactly. Even if she was the one who spoke up, it was still a decision we all made together.
Rinko: Yes... Even if she hadn’t said anything, I think we still would’ve chosen to perform at All Music Fes...
Rinko: But... I think there’s still a part of her that can’t come to terms with it.
Yukina: Thank you, Rinko... I’ll go speak with Lisa myself.
Yukina: We said it was a break, but let’s end practice here for today.
Yukina: We all need time to heal our wounds.
(Black cut)
(Background change)
