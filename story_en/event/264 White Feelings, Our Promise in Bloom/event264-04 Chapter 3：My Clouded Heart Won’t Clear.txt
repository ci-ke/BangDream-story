Concerned about Lisa, Yukina accepts Rinko’s offer to talk...

(Character: Yukina Minato, Rinko Shirokane)

Rinko: I see... you’re worried about Imai-san.
Yukina: From your perspective, how does Lisa seem to you now?
Rinko: Let’s see... it looks like she’s trying to recover little by little...
Rinko: But knowing Lisa-san, I think she’s pushing herself a lot for our sake too...
Yukina: Yeah...
Rinko: ......
Yukina: ......
Rinko: Um...?
Yukina: Hm? What is it?
Rinko: Oh... it’s just... you seemed lost in thought, so I wondered if maybe you were seeing things differently than I was...
Yukina: That’s not it. I feel the same way. But...
Rinko: ......
Yukina: There’s this feeling like I can’t just sit back and do nothing...
Rinko: I see...
Yukina: Sorry. You were kind enough to reach out, and I ended up rambling aimlessly.
Rinko: No, I’m the one who should apologize for not being more helpful...

Yukina: Rare?
Rinko: Usually, when you come to talk, it’s about Roselia or our music...

Yukina: Is that so...?
Rinko: Maybe... you usually talk to Lisa-san about these things.
Rinko: I think there are things only childhood friends can truly understand...
Yukina: ...That might be true.

Rinko: It did...?
Yukina: ...I think so.
Rinko: Fufu...
Yukina: Hm?
Rinko: Ah, sorry...
Rinko: I just thought—it’s kind of sweet how someone like you, who always writes such beautiful and sharp lyrics, is fumbling for words when it comes to Lisa-san.
Yukina: Yeah... I’ve always been clumsy like this. It’s frustrating.
Yukina: ...Come to think of it, during All Music Fes, you stayed by Lisa’s side the whole time, didn’t you?
Rinko: Y-Yes...
Yukina: Thank you, Rinko. For being there with her.
