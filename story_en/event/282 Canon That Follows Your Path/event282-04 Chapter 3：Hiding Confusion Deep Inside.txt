event282-04 Chapter 3: Hiding Confusion Deep Inside

Mashiro hears Rui’s worries, but she can’t exactly share them with the others...

(Character: Mashiro Kurata, Toko Kirigaya, Nanami Hiromachi, Tsukushi Futaba)

[A Few Days Later]

[Tsukinomori Girls' Academy - Garden Gazebo]

Nanami: Hey, Tsu-chan, Tsu-chan. I brought that manga you asked for the other day—I'll give it to you later.
Tsukushi: Wah, thank you! Ehehe, I've been dying to know what happens next.
Nanami: Fufufu, even Moca-senpai praised that manga. She also recommended the author's previous work too~.
Tsukushi: Oh really? I’ll try looking for it at the bookstore! I’ll bring you one of my favorites next time too!
Mashiro: Oh, you two are already here.
Toko: Fusuke, are you really planning to bring manga? I didn’t know you were such a rebel~.
Tsukushi: I’m not a rebel! ...Wait, Toko-chan. Didn’t you listen to what the principal said the other day?
Toko: Huh? About what?
Nanami: Bringing in magazines and manga is okay now~ As long as you don’t read them during class, or they’ll get taken away immediately.
Tsukushi: And it’s still not allowed to read them on your way to or from school.
Toko: ...Was that really a thing?
Tsukushi: Geez! You weren’t listening at all! You’re a class rep too, you know—
Toko: Okay, okay, I got it! ...Man, you sound just like Rui~.
Nanami: Huh? Speaking of which, where’s Rui-rui?
Mashiro: Ah, I talked to her just before coming. She said she wants to use lunch break too since her composing still hasn’t progressed.
Tsukushi: I see... I hope Rui-san's okay.
Toko: ...Well, if she says she’s fine, then I guess she is? Even if we offered to help, she’d just say “I don’t need it.”
Tsukushi: That’s true, but still...
Nanami: But I get how Tsu-chan feels. It’s rare for Rui-rui’s song to take this long.
Mashiro: ......
Mashiro: (Everyone’s worried... But Rui-san asked me not to say anything...)
Nanami: ...? Shiro-chan, something wrong?
Mashiro: Ah, um...
Mashiro: ...S-so...
Toko: ?
Mashiro: H-has it ever happened to you that after finally achieving something you really wanted, you didn’t know what to do next?
Tsukushi: ......?
Mashiro: A-ah, I mean... my friend was talking about something like that... and I wondered what you all would do...
Toko: Hmm~... I’m not sure, but I feel like that doesn’t happen to me?
Mashiro: Huh?
Toko: I mean, if you did something you wanted to do, wouldn’t you immediately find the next thing you want to do?
Tsukushi: Ahaha, that’s so like you, Toko-chan.
Tsukushi: Hmm, for me, I think I’d just keep doing what I can until I find a new goal.
Mashiro: Fufu, that sounds like you too, Tsukushi-chan.
Mashiro: What about you, Nanami-chan—
Tsukushi: Ah, look at the time! I haven’t finished my lunch yet~!
Toko: Then I’ll help you eat it! Fusuke’s bento is seriously delicious!
Tsukushi: Hey! Don’t take my karaage~!
Nanami: Ahaha, if you keep that up, you’ll really run out of time~.
Mashiro: (Hmm... I got to hear what everyone thinks, but... it doesn’t feel like any of it really answers Rui-san’s problem...)
Nanami: ......
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[After School]

[Tsukinomori Girls' Academy - Class 2A]

Mashiro: There’s no practice today, so I guess I’ll just head home...
Mashiro: (I’ve been trying to think of something I can do for Rui-san, but... maybe it really is too hard...)
Nanami: Ah, there you are, Shiro-chan~.
Mashiro: Oh, Nanami-chan?
Nanami: Shiro-chan, do you have time after this?
Mashiro: Y-yeah... I’m free...
Nanami: That's a relief~. So, will you come shopping with me for a bit~?
