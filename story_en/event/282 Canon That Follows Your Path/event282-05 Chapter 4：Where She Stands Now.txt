event282-05 Chapter 4: Where She Stands Now

After shopping, Nanami opens up about why she invited Mashiro along—

(Character: Mashiro Kurata, Nanami Hiromachi)

[Walking Home]

Nanami: Man~ I’m really glad you came with me today, Shiro-chan~. Thanks for tagging along on such short notice~.
Mashiro: No worries. I don’t usually go to art supply stores alone, so it was a refreshing and fun experience.
Nanami: I’m glad to hear that. Your input was super helpful~.
Mashiro: R-really...?
Nanami: Yeah. When you described the paint colors—like “this one’s like the blue of a deep ocean” or “this green feels like a summer meadow”—it really helped me decide what to go with~.
Mashiro: I see... Fufu, I’m glad I could help, then.
Nanami: Actually, I’ve been thinking of trying out some figure drawing too~.
Nanami: Oh, Maybe I should use Shiro-chan as my model~?
Mashiro: Eh, th-that might be a little embarrassing...
Nanami: Aww, too bad. I bet Toko-chan would be totally into it though.
Mashiro: Ahaha, you’re probably right.
Nanami: ...Hey, Shiro-chan.
Mashiro: ?
Nanami: What you were talking about during lunch today... was that about Rui-rui, by any chance?
Mashiro: Ah...
Mashiro: U-um... well...
Nanami: Ahaha, your eyes are darting all over the place~ You’re not fooling me, you know?
Mashiro: Ugh... actually...
(Black cut)
Nanami: I see~ So Rui-rui’s feeling that way~...
Mashiro: Ugh, Rui-san... I’m sorry...
Nanami: Well, well. Let’s just say I pried it out of you~. But the fact that you brought it up means you’re worried about her, right?
Mashiro: Y-yeah... She said it’s something she has to work through herself, but I keep wondering if that’s really okay...
Nanami: So, what did you think during lunch, Shiro-chan?
Mashiro: M-me...?
Nanami: Yeah. You asked what we thought, but it didn’t seem like any of our answers really resonated with you.
Nanami: I just felt like maybe something came to your mind when you heard what Rui-rui said.
Mashiro: Um... I don’t know if I can explain it well, but...
Mashiro: It kind of reminded me of a little boat out on the ocean.
Nanami: A boat?
Mashiro: Yeah... without a compass or map, not knowing where you are or which way to go...
Mashiro: It just felt... like how I used to be.
Mashiro: I wanted to become something special, but I had no idea how...
Mashiro: So I thought maybe something would change if I entered Tsukinomori. But that wasn’t enough...
Mashiro: Even so, once I met everyone in the band, I finally started to see what I wanted to do, what I wanted to become... little by little...
Nanami: .........
Mashiro: Ah, um... so what I mean is... Ugh, I’m not explaining this very well...
Nanami: ...Fufu, but I think I get it now—what you can do for Rui-rui~.
Mashiro: Huh?
Nanami: Hearing you say all that made me realize something.
Nanami: I think... what Rui-rui needs most right now is the courage to set sail again.
Mashiro: Courage...?
Nanami: You wanted to change something, so you entered Tsukinomori, met the band, and formed Morfonica...
Nanami: You've kept rowing that boat forward all this time—That's the kind of courage she needs, Shiro-chan.
Mashiro: .........
Nanami: So I think... you should just tell Rui-rui exactly what you just told me.
Mashiro: What I said...
Nanami: And even if you don’t phrase it perfectly, Rui-rui’s smart—she’ll understand.
Mashiro: ...Fufu, yeah. That’s probably true.
Mashiro: ...Thank you, Nanami-chan. I’ll talk to Rui-san.
Nanami: No problem~ You can repay me by being my figure model~.
Mashiro: Ehh, that’s still kind of...
Nanami: Kidding, kidding~. I’m counting on you to look after Rui-rui, Shiro-chan.
Mashiro: ...Yeah!
