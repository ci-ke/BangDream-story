event282-07 Ending: The Light That Guides Me

Everyone is excited about the long-awaited new song—until they take a look at the score...

(Character: Mashiro Kurata, Toko Kirigaya, Nanami Hiromachi, Tsukushi Futaba, Rui Yashio)

[A Few Days Later]

[Tsukinomori Girls' Academy - Garden Gazebo]

Rui: ...Sorry to keep you waiting.
Tsukushi: Nope! We just got here too. Great job with your student council work today!
Toko: More importantly~! The new song’s done, right? Come on, show us, show us!
Rui: Right. I’ve kept you all waiting long enough. This is... our new song.
Mashiro: Thank you, Rui-san. I’ll take a look right away.
Toko: Me too, me too! Let’s all look together!
Mashiro, Toko, Nanami & Tsukushi: .........
Rui: .........
Toko: ......This part’s super tricky, right? The riff here is nuts!
Tsukushi: M-my drums too... I wonder if I can even play this...
Nanami: Fufufu, but you know, I think this shows how much trust Rui-rui has in us.
Rui: ...At the very least, I didn’t write anything I thought you couldn’t play as you are now.
Tsukushi & Toko: ......!
Toko: Geez~! If you’re gonna say that, I’ve got no choice~! I mean, living up to expectations is what I, the great Toko-sama, do best!
Tsukushi: And if we can pull it off, it’ll be a super cool song! I think the audience will love it too!
Mashiro: Fufu... Yeah, I think so too.
Rui: ...What do you think of the song, Kurata-san?
Mashiro: Um...
Mashiro: It feels like... a lighthouse gently shining down and guiding you when you’re all alone in a dark sea... That kind of song.
Rui: ...Is that so.
Toko: Shiro’s metaphors are always tough to follow~. And wait, Rui asking for feedback on a song? That’s rare, huh?
Nanami: Fufu, I’m glad for you, Rui-rui~.
Rui: ...I just wanted to confirm whether it matched the lyrics Kurata-san wrote.
Nanami: I see, I see~ Good job, Rui-rui.
Tsukushi: Now that the new song’s finally done, let’s start practicing today!
Mashiro, Toko & Nanami: Yeah~!
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[After School]

[Tsukinomori Girls' Academy - Hallway]

Mashiro: Sigh, I can’t believe the teacher dumped something on me all of a sudden...
Mashiro: And Toko-chan disappeared before I even realized it...
Rui: ...Kurata-san.
Mashiro: Wah, you startled me~... Are you heading to the atelier too, Rui-san?
Rui: Yes, I needed to turn in some paperwork to the principal. Also, there’s something I want to give you.
Mashiro: Is this... a score?
Mashiro: ...Huh? This isn’t the same song you gave us earlier at lunch...
Rui: This is the music that embodies what I’m striving for right now.
Mashiro: ...!
Rui: The song I gave the band includes the skills we’ll need to one day perform this piece.
Rui: I... want to perform the song I gave you with Morfonica someday. That’s the destination I’m aiming for now.
Mashiro: I see, this song is... But why give it to me?
Rui: You’re the one who’ll be writing the lyrics, right?
Mashiro: Ah, yeah, that’s true...!
Rui: When the time comes and you feel like you want to perform this song live, I want you to write the lyrics for it.
Rui: Until then, I’m entrusting this song to you.
Mashiro: ...Okay. I’ll keep it safe.
Rui: All right, thank you.
Mashiro: ...But—
Rui: ?
Mashiro: Th-this is a lot of pressure... I usually write the lyrics first...
Rui: ...Maybe it’s good for you to be in my shoes for once, Kurata-san.
Mashiro: Ah, did you just smile, Rui-san...?
Rui: ...You’re imagining things. Come on, let’s go.
(Black cut)
(Background change)
