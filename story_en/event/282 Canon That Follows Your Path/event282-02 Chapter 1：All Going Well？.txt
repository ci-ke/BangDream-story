event282-02 Chapter 1: All Going Well?

Practice for the next live show is going smoothly—but when the topic of the new song comes up...?

(Character: Mashiro Kurata, Toko Kirigaya, Nanami Hiromachi, Tsukushi Futaba, Rui Yashio)

[A Few Days Later]

[Hiromachi Residence - Atelier]

Tsukushi: Alright, let’s take a break for now!
Toko: Man~, my performance just now was flawless~! Even you’ve gotta admit it, right Rui?
Rui: That was far from what I’d consider flawless. Let’s just say it was passable.
Toko: What~!? Rui, you still don’t get it~.
Mashiro: Ahaha...
Nanami: Still, hearing you say “passable” is pretty rare~ You’re usually much more strict.
Toko: Yeah, true... Guess it just shows how great our performance really was if Rui had to acknowledge it!
Rui: ...You’re free to interpret it however you like.
Tsukushi: But everyone’s sounding really good lately! Mashiro-chan’s voice has even more range than usual!
Mashiro: Ehehe, really...?
Tsukushi: Fufufu, in that case, maybe...
Nanami: ? Tsu-chan, What’s up? You’re grinning kinda weird...
Tsukushi: Actually, a junior came up to me today and said they’re coming to our live!
Tsukushi: I didn’t know them, but they said they’re really looking forward to it!
Toko: Man~, our time has totally come!
Tsukushi: So get this—turns out they’d never been to a live house before, so I gave them a quick rundown!
Mashiro: Fufu, that reminds me of the first time I went to a live show with you, Tsukushi-chan.
Tsukushi: T-this time I explained everything properly! They’ll be fine!
Toko: Whoa, that takes me back~! If we hadn’t gone to that live, Morfonica might never have formed!
Tsukushi: You were so random back then, Toko-chan... You kept calling me stuff like Yamada or Tanaka...

Rui: ...Not something you should be saying.
Nanami: Fufu, if students from Tsukinomori are coming, we’d better bring our A-game~.
Toko: I’ve been inviting a bunch of people too, so the next live’s gonna be packed for sure~!
Toko: Oh, I invited that girl too! You know, that girl from Tsukinomori who was at the sakura party!
Nanami: At the sakura party... Ah, was it Soyo-chan?
Toko: Yeah yeah, someone like that! She said she’d come if she was free!
Tsukushi: That kind of sounds like a soft no...
Toko: Nah nah, she’ll definitely come! She had that look!
Mashiro: Ahaha...
Rui: ...Are we done with practice for today? If there’s nothing else, I’ll be heading out.
Toko: Oh right, the new song! How’s it going? Think it’ll be cool?
Rui: ...It’s still incomplete, so I can’t say yet.
Tsukushi: But, that’s unusual. Normally by this point you’d already have something ready.
Rui: ...
Tsukushi: Ah, s-sorry! I didn’t mean to rush you or anything...!
Rui: It’s fine. It’s true I’m behind compared to usual.
Nanami: Are you not feeling well or something?
Rui: (...Should I say I'm just not feeling well?)
Rui: (I’ve been working on it every day since getting the lyrics, but I still can’t settle on anything—not just that phrase, but the whole song...)
Rui: (...Maybe the reason is—)
Mashiro: ...Rui-san?
Rui: Sorry, I was just lost in thought.
Tsukushi: If the student council work is too much, I’m always happy to help!
Rui: No need. If I ever do need help, I’ll let you know then.
Tsukushi: If you say so...
Toko: If you’re in a slump, just tell us, okay~? I’ll whip up a super cool phrase for you!
Nanami: Well, there’s still time before the live. Let’s call it a day for now.
Tsukushi: Sounds good! Good work, everyone!
Mashiro: Rui-san... could it be...
