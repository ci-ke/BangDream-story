event282-01 Opening: Toward a New Challenge

While having lunch at the gazebo, the Morfonica members talk about their upcoming live show.

(Character: Mashiro Kurata, Toko Kirigaya, Nanami Hiromachi, Tsukushi Futaba, Rui Yashio)

[Recess]

[Tsukinomori Girls' Academy - Garden Gazebo]

Tsukushi: Sorry to keep you two waiting! The teacher asked me to run an errand...
Mashiro: No worries, we just got here too.
Toko: Yeah yeah. Plus, Rui isn’t here yet either.
Nanami: Another student council thing?
Mashiro: Yeah. She said she’d come once she’s done...
Tsukushi: Oh, a message from Rui-san. “I’ll join you in about ten minutes.” Glad it won’t take too long.
Toko: Okay then, let’s eat lunch while we wait!
Tsukushi: Right. Let’s put our hands together.
All: Let’s dig in~!
Nanami: I’m really glad we’re allowed to use our phones at school now. Times like this really make me feel that~.
Toko: For real! It used to be such a pain without them!
Mashiro: Yeah, but...
Nanami: ?
Mashiro: Toko-chan, didn’t your phone get confiscated during class the other day because it rang...?
Toko: That was totally not my fault! I just forgot to put it on silent~. I kept saying it wasn’t on purpose—teachers are so uptight~.
Tsukushi: Even if it wasn’t on purpose, it’s still not okay! You’ve got to follow the school rules properly!
Nanami: But if you write a reflection essay, they’ll give it back, right?
Toko: Yep! So for a reflection essay master like me, it’s easy-peasy!
Mashiro: I don’t think being a “master” of those is something to brag about...
Rui: ...She’s absolutely right. You are the class rep after all—show some responsibility.
Nanami: Oh, Rui-rui. Thanks for your hard work with the student council~.
Tsukushi: Rui-san, what kind of work did you have today?
Rui: Remember that survey we did about school rules? The principal had some questions about it.
Mashiro: Oh yeah, it was to get more student opinions, right...?
Toko: I wrote so much on that one, I ran out of space!
Rui: We got a bunch of requests, so it’ll take time to sort through, but it sounds like some of them might be considered.
Nanami: That’s good to hear~.
Rui: Of course, it’s unlikely they’ll approve everything.
Tsukushi: Right, we wouldn’t want even important rules to be loosened.
Toko: Well, enough of that serious stuff! You all saw the message I sent yesterday, right? What do you think?
Nanami: You mean performing at a new live house? I mentioned it in the chat, but I'm all for it~.
Tsukushi: Me too! But why a new venue? I thought CiRCLE or RiNG would be fine...
Toko: C’mon! We’re Morfonica—the band that shook up the whole school with the Tsukinomori Music Festival! We’ve gotta expand our range!
Mashiro: I-I don’t know if we shook up the whole school exactly...
Nanami: Fufu, but we do get people saying they’ll come see us live. That makes me really happy~.
Tsukushi: Yeah yeah! And we’ve been focused on Tsukinomori Music Festival for a while, so we haven’t done any shows lately.
Toko: Alright, sounds like almost everyone’s in! Rui, you good with it too?
Rui: Yes, I don’t mind.
Toko: Okay! I’ll go ahead and make the arrangements!
Nanami: And I think you said you wanted to do a new song too, right, Toko-chan?
Toko: Of course! It’s our first live in a while—we’ve gotta challenge ourselves!
Tsukushi: I’m fine with it if Mashiro-chan and Rui-san are...
Rui: Same here. If Kurata-san’s okay with it, I am too.
Toko: Great! How about you, Shiro? You’re in, right?
Tsukushi: Toko-chan, don’t come on so strong—Mashiro-chan needs some space to think!
Mashiro: Ah, um... actually...
Tsukushi: Hm?
Mashiro: I brought lyrics for a new song today...
Toko: Eh, really!? Shiro, you’re really fired up!
Nanami: You must’ve really been looking forward to the show~.
Mashiro: Fufu, maybe I was. Um, then... Rui-san, I’ll be counting on you.
Rui: Alright, I understand.
Toko: Make it a super cool one, Rui!
Rui: ...I’ll do my best.
Tsukushi: Fufu, the show’s gonna be so fun, right Mashiro-chan?
Mashiro: Yeah...!
