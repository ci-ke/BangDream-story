event261-06 Chapter 5: Because We Promised

Kasumi visits Arisa and opens her heart.

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Tae Hanazono, Rimi Ushigome, Saya Yamabuki, Arisa Ichigaya)

[Ichigaya Residence - Basement]

Kasumi: Arisa, are you here...?
Arisa: Kasumi...? I thought everyone was coming together.
Kasumi: The others said they'll come by after the festival meeting.
Kasumi & Arisa: ...
Kasumi: I'm sorry about the other day.
Arisa: Huh...?
Kasumi: I made you feel let down, didn’t I?
Kasumi: But it’s not like I wasn’t taking the festival seriously, okay? You looked down, so... I just wanted to cheer you up.
Arisa: ...!
Kasumi: I just... didn't know you were feeling that way, Arisa.
Kasumi: I'm sorry I didn’t notice...
Arisa: You don’t need to apologize... It’s not like this is something you need to say sorry for...
Arisa: I just thought we’d always be together—the five of us. In college, during lunch breaks, over summer and winter breaks...
Arisa: I just thought... it’d be nice if we could stay the same...
Kasumi: Arisa...
Arisa: I know it's childish. I know we can't stay together forever...
Arisa: But it's not something you can just accept that easily. I really thought... maybe we'd still have a little more time.
Arisa: So why is everyone else okay with this...? Why is no one upset that Poppin'Party won’t be the same anymore...?
Kasumi: ...Is it because Rimi-rin’s going away?
Arisa: Exactly! We won't be able to play together like we always have!
Kasumi: ...
Kasumi: At Rocking Star Festival...
Arisa: Huh...?
Kasumi: Back then... even people far away were still connected, right? Even though Glitter*Green was apart, they still played together just like always.
Kasumi: If we could be like that... No—I'm sure we can be like that!
Kasumi: If we can, then even if we're far apart... we'll be okay!
Arisa: That would be nice... I want to believe that. I really do...
Arisa: Before I met you—and everyone—I was totally fine on my own.
Arisa: I get nervous around people, and it's easier being alone.
Arisa: But... Poppin’Party... you’re the first real friends I ever made.
Arisa: Being with everyone every day... it was seriously the most fun I’ve ever had.
Arisa: So the thought of that going away... It scares the hell out of me.
Kasumi: Arisa...
Rimi: I'm sorry, Arisa-chan...
Arisa: R-Rimi!?
Arisa: And you guys too...?! W-when did you—?!
Tae: Sorry, we weren’t trying to eavesdrop...
Saya: You two were talking, and we didn’t want to interrupt.
Arisa: ......
Rimi: Arisa-chan... I'm sorry I made you feel that way...
Rimi: But please believe me when I say this—I really care about all of you. I love being with Poppin'Party just as much as you do.
Rimi: But... I just couldn’t help feeling sparkling, heart-pounding.
Arisa: Sparkling, heart-pounding?
Rimi: Studying abroad. It makes me sparkling, heart-pounding...
Rimi: Do you remember the first time we stood on the SPACE stage?
Arisa: The first time... You mean when Kasumi sang "Twinkle, Twinkle Little Star"?
Rimi: Yeah. Back then, seeing you and Kasumi-chan gave me the courage to step onto the stage.
Rimi: After that, we got through so much together... and I realized something.
Rimi: What makes me sparkling, heart-pounding... is having the courage to believe in myself and take that first step.
Rimi: That's what studying abroad is for me too. I just... really hope I can do it.
Arisa: Rimi... I get how you feel. I want to support you too...
Kasumi: When I first heard Rimi-rin talk about studying abroad... I got so excited.
Kasumi: I didn’t know why at the time, but now I think I understand.
Saya: What do you mean?
Kasumi: We've done so many things together, haven't we?
Kasumi: The SPACE audition, our self-hosted live show, the Girls Band Challenge, and the Rocking Star Festival!
Kasumi: Every one of them was something completely new, but once we pulled it off, it was just—so incredibly sparkling, heart-pounding!
Kasumi: That sparkling, heart-pounding came from stepping into new experiences and seeing our world expand!
Kasumi: So I thought, if Rimi-rin studies abroad and broadens her world... maybe Poppin'Party's world will expand too!
Kasumi: Maybe we'll find a whole new kind of sparkling, heart-pounding we haven’t even seen yet! That thought just filled me with excitement!
Arisa: ...
Kasumi: Saya told me this the other day.
Kasumi: “If it's the five of us, I know we can find it. We’ll find the best answer for us.”
Kasumi: And I really believe that. All the sparkling, heart-pounding we've found until now... we only found it because it was the five of us!
Saya: Kasumi...
Rimi: Yeah, I think so too. Because I was with everyone, I realized that taking that first step is something important to me.
Tae: Same here. Being with everyone made me fall even more in love with music.
Arisa: ...Me too. It’s because the five of us were together that I found something I care about this much.
Kasumi: Being together now is already sparkling, heart-pounding, but it's more than that...
Kasumi: If we can be five people who go out searching for sparkling, heart-pounding, that would be even more amazing!
Kasumi: And if we can feel sparkling, heart-pounding from something one of us discovers, that’d be seriously awesome!
Kasumi: That’s the kind of five I want us to be!
Kasumi: No—scratch that. I will make us into that kind of five! I promise!
Tae: How are you gonna make that promise?
Kasumi: I’ll figure that part out later!
Saya: ...
Arisa: That complete lack of logic is so you, Kasumi...
Tae: But if we’re going to start thinking about things...
Rimi: Then we all do it together, right?
Kasumi: If everyone’s in, then maybe I’ll make another promise—we’ll hold a festival every year!
Arisa: The festival too!?
Arisa: We haven’t even pulled off the first one yet—don’t go making promises!
Kasumi: But we'll do it!
Tae: If Kasumi says it, it feels like we actually could do it every year.
Rimi: Then I guess I’ll have to fly back to Japan before each one and work hard at practice.
Arisa: Isn’t this all getting kinda out of hand...?
Tae & Rimi: Ahaha!
Saya: ...
(White cut)
(Background change)
