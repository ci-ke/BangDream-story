event261-04 Chapter 3: Conflicting Feelings

Working at RiNG Café, Kasumi and Saya can’t stop thinking about Arisa…

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Saya Yamabuki)

[RiNG Café]

Saya: Kasumi, can I ask you to restock the supplies?
Kasumi: Ah, sure. Leave it to me. Let’s see...
Saya: Ririko-san and the others seem really busy today.
Saya: Looks like they’re tied up with live preparation.
Kasumi: Both of them caught a cold.
Saya: Yeah... Once it gets busy, we’ll have to handle things between the two of us.
Kasumi: No problem. I’ve totally gotten the hang of running the café by now.
Saya: Ah yeah. You’re right...
Saya: But I’m pretty sure that’s not the shelf we’re supposed to restock...
Kasumi: Eh? Ah, r-right...! That’s right~!
Kasumi: Sorry, I was spacing out a little~...
Saya: ...
Kasumi: Um, it was this shelf, right? This is the one we have to restock~...
Saya: ...Are you okay?
Kasumi: Huh? What do you mean “okay”?
Saya: You’ve been thinking about Arisa this whole time, haven’t you?
Saya: You haven’t said a word to her all day.
Kasumi: ...I just don’t know what to say to her anymore.
Kasumi: We’ve always been together but I still didn’t notice how much Arisa was hurting.
Kasumi: No... I made her hurt more.
Kasumi: She was scared that Poppin’Party might change...
Saya: ...
Saya: Rimi-rin... if she really goes far away, Poppin’Party wouldn’t stay the same as we've always been.
Saya: It’s natural to feel uneasy. We’ve always been together as five...
Kasumi: Saya...?
Saya: When Rimi-rin told you about studying abroad... what did you think, Kasumi?
Kasumi: I...
Kasumi: I was excited!
Saya: Excited...? How come?
Kasumi: Hmm... I’m not really sure myself, but...
Kasumi: I didn’t feel scared or anxious about what might happen to Poppin’Party. Before anything else, I just felt excited.
Kasumi: Rimi-rin is amazing... So I just thought I want to cheer her on.
Saya: ...That’s such a Kasumi thing to say.
Kasumi: But maybe that’s why I didn’t notice how Arisa was feeling.
Saya: Kasumi...
Kasumi: The way I felt when I heard about Rimi-rin’s dream, and the way Arisa felt- those were different...!
Kasumi: But if Arisa’s struggling, I want to listen, think it through, and find an answer with her.
Kasumi: Still.. what should I even say to Arisa...? If I hurti her all over again...
Saya: ...
Saya: Let’s figure it out... together, okay? When we’re struggling, we don’t do it alone, right?
Kasumi: Saya...
Saya: Come on, we’re Poppin’Party.
Saya: We’ve gotten through so many hard times before.
Saya: During the audition for SPACE and the Girls Band Challenge.
Saya: That’s why we’ll be okay moving forward too. Let’s figure it out together—just the five of us.
Saya: Because when it’s all five of us, we can always find the answer that’s best for us.
Kasumi: ...Yeah. You’re right.
Kasumi: Thanks, Saya! I can't just stay feeling down!
Saya: That’s more like you, Kasumi.
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Saya: ... Though my heart aches too.
Saya: I told you we’d find the best answer together as five, because I wanted to cheer you up, Kasumi.
Saya: But the truth is, I didn’t believe my own words enough to say everything’s going to be okay...
(Black cut)
