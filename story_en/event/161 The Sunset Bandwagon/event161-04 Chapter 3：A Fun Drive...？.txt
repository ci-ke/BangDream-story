event161-04 Chapter 3: A Fun Drive...?

Himari and the others agree to be the navigators, but the van's navigation system is apparently pretty old...

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa)

[Marina's Van - Inside]

Marina: Ran-chan was half-asleep, huh~? You're right, she doesn't seem like the type.
Himari: I'm surprised too! You shouldn't push yourself so hard, okay~?
Ran: There you go again... Could you just let it go...?
Marina: Seems like we're off to a bit of a rocky start~.
Marina: Himari-chan forgot her wallet, Ran-chan's sleep-deprived, and Tomoe-chan's... What was it again?
Moca: Tomo-chin's bugging out from excitement~.
Tomoe: Whoa, whoa. I've gotten myself under control now, okay...?
Marina: Oh yeah, you did seem a bit over-excited. Fufu. At this rate, I have a feeling there'll be a few more bumps to deal with...
Himari: Marina-san~! Please don't say scary things like that~!
Marina: Ahaha, sorry...! Hmm?
Tsugumi: What is it?
Marina: Oh, it looks like there's some traffic ahead.
Marina: We could exit the highway, but I'm a bit worried. This car's navigation system is pretty out-of-date.
Tsugumi: But if we don't, we'll be stuck in traffic. What do you think we should do...? We can't afford to be late...
Marina: ... Well, worrying about it isn't going to do us any good. Let's get off the highway.
Marina: Could you girls keep an eye on the navigator, though? I don't entirely trust it.
Tsugumi: Okay, leave it to us!
Moca: Bump number one, here we come~.
Himari: Don't jinx it, Moca~!
Marina: Fufu. You girls ready to do this?
Himari: Yeah!
(Black cut)

[After Some Time]

Tsugumi: Since it's fifty meters ahead, um, that probably means making a right before the next convenience store...
Tsugumi: Then a left at the third traffic light-
Tsugumi: (Wait... There are only two lights?!)
Marina: ...? What's wrong, Tsugumi-chan?
Tsugumi: Ah, i-it's nothing!
Tsugumi: (The navigation system really is old, but I'll tell Marina-san to turn at the second light. We should be okay as long as we keep heading in the same general direction...)
Tsugumi: (The others aren't their usual selves, so I have to be the one to keep it together...!)
Tsugumi: ... Please make a right before the next convenience store, then a left at the second traffic light!
Marina: Got it. Thanks!
Tsugumi: Y-you're welcome...!
Marina: ... Weird.
Himari: Why's there a dead-end...? According to the navigator, there's supposed to be a road...
Tomoe: I know you said it's old, but I didn't think there'd be an entire road missing...
Tsugumi: I-I'm so sorry! I thought this would be the correct route...!
Himari: It's not your fault, Tsugu. There's not much you can do when there's no road at all.
Tomoe: Himari's right. And I shouldn't have dumped all the navigating on you.
Ran: Look at me, I wasn't even awake...
Tsugumi: But I thought something didn't feel right...
Tsugumi: I should've brought it up earlier...
Tomoe: Seriously, it's not your fault! Right, Moca? Don't you agree?
Moca: Mmm~...
Tomoe: Moca?
Ran: What's up? You've been awfully quiet.
Moca: Oh~, it's nothing~.
Tomoe: You sure? Well, if you say so...
Moca: ...
Tomoe: ... Wait, no. You don't sound alright at all.
Moca: Mmm~...
Tomoe: What's going on, Moca?
Tsugumi: I don't think you should shake her that hard, Tomoe-chan...!
Moca: Yeah, show a little mercy, will you~? I'm in the middle of a fierce battle.
Ran: ...? With what?
Moca: You see~... I'm feeling... really, really~...
Moca: Sick...
Tsugumi: Sick...? Wait, are you carsick?!
Tomoe: Uh, water! And, um... Ah! Drug store! We need meds!
Marina: G-got it, drug store. To get to one, I need to make a left here, and then...
Ran: ... Does motion sickness medication work once you're sick...?
Tsugumi: Probably... not...
Marina: What?! I already turned!
Himari: C-c-c-calm down, guys! For now, we need to let Moca rest...!
Moca: Urgh... I never thought I would become bump number one...
Moca: It's all up to... you guys... now... Slump.
Tomoe: Moca! Hang in there! Moca...? Moca~!
(White cut)
