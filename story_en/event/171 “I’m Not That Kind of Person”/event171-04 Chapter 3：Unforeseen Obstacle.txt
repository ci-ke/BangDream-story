event171-04 Chapter 3: Unforeseen Obstacle

Tomoe finally gets a chance to speak with Rinko on the day of the event, but...

(Character: Saya Yamabuki, Arisa Ichigaya, Tomoe Udagawa, Ako Udagawa, Rinko Shirokane)

[The Day of the Hanasakigawa Community Festival]

[Hanasakigawa Girls' Academy - Front Gate]

Arisa: Phew... Seems like the flow of people has finally settled down. Next, I'll go check on the bazaar in the gymnasium-
(White cut)
Arisa: ... What the- Saya?! Don't take my picture so suddenly! My face definitely looked terrible just now.
Saya: Fufu, no it didn't. You looked like you were working as hard as you can.
Arisa: I-I did...? More importantly, forget about me and take pictures of the people that came to the event. The newspaper club was looking forward to your pictures.
Saya: Okay. I'll do what I can to meet their expectations.
Ako: Saya-chan~, Arisa~! We came to visit~!
Tomoe: Hey, you two! This place is bustling~!
Saya: Yeah, I didn't think this many people would come. I wonder if it's because this year there's an orchestra performance at the end.
Arisa: Ah~, you mean Hanasakigawa Orchestra. It's a small civic orchestra that's not very well known, so apparently, this is doubling as publicity for them.
Ako: I see! By the way, where's Rin-rin? I thought she'd be with all of you...
Arisa: Ah, Rinko-senpai's helping some other students with their work over that way. I think she'll be done soon.
Saya: I guess the day of the event must also be busy for her.
Arisa: Yeah. That's because she listens so compassionately when you go to her for advice. Everyone heads to her whenever they have a problem.
Arisa: She'll do other work on top of her own though, so that worries me...
Tomoe: I see. Rinko-san seems busy today...
Saya: You don't have to worry that much. I'm sure she'd have time to talk for a little bit.
Ako: That's good~! We wanted to talk to her since we came all this way! ... Ah, Rin-rin! Welcome back~!
Rinko: Ako-chan...! A-and Tomoe-san... Uh, w-welcome...
Tomoe: Th-thanks. Um~... Uh, hello...
Rinko: Y-yes... Hello...
Rinko & Tomoe: ...
Ako: ...? Rin-rin, Sis, what's wrong? The two of you seem kinda different from normal.
Tomoe: Nah, nothing's wrong. We're the same as always!
Ako: Really? Okay then...
Saya: Come on, Tomoe. Isn't now a good chance to talk about that thing?
Tomoe: Y-yeah, you're right... But how do I start...?
Saya: You just need to speak honestly. Just give it to her straight like you always do.
Tomoe: Okay... Give it to her honest and straight...!
Tomoe: Rinko-san! U-um... About what we talked about when we met downtown.
Tomoe: Y'know, when you said I was like a hero. That didn't really feel right...
Ako: Really? I think it's perfect. Would something cooler have been better?
Rinko: A cooler metaphor than a hero... Hm~... I wonder what would work...
Tomoe: Ah~, that's not what I'm talking about! I was just thinking that calling me a hero is definitely an exaggeration~...!
Rinko: Is it...? From what I've heard of you, that was my impression...
Rinko: You may not think that... you are doing anything special... but I think there are many people that are grateful to you...
Tomoe: Hrm~... Th-this is hard... How do I explain this...?
Saya: Um, I think that what Tomoe wants to say is that the word "hero" feels heavy, or rather, she's not as great as that.
Tomoe: S-Saya~...!
Saya: Unlike a hero that wouldn't give in to anything, Tomoe has times when she feels down just like all of us...
Ako: She does...? I've never really seen her get down...
Ako: But even if she did, I don't think she would give in so easily. Because she's strong enough to always handle things with a boom!
Tomoe: Ah~... Th-that's right! I can always handle stuff with a boom! Ahaha!
Rinko: With a boom... You really are strong, Tomoe-san... You are so cool...
Tomoe: Ahaha! Haha... I'm not that great~...!
Saya: Hey, Tomoe~, what gives...?! You won't be able to clear up the misunderstanding like this...!
Tomoe: S-sorry...! Whenever I'm with Ako, I can't help but say things like that...
Saya: I see... So for you to say things properly, Ako can't be around...
Saya: Then let's do this. Whisper whisper whisper...
Tomoe: Ohh, that's a great idea. I'll give it a try...!
Tomoe: Ako, I'm a little hungry. There are chocolate-covered bananas at that booth over there, so could you go buy me one?
Ako: Whoa~, you're right! They look so good! I'll go buy some~!
Tomoe: Breathe in, and out... Okay. Rinko-san, I wasn't able to say this before, but I'll speak honestly.
Tomoe: You said that I'm like a hero, but the real me is nothing like tha-
Ako: Sis~! They have a bunch of colors! Which one do you want~?
Tomoe: Huh?! Um... I'm fine with anything you choose for me!
Ako: Okay! I'll get you the coolest color~!
Tomoe: Phew... Sorry for the interruption. So, what I'm trying to say is-
Ako: Sis~! There's character cookies that go on top of the bananas! Which one do you want?! Did you want Invinci-Red?
Tomoe: Uhh... Yeah! That's fine!
Rinko: Invinci-Red... Wasn't that the leader of a superhero team long ago...?
Tomoe: Rinko-san, you know about them?
Rinko: I've heard about them from Ako-chan...
Rinko: They were relied on by their friends, as well as the townspeople, and they had the strength to stand up against an evil organization...
Rinko: Invinci-Red is... somewhat like you, Tomoe-san...
Tomoe: Huh?! I-I'm telling you I'm not like that!
Saya: Hrm~, Ako's quite a tough opponent...
