event171-02 Chapter 1: The Greatest Hero

Rinko is feeling discouraged after a meeting when she runs into Tomoe...

(Character: Arisa Ichigaya, Tomoe Udagawa, Ako Udagawa, Rinko Shirokane)

[Hanasakigawa Girls' Academy - Student Council Room]

Rinko: ... Um... As everyone is here now... I would like... to begin this meeting right away...
Rinko: T-today... we have... members from the neighborhood association joining us... They will be assisting us with the "Hanasakigawa Community Festival"...
Arisa: Th-thank you for joining us!
Association Member: No, thank you for having us.
Rinko: N-now then... The items on the agenda are- Ah... First, I will explain... the goal of this event...
Rinko: This event... aims to... form connections between Hanasakigawa Girls' Academy... and members of the local community...
Rinko: Um... Uh...
Arisa: Rinko-senpai, you can take it slowly...!
Rinko: O-okay...!
Rinko: Th-this event... aims to form connections with members of the local community...
Arisa: Um, you already explained that, so... please explain the next part about the bazaar and the booths...
Rinko: Ah... Um, we would like to ask... the members of the neighborhood association... to take care of the bazaar and refreshment booths...
Rinko: We would like to ask... student council members to work with student volunteers... in handling entertainment on the event stage and... t-trial lessons...
Arisa: Ah, um... That's not something we need to ask now as we've already decided that we're going to handle that...!
Rinko: Ahh... Um~...!
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Walking Home]

Rinko: Sigh...
Rinko: (I wasn't able to speak well at all...)
Rinko: (I still get really nervous when speaking with people for the first time... Particularly when they're adults...)
Rinko: (I even caused trouble for Ichigaya-san...)
Rinko: I'm such... an unreliable student council president...
Ako: Huh? It's Rin-rin. Hey~, Rin-rin~!
Rinko: A-Ako-chan...! And Tomoe-san too...
Tomoe: Hello! Are you on your way home?
Rinko: Yes... I just had a student council meeting...
Rinko: Tomoe-san, why are you wearing an apron from the tofu shop...?
Tomoe: Ohh, this thing? The tofu shop owner hurt his back, so I'm helping out just for today.
Ako: He said he could trust her to look after the shop. Isn't that awesome?! That's my sister for you!
Tomoe: She says that, but I just stand here and talk with the customers.
Rinko: You're incredible, Tomoe-san... Adults trust you... and you can even handle looking after a shop...
Rinko: I need to be the kind of student council president that can be relied on like that...
Ako: ...? Rin-rin, did something happen?
Rinko: Ah... The thing is, our school will be holding an event soon...
(Black cut)
Tomoe: Huh, so that's the kind of events you do at Hanasakigawa.
Ako: Having a bazaar, booths, and a bunch of other stuff sounds so fun!
Rinko: Yeah... except some things are not going so well... so I'm concerned...
Rinko: But I'm the student council president... so it's not right for me to feel so discouraged...
Rinko: I have to be stronger... so we can make it an event that everyone can enjoy...
Tomoe: Whoa... You're thinking so deeply about it, Rinko-san.
Rinko: Huh? Wh-what do you mean...?
Tomoe: Oh, I was just thinking that the strength behind your thoughts on the event are on a different level.
Tomoe: You trying to be stronger means that you're trying to increase the things you can do to make the event even better, right?
Tomoe: You being able to do that much for a single event... is just so intense. That's really cool.
Rinko: Huh...? Um, that wasn't really my intent-
Tomoe: Even now you seem like you're quietly fired up... And that passion makes you even stronger.
Tomoe: I've always thought that you're like the protagonist in a battle manga. I wanna be someone with passion like that too!
Rinko: What...?! A b-battle manga...?!
Ako: You're really cool when you're trying your best!
Tomoe: Yeah. Not being satisfied with how things are and always aiming higher... That's seriously cool!
Rinko: U-um... I'm not like that...! I'm not trying to be like that. Uh... I just want to make sure I don't cause trouble for everyone around me...
Rinko: Besides... you're way cooler than I am, Tomoe-san...!
Tomoe: Huh...?
Rinko: You're depended on by the people around you... and you have such a strong state of mind...
Rinko: I always think... that you really are like a hero...
Tomoe: A h-hero?! No way, I'm not-
Rinko: Even you helping out at a shop like this... It proves that everyone in the downtown area trusts you...
Rinko: I wish... I could be... as dependable as you are...
Tomoe: W-wait a second! They just happened to ask for my help, so it's nothing special!
Tomoe: It's nothing that praiseworthy... More importantly, you're hands down way more incredible than I am!
Rinko: What~...?! I-I'm not like that at all... I think you're the incredible one...!
(Black cut)
(Background change)
