event166-02 Chapter 1: Our Promise

LAYER and Tae talk about their past. When they finish, Tae has a proposal...

(Character: Tae Hanazono, Arisa Ichigaya, Rei Wakana)

Rei: ... Sniff...
Tae: Hi.
Rei: Are you from the music school?
Tae: Yeah. Wanna sing?
Rei: I can't... Everyone says I don't sing like other kids...
Tae: Oh? La~, la~♪ Onward~, Hanazono Land~♪ Hmm, that didn't sound right. I wonder why~.
Rei: Fufu...
Tae: Here. Have some candy.
Rei: Thanks. Did you come up with that song?
Tae: Yup. It's the Hanazono Land song. It just flows into me so freely when I play the guitar.
Rei: Freely...
Tae: Yeah. Doing things freely is fun. Wanna try together?
Rei: Sure...!
(Black cut)
Tae: You can hear it too, right?
Rei: I don't know... but... I haven't enjoyed singing this much in a while.
Tae: I can see that. Your tears are gone. I'm Tae Hanazono.
Rei: Rei... Wakana.
Tae: Rei, let's sing again!
Rei: Okay...!
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Tae: I still sing that song, you know?
LAYER (Rei): Really? It was a pretty weird one...
LAYER (Rei): And after that, we'd sing together at the park.
LAYER (Rei): But then the day came when I had to move because of my parents' work...
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Tae: Are you going already?
Rei: Mm-hmm, I can't keep my family waiting. I'm sorry, Hana-chan...
Tae: Don't cry. We'll meet again. I want us to be smiling when we say goodbye to each other.
Tae: And when we do meet again someday, let's sing this song together...
Rei: Okay... I promise, so promise me, okay?
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Arisa: I had no idea you guys made a promise like that...
LAYER (Rei): After that, I picked up the bass so that I could be in a band someday with Hana-chan.
Arisa: And you two not only met, you even both got up on stage at the Budokan... It's pretty incredible stuff if you think about it.
LAYER (Rei): I still remember hating the fact that I had to move~, fufu.
Tae: I bet we would've made a band if you hadn't.
Arisa: You guys in a band, huh...? For some reason, I have this image of you two performing on the streets.
LAYER (Rei): Fufu, I'm sure that kind of a future might've been possible, but it's just a what-if.
Tae: I want to make it happen... Let's do it, Rei.
LAYER & Arisa: What...?
LAYER (Rei): You mean... a band?
Tae: Yeah! And then let's do a concert!
Arisa: You serious... O-Tae?
Tae: Dead serious! I want us to keep the promise we made!
Tae: What do you think, Rei?
LAYER (Rei): I...
LAYER (Rei): I want to do it...
Tae: You do? Hooray~!
LAYER (Rei): But we should get permission from our bands. I don't want to make them worry.
LAYER (Rei): And if we do it... It should only be for a day. Or else...
LAYER (Rei): (The feelings I put behind me will come back again...)
Tae: Hmm?
LAYER (Rei): Oh, it's nothing...
Arisa: ...
Tae: Okay, I'm going to bring it up with everyone. I don't want to make them worry again.
LAYER (Rei): Alright, I'll do the same with RAS.
