[After Practice]

Lisa: Phew~, I'm so tired~. I can't believe it's this dark already. Maybe I shouldn't have asked for that extension just because I was doing great~... Wait, LAYER?
LAYER (Rei): Ah, Lisa-san. Hello.
Lisa: Hi♪ Is practice going well on your end?
LAYER (Rei): It is. Hana-chan's guitar is so easy to play with...

LAYER (Rei): Oh, great... Is it that obvious again?

LAYER (Rei): The joy of getting to play with Hana-chan made me feel like everything is how it used to be.
LAYER (Rei): But Hana-chan hasn't been satisfied with that. She's been searching for the music that can only come from who we are now...

LAYER (Rei): Yes, exactly. It really is like that.
LAYER (Rei): I've always thought of the future that could've been, and I realize now that it doesn't exist...
Lisa: You did say before that you picked up the bass just so that you two could be in a band together.
Lisa: It shows how much this means to you.
LAYER (Rei): I thought I'd known all along, but... Sigh, I didn't expect the truth to hit this hard, you know~...? Fufufu.
Lisa: LAYER...
LAYER (Rei): But that's also why I now know what I need to do. Even if what we have isn't going to last forever, I want to cherish our time together.
LAYER (Rei): I'm not going to waste a single second. It's what I need to do to come to terms with my feelings and to move on from the past...
LAYER (Rei): That's how I feel right now.
LAYER (Rei): I don't want to be less than the me from the future that could've been, so I'm going to make sure the music of who I am now resonates loudly and clearly.
