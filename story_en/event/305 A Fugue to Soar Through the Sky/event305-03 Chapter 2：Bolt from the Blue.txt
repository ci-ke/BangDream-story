event305-03 Chapter 2: Bolt from the Blue

Toko's practice is running into trouble, and she seems to have something to say to Rui...?

(Character: Mashiro Kurata, Toko Kirigaya, Nanami Hiromachi, Tsukushi Futaba, Rui Yashio)

[The Next Day]

[Hiromachi Residence - Atelier]

Toko: ...Rui! Um... so...
Rui: What is it?
Toko: Well, um... so...
Tsukushi: What's wrong, Toko-chan? You seem kinda down today.
Toko: Huh? No, no, I'm fine! It's just... I need to ask Rui something...
Mashiro: Ask Rui-san...?
Nanami: Do you have something to discuss or propose?
Toko: A discussion... Well, I mean... how should I put it... Ugh~~~...
Rui: Sigh... If you have something to say, can you please just say it clearly?
Toko: Ugh... Ah~... Alright!
Toko: ...Rui! Train me until I can play that phrase!
Rui: Huh...?
Mashiro: Wh-whaaaat?!
Nanami: That Toko-chan... asking Rui-rui for a favor...!
Tsukushi: And asking Rui-san to train her... It's obvious it's gonna be grueling practice...!
Toko: Well, I was torn about it too. It seems hard to ask Sayo-san and the others, and it'd be too sudden to ask other upperclassmen...
Toko: ...So when I thought about it, I figured I should ask Rui, who actually understands my guitar playing right now.
Toko: Plus, it wouldn't mean anything if Rui doesn't approve in the end, right?
Tsukushi: That's true, but...
Toko: So, I'm counting on you! Rui!
Rui: Don't just push the conversation forward on your own.
Rui: I haven't said a single word about accepting. First of all, what's the benefit for me in helping with your training?
Toko: Huh, but you wanna see me looking cool, right?
Rui: I don't particularly want to see that.
Toko: Seriously?! Why not?!
Rui: ...
Toko: But well, if you're gonna say that... Hmm...
Toko: Oh! If I can play this, don't you think Morfonica's shows will get way cooler than we are now?
Toko: We'd be able to churn out songs with phrases like this!
Rui: So you're saying that by helping with your training, it would have a positive effect on the band's overall performance... It would broaden the range of songs we can create?
Toko: Yes, that! Exactly!
Rui: ...That's a decent argument coming from you.
Rui: (It's desirable for members' performance skills to improve. Plus, there's the matter of the song I entrusted to Kurata-san...)
Mashiro: Um... Rui-san. Is it really impossible?
Rui: Kurata-san?
Mashiro: Just like how everyone's helping with my goal of doing a joint show with Poppin’Party... I want to support what Toko-chan wants to do too.
Mashiro: If there's anything I can do, I'll help with anything...
Nanami: Rui-rui, I'm asking you too~.
Tsukushi: Me too! Please, Rui-san!
Toko: Shiro, Nanami, Fusuke...! Wow, you guys are seriously so nice!
Tsukushi: Jeez! Toko-chan, you need to ask properly too! You're the one being taught, so you need to act accordingly!
Toko: Okay, okay! Um...
Toko: So... Rui! No, Yashio-sensei! Please, I'm counting on you!
Rui: ...I was planning to accept even without you asking me like that.
Toko: Really? Come on, Rui~!
Rui: However, while I'm teaching you, don't argue emotionally against my opinions. Complete all the assignments I tell you to do properly. And...
Rui: One hour per day. Any more than that isn't worth the cost for this possibility.
Toko: Sure, sure! Leave it to me!
Mashiro: That's great, Toko-chan! Do your best with the training...!
Toko: Thanks! I'll be able to play it in no time! Right, Rui!
Rui: ...Sigh.
