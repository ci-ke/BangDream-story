event206-01 Opening: The Unknown Path Ahead

An invitation from Kaoru causes Chisato to recall the past.

(Character: Kaoru Seta, Chisato Shirasagi)

[Elementary School]

Classmate A: Guys, did you watch it? The final episode of "The City of Solitude"!
Classmate B: Yeah! It was amazing!
Classmate C: I saw it too! The part at the end where everyone leaves the city was so good...!
Chisato: ... Good morning.
Classmate A: ...! G-good morning, Chisato-chan! We saw City of Solitude!
Classmate C: Yeah, I'm so glad it was a happy ending! Everyone was safe...!
Chisato: Yes, my character included.
Classmate B: R-right~...
Chisato: Oh, good morning, Kao-chan... What is it? Why are you making that face?
Kaoru: ... Chii-chan~!
Chisato: Whoa. What's wrong? Did something happen?
Kaoru: Seeing your face made me think of yesterday's City of Solitude... and it made me feel really sad...
Chisato: Sad? But why? The show had a happy ending, didn't it?
Kaoru: But...
Chisato: Fufu, have you forgotten how it went? Oh, I know. Why don't we try re-enacting the final scene? That should help you remember.
Kaoru: What?! I can't do something like that~.
Chisato: Don't worry. I'll take the lead, and it's alright to make mistakes. You can just do the parts that you remember, okay?
Kaoru: O-okay...
Chisato: ... "Hurry up and leave the city. There's nothing here anymore."
Kaoru: U-um... "W-wait! What about you...? Aren't you coming?!"
Chisato: "How ridiculous. Why would I want to go with all of you? Now get out of my sight."
Kaoru: ...
Chisato: ... What's wrong? It's your line next.
Chisato: This is the part where the main characters turn around and run off without ever looking back. Don't you remember?
Kaoru: ... B-but...
Kaoru: Are you really okay with that...?!
Chisato: ...!
Chisato: ... Oh, Kao-chan...
Chisato: "In that case... will you stay with me?"
Kaoru: ...! "Yeah, of course!"
Chisato: ... Fufu. If that had been for the show, our ad-libbing would have been considered a blooper.
Kaoru: Really?! Bloopers are bad, though, right...?!
Chisato: Yes... So it would've likely been rejected during actual filming.
Chisato: ... Fufu... I don't mind this alternate version though.
Chisato: That's why this'll be our little secret. We'll be the only ones who know this version of the story. Okay, Kao-chan?
Kaoru: ... Okay!
(White cut)
(Background change)
Chisato: (... Well, I certainly seem to have gone quite a ways down memory lane.)
Chisato: (I suppose this is what it's like to become susceptible to distractions when there's something especially important that needs to be done.)
Chisato: (I've heard of people who end up cleaning their room or reading manga...)
Chisato: (... Looks like I'm no different. I ended up going through an old photo album.)
Chisato: (... It's all because of this. This ticket...)
(White cut)
(Background change)
Kaoru: "Before I graduate, I wish to offer the little kittens a most fabulous Christmas present."
Kaoru: "It shall take the form of a Christmas play organized by none other than myself. I would very much like for you to attend. Will you, Chisato?"
(White cut)
(Background change)
Chisato: (... You're supposed to have entrance exams just like the rest of us... Not to mention, you already got your farewell performance in fall.)
Chisato: (How very typical of you.)
Chisato: And you've even remained that way right up to the end...
Chisato: (... What am I saying. The Kaoru from back then is gone. I thought I accepted that already.)
Chisato: (People change and move on. Aya-chan, everyone else in the band, and even myself.)
Chisato: (Of course, Kaoru is no exception... Although, in her case, I feel the change has been somewhat extreme.)
Chisato: (Which isn't to say I dislike the way she is now. The person she's become is what made our current relationship possible.)
Chisato: (That's why I shouldn't be thinking about the way things used to be. The old Kaoru is gone.)
Chisato: (If I keep dwelling on it, I'll be left behind. By everyone in the band, Kaoru, and even the rest of the world.)
Chisato: (That's why...)
Chisato: (It's about time I also graduate and move on.)
(White cut)

(Memory cut-in)

(Background change)
(White cut)

(Memory cut-out)

(Background change)
Chisato: ... Isn't that right, Kao-chan?
Chisato: ...
Chisato: (I've taken a long enough break. I need to get back to studying.)
Chisato: (Applying for comprehensive selection admission doesn't mean I don't have to study for general selection admission...)
Chisato: (I could fail Yotsuba Women's University's comprehensive admission or decide not to submit this application even...)
Chisato: ...
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Chisato: (Yotsuba's Faculty of Humanities... The higher-rated Keiho Women's University's Faculty of Letters... Even Yotsuba's Faculty of Arts...)
Chisato: (... Multiple paths lie before me.)
Chisato: (And there's no way to tell where each one leads...)
Chisato: (... That's why I...)
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Chisato: ...
Chisato: (... Ngh...)
Chisato: ... Now really isn't the best time to be remembering the past.
