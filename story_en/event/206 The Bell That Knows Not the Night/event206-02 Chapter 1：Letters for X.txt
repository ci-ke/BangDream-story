Chisato is relaxing at Hazawa Coffee when Himari and Rimi make a suggestion.

(Character: Rimi Ushigome, Himari Uehara, Tsugumi Hazawa, Kaoru Seta, Chisato Shirasagi, Eve Wakamiya)

[Hazawa Coffee]

Tsugumi: Welcome to Haza- Ah, Chisato-san!
Chisato: Hello. I decided to come over to relax since all I've done lately is study.
Tsugumi: Fufu, you've earned a break for sure then. Please have a seat over here.
Eve: Chisato-san, enjoy your break from your studies!
Chisato: And good luck to you with work, Eve-chan. May I have an Earl Grey and the cake of the day?

(Black cut)

Chisato: Yes, I've heard. It's very like her to plan a play even with entrance exams coming up.

Chisato: I'm sorry?

Chisato: Y-yes... but I...
Himari: You should write one, too, then! I'm sure Kaoru-senpai will be so happy to get a letter from you!

Chisato: H-heartfelt...? I haven't even decided yet if I'm going to write one.
