event306-03 Chapter 2: A Greedy Set List

Preparations for the show are right on track — and it's not just the music they're getting ready for...

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa)

[The Next Day]

Tsugumi: -So we'll put this song last... Okay, how's this look?
Ran: Yeah, not bad.
Himari: Then the set list is decided with these three songs~!
Tomoe: Alright, now we just gotta practice! How many times can we meet up before the show again?
Moca: I think it was about three more times~?
Tsugumi: I see. We've got a song we're debuting at the live too, so it's cutting it pretty close...!
Ran: Well, we'll manage somehow. Unless a certain someone keeps throwing in new phrases.
Moca: Wonder who that could be, Tomo-chin~.
Tomoe: Yeah, who could it be~?
Tsugumi: Fufufu.
Himari: Wait, aren't you guys forgetting something important?
Ran: Huh... Did we miss something?
Himari: The band's social media account! We're doing a live, so there's tons of stuff to post, right?
Himari: We gotta announce we're doing the live, upload practice footage, post a 'we're nervous but excited~!' right before the show, and then a thank you after the live and pics from the afterparty-...
Ran: Himari, stop. ...Isn't that a lot?
Moca: It's fine, isn't it~? Our social media ambassador's all fired up anyway~.
Tsugumi: Come to think of it, we haven't posted anything for the band recently.
Tomoe: Really? Well, doing things casually like that is more our style anyway.
Himari: I wanna post more~! I've been holding back, you know!
Tomoe: Ahaha, then we can't not do it.
Tsugumi: You're right! Um, what should we start with?
Himari: First up, announcing the live!
Ran: Fine, but we don't have any photos or videos to post, do we?
Himari: That's why we're taking them now! The rooftop's got that Afterglow vibe too, don't you think?
Tomoe: What, now? That's sudden~.
Himari: Come on, hurry up or recess'll be over!
Himari: Um, the light's coming from the wrong direction here... Everyone over here! Gather on this side!
Moca: Okay. Like this~?
Ran: Hey Moca, you're blocking.
Tsugumi: Tomoe-chan, maybe move in a bit closer.
Tomoe: Uh... This much?
Himari: Okay, I'm taking it~. Three, two, one...
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[That Night]

Moca: Afterglow's post is... Ah, there it is.
Moca: (Hii-chan seemed like she was having fun today.)
Moca: (After that, Tsugu said we should upload live videos too, and Tomo-chin got all bummed about how hard editing would be, and seeing that made Ran laugh.)
Moca: ...Oh, I got a feeling a good phrase is coming to me~.
Moca: Yeah, nice feel~. Should I write it down somewhere~?
Moca: ...Fwahh... I'll do it later~...
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[The Day of the Show]

Ran: Moca, isn't that a lot of bread for today?
Moca: The more bread there is, the more Moca-chan's guitar shines~.
Ran: Uh-huh...?
Himari: Ugh, I feel like I'm forgetting something...! Everyone's got their outfits, right?
Tomoe: Yeah, we've got 'em. You already checked that earlier, didn't you?
Moca: Whether the outfit I brought is the right one won't be revealed till I open my bag~.
Tsugumi: Huh? I-it's fine today... right?
Ran: Tsugumi, don't worry about it. Actually Himari, aren't you weirdly nervous?
Himari: Not nervous exactly... It just feels like the day of the show came so fast.
Tomoe: Well, we didn't have much time to practice.
Tsugumi: The rushed prep kinda felt like before the culture festival.
Himari: I know~! Like, I'm excited, but I keep double-checking if we got everything ready!
Moca: Well, even if we forgot something, we'll figure it out~.
Ran: Yeah. We just came to do a regular live like always.
Moca: Ooh, Ran, so reliable~.
Ran: Moca, you're loud.
Himari: But you guys are right! We just came to do a regular live!
Tomoe: Exactly. So leader, should we go say hi to Marina-san and the others first?
Himari: Yeah! Everyone, let's go!
(Black cut)
(Background change)
