event306-06 Chapter 5: An Extra-Special Memory

The roof. Bread, a sunset, and dear friends. Everything that matters, right here.

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba)

[The Next Morning]

Moca: ...Mmm-hmm♪
Moca: Morning~, Ran.
Ran: Morning. Aren't you a bit early today?
Moca: Yeah, just happened to be... Wait, huh? That paper bag...
Ran: You already noticed. ...Here, you were curious about the bread from this place, right?
Moca: Yeah, but what's this about~?
Ran: Yesterday, I happened to find it on the way back from the flower arrangement gathering. I remembered you were talking about this shop.
Moca: ...That's it?
Ran: Huh? Yeah...
Moca: I see... I see~.
Ran: What, now I'm curious.
Moca: Nope, nothing at all~. I'll gratefully accept this.
Ran: ...Yeah. Eat that for lunch today.
Moca: Nah~, it'd be a waste to make it just regular lunch~.
Moca: Ran, you don't have lessons today, right~? Hang out with me after school~.
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[After School]

Moca: Bread bestowed upon me by the divine Ran-sama... How blessed~.
Ran: So dramatic. Just eat it normally.
Moca: Well then, time to dig in~. *munch*... *munch munch*...
Moca: Fufu... Sho good~~!
Ran: Fufu... Looks like you could win that eating deliciously contest after all.
Moca: Sho good I can't shtop~. But if I eat it, it'll be gone~.
Ran: When it's gone, just buy more.
Ran: That shop has so many types of bread that even I had trouble deciding, so I'm sure you'd be even more indecisive.
Moca: Oho~. You had trouble deciding?
Ran: A little. I ended up getting the one with the recommendation sign.
Ran: Well, take your time eating. I'll work on some lyrics.
Moca: You take your time too, Ran~. *munch*
Ran: ...Ah, right.
Ran: Moca, are you having fun with the band lately?
Moca: Mm... Why~?
Ran: Just wondering.
Ran: When you were writing down phrases before, I could tell you were getting more ambitious about guitar, but lately it feels different.
Ran: I was wondering if maybe you've been turning the feeling of just having fun into phrases more often.
Moca: ...
Moca: As expected of ace producer Mitake~. You noticed Moca-chan's guitar evolution~.
Ran: Sigh... There you go deflecting again.
Ran: Well, whatever. I just thought I get that.
Moca: Get what~?
Ran: Turning what you feel during completely ordinary moments into music.
Ran: Like when something makes you happy or is just a bit fun, you easily forget the small things.
Ran: But when you write it down in lyrics or melodies and look back at it later, you can remember everything.
Moca: I see~... I didn't know that 'cause I lose mine.
Ran: The other day it got blown away by the wind.
Moca: Yeah.
Moca: (The bread Ran bought for me was delicious. She thought about it a bit and bought the recommended one.)
Moca: (The taste of ordinary bread on an ordinary day.)
Moca: *munch*... *munch munch*...
Moca: (I'd hate it if this got blown away.)
Moca: ...Phew~, thanks for the meal~. Thanks, Ran.
Ran: You're welcome.
Moca: Thanks to you, I got a feeling a good phrase is coming to me~.
Ran: Yeah, sounds good. It feels really warm.
Moca: Then I'll write it down.
Ran: Which handout are you using today?
Moca: Mm~... I'll settle it down in my Japanese notebook for now~.
Ran: ? I see...?
