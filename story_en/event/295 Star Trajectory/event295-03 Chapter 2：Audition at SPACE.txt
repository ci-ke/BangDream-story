event295-03 Chapter 2: Audition at SPACE

As they visit memorable places, Tae leads them to her pick—SPACE.

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Tae Hanazono, Rimi Ushigome, Saya Yamabuki, Arisa Ichigaya)

[The Ruins of SPACE]

Tae: We’re here~!
Arisa: Whoa... it feels like forever since all five of us came here together.
Kasumi: It really brings back memories~! Like when we first met O-Tae!
Saya: Yeah, when it comes to O-Tae, SPACE is definitely the place.
Rimi: You worked here for such a long time, O-Tae-chan.
Tae: Yeah. I learned so much here... and without this place, I never would’ve met Poppin'Party.
Tae: That’s why, to me, this place is full of memories.
Saya: It really is nostalgic. Like when Kasumi suddenly announced she was auditioning at SPACE...
Arisa: That was seriously rough... Though honestly, Kasumi still drags us into her spur-of-the-moment ideas. Some things never change.
Kasumi: Ehhh~~!?
Tae: But thanks to that, I was able to become part of Poppin'Party.
Tae: At first I thought there was no way I could pass that audition, but after Kasumi invited me, and we all started playing together... I started to believe we really could do it.
Tae: Back then, Kasumi... all of you... you moved me.
Tae: That feeling... it’s still with me. Deep in my heart.
Saya: ...Yeah. This place really gave us something important to hold on to.
Rimi: Which is why... it really is sad that SPACE is gone now...
Tae: It’s not gone.
Kasumi, Rimi, Saya & Arisa: Huh?
Tae: The sights I saw on that stage, the sounds, the feelings—everything’s still inside me.
Tae: Those things support the way I play now.
Tae: So SPACE isn’t gone at all.
Kasumi: ...Yeah! It’s still here!
Arisa: Somehow, you just casually say something really good, O-Tae~...
Saya: Still, getting ready for the audition at SPACE really pushed us hard—mentally, and in terms of our playing too.
Rimi: We got a lot of harsh feedback.
Kasumi: Yeah, yeah. No matter how many times we tried, he kept saying “Nope,” and we kept failing the audition.
Arisa: Yeah. We all tried so hard to figure out what exactly was wrong, and talked over the owner’s words again and again.
Rimi: I especially remember that line.
Tae: “Did you give it everything you've got?”
Kasumi: No, no, more like this! “Did you give it everything you've got?”
Tae: Wow, Kasumi... that was super accurate! Show-off...!
Kasumi: Ehehe, it’s engraved in me~. And when she was being gentle, she’d say~...
Arisa: Okay, seriously, enough with the owner impressions already!
Arisa: Still... I end up remembering that line in all kinds of moments.
Rimi: Same here. I recall it a lot when writing songs or on stage. “Did you give it everything you’ve got?”
Saya: Fufu, so it wasn’t just me. That’s how strong those words were.
Saya: It was tough back then, but... we learned something really important for continuing as a band.
Arisa: Yeah. Like O-Tae said, the feelings from that time are still a huge support for Poppin'Party now.
Kasumi: Then let’s take a picture here so we never forget it!
Tae: Good idea! Um, the entrance to SPACE was around there, so let’s gather—
Tae: Huh?
Rimi: What’s wrong, O-Tae-chan?
Tae: Look over there. There’s a cat.
Saya: You’re right, cute~. Hey, can we take a picture of this first?
Kasumi: I wanna take one too~! Oh—look, more are coming~! Okay everyone, smile-smile~!!
Rimi: Fufu. Cute animals really make you want to take pictures.
Arisa: Still, these cats aren’t scared at all~. They’re super friendly...
Tae: I see them a lot when I walk past here. Maybe it’s a spot for cats—and a catlike girl too.
Arisa: A catlike girl...
Kasumi: Oh! You mean Rana-chan!
Saya: Fufu, she is kinda catlike.
Saya: Speaking of which, the other day at RiNG, Rana-chan and the owner were chatting.
Kasumi: Eh, I wasn’t there for that! Lucky~ I wanna see the owner again too~.
Tae: I’m sure we’ll meet again, as long as we keep playing music.
Saya: Right. Okay everyone, the camera’s set—come over here.
Kasumi: Okay~!
Saya: Alright, here we go~—
