event295-02 Chapter 1: Downtown’s Support Song

The girls head out for a photo, when suddenly a familiar song they once made for downtown begins to play...

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Tae Hanazono, Rimi Ushigome, Saya Yamabuki, Arisa Ichigaya)

[Downtown]

Kasumi: Hmm-hmm~♪ Hey, hey! Where should we go first!?
Arisa: You charged out all excited, but of course you didn’t plan anything, huh...
Kasumi: Muu~~! Then what about you, Arisa? Did you plan something?
Arisa: Me? Not really...
Kasumi: See? You're totally the same, Arisa~! Oh, I know! Wanna go grab some of Hagu's croquettes first?
Tae: Sounds good! I heard they released a new flavor recently.
Rimi: What? Really!? Then we have to go try it right now...!
Arisa: Hey, hey, this is totally getting off track!? Anyway, let's just find a spot that'll look good in a photo...
Arisa: Oh, this song...
Kasumi: It’s Rimi-rin’s song!
Arisa: It’s been a while since you wrote it, huh? It’s practically the theme song of downtown now.
Saya: Fufu, when I’m cleaning in front of the shop, I sometimes hear it and end up humming along.
Saya: It just makes me so cheerful—like suddenly cleaning becomes fun!
Kasumi: Yeah yeah! My body just starts moving on its own~!
Tae: I listen to it a lot when I go running. You’re amazing, Rimi—writing a song like that.
Rimi: I-I get all flustered when you praise me like that...
Rimi: But whenever I hear it myself, I still feel encouraged. It was really hard to make at the time.
Tae: Sorry, Rimi. I said a lot of things back then.
Rimi: No, it’s okay. Honestly, I’m glad you were honest with me—it helped me realize something really important.
Rimi: I used to be afraid—of being influenced by others, and of facing the emptiness inside myself...
Rimi: But everyone accepted me, even when I was like that. And because of that, I found the courage to write a song to cheer myself on.
Arisa: Once you found your answer, you were amazing. You finished the song in one go.
Tae: And you were so particular about it, too. You didn’t compromise on the sound or the lyrics at all.
Rimi: Back then, I was just lost in it...
Rimi: But the feeling I put into that song—wanting to grow little by little—it's still with me.
Rimi: It’s my precious treasure.
Saya: Yeah. Rimi-rin, you've grown so much! You're still taking on all kinds of challenges, too.
Tae: You even tried horror movies recently.
Arisa: Yeah, you were super into it. You rented out a studio and even filmed in the woods at night.
Kasumi: Ugh...! Don’t remind me...
Rimi: S-sorry, Kasumi-chan... But... I might ask for help again someday...
Saya: Fufu, maybe Rimi-rin’s actually the one who’s grown the most in Poppin'Party.
Arisa: Could be. Honestly, it feels like lately we’re the ones getting inspired by you.
Rimi: Oh, no... I’ve still got a long way to go. I’m always relying on everyone...
Rimi: But it's because I have all of you supporting me that I feel like I can try—like I can take that step forward.
Rimi: So... thank you again—for watching over me as I grow.
Saya: Rimi-rin...
Kasumi: Hey, let’s take a picture here! A commemorative photo!
Arisa: Here? I mean, it’s seriously just an ordinary spot. Just a random corner of downtown.
Kasumi: So what! Even if there’s nothing special here, we’ve got so many memories in this place!
Tae: Yeah. We could get a picture that really feels like us.
Rimi: Then let’s all line up.
Saya: Okay. Let me set up the camera.
Saya: Timer... there, done...
Kasumi: Saya, hurry hurry!
Saya: Coming!
Saya: Thanks for waiting! Okay everyone—smile!
Saya: Let’s see how it turned out~.
Kasumi: Wah~! I can’t wait, I can’t wait!
All: Oh~~!!
Kasumi: Wow, doesn’t it look great!?
Saya: Maybe it’s because we were reminiscing, but we all have such nice expressions, fufu.
Tae: Yeah. I love this picture. Everyone’s smiling.
Arisa: You know, maybe taking photos like this actually captures us the best.
Rimi: Oh! I just thought of something!
Saya: Hm? What is it, Rimi-rin?
Rimi: Hey, why don’t we go visit all our memorable places together?
Arisa: Our memorable places?
Rimi: Yeah. Talking with everyone like this made me nostalgic—and kind of motivated too...
Rimi: So I want to hear everyone’s stories at their special places.
Kasumi: As expected of Rimi-rin—what a great idea~!
Saya: That sounds nice. I bet there are things we felt back then... and things we can only say now.
Tae: Then, there’s somewhere I want to go with everyone.
Kasumi: Oh, nice~! Where, where!?
Tae: This way. Come on, everyone—follow me!
(Black cut)
(Background change)
