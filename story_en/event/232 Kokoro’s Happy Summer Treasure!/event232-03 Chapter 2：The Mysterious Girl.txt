event232-03 Chapter 2: The Mysterious Girl

As the girls walk through the cave, the girl they met says something strange...

(Character: Kokoro Tsurumaki, Kaoru Seta, Hagumi Kitazawa, Kanon Matsubara, Misaki Okusawa)

[Seaside Cave]

Kokoro: Wow! There's a river flowing inside the cave!
Hagumi: I bet this is seawater! It's so blue and sparkly and pretty~!
Kaoru: What a mystical view... One can feel the fleetingness of nature.
???: Why did this happen...? I have to make them go away...
Kokoro: Hey, you can join us! Come explore with everyone!
???: Hmph, no way! Why would anyone want to be with scoundrels like you...?
Misaki: She's been glaring at us this whole time, huh?
Kanon: Yeah... Do you think she lives on Happy Happy Island? Maybe she plays in this cave a lot.
Misaki: She's probably angry we just barged in suddenly... We should've told her about our situation first...
Kokoro: By the way, what's your name? I'm Kokoro Tsurumaki!
???: ... Happi.
Kokoro: Happi! That's such a wonderful name!
Hagumi: I'm Hagumi! And they're Kaoru-kun, Mii-kun, and Kano-chan-senpai! We're all members of Hello, Happy World!
Kaoru: We are a band that makes the whole world smile. Including Michelle, the bear DJ who is not with us presently, the six of us make up the band.
Kokoro: Oh yeah, we have Mini Michelle with us, though! Look at this!
Happi: ...! Th-that stuffed toy...
Kokoro: It's made by needle felting. Michelle made it for us so we wouldn't feel lonely!
Kokoro: Isn't it soo cute and fantast-erific? Mini Michelle wants to play with you too!
Happi: ...! I-I don't care!
Kanon: Fufu, Happi-chan seems to like Michelle.
Misaki: Yeah. My little sister acts that way when she's sulking too.
Kaoru: Oh? It appears the path diverges here.
Kokoro: Which way should we go? Hagumi! Where is the stone pointing towards?
Misaki: I told you, it won't glow inside the cav-
Hagumi: Don't worry! It's been glowing ever since we came inside! I think we go this way!
Misaki: Huh? Why? I thought it lit up because it reflected the sunlight.
Happi: ...! That stone... Why do you have it?
Kanon: Happi-chan, do you know something about it?
Happi: Of course I do! That's the stone I lost 200 years ago!
Hagumi: 200 years ago?!
Kaoru: Wh-what a long life you have led...
Misaki: There's no way... I'm not sure, but there's probably a story like that, right?
Kanon: Yeah, maybe she got influenced by a picture book or something.
Kokoro: ...? Why do you think so? Did she say something weird?
Hagumi: I mean, 200 years ago is a super long time, right? I've never seen anyone live that long before!
Kaoru: ...! A-are you implying that Happi-chan is not of this world...?!
Kokoro: Oh, my! Are you a ghost, then?
Happi: ... So what if I am?
Kokoro: That's amazing! It's my first time being friends with a ghost!
Happi: Huh...?
Kokoro: There's so many things I've wanted to ask a ghost if I ever became friends with one! Can I ask you some things, Happi?
Kokoro: Are you better at hide-and-seek or playing tag? I wanna know what your favorite food is and see your smile!
Happi: Th-that's none of your business! Also, we're not friends! Just get out of here already!
Misaki: S-sorry, Kokoro can be a little pushy sometime- Huh?
Kanon: Gasp, it's suddenly windy...!
Hagumi: Also... do you guys hear something?
Happi: There's no use trying to get me on your side! I'll never hand over my treasure to anyone!
Mysterious Voices: Groan...! Groan...!
Mysterious Voices: Fufu... Fufufu... Fufufufu...
Hagumi & Kaoru: Ahh~~!
Misaki: Wh-what are these voices...?! I hear something from within the cave...!
Hagumi: And it's not just one or two people...! There's gotta be dozens of them!
Kanon: Fuee...! A-are there actual ghosts...?!
Misaki: N-no, that's ridiculous... Yeah, it's the wind! The sound of the wind passing through the cave just happened to sound like voices...!
Mysterious Voices: Groan...!  Fufu... Fufufu...
Kaoru: Ahh... I apologize, my little kittens... It seems I am at my limit...
Misaki: Kaoru-san?! Hang on, get it together! Kaoru-san!
Happi: ... You understand now, right? If you don't leave immediatel-
Kokoro: Wow! There are so many ghosts here besides you! Let's all play together!
Happi: Wh-why aren't you scared?
Kokoro: Because the ghosts are laughing! They sound like they're having fun!
Kokoro: Maybe they're playing hide-and-seek, and that's why we can't see them! We must be "it"! Happi! Let's look for them together!
Happi: Huh?! How'd you get that idea?!
Happi: It's not hide-and-seek! I wanted to scare you, so I manipulated the wind... There's no one else here...!
Misaki: H-hey, Kokoro, where are you going?! Don't go too far, you hear me?! Kokoro~!
(Black cut)
