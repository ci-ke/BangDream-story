event40-06 Chapter 5: Mii-kun's Words

It's the last contest, and Michelle seems like she's about to give up when Marie shares a few words with her.

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Tae Hanazono, Kokoro Tsurumaki, Kaoru Seta, Kanon Matsubara, Misaki Okusawa)

[Fluffy Mascot Competition - Team Michelle]

Kanon: Are you all set, Misaki-chan?
Michelle (Misaki): Yeah, probably... I can't believe the last event is a marathon...
Kanon: They said you have to run around to all the famous spots in Hanasakigawa and tell the townspeople why you're the best mascot.
Michelle (Misaki): So in other words, it's more about making a good impression than finishing first, right?
Kanon: Yeah, I think so.
Kokoro: Michelle, it's time for the final challenge! If anyone can take first place, it's you for sure!
Michelle (Misaki): Hold on, were you not listening~? I just said this race is more about interacting with people, not being the fastest~. Besides, Marie is definitely the better athlete here...
Kokoro: That's true, she's very athletic, isn't she? In that case... we could stick a jet engine on you. Does that sound good?
Michelle (Misaki): No, no it doesn't~! That sounds like an awful idea~... Kinda like you want me to get hurt~.
MC: All mascots participating in the marathon, please come to the starting line.
Michelle (Misaki): Ah, it's starting! Gotta go! Wish me luck~!
Kokoro: You can do it, Michelle~! We'll be following you to cheer you on!
Marie: ...
Michelle: (... This is nerve-racking.)
MC: Looks like we're all here. On your mark, get set...
Kasumi: Go, Marie~!! I mean... Hagu? You both can do it~!
Kokoro: Good luck, Michelle~! Good luck, every one of you cute mascots~!!
Michelle: (Ahaha... Kokoro's cheering on Marie too, huh? It doesn't seem like she's realized who she is, though.)
Michelle: (... That's probably for the best.)
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Riverside]

Michelle (Misaki): Huff... Puff... Oh man... This sucks...! Why are we doing this? Wait, this isn't even what mascots are meant to do in the first place...!
Kaoru: She appears to be struggling. Poor, poor Michelle... If only I could somehow take her place, but alas...
Kanon: (Is Misaki-chan... okay? Marie's already taken a considerable lead...)
Marie: ...
Michelle (Misaki): Huff... Puff... I-I can't take it anymore...
Kaoru: Michelle... She's taken a knee. Is she alright...? Perhaps we should attend to her...
Kokoro: Ah, look! Marie's headed over to her!
Marie: Michelle!
Michelle (Misaki): No, why'd you turn back...? Huff... Puff... If you keep going... then you'll win...
Marie: And what about you, huh? What about you, Michelle?!
Michelle (Misaki): Me...? No, I'm done for... I can't go on... Besides, it wouldn't be so bad if you won, Mari-
Marie: You can't give up!
Marie: If I win because you gave up, that wouldn't make me happy! Hagumi Kitazaw- I mean, Marie does not like to win that way! That would only make me sad!
Michelle (Misaki): ...!
Marie: I asked Mii-kun to tell you that I wanted to compete fair and square... Was she too busy to remember to give you my message...?
Michelle (Misaki): ... Fu, fufu.
Michelle (Misaki): Uh ohhh~, you just called yourself Hagumi... Welp, you still got a point.
Marie: Michelle...?
Michelle (Misaki): Up we go... I doubt I can go at the speed I was going before, but I'll make it to the finish line. Even if I have to walk.
Marie: ...! Yeah! Let's pass the finish line together!
Michelle (Misaki): No. Don't worry about me. You keep running. I want you to race with everything you've got. Pretty sure that's more 'fair and square'.
Marie: Oh...! You're right, I have to do my best! And so do you, Michelle!!
Michelle (Misaki): Ahaha... I will, Hagumi. I will.
Kaoru: Ah...! Michelle and Marie have taken off once again!
Kanon: Misaki-chan, Hagumi-chan! You can do it!
