event215-03 Chapter 2: Approaching Farewell

Arisa and Misaki are preparing for the graduation ceremony when the door opens.

(Character: Arisa Ichigaya, Misaki Okusawa, Rinko Shirokane, Rui Yashio)

[Hanasakigawa Girls' Academy - Student Council Room]

Arisa: Alright... The schedule for the graduation ceremony should be good.
Misaki: Ichigaya-san, I put together the pamphlets for the guests. Could you do a final check later?
Arisa: Okay, got it. Okusawa-san, seriously, thanks. Really appreciate the help preparing for the graduation ceremony.
Arisa: It's just such a big task... No matter how many people we have, it's not enough...
Misaki: It's, like, finally gonna happen. I wonder how it'll turn out...
Misaki: Oh, is it true that you volunteered to give the farewell speech to the graduating students?
Arisa: I-I didn't volunteer or anything major like that. Wait, how'd you know...?
Misaki: Toyama-san was talking about it so happily like it was about herself.
Arisa: Ugh, I can't believe her~...
Misaki: So, how is it? Is it going well?
Arisa: Well... Our seniors did a lot for us, and I want to convey how grateful we are.
Arisa: But when I try to put it into words, I can't seem to get it right... Honestly, I'm pretty stumped.
Misaki: I see... Yeah, I can see why that'd happen.
Arisa: Sigh~. I didn't think it'd be this hard...
Misaki: Then, do you wanna skip the ceremony?
Arisa: Jeez... There's no way I can do that. It's a big moment for our seniors.
Misaki: Fufu, then you have no choice but to write it... Do you want me to read what you've written so far?
Misaki: I don't know if I can give you advice, but sometimes having other people look through your work can help you sort out your thoughts better.
Arisa: N-nah, I'll pass. It's kinda embarrassing...
Misaki: Everyone's gonna hear it during the ceremony anyway, you know?
Arisa: I know that, but it's not the same...
Rinko: Hello...
Arisa: Rinko-senpai! You came to school...?!
Rinko: Were you doing work, perhaps...?
Arisa: No, not at all. More importantly, congratulations on getting accepted to the Yotsuba Women's University's Faculty of Arts Music Department!
Misaki: Even though you didn't have much time until the exams, it's like you said you were going to do it, and you actually managed.
Rinko: Thank you so much... I'm just relieved... that it turned out well...
Rinko: I'm here today... to tell my teacher about it... and... I wanted to take in the school's atmosphere again...
Arisa: The school's atmosphere?
Rinko: I... have been writing the speech... for the graduation ceremony... but I can't gather my thoughts properly...
Rinko: When I hold my pen... I don't know what to write... So many things happened in the past three years...
Rinko: They are all... special memories for me... I end up wanting to include everything in my speech...
Arisa: (Oh... That's just like me...)
Rinko: So... in order to organize what I want to say, I decided to go around the school one more time... and take in the atmosphere...
Arisa: So that's why you're here...
Rinko: Yes... To me, this place is... where everything... started.
Rinko: I've made memories in this student council room... Too many to count...
Rinko: If I hadn't come to Hanasakigawa... and... if I hadn't joined the student council... I probably... wouldn't be "who I am today..."
Arisa: ...
Rinko: All the "small steps forward" that I took... are what made me... "who I am today..."
Rinko: ... I think I know... what I should say in my speech...
Rinko: Oh... I-I'm sorry... I talked too much about myself...
Misaki: No! Don't worry, you didn’t at all.
Arisa: I'm glad we got to hear that.
Rinko: Well then... I should go now... Good luck... with your work... And lastly... please lock up before you leave...
Arisa: I will! Thank you...
Misaki & Arisa: ...
Arisa: ... Hey, Okusawa-san.
Misaki: Yeah?
Arisa: Could you take a look at my speech...? I'm not done yet, though...
Misaki: ... Sure. If you're okay with me doing it.
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Café]

Rui: ...
Rinko: Yashio-san... I'm sorry for... being late...
Rui: No, do not apologize. Thank you for coming.
Rinko: So... what did you want to talk about...?
Rui: ... Well...
Rui: Shirokane-san... Congratulations on graduating.
Rinko: Oh... Thank you very much...
Rinko & Rui: ...
Rui: ... By the way, Shirokane-san, it appears you decided to go to Yotsuba Women's University instead of Keiho Women's University.
Rinko: Yes... I really wanted to study piano at the Yotsuba Women's University's Faculty of Arts Music Department...
Rui: ... I think that is extremely brave of you.
Rinko: Huh?
Rui: I ran away from the violin...
Rui: If... I was in your place, Shirokane-san...
Rui: I would have chosen Keiho Women's University.
Rui: That is why I have tremendous respect for you.
Rinko: I haven't done anything to deserve your respect...
Rinko: But...
Rui: ?
Rinko: Yashio-san, you are... playing violin now.
Rui: ... Yes. I couldn't forget that sound.
Rui: I'm sorry, I did not mean to talk about trivial things. My business here is done, so I'll take my leave...
Rinko: Um... if you don't mind... would you like to... talk for a while longer...?
Rui: ... If you wish, Shirokane-san.
(White cut)
(Background change)
