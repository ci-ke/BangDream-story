event309-02 Chapter 1: disappointment

The Monday after the study abroad fair. Something seems a little off about Rimi...

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Tae Hanazono, Rimi Ushigome, Saya Yamabuki, Arisa Ichigaya)

[Monday]

[Riverside]

Kasumi: Arisa~! Hurry, hurry~! Homeroom's about to start~!
Arisa: It's fine. We've still got plenty of time...
Arisa: ...Jeez, why are you so energetic first thing on a Monday~...? You even went back for seconds like crazy on the breakfast my grandma made.
Kasumi: 'Cause we get to see everyone in our class! How could that not be fun?!
Arisa: Yeah, well, for me it's more like... Ugh, another week already...
Kasumi: Hey, look, Arisa! There's a pretty flower blooming! What kind is it~? Dandelion...? Wait, no, right?
Arisa: That's... autumn hawkbit. It blooms in the fall, but... yeah, I guess it is that time of year.
Kasumi: Whoa~! Ehehe, lucky us to run into something like this first thing in the morning! It's such a cute flower!
Arisa: Haa~... I want you to share some of that energy with me...
Arisa: Hm...? Is that... Rimi over there?
Kasumi: It is! Heeey, Rimi-ri~n!
Rimi: Ah... Kasumi-chan, Arisa-chan.
Kasumi: So how was the study abroad fair?! Did you get to talk to lots of people?! What'd the teacher say?!
Rimi: Ah, um...
Arisa: Hey, don't bombard her with questions all at once. You'll make Rimi panic.
Arisa: So, Rimi. Did you learn anything new?
Rimi: Uh, yeah... I guess... I learned a lot...
Arisa: ...Rimi?
Rimi: N-no! Um, let's get going. If we don't hurry, homeroom's gonna start.
Kasumi: O-okay...!
Kasumi & Arisa: ...Huh?
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Recess]

[Hanasakigawa Girls' Academy - Courtyard]

Tae: Bzzz, wrong~. That was Shiroppi just now.
Kasumi: Whaaat?! I was sure it was Doro-chan!
Arisa: How long are you guys gonna keep playing this "Guess O-Tae's rabbits by their voices" quiz...?
Saya: No matter how many times I hear them, I seriously can't tell the difference~...
Tae: That's not true. They're totally different. Right, Rimi?
Rimi: Uh, yeah. I think they're different, fufu.
Kasumi, Arisa, Tae & Saya: ...
Kasumi: Rimi-rin... She's smiling, but she clearly doesn't look okay...
Arisa: Yeah... She's been like that since this morning...
Saya: Maybe something happened at the study abroad fair...
Tae: Hey, Rimi. What's wrong? You haven't been smiling much for a while now. Did something happen at the study abroad fair?
Arisa: H-hey, O-Tae...!
Rimi: Uh, um... You think so? I don't think that's true, but...
Tae: But you haven't eaten much of your bento, Rimi.
Tae: Before we went to the fair, you were totally sparkling. Now you're not sparkling at all. Rimi, did something happen at the study abroad fair?
Rimi: ...
Kasumi: Rimi-rin, if you want... I'd like you to tell us, too.
Saya: Yeah. If something's bothering you, let's worry about it together.
Arisa: I mean... we're Poppin'Party, after all.
Rimi: ...Everyone.
Rimi: Um, so... on Saturday, I went to the study abroad fair...
Rimi: At the start, the organizer said this to everyone there.
Rimi: "Studying abroad isn't a vacation."
Rimi: And those words kinda... stuck in me like a thorn...
Rimi: After that, we did some group work, and I talked with other kids our age...
Rimi: Like how they wanted to learn languages to become a flight attendant, or study economics and make it big around the world...
Rimi: Everyone was talking so specifically about what they wanted to do after studying abroad...
Rimi: I... couldn't say anything...
Kasumi: Rimi-rin...
Arisa: B-but, y'know, it's not like everyone has some crystal-clear goal, right?
Arisa: I mean, I'm sure some people study abroad to find their dream...
Saya: Yeah... I even talked it over with Dad and decided I'd go to college to figure out what I want to do, so I don't think you need to feel that down...
Rimi: ...But studying abroad takes a lot of time and money... And the lecturer said that's why it's important to be clear on your purpose...
Rimi: And... I heard a lot of stories about people messing up, too.
Rimi: Like people who studied abroad without any real purpose, and came back without learning a thing...
Rimi: Or people who only talked to other Japanese students and never got to connect with locals...
Rimi: Somehow, it all felt like it was being said directly to me, and the more I listened, the more embarrassed I got...
Rimi: I realized... trying to study abroad without a proper goal... I was being so naive...
Kasumi: I-it's okay! Because, Rimi-rin, you felt sparkling, heart-pounding, right? So...!
Rimi: Y-yeah. I'm okay. I'm not down anymore.
Rimi: If I haven't decided on a goal, then I just need to find one from here on out. I'm not gonna give up over something like this.
Rimi: ...Oh, Kasumi-chan, that side dish looks really good. Wanna trade with me?
Kasumi: O-of course! This omelet is suuuper good! Here you go!
Rimi: Munch... Fufu, you're right. It's tasty.
Kasumi: R-right?! Aa-chan and I made it together...!
Saya, Arisa & Tae: ..........
(Black cut)
(Background change)
