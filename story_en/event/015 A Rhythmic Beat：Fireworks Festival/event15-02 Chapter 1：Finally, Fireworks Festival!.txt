event15-02 Chapter 1: Finally, Fireworks Festival!

The festival is buzzing and overflowing with people... That's when Arisa makes a suggestion.

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Tae Hanazono, Rimi Ushigome, Saya Yamabuki, Arisa Ichigaya)

[Station Entrance]

Kasumi: Here we are! Today is the day! The fireworks display we've all been waiting for~!!
Kasumi: Fi-re-works! Fi-re-works!
Saya: Kasumi, settle down~.
Kasumi: What~? How could I settle down?! Do you know how long I've been waiting for this day?!
Saya: Yes, actually, I do... K-Kasumi, cut it out. You can't take big steps like that while wearing a yukata!
Kasumi: Okay~! ... Where's everybody at~? Think they're almost here~? Ah~, I can't wait!
Rimi: Ah, Kasumi-chan, Saya-chan!
Kasumi: That sounds like Rimi-rin~! ... Huh?
Rimi: Sorry, it took a while to get changed. Have you been waiting long...?
Kasumi: ... R-Rimi-rin. You're wearing your...
Rimi: Remember what O-Tae said the other day? She said she would be wearing her yukata... So, I put mine on too. Is that why you're wearing yours, Kasumi?
Kasumi: ... M-mhm...
Rimi: So we were thinking the same thing, then... Uhm, Kasumi-chan? Is everything okay? You've been kinda... staring...
Kasumi: Yeah... It's just that seeing you in your yukata... I couldn't help but be amazed. You're so beautiful... Ehehe~.
Rimi: B-beautiful...?! N-no... I-I think if anyone is beautiful here, it'd be you, Kasumi-chan!
Kasumi: Huh...? Really?! Yay~! I'm beautiful! Saya helped me put it on. Isn't it great?
Saya: Kasumi, what did I just say? No jumping around in your yukata...!
Rimi: Saya-chan, you know how to put on a yukata? Wow.
Saya: I know a little bit. Nothing amazing, really.
Rimi: But it is. I think it is amazing. Also, seeing you in your yukata is exactly what I thought it would be like. It makes you look very mature.
Saya: Y-you think so? Thanks. Ahaha... I think I know how you were feeling now. Hearing you compliment me with that smile on your face... Feels really funny.
Tae: Ah, everyone's wearing a yukata...
Kasumi: Wah~! O-Tae, you look so nice with yours on!
Saya: Yeah... Especially with that silky hair of yours, you've got a look that totally says, "Japanese beauty."
Kasumi: And all this because O-Tae said she was gonna wear hers~.
Rimi: I wonder what Arisa will be wearing. It'd be nice if we were all wearing yukata together...
Tae: Ah, there she is.
Arisa: Sorry I'm late... Y-you guys all... came in your yukata, huh...?
Kasumi: You say that like you're not wearing yours!
Arisa: ... I figured we'd all be on the same page...
Arisa: ... Though I guess that means Kasumi and I were thinking the same thing... Dear God...
Rimi: Arisa-chan, you're really pretty... You're so captivating, wearing that. I can't help but stare...
Arisa: What?! Y-you can't just say stuff like that?!
Tae: Arisa, you're blushing.
Arisa: N-no I'm not!
Saya: We know, we know. You're not blushing. You're just red, but... that's not blushing, right...?
Arisa: Sh-shut up!
Kasumi: Ahahaha! Alright, now that everyone's here, let's go! Fireworks, here we come~!
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Fireworks Festival Area]

Kasumi: Whoa, look at all these people! You think they're all here for the fireworks?
Arisa: That's what I was saying. The amount of people that come to this thing is insane...
Saya: It is a pretty big deal. There are also food stands on the other side.
Rimi: Where do you think the fireworks will be?
Arisa: Uhm... I'm pretty sure it's this way...
Rimi: If that's true, we might not be able to see much...
Kasumi: Aww~, we're not gonna see them?! No~, I wanna see the fireworks~!
Saya: And it looks like people have been holding on to all the good spaces for a few days now...
Kasumi: No~!! Fireworks~!!
Arisa: ... Sigh, I guess I have no choice. I actually have a place I wanted to keep secret, but... if you want, we can go there.
Kasumi: ... Huh?! Arisa, you know a good spot?!
Arisa: There probably won't be that many people, so I think so.
Kasumi: Yes! Absolutely! Let's go!
Arisa: It's a bit of a walk from here. Is everyone okay with that?
Kasumi: Of course! Right, guys?!
Rimi: That's fine, but... with a crowd like this, we have to make sure we don't get separated.
Kasumi: Mhm, you're right!
Saya: How about we hold hands? It's what I do in a crowded place when I'm with my little brother and sister.
Saya: Anyone who wants to hold hands, say aye!
Tae: Aye.
Arisa: O-O-Tae... You just "aye" with no shame...? You're not embarrassed? We're not babies, you know...
Tae: But... I don't wanna get lost.
Saya: Fufu! That's a good girl. Alright, let's hold hands.
Tae: Great. I feel safe now.
Kasumi: Aye! Aye! Me too! Me too! Time to hold Saya's hand and... off to Arisa's secret spot!
