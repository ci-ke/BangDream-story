event15-03 Chapter 2: A Secret Spot?

The girls are headed for Arisa's recommended spot, but what happens when Kasumi notices a fair going on...?

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Tae Hanazono, Rimi Ushigome, Saya Yamabuki, Arisa Ichigaya)

[Fireworks Festival Area]

Kasumi: Hey, Arisa... Where's this secret place you have in mind?
Arisa: I guess you'll just have to wait and see.
Kasumi: Aww~, why won't you tell me~?
Arisa: It won't be any fun if I tell you now.
Saya: Ahaha, I think I know what you mean... But you know, it looks like we're the only ones walking in the opposite direction of the crowd.
Arisa: That's why I'm sure no one will know about this place. They wouldn't have thought about how easily you could see the fireworks from there.
Tae: Arisa, you're always full of confidence, but you're even more full of it now... I trust you.
Arisa: I don't know if telling me I'm "full of it" is actually a compliment...
Saya: Fufu, it just means she believes this place of yours will be great. More importantly, is everyone here? No one's lost?
Rimi: M-mhm... I'm okay, even with all of these people... It's easy to keep track of Kasumi-chan's yukata.
Tae: I'm okay too. Since I'm holding Saya's hand.
Kasumi: I'm holding Saya's hand too, so- Huh? Whoa~!! Look, look! They're having a fair over there!
Kasumi: There are so many booths! Wow~!
Kasumi: Hey hey, Arisa!
Arisa: Don't tell me. You want to walk through it?
Kasumi: ... Wait, h-how did you know?
Arisa: Well, your train of thought is not really that hard to follow.
Kasumi: Ehehe, of course it isn't for you! You know me so well! Alright then, let's walk through the fair you gu-
Arisa: Stop. Have you seen how many people are there...? Moving through that crowd would be absolutely exhausting.
Kasumi: Aww~, c'mon~. We should all have takoyaki together while we watch the fireworks~.
Arisa: Nope.
Kasumi: Aww~, but I wanna go to the fair~. Look, even Rimi-rin's face has "yes" written all over it.
Rimi: M-me?! I- Well, uhh...
Saya: To be fair to Kasumi, it does look pretty exciting. I definitely know how she feels.
Kasumi: ... Ahh~, I can't!! I just have to see it! I'll be right back...!
Arisa: H-hey! Kasumi! Hold on...!
Arisa: Sigh... She did it. She actually went.
Saya: Yeah. I guess Kasumi would never really be able to just walk past a fair, huh?
Arisa: But still... That doesn't mean she should just disappear into the crowd... Going by herself is clearly not a great idea...
Rimi: Y-you're right. She shouldn't be by herself... I-I'm gonna go look for her! When I find her, I'll call you!
Arisa: Ah, Rimi! Come on, now Rimi's gone too...?
Tae: Should I go look for them next?
Arisa: No! No way! The only thing you'll do is get lost! I can see it now!
Saya: Yeah, I think it'll be better if the rest of us stick together.
Saya: Okay then... Why don't the three of us go to Arisa's spot first?
Saya: Besides, if we do that, I think it'll be easier to meet up when Rimi-rin calls us.
Arisa: ... Probably. Alright, let's do that.
Saya: Great. Time to secure the spot! Let's go, team!
Tae: Arisa... Aye...
Arisa: Hm? Aye...? Why are you holding your hand out...?
Tae: Wouldn't it be better if we were holding hands? It's even more crowded than before, too.
Arisa: D-don't be stupid! I don't wear diapers anymore! No way am I gonna hold your hand!
Tae: Yeah, you were saying that before too, but you don't have to wear diapers to hold hands.
Arisa: No no no no no! I-I absolutely will not!!
Arisa: C-come on! Let's get outta here already! The spot is this way...!
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Fair]

Saya: ... Hey, O-Tae. Could I see your phone for a sec'?
Tae: My phone? Why?
Saya: Do you have service?
Tae: Uhm... Ah, no signal.
Saya: I thought so... I haven't had service for a while. Maybe because of the crowd...?
Tae: I'm not sure, but... If we can't use our phones...
Saya: Exactly... I'm not sure how they're gonna be able to reach us...
Saya: Hey, Arisa. What do you thin-
Saya: ... Huh? Arisa...? ... She's... gone...
Saya: N-now what...?! We lost Arisa!
