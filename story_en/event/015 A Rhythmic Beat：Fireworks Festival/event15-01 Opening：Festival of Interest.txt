event15-01 Opening: Festival of Interest

Poppin'Party has just finished rehearsal. However, Kasumi seemed to have her head in the clouds...

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Tae Hanazono, Rimi Ushigome, Saya Yamabuki, Arisa Ichigaya)

[Ichigaya Residence - Basement]

Rimi: Kasumi-chan... Were you feeling okay during rehearsal today?
Kasumi: ... Huh? Why? Did I make a mistake?
Tae: Not a mistake. Many mistakes, Kasumi.
Kasumi: R-really? Sorry... Actually, wait... Has anyone seen my sheet music?
Saya: Isn't this one yours? I found it under this magazine...
Arisa: Hey, come on. Get it together, Kasumi~. Aren't you supposed to be the leader here?
Kasumi: S-sorry...
Saya: Kasumi, is something wrong? During rehearsal, you were all fidgety...
Arisa: Yeah, you were spaced out. It's kinda weird. Normally, you're all like, "Yay~, rehearsal!" and way too over the top.
Kasumi: ... Well...
Rimi: D-did something happen, Kasumi-chan?
Kasumi: Mhm. Before I came to rehearsal, I passed by the bookstore and learned about something totally awesome...
Saya: And what would this totally awesome thing be?
Kasumi: So in the magazine that was on top of my sheet music-
Tae: This one?
Kasumi: That one! Take a look at the featured story!
Tae: Featured story, featured story... "The Best Spots for Watching Fireworks in the City."
Kasumi: That's it!
Rimi: Fireworks... I can't believe it's already that time of the year. I feel like the school entrance ceremony just ended... Time went by so fast.
Kasumi: I know, right?! And you know what?! According to that article, there's a fireworks festival in this very neighborhood! Did you know?!
Saya: Did we know...? Uhm, it's a yearly event for the people in town.
Tae: Everyone makes a pretty big deal about the whole thing, right?
Arisa: And visitors come swarming in to see what all the buzz is about.
Kasumi: Are you serious?! How come no one told me about it~?!
Arisa: You're aware that there are posters hanging up all around town for this thing, right? Wouldn't it be weirder if someone didn't realize...?
Kasumi: No way~. Think about it! I had no idea it was happening!
Arisa: Yeah, no surprise there.
Kasumi: H-hey~! What's that supposed to mean~?!
Saya: Now that you mention it, isn't the festival this weekend?
Kasumi: Yeah! That! That's what I wanted to talk about! We should all go if you guys are free!
Rimi: Oh, okay. So the reason you were so fidgety was that you really wanted to go see the fireworks.
Kasumi: Saya used the word fidgety too, but I don't know... I would say it was more like I couldn't sit still.
Arisa: So... basically the same thing they said...
Kasumi: Guys~, come on~. Let's go see the fireworks~.
Arisa: Give it a rest. It's supposed to be about the fireworks display, but the crowds are gonna be massive. It might as well be called a people display instead.
Kasumi: I don't wanna give it a rest!! I wanna go with you guys to see the fireworks~.
Arisa: What are you, five...? Actually, hold on a sec'. Aren't we supposed to be finishing up our homework this weekend?
Kasumi: ...?!
Arisa: And if I remember correctly, a certain someone said, "I don't know what I'm gonna do about all this homework! Let's do it together, guys!" ... Didn't they, Kasumi?
Kasumi: Ah-ahaha... So... you remember that...
Kasumi: But wait, hold on... That was the me before I knew about the festival, and this is the me that knows about it... Two completely different people!!
Arisa: That makes no sense!
Saya: I see. So you didn't know how to bring it up because you were the one who asked to do homework together, right?
Kasumi: ... M-mhm...
Kasumi: A-alright, what about this?! Change of plans! I will absolutely, positively finish all of my homework before the day of the fireworks! If I do, we can go, right?!
Arisa: ... Well, I guess that means we're not going...
Kasumi: I will! I promise!
All: ...
Kasumi: ... How come no one believes me~?
Tae: I guess because... this is classic Kasumi?
Arisa: O-O-Tae... How do you keep a straight face when saying things like that...?
Kasumi: I got it... From this day forward, Kasumi Toyama will be reborn!! All of my homework will be done by the day of the fireworks festival!
Kasumi: So... You guys~, come on~. Saya~, Rimi-rin~, O-Tae~, Arisa~. I want to go together~.
Saya: Calm down, calm down. No need to get extra huggy. Sigh... I guess if you're gonna keep on going on about it... Right, guys?
Rimi: Ahaha... Mhm. Right. Okay, why don't we go with her? Arisa-chan, O-Tae-chan. You'll come too, right?
Arisa: Sigh... Geez, if we have to... But, I mean, she did say she'll actually do her homework... O-Tae, come with us.
Tae: ... Ah. I'll be wearing a yukata to the event.
Arisa: You're already preparing?!
Kasumi: Yay~! Thanks, you guys! We're gonna have so much fun~!
