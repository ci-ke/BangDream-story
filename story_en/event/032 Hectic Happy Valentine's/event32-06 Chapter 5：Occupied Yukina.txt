(Character: Arisa Ichigaya, Moca Aoba, Tsugumi Hazawa, Kanon Matsubara, Yukina Minato, Lisa Imai)

Arisa: Sh-she's talking to the cats!
Lisa: A-ahaha... Let's just keep this between us, shall we?

(Black cut)
