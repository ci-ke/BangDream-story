event275-05 Chapter 4: Pippi and Kyu

Assigned as dolphin trainers, Kanon and Kokoro greet their dolphin partners.

(Character: Kokoro Tsurumaki, Kaoru Seta, Hagumi Kitazawa, Kanon Matsubara, Misaki Okusawa)

[Aquarium - Dolphin Show Area]

Dolphin trainer: It looks like Tsurumaki-san and Matsubara-san have finished changing. Now then, I’ll go ahead and take you to the dolphins.
Kanon: Th-thank you very much...!
Kokoro: Oh my! It's the dolphins from the show earlier! I'm so happy to see them again!
Kanon: Wow... they're even bigger up close than I thought...
Kokoro: Yes, they do look bigger than they did from the seats!
Dolphin trainer: Each of them weighs close to 300 kilograms.
Kanon: R-really? That’s a bit surprising.
Kokoro: Pippi, Kyu, we’ll be doing the show together! Let’s make it great!
Pippi & Kyu: Pip! Kyuui!
Kanon: W-wow, they’re responding...! That’s amazing, Kokoro-chan.
Kokoro: Fufu, I think we’ll get along wonderfully!
Dolphin trainer: Alright then, from here on, you'll pair off and begin training. Each dolphin has a different specialty—do you have a preference?
Kanon: Umm... Pippi-chan’s specialty is the big jump, and Kyu-chan’s is swimming with a trainer on its back, right?
Kokoro: Then I think you should pair up with Kyu, Kanon!
Kokoro: The show’s climax was with Kyu and the trainer, right? I think you’d have the brightest smile up there!
Kanon: B-but... wouldn’t that suit you better, Kokoro-chan? Even in the band...
Kokoro: Oh? Shouldn’t it be about whether you want to do it, Kanon, not whether it suits me?
Kanon: Do I want to do it...
Kanon: (They looked like they were having so much fun during the performance... If I could do that too...)
Kanon: ...Yeah. I’m still not confident, but... if you’ll be cheering me on, Kokoro-chan... then I want to try...!
Kokoro: Then it’s settled! Let’s do our best, Pippi!
Pippi: Piii!
Kanon: P-please be kind, Kyu-chan.
Kyu: ......
Kanon: H-huh...?
Dolphin trainer: Alright, now that everyone has their partners, let’s start by practicing some simple tricks.
Kokoro: Got it!
Dolphin trainer: First, raise your right arm like this and make big circles in the air. When you do that—
Kanon: Wow, it’s spinning in sync with the trainer.
Kokoro: So that’s how they did the performance earlier! Raise your right arm... like this?
Pippi: Pip!
Kanon: Wow! It’s spinning with Kokoro-chan too...!
Dolphin trainer: This is quite surprising... Maybe you two have exceptional compatibility?
Kokoro: Fufu, I used to play with dolphins all the time on Happy Happy Island, so I just kind of know how to ask!
Dolphin trainer: All the time...?
Kanon: Ahaha...
Kokoro: Kanon, you try it too!
Kanon: O-okay...! Um, right arm up and...
Kyu: ......
Kanon: Kyu-chan? Um, can you see me...?
Kyu: ...... Kyuu
Kokoro: Huh? It swam away...
Kanon: Fuee!? W-wait~!
Dolphin trainer: It’s okay. That’s normal at first. Dolphins are very intelligent, so treat them like you would a person.
Kanon: R-right...
Dolphin trainer: Let’s try calling it back.
Kanon: K-Kyu-chan, over here~...!
Kyu: ......
Dolphin trainer: Hmm, that’s odd... I’ll step in for a moment.
Kanon: (I-Is it because I’m not good enough...? What should I do... Can I really pull this off in the real show...?)
