event200-06 Chapter 5: Sunset Ferris Wheel

Tomoe and Moca escape into a Ferris wheel pod and look back at the crazy day.

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa)

[Amusement Park - Ferris Wheel Car]

Tomoe: Huff... Huff... Sigh~, somehow we lost them~.
Moca: Jeez Louise~, we were running for our lives back there~...
Tomoe: Ah, Moca, check that out. See? Himari is down there shouting something.
Moca: Yep, there she is~. Hmm... She might be saying, "Argh~, you two come down here this instant~!"
Tomoe: And maybe "I'm so angry with you~!"...
Tomoe: I guess I can't blame her. If I were her, I'd probably be just as angry too.
Moca: Yeah~, this was an epic prank~.
Tomoe: Haha, it sure was. But I never imagined we'd blow our cover like that. That was embarrassing.
Moca: Jeez, Tomo-chin, our prank only fell apart at the end because you slipped up~.
Tomoe: Wait, if we're both being honest, you're the one who really gave us away.
Moca: Huh~? I did~?
Tomoe: You were the one who said, "Uh-oh." I guess... we both kind of dropped the ball, huh?
Moca: I'm glad we made a run for it, but we'll still need to make our peace with the others, won't we~? Ahaha~.
Tomoe: I guess we should be ready to get chewed out once we get off this Ferris wheel.
Moca: Yeah, I guess so.
Tomoe: ... This really was a kind of weird day~.
Moca: I never thought a little random prank I came up with would go this far~... Thanks for doing it with me~, Tomo-chin.
Tomoe: Haha, why are you getting serious on me all of the sudden?
Tomoe: It was a weird day but not a bad one. It's not every day that you get to be someone else.
Tomoe: I think I kind of get why people like to wear costumes on Halloween.
Moca: It was fun being you~.
Tomoe: And after a day of trying to think like you, I feel like I understand what's going on in your head a little better.
Tomoe: Like, how you'll say silly stuff to try help us relax. Or how well you notice things about us...
Tomoe: So, um, what I guess I'm trying to say is... it was fun playing this prank with you.
Moca: Tomo-chin...
Moca: But I'll never forget what happened with the toasted sandwiches... Oh, how I weep~.
Tomoe: I'm the one who won't ever forget. I thought my poor stomach was gonna burst.
Moca: Ahaha~, sorry 'bout that~.
Moca: By the way, I haven't forgotten my promise to treat you to three bowls of ramen~. Just lemme know when you wanna go.
Tomoe: Ah, that's right. I totally forgot about that.
Moca: Oh~, Tomo-chin~. You were today's star, so order as much as you want~. You can even get extra toppings~♪
Tomoe: Whoa! Really? In that case, I guess I'll accept your offer~! I wonder what I'll get~. Well, today I'm feeling like... tonko-
Moca: Pork bone soy sauce ramen with tons of roasted pork and a mountain high pile of bean sprouts~.
Tomoe: Wow! When you know, you know! That's exactly what I'm in the mood for!
Moca: Fu fu fu~, I read your thoughts perfectly~.
Moca: ... Fufu, I guess calling it "just a feeling" really is the only way to explain it~.
Tomoe: See? It's just a feeling you get!
Tomoe & Moca: Pfft... Ahaha!
Tomoe: Oh wow! Moca, look! The sunset is so beautiful.
