event200-04 Chapter 3: Toasted Sandwich Chaos

Tomoe has managed to pretend to be Moca so far, but a crisis strikes!

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa)

[Amusement Park - Food Cart]

Tomoe: I'm so hungry~. I can't wait any longer~.
Food Cart Clerk: Here you go! These are your free special toasted sandwiches!
Himari: Oh wow~! It's this big?! It looks like it's as thick as a dictionary!
Moca: A big, juicy hunk of Hamburg steak in between slices of crispy, crunchy bread. It looks great~... Don't you think, Moca?!
Tomoe: Yep, this is epic~!
Himari: There's no way I can eat all of this. Moca, I'll give you half of mine.
Moca: Really?! I will be forever grateful, o blessed Hima-sama~!
Himari: Huh...? I said I'd give it to Moca, not you, Tomoe...
Moca: A-ahaha! Did I fool you? I sounded just like her, didn't I?
Tomoe: Y-you did, you did~! Ahaha~.
Ran: I'll give some of mine too. Here, Moca.
Tsugumi: Umm... In that case, maybe I'll give half of mine too...
Tomoe: W-woohoo~! Thanks guys~! Moca-chan is so happy~!
Tomoe: (Ugh... This is so much food. Can I even eat all this?)
Moca: Th-there's no way you can eat that much, right~? How about I help you a little?
Tomoe: Really? Yeah~, I think this is a lot, even for me...
Himari: Huh?! That's too much food... for Moca?!
Ran: But you were so excited about this...
Tomoe: (Ugh...! They're right. The real Moca could wolf this amount down with ease...)
Tomoe: Ps-psyche! I was only kidding~! I'm gonna eat it all~!
Tomoe: Munch... It's sooo good~. Hooray for toasted sandwiches~.
Tsugumi: Y-you're really digging in...! I didn't know anyone could look that happy when eating...
Tsugumi: Only the real Moca-chan could eat like that... Right...?
Tomoe: Mmm~, sooo good~! I could never eat enough buns~.
Tomoe: (Ugh... My stomach's starting to hurt... Crud~! Just what is Moca's darn stomach made of~?!)
Moca: (Ohh~... It should've been me eating those~...)
(Black cut)

[Amusement Park]

Moca: Sigh~... My... toasted sandwiches...
Tomoe: What other choice was there? I had to eat them. And we almost got caught because you slipped out of character.
Moca: No one can hide their true nature in front of buns...
Tomoe: Nah, I think that's just you. Isn't it time we came clean?
Moca: What~? After I've sacrificed my darling toasted sandwiches. There's no way I could stop now~.
Tomoe: What do you mean, "sacrificed"...?
Moca: Since we've held out for so long, let's keep fooling the others until we go back to the coffee cups in the evening~.
Tomoe: But our prank turned out pretty good, so shouldn't we end it now? We're going to have to tell them the truth sooner or later...
Himari: Ah, so this is where you two are! Tsugu~, Ran, over here.
Tomoe: H-huh~? What's up, guys~?
Ran: You said you were just going to throw away the trash, but you never came back.
Moca: Oh~, my bad, my bad. Didn't mean to make you worry.
Tomoe: ... Guys, there's something we need to tell you. Um... You see, the thing is, we've-
Ran: Listen, if you're not feeling well or something, just say so.
Tomoe: Huh?
Ran: You know, because, um... if you've switched bodies, you might feel a little weird.
Tsugumi: Right. Your bodies are going through something really extraordinary.
Himari: Yes, exactly. I want us to all have fun at the amusement park, but I'm more worried about how you two are feeling.
Tomoe: ... Ah, okay.
Ran: Hey, wasn't there something you were trying to say a minute ago?
Tomoe: Huh~?! Well~... Th-the thing is~...
Tomoe: The thing is, I was telling Tomo-chin that I wanted to go on the water ride~! Right, Tomo-chin?
Moca: Huh?
Tomoe: Right?! Tomo-chin?!
Tomoe: Right?!
Moca: Ah! Yeah!
Himari: You two really like thrill rides, don't you~? Tomoe, you always scream so loud.
Moca: What kind of person goes on a thrill ride and doesn't scream, right?! Screaming makes the ride so much better~.
Ran: Okay, then let's go on the water ride next. Which way was it?
Tsugumi: Give me a second. I'll check the map. Um, we're here, so...
Tomoe: ...
Moca: Hrm~? Tomo-chin, are you sure you don't want to tell them the truth~?
Tomoe: Ugh, y-you see how they are! How am I supposed to tell them now?! I think they totally believe we've switched...
Moca: Jeez, you're such a wimp~.
Tomoe: Wha- You don't have to be so blunt about it!
Moca: It's true, but that's one of your good points~.
Himari: Hey, you two~! You walk so slow~! Pick up the pace~!
Tomoe: Sorry, we're coming~!
Moca: Heh heh~, let's keep this show going~.
Moca: You're up to it, right? Moca?
Tomoe: Argh~, fine! I've come this far, so I'll keep being Moca until the end...!
Tomoe: Aye aye~! Here we go, Tomo-chin~!
(Black cut)
