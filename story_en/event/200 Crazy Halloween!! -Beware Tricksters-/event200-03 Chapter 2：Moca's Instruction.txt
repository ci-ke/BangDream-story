event200-03 Chapter 2: Moca's Instruction

Tomoe and Moca decide to keep up the act, and the girls enter a haunted house.

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa)

[Haunted Mansion]

Himari: ... Hey, did you just see something over there?! Look, there! Behind the grave!
Ran: Cut it out, Himari...!
Himari: I-I'm sorry... I'm just so scared~.
Moca: You were the one saying we should just go in since we came all this way, remember?
Himari: Well~, I didn't think it would be this scary...
Tsugumi: Yeah, the outside was cute and colorful...
Himari: D-don't let go of me, Tomoe...
Tomoe: Ouchhh~. It hurts when you hold me so tight~, Hima-... Hii-chan.
Moca: Besides, I'm over here now.
Himari: Ah, that's right. Ohh~, this is just so weird~!
Moca: Haha, I'm sure you'll get used to it.
Tsugumi: ... Guys, did you hear something? It sounded like someone dragging their feet...
Ran: Oh no... Is there something behind u-...
Zombie: Ughhh...!!
Tomoe, Ran, Tsugumi & Himari: A-ahhh~!!
(Black cut)
Moca: We're okay here. Somehow we outran that thing... These zombies are crazy scary~.
Tsugumi: Yeah, they are. For a moment, I thought they might actually be real...
Himari: Ugh~, I so don't want to be here anymore~. I've screamed so much my throat hurts...!
Himari: Huh? Now that I think about it, Moca, weren't you screaming too?
Tomoe: What?! Ah, well~, I , uhh...
Ran: Who cares about that right now...! We need to get out of here as fast as possible.
Himari: Right! Um, the signs say the exit is probably this way...
Moca: ... Hey, Tomo-chin. Moca-chan doesn't scream like that~.
Tomoe: Okay, but... what's done is done, alright~?!
Moca: Come on~, you've gotta think and act more like me~. Or the others will catch on~.
Tomoe: Ugh, I know... Think and act like Moca, huh?
Moca: Exactly.
Zombie: Ughhh!!
Ran, Tsugumi & Himari: Ahhh~!!
Moca: Ahhh~! They're back~!
Tomoe: (Huh?! Is that what I'm like?!)
Tomoe: (But I guess maybe I really would act like that...)
Ran: No, no, no! I can't take this anymore!
Tomoe: Ah, Ran! Where are you going? Wait for us~!
(Black cut)
Tomoe: Um... A-are you okay~? Ran?
Ran: I hate this...
Tomoe: We lost the others~. C'mon, Ran. Stand up~.
Ran: ... This is why I said I didn't want to come here...!
Tomoe: (What would Moca do at a time like this...?)
Tomoe: (She tried to scare and tease all of us that time we went back to school at night to get something.)
Tomoe: (I couldn't understand why she was playing around at a time like that...)
Tomoe: (Maybe that was just her way of trying to help calm everyone down. In that case...)
Tomoe: I-If you're that scared~... I'll let you hold my hand~. Right now, it'll only cost you one melon bun~.
Tomoe: (H-how was that? That was really Moca-like, right?)
Ran: What are you talking about? Don't tease me at a time like this...
Tomoe: Ahaha~. My bad, my bad.
Ran: ...
Tomoe: Wh-what is it~, Ran~?
Ran: It's just... are you really Moca?
Tomoe: ...! C'mon now~. Like I've been telling you~, I'm the one and only Moca-chan~.
Ran: Fufu... I guess you did seem a lot like her just now.
Tomoe: (Oh, my gosh! Is Ran... starting to believe me?! Moca~! I pulled it off~!)
Himari: Ah! Ran~, Moca~! We finally found you~!
Moca: You two ran off, and we had no idea where you went. We were worried.
Tomoe: Oh~, so sorry about that~.
Tsugumi: Ran-chan, are you okay?
Ran: Yeah. I was with Tomoe- I mean, Moca. We kind of had a bit of a laugh too.
Moca: Really~...? Okay, then.
Himari: We're all back together now, so let's get out of here pronto. I'm dying to be outside in the light!
Ran: Yeah, let's hurry.
Moca: Tomo-chin, sounds like you were a good Moca-chan~.
Tomoe: Hehe, I guess so. We've all known each other forever, so I can guess what you'd probably say if I think about it.
Moca: Keep it up~, Moca-chan No. 2.
Tsugumi: What's wrong, you two?
Tomoe: Nothing, nothing at all~.
(Black cut)
