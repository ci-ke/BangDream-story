event200-05 Chapter 4: The Prankster Duo Method

The sun is setting. Can Tomoe and Moca keep up the act until the end?

(Character: Ran Mitake, Moca Aoba, Himari Uehara, Tomoe Udagawa, Tsugumi Hazawa)

[Amusement Park]

Himari: The water ride was amazing~! It was way higher than I expected.
Tsugumi: Yeah. The moment we went over the edge, I could feel a chill deep down in my stomach.
Moca: You all screamed like crazy.
Ran: Moca- I mean, Tomoe, you screamed the most. I was sitting next to you and thought my eardrums were going to burst.
Tomoe: Tell me about it. Tomo-chin really screamed the loudest~.
Moca: Huh~? Really? I thought I was just as loud as everyone else~. Screaming at the top of your lungs feels good!
Himari: So what should we ride next? There's a swing ride over there.
Tomoe: Before we go, how about we take a little break~? After all that screaming, maybe we could use a drink~.
Tsugumi: Okay, I'll get us something from that café. What would you like?
Himari: I'll have some orange juice!
Moca: I'll take a lemon soda.
Tomoe: I'll take~... an iced caramel latte with lots of whipped cream~.
Moca: ...!
Tsugumi: Fufu, okay. How about you, Ran-chan?
Ran: I guess I'll go with you. You won't be able to carry everything by yourself.
Tsugumi: Thanks, Ran-chan.
Himari: I'll go too~. You two wait here.
Tomoe: ... Phew, they're gone. You alright, Moca? It kind of seemed like you were zoning out.
Moca: Well~, I guess I was just a little surprised at the drink you ordered~. It was exactly what I wanted just now~. How'd you know?
Tomoe: How'd I know...? I guess I just had a feeling.
Moca: Really?
Tomoe: I was thinking about what you'd say in this situation, and then, snap, it just kind of came to me. Argh~, it's hard to explain.
Tomoe: All I can say is that I had a feeling!
Moca: Hmm~, is that so~? I feel pretty safe knowing you've tapped into your inner Moca-chan~.
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Amusement Park]

Tsugumi: It's gotten pretty dark. Ah, the coffee cup ride is running again.
Ran: If it'll help you get back to normal, then you need to go. What are you going to do?
Tsugumi: You should be able to get on right away if you go now.
Himari: Ah, look at that! A parade is starting~!
Tomoe: Oh~, nice~. Why don't we wait until the parade is over to go to the coffee cups ride~?
Moca: The costumes for the park cast are no joke! There are mummies and Frankenstein's monsters and skeletons... They look like real monsters.
Himari: There's a marching band of black cats! Aww~! They're so cute~!
Ran: That's amazing. The park cast is playing instruments and dancing in cat costumes. I wonder how much they can really see.
Tsugumi: Ah, Moca-chan, there's a jack-o-lantern giving out candy.
Tomoe: Oh wow~, you're right~. Ah, it went over that way there~.
Ran: Ah, Moca, there's a spider-looking guy coming this way.
Tomoe: Oh wow~, that's amazing! They're floating~! I wonder how they stay in the air~!
Himari: Look at that, Tomoe! That black cat on the snare drum is in the zone!
Tomoe: No kidding! There's just something about the drums~! Sigh~, this has got me wanting to pound out some beats~!
Ran, Tsugumi, Himari & Moca: Huh?
Tomoe & Moca: Ah.
Ran, Tsugumi & Himari: ... Huh? Huh? What?
Tomoe: Uhh, that, uh... wasn't what I wanted to say. I didn't mean drums. I, uh...
Moca: You meant to say guitar, right? It was just a slip of the tongue. Yeah, Tomo-chin?
Tomoe: Moca!
Moca: Ah, uh-oh~.
Himari: What's going on?! I-is Moca back to being Moca?! ... Wait, that's how it should be, right? Right?!
Tsugumi: Umm... From the looks of it, it's not like you two have gone back to normal, but...
Ran: I knew it. You two never switched bodies, did you?
Tomoe & Moca: Gulp!
Tomoe & Moca: Ugh...
Himari: Huh? Wait a minute. I don't understand what's going on~! What's happening?
Tomoe: Ugh...! W-well, you see~...
Moca: Welp~, I guess people can't hide their true nature in front of music~. The jig is up.
Tomoe: Now's not the time for that! Wh-what are we going to do, Moca?!
Moca: I call for a retreat~! Tomo-chin, to the Ferris wheel!
Tomoe: O-okay!
Ran: Ah, they ran away. After them!
(White cut)
