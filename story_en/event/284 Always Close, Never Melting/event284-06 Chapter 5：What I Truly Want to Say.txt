While others handled different tasks, Sayo stepped in to help Lisa with the dishes.

Sayo: Ah, sorry. I didn’t mean anything by it.
Lisa: No, it’s fine. It’s true I was struggling back then.

Sayo: No, it was the least I could do...
Sayo: But that’s exactly why I think it’s amazing how you managed to get back up from all of that.

Sayo: Um, even though you were the one going through a hard time, your first instinct was to thank everyone else.
Lisa: That’s because... I really did get help.
Lisa: From you guys, of course, but also from friends, and people I started working with after going pro.
Lisa: I think I’ve come to feel even more strongly that who I am now is because of all the people who’ve supported me.
Sayo: ...I think I understand how you feel.
Sayo: (But while Imai-san expresses her gratitude so openly... have I really managed to do that myself?)
