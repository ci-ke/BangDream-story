Yukina: Sayo, is this what it’s supposed to look like when the butter turns pale?
Sayo: Yes, that’s just right. Now, let’s add the eggs in a few separate batches.
Yukina: ...Is there a reason we can’t add them all at once?
Sayo: Eggs have a lot of moisture, so if you dump them in all at once, they won’t blend well and can separate from the butter.
Yukina: I see... so there’s a reason for each step.
Sayo: Exactly. It might feel like a hassle, but taking care with each process is important.
Sayo: Managing the temperature and amounts precisely is what makes baking tricky, but it also means it always pays off when you get it right.
