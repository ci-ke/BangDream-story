(Character: Yukina Minato, Sayo Hikawa, Lisa Imai, Ako Udagawa, Rinko Shirokane)

[After the Live Show Meeting]

[Music Agency - Staff Room]

Ako: Harumi-san looked super moved! I was a bit surprised when she said, “I’ll treasure this forever!”
