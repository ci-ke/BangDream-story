event287-07 Ending: Beyond the Unseen Light

Arisa’s short-term job ends without a hitch. The Poppin’Party girls gather for a “great work” party!

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Tae Hanazono, Rimi Ushigome, Saya Yamabuki, Arisa Ichigaya)

[Ichigaya Residence - Basement]

Kasumi: Arisa, great job on finishing your part-time job~~~!
Saya, Rimi & Tae: Good job~~!
Arisa: Come on, throwing a party over something like this... isn’t that a bit much?
Kasumi: Eh~~! But Arisa, your part-time job ended without a hitch! We have to celebrate!
Arisa: Even you sound like some kind of parent... Well, thanks, though.
Saya: I really wanted to see you working, Arisa~. Rimi-rin, you went, didn’t you?
Rimi: Yeah, just for a bit before cram school. Arisa-chan was super sharp and cool.
Arisa: T-that’s just normal for me, seriously.
Tae: Our Arisa’s a real capable one.
Kasumi: Hey hey, O-Tae. What was Arisa like!?
Arisa: Ah jeez~! Don’t you dare tell her, got it!?
Tae: Yeah. That side of Arisa is something only those who’ve overcome hardships together get to see.
Arisa: That’s not quite it... but sure, let’s go with that!
Kasumi: Ehh~!? Arisa, you’re such a spoilsport~! Come on, just tell me.
Rimi: I-I kinda want to know too...
Saya: Should’ve gone to see for myself, I guess.
Arisa: You too, Rimi and Saya...? Gimme a break already~.
Tae: Come to think of it, Arisa, the boss asked if you’d keep working there, but you turned it down, right?
Arisa: Hm? Yeah... I really appreciated the offer, but... right now I want to take another look at what I really want to do.
Kasumi: As expected of Arisa! Getting invited by the store like that! ...So? What is it you want to do?
Arisa: What I want to do, huh...
Arisa: ...
Arisa: I guess... I want to be like you guys. Able to throw myself into something completely. I’m still not sure what that “something” is, though...
Kasumi: Arisa...! I’m totally cheering for you! I just know this will lead you to your own kind of sparkling, heart-pounding!
Tae: I’m cheering you on too!
Arisa: Cut it out already! Well... but thanks.
Arisa: A-all right, let’s start practice already!
Rimi: Fufu, it’s been a while since we all practiced together, hasn’t it? I was really looking forward to this.
Saya: We’ve all been pretty busy lately. Wanna run through the setlist from our last live to start?
Kasumi: Sounds good, let’s do it!
Saya: Okay, then let’s clean up the snacks and drinks first... Time to get ready~.
Kasumi, Rimi & Tae: Yes~!
Arisa: (...My own sparkling, heart-pounding, huh...)
Tae: Arisa, practice time~!
Arisa: Yeah.
(Black cut)
Kasumi: Mmm~! It really feels great when all of us play together! I practiced at home too, but... what do you call this feeling again... Ah! It tightens up my body!
Arisa: You mean it’s supposed to tighten your mind, not your body. Geez... way to ruin the moment right when we’re getting started...
Saya: Well, well.
Rimi: W-well, it does sound kinda similar...!
Tae: Off by just one word—so close.
Kasumi: Ugh... Thanks, Rimi-rin, O-Tae...
Kasumi: Alright then, let’s do this for real—here we go~!
(White cut)
(Background change)
