event287-04 Chapter 3: A Visitor Who Lights the Heart

Arisa ends up working alone while Tae is away. Though uneasy, she powers through her tasks.

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Tae Hanazono, Rimi Ushigome, Saya Yamabuki, Arisa Ichigaya)

[Music Store]

Arisa: O-Tae~. On the way home today, there’s somewhere I want to stop by... Ah...
Arisa: (So she was talking with the boss...)
Tae: ...Sorry about that. I’ll be counting on you.
Manager: Don’t worry about us. Good luck!
Tae: ...Huh? Arisa, did you need to see the boss too?
Arisa: Nah, I came to talk to you, O-Tae... Looked like you were getting cheered on, though?
Tae: I was just asking the boss about tomorrow’s shift.
Arisa: Asking? Did something happen?
Tae: Yeah. I’ve got a studio musician gig tomorrow, so I asked if I could take the day off from the shop.
Arisa: I see. You mentioned someone reached out to you for that before.
Tae: I’d actually been talking it over with the boss for a while, and I just checked if it’d be okay for me to step out since things are busy right now. Sorry though, especially since you’re covering the shift.
Arisa: Nah, don’t worry about me.
Arisa: (...So O-Tae won’t be around tomorrow...)
Tae: You don’t have to look so anxious... There’ll still be other staff around.
Arisa: Y-Yeah...! I’ll handle things here, so you focus on your studio gig!
Tae: Yeah, thanks! Alright, let’s get changed and head home.
Arisa: (...Right. O-Tae’s got her studio musician work too...)

[Music Store]

Arisa: I brought the restock items from the back.
Staff: Thanks, Ichigaya-san. You’re fast—really helps~.
Arisa: No, no, it’s nothing... Ah, I’ll go restock the shelves.
Staff: Alright, I’ll leave it to you. I’m heading to help at the register.
Arisa: Okay, let’s do this. Well... this goes over here, and the limited-time items are... Phew~... Stocking shelves is harder than it looks...
Arisa: (I see... O-Tae usually noticed and offered to help around this time... She must’ve been quietly looking out for me in her own way...)
Arisa: (I took this job to gain experience for the future, but it had kind of become normal to work with O-Tae...)
???: Oh, there you are, Arisa-chan.
Arisa: ...Huh? Rimi!?
Rimi: Ehehe... I had some free time, so I came by. I heard from O-Tae-chan that you’d be working alone today.
Rimi: How’s the job going? Getting used to it?
Arisa: ...Yeah, I think I’m getting the hang of it. I was really clumsy at first, even just talking to customers felt hard, and talking to staff besides O-Tae took a lot of courage.
Arisa: But now I can start conversations myself and handle things a lot smoother...
Rimi: That’s amazing, Arisa-chan!
Arisa: Nah, it’s all thanks to O-Tae. She set things up to make it easier for me.
Arisa: She’s really observant. When someone’s in trouble, she always reaches out to help. She’s helped me tons of times.
Arisa: It’s like... I’ve seen a different side of her than when we’re in Poppin'Party. It’s kind of refreshing... Ah! But don’t tell her I said that, okay!?
Rimi: Yeah, got it.
Arisa: What about you though, Rimi? Isn’t cram school test prep super hard?
Rimi: Yeah, there’s still a lot I need to study and get done... But I’ve made up my mind to study abroad.
Arisa: ...I see. Then good luck, Rimi!
Rimi: Yeah! Thanks, Arisa-chan. ...Oh, I should get going.
Arisa: Thanks for dropping by, even though you’re busy.
Rimi: No worries. Let’s both keep doing our best!
Arisa: Yeah, you too, Rimi.
Arisa: ...
Arisa: ...Everyone else has already chosen their paths, but I still haven’t picked anything...
Arisa: (It’s not like I seriously want a job related to instruments... So what do I want for my future...?)
