event300-04 Chapter 3: An Unexpected Pitfall!?

The start goes smoothly, but unlucky twists gradually put Pastel＊Palettes on the back foot...

(Character: Aya Maruyama, Hina Hikawa, Chisato Shirasagi, Maya Yamato, Eve Wakamiya)

Host: Next up is the segment “Link It☆Five Star!” Each of the five members will be responsible for one character, and together you’ll take on quizzes where the answers are five letters long!
Host: Pastel＊Palettes did amazing in the fast-answer round—how confident are you all when it comes to teamwork?
Aya: Of course! Our hearts are always one!
(Black cut)
Host: Too bad, the answer was “Hyacinth”! That’s three wrong answers in a row~!
Aya: What~!?
Host: Looks like the guest team’s hearts are united... but their answers sure aren’t~.
Eve: Even though Chisato-san and Maya-san have been right the whole time... I’m so sorry!
Hina: Hmm, that question didn’t really make me boppin'♪ this time~.
Chisato: It seems like Hina-chan and the others are a bit inconsistent with their answers...
Maya: Ahaha... Let’s take a deep breath and focus, everyone.
Host: Alright, time for the final question for the guest team!
Host: “This variety of chili pepper is known for being praised by Hiraga Gennai, a physician of the Edo period, in his own writings, where he stated that it should be considered the finest for consumption. What is this variety called?”
Aya: H-Hiraga Gennai praised a chili pepper...!?
Maya: (A five-letter variety of chili, something traditional in Japan... Aya-san, don’t get thrown off by the wording!)
Eve: This is... what I think it is, right?
Host: No sharing hints with each other, okay~? All right, has everyone finished writing?
Aya: Ugh, I’m not confident... but please let it be right!
Host: Then let’s reveal the answers one by one!
Maya: “Ta!”
Chisato: “Ka!”
Hina: “No!”
Eve: “Tsu!”
Aya: “No!”
Host: Too bad! Hawks don’t have horns~.
Hina: Ahaha! It’s claws, not horns, Aya-chan.
Aya: Ahh~! That’s it!
Aya: I totally knew it, but all I could think of was “something spicy”...
Host: The guest team had a great start, but the gap between them and second place has really closed in now~.
Aya: Ugh, sorry everyone!
Chisato: It’s fine. We can make up for it in the next round.
(Black cut)
Host: We’re entering the second half of the show! In the “Draw It☆Illustration Star!” segment, you’ll convey quiz answers through your drawings!
Eve: So that means even if we know the answer, we’ll have to be able to draw it too!
Host: Exactly! Now then, I’ll draw a slip to decide who will be in charge of the illustrations for the guest team!
Host: Here we go! The illustrator will be... Chisato Shirasagi!
Chisato: If I mess up here, we could get overtaken... This is a big responsibility.
Hina: Hmm, Chisato-chan’s drawings are kinda unique, so this might be tough.
Chisato: What do you mean by that, Hina-chan?
Chisato: Even if my drawing skills fall short, I’m good at summarizing key points. So don’t worry.
Host: All right then, Shirasagi-san, please take a look at this question and draw your answer on the flip board!
Chisato: Understood.
Maya: Was that a setup just now...?
Aya: I think she’s totally serious... She never slacks off.
Eve: I hope we can read her intentions well...
Chisato: I’m done.
Host: Okay, now show the illustration to your teammates!
Chisato: Here’s my answer!
Aya, Hina, Maya & Eve: This is...!?
Aya: ......Um, what?
Host: Even knowing the answer, I’m kinda... stumped...
Eve: It looks like food... or maybe an animal...?
Maya: Is this... a concept or something...?
Hina: Ahaha, I have no clue~.
Chisato: Come on, everyone, it’s not something you need to agonize over this much! Look—especially right here...
Host: Oops, no hints allowed~. But it looks like you’re having a bit of trouble, so... this time, we’ll count it as correct if even one of the four of you gets it right!
Chisato: I’m counting on you all...!
Aya: Hmm...
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Host: Okay, time’s up! Reveal your answers all at once!
Aya: “Cat!”
Eve: “Omelet rice!”
Maya: “Slime!”
Hina: “Galaxy!”
Host: Wow, all completely different! Let’s read the full question now~.
Host: “This sea creature lives mainly in shallow ocean floors, often brightly colored, and is named for the horns on its head resembling those of a cow. What is it?”
Chisato: The answer is “Sea Slug”!
Aya, Hina, Maya & Eve: That was supposed to be a sea slug!?
Aya: Ah! But I did kinda get that it was some kind of living creature...
Maya: Sea slugs come in so many forms—it’s hard to capture in a drawing!
Eve: Y-yes, I thought the same!
Host: Ahaha! Maybe you should stop with the comforting around there, don’t you think?
Hina: Well, at least it made for a good moment!
Chisato: Y-yeah... Though we’ve lost the lead now...
Maya: The next segment is a written quiz, so let’s work together again and aim for a comeback!
