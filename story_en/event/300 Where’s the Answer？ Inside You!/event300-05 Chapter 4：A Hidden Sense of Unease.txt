During the latter half, as they struggle to make a comeback, Maya tries to derive her own answers, but...

(Character: Aya Maruyama, Hina Hikawa, Chisato Shirasagi, Maya Yamato, Eve Wakamiya)

[During Recording]

Aya: Our answer is also “Tapioca”!
Host: Correct! The guest team is making a comeback~!
Maya: Aya-san’s comment just now ended up being a huge hint.
Eve: If we unite our strengths, there’s no quiz we can’t solve!
Host: Alrighty, will those words come back to haunt you? Here’s the last question of this segment!
Host: Arrange the following World Cultural Heritage sites in order from the most recently completed! “Nijo Castle,” “The National Museum of Western Art,” “The Taj Mahal,” “The Sydney Opera House”!
Aya: Eh!? “Most recently completed”? That’s tough...
Chisato: As expected from the final question of the round—it’s a tricky one.
Hina: Hey, hey, Maya-chan, there’s at least one we definitely know, right?
Maya: Yes, I was thinking the same! When we went on our school trip, I learned that the Opera House was the most recent among World Heritage Sites.
Aya: Whoa, you two are amazing~!
Chisato: So that would make the National Museum of Western Art—also modern architecture—the next newest.
Eve: That leaves the Taj Mahal and Nijo Castle... I do know Nijo Castle was built in 1603...
Aya: Wow, impressive. But yeah, we won’t know unless we figure out whether the Taj Mahal is older or newer than that.
Chisato: To be honest... I have no clue.
Maya: (If I’m remembering correctly from when I studied world history for exams... it should be newer than 1603...)
Aya: But like, if you just look at photos, doesn’t the Taj Mahal feel older?
Hina: Hmm... yeah, kinda, but also not really?
Eve: Still, intuition like that can be important!
Chisato: What about you, Maya-chan? Any thoughts?
Maya: I think...
Maya: No, it’s okay! Let’s go with what Aya-san said—let’s assume the Taj Mahal is the oldest.
Chisato: Are you sure? If you're fine with it...
Host: All right, it looks like all teams have submitted their answers, so let’s reveal them!
Host: From newest to oldest, the correct order is “The Opera House,” “The National Museum of Western Art”... then, “The Taj Mahal” and “Nijo Castle”!
Aya: Ahh~!
Host: You had a solid line of reasoning going, but missed the last 50-50~.
Aya: I really wanted to show what we can do~!
Host: Ahaha, that’s some refreshingly honest frustration. With that kind of spirit, the match is still anyone’s game!
