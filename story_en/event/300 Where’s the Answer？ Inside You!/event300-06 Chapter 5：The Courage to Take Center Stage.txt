event300-06 Chapter 5: The Courage to Take Center Stage

The final challenge is a gesture quiz. And the member chosen to represent Pastel＊Palettes is—

(Character: Kaoru Seta, Aya Maruyama, Hina Hikawa, Chisato Shirasagi, Maya Yamato, Eve Wakamiya)

Host: And now for the final segment you’ve all been waiting for! “Express It! Gesture Star☆”!
Host: The rules are simple! One team member is chosen as the representative, and they’ll use gestures to communicate quiz answers to the rest of the team!
Eve: Ah, so it’s like the illustration segment earlier, but with gestures instead!
Chisato: Exactly. But this time, you’ll need to express multiple answers back-to-back, so the pressure’s much higher on the rep.
Host: So, who will the guest team choose as their representative?
Hina: Wanna do rock-paper-scissors to decide?
Aya: Actually, since Maya-chan’s gotten the most correct answers so far, wouldn’t she be best?
Maya: M-me!?
Eve: That’s true! And we still have a chance at a comeback too!
Maya: (But wouldn’t it make the show more exciting if Aya-san, who’s been carrying the mood all along, did the gestures instead...?)
(White cut)
Chisato: But you know... that’s exactly why it would be perfectly fine for you to take center stage sometimes, Maya-chan.
(White cut)
Maya: I...
Aya: It might be tough, but I’ll do my best to figure out what you’re trying to act out! So don’t worry!
Maya: Aya-san...!
Maya: (That's right... everyone isn’t stepping back or pushing forward because of their roles.)
Maya: (They’re just throwing themselves in, doing everything they can in the moment. So if that’s the case—)
Maya: I’ll do it! Please let me!
Chisato: Maya-chan...!
(Black cut)
Host: The current leader, the actor team, has seven correct answers and 360 points!
Host: Can the guest team pull off a comeback!? Let’s welcome their representative, Maya Yamato!
Maya: Yes!
Aya: You’ve got this, Maya-chan~!
Maya: (...In such a gorgeous studio, surrounded by famous guests, with all eyes on me...)
Maya: (And this scene is going to be broadcast nationwide, right? I can’t believe I’m the one standing here as Pastel＊Palettes’ representative.)
Maya: (Am I nervous...? ...Haha, of course I am. My heart’s pounding so loud it’s almost distracting.)
Maya: (But right now, I’m the star of this stage!)
Host: Alright, gesture time—start!!
Maya: (“What sea animal is written with the kanji for ‘sea’ and ‘pig’?” The answer is—)
Aya: She's wiggling?
Hina: Or is she swimming?
Chisato: A marine creature... ah, she jumped!
Eve: A dolphin! It’s a dolphin!
Maya: (“What food gets its name from the French word for crescent moon, based on its shape?” That’s gotta be—)
Chisato: Hmm, it’s about palm-sized...
Aya: She’s eating it! It’s food!
Hina: With your hands? Looks like bread?
Eve: The shape seems important! It’s curved...
Aya: I got it! A croissant!
Maya: (As expected, everyone’s so quick on the uptake! If we can keep this pace until the end!)
Host: All right! That puts you just 10 points behind the top team! If you get this final super-hard question right, the guest team snatches a come-from-behind victory!
Maya: (Last question...“Set against the feud between the Capulets and Montagues, what work is titled after two lovers who pursue a forbidden love?”)
Maya: (This is my lucky break! The answer is “Romeo and Juliet”—so I’ll just—)
Maya: (Wait, what!? How am I supposed to gesture that...!?)
Eve: She seems uncertain...
Aya: Maybe she doesn’t know the answer?
Chisato: No, I think she knows—it’s just too hard to express through gestures.
Hina: Still, doesn’t it look like she’s acting out two people? Like a conversation or something...
Eve: Is this a scene from a play? But which...?
Maya: (Ugh, the key part isn’t coming through...! I need to go all in—make it real—)
(White cut)
(Background change)
Juliet (Chisato): “O Romeo, Romeo! Wherefore art thou Romeo?”
Romeo (Kaoru): “Juliet! Over here!”
(White cut)
(Background change)
Maya: (That’s it! I already had the perfect example shown to me back then!)
Maya: (Back then, I was the one shining the light on others... but now, I’m the one—!)
Maya: O Romeo...!
Aya, Hina, Chisato & Eve: Ah...
Maya: ...Ah.
Maya: Sorry! I spoke!
