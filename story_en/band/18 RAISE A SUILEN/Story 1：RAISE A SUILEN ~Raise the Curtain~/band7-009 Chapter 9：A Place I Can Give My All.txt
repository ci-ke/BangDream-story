band7-009 Chapter 9: A Place I Can Give My All

Rokka becomes a temporary guitarist for RAS. She practices with her new guitar.

(Character: Rei Wakana, Rokka Asahi, Masuki Sato, Reona Nyubara, Chiyu Tamade)

[Several Days Later]

[CHU²'s Penthouse - Studio]

CHU² (Chiyu): What's more, here. I got you a new guitar. Use this.

LOCK (Rokka): Huh?! Wait, does that include all the different parts? The guitar, bass, drums, keyboard, and the DJ too... You really can do anything.
CHU² (Chiyu): ...

LOCK (Rokka): Yes...!
(Black cut)
CHU² (Chiyu): No! Stop. Do it again.
LOCK (Rokka): ...?!
LOCK (Rokka): (If I want to become a true member of the band, I need to do my best here...)
