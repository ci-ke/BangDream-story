band7-033 Chapter 8: It’s Not a RAS Song

When the girls express their confusion over the song, a fissure begins to grow.

(Character: Rei Wakana, Rokka Asahi, Masuki Sato, Reona Nyubara, Chiyu Tamade)

[A Few Days Later]

[CHU²'s Penthouse - Studio]

CHU² (Chiyu): Get it together, people! You're all out of sync!
CHU² (Chiyu): LOCK! Stop going at it like you're just playing solo! Pay more attention to everyone else's music!
LOCK (Rokka): I-I'm sorry!
CHU² (Chiyu): And PAREO! What's with the hesitation? Are you trying to enhance the music, or are you trying to take the lead?! Make up your mind!
PAREO (Reona): M-my apologies, master...
CHU² (Chiyu): And MASKING! You-
MASKING (Masuki): I just don't get it, alright?
CHU² (Chiyu): Huh?
MASKING (Masuki): Like that phrase just now. What damn emotion am I supposed to be puttin' in it?! I don't know what I should be feelin' when I play that part!
CHU² (Chiyu): ...
MASKING (Masuki): We've been at it for a few days, and I still can't figure the song out... I'm not gettin' any sorta image...
MASKING (Masuki): Probably my lack of understandin', but the whole thing keeps stayin' out of my damn reach!
CHU² (Chiyu): W-well...
CHU² (Chiyu): A-alright... Then what don't you like about it? Just come out and say it if you have any problems.
CHU² (Chiyu): I'm still working on it, so I might even consider your opinion if it has any value.
MASKING (Masuki): It's not that I don't like it...
LAYER (Rei): ...
PAREO (Reona): N-now, now... We just started practicing♪ I am sure it will get better!
LOCK (Rokka): Yes! I should try harder too~!
LAYER (Rei): Hey, CHU²... Do you mind?
CHU² (Chiyu): What is it?
LAYER (Rei): This song... I've had this feeling for a while that there's something off...
CHU² (Chiyu): Something off...? What's that supposed to mean?!
LAYER (Rei): Just like Masuki, I've been having trouble getting a feel for the song.
LAYER (Rei): I thought maybe the feeling would go away once I got to know it, but the feeling's only gotten stronger.
LAYER (Rei): And I think I've finally figured out why.
CHU² (Chiyu): What's your point?! If you have something to say, then say it!
LAYER (Rei): Okay, I will.
LAYER (Rei): This song has all the signature elements of our music, and it's a good song.
LAYER (Rei): But...
CHU² (Chiyu): ...
LAYER (Rei): It doesn't surpass my expectations.
CHU² (Chiyu): ... What did you just say?
LAYER (Rei): Every piece of music you've ever written has always managed to amaze me. There's always been a sense of wonder over how you get your ideas.
LAYER (Rei): ... But it's not just about the ideas. The passion you put into your music has always made my blood surge with excitement.
LAYER (Rei): None of that's happening with this song though. My body isn't reacting.
LAYER (Rei): It's not a bad song by any means. And if I had to put it into words, I'd say it's a good song that delivers exactly what I would expect.
CHU² (Chiyu): ...
LAYER (Rei): You know it too, don't you, CHU²? That's why you're asking for our opinions more than you usually do.
MASKING (Masuki): Huh... Now that I think about it, you pretty much never ask us what we think about your songs.
LAYER (Rei): CHU²... The time I've spent with RAS has been engraved into my body, and it's my body that's telling me this.
LAYER (Rei): This song...
LAYER (Rei): It's not a RAS song.
CHU² (Chiyu): ...
PAREO (Reona): U-um...
CHU² (Chiyu): Get out...
PAREO (Reona): CHU²-sama...
CHU² (Chiyu): Get out of here right now!! All of you!!
(Black cut)
