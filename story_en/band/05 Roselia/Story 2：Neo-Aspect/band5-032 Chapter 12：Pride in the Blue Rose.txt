band5-032 Chapter 12: Pride in the Blue Rose

As Ako and Rinko continue making Roselia's new costumes, they get some visitors. Who could it be?

(Character: Sayo Hikawa, Lisa Imai, Ako Udagawa, Rinko Shirokane)

[Shirokane Residence - Rinko's Room]

Rinko: The outfits...
Ako: Are done!!
Rinko: We did it, Ako-chan... Thank you for all your help...
Ako: I should be thanking you! While I was making these, it made me realize again just how much I love Roselia!
Ako: I can't wait for everyone to put these on!
Rinko: Mhm... I'd like... to see them... try them out too...
Ako: Hm...? Is that someone at the door?
Rinko: I'm sure... it's just a delivery or something... I don't think it's for me...
Lisa & Sayo: Heyya~! Hello.
Rinko: Huh...?!
Ako: Lisa-nee!! Sayo-san!!
Lisa: Sorry to drop in on you like this. I just didn't think we'd be able to get a hold of you otherwise.
Sayo: We heard from Tomoe-san that you two have been making outfits here recently.
Ako: O-oh, I see...
Rinko: Uhm... W-we-...!
Lisa: We know why you two ran out during rehearsal the way you did.
Sayo: Which is precisely why we would like your help.
Ako: What are you guys talking about?
(Black cut)
Rinko: We were considerate... of the band...?
Sayo: Yep. You two were the only ones who were looking out for Roselia this entire time.
Lisa: We think that in order to bring Roselia back, we all need to become more mindful of the group once again...
Lisa: It's actually really embarrassing to admit this now since I was supposed to be a member of the entire band...
Rinko: W-we've been thinking too... this whole time... about how to get Roselia back...
Sayo: Oh, really?
Rinko: Yes... We want to continue being together... That's why Ako-chan and I... have been here making outfits...
Ako: While we were doing that, we just kept thinking of you guys! And that was a lot of fun... It made me wanna be a part of Roselia even more...
Ako: I know I'm not supposed to think of it as being fun... That I'm supposed to take our music seriously...
Ako: But I just love Roselia too much...!
Sayo: I think that sentiment will be very valuable to the group.
Sayo: It means that when you think of us, it fills you with pride.
Ako: Y-yeah! I think so too! Roselia makes me feel proud!
Rinko: I-I feel the same as Ako-chan... The band is special to me...!
Lisa: Ako, Rinko, thank you for helping us realize something so important.
Rinko: D-don't thank us... We didn't do anything... Besides...
Rinko: I think the reason you two wanted Roselia back... is that... you love it...
Lisa: Hmm... Yeah, I think so. And I'm sure we didn't realize it before because we weren't paying attention to the band as a whole.
Lisa: I'll have to do better.
Sayo: Certainly... I have some reflecting to do as well.
Lisa: Say... Do you all think Yukina loves Roselia too?
Ako: There's no question about it...! I don't know why... but I'm sure she does!
Ako: I know she's really strict, but she's only like that because she believes in us, right?
Ako: It's because she believes that Roselia has what it takes to be better, isn't it?
Rinko: Can we all... go talk to Yukina-san... one more time...?
Rinko: We'll tell her... that we all... take pride in being Roselia...
