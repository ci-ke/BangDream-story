band5-030 Chapter 10: Giving Precious Feelings Form

Unable to bring herself to attend band practice, Ako asks Tomoe for advice...

(Character: Tomoe Udagawa, Ako Udagawa, Rinko Shirokane)

[Udagawa Residence]

Ako: Sigh...
Tomoe: Ako, what's been going on with band rehearsals?
Ako: Ah, Sis... Actually...
(Black cut)
Tomoe: Oh, okay~. And so it's been hard to go, huh?
Ako: Mhm... I know what I'm doing isn't right, but still...
Tomoe: Ako, do you like Roselia?
Ako: Huh...? Y-yeah, I love Roselia!
Tomoe: And the members?
Ako: Yes, of course! Why do you ask?
Tomoe: Hmm~? I was just making sure. The other girls of Afterglow and I love the band and each other so much, that we've had times where things didn't go so well because of it.
Tomoe: Is it possible that Roselia's going through something like that right now?
Ako: You mean... everyone loving the band?
Tomoe: Yeah. And it could be tripping you guys up. For times like this, I bet if you all just scream, "I love Roselia," your problem would be solved.
Ako: Ugh~, what are you talking about~?!
Tomoe: Ahaha, sorry! I'm just kidding! Half kidding, actually. If you all tell each other how precious the band is to you, and that you feel like it's the place you belong...
Tomoe: I bet it'll naturally turn back to the way it was before.
Ako: Are you sure about that...?
Tomoe: I'm telling you...! Oh, I know! If you don't think you'll be able to put your feelings into words, then~... why don't you try putting them into something else?
Tomoe: Like with Ran, she's bad at telling others how she feels, so she'll communicate that through her songs and poetry. So you could do that kind of thing.
Ako: I don't know if I could write something like that~...
Tomoe: Let's see~... Ah, what if you and Rinko-san make outfits together?
Ako: Oh, outfits...!
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Shirokane Residence - Rinko's Room]

Rinko: (Even without going to rehearsal... I keep thinking about Roselia...)
Rinko: (Ah... These threads are coming loose... I'll have to fix it...)
Rinko: ...
Rinko: (This outfit is special to me... It allows us to be Roselia... I wish I could wear it again...)
Rinko: Ah...!
Rinko: A message...? It's from Ako-chan...
Ako: "Rin-rin! Let's bring Roselia back!"
Rinko: Ah... She must mean...
Rinko: "Ako-chan... I was just thinking the same thing as you."
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Rinko: "... In that case... what do you think of this theme...?"
Ako: "Hmm~, I think that might be too cute... Can't we come up with something that's more Lisa-nee's style, something cooler?!"
Rinko: "Fufu..."
Ako: "What happened, Rin-rin?"
Rinko: "I was just thinking... You've been calling the others 'cool' since the very beginning..."
Ako: "Why wouldn't I~?! Every single member is super cool! That's why I want this outfit to be something that can show that off!"
Rinko: "Yeah... I agree..."
Rinko: "I love sitting here... and thinking of the others. It really warms my heart..."
Ako: "Mhm, I do too!"
Rinko: "Ako-chan... Let's bring Roselia back... Together...!"
Ako: "Yeah!"
