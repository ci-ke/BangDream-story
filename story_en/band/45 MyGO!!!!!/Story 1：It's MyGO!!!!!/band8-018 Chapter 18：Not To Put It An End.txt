band8-018 Chapter 18: Not To Put It An End

Soyo and Taki talked to each other on the fire escape of RiNG. Taki confided all her feelings…

(Character: Tomori Takamatsu, Anon Chihaya, Soyo Nagasaki, Taki Shina)

[RiNG Emergency Stairs]

Soyo: Tomori-chan must've had trauma from what happened to CRYCHIC...
Taki: ...
Soyo: She doesn't want to play live... She said the band would be over if we played live. She's been carrying that trauma with her ever since that time.
Taki: I know... Tomori is hurting...
Taki: But even then, I still...
Soyo: I think we should stop playing live...
Taki: ...!
Soyo: We shouldn't play live shows ever again, just playing in the studio only with the band...
Soyo: I'm sure that way, she'll feel more at ease.
Taki: ... I want to play live.
Taki: When I first heard Tomori's song, I thought it was about me... The feelings I can't express, she turns them all into song...
Taki: Even though I'm like this, it's okay for me to be here, to live.
Soyo: Taki-chan......
Taki: That's why I... I was so happy to hear that we're going to play in a band again... I thought it was the same for Tomori too, but...
Soyo: ... Hehe, why don't you tell that to Tomori-chan?
Taki: It's just my selfish wish.
Soyo: ... Why is it hardest for people to say what's important? That's why CRYCHIC disbanded.
Soyo: But we don't want the same thing to happen again, right?
Taki: ...
Soyo: Why don't you try to convey your feelings properly to Tomori-chan?
Soyo: Just like how her song reached your heart, your feelings will reach her too... I'm sure she'll understand.
Taki: I...
Taki: I don't want Tomori to ever feel lonely again.
Soyo: Yeah... I knew Taki-chan would say so.
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[RiNG Cafeteria]

Tomori: ...
Anon: So, what do you want to talk about...?
Soyo: I wanted to talk to you today about what we're going to do.
Soyo: Right now, we're kind of a divided group. Is there any point in being in a band?
Anon & Tomori: ...!
Soyo: When we decided to be in a band again, we said we'd talk it out so we wouldn't have misunderstandings again.
Soyo: But it's already happening again... If we keep going like this, we'll break up again...
Taki: ... I think we should perform live.
Tomori: !
Taki: For us to move forward as a divided group, I think we need a goal.
Taki: We can't practice because we've got nothing to aim for, and some of us here aren't even trying...
Taki: I want everyone to listen to Tomori's songs. That's why...
Taki: I want to perform live so that we won't come to an end.
Tomori: ...!
Anon: No..... Tomori-chan said she doesn't want to do a live show!
Anon: I mean, even if we're going to perform live, isn't this time too sudden? Let's prepare properly and do a live show in perfect condition!
Tomori: Anon-chan...
Anon: If we do that, Tomori-chan will be more prepared too...
Taki: ... What is it with you?
Anon: ... Huh?
Taki: You've been pretending to be on Tomori's side, but you just don't want to perform live.
Taki: What's bothering Tomori is, if we play live, it might be the end of the band.
Anon: That's... because --!
Taki: You're just running away, aren't you?
Anon: ...!
Taki: You're running away from your guitar.
Anon: Um... I'm not running away from...
Taki: You can't play guitar, can you? I doubt you're even practicing properly.
Anon: I- I'm practicing!
Taki: You only show any motivation in things like your position, the band name, G, F, and so on. You're the one who's absolutely running away if we don't decide to perform live.
Taki: That's what you've been doing all along --
Anon: !! I'm not running away!
Tomori: ! Anon-chan...!
Taki: Oh...! Tomori...!?
Soyo & Taki: ...
Soyo: ... Someone had to say it at some point. Taki-chan, you're not wrong.
Taki: ...
(Black cut)
(Background change)
