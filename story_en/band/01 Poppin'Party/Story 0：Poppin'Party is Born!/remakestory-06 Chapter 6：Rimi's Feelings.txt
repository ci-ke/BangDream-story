remakestory-06 Chapter 6: Rimi's Feelings

Rimi says she can't be part of a band after all. What will Kasumi do after being turned down?

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Rimi Ushigome, Saya Yamabuki, Arisa Ichigaya)

[Hanasakigawa Girls' Academy - School Gate]

Kasumi: Good morning!
Student Council Member: ...
Arisa: Cut that out. What are you doing playing your guitar at school anyway?!
Kasumi: I can't help it! I'm just so happy!
Student Council Member: Personal belongings will be confiscated. Hand it over.
Kasumi: What~?! I can't hold on to my guitar?!
Student Council Member: We can't have you playing it during school hours. You can come retrieve it from the student council room after classes are over.
Kasumi: Aw man~.
Rimi: ...Ah, Kasumi-chan.
Kasumi: Rimi-rin~! They took my guitar~!
Rimi: I-I see. Uhm...
Kasumi: Ah, about practice! We should have it at Arisa's house after school!
Arisa: Don't just go inviting people to my place!
Rimi: Ngh... I'm... I'm sorry!
Kasumi: For what?
Rimi: I can't join the band after all...!
Kasumi: Huh...? Why not? Did your parents tell you no?
Rimi: No!
Kasumi: Did someone threaten you?
Rimi: Nuh-uh!
Kasumi: So... It has to do with me...?
Rimi: N-no... I'm sorry...!
Kasumi: Ah, Rimi-rin!
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Recess]

[Hanasakigawa Girls' Academy - Courtyard]

Saya: Guess Ushigome-san's not coming.
Kasumi: Hmm~, I wonder what happened to her.
Saya: ... Huh? Ichigaya-san?
Arisa: Sorry to intrude. We have an agreement.
Kasumi: Arisa said she wants to have lunch together.
Arisa: No I didn't.
Kasumi: You did.
Arisa: That's not how I said it.
Saya: Interesting. When'd you guys become such good friends?
Arisa: We didn't.
Kasumi: What are we supposed to do, Arisa?! Why can't Rimi-rin be part of the band~?!
Arisa: I don't know.
Kasumi: At least take moment to think about it~!
Arisa: Just ask her!
Kasumi: But all she said was she's sorry~.
Arisa: Well, there's your answer.
Kasumi: Arisa~!!
Arisa: Shut up, shut up, shut up!
Kasumi: What if I give you my egg?!
Arisa: ...! Is this that thing where you trade side dishes?
Saya: Ahaha, you're funny, Ichigaya-san.
Arisa: I-I was just-...
Kasumi: I'll trade you too, if you want.
Arisa: No thanks!
Saya: Ahaha, I'm sorry. This is just too cute.
Arisa: No it's not!
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[After School]

Rimi: ...
Tourist: Excuse me.
Rimi: Huh?
Tourist: Could you help me with some directions...?
Rimi: Ahh, what do I do?! They clearly need my help, but-
Kasumi: What's wrong?
Rimi: Kasumi-chan!
Kasumi: Ohh, a tourist!
Kasumi: Hello! I'm Kasumi, and I play the guitar!
Tourist: What?
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Park]

Kasumi: I'm glad we could help them find what they were looking for!
Rimi: Thank you... I froze up and started panicking. That was the exact opposite of cool...
Rimi: You're so impressive, Kasumi-chan.
Kasumi: Huh? You think?
Rimi: You looked like you were having so much fun introducing yourself and talking about the band...
Rimi: I was really happy when you asked me to join the group. But getting up on stage is too scary.
Rimi: My mind goes completely blank when everyone is looking at me, and I completely freeze.
Rimi: I can't be as cool as my sister. And if I mess up, it'll make things harder for everyone else. I'm sure I would only disappoint you.
Kasumi: Oh...
Rimi: I'm sorry.
Kasumi: Don't worry about it. I'm just happy we could talk again!
Rimi: Kasumi-chan...
Rimi: S-so my sister... She and Glitter*Green are having another show soon. If you're interested, you and the others should come.
Kasumi: A concert with Yuri-san?! Woo! Yeah, of course I'll go!
Kasumi: ... Wait... Aren't the third-year students on a trip in Okinawa?
Rimi: Mhm. Their show is going to be on the day they come back. There's supposed to be a lot of other bands performing too. I bet it'll be really fun.
Kasumi: Awesome, I can't wait!
