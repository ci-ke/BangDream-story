The basement is tidied, and Kasumi has Arisa show her Random Star, but...

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Arisa Ichigaya)

[Ichigaya Residence - Basement]

Arisa: Finally finished cleaning up.

Kasumi: Ehehe, thanks! Okay, here we go-

[Several Hours Later]

Kasumi: Ohhh, I'm so glad it's fixed~!
