band1-026 Chapter 6: Through Our Hands

After an awful ending to rehearsal, Kasumi and Saya are on their way home. Kasumi thinks about what Tae said to her.

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Saya Yamabuki)

[Walking Home]

Kasumi: Sigh~... I kinda messed up, huh...?
Saya: Kasumi?
Kasumi: When O-Tae asked me if I felt sparkling and heart-pounding, I couldn't even answer her.
Kasumi: And she was right. I didn't feel that way at all today.
Kasumi: Up until recently, I would feel it as long as we were all together... And now I have no idea how to get that feeling back...
Saya: Mhm... I can see that... Yeah.
Kasumi: Guess I should've listened to you.
Saya: Hm? About what?
Kasumi: I should've let Arisa have some time to herself like you said...
Kasumi: But I forced her to join us for rehearsal... And she...
Kasumi: (She ended up getting hurt when she didn't have to because of me...)
Kasumi: Saya, what are we supposed to do...? First Arisa and Rimi-rin, and now we don't even have O-Tae... At this rate, Poppin'Party will fall apart...
Kasumi: Sniff... Ngh...
Saya: Whoa, Kasumi! Come on! If you don't stay strong, we're in trouble!
Kasumi: But I... I...
Saya: Don't worry! This is us we're talking about! I'm sure we can overcome this! We've been through a lot, and nothing has stopped us so far! We'll be okay...!
Kasumi: Saya...
Saya: No... Everything I just said felt really fake... Even I started feeling uncomfortable partway through, and I was the one saying it...
Saya: I'm sorry, Kasumi. I actually don't know what we should do either.
Kasumi: Huh?
Saya: You know the band I was in, CHiSPA? I watched it fall apart right before my very eyes...
Saya: Or maybe... I was the one who destroyed it with my own two hands...
Saya: We were close friends, always hanging out with each other... Then suddenly, I was always running away and avoiding them...
Saya: Let's say I saw my bandmates at the other end of the hallway. If I did... I would hide...
Saya: We hung out with each other every day before that started happening. Hard to believe, huh?
(White cut)
Saya: Our band falling apart could mean... that we're unable to have the same relationships that we use to have... Relationships with friends we were always with, up until yesterday...
Kasumi: Sniff... Saya...
Saya: And that's the reason... I'm so afraid... We finally find and create a place where we belong... And I'm starting to think... I might lose that place... again...
Kasumi: No... That can't happen... We can't let that happen...!
(White cut)
(Background change)
Saya: I actually think you're really amazing, Kasumi.
Kasumi: That's not true at all... Things turned out this way because I keep doing things I'm not supposed to. I keep stepping over the line...
Saya: Well, when CHiSPA started falling apart, I couldn't do anything... Not even step over the line.
Saya: No matter what happens, you always take action right away. I have to stop and think, but you don't. Whenever something comes up, you're right on it.
Saya: That's definitely something only you can do. And while you were talking to Arisa, I just kept thinking about how impressive that is.
Kasumi: But... everything still ended up being a mess... I just... don't know what to do anymore!
Saya: Say, Kasumi... Would you like to hold hands?
Kasumi: ... Hold hands?
Saya: Remember how during the fireworks festival we all held hands? And we were able to communicate how we were feeling to each other by doing that?
Kasumi: Mhm...
Saya: Right now, I feel like I just really want you to understand... Not my words, but my feelings...
Saya: So... Here. Grab my hand?
Kasumi: Okay!
Saya: Go, feelings! Go...!
Kasumi: ... Saya.
Saya: Ey!
Saya: Ey! Ey! ... Ey!!
Saya: Well? Did I communicate my feelings?
Kasumi: Yeah. I think they're the exact same as mine.
Kasumi & Saya: I love Poppin'Party!
Kasumi: ... Saya, thank you. I'm gonna do everything I can. No holding back!
Saya: No no no. Not just everything "you" can. We'll do everything "we" can!
Kasumi: Yeah!
Kasumi: Uhm, Saya... Can I say one thing?
Saya: Hm? What is it?
Kasumi: You squeezed a little too hard... It really hurt...
Saya: Ah! I'm so sorry! I put too many feelings into it...
Kasumi & Saya: Haha... Ahahahahaha.
