band1-033 Chapter 13: Tae's Request

Poppin'Party is finally starting to become united once again. Kasumi and the others go to apologize to Tae, but...

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Tae Hanazono, Rimi Ushigome, Saya Yamabuki, Arisa Ichigaya)

[After School]

[CiRCLE Café]

Kasumi: O-Tae ran off at the end of class again today. Not a second to waste.
Rimi: It did kind of look like she was in a rush.
Saya: I hope she's here at CiRCLE like Arisa said...
Arisa: I only said that because I happened to run into her here the other day. I can't say for sure that we'll find her.
Kasumi: Welp, no point in thinking about it! Let's just check it out!
Tae: Later, Marina-san. Thank you so much.
Kasumi: Ah! It's O-Tae! Arisa was right, she's actually here!
Saya: Great! Hey, O-Tae!
Tae: Ah, you guys... What's up? What are you all doing here?
Kasumi: O-Tae... I'm sorry about rehearsal the other day! I kinda let myself get distracted and then everything got all weird... It makes sense that you wanted to go home...
Kasumi: I'm really sorry... I won't let that happen again...
Tae: ...?
Rimi: No, O-Tae-chan! Everything was my fault, really! All of that happened because I didn't realize that something was bothering Arisa-chan... I'm sorry, O-Tae-chan.
Arisa: That wasn't it either. If it wasn't for my stupid stubbornness, none of this would have happened in the first place. This was my fault... Sorry...
Saya: So please, O-Tae... Come back!
Tae: ...
Tae: ... Come back? What are you talking about? Actually, why is everyone apologizing?
Kasumi: ... Huh? W-well-
Tae: Oh, but you all have great timing. I wanted to ask you guys for a favor.
Kasumi: A-a favor? What do you want us to do...?
Tae: Let me get these~... 'Kay, here you go.
Kasumi: ...Hm? P-Paper bags? What's in them...?
Tae: What do you mean? They're concert flyers. I want everyone to hand them out.
Kasumi: ... What?
Arisa: ... Wh-what is she going on about?
Saya: No idea...
Rimi: O-O-Tae-chan... What is this about...?
Tae: Well, we're planning to put on a show, so I made flyers. I designed them by hand. Is that okay?
Kasumi: Our... concert... flyers?!
Tae: Mhm, that's right. I mean, you can't have a show without them, can you?
Kasumi: O-Tae... H-have you... been preparing these all by yourself...?!
Tae: Yep. I can take care of this kind of planning for a show just like anyone else, but the only one who can bring the band together is you, Kasumi... Right? I trusted you would take care of that for us.
Saya: Okay, so the reason you've seemed so busy recently is...
Tae: I was doing pre-show prep, yeah. But that's done now. Let's have lunch together tomorrow, okay?
Kasumi: O-O-Tae... O-Tae~...!
Tae: ... Wait, did I mess something up?
Kasumi: No...! You didn't, not at all! O-Tae...! Thank you~!!
Tae: Let's get this show on the road already. I've been looking forward to performing this whole time!
Kasumi: ... Yeah! You're right...! Alrighty, then let's get back to rehearsing, starting today!
Rimi: Uhm, can I say something...?
Kasumi: Oh yeah! I'm sorry, Rimi-rin! You're still not 100 percent better! We'll start up again once you're over your cold...
Rimi: No, that's not it... I didn't really know when I should say this, but I brought something for you all too...
Rimi: Here, Kasumi-chan. Uhh, this is Saya-chan's... This one's for O-Tae-chan. And this last one's for Arisa-chan.
Saya: Is this the score... for our new song?
Rimi: Mhm. After you two visited, my sister said something to me. She said I have to come up with an answer on my own for this kind of problem...
Rimi: And so, this is my answer. This song is filled with my feelings toward Poppin'Party. I worked on it all day yesterday and was able to finish it somehow.
Kasumi: Th-this... is amazing! It's completely different from the one you showed us before!
Rimi: Yeah. While I was writing, I was thinking about you all, and I just kept coming up with new ideas. When I was finally done, it ended up being nothing like the ones I made before.
Rimi: This is the answer I came up with. I know it's nothing like the last one, but... would you guys be okay with practicing this one, starting today?
Kasumi: Of course! Guys! We're gonna set our sights on that performance and do our very best, okay?!
Saya, Rimi & Tae: Yeah!
Tae: Ah, I made a temporary set list. Rimi's new song is first, obviously. It's noted on the flyer as well.
Rimi: Oh, it is. Right here.
Tae: I was certain you'd come up with something good. This seems like it's gonna be even better than I thought, though.
Rimi: I hope so! Thank you, O-Tae-chan!
Arisa: A-a moment...? Sorry, I don't mean to rain on the parade here, but... can I ask you a question, O-Tae?
Tae: Hm? Yes?
Arisa: If I'm understanding this right... it says here, "Another Show in that Basement!" This couldn't possibly be referring to my basement... Could it?
Kasumi: Huh?! That's where we're doing it?!
Tae: Yeah, that's correct. I went around asking live houses about the date, but no one had any openings, so that's where I decided we would have it.
Arisa: Excuse me?! You decided?! Y-you just do whatever you want without asking...?!
Tae: I already got permission from your grandma, and there are arrangements set for getting the equipment we need from CiRCLE, so it'll be okay. That's why I'm here today.
Arisa: W-wait, grandma said it's okay...? What is she doing...?! She didn't even ask me about it...
Tae: Hm? But a concert in your basement is still a concert, right?
Arisa: Th-that's true, I guess, but still... We're gonna do another one of those? Of all things...?
Tae: Alrighty, hand out those flyers, guys! Oh, I have tickets too. Give these out as well.
Arisa: ...?! Hey! How big is this stack?!
Tae: I made 200 for now... Think that's enough?
Arisa: Wh-?! 200 tickets?! O-Tae, you~!!
