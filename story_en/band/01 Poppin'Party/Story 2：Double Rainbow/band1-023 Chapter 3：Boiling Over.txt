band1-023 Chapter 3: Boiling Over

Kasumi and the others are busy writing a new song for the performance. After Arisa arrives late, the others try to talk to her, but...

(Character: Kasumi Toyama, Tae Hanazono, Rimi Ushigome, Saya Yamabuki, Arisa Ichigaya)

[A Few Days Later]

[Riverside]

Kasumi: Sigh, classes are finally over. Man, I'm so tired today~.
Rimi: Well you were up late last night, Kasumi-chan. I was so surprised to be getting a message at that time... Cough...
Kasumi: I'm sorry about that, Rimi-rin. I just couldn't think of any lyrics for our new song~. I thought you might be sleeping, but I tried messaging you anyway...
Rimi: No, that's okay. I was starting to worry. I couldn't bring together the image for the song either. I'm glad I was able to talk to you.
Rimi: Although, I still haven't been able to settle on anything concrete... Cough, cough...
Tae: Rimi... You've been coughing a bunch. Are you alright?
Rimi: Y-yeah... I think I caught a little cold though... Cough...
Saya: You two... Don't go pushing yourselves too much. You're studying for tests and you're preparing for a concert... Just doing those two things is hard enough.
Rimi: But if we don't have a song, we can't practice. And we're supposed to rehearse together in Arisa's basement today... I'm sorry, you guys.
Kasumi: You don't have to apologize! For now, we can work on getting in sync for the parts you do have... Arisa is supposed to be a little late, right?
Tae: She stayed behind to do something in the classroom. She said we should start without her.
Kasumi: Oh... Ah, that reminds me! O-Tae! Does it seem like we'll have a place to perform?
Tae: Not yet, but I've contacted a number of people.
Kasumi: Ah, I know! Maybe you should ask Arisa to help you out. Saya can't. She's gotta help out at her store! We can ask Arisa about it later.
Saya: Well, anyway, I don't want any of you overdoing it. Your body has to hold up too. Otherwise, there's no point in doing all this.
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Ichigaya Residence - Basement]

Kasumi: Saya~, can I ask you to give me a hand with something...? I can't come up with lyrics for the chorus. Right here... What do you think I should do~?
Saya: Is that the part you stayed up late thinking about?
Kasumi: It is. The idea I'm going for is something where we'll be all sparkly and heart-pound-y...
Saya: Sparkly and heart-pound-y, huh? That sure would be difficult. Let me think about it for a bit.
Kasumi: Thanks! Ah, you too, O-Tae! Help me think! Please!
Tae: Yeah, okay. I just have to come up with something sparkly and heart-pound-y, right?
Rimi: Hey, Saya-chan... Cough... Sorry to bother you. Do you think you could help me out too?
Saya: Mhm, no problem, but... are you sure you're okay, Rimi-rin? You really don't look so good. Maybe you should head home early today.
Rimi: No, everyone's working so hard. I can't be the only one to leave. And I can't even put together the part leading up to the chorus, let alone the chorus itself. I have to at least come up with something that everyone can practice.
Rimi: Uhh, so these are the two melodies I've thought of so far. The one written at the top, and-
Kasumi: Hey, Rimi-rin. We told you, you don't have to rush it. There's no point if you end up with something you don't even like.
Rimi: I know, but... I asked you and O-Tae-chan for your opinions at school earlier, right?
Rimi: So I was hoping to see what Saya-chan and Arisa-chan think too... Cough...
Saya: If I had to choose... I'd say I prefer the one on the bottom.
Rimi: I knew it! I liked that one better too. Now I just need to ask Arisa-chan...
Tae: ... Ah, there she is.
Arisa: ... Sorry to make you wait, guys... Phew... I'm beat...
Kasumi: Ugh~, Arisa~! You're late~! What have you been doing all this time~?!
Arisa: ...!! ... Sigh... I'm sorry. I had something to take care of.
Arisa: (There's no way I could say that I was studying on my own...)
Kasumi: So, Arisa. O-Tae's been looking for a place for us to perform, but she said she's having a hard time finding anything!
Kasumi: If you don't mind, could you help her out? You have time after school, right?
Arisa: Huh?! No, I don't! Just like everyone else! I have a lot of stuff to do!
Kasumi: I see~... Ah, also! I wanted to ask you for help with something. It's about these lyrics-
Arisa: Stop. Do you have to shout right into my ear? I can hear your voice echoing in my head!
Kasumi: Whoa. I'm sorry, Arisa~. You don't have to get so angry~.
Arisa: I'll leave it to you to decide! The lyrics are your job. Don't worry about what I'd think!
Kasumi: Don't say that~. It wouldn't hurt for you to help me just a bit.
(White cut)
Rimi: E-excuse me, Arisa-chan. I wanted to ask you something too. I have these two ideas for the melody leading up to the chorus, and I'm not sure which one to go with...
Arisa: ... Sigh. I don't care. Either is fine. Rimi-rin, you're the one writing the music, right? You decide... And no more asking me to do all this extra crap!
Rimi: ...?!
Rimi: ... ... I-I'm sorry... Arisa-chan...
(White cut)
(Background change)
Saya: Ah, Rimi-rin?! Come back!
Arisa: ... Ah.
Kasumi: Arisa! That was so mean... Rimi-rin's not feeling well, and yet here she is, trying her best! You can't talk to her like that!
Arisa: ... I'm trying my best too...
Saya: Ah, no good... Looks like she took off... I'm... gonna get her stuff and go after her.
Kasumi: Thanks, Saya.
Kasumi: You know, Arisa, you're acting really weird today. It's like you've been mad the whole time.
Arisa: ... You- Shut up! What the hell do you know about me anyway?! Know what?! Today's rehearsal is over! Get out!
Arisa: I said get out! Now!!
Kasumi: Arisa?! What are you-...
Tae: ... Kasumi, let's go.
Kasumi: But-!
Tae: Just come on... Get your stuff.
Arisa: ... Sigh... What the hell do they want from me...?! I have it hard too! They have no idea, and they just go on and on, saying whatever they want...
Arisa: ... Get the hell outta here with that!
Arisa: ... Just... get out of here...
Arisa: ...
Arisa: Ahh... I can't take this anymore... That was so... messed up... I can't believe I did that...
Arisa: ... Rimi... I'm so sorry... Ngh...
Arisa: ... Sniff...
