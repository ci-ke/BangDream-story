band4-019 Chapter 19: Give me another chance!

On the day of the live show, Aya tells the staff that she wants to sing. What will they do?

(Character: Aya Maruyama, Hina Hikawa, Chisato Shirasagi, Maya Yamato, Eve Wakamiya)

[Event Day]

[Dressing Room]

Staff: How are the stage preparations?
Staff: Get me ten copies of the script!
Staff: Okay!
Maya: Everyone's so busy, rushing around...
Eve: It's almost time for the show! I'm so nervous I could eat a horse!
Maya: Eve-san, are you really sure that's right?
Eve: Yes! Of course!
Hina: Hahaha~. Well I don't know about her, but I'm getting pretty excited~! I'm excited, I'm ready and I'm boppin'!
Maya: Hahaha... Well I'm just plain old nervous, myself... Uuu...
Hina: Don't worry about it! It'll be over before you know it. You've just gotta boom your way out there!
Aya: (It's finally here... and I know exactly what I need to do.)
Staff: Hi everyone. Are we all here.
All: Hello!
Chisato: Yes, we're all here.
Staff: Then let's go over the plan for today. Does everyone have their scripts?
Aya: E-excuse me...!
Staff: Yes?
Aya: Umm... I'd like to say something first, if that's okay?
Staff: Of course, go ahead.
Aya: I... I want you to let me sing!
Staff: What...?!
Aya: I want you to let me sing in today's show!
Aya: I know that I've always had bad stage fright. I know I can't deal with even the simplest of problems, but... I...
Aya: I put in every last effort to make sure this show is a success!
Aya: I'm making fewer and fewer mistakes, I'm singing parts I couldn't sing before... I'm gaining a confidence in myself that I never used to have...
Aya: All the effort I've made that has carried me this far... it has made me who I am. Aya Maruyama.
Aya: I don't have the talent. But I work extremely hard. And I don't want to see all my hard work go to waste. If you take that away from me, I have nothing left at all!
Chisato: ......
Aya: I... I am Aya Maruyama, of Pastel＊Palettes! I want the people out there to see exactly who I am. So please, give me this chance! Please!
Staff: ... I mean, the show's about to start...
Eve: Aya-san has worked so hard for this! She has practiced more than any of us! I just know she can do it! Please...!
Maya: As a member of Pastel＊Palettes, and someone who wants to see the real Aya Maruyama out on that stage, I have to ask too! Please!
Hina: Yeah. I mean, Aya-chan's good now. She's not gonna fail out there... I know it.
Staff: You guys...
Chisato: We all know there is absolutely no room for mistakes up on that stage. We know there is a risk of Aya-chan getting stage fright again. We understand that.
Aya: ... Chisato-chan...!
Staff: ... Okay. Give me a moment. Let me talk to the event staff.
Aya: ...! Thank you!
Eve: That sounds promising, Aya-san!
Aya: It does...! Thank you all so much!
Hina: I knew you'd bring a real sparkling solution to all this. I'm glad to see it worked out. Right, Chisato-chan?
Chisato: What are you talking about?
Hina: Hmm? Fufu. Never mind~.
Eve: Let's make this live show a complete success!
All: Yeah!
