band4-012 Chapter 12: Start Again

Aya comes up with something she can do for the show. What happens when she actually does it...?

(Character: Aya Maruyama, Hina Hikawa, Chisato Shirasagi, Maya Yamato, Eve Wakamiya)

[A Few Days Later]

[Station Entrance]

Aya: (We are getting better at performing, but... I just wish there was something else I could do.)
(Black cut)
(Background change)

(Memory cut-in)

Aya: Ah... Ah...!
Aya: (I can't make a sound...!)
(Black cut)

(Memory cut-out)

(Background change)
Aya: (I don't want to stand here doing nothing and just regret it later... I can't have any regrets... Ah, that's it!)
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Talent Agency - Meeting Room]

Aya: Good morning! Do you have a moment?
Staff: Aya-san. Of course. What is it?
Aya: Umm... I was wondering if we could sell tickets to our next event ourselves.
Staff: Sell them yourselves...?
Aya: There are a lot of people out there who wrote us off after our debut show.
Aya: I was thinking it would be good to have a fresh start and let them get to know us all over again...
Staff: I understand how you feel... however, meeting with fans like that is potentially very dangerous.
Aya: We can cancel the event at the first sign of danger. Please...! I don't want to give up before we even try!
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Talent Agency - Rehearsal Studio]

Aya: Good morning, everyone! ... I have something to talk to you about.
Eve: Ooh, what is it?
Aya: I talked to the staff this morning and received tickets for our next show! I thought we could go out and sell them to people ourselves.
Hina: Huh. Why, though? Can't they just buy them online?
Aya: We've been stuck inside this studio ever since our debut. I think we should show our faces and talk to our fans... let them get to know us all over again.
Aya: I want to have a fresh start, and let the people out there see the new us…
Maya: Okay. Now that you mention it, I did know a band that used to hand out their show tickets themselves.
Eve: Let them get to know us... What a lovely idea! I want to go and meet the fans as well!
Hina: Sure, okay. I guess fans are "other people," too. The more "other people" I can meet, the better.
Aya: Thank you, everyone! Let's start right away, today!
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Station Entrance]

Eve: Are we going to start here?
Aya: Yeah. Our agency owns this theater here and they said we could give out our tickets outside.
Maya: You really worked hard to get this together, huh Aya-san. Thank you.
Aya: ... Phew... Okay...
Aya: Hi, everyone~! Pastel＊Palettes are here selling tickets to our next live performance~!
Chisato: (... Is that... the band...?)
Passerby: Hey, aren't they the girls from Pastel＊Palettes?
Passerby: The band that got busted lip-syncing?
Passerby: Yeah, that one. It turned out they weren't even playing their instruments. I'm surprised to see they're still going... I thought they broke up.
Chisato: (... Are they're selling their own tickets? She really doesn't give up, that girl...)
Aya: Please come and watch our show♪
(Black cut)

[One Hour Later]

Maya: Come watch us play!
Eve: You will really enjoy it!
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[One Hour Later]

[Talent Agency - Rehearsal Studio]

Hina: Ahh~... I'm so tired~... We barely sold any tickets, too.
Eve: I heard people saying things like "I didn't know they were still around," and "I thought they broke up..."
Eve: It's so sad. We are all trying our very hardest to perform this new show... but the people have already forgotten us.
Maya: Well we spent all this time avoiding the media spotlight. I guess it makes sense that people would forget about us...
Aya: If they've forgotten us, then we need to make them remember again. At the very least, the people we met today all know that we're back, don't they?
Eve: ... Don't you feel sad, Aya-san? That we've been forgotten.
Aya: ... Of course I do.
Aya: But I want to do everything that I can to make things right. I won't let sadness overcome me. I won't let it force me to give up. And I won't let it make me regret.
Aya: Whatever happens, I just want us to get back out onto that stage.
Aya: ... Anyway, it's time to start our class! Come on guys, let's do this!
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Talent Agency - Hallway]

Chisato: ......
