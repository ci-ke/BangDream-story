band4-010 Chapter 10: It Takes More than Effort

The girls finally get a show scheduled. What does Chisato say to Aya?

(Character: Aya Maruyama, Hina Hikawa, Chisato Shirasagi, Maya Yamato, Eve Wakamiya)

[A Few Days Later]

[Talent Agency - Rehearsal Studio]

Maya: Aya-san, your timing was perfect that time!
Aya: Really? Phew... I'm so glad I practiced~!
Chisato: Hello, everyone.
Aya: Chisato-chan!
Hina: Rare sight to see you here, Chisato-chan.
Chisato: Well I just had to share with you the great news. Take a look at this.
Hina: Fresh♪ Idol Festival Vol. 8...?
Aya: I know this event. Only idols that debuted within the last year are allowed to take part, right?
Aya: A lot of really famous idols caught their big break here. It's a very important event for the industry.
Hina: Wow. You sure know a lot about it. But why are we talking about it right now?
Chisato: Because we are going to be performing at it.
Aya: We are...?! R-really...?!
Chisato: Really. I just heard confirmation from the staff.
Eve: Wow...! That means Pastel＊Palettes is going back up on stage!
Aya: It's thanks to all of our hard work! I'm so glad we spent all this time rehearsing...!
Chisato: ......
Aya: Even our teacher was surprised at how far we've come. It just goes to show how important dedication is...
Chisato: Do you really believe that we got this through hard work and dedication?
Aya: What...?
Chisato: Yes, your teacher is impressed with your improvement. That is no doubt thanks to the great effort you've all put in.
Chisato: But tell me... what part of this is any different to what you already did in training?
Aya: I... That's...
Chisato: You're doing all the same things as last time, but now suddenly you have a live performance lined up. I wonder why that could be.
Chisato: If all it takes is effort to get your debut, you'd have graduated a long time ago now, wouldn't you?
Aya: ... But, if I hadn't made any effort at all, then...!
Chisato: Enough with the effort. Enough with the dreams. You know...
Chisato: Dreams don't come true through hard work alone.
Aya: ...!
Eve: Chisato-san...!
Aya: ... Hahaha... I thought they did. I really thought they did, but... I guess I was wrong.
Maya: ......
Aya: I'm just... some kind of idiot, then... ... I'm sorry.
Chisato: ... I've got another appointment. Let's give this live performance our all.
Hina: Aya-chan? Are you okay?
Aya: ......
Eve: Is it a bad thing to make an effort? Am I not supposed to have dreams...?
Eve: None of your hard work has gone to waste, Aya-san. You've worked so hard! It wasn't all for nothing... was it?
Aya: I don't know... I just don't know any more...
Eve: ... Aya-san...
Aya: I wonder... I wonder how dreams really do come true...
