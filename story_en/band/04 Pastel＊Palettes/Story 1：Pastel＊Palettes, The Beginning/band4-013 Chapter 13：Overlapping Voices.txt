band4-013 Chapter 13: Overlapping Voices

Aya sells tickets in a downpour. What happens when Chisato sees...?

(Character: Aya Maruyama, Hina Hikawa, Chisato Shirasagi, Maya Yamato, Eve Wakamiya)

[A Few Days Later]

[Station Entrance]

Maya: W-we hope you'll come see us...
Hina: ... How long have we been doing this now?
Eve: Today makes... five days.
Hina: Five days and we've only sold this many...
Aya: Please come and see us~!
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Hina: Mm...? Rain...?
Maya: It's really pouring! We've got to pack all this stuff away!
Hina: What? I can't hear you! The rain's too loud!
Eve: That's enough for today! We should go home!
Hina: Okay! Aya-chan! Let's go home!
Aya: Please! Come and see us!
Eve: Aya-san! Let's go!
Aya: I'm going to stay out a little longer. You go on ahead.
Hina: Aya-chan! There's not going to be anyone out here in this weather! And this is no time to be catching a cold!
Aya: I... I have to do it. I just have to. Go on without me.
Eve: Aya-san!
Aya: Please come and watch our show!
Hina: Aya-chan!
Aya: ... Don't stop me. ... Please.
All: ......
Hina: ... Let's go.
Aya: I'm here selling tickets! Come and watch Pastel＊Palettes live!
Aya: (Nobody can hear me through all the rain.)
Aya: ... P-please! Please!
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Talent Agency - Meeting Room]

Hina: Ahh~, I am completely soaked through~!
Maya: I'll go get us some towels.
Maya: ... Oops, sorry! ... Oh, Chisato-san!
Chisato: Hello. ... Why are you three drenched?
Eve: Chisato-san! We were selling tickets outside by the station. But then it started to rain very heavily...
Chisato: ... Where is Aya-chan?
Eve: Well...
Hina: ... Aya-chan said she had to keep on going, she wouldn't listen to us.
Maya: She said she wanted to do whatever she could. Said she didn't want to have any regrets.
Chisato: So you mean she's still out there selling tickets?
Maya: ... Yes. She was so set on going through with it, it wouldn't have mattered what we said... I'm sorry. We couldn't get her to leave.
Eve: I think Aya-san is feeling a lot of pressure right now. Maybe we should go back to her after all...!
Chisato: ... I think you should leave these matters to the staff. I'll let them know what's going on.
Eve: Chisato-san! Are you leaving...?
Chisato: I'm sorry. I have another appointment. I'll see you all again.
(Black cut)
(Background change)
Chisato: (Why... Why do you have to go this far...?)
(Black cut)
(Background change)

[Station Entrance]

Aya: (I'm an idiot... I should have listened to Hina-chan. No one's going to buy tickets from me in weather like this...)
Aya: Come see our show...
Aya: (No one can hear me. Not that it matters, there's no one around to hear me anyway. But I just have to keep trying...)
Aya: (My hopes, my dreams... I cannot let them get washed away by the rain...)
Aya: ......
Chisato: ...! Aya-chan... She really is here...!
Aya: Please... come and see our show...
Chisato: Come and watch us perform!
Aya: ... Chisato... chan...?
Chisato: We are selling tickets for the upcoming Pastel＊Palettes live performance. Please come and listen to us play!
Aya: ... But why...?
Chisato: What? Look at all these tickets we still have. You don't want any regrets now, do you?
Aya: ......
Chisato: The rain is just too loud. We'll have to raise our voices higher, so that everyone around can hear us.
Aya: ... Okay!
Chisato: Come and watch Pastel＊Palettes' live show!
Aya: Hear us perform live!
Aya & Chisat: Please come and watch our show!!
