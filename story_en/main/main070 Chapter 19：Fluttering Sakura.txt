main070 Chapter 19: Fluttering Sakura

Rokka finishes her first student council job and meets with Asuka and Ako...

(Character: Ako Udagawa, Mashiro Kurata, Rokka Asahi, Masuki Sato)

[Haneoka Girls' Academy - Front Gate]

LOCK (Rokka): Pant...! Pant...! Sorry to keep you waiting~!
Ako: Ohh~. Rokka, you're late~!
Asuka: Good to see you. How was your first student council session?
LOCK (Rokka): O-oh...! We were compiling the records for last year's student council, but it didn't go well at all...
LOCK (Rokka): I had to upload the documents to a shared folder... Tsugumi-senpai stood next to me the whole time, teaching me how to do everything...
Ako: Ahh~, stuff like that is hard, huh~?
LOCK (Rokka): Yeah... I'm so unaccustomed to everything, so I might cause a lot of trouble for everyone...
LOCK (Rokka): But it feels like something new has started...! I'm super excited...!
Ako: Ooh~! Rokka, your eyes are sparkling!
Asuka: Do your best. We're rooting for you.
LOCK (Rokka): Thanks! I hope that from now on, I'll be able to increase the number of things I can do.
Ako: Okay~! Then, to celebrate Rokka's first student council job, let's get something sweet to eat today~!
Asuka: That's just what you want to do. Plus, we still have to ask Mashiro-chan for her vote... By the way, Rokka, is there a place you want to go to?
LOCK (Rokka): A place I want to go, umm~...
MASKING (Masuki): Hey, LOCK!
LOCK (Rokka): M-Masuki-san?! Why are you here?!
Ako: Fu, fu, fu~. Were you surprised? I called her here!
LOCK (Rokka): What?!
Ako: I figured I'd have King come with us to celebrate you joining the student council!
MASKING (Masuki): She contacted me out of the blue yesterday. Ah, pleased to meetcha. You're Kasumi's younger sister, uh...
Asuka: Ah... I'm Asuka Toyama. It's nice to meet you. If I remember correctly, you're RAS's drummer... I've heard a lot about you from Ako and Rokka.
Asuka: But now that I've actually met you... You have quite the impact. I thought some delinquent was at the school's front gate...
MASKING (Masuki): Huh~? Where're ya gonna find a delinquent like this?
LOCK (Rokka): Masuki-san... You're the perfect image of a delinquent...
Ako: You don't have to be scared. King's really nice!
MASKING (Masuki): Oh, shut it! Ya don't hafta mention that.
LOCK (Rokka): Okay. The only one not here yet is-
Mashiro: Pant... Pant... S-sorry to keep you waiting...!
Ako: Ah, Mashiro!
LOCK (Rokka): You're late. Did something happen?
Mashiro: I got carried away chatting with Nanami-chan and the others... Huh? MASKING-san...?
Ako: King's also coming with us today!
MASKING (Masuki): Happy to hang with ya, Mashiro!
Mashiro: S-same here...
Asuka: I'm looking forward to hanging out with you, too, Mashiro-chan. I've seen you on stage, but have we actually talked before...?
Mashiro: Ah... I think... this is the first time...
Asuka: Ah, Mashiro-chan. Hold still for a second. You've got a sakura petal on your head.
Mashiro: Th-thanks, Toyama-san...
Asuka: Asuka.
Mashiro: Huh?
Asuka: You can just call me Asuka, Mashiro-chan.
Mashiro: S-sure... Asuka... chan.
Ako: Okay, everyone. Let's go! Rokka, where did you wanna go?
LOCK (Rokka): I think... I'd like to eat parfaits with all of you.
Asuka: Okay. Then it's settled.
MASKING (Masuki): I know a place with good parfaits.
Ako: Ohh~, that's just what I'd expect from King! Alright~, let's move out!
All: Yeah~!
